It's been a LONG time since we were in San Fran, so we don't have any direct
letterboxing experience there. However I'd suggest some boxes by Lisascenic
and Grumpy Grinch. You might want to send them a note via "Contact the

type of hunts you'd like to be on (short, long, etc).

I'm not sure when you are leaving for your trip but we went to northern
CA in July of 2006 and found some great stamps. I would highly
recommend "Sea Ranch" by Team Tysonosaurus. It is listed on AQ.

find the clues in my stash right now but it's probably listed on AQ.
The other really good ones we found were in the Napa Valley area.
Hope that helps and that I'm not too late!
