Warning Dream

From: cjosada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-01-08 04:40:39 UTC
I have a dream to share with fellow letterboxers. Sometimes
letterboxing can have unseen hazards. My dream was this...I was
searching for a letterbox when I came to a ledge. I looked down and
realized the box was hidden under the ledge. All I had to do was jump
down about 3-4 feet to the lower ledge and the box was mine. So I did,
I safely jumped down to that ledge and found the box. Then it hit
me....I can't jump back UP! YIKES! Needless to say, this dream might
have been a warning to me to be more careful and to always think before
I leap. Finding a box becomes a compulsive challenge, but we always
have to remember to be careful, and bring along a buddy...with a rope!
LOL! Today, I'm nursing a sprained knee caused by hiking in the mud.
I slid and I must have pulled or twisted something.

Happy New Year and Safe Hiking To All!

Beetle
