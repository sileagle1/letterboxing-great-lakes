From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-01-16 04:59:55 UTC
Great day today! Had a very nice time at another laid back "Dead of Winter" event. Weather was very good - sunny, 20s and not much wind which was good for the outdoor boxes w/the subtle clues. We also had fun looking for the clued indoor boxes.

The highlight was reconnecting w/so many long-time LB friends and the opportunity to do a few new exchanges. We were also very pleased to meet the contented Atomic "beanie baby" at her very first event. Not even 3 months old yet, but she has plenty of boxes in her find count, jumping into the fun when she was only 2 weeks old!

Thanks to gggHiker for hosting another fun event!
