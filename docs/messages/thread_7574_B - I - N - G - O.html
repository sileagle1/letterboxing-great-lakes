From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-12-17 22:37:40 UTC-06:00
You use the word list to fill the 25 squares on your card:



. In order to put a letterbox on your BINGO card, you must have
either found the box or planted the box.



. In order to put a trail name on your card, you must have exchanged
with that person or BE that person.



. In order to put an event on your card, you must have attended the
event.



. You can put ANY General Term on your card.



When the words are called, beginning JAN 1, you can cross it off your card
if you have included it in one of the squares.
