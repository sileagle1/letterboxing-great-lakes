Yes! In fact, we had the chance to find 7 of the Nancy Drew boxes
while in San Francisco last summer. We found the "Haunted Mansion"
series, which is a nice loop-walk through a park in the Presidio near
the Golden Gate Bridge, as well as the "Haunted Carousel" and "Pony
Ride" boxes at Tilden Park, out in Berkeley. They're by Princess
Lea -- a prolific Bay Area boxer -- and are very nicely done. I
recommend them to anyone who's headed out there.
