RE: [LbGLK] Re: Anyone done any boxing in San Francisco?

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-02-15 17:42:48 UTC-06:00
If you want to walk along the coast, the Under the See series was nice (but
I believe one of the 6 boxes was missing and it is a 6.5 mile walk).



We really liked Redwood Regional Park and Mount Diablo, which are further
inland (but between SanFran & Yosemite). There are many boxes at each of
these locations. We would particularly recommend the Le Chemin Francais
series and the Seven Sins of Letterboxing series, but they are on hikes of
4-5 miles. Anything by OB Juan, Princess Lea, lisascenic or Doublesaj and
Old Blue should be good.
