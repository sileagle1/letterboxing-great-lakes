The purple skull guy is a phantom letterboxer whose little purple skull stamp has been mysteriously showing up in letterbox and personal log books across the country.

There has been great speculation at some of the talk lists and discussion forums as to the possible identity of this mystery letterboxer. It appears he/she has been present at several gathering but has not made him/herself known as several people have reported that the stamp appeared in their log book at the gathering, but nobody has actually met this person.

The mystery letterboxer is quite well-traveled as stamps have been spotted from California to Washington to Maine to Florida. Although they seem to be concentrated the east and west coasts, there have been a few isolated spottings mid-country and I did recently see one in a box logbook in West Michigan.

SpringChick
