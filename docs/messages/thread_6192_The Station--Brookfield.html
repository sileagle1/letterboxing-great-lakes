A few weeks ago, we decided to escape the construction chaos (and the lack of a kitchen) and go out to dinner. We went to the Station (site of the upcoming Bunny gathering). I figured it was the perfect plan--get a nice dinner AND a letterbox (beautiful stamp BTW). We asked for the letterbox, which our waitress brought right over. As we were finishing our meal, a lady (manager? owner?) came over and asked if we were some of those letter people. She started talking about the upcoming gathering--seemed quite excited about it. Wish we could come, but we will be doing our Boar's Head Festival at church.

The food was marvelous. The kids enjoyed pizza, DH had a burger, and I had the meatloaf. And we DEFINITELY recomment the beer battered cheese curds. YUM!
