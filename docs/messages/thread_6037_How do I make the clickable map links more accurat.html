From: cpjenks2001 (cpjenks2001@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-08-30 02:04:49 UTC
I just planted my first box and posted the clues on atlasquest. In the posted clue, there are links (put there by atlasquest, not me), to maps by google, yahoo and mapquest. All are pinpointing the city, not the park where the box is hidden. I tried entering the intersection that is by the park, but it must not be recognizing it. I don't know the actual address of the park. I DO have the coordinates (both as degrees and as decimals), but even if I enter that instead of the park name, it still maps just to the center of the city.

Any ideas how I can get the maps to be closer to the actual location? Thanks!

Pam

From: xxxxxxxx (BrighidFarm@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-09-03 05:53:50 UTC-05:00
This isn't a direct answer to your question but do you really need a map?
Let finding the park be part of the clues. Just give the name of the park.
Or give the coordinates and let folks figure it out that way.

As far as a direct answer goes -- many parks have an address someplace
online. If you google the park, you might bring up references that will
give you an address.
