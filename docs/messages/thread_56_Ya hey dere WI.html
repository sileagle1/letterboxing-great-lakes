From: lavandarfields (MG02zion@aol.com) | Date: 2004-03-30 15:33:32 UTC
I had the pleasure of living in Two Rivers WI, N. of Manitowac for a
year. Loved it, loved the people and when I went to California and
told my new neighbor that my cat's name was Otty, she forever after
pronounced it just like me - Oouuhdy :) I'm saying I liked the
accent so much, I took it with me! My reason for posting is that I
searched the LBNA site for any boxes located in Maribel Caves county
park. Did I miss it? Has anyone been there? I swear it's the best
kept secret in all of WI! Especially on a hot day! Everywhere you
walk in the woods, their are little vents in the ground pumping out
air conditioned like cooooool air from the labyrinth of caves below.
But beware of the stinging nettle patch, I'm thinking that was worse
than the Cholla Cactus! I'd love to hear if anyone is familiar with
the place.

thanks,
Milly still in MI
