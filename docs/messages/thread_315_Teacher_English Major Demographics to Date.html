Teacher/English Major Demographics to Date

From: moonsunmagik (moonsunmagik@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-21 13:55:36 UTC
Based on my groggy, caffeine-deficient, Monday morning calculations,
it appears that of this list, 14 folks are/were teachers, and 5 were
English majors of some fashion. We also have 2 "Tech" career folks,
and one out and proud "Mutant Life Form".

Why so many teachers? It could be the intellectual inquisitiveness
that brought us to teaching and "the life of the mind" is be such a
powerful characteristic that it carries over to our leisure pursuits
as well.... Restless brains. (Oh! What a gross/creepy/cool stamp
image that could be!)

Or it could be that given how much teachers get paid, letterboxing
is the only hobby we can AFFORD! ;)

I'm interested to hear more... So far my teacher theory seems to be
holding up.... Anyone else care to support or refute it?

Have a great day, everyone!

Melinda, Team Taco
