Michigan Spring FLING - this Saturday!

From: Tom & Jill (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-05-03 14:08:02 UTC
We're very excited and looking forward to seeing several of you at
this Saturday's mini-meet in southeast Michigan. The weather forecast
isn't looking to be quite as nice as today and tomorrow, but it's sure
to be a fun day nonetheless.

For those who like to get a head start on planning their day's
adventures, or who want to start their hunts prior to 10am on
Saturday, we've posted the booklet containing the clues to 11
temporary boxes, and all 4 permanent boxes. You can get it at:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/files/Gatherings/2006%20Michigan%
20Spring%20FLING/

Of course, this booklet will be available in printed form at the
event. Also, we know some other letterboxers have or will be planting
additional temporary boxes, so this is by no means a complete
listing. If you plan to look for other boxes outside of Maybury,
though, please bring your own clues for those.

Safe travels and happy 'boxing! See you on Saturday, and as always,
let us know if there are any questions.

--TJ_Mich, The Nickerboxer, and Little WoodChuck
