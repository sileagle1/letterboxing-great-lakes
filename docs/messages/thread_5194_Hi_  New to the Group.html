Hello and welcome Night Wisps!

We just got back from a family vacation in the Keweenaw Peninsula on
Sunday. We looked for those very two boxes, but could not find them!

post that you've just discovered letterboxing but couldn't find the two

was a large pile of pine needles and lawn debris. I even put on my
gloves and dug through the pile (did you see my diggings???) but no
luck. And we've been at this for 5 years!
