I can not tell a lie

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-13 14:04:05 UTC
In light of the upcoming President's Day, I have chosen to take the high road and follow in
the lead of our 1st President, George Washington, regarding his famous cherry tree incident,
and our 16th President, good ol' honest Abe, and come clean about my Bingo streak.

In all of the excitement of our Iditarod victory, I missed a few days this week of Bingo words
and asked Tonto to sit down with me and double check our cards this morning. As I read the
list of called words he noticed that on January 8, I had overlooked the word "PACE" on my
card! You can only imagine the shock!

So, it is with great humility that I must abdicate my crown to one more deserving. I will let
the letterboxing parliament (or the keeper of such records) make the decision as to the
rightful successor. Is it "Familyof6" with the longest losing streak from the beginning, or the
crazed chicken dancin' "Birdwoman" who has the longer number of days without words
called? Or could there be one of the "silent" bingo players that might qualify? Nonetheless,
let it be said that I was happy to serve as your letterboxing bingo queen even though I was
innocently fraudulent in my position.

TIptoe...(who hopes the Iditarod points still stand because she still only has 5 Bingo words
and has no hope of winning any Bingo games this year!)
