I have just returned from the site of tomorrow's Cereal City Circus
and I urge you to (1) join with me in encouraging the weather makers

trails.

Looking forward to another grrreat MI Fall Gathering -- TJ_Mich we will sure miss you guys, but I'm making that yummy Rice Krispie treats roll thing in your absence (they seemed fitting)!
