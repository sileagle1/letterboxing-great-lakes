Now that it's getting warmer in the Great Lakes area, I think it would be a good idea for everyone, even beginners, to design and plant a new letterbox. That would mean more boxes for everyone to find. A win win for everyone! April 1 (April Fools Day) might be a good day go outside and 'fool around' and plant new boxes, but April 1 falls in the middle of the week. Maybe we could try to plant new boxes the first week in April. What does everyone think? Is anyone else still awake? I'm a night owl.
