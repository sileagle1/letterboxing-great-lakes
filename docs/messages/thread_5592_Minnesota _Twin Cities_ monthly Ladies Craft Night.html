Minnesota (Twin Cities) monthly Ladies Craft Night

From: momblees (school@blees.com) | Date: 2009-01-26 13:49:00 UTC
On the east side of St. Paul, my church hosts a monthy Ladies Craft
Night. Everyone is invited on the 2nd Friday of the month 5 -
midnight.

Most ladies work on scrapbooks, but sometimes I work on cards or
LTCs, some knit or organize photos. Everyone brings a snack to
share. Usually someone brings their Cricut maching (you are welcome
to use the machine if you bring your own cutting mat).

Sounds like the Janna's post where there is a day set aside to get
some scrapbooking done. Lots of fun and fellowship!

E-mail me if you would like to join us!
