RE: [LbGLK] Re: Bingo - 1/27/08 and a quiz for WiHi & MM

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-01-27 22:25:15 UTC-06:00
Great quiz! Although we've found quite a few boxes in 4.5 years, it's
funny how we can remember individual boxes. We don't keep notes in our
logbook, other than box title, city & state and date we found it. But when
you described the ant hill, I immediately could picture the trail & park I
think you're talking about. I looked through our list of boxes found and
checked the clues for a couple in a specific park and then was pretty sure I
knew the answer to your quiz. I then told Martini Man about your "quiz"
since he hadn't checked his email yet after we got home from a party
tonight. He immediately said Florida, then west coast, then off the top of
his head named the same park I had come up with. Then he amazingly named
the box from memory - the same one I came up with after consulting my list
and clues! (Martini Man is definitely the person you want on your team for
any trivia game!!)

So, are we right in guessing: "You Can Paddle All Around It In A Big Canoe"
in Fort Meyers, Florida? We found this box in April 2006, so it's been a
while...

Hope you're having a great time on your vacation!

Wisconsin Hiker (& Martini Man)
