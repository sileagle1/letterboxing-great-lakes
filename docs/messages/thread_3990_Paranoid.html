I really don't get that paranoid when I am out boxing, its putting the
box out that makes me paranoid. When I was doing that gathering I had
to place 32 boxes in one day. I had no look out with me so everytime I
went off trail to plant a box my heart started to pound, and I thought
at any moment some park ranger was going to pop out behind a mushroom
to arrest me. Or some axe murderer, it was a 50/50 toss up. I get so
nervous when I plant a box I almost dread doing it.
