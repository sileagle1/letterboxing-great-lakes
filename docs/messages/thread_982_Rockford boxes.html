The box at the Arlington Cemetery is a good one, but requires tromping thru
the cemetery/ Wear your boots.... Haven't been there for a few weeks, but it
was alive and well then.

The city of Cherry Valley is right near here, as you probably know if you are
familiar with the area, but if not, there are a few there. You should stop
by The Goose Barn while you are in town and say hi! I'm Beckyjo aka Dumpster
Diva and would love to meet you. Just let me know when you might be coming so
I can be sure to be there! The address is 5311 Charles St. between Alpine and
Mulford, directly across from The First Baptist Church. There is a big Betsy
Ross flag painted on the Barn and we are upstairs in the shop! The shop is
only about 3/4 of a mile west from 4 ft Under at the Arlington Cemetery, which
is also on Charles St.

Call if you need directions and to let me know if you are stopping by.
397-9391 or 397-9353

Have a great trip.
