Hi everyone.

I lurk on this list, but I wanted to post a quick note to let you
know that an event box has been placed in Kane County Illinios. It's
called Celtic Knot and has been placed in conjunction with St.
Catherine's Irish Fest. The box is only available this weekend. You
don't have to attend Irish Fest to get the stamp but it's a fun time if
you like Irish music and merriment.

Hope you like it.

SingularGirl (and Skywatcher)




Re: Event Box in Northwest IL

From: amylouc3po (altadlock@juno.com) | Date: 2005-09-13 14:40:39 UTC
Thanks to those who braved St. Catherine's Irish Fest to get the
Celtic Knot letterbox. We realize that some may have stayed away
because of the crowds so we decided to keep the box in place for the
rest of the month. It's in the same location but check the LBNA
website for a couple of updates to the clues. (Just as an FYI... the
box is on private property --church grounds-- with permission.)

If you come during regular church office hours (M-F 8:30-4:30) and
you want to do an exchange, go into the church office (enter through
the double doors next to the sign that lists Mass times) and ask the
secretary if you can sign up for letterboxing classes. She'll let you
know if Skywatcher and/or SingularGirl are around for an exchange.

~SingularGirl
