From: (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2011-07-26 22:38:21 UTC-04:00

Ok, so what happened yesterday??

I had my hopes set on Madison, had the boxes plotted in the GPS and my backpack on the ready.

When I got to work all heck broke loose.... we had a Madison, Kankakee, 2 Chicago stops, West Burlington, IA -- WoW, we had a lot of looooonnnnggggg deliveries. I got the one I was least interested in . . . West Burlington, IA (I've been there many times already), however, there were 4 new boxes PLUS a 5th box I was unable to find last time I was in town.

I used my 1/2 hr lunch + two 15 min. breaks (one hour total) to collect all 5 boxes!!

I got back to the office and spent the last two hours of my day there :-(

I leave for the day at 5:30 and at 5:15 we got a call from a patient having trouble with a food pump. The pt lives in . . . . . . . Danville, IL!!! Holy smokes, 3 1/2 hrs ONE WAY!! I cleared it with Becki and got the green light to take it myself (even though I'm not on-call).

I printed a bunch of clues for Bloomington, Champaign/Urbana & Danville and headed out.

The beautiful thing of having a route after hours is that I can take my time and box without rush to get back to the office -- of course I subtract the time I boxed from the total time so I don't get paid for play!!

I found "Dorothy Gage" by Walkabout in Bloomington and found a couple great Cemetery boxes by CroWoman in Urbana . . . unfortunatly, I forgot my glasses and couldn't read the names on the stones in the dark. As all the clues were based on the names on the stones I had to give up after finding 3 (I count myself lucky to get 3 or the 8).

I headed straight for Danville to make my stop by the time I told the patient I would be there and grabbed a bite to eat. Then more boxing..... I WASTED an entire hour in Danville with no luck.... including the rest area just over the boarder in Indiana -- I would've LOVED to found a box in each Iowa, Illinois & Indiana all in one WORK DAY!!

Shucks!

Back to Champagn/Urbana where I picked up another 4 boxes -- a total of 13 boxes in one working day (by far a record)!!

I clocked myself out at 1:30 in the morning, but didn't actually get home until 3:30!!

What a GREAT day yesterday was!!

hx6
