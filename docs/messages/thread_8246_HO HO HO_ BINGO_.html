On Dec 15, 2015 12:00 PM, "kotlarek@wi.rr.com [LBGLK]" <LBGLK@yahoogroups.com> wrote:
 

The end of the year is approaching fast and so is the deadline for getting your entries in for the upcoming 2016 Great Lakes BINGO game.  I suspect that due to this nice weather, some of you are still trying to find a few more boxes so you can use them on your card.


As most of you know, part of our winter fun is playing the Great Lakes Letterboxing BINGO game started by SpringChick back in 2006. She ran the game for 5 years, then the illustrious Mr. Familyof6 took the reins for two years and now I'm honored to be hosting the BINGO game for a fourth  time. 2016 will be our 11th year of Great Lakes Letterboxing BINGO. We're hoping to have a lot of participants for the 2016 game, so spread the word. You sure wouldn't want to miss experiencing the slow motion thrill of victory and agony of defeat that this game is famous for!

The necessary files are ready for your use in the "Files" section of the message board (just scroll down to the "Great Lakes Letterbox BINGO! folder, click on it, then click on the 2016 BINGO Game folder).

 

General instructions are in the Bingo 2016 FAQ.pdf file, the terms to fill your card are in the BINGO 2016 Word List.pdf  file and the blank card is found as two formats: BINGO GRID 2016.doc and BINGO GRID 2016.pdf.  You may wish to use the MS Word version to type (or cut & paste) your terms directly into the grid. You can then save a copy and send it as an attachment in an email to me. Then, if you haven’t already done so, make sure you mail a booty prize and $2 to my address (see FAQ file) by December 31 or you can give me your booty and $2 in person if you want to save the mailing costs. The absolute deadline for getting your card and booty to me is DEC 31, because the game starts on January 1, 2016 and we will be traveling and unable to receive packages after that date .

We will once again have a prize for the BIGGEST LOSER this year - the person who has the LEAST squares crossed off at the completion of the game.


If you have any questions, just shoot me an email, or post the question to this board.
