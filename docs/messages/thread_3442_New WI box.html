New WI box

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-12-12 01:38:15 UTC
Woo hoo! WOO HOO!!! WOO HOO!!!

Clues for a new Wisconsin box were posted today by Kayakin' Monkey, a
very new boxer. Guess where she placed it? Chippewa
county! ...which means that WI is now ALL GREEN!! So instead of my
hand-colored map, you can see the official letterboxing.org map here:

http://letterboxing.org/BoxStateRegion.php?state=wi

(thanks to the letterboxing.org volunteers for getting the map
updated so quickly!)

I've invited Kayakin' Monkey to join this message group, which she
didn't know about. She and her mom apparently started boxing very
recently - I think she said the first box she found was on NOV 24.
However it sounds like her mom is also very enthusiastic and they
have plans to place more boxes in Chippewa county. So even though it
was the last county with a box, it sounds like there will be more.
Maybe "seeding" some of those counties will pay off with a few more
boxers in that part of the state. I know these two "newbies" already
found one of the boxes we planted on Thanksgiving weekend
(FungusWoman's tribute box to her dad).

Thanks to everyone who helped get WI "totally boxed"! We started
publicizing this little project mid-year. At that time there were
boxes in 42 counties and 30 counties without a box. Martini Man & I
pitched in to cover several of them and were pleased to see that
others also got out there to help the cause. These placers even
included some out-of-staters! Here's a list of those who helped in
the project:

Thanks for making us a GREEN state!
