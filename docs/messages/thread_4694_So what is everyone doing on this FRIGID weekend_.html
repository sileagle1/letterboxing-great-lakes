Yes, I did. On Saturday, we decided to go 'joy riding' rather than sit home. I planned ahead and printed out only one Lb clue. It was a very short hike. It wasn't all that cold this weekend. One of our extra bedrooms was turned into my "fun room." It's sewing, quilting, card stamping, scrap booking, and letterboxing all rolled into one packed space. I admire you for attempting to clean up your computer room. I'm not as motivated, although it certainly would be time well spent to tidy up in my fun room. But that wouldn't be 'fun.'

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-01-23 08:24:12 UTC-08:00
Back in Cleveland and all caught up on our Bingo words
(two out of five, not too bad, but our card is getting
a tad bit lop-sided now!) and this past weekend we
were letterboxing in Virginia. One of my cousins who
lives in the Williamsburg area got married on
Saturday, so of course I printed out some clues so we
could squeeze a few quick finds in early Saturday
morning before the festivities began.

Of course, down in Virginia it was the equivalent of
frigidness for them. All weekend long the newscasters
were going on and on about the "one to three inch snow
storm" headed their way while us Clevelanders just
laughed, "THIS is a storm??" I have to give my cousin
credit, though. His bride wanted a winter wonderland
themed wedding, complete with fake snow all over the
ground of the hotel floor where the ceremony took
place, and just as the wedding ceremony began, real
snow started falling outside. We were amazed at his
bride's control over the weather :)

Doodle Bug.
