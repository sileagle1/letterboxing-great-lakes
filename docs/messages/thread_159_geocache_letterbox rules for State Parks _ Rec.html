Jamie Rich (Sandrich) is the Migo member who is talking with the State
Parks & Rec department, so I'll quote him, and thereby not get it wrong.

[quote]
The State Park and Recreation Bureau lands; require us to register our
caches with the specific park manager. The caches may stay in place
for 1 year. There is currently a $35.00 cost, but that is supposed to
cover for multiple caches. Right now, the P&RB hasn't yet published
the revised guidelines, but we have been assured that these are the
current agreements.
[/quote]

To throw in my own two cents on this one, the only thing that I really
don't like about the current agreement is the part about registering
for a specific park, rather than for the whole park system. If anyone
on this mailing list shares my sentiment on this, please log on to
Migo and let Sandrich know.
