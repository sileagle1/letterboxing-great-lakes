From: TJ_Mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-10-15 07:52:13 UTC-07:00
Just wanted to add our thanks to everyone else's. The event had something for everyone... the day was perfectly arranged, the boxes were creative, the trails were immaculate, the weather was beautiful, the friends were aplenty, the food was delicious. We were thrilled to make the trip back to Michigan to join in all the fun.

In the end we found 5 of the Wing boxes (3 on Saturday, and 2 on Sunday morning before heading home), so were nowhere near all you pacesetters. But for us, the good times on the trail and at the lodge made up for all the frustrating moments thinking "But it HAS to be THAT log with the green end!!" Perhaps we'll have a chance to pick up a few more someday.

Thanks to all!
--TJ_Mich, Nickerboxer, Little WoodChuck, Samtrack

P.S. For anyone heading back to find more boxes - please note that our "Wing Man" and "Winged Wheelers" were day-of-event-only boxes, and were removed on Sunday, unlike the official series of 16 in the main clue packet.
