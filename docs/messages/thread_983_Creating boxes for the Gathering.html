Creating boxes for the Gathering

From: Cari Donaldson (donaldson_home@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-01-21 15:05:23 UTC-08:00
If you want to contribute a box for the Gathering, you can do one of two things:
1. create a box based on the theme, "Mo' boxes in Motown"
2. create a box based on anything else
3. create a box based on nothing, but I'm not quite sure what that would look like

As for hiding, you also have choices (isn't this sport great?)
1. send it to the organizers with enough time for them to plant and create clues
2. hide it yourself, creating your own clues to pass out (or keep secret) at the Gathering

But it'll be a regular LB, unless, of course, you don't want it to be. At the one Gathering I got to go to before I was ripped away from kith and kin (so this, of course, makes me an expert), there were all sorts of boxes. Traditional, word of mouth, super secret boxes you could only get the clues to by knowing a secret trigger word, nighttime boxes, boxes that actually defied the time/space continuum and existed only in sub-space wormholes, etc. etc.
Closer to the date, I'm sure Team Rabbit will tell you where to send them if you want them to hide it. And even if they tell you to, don't send it to me. *grin* 'Course we COULD use more boxes down here.

Cheers,
Del
