From: trailtroll1 (kanderson2000@aol.com) | Date: 2011-04-18 11:06:20 UTC
Oh Wonderful Familyof6
You make the most fabulous picks
Pulling each word
As your devoted herd
Each day waits for your magic tricks.

One hundred and six days I waited
To see what the bingo chief fated.
Hopes soaring - then dashed -
I pled, unabashed,
For favors (tho' oft I berated).

Then last Sunday "pathtag" was called.
and I was so over-enthralled
for now second place
in this exciting race
was mine I was no longer stalled!

So thank you, oh captain of games
for fulfilling all my bingo aims
You made this exciting
so now I am writing
to praise your most wonderful name.

And now who will finish third place
in this wildly exciting race?
I'll offer a prayer
for an east Michigan player
And we'll fill up a whole trophy case!
