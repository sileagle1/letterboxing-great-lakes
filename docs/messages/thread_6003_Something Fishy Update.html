From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-08-05 03:50:20 UTC
--- In LbGLK@yahoogroups.com, Toni Murray wrote:
>
> It looks like my one and only letterbox was removed. And by someone cruel. Part of my clue was about the fox family in the area; in place of the box, a fox skull was there.

Back in the summer of 2005 we found 2 letterboxes, both in separate locations in the Chicago western suburbs and planted by two different letterboxers - both familiar. Each box had a cleaned cat skull right next to the box. Being the nature nerd that she is, Tiptoe took them home to admire along with other assorted natural specimens she has accumulated. This was the start of a small skull collection requiring a designated shelf that our son affectionately refers to as his mother's "shelf of death"! BTW - she doesn't have a fox skull!

Our experience, although weird, turned out to be a nice find. However, both letterboxes were intact unlike your unfortunate situation. We thought it strange that someone would place a cat skull near the box. ( We presumed that they were placed there as no other part of the skeleton was around.) We've entertained each other with various hypothesis....

We are sorry for your loss...Tiptoe says..start a collection...Tonto says...don't loose heart!
Tiptoe & Tonto

Nobody in our family ever thought this might be intentional until I'm
reading these posts.

It was either back in 2004 or maybe 2005 that my sister did the same
thing -- was finding letterboxes with skulls next to them out in the western
suburbs. She didn't touch them however (altho she does have a collection of
sharks' teeth -- not collected letterboxing). And I don't remember what
type of animal it was. The boxes were intact, no vandalism or theft.

But it's interesting to think that they might have been placed there
intentionally. Hopefully it was maybe just a harmless taxidermist who had a
few too many skulls lying around and no skins to cover them. Or who's maybe
a geocacher and skulls are what he/she uses for trinkets.

~~Mosey~~
