From: Glenn Bergdorf (bergdorf@juno.com) | Date: 2005-12-04 10:15:32 UTC-05:00
Hello....Safari Man checking in. I and a buddy, Horse Trader, have been
boxing for about a year now and just discovered this group. We are on
the outer perimeters of several groups and a long way from most of the
action. I enjoy reading posts more than posting posts so you will
probably not hear a lot from me. We have attended one Event and want to
try more. If you are ever in the S.E. Ohio area (Marietta) give us a
shout and one or both will be glad to get together for an exchange, plus
there are lots of drive up boxes within a short distance. A bonus is
that Marietta has lots of history and stuff to do.

Glenn aka Safari Man
