WI County Update

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-12-01 04:05:07 UTC
Well, we are CLOSE to having a box in every county. Thanks to
ArtGekko from Minnesota because she managed to place a box in Barron
county on Thanksgiving Day! Martini Man & I took a long journey
after Thanksgiving to get boxes in some other counties. The weather
was quite nice, although we did need to be aware of where those deer
hunters were! We actually saw a magnificent buck on Sunday morning
when we were planting a box in Shawano county. We were on a hill
alongside a river when I heard a noise below us. I saw the buck
leaping toward the river, and quickly told Martini Man to look. The
buck proceeded to leap through the river, with the water reaching
chest height on him, and he then proceeded running on the other side
after he crossed through the water. An impressive sight!! (No
hunters in sight here.)

Well here is the map showing the one remaining county. If you'll be
in that area, please consider taking a box along to plant so WI can
be completely "green"!

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

Wisconsin Hiker
