Boxing last night

From: Pale Puppy Posse (advantagek9@voyager.net) | Date: 2005-07-21 10:20:16 UTC-04:00
Naughty dog can be good, he asked our secretary to print out boxes in the
lower SW corner as we were going there last night for some home
inspections. Well we had just been in that area not too long ago and
thought we had gotten all but the Three Oaks boxes.... well seems that you
guys have been busy this past weekend and the secretary (who is now a boxer
too.. look for The Blowfish soon!) was able to find some boxes that were
recently hidden.
We found SpringChick's I-94 traffic crawl box, flyin' down the highway...
very cool! Neat little area. It was cool to see how nature had weaved its
magic due to the storm that had just hit before we got there. Off to CMoon
and Kookaburra's Chalet on the Lake. This was more difficult to locate as
the street did not have a road sign and is called something different on
the other side of the road. Thanks to locals out walking we managed to
located the little beach and the box. Naughty dog exclaimed "no way, it
can't be over there" as I proudly produce the box for him to see! Very nice
stamp CMoon, were you just there on vacation?
Next SpringChick's circle tour box at Warren Woods. The name escapes me at
this moment. This was difficult to find as there were no signs for a state
park anywhere. Naughty dog was not paying attention and I finally found the
back side of the park as he tells me he just now found it on the map.
Geesh! It was getting dark and we didn't have much time left. We found the
spot but unfortunately too many options with not enough light. Even a
flashlight didn't help us much... have to save that one for another time.
I just love getting paid to go letterboxing...
Everyone have a good time at the Lazy Days gathering as I will be canoeing
on the Pine River and yes, I will be getting the Serendipity box this
year...!!!! Come hell or high water!!
