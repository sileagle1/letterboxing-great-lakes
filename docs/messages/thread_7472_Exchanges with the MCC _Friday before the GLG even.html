Yes, we are hosting an event before the GLG!!

See you there,
Dartmoor Dreamer of the Minnesota Camping Contingent (MCC)

The Minnesota Camping Contingent (MCC) invites you to meet fellow letterboxers the night before the GLG and get a lot of exchanges done.
Find the MCC near camping sites 48-52 at Blue Mound State Park (WI)
7:30 10 pm
Bring your own chair.
Bring your own beverage.
Bring your own bug spray.
Bring your signature stamp and logbook.
Optional: bring a treat to share (or something to bake in the campfire)
Supervise your own children.

You must have a WI State Park vehicle sticker (daily or annual)
If you drive in, you will need to park in a parking lot (contact Dartmoor Dreamer if you need accessible parking)
If you are not a registered camper, you need to exit the park before 11 pm.
