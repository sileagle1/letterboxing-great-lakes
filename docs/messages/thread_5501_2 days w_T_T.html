We have added a few photos of our own here on LbGLK in the photo section (last page
under Tiptoe & Tonto's Excellent Adventures) They are out of order, but then again so are
we! Today was right up there as one of our favorite hikes and day spent
letterboxing..Even though, Tiptoe lost her water bottle, MM lost his favorite compass,
Tonto lost the clues in the rushing water and Wisconsin Hiker just lost her mind with all of
us! We also revisited a box from 2 years ago where Tonto lost his watch! Sad to say, it is
still missing!

Lots of laughs and letterboxes..and today (unlike yesterday!) we didn't get lost ...not even
once!
We couldn't have spent a better New Years than traipsing up and down a water and rock
filled canyon with Wisconsin Hiker, Martini Man and a million other hikers! Tiptoe has
found a new calling. She became very good at greeting hikers on the trail with a hardy
and loud "Happy New Years!" to distract the boatload of passing hikers, and to alert Tonto,
MM and WiHi who were hiding behind the trail looking for a letterbox that "woodpeckers"
(our code for muggles) were close at hand. Seriously, in less than five minutes at least
eight to ten GROUPS of hikers passed within feet of us! It was a lot more effective than
Tonto shouting repeatedly at rapid fire speed "Woodpecker! Woodpecker! Woodpecker!" as
hikers were coming upon our letterboxing scene!
Great fun!
BTW...We are giving a New Years toast to trekking poles!
Happy New Years Everyone!
Tiptoe & Tonto
