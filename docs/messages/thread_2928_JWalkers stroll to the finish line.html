JWalkers stroll to the finish line

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-10 09:30:00 UTC
The Michigan team of JWalkers completed their journey "Around the
World" last weekend. And their documentation was an 80 Days first
all passport control points were stamped onto one side of the
authenticator card. Previous travelers will know what a feat that was!
JWalkers becomes the nineteenth team to conquer the worldly tour and
will soon join the 80 Days honor roll.
Check out their fellow travelers at:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_travelers.asp

Rumors suggest that the trails may be busy again this weekend on the
80 Days circuit as some teams are lining up for a run at the record book.

Stay tuned.
And congratulations to JWalkers for their success!
