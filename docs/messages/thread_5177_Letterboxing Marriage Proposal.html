Letterboxing Marriage Proposal

From: the_cichlid_guy (the_cichlid_guy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-11 16:32:09 UTC
Hi everyone, I know it has been awhile since I posted last but I wanted
to let everyone know that I just got engaged. I decided to use
letterboxing as my way of asking. I created a pirate themed letterbox
with a stamp that said will you marry me and had it planted near the
Lake Huron coastline. After she stamped the box stamp and saw what it
said I pulled out the real ring and asked her. I am proud to say that
she said yes. Thank you BrownRabbit from Team Rabbit for helping me
plant the box while I was back in Michigan.

Ryan
The Cichlid Guy
