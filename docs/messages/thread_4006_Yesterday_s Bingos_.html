Yesterday's Bingos!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-04-24 10:17:31 UTC
I've checked through the cards of the people who called Bingo
yesterday and it looks like there are 2 confirmed 3rd place winners
at this point -- Congratulations to the Waltzing Pigs and to J-
Walkers!

If anyone else had a Bingo on yesterday's word, please come forward
within the next day or two. I will be working to get the prizes out
to all winners later this week.

A recap of the winners:

Straight Line
1) 2/6/ 07 - M&8X & Pastor's Wife (tie)

"X"
1) 4/2/07 - P. Granola

Four Corners
1) 3/14/07 - Trail Troll & TJ_Mich (tie)

Perimeter
1) 4/10/07 - K of the Christmas Elves

Full Card
1) 4/13/07 - WI Hiker
2) 4/15/07 - Azobox
3) 4/23/07 - Waltzing Pigs & J-Walkers (tie)


With that this year's Bingo game is officially over!
Congratulations to all of the winners and thanks to everyone who
played and enthusiastically awaited the word each day. I'm glad you
enjoyed it; I know it is something I've come to look forward to
during the winter months. Watch for details on next year's game
around the beginning of December.

But for now, it looks like the weather is going to cooperate and it
is finally spring! So get out there and box!

SpringChick
