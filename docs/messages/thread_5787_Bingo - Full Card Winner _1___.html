From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-04-07 10:19:03 UTC
With the calling of yesterday's word, we have a confirmed winner of the Full Card game! WalesflyerMary, who was also the winner of the "X" game, has claimed the win. All of her words were called with the exception of 1, for which she was able to use her wildcard (Wisconsin).

Remember, the full card game plays for 1st, 2nd and 3rd places so don't throw your card away just yet! And don't forget about the wildcard -- if your wildcard pick has been called, you can substitute it for any word on your card.

A huge congratulations to WFMary on the win and good luck to all for the remaining winning spots!
