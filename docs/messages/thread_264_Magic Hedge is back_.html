Magic Hedge is back!

From: Matt Rat (ratpatrolhq@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-07 10:47:41 UTC-07:00
Hi all,

The Magic Hedge box is finally back in business. I
found an amazing new hiding place, but ran into some
challenging technical difficulties getting the box to
work there. At last it is set up and hopefully will
not be discovered by any of the shady characters who
are too common down there. Really, I don't want to
discourage anyone from exploring the Magic Hedge! Its
a wonderful place with beautiful Lake Michigan all
around and more birds than any other park area in the
city, but it does have its creepy side, just enough to
feel like a real adventure without going deep into the
woods.

Matt the Rat
