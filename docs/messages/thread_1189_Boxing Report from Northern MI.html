Boxing Report from Northern MI

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-04-18 16:10:04 UTC

I spent a wonderful weekend up in the Traverse City area -- checking
on boxes, finding a few and planting a few. It was perfect for
letterboxing -- sunny and warm, no bugs and not a lot of tourists yet.

Here's the run down:

Old Stronach Cemetery, Manistee (BindleBabe) -- First Finder at this
one, my second find in BindleBabe's Hauntings series -- Cool Boxes!

Two Half Hitches Knot, Manistee (BindleBabe) -- Checked on this one I
had previously found and it is in good shape.

Half Hitch Knot, Arcadia (BindleBabe) -- Didn't see this one that I
had previously found in it's hiding spot, hopefully I just didn't
look good enough or remembered the spot incorrectly.

Point Betsie Lighthouse (originally placed by Esperanza, adopted by
me) -- Had read reports on the geocaching site (this box shares space
with a geocache) that this LB was in bad shape, so came prepared to
do some TLC. Found the box soaking wet and really dirty. Cleaned it
up and put in a new log book and it's back in business. Lovely spot -
- I enjoyed the revisit!

Old Mission Peninsula (originally placed by Esperanza and Matt the
Rat) -- Checked on this one I had previously found and it is in good
shape. Found a nice little surprise here!

Asylum Tragedy (BindleBabe) -- Another in BindleBabe's Hauntings
series. This place is incredible! Just thinking about the history
and the previous lives of these old buildings gave me the heebie-
jeebies! Box was in good shape and has had a couple of visitors.
Nice find!

Central Elementary School (BindleBabe) -- Yet another in the
Hauntings series. I wasn't able to find this one. I'm not quite
sure I had the clues right and it was really busy so I didn't poke
around as thoroughly as I would have liked. Hopefully I just missed
it as I would like to get this one on a future trip!


My boxes checked and in good condition:

- *Inspiration Point (Mystery Location -- consider this a hint)
- *She Flies... (Mystery Location -- consider this a hint)
- *Whaleback (Whaleback Natural Area, Leland)
- *Cathead Bay (Leelanau State Park, Northport)
- Grand Traverse Lighthouse (Leelanau State Park, Northport)
- Old Mission Point Lighthouse (Old Mission Park, Old Mission) --
this is my most visited letterbox, partly because there was a
geocache hanging in the tree above it for a while. The geocache was
removed last year, but this continue to be a much-visited box.

(*) the asterisk indicates these boxes have not had any finders yet ;-
(

Clues to new boxes to come within the week.

SpringChick
