Unfortunately the newest changes that Yahoo! has made do not
encourage this. If you are receiving the group messages in e-mail
and have your format set to HTML (I believe this is the default when
you sign up at the group), you cannot delete part of the message
when replying. Your choice is all or nothing, and deleting the
entire body of the message you are replying to often leaves people
wondering what your comment is about.

You can get around this one of two ways:

1) Change your e-mail preferences to the traditional style. If you
go to the home page and then click on "Edit My Membership" up at the
top, you will see your Message Preference setting. The HTML setting
is now called "Fully Featured." Change this to "Traditional."
Unfortunately while this will allow you to delete portions of the
text in messages you are replying to, it does not enable you to take
advantage of some of the graphical things that Yahoo! is doing. So
as an alternative, you can...
2) Reply to messages you wish to snip at the list online.
