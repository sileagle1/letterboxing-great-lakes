From: sunnysideupm (sunnysideupm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-05-16 00:31:43 UTC
Well, firstfinderitis would be a tough disease to have around here, I have some stiff competition, if you know who I mean! Though it quite a coup when I beat them! :)

Am struggling a little with the idea that I don't have to get EVERY box in a particular area. In my case, I am reluctant to have to pay admission to get some boxes if my only reason for being there is the boxes. I've gotten some at places I'm already a member of - or I've gone on free days. Hmm, better check if the place in question has a free day.

As for the addiction nature of LBing - I'm moving to doing more of the supply-side of the hobby. I lag in the listing of the clues, so I have like 9 to write up and 8 more to plant. yikes. Having a hard time with the idea of planting my Rosie the Riveter stamp as far as (only) an hour away from me - she may end up closer to home. I spent so much time on it - and had doing this one in mind for such a long time as I wanted my skills to be up for doing the subject justice.

- Sunny

We too have many sitting around to plant. I was worried at first about planting something I thought was very good. All the hard work going to waste.
