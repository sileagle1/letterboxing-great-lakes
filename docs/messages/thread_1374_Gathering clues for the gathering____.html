Gathering clues for the gathering....

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-01 18:45:01 UTC-07:00
Since Deedle & I are headed out to Michigan a day
early for the gathering, and since the gathering is -
wow - in only a matter of weeks, I pulled out the pile
of clues from when we went to SE MI last August to
sort through what we haven't found yet. Wow, how
things change in less than a year. All the *sniff*
Delirium boxes that we never had a chance to find
before she moved, all the *sigh* new boxes that have
been placed so that we'll never have enough time to
find them all....

But I digress. I was hoping that some of the SE MI
natives might be able to let me know the status of
some boxes which are no longer listed on
letterboxing.org - basically, do we know if these
boxes still exist?

Squirrel's Stash in West Bloomfield (Oakland)

Woodward Dream Cruise 2004 in Ferndale (Oakland)
(these clues ARE on letterboxing.org, but the way the
title's written, was this a one-day-only plant?)

The Finding Your Way series in Grosse Ile (Wayne) -
these I think were planted by the historical society
or some other organization so they probably were
pulled. Darn that bridge being out when we tried to
find them in August!

Not that we really need any more boxes to look for,
but if anyone knows about any of the above, let us
know.

Thanks!

Doodle Bug.
