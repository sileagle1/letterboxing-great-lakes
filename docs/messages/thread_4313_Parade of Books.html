Parade of Books

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-07-15 03:34:40 UTC
A group of boxers, including aimlesst and LibraryGrrl, created a series of book-related boxes on a bike trail way back in July 2007. They seeded the trail with 12 boxes and a few boxes were added the following year. They encouraged others to add to the series and SunnySideUp took up the challenge. She has planted 8 more boxes so far. One of the most recent ones included a stamp carved by ArchaeoFrog and a description of a book we weren't familiar with - "The Invention of Hugo Cabret". It sounded intriguing so I got it from the library yesterday. Both Martini Man & I have already finished reading it and we recommend it if you are interested in an unusual & fun book!

Here's a link to the box clue:

http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=57966&boxname=Invention_of_Hugo_Cabret_:_Parade_of_Books_#21

Thanks to both ArchaeoFrog and SSU for bringing this book to our attention!
