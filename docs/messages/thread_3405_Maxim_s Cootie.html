The frozen letterboxers who met at Maxim's for the post gathering for
food, friendship, and a place to thaw out, may have also come in
contact with The Maxim's Cootie that began spreading around the table.
COoties will do that. If so, I have listed The Maxim's Cooties on AQ.
How many people were infected? Pass it on!!!

We received the cootie later in the evening and didn't have a chance to stamp in and pass it on that night. We will pass it on the next time we meet some fellow boxers.
