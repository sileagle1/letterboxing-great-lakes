I have a special box I'll be bringing (or sending, if it works out that I
can't make it) to the gathering. It's basically an event stamp, but will
also be visiting a few out of town friends as a postal. It's called The
Blue Dog, and its purpose is hurricane relief awareness. Those of you who
are familiar with New Orleans' artist George Rodrigue's Blue Dog paintings
will know what I'm talking about. (see www.georgerodrigue.com
) As a postal, I've made the requirement
to receive it a donation to the American Red Cross or other relief agency of
your choice. For the gathering, I'm not going to collect money, but would
appreciate (honor system) that you not stamp in unless you have made a
donation (large or small, I won't check up on you!).



Anyone who can't make the gathering, but would like to receive it as a
postal, please sign up on AtlasQuest.
