From: walesflyermary (drewes@WI.RR.com) | Date: 2008-12-21 01:17:00 UTC
Today Peabody and I went to look for Wisconsin Hiker's Year of the Pig
LB. I've tried 3 previous times - she and Martini Man checked on the
box after our third try, and replaced the last landmark. Why go out in
snow that's over our knees in places? To meet my quest to find all
the "findable" boxes in the county by the end of the year - this was
the last one! Of course we had to talk with the nice police officer who
happened to pull into the park right after us. I thought we were going
to have to 'fess up about LBing after he asked in a friendly way what
we were gonna do - we said hiking. This place is a former search radar
site that protected the skies of Milwaukee until the '80's. He
suggested trying snowshoeing as it is lots of fun, "Like walking on a
cloud". That's two conversations with police officers for me in a
week - NOT normal. The other one was a warning about lights not
working. Anyway. We found it! THANK YOU Wisconsin Hiker and Martini
Man for your help with finding this box - and for your kind patience
with my many questions and comments over the last six months!

- WFMary
