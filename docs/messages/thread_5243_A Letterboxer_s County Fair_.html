A Letterboxer's County Fair!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-08-10 19:23:00 UTC
Plans are progressing nicely for the annual MI Fall Gathering being
held on October 18! The location has been finalized and we will be
returning to Pigeon Creek County Park in West Olive, MI again this
year. This location was a hit last year because of the wonderful
enclosed lodge, the great trails and the convenient location. But
don't think just because we are going back to the same place that this
event is going to be the same 'ol thing!

This year's event theme is "A County Fair" and promises to provide
lots of old-fashioned family fun! In addition to purchasing clues with
tickets, attendees will have the option of participating in a
variation on the cakewalk game, a traditional carnival favorite. An
assortment of carnival themed boxes will await on the trails with
plenty of carnival snacks back at the lodge.

The AQ event listing has been updated with lots of additional
gathering information. We look forward to seeing you there!
