A plea for sympathy

From: (kanderson2000@aol.com) | Date: 2014-04-08 07:28:37 UTC-07:00


Have I offended dear WiFi

In some unintended way?

It would take a week to finish

If she called my words each day


Day after day the words are called

Avoiding those I chose

Is there a curse upon me?

It’s just random, I suppose.


For the words that I did choose

I wait with baited breath

But filling in my empty blanks

Is going slow as death


My selected words must be in hiding

Or, again, they’re cursed

Because my luck in this year’s game

Is, it seems, the worst


I’ve swung a chicken under the moon

And burned some hemlock reaped in June

At midnight sung a satanic tune

And offered praises to a loon.


Witches’ brews and sorcerer’s cures

And still my failing luck endures!!
