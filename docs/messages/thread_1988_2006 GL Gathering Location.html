2006 GL Gathering Location

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-11-11 15:48:14 UTC
How it will work...

Date - Saturday, June 3, 2006

Location - There have been some suggestions and bids tossed around,
but nobody currently has claim to the 2006 gathering. This is open
for volunteers and is open to anyone - even states that have
previously hosted.

I will give 1 week for interested volunteers to respond. Even if you
have expressed interest before, please e-mail me again as I am
starting fresh now that there is a specific date. A poll will go up
on Friday, Nov. 18, consisting of interested states that have not
previously hosted the Great Lakes Gathering. In the event there is
only a single volunteer from these states, that location will be
chosen and there will not be need for a poll. In the event that there
are no volunteers from these states, those that have previously
hosted will then be put into the poll. The poll will remain open
until the end of November, with each person being allowed one vote.
The location with the majority vote will be chosen as the site of the
2006 gathering.

Once the 2006 location has been established, I will put together a
state rotation for subsequent years, alternating east-west within the
Great Lakes region.

If you are interested in hosting the 2006 GL Gathering, and are able
to do so on Saturday, June 3, please contact me via private e-mail --
please do not post to the list as I do not want this to erupt into a
struggle or for anyone who may be interested to feel intimidated.
When e-mailing me, let me know how many people would be working with
you, where you are from and the general vicinity where you would plan
to hold the gathering (i.e. NE Indiana, Milwaukee area, SW Ohio,
etc.).

Thanks!

SpringChick
