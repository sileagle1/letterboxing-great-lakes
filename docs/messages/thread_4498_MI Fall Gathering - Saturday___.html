MI Fall Gathering - Saturday!!!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-10-12 18:20:21 UTC
Tomorrow is the day! Are you excited? We are!!! We look forward to
this gathering each fall to see our letterboxing friends from previous
years and meet new letterboxers in the area.

Pigeon Creek Park
West Olive, MI
9:30 a.m. - 7:00 p.m.

Note Time Change!!! We moved up the start time by a 1/2 hour to 9:30
a.m. to accommodate those who were coming a little early as
temperatures are supposed to be a bit cool and the heated lodge will
be a welcome start to the day! Don't worry if your plans revolve
around the 10:00 a.m. start time -- we will still be there when you
arrive! The end time was extended to 7:00 p.m. as we have quite a
large group coming and didn't want to have to rush through dinner.

Clues: We've been busy hiding letterboxes and preparing clues and
have a great batch of creativity in store for you! The clue packet
will be uploaded to the files section of this list early this evening.
You do not *have* to print out a copy as they will be available at
the gathering. But if you want to come early and get a jump on
finding boxes, they are available for you. If you are planning to
hunt down any of the other area letterboxes, you must bring your own
clues as we will only have clues available for the event boxes.

Food: Reminder that the potluck will be at dinner this year (5:00
p.m.). Table service and hotdogs/buns/condiments will be provided.
Please plan to bring a side dish to pass and your own drinks. There
will be no formal break for lunch, but you are encouraged to pack a
lunch for your family and welcome to picnic in the lodge or on the
trail.

Workshops/Activities: There are no formal workshops scheduled for the
event, but we learn best from one another so if there is something you
would like to share or something you want to ask/learn about, please
don't hesitate to bring it up! Also, since there is a possibility of
rain, you might want to bring along your carving materials and if the
weather thwarts our box hunting, we can sit around a carve for a while.


Things to remember:

Potluck food item
Drinks for your group (no alcohol at this park)
Clues for other area boxes
Carving stuff, etc. (rainy day activity items)
Warm clothing


Directions to the Park (from the north or south):

Take U.S. 31 (NOT U.S. 131)
Turn east onto Stanton Road -- north of Holland/south of Grand Haven
Park is located on Stanton between 128th and 120th Ave.

If anyone gets lost on the way to the event, you can call my cell
phone at 231-327-8068.
