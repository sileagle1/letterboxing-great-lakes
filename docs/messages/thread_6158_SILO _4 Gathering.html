SILO #4 Gathering

From: carol_beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-11-11 02:43:52 UTC
What do midwest letterboxers do in the winter? They head to Lake Bluff,Illinois for the annual S.I.L.O. #4 Special Indoor Letterboxing Outing.

Tentatively, SILO #4 will be held on Sunday, February 21, 2010 at the Silo Restaurant. Your co-hosts Beetle and Lock (Lock Shock Barrel) have met and the planning has begun.

Event theme: Comic Relief!!! In the winter, we all need some 'comic relief' because it's increasingly, sometimes impossible, to find letterboxes in the midwest during the winter. Event stamps will focus on comic book characters and comic strip characters ie. Superman using his xray vision to 'cheat' and look for letterboxes hidden inside trees.

Lock will be posting the event on AtlasQuest soon. Mark your calendars now. See you then.

With LOVE From Me To You ---Beetle
