Stinging Nettles

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-08-12 02:14:14 UTC

Now, I know many of us on this board have had unpleasant encounters with
stinging nettles. How many of you would be brave enough to try eating
this pizza?

Wild Nettle and Pecorino Pizza From Pizzaiolo: Oakland, California
Five years ago, Chez Panisse veteran Charlie Hallowell stripped a
hardware store down to its bare brick walls, installed a wood-fired oven
and started producing spectacular seasonal pies. For this winner, he
starts with mozzarella and pecorino and adds shaved red onions and
fistfuls of stinging nettles, a spiky-leafed herb. Cooking neutralizes
the nettles' sting and wilts them into a tangle of flavorful greens.
$17; 5008 Telegraph Ave.;

I'm not sure I could trust that those nasty things could be
"neutralized" into something tasty...

Wisconsin Hiker
