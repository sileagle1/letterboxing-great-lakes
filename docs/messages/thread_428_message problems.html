I think it's a generic Yahoo problem -- this kind of problem is quite
common for Yahoo Groups users. I've noticed the same thing today with one
of my Yahoo groups where I was not getting messages that were on the site;
on other groups I was getting replies before the original messages. But
within the last hour or so, I received several dozen Yahoo Groups messages,
including ones that had been posted hours earlier.

It might also be a problem somewhere between Yahoo and your ISP where your
messages are being delayed (and this might be the case even if you are
receiving other email messages fine). You can check to see if this is the
case by going to the LbGLK groups site, then clicking "My Groups" on the
right side above the blue bar, then click "Email preferences." Click
"Bounce history" (for the email address you use with the group, if you have
more than one email address set up in your Yahoo profile) and see if there
are a lot of soft bounces today for your messages. (A "soft bounce" just
means that Yahoo couldn't connect to your ISP's mail server for some
reason; they will try several more times to connect before they give up and
don't send a message. A "hard bounce" is one where your ISP's mail server
says that they have no account with your user name, for instance.)
Occasional soft bounces are normal, so there's nothing to worry about, but
if you have a lot in recent days, it may be that your ISP is having problems.

My guess is that this is a Yahoo problem that will go away soon enough....
