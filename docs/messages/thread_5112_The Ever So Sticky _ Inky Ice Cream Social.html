The Ever So Sticky & Inky Ice Cream Social

From: mullygang (chrissy6253@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-06-11 03:30:50 UTC
Want to get together with some people that are crazy about letterboxing
and have some yummy ice cream too? Then this is the place for you!

Please join us for a memorable afternoon getting sticky & inky together
at the Yum Yum Tree in heart of downtown Brighton. Address is 140 W
Main Street, Brighton, MI.

http://yumyumtreebrighton.com/index.html

When: July 12th, 1:00-4:00 PM

There are many boxes located within a short driving distance of this
location. There is also a beautiful board walk around the Mill Pond.
Across from the Millpond is the Imagination Station, a giant wooden
playstructure.

This event has been prompted by the availability of "mudflinginfools",
from Maine, traveling ice cream social stamps...complete with cooties :)

See you all then! It can now be said "the more the merrier & cherrier",
he he he!

Mully :)
