I was all set to do a Ms. PacMan box (since I do love that game--even
got it for Christmas 2 years ago), but I may have to seriously think
about doing "Where's the Beef!"--since it has a connection with one
of our boxes and because I have such funny memories of that phrase
(so I can't believe I forgot that phrase in my list of 80's stuff).

In the early 80's, I was working as a nurse in one of the big
teaching hospitals in Chicago (still am--just a different hospital
now). One of the interns with a warped sense of humor told one of my
very demented patients that if she needed her nurse, she should just
yell "Where's the Beef". Well, I was sitting in the conference room
doing discharge rounds when I hear this voice in the hallway
bellowing "Where's the Beef! Where's the Beef!". I go out into the
hallway and see that it is my patient doing the yelling--and the
intern is REALLY rolling on the floor laughing when he sees my face.
Frankly, I can't believe that she remembered what he told her. We
medical folk do tend to have a sick sense of humor about our
work :~)
