Trolls

The beginning of time the two great monsters, Sauli and Taina, sauntering
Nuuksio forests. At that time the area is not known Nuuksio - Perhaps it was
not at all a name. Trolls asustelivat peaceful woods eating berries, pine
cones and pebbles. Big, of course trolls were, but quite harmless.
One day, Sauli and Taina angled ruuhessaan lake. It was quite a punt, punt
when trolls were: measuring over 73 feet! Ummoinen fish very fortunate not
Saul and Tainalla been a few funny perch Taina was received. Between the
sunny day rose into the sky suddenly a large black thundercloud. Sauli and
Taina htkhtneet are tiny, but continued quietly angling, although the
thunder jyrhteli quite close. But then the cloud began to vihmomaan
granules. They were initially quite small, but soon grew to egg size. While
the trolls were big and strong, this was already a little too much. Occurred
in the skin of both exported! Was forced to get out of the deaf over.
Trolls meloivat wildly in the west end of Lake soukkaan, ruuhen pulled
ashore and left to run jolkottamaan forest along the road. Dwarven mine tiny
railway ran thereabouts. Sauli and Taina rymysivt road until the railway
came to an end and the road turned left, south. Trolls decided to go to bend
to the right exporting track. As soon as they have already passed Vihdin
maahisvaltakunnan border. But the hail only continued.

Showed how the trolls? At the end of the story you will find Trolls,
lodjusta. Spot, which ended up in that trolls, the chunks of stone that
formed the rock wall beside the cave. When you walk cave direction 102
degrees (1700 points to), you'll rock at the foot of the birch will grow -
and you'll see lodjun. Careful not to break moss!



Seasons: Whatever.

Severity: Nice outing, as long as you have an iota of wit and good
hoksottimet.
