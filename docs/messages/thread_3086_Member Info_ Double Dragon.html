We are Double Dragon (DD). My name is Carrie and my husband is Jamie.
We have been boxing for over 2 years. We live in SE MI. near lake St.
Clair. We have no kids, but do have a wonderful cat that we consider
our daughter. The fist letterboxes we did were The Rabbits. Soon we
were hooked (or at least I was). We only have a couple of boxes
placed, and are just now working on carving our own trail stamp. One
of the most interesting places we boxed was Hawaii on our honeymoon.
We never have been to a gathering but decided "what the heck we'll
give it a try". So we planed the SE MI fantasy gathering. I'm glad we
did, it was lots of fun and I got to meet some more obsessed LBers!
I'm looking forward to meeting more!
DD
