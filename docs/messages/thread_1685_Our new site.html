Our new site

From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-08-08 14:03:24 UTC
Hi all,

B and I have started a blog to record all of our letterboxing
adventures at: http://jnbbook.blogspot.com/. At this point, it is a
little content-lite, as we are mostly using it to keep track of our
finds (on the Boxes Found link) and to keep a journal of what we enjoy
about 'boxing for our family and friends. However, we will be adding a
Boxes Planted link very soon (we have our first stamp almost all ready
to go!) and have big plans for several boxes in the Ann Arbor area. We
will probably post the clues here, as well as on the LBNA site. Also,
we are noting which boxes we have been unable to find (sadly, we did
have one come up missing this weekend!).

Anywho- enjoy!
J&BBooks
