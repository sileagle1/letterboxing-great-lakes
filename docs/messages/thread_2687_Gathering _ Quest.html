From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-06-19 03:31:39 UTC
First, we like to announce that the "Migrating Coconuts" hitchhiker
has migrated a short distance and is now located in the "Run Away!"
box. If you don't know what this is about, check this link:

http://geocities.com/the_lb_dragon/Search_for_the_Holy_Grail.htm

Second, we picked up two of the Gathering temporary boxes, so
the "Mock Turtle" and "Marriage is a Wonderland" boxes are no longer
available. They will be mailed back to their creators sometime this
week.

Third, it seems something else "migrated" besides the coconuts. We
had received some reports that one of the Gathering boxes might be
missing and we were dismayed to hear that it was the Cottontails
box. They have had such bad luck recently that we REALLY hoped we
could find it today. Hurray!!! We did in fact locate it. It wasn't
in the same spot we originally hid it, but had migrated to the side
of one of the fallen trees. When I looked at the log, I saw that the
persistent Lizards WERE able to find it yesterday, but for those of
you who missed it - it is now back it the original location described
in the clues. Hopefully you will have another chance to find it
before the end of the month.

Fourth, there are two temporary boxes still in the park. Since we'll
be out of town over the 4th of July weekend, they will remain in the
park until July 6. This will give you a few more days to find them
if you haven't done so already.

Wisconsin Hiker
