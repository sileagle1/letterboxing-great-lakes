As if the GLG wasn't wonderful enough, I also got to pick up my booty from winning Bingo this year.  What a haul!  It included:

- a box of stampin' pastels

- a deck of wilderness survival cards (do YOU know how to survive frostbite, signal a helicopter and recognize the symptoms of dehydration?  I do!)

-$5 Michels' gift card (very useful)

- $10 Subway gift card (yummm)

-$15 Starbucks gift card (frappucino - yay!)

- a block of white carving block

- a lock-n-lock, eraser, 2 inks, 2 logbooks, 4x6 plastic baggies, and static shield bags 

- keyholder wristband, chapstick

- awesome fake book to use an a letterbox indoors (library)

- no-see-um head net - these are dorky but SOOO effective

- packets of hand warmers and mosquito wipes

-- and a supercool coffee mug with the Letterboxing Great Lakes logo on it.


Thanks again to WiHi for running the game, and delivering my goodies, and thank to all you players who contributed such wonderful loot!
