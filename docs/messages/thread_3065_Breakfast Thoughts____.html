Americans buy 2.7 billion packages of breakfast cereal each year. If
laid end to end, the empty boxes from one year's consumption would
stretch to the moon and back (beginning and ending somewhere in west MI).
Americans consume abut 10 pounds, or 160 bowls of cereal, per person
each year. But America ranks only fourth in per capita consumption.
Ireland is first, England ranks second and
Australia is third.

Fascinating!
