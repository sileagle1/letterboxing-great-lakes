Are you playing bingo this year? if so, you need to sign up ASAP as the game starts on January 1st. Hey...I'll even make it easy for you. Go to the files section and look for the bingo FAQs, rules, word list, gameboard, etc. You can open up the bingo grid document, fill it out on your computer, save a file, and then email it to me by the deadline. Then, mail out the paper copy, the fee, and prize later. it is that simple. Ideas for prizes? Giftcards!!! (Gas, retailers, etc.) they are cheap to mail and can go right into an envelope with your gameboard.
