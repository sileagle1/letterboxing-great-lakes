From: xxxxxxxx (BrighidFarm@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-10-19 08:53:22 UTC-05:00
Well...............I do have 2 extra rolls of it that I picked up fairly
recently at the Walmart in Villa Park.

I won't be using it anymore this year -- ended up with all my boxes covered
and these 2 rolls left over. If anyone needs it for this year and is having
trouble finding it, I'll gladly just stick these 2 rolls in a small box and
mail it out parcel post/standard mail......free of charge.......because now
I feel guilty....... :-) I'll have to drop over there and see if they have
anymore. At the time, I DID tell 'em to reorder it. Really truly I DID.

I went in there once myself to buy it and got told by a young man that
someone else had been there the day before and bought 5 rolls of it, and he
couldn't figure out what the heck for. I've gotta say, I've never bought
FIVE rolls at once. But I have bought four.

I don't know if it was a letterboxer, a hunter, or just a guy who wanted to
dress up in camotape for Halloween who bought the 5 rolls. The young man
said the guy was about 40 years old, tall, and skinny, crew cut hair,
wearing blue jeans, t-shirt, and work boots. So that could fit a lot of
folks. I said I was amazed he remember the guy so well. The young man
replied that he was a little worried that the guy might be a terrorist of
some sort. yikes. Had to wonder if that's the impression that I give when
I'm buying the tape? Would a fat, bald, old lady with rolls of camotape in
her arms appear to be a terrorist? :-)

So, anyway, Bali, if you would like a couple of free rolls, email me. :-)
Or if Bali doesn't need it, maybe someone else does.
