Duck Tape *sniff*sniff* MISSING!

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-14 08:09:51 UTC-07:00
Yes, it appears that The Duck has flown the coop.
Deedle & I took a break from our wedding planning
mayhem this past weekend to do some letterboxing
Toledo-way, and on the way home we had a chance to
check on our Duck Tape box. His home, the 6 trunk
tree with his middle initial carved into one of the
trunks, had been remodeled - and we don't think the
remodelers had his permission. Two of the trunks had
been sawed off, and a third, the one with his initial,
had been cracked off. The Duck was nowhere to be
seen.

We will probably replace this box, since The Duck is
near and dear to our hearts, but he won't make a
reappearance until late summer at the earliest.

The Duck may be gone but not forgotten, and thank you
to all who made the journey to visit him!

Doodle Bug.
