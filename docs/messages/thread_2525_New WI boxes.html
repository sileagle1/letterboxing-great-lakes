New WI boxes

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-22 03:12:00 UTC
Just a note to let you know that Tim the Enchanter has now been
placed in the Green Bay area as part of the Grail series, and the
clues have been posted on the Grail site. Considering the wonderful
weather we had this weekend, I expect to get a bunch of new Grail
clues in the next couple of days (hint hint)!

Although the Tim stamp was created by me, it was planted by
Papeseed. I just also happened to take Saturday off of school work
and went boxin' up to Green Bay. Not being able to cross the river
to get to Sir Galahad, I spent some fruitless time trying to find
Push the Pram-a-lot. Although I did not find the baby, I did have
my first experience of walking in on a couple in the woods there
trying to make a baby... anyway, we're hoping that the Pram did not
meet an untimely end.

While in GB, I also took the time to finally plant my Art in the
Park series of tessellating stamps. The six box series is dedicated
to little Escher who went out today and found them with his parents,
who now stand ever so close to F100.

I hope you all had a great weekend of boxin' like I had!

Dragon
