From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-04-06 21:48:38 UTC-06:00
Lbers were out looking for my boxes, but I, Beetle, was at a place called Wonderland in Camp Lake, WI. conducting a Lb workshop and mini letterbox gathering this weekend. Now, there are a dozen new Lb addicts who are well educated in the proper etiquette of being sneaky. This weekend, I placed 4 temporary boxes, but I was also able to place another permanent, traditional box for everyone to seek and find. I gotta add it to AQ tonight. The hardest part....where is Camp Lake, WI? Blink and you'll miss it. LOL!

All that warm sunshine felt so good today. I was driving home and I drove past three locations where I knew there were new letterboxes by Lock Shock and Barrel. GRRR...and I didn't have the clue sheets with me. Then I thought, such a nice day, there's bound to be a letterboxer dropping by the area soon, maybe I should just sit and wait for one to show up. LOL! But I went home instead. I didn't think I could keep my eyes open nor did I think I had enough strength left in me to hike and seek. I am totally sleep deprived from all the fun I had this weekend in Camp Lake. 6 1/2 hours sleep over three days....YAWN!!! Fun has it's price.

Beetle
