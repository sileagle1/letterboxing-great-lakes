I don't know if it will help or not, but you can tell the kids that there
are no reported cases of West Nile in humans in WI this year and it
hasn't been found in any mosquitoes there to date either (it has been
found in birds, but you don't have to tell them that). If they are old
enough to keep a watch on these things themselves, the CDC
(http://www.cdc.gov/ncidod/dvbid/westnile/) updates their country map
weekly and the WI Dept of Health and Family Services has information
including a state map by county at
http://dhfs.wisconsin.gov/communicable/WestNileVirus/Index.htm
(I've no idea how often they update their info). The CDC also has links
to other's states' websites, if you want to see what's going on elsewhere.

Katy
