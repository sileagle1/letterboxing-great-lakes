From: (bweller1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-10-18 11:07:13 UTC-07:00
Kudos to all who worked on putting together another great event! It was a rousing success, as always! Great boxes, great clues, a great collection of boxers and great food! What more could we ask for? The weather was perfect for boxing, too! (My water bottle was still frozen when I got home at 10 pm!) Many thanks to Azobox, Springchick, Doglvrs, Grandvilla and Mobiusklein for putting together this great day of boxing and comaraderie.
Special thanks to our boxin' buddy for the day, TrailTroll, for putting up with us all day and helping us find all the boxes and many 'regular' boxes, too! We really enjoyed her company and swapping boxing stories with her.
It was great to see everyone again and to spend time sharing stories with all of you.
Already eagerly anticipating the next event!
JustHappy2CYa
