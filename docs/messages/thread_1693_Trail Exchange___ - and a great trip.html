Trail Exchange!!! - and a great trip

From: M Clan (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-08-11 23:06:39 UTC
Well, we've heard about them, but never thought we'd experience it.

We found box #3 in the Henes Park Series in Menominee, MI and were
replacing it in its hiding spot when we noticed four individuals
scouring through the woods. Mushroom hunters? Nope - fellow
letterboxers. I went up to them and said, "Looking for this?"
because they had obviously found the hiding spot. It was "Loki's
Legion" - new boxers from SE MI and we got an on-the-trail stamp
exchange.

Our trip from WI -> MI didn't have a ton of letterboxing, but we did
find the following:

Henes Park Series (3 boxes) - our clues said that box #1 was
missing, but after the exchange with Loki's Legion, they let us know
it had been moved and graciously gave us their clues.

Do the Wave (Indian River, MI) - nice rest stop and a hitchhiker
(Tree Tops).

MI-CAT (My Cat) - what a log book! Neat to see a lot of you there.
Great stamp!

Sail Away - another quick Mackinaw City box with a great stamp.

The Old Well - great roadside park on Lake MI.

Beach Bum - loved the stamp, TJ_Mich. Thanks.

We're excited because there's a half dozen new boxes in SW WI upon
our arrival. Hoorah!
