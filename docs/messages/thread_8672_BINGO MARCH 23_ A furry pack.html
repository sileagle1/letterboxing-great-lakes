BINGO MARCH 23: A furry pack

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-03-23 07:07:39 UTC-05:00
A matron of a pack,
Who takes the lead,
With northern cubs,
she's a rare breed,

A box she's hidden,
near a paciderm,
Is worth the stop,
I can so affirm.

I - Hartwolfzell
