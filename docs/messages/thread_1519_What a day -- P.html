Took the long drive from Lansing to WI today and picked up the
following boxes:

1. Fenner's First -- Lansing, MI (Really nice nature preserve; got
there before the Nature Center opened and had to wander the trails
mapless guessing which was the biggest rock. Got it after a couple
extra loops.)

2. Brokey Key Letterbox -- Lansing, MI (WI placer, WI finder; nice,
quick stop)

3. Giant Caster - Lansing, MI (Last time I was there, there were too
many people to root around; got there early and found it no problem.)

4-8. Superhero Series - Holt, MI (Great series, quick, interesting,
but "Holy mosquitoes, Batman!"; I set world records with stamp-in times.)

NOTE: Lansing's starting to get some really nice boxes! Way to go.

9. Asylum Birds - Kalamazoo, MI (Couldn't find a few numbers, but
figured it out anyway. Great hike!)

10. Home in the Pines -- Kalamazoo, MI (Was looking for "Al Sabo Land
Preserve" and found the sign near this box; picked it up, but never
could find "Al Sabo".)

11. GLK Exchange: Octagon House -- Portage, IN (Wonderful conclusion,
great stamp, really interesting box; I need to find more from this
Exchange.)

12. Bonus Box -- ???, ?? (a very welcome surprise)

Wow was that fun, but I'm tired. This is by far my most "productive"
day of letterboxing. Aside from gatherings, what have been some of
your "productive" days? I'm sure they dwarf mine.

***I put "productive" in quotes because I've had some days were I've
only found one box, but have felt pretty dang productive.
