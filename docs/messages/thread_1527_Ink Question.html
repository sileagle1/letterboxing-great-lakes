Ink Question

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-06-28 23:20:09 UTC-05:00
Tap your stamp all over the pad's surface (to avoid over-inky areas as well
as under-inked spots). If you are using a dye-based pad, store it level and
upside down so the ink stays near the surface. Also keep it cool - avoid
hot areas. Some people refrigerate their dye-based pads in hot weather. I
do not believe it is necessary to store pigment based upside down, but I
don't know for sure. Does anyone out there know?
