If it's a ballad you require,
I do my best to comply, my sire!
You're correct that I did not pick Ms. Pickett,
and your choice has again left me in the thicket.

Yesterday's sonnet was less than flattering,
So I knew my hopes were at risk of shattering.
Now please, bingo man, I do beseech you -
How on earth can I try to reach you?

If you forgive my past mud-slinging
Then again to hope I may be clinging.
If the "dope" epithet is stuck in your craw -
At least I've got wine on Leelanau!

Lady TrailTroll
