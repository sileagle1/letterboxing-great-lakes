I am thinking of putting together a mini-meet for October. Here is
the synopsis:

October is national popcorn month, national desert month, and national
pizza month so I am thinking of putting together a mini meet around
those themes. It would start off with us meeting for popcorn and a
movie at our downtown theater, The Majestic. After the movie we could
go the ice cream parlor across the street for some letterboxing
activities, or if I can get the community center at our city hall we
could go there for letterboxing and everyone pitch in $5 for pizza, or
the third option go to the community center and everyone brings a
desert dish to pass.

I am waiting for the owner/operator of the theater to post his October
movie showings and then will go from there as far as scheduling, etc.

Sil Boxer
