A lousy day boxing

From: Sharon (Rhody) Jacksack (sjacksack@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-12-31 23:13:12 UTC

.....is still better than a great day working!

We went out to Graue Mill today to find Pippi's box (wave), despite
the fact that it is closed for the season, there were swarms of people
enjoying the warm weather. So, we didn't look too hard. Not hard
enough to find it, anyway. Despite that, we spent nearly an hour and
a half walking along the salt river, the girls jumped from stone to
stone, climbed around on the old wall pieces left by the river, found
clams, feathers, and other "treasures," and became acquainted with a
lot of dogs out for walks. It was a fun way to spend a very warm New
Year's Eve.

Thanks Pippi, for getting me outdoors today,
and a warm Happy New Year to all of you!
