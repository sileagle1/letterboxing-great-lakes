As for things to see in the U.P., I can recommend some...I'm sure NorseGnome can do so, too.  If you are going back via US-2, you could stop in Manistique.  Kitch-iti-ki-pee (sp) is nearby, as well as Fayette, and a few boxes.  The Upper Crust Deli is good to eat at there, but has limited hours.  There is a nice hike about 8 miles long, maybe 10, about a half hour away, called Bruno's Run.  I want to plant boxes there, just haven't yet. 

There's plenty to do/see up top along Lake Superior.  If you think you may get up there, let me know.
