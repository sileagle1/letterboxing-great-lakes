BINGO - Ways to Win

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2014-01-17 13:49:16 UTC-06:00

Since over 1/3 of the players have 5+ words crossed off on their cards, I figured it would be good to remind you of all the ways you can win a Bingo:

 

THE 6 WAYS TO WIN A PRIZE ARE:

Squares Needed

1

4 corners: first person to have ALL 4 corners of their card filled in

4

2

Straight line: first person to cross off 5 items in a straight line anywhere on their card (across, down or diagonal)

5

3

6-Pack: first person to cross a 2x3 (or 3x2) block of items, anywhere on their card

6

4

"+": first person to cross off the items in an "+" configuration (N column and middle row)

9

5

10 Spot: first person to mark off 10 items on their card – no special configuration, just any 10 items within the 5 x 5 grid

10

6

Full card 3 winners

25 (can include wildcard)

Wisconsin Hiker
