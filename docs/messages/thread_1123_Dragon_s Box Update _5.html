Here's more info for those interested...

I started the day by checking on Calatrava/Green One and I'm happy to
say that Calatrava is there (cleaned up with new baggies too), but I'm
sad to report that the Green One is completely gone. I have plans to
replace Green One as soon as I contact Birkie Bob and Moofie and Blaxe
(read on for info on them!)

German LB - ok, two visitors so far...

MCP/Oak Leaf Grant Park - found the area that it is in to have
sustained considerable damage do to weather. Also found the box on
the ground out of place, so I found a new location for the box and
updated the clues. If you thought this one was missing, it was not,
so look up the new clues and go find it!

Then I traveled to check on Stampin' Kitty as I heard it was gone.
I'm happy to report that it is still in place. I cleaned it up and
gave it new bags too.

Since I was in the area, I decided to actually go and FIND a box! So
I went to downtown Racine and found Joshua'a Journey. What a treat!
Good job girls! Give us more!

MCP/Oak Leaf Mitchell Park - replacement box seems to be doing fine in
its spot. Only one visitor to new spot!

MCP/Oak Leaf Doyne Park - found it with a huge white bag around it,
apparently to keep the water out because the box wouldn't close. Why
wouldn't the box close? Because someone turned it into a geocache and
left a bunch of kids toys! It is now a letterbox once again and in
working order.

Then I stopped to do Figure Eights. This mathematician couldn't get
the math to work either, but I guess I used the wrong number too (note
the Czeltic Girl, that number IS an ID number too, not a phone number,
as you find out doing the TTE series).

AND!!!!!!!!!
MOOFIE AND BLAZE ARE ALIVE!!!!!!! THEY WERE AT FIGURE EIGHTS ON
3/27/05 DOING THEIR "FIRST BOX OF SPRING"!!!!!! WILL YOU TWO PLEASE
POST TO THE LIST SO WE KNOW HOW YOU'RE DOING???!!!!

ahem, ok now, then I went on....

Watery Grave - ok
Squished Pennies - ok, lots of trains again

New WI letterboxers! - Tooters Too from Tosa and others!

I then snuck in to check the Creative Writing box and HH Hostel. If
you haven't been there for a while, you might want to go check those
boxes again. I removed the mechanical device from CW because it was
FULL!!! Time for some fun!!! Watch for a post to the GL site soon.

MCP/Oak Leaf Hansen - ok

Mathman 9 Lockbox - missing as feared. I have plans to replace it.

Mathman 10 Zoo Box - didn't check, but still assumed gone. I have
plans to replace it yet too, but I need to talk to the students
involved next week when school is back in session.

Whew! One more day of vacation and just a couple of boxes to check
yet by home! THEN I CAN GET OUT AND BOX!!!!!
