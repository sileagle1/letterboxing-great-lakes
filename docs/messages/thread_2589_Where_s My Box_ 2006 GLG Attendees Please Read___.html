From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-05 19:09:18 UTC
Ohhhh...This may be helpful in finding the missing box...sometimes
at gatherings (like this one) when several traveling parties come
upon others stamping into a box, the first group hands off the box
to the next giving only a "it goes back in between those two logs"
type of instruction. (In hindsight, probably not a good idea...) As
I recall, help me here Howling Coyote, but we came up to a large
group with CMoon finishing up stamping in the "Through the Looking
Glass" box. CMoon gave us the verbal instructions which we nodded
that we understood the location, and we proceeded to stamp in. Mind
you we were all exhausted at this point of the evening! As we were
finishing up, FungusWoman and troop came by to stamp...We again gave
her the verbal instructions. My guess is that the box is hidden
around the log, but quite possibly not in the exact spot it was
supposed to be. Anyone going out that way please look all around
that big log! FungusWoman... maybe you can give more exact
instructions..or did you also give verbal instructions to yet
another group?
This is the letterboxing equivalent to "playing telephone"!
Hope this clears up the missing fabulous box! My guess it is still
there just in another off location.
Tiptoe
