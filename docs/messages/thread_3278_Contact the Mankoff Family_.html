Now, on a personal note... I would strongly encourage you *not* to
write vicious and venomous notes to this family. Okay, they messed
up (at least in our opinion), but throwing darts is not going to
change what is done and really will make us (collectively, as
letterboxers) look like a bunch of jerks. We were all new at one
time and many of us have done something stupid along the way. It is
one thing to voice our opinions and frustration over the article and
those who participated as a group here in this forum, but to attack
this family by sending them spiteful mail is going too far. If you
really feel strongly about voicing your disapproval, write to the
reporter, who wrote a less than responsible article.
