Re: Woodstock - Tonight

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-07-30 21:10:30 UTC
We also had a great time last night! It was fun to see Der Mad Stamper & RaqsEnigma again and a pleasant surprise that they brought along the historic patch collection they compiled last year. If you're interested in more info about the patches, you can find it here:

http://www.weliveandbreathe.com/history_patches.htm

They also very generously distributed copies of the booklet that was created for the "We Live and Breathe Letterboxing" event last year to celebrate the 10th Anniversary of LBing in the USA.

Of course it was also nice to chat with our local LB friends and we were happy that we had such a good turnout. Lastly, a special thanks to Acorn for suggesting such an accommodating pub!

I've posted a few photos in the 2009 Miscellaneous folder on this message board. If anyone else has photos to add, feel free to do so. You might be able to get to them via this link:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/photos/album/1160315258/pic/list
