Had to share! - found a "missing" box

From: Sara (fivestmc5@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-07-09 14:17:20 UTC
I took Friday off to drive to my cousins wedding and just spent the
day letterboxing once down there. There was a box at a rest stop
marked as missing but my 3-year-old needed me to stop there anyway so
I thought what the heck I'll go look for it and IT WAS THERE! it has
been marked missing since October 06, last found in July 06 and I
FOUND IT! It was my first box of the trip and was very exciting and
energizing for this newbie.

I had 9 boxes mapped out, found 6 including the lost one and ran out
of time searching for the last one in a library where one of the
books with the clue to it was checked out of the library (the clue
said ask for help if needed and I did so they gave me the next clue
and I found it) but that took me over 2 hours and I had a wedding
rehersal I had to get to that was still an hour drive away. but the 4
left overs are all close to one area and close to the interstate so I
should be able to go back to those someday when visiting with my
family. (and when I got home I saw one of the ones I skiped was
reported missing this weekend anyway so that gives it time to be
replaces I guess.) But now I know to go ahead and look when someoen
says it's missing.

I sent a message tot he planter using both LbNA and AQ but have not
heard from them again, but I get the impression from the listings
that they are not active anymore.

MIgirl from SITE Sleuths
