Hi everyone,
My cousin is getting into planting some boxes in MN, and wanted to plant one at a state park. I know many of you had some chat a while back about the rangers taking them and such. She went to the station to ask for a permit, but got a newbie worker that had no idea what she was talking about. Do you know the name of the permit she should be asking for so that she can get it planted? And is there a fee to place in MN state parks?
Thanks for the help
BootyBandits



Re: MN state parks

From: artgekko_mn (artgekko@frontiernet.net) | Date: 2010-04-08 13:42:30 UTC
The form she should ask for is the Geocaching permit form. She can also download it here:

http://files.dnr.state.mn.us/destinations/state_parks/geocaching_form.pdf

There is no fee for planting in the parks, aside from the usual entrance fee.

Cheers,
-AG
