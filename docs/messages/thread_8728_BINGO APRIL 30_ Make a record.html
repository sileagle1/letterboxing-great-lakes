BINGO APRIL 30: Make a record

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-04-30 11:47:37 UTC
Two generals in a row,
then onto May.
Should be nearing a finish?
I have no say. 

I hope your having fun, 
At the edge of a chair. 
This winter diversion soon,
Should be out of my care.

Check the word, 
Then check once more. 
Don't miss a call, 
And lose and be sore.

I - Logbook
