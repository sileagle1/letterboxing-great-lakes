From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2010-06-17 04:52:28 UTC
I want to reiterate . . . you guys are the best bunch of folks I know!!!

Through the mud, rain, slips, falls, tornados, rabid raccoons, broken tents, finding shelter in outhouses, calls to the EMTs, etc etc etc . . . you guys simply amaze me with your good attitudes & graciousness!

During the bad weather on Friday night, a HUGE number of you ventured out (including two that escaped their hotel while management had it under lock down during the tornados), to meet up at the after-gather. What was the final number? 30, 45? Either way, you guys made it and had a good time sharing horror stories of the day over some good food. Nothing like closing down a place at midnight . . . oh, and for our two fugitives that escaped from the hotel, they went back out to box the night away!!

My words can only go so far. I've never known a heartier group of folks that love nothing more than to smile and laugh through lifes slips and falls . . . oh, and there's that planting and finding of rubber stamps in the woods too.

Thank you! Thank you! Thank you!

Kudos to all of you, I'm privileged to call you friends!!
