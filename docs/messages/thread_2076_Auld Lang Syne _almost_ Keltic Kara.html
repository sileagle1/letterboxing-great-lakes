From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-05 23:55:28 UTC
It's not quite New Years yet, but you know how the song goes, "should
old acquaintances be forgotten, and never brought to mind?"

Well, I know many of you remember Keltic Kara (see Lumber Town series)
moved from MI to ME and now I have gotten an email letting me know she
did a quick visit back to the area. I've not heard of Can Man, but
have let Thumbs Up know to pass on the address for our list, in case
he wants to join.

Here's the 'contact the placer' note I got recently:

> Anyway, on 11/20, I was out with Becky from Midland, SISU and Can
Man from Muskegon, and KelticKara from Lubec, ME (formerly of
Muskegon) and we found the four boxes out at Henderson Lake Nature
Preserve:
- Compass Rose Letterbox
- Old Man Winter
- Elvis Lives
- Here Kitty Kitty

I really liked the location (and it's always fun to find more than
one at any given place!). I really enjoyed the triangulation for
Compass Rose! We were together for an early Thanksgiving celebration
and what better way to spend time with people you love?!

All 4 are in great shape and well hidden. Thanks again for another
afternoon of fun! From: Jessica aka Thumb's Up!<

Hope you ENJOY!ed this 'blast from the past' brought to you by:

---Bindle Babe
