Hey Mark, glad to hear you and Sue will be playing Bingo with us!

In answer to your question... Yes, anyone who is a member of the GL
List can play, no matter if they actually live in the region or not.
Now, the more you've letterboxed in this region, the more choices you
will have when making up your list (and there may be a slight advantage
to having some unique items on your list), but the game is set up so
that even if you have never found a box in the GL region or exchanged
with any of the folks on the list, there are still 30 items you can put
on your list.

The more the merrier!
