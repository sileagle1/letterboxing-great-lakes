From: Familyof6 (familyof6portland@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-01-23 23:33:27 UTC
Hi Everyone. If you are new to the talk that is going on on this board about the Olympics and such, please be aware that it is in context to a winter mystery letterboxing event being held in Michigan in February. Some of the talk may possibly be seen as derogatory and a bit mean spirited, however, I would just like to say it isn't. Comments, websites, and such are not meant that way or to make anyone feel uncomfortable. Heck, we even welcome you to participate!

However, if the line does get crossed, please, let us know. Speaking for myself (and most likely much of those involved in this), we would never intend to make anyone feel uncomfortable in any way.

In the meantime, we hope you are having some fun during this dreary winter season with the anticipation building up to the Olympic Event. If you haven't signed up and would like to, be go to this link: http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1551

I, for one, am looking forward to meeting everyone and having a whole lot of fun with this event (and you should too, because who knows what "mystery" is in store) wink...wink...;-)

Andrew (Familyof6)
