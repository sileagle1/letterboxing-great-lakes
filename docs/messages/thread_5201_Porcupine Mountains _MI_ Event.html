I (mis)stated in a previous e-mail that the Porcupine Mountains

6-7. There will be an information table/booth about letterboxing.

event-related stamp available to anyone who visits the information table.

difficult, you basically sit there and talk to people who stop and
inquire what letterboxing is, etc. I realize that the Porkies are not
exactly local to many people, but if you are going to be up that way
or can arrange a trip and would be willing to help out, please contact
me offlist.
