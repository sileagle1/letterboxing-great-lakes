MayDay Mini Meet-long version

From: Doglvrs (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2005-05-02 14:00:16 UTC
I finally have a minute to sit and write a little something about yesterday!
***IT WAS TOTALLY AWESOME!!!!!!!!!!!!****

Ok - now the ones that met me yesterday are laughing, because they can hear me saying
it! How cool is that??

We had so much fun! There were some new boxers there -
TinksDust & HaveyBeaks and Team Strad-agy...so glad you guys could make it!
We were also able to meet boxers who are new to our list - Sheheradaze & Puss-in-
Boots...so glad to meet you guys!

And veterans as well:
Armadillo Jo
BindleBabe & Enders Angel
Pale Puppy Posse
Team Rabbit
The Cichlid Guy
Lipstick Chick with her bunch of newbies

WOW! SOOOO FUN!

After having a great lunch ~ and exchanging stamps and a few personal travelers - we
were off.

On the way to my HH Hostel we stoped at Little Piece of Heaven 2 - an easy quick find...I
wonder what people thought of us all pulling into that little cemetery! Got a call from "R"
of our team, and told him to meet us all at the HH Hostel!

We headed out to the Heartbreak Hostel - that was a hoot! All those HH's! Picked up The
Tree of Life - still giggling about that one! You should have see them from far away ~
checking every tree....and the picker bushes! teeheehee!

Headed back to the parking area and a couple of teams headed home...it was starting to
sprinkle, and I was getting a little nervous.....

Headed over to Kirk Park, driving through some rain - but hey! We're hard core boxers
here! And then when we got there - IT WAS SUNNY!!!! CAN YOU BELIEVE IT??

So we hit the trail and I must say - R, S, W & me....we laughed our stinkin' head off!!!! It is
soooo funny to see other people (in a group no less) following the clues that you have
written! Oh my gosh...I'm laughing right now just thinking of it.
I can hear Brenda of Pale Puppy Posse now..."So me a bench! I'm not coming over there
until you can show me a bench!" And Armadillo Jo - "I want to start at the trail head!" So
unbelievably funny!!!!

We were sucessful on all accounts ~ and after the trek to C.J's Loathing - we just had to
celebrate with ICE CREAM!!!!!!!!

After a tasty treat at Dairy Queen - and more laughing and chatting...we called it quits!

A great time was had by all!!!

Thanks everyone for all the kind words and all the laughs! I would do this again in a
heartbeat!!! Except for that, I'm not a very social person......LOL - ok, who am I kidding!


"A" of Doglvrs
