From: (dlkotlarek@gmail.com) | Date: 2017-01-02 15:33:37 UTC-08:00

For those of you who have found letterboxes by Half Empty (formerly Kitty Mail when he lived in Wisconsin), or if you just have an off-the-wall sense of humor, you may be interested to know he has published a second coloring book for "Mature Colorists". Look carefully at the illustrations in this book and you may finding something you recognize.


https://www.amazon.com/Grand-Canyon-National-Park-Cautionary/dp/1591521890/ref=sr_1_2?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1483399476&sr=1-2


And if you missed his first book, here's a link to it. The books are a unique gift for those visiting the park and include some useful tidbits in addition to the "cautionary" illustrations.
