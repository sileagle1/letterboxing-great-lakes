Another reminder: Based on past years, I'm guessing that three interim
BINGOs will probably occur within the next month. [Bingo Trivia: The
earliest that an interim Bingo has been called was JAN 31 in 2009 and in
2011. Could we beat that this year???]

Keep your eyes on your card watching for:

10-spot Bingo: any 10 spaces on your card - no special configuration or
location, simply the first person to get 10 words crossed off.

6-pack Bingo: a block of 6 spaces - 2x3 or 3x2, anywhere on your card.

Straight Line Bingo: 5 spaces in a row - vertically, horizontally or
diagonally, anywhere on your card.

It will probably take longer for someone to get the 4 Corners and "+"
Interim Bingos.
