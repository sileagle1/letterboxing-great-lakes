Wow - I'm so glad I got up the courage to go to an event full of strangers in a new city and a new state. You guys are terrific! I had such a fabulous time at the Mo' Boxin' gathering, I'm still floating. I want to say thanks to everyone for being so friendly and welcoming yesterday.

Did anyone else have the experience on the drive home of all the road signs looking like stamps? I kept thinking how well carved they looked and how they should really be stamped into my logbook... Anyway, I had a fabulous time, and as a thank you, I left you all a little present...

http://atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?boxId=6925

Thanks again,
Mama Bear (from Ithaca, NY)
