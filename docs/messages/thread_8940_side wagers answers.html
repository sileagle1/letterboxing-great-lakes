There are winners with their full cards.

And losers with their poems like bards.

Side wagers is our friendly side pot,

to bet on when we will see the top spot.

Players pick their favorite date.

and then we sit around and wait.

Occasionally I send a cryptic clue,

to let you know who’s in the queue.

ASLGirl, Fungus Woman and MamaStork

are hoping they get to pop a cork!
