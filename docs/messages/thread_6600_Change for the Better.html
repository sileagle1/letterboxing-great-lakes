Change for the Better

From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2010-05-15 19:10:57 UTC-07:00
As you know, I kind of have started my own personal tradition of organizing a service project at some of the major gatherings (collecting drink packets, pajama pants, batteries, childrens books, etc.). This year I'd like to continue the tradition at the GLG with "Change for the Better".

There will be a change jar at the gathering. Make a donation...as many coins (or bills...or even checks!) as you'd like, and I will turn over the donations to the brand new (due to be dedicated this month) Fisher House at Hines VA Medical Center for use in purchasing items on their wish list. In return, you can get the image of the stamp I will carve of the Fisher House logo. (For those of you not familiar with the name Fisher House, it's like a Ronald McDonald house for Vets and their families.)

Those not able
to attend the gathering but who'd like to donate can mail a check. Let me know you made a donation and I'll mail you an LTC with the image.

Here are a few links explaining more about it, including directions for writing and mailing in checks.


http://fisherhouse.org/aboutUs/aboutUs
http://www.fisherhouse.org/inTheNews/VACenter_06_11_TCN
http://www.hines.va.gov/fisherhouse/FHWish.pdf

--klk/fw
