REPORT: WILL COUNTY / COOK COUNTY IL

From: Mosey (PonyExpressMail@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-05-22 14:39:11 UTC-05:00
Where ARE all the letterboxers?!? This is prime boxing weather!

Anyway, we headed out early this morning for two boxes.

1. (Cook County) 2005 GL Exchange: Barn Theater -- placed by FungusWoman
on May 12th -- originated with Pale Posse Puppy and Naughty Dog.

May 12! That's a whole 10 days ago! And we were the first finders! I
couldn't believe nobody had been to the box before us who might live closer.
We took our bikes but when we came to the dirt trail, it just didn't look
promising for our hybrid bikes. It's not that I CAN'T change a flat tire on
the trail, I just don't WANT to. Been there done that & it wasn't all that
much fun. So it might have been just fine but we didn't take any chances.
We walked it. Nice, pleasant walk. One chow wanted to get up-close and
personal with us but he was on a tie-out in his backyard, so he wasn't able
to come over and say hello. He seemed so friendly.

Box was in fine condition. Nice hiding place. Nice stamp. We didn't put
the clue together with the stamp until we found the stamp. Duh...

2. (Will County) Lockport I&M Canal -- placed by Matt the Rat back in Jan,
2003.

Great hiding spot! How did he ever find that particular hole??? We love
the history of Lockport and the various towns along the canal. "Abraham
Lincoln's Dog House" threw us a bit. But we knew where we were going,
because I knew the "dark cave-like opening" he was referring to.

Anyway, the box was in fine condition. And waiting for a whole lot more
people to come and enjoy the history of Lockport.

Stopped and lunched with a whole mess of senior citizens in town. Nobody we
knew -- they just included us in the pack as long as we were in the right
place at the right time -- very enjoyable lunch -- Lockport tends to have
some pleasant, friendly folks.
A few of them were originally from Braidwood. Filled us in on Braidwood's
rather "colorful" past. Braidwood definitely sounds like it needs a
letterbox somewhere.

~~ Mosey ~~
