From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-03-03 05:56:23 UTC
Since it is Dr. Seuss's birthday today, I'll contribute a simple poem (but it is no competition to the elegant verse of the Court Poet!)

I do not like The Words You Pick
I do not like them.
Bingo Man.

I do not have them here or there.
I do not have them anywhere.
I do not have them in my yard.
I do not have them on my card.

I do not like The Words You Pick
I do not like them.
Bingo Man.

I do not see them, what a pain.
I do not see them, I look in vain!
I do not have them in a box.
I do not have them, what a pox!

I do not like them in my house.
I do not, do not, so I grouse.
I do not like The Words You Pick.
I do not like them.
Bingo Man.

Wisconsin Hiker
