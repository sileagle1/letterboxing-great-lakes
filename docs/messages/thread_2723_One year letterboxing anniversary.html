From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-29 13:46:04 UTC
I was going through our logbook last night, and noticed the date of our
first find... which was June 29th 2005. As of today, we have been
boxing for one year! I can't believe how quickly it has gone by. Both B
and I love this hobby and have a hard time imagining NOT
letterboxing... we scratch our heads and ask, "What did we do with our
weekends before we 'boxed?"

We have met so many wonderful people and gone to so many new and
beautiful places this last year... thank you all for making
letterboxing so enjoyable!

Sappily,
