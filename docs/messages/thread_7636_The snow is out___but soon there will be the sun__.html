It is around this time of year...a few months into winter with a few
more to go...that I start to look forward to the upcoming boxing season
when the days get warmer. I know it is also a good time to do box
maintenance for those you've already planted (and remember, there is a
prize so add your boxes to the file in the database section!), but it
also is a nice time to think about fun series or tricky mystery boxes to
plant so carving can commence. Plus, if you check-out a potential
plant in the winter, before the bushes and trees bloom, it is much
easier to find good hiding spots!

So, I was wondering (and perhaps looking for ideas to steal!), if anyone
had some fun ideas they'd be willing to share?

On my short list, at the moment, are a few 'Stroller Boxing' boxes I'd
like to plant. For those of you who aren't in our area and haven't done
them, these are kid-themed boxes that are planted in locations that can
be done with a stroller in tow. We like to make them 1-2miles so the
effort of hauling a big trail-worthy stroller is rewarded with a nice
hike. The ones we had planted before were mainly Seuss themed boxes,
but I think our new series will be related to some other titles.
Related to (or perhaps just nearby) some of those plants, I'd like to
include a series of photoclues. We have one planted that I really like,
so I'd like to make another one (or two) of those. These might/probably
will be a bit more tricky so, ideally, one of the two series will
interest everyone.

I thought I'd share some thoughts since it seems like things are a bit
quiet expect for just checking the board to see if they have the daily
BINGO word (and if you are like me, more often than not, you don't)!
