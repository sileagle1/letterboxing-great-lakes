Tails from the trail - day one

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-18 11:22:02 UTC
Mrs DogLvrs and I got a nice early start and arrived in the Kzoo area
just when we thought we'd arrive.

Our first box goal was "Mark in the Park" placed by Belgin Mom back in
July 2003. With a hint, we did find the box with it's hand-carved
stamp. However, the clues badly need updating. The sign for French's
Island has long been down and we never did find a trail marking for
Memory Lane. The trails are awash with tiny white and pink Dutchmans
Breetches. (See picture in photo section)

Next it was on to Asylum Birds placed by AZObox in June of 2003.
Lessons learned on this one: don't spend 3 hours walking the trails
trying to make the clues fit what you're seeing. If it takes that
long, something is wrong! Geez oh pizza ... it might even mean the box
is only a short trip up and back.

All in all we found 6 boxes and did not miss any we had gone in search
of. We'll email the placers with box reports.

Lunch-time today, we may have a mini-meet up with some of our fellow
GLK letterboxers, where mayham or mischief may occur .... you never
know with this group.

Sail on!
---Bindle Babe
and MrsDogLvrs
on the road in Kzoo
