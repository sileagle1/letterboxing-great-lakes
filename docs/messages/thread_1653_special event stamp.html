The official name is "Veterans Memorial Circle. Maple is also
called 17th Street north of Brookfield, in North Riverside and
Broadview.

Probably the easiest way to find it is to come from Ogden Avenue (US
34)(west of First Ave/ IL Rt 171), Take Maple north, over the
Burlington tracks to the Circle - maybe about a mile north of Ogden.
