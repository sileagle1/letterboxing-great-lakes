Bingo Miscellany

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-01-10 11:11:18 UTC
I have set up a database with the Bingo words that have been called
so if you are away from the list you don't have to hunt through the
posts to find the words you missed (although I cannot imagine who is
not sitting on the edge of their chair anxiously awaiting the next
word to be called ;-)

A reminder on the games that are being played:

FULL CARD - this is the big game; all spaces on your card crossed
off (settle in, this will be a while, I think last year we went into
the first week of May, although there are a few less words this year
so it shouldn't be quite that long)

STRAIGHT LINE - 5 words crossed off in a straight line anywhere on
your board (across, down or diagonally corner to corner)

"X" - all words on both diagonals crossed off in an "X" configuration

4 CORNERS - the words in the each of the 4 corners of your board
crossed off

PERIMETER - the words all around the outer edge of your card crossed
off (straight lines across the top, bottom and down each side)

There will be 1st, 2nd and 3rd place for the full card game; each of
the others will have only a 1st place winner. A person can claim a
win in more than one category.

When you have a BINGO in any of the above listed ways, please e-mail
me offlist at letterbox@comcast.net, within 3 days of getting the
Bingo and I will verify against the copies I have here (you can also
feel free to gloat to the list if you want). The winner of a
particular game is determined by whose winning word was called
first, not necessarily who yelled BINGO first, so if you miss a day
or two and your winning word is called while you are gone, you won't
lose out. If more than one person wins on the same day/word, a tie-
breaker will be used.
