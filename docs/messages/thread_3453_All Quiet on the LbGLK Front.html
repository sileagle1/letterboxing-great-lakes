Yesterday we had our dept Christmas luncheon. It was so much fun. First we had a bingo
game, where each square was a "clue" that referred to a person, and you had to walk
around asking people questions ot see if the clues applied to them -- like "Did you used
to ride with the Hell's Angels?" or "Did you used ot play professional hockey?" I was late so
no one got my clue right. Then after lunch we had Christmas Mad Libs. Each table had a
Christmas story and had filled it in, and one of the table residents had to stand and read it
aloud. Oh there was a LOT of howling laughter at what we came up with!

I came late and left earlier than anyone else -- I am so so swamped at work. We are doing
a math program for Texas and everything has to go out in less than a month. And then we
have 3 weeks to make changes and send it out for California. I am doing the Teacher's
Edition, and they keep making design changes and rewriting parts, so I am REALLY
swamped.
