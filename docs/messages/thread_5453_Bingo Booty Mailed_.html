Well, even though I could have constructed my card with only 1 General term,
I picked 8 of them. Even though each term has an equal chance, the first
year of BINGO it seemed like it was always the general terms being called.
I therefore always include them in my card, even though I could instead
include a more unique term. (Martini Man also could have used only 1, but
instead has 9 on HIS card.)

So those of you who are newer, don't worry if you only have General terms!
