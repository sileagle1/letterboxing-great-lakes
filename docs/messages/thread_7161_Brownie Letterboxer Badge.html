Brownie Letterboxer Badge

From: Beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2011-07-30 07:00:45 UTC
The new Girl Scout Catalog has just been released. New badge for Brownies called Letterboxer. Several years ago, GSUSA discussed all the aspects of the internet hobby of letterboxing and determined that it was only an age appropriate troop activity for the older girls. Now, it's OK for troops of girls as young as 2nd grade. I have not seen the requirements yet.
