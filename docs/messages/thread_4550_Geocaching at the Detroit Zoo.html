Re: Geocaching at the Detroit Zoo

From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-11-03 18:10:02 UTC-07:00
I started with an under-the-radar box at Brookfield (IL) Zoo. I'd heard in the past they were not receptive. A while back CinemaBoxers said she'd gotten permission to place boxes there, but then she dropped out of the letterbox community, so I contacted the person she spoke with (a PR-type person). I assured her letterboxers follow zero-impact, and we would do no harm to zoo property. She gave me her blessing (they like any idea that brings in paying customers!) and I have since placed a total of four boxes there. It is kind of funny though - she must not have informed grounds crews or anyone else about it, because I've since spoken to some of the groundskeepers and they knew nothing about their existance.
