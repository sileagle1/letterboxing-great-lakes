Spring Lake, Streator IL

From: Keith Risley (dispatcher_riz@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-24 15:43:24 UTC-07:00
Last week, I posted about the City of Streator
contemplating selling the Spring Lake Park property.
Spring Lake is where I have planted the Terminal Tower
Box from Doodle and Deedle Bug as part of the Great
Lakes Exchange.

Well it's official, the council has voted to sell the
property. The sale will be finalized at the next
council meeting which is the third Wednesday in
August.

I will leave the letterbox in through August 10th,
after which time it will be pulled until I find out
further as to the fate of this park and a new home for
the box.

There is a petition going around to save the park, I
will post the link here later.

Sil

Re: Spring Lake, Streator IL

From: silboxer61364 (dispatcher_riz@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-27 22:37:54 UTC
Well I went out and pulled the box today, I was surprised to find
all the log entries.

Just to post an update regarding Spring Lake, the city found that
they could not sell it, because it needs to be kept for recreational
use. However, the council is now claiming that because they are
aware of people swimming, geocaching, letterboxing, bike riding, etc
that it has become a liability for them. The council has decided
instead to prohibit access, fence the area off and post it as no
trespassing. I personally would like to see it changed into a
letterbox park....I don't think one exists anywhere..does it? Any
letterboxers who would like to sound off on this decision and see a
lb be kept there, or have been to the park and enjoyed it, you can
email city manager John D. Kolata at: email@ci.streator.il.us

Thanks,

Sil Boxer
