Kentucky and the 24th

From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-25 17:14:15 UTC-07:00
Well...this weekend I went to Louisville for
Lebowskifest...and found several local boxes with Luke
and Co. from South Bend. Louisville is a great city!
We loved their parks! First off we headed to Cherokee
Park to find the 2 Louisville boxes. Found the first
one after wandering for a bit...the second we are 85%
sure we found the right spot, alas the box was not
there. It was a great walk nevertheless by a beautiful
creek...and we decided to get some beverages after
that one, as it was blazin' hot! After some
refreshment we headed to the park where Bubbles Blue
was. Fun, easy find! We definitely wanted to check out
the whole Paranormal Researchers of the Ohio Valley
thing, as both Crystal of Luke and CO. and I dearly
loved Unsolved Mysteries...so we went looking for the
Thantoposis box. Another fabulous park and good find.
The log book was WAY creepy as was the cemetery. We
had one more clue....Crazy Cat...that we were initally
convinced that we'd never find. We had bought a good
map in order to find Thantoposis and after a good look
at the map realized that we COULD find Crazy Cat or
we'd die trying! Dave from Luke and Co. just rolled
his eyes as Crystal from Luke and Co. and I manically
searched for Crazy Cat. We did the happy dance when we
found that Crazy CAT even though both of us are
allergic to them! It was a great day for letterboxing
and Louisville is definitely our kind of place.
Yesterday was my one year letterboxing anniversary,
and we couldn't find the River View box to mark it! We
couldn't even find the park! So, my one year stats are
P 30 something, F 230 something, x seventy something,
misc 30 something. Here's to another great year of
letterboxing!

--Armadillo Jo, who miraculously had no major
tragedies this trip!
