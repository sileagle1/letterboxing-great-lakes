From: rlovely1@sbcglobal.net (rlovely1@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2010-08-30 02:09:49 UTC
I recently returned home from a week long vacation to Wisconsin where I spent three days in Door County. Thank you, thank you, thank you to all the carvers and planters who planted letter boxes! Because of you, we were able to experience places we might not have seen (Bailey's Harbor Lighthouses and the Sturgeon Bay area to name a couple) if not for this great hobby of letter boxing. We were only able to do the drive by boxes but we look forward to returning to the area. Even my hubby who is only a boxer by marriage had a fun time. Thanks again.
