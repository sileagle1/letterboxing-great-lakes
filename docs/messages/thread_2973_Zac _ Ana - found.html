Zac & Ana - found

From: LindaFay (coachlindafay@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-19 13:02:35 UTC
The mystery box I planted over a year ago was found! The story is a
mini prequel to "A Treasure Trove" the treasurer hunters book that
sucked up hours and hours of my time last year. So I planted this box
to honor it. I even checked it out recently at a wedding I was
attending at the exact location.

Any way GL Lady and her group found it and let me know.

I saw that Mark Pepe had an article about mystery boxes and why they
languish. In the winter, I sit around and solve them - or at least get
a very good idea where they are - but alas - I must work and can't
always get to the location.

Happy day for me and Zac & Ana and happy boxing.

LindaFay
