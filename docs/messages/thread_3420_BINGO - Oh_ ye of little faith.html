BINGO - Oh, ye of little faith

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-06 16:19:11 UTC
To the pessimists among us who fear their lack of letterboxing
experience makes BINGO play unthinkable:

There are a few of us who played in 2006 who claimed lots of obscure
boxes, exchanges, etc. on our lists only to discover that it made
little or no difference in the outcome or even of one's changes of
scoring in the BINGO game. We didn't do too well.
Right, Wisconsin Hiker?

So, even if you're pretty new to the game, I'd say jump in and join
the fun.
We've got several months of tough sledding ahead of us and we can use
all the diversion we can find as we await the daily announcements from
SpringChick.
And, as I recall, last year's winner was a mighty happy letterboxer!

Details on the infamous Side-Wagers game will be announced in a week
or so.


azobox
