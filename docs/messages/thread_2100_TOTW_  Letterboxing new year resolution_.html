From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-20 05:57:02 UTC-08:00
I know we're all busy with the holidays so quickly
approaching and compiling our Bingo cards in time, but
since I don't believe we have a current topic of the
week going, speedsquare's comment made me think,
"Hmmm, I wonder, does anyone else have any
letterbox-related new year resolutions to share?" Or
not so much resolutions, as plans for the new year
concerning letterboxing?

I guess it's only right that I share our plans. I've
been working on what I consider a letterbox with a
"twist" to plant in the coming year (since I didn't
get to before the snow started to fall). The concept
is all together, my "twist" has been tested, the image
for the stamp, although not carved, has been designed,
just need to scout out a good hiding spot sometime
next year. So keep your eyes open for "Invisible Ink"
in 2006.

Deedle & I are also planning on registering for some
camping equipment, to aid us in our longer-distance
letterboxing trips. Lots of letterbox-saturated areas
in Southern Ohio we'd like to make weekend trips to.

What about the rest of you?

Doodle Bug.
