I have to say that all this positive feedback makes me super happy. Mr & I had a few heated discussion's over this little series, and we eventually had to agree to disagree. (He's come around now)

The whole idea stemmed from the beauty of these little country cemeteries. For much of my childhood I spent time in many little country cemeteries with my Grandmother (an avid genealogist) finding the correct grave stones, and getting rubbing's of the information on them. These times are some of my fondest memories - just me and my Grandma driving along an old country road and cheering when we finally stumbled upon the tiny cemetery we were looking for. She taught me respect for those that had passed and also that a cemetery is not a place to fear - but a place of beauty to be treasured and visited...often.

Thanks again ~ Mrs. Doglvrs
