FYI... AQ Change

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-04-29 12:37:37 UTC
I wanted to make everyone aware of a change that is being implemented
at AtlasQuest since I know many people here list clues there but are
not active AQ users.

Ryan has implemented a new *feature* that for any box clue that is
listed there whose owner has not logged into AQ for a year, he will
put the box up for adoption. Now I know that chances are in a year's
time, an active letterboxer who has clues listed at AQ will more than
likely log in there either to post new clues, change existing clues,
search for boxes, use the message boards, bcheck events, etc., but...
sometimes life happens and time gets away from us.

So, if you are one who just puts your clues there or has placed clues
there in the past, but really does not use the site regularly, make
sure you log in every now and then anyway to keep yourself active.
Would hate to see anyone's box get put up for adoption while they were
out tending to life.

SpringChick
