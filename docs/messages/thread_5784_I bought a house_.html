Hello, Letterboxers. :)

(This is related to letterboxing.)

I've been a little busy buying a house lately... which was not fun, due to weird economic situations like Central States Mortgage closing.

Anyway...without trying to spoil anything, I found a certain WiHi and Martini Man box a long while ago. In the logbook I wrote about what a nice Milwaukee Neighborhood it was in. Recently, searching online for homes, I found a house listed in that very neighborhood and bought it!

You just never know where the hobby of letterboxing will take you.

Thanks WiHi & Martini Man for bringing me to the neighborhood! And thanks Aimlesst, Musical Muggles and Punxygal for reminding me of my logbook entry. :)
