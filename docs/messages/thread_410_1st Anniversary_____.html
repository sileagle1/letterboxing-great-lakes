Martini Man & I found our first letterbox, E-Way Trails in Madison,
WI, one year ago today. We've had lots of fun over the past year,
finding some boxes every single month.

Our stats after one year are: P12 F170 X36 E2 (plus an adopted
box, some hitchhikers and miscellaneous stamps)

Thanks to everyone who plants boxes and thereby makes this fun
activity possible!!
