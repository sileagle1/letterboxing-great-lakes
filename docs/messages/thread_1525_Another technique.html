Another technique

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-06-28 16:34:53 UTC
I just wanted to share another technique that helped us last night.

We went out to look for a new box in the area, and when we got close,
we saw (& heard) 2 young men lighting and tossing firecrackers and
drinking beer. We walked past them along the trail, looking for the
landmark. After a bit we figured out that where they were standing was
exactly where we needed to be. So we turned around and walked past
them again. We walked a bit further down the trail then stopped. I
looked back at them and then started taking notes on my clue sheet,
looking over at them occasionally so they might think I was taking
notes about them. As soon as they saw what I was doing, they picked up
their mini-cooler and firecrackers and hightailed it out of there! We
then strolled back to the spot and retrieved the box. Success!

Wisconsin Hiker
