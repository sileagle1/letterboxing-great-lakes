Re: Warning - SE WI LB vandalism

From: twila75 (twila75@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-16 20:58:35 UTC
The note essentially said "Get a life you dumb f**ks." It was not
given to authorities. For all I know, the note was left by a family
member with a loved one buried in the cemetery who may have stumbled
upon the "box" if it wasn't hidden well enough, or noticed the last
finder while they were looking for it. Given the state of many of
the gravestones, it looks like there has been more than one incident
of vandalism over the years. I wouldn't have necessarily connected
the incidents except that I noted the vandalism in my mind when I
was walking out of the cemetery and then mentioned it in the e-mail
to Dragon as more of an FYI for if he was going to replace the box.
Personally, I wouldn't worry about other boxes being comprised
because of this one, but I wouldn't recommend replacing the box in
the same spot.
