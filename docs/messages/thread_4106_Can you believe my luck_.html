Can you believe my luck?

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-05-28 16:17:59 UTC-04:00
Ok, seriously. I am the queen of planting boxes on top of boxes!

You may recall back in the fall when we had the contest to fill in
all the MI counties, I basically planted a box on top of a box that
TrailTroll planted about 4 hours earlier. Of all the places in all
the county, I put my box no more than 20 feet from hers. I really
couldn't believe the odds of THAT occurrence!

Now, today, I got a status report from a geocacher who said they
found my box because it was on top of a geocache they were looking
for. How in the world? My box is in the nook of a large tree where
the trunk starts branching out into smaller trunks. It's a little
hard to see up there, you kind of have to reach up and into the
cranny to get the box. I looked up in there and moved the leaves and
stuff around to cover the box when I planted it. I didn't notice any
other box. I've had a lot of statuses since it was planted and no
one else reported a geocache!

I think I just found the geocache clue. By the google map, it's in
the same general place and was planted a week before mine. There
were three or four other geocache finders and none of them noted in
their statuses that they found my letterbox. I can't believe the odds!
