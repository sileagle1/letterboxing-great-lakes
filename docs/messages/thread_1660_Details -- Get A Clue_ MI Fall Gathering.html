Details -- Get A Clue! MI Fall Gathering

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-07-31 17:05:49 UTC
Okay, we've worked out a lot of the details for the Get A Clue! MI
Fall Gathering at Yankee Springs Rec Area in Middleville, MI.

Gathering Web Page:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/051022_main.asp

This will be a traditional type of gathering at the Yankee Springs
picnic shelter with a potluck at noon -- there is a grill there and
also electricity. The gathering will get going about 8:00 a.m. with
exchanges, meet/greet old new friends, etc.

A few other highlights:

RHYME TYME CLUE EXCHANGE GAME: We will be playing the Rhyme Tyme Clue
Exchange Game, a variation of the Clue Exchange Game devised by Scarab
of the Doubtful Guests. The premise of the game is similar to the
Clue board game, in which there are a variety of SUSPECTS, WEAPONS
and ROOMS, and the object of the game is to use cards/questions to
determine which suspect killed Mr. Boddy, with which weapon, in which
room.

Similarly, in our game there will be SUSPECTS, WEAPONS and LOCATIONS,
with the object being to determine which suspect killed Mother Goose,
with which weapon, in which location. Instead of cards/questions,
players will collect stamps and extraneous clues to eliminate the
innocent and pinpoint the guilty.

(MORE DETAILS IN SEPARATE POST OR AT THE GATHERING WEBSITE)


WORKSHOP: We're trying to keep the schedule loose and flexible and
have decided to have only one workshop -- topic yet to be decided (we
are looking for suggestions for workshop topic and welcome ideas,
particularly topics that have not been covered at previous gatherings).


LETTERBOXES: There are a handful of letterboxes already in this
park. We think this is an awesome park with a great set of hiking
trails (over 15 miles of trails), not to mention the central location
between Grand Rapids and Kalamazoo. We definitely think this park
could use some more permanently planted letterboxes so we are
encouraging anyone who is able to plant some boxes here between now
and October.

Alternately (we know it if not feasible for everyone to just hop on
over to Yankee Spring to plant letterboxes), you can create a box for
the gathering and mail it to us to plant and do clues. There is no
specific theme for gathering boxes, so let your imagination run wild!
How about Halloween themed boxes, maybe Autumn themed or even playing
off the name/history of the park (do a little research on this).
Another great theme idea would be fairy tales to coincide with the
Clue Exchange Game theme... be creative and have fun with these!

For more information and complete details (and also instruction on
sending letterboxes and how to RSVP), see the gathering web page here:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/051022_main.asp
