Mirror, mirror on the wall
Who's the best bingo caller of all?
The titular leader of Familyof6
Is making really excellent picks!

(To tell the truth, oh friendly mirror,
my feelings should be made a little clearer.
The words he calls are usually awful
My percentage of hits should be unlawful!

But it does no use to complain and cower
Because he holds all bingo power
So sucking up seems the smartest plan
Maybe I can placate the man!)

So bingo highness, call away.
I remain hopeful that another day
More of my words may soon be matches
and fill in all my bingo-grid patches.

Your obedient servant,
TrailTroll
