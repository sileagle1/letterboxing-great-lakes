Funny thing is, Door County is MY personal Mecca! My dad was from
Algoma, and for the first 20 years of my life my family rented a
cottage on the lake side of Door County, just outside of Sturgeon Bay.
Literally lakeside - the water was maybe 50 yards from the door. I
haven't been there in 13 years, and it eats at my psyche and soul.
Last weekend I was in Duluth and swam in Superior for the first time
since high school, and all that did was fuel the fire rather than ease
the pain.

I dream of Glidden Drive, Cave Point, Al Johnson's with the goats on
the roof, going to church in Institute, and the smell of lake, sand
and sumac in the hot sun. And "Jag" billboard signs. Do they still exist?!

My sister and I are plotting a return next summer.... =D

-AG

A Jag billboard is is a billboard owned by Jag, Inc. They're
distinctive b/c of the bright orange dot on the "j" at the bottom of
the sign. They're only in eastern Wisconsin (see the map at

us, seeing who could spot the most, first.
