Winter Fun and Caution

From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-04 00:55:06 UTC
Just a reminder to be careful around rivers, creeks, streams and bogs ... the ground is very
saturated in Indiana now; Snowfall was 8" above average for December and now that the
snow has melted, the ground is very soft.

Hoosier Honey Bee and I went out yesterday. The weather was balmy and we stayed clear
of rain and storms most of the day.

The path to one box along the Wabash in Lafayette is cut off/flooded. We observed rapid
currents on the Wabash River, Wildcat Creek and the Mississinewa River. My hiking stick
and shoes got stuck in mud now and then.

The good news is my box, The Life of Riley, is alive and well after 14 months! Completely
dry and several visitors. After the big floods in spring of 2004, I wasn't sure I placed this
box high enough.

Happy winter boxing,
speedsquare
