Belated Gathering Thanks!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-06-08 18:58:50 UTC
We'd also like to add our belated thanks to Speedsquare and her helpers for another fun annual event! We were glad that the weather cooperated and we enjoyed seeing several of our old friends as well as meeting some new ones. The trails were nice, the carvings fantastic (thanks to all of you who contributed your talents!), and the food tasty. We especially enjoyed those yummy sloppy joes after being out on the trails (and of course the Revel bars!)

Thanks also to the Atomic Beans for hosting the get-together at their campsite on Friday. It gave many of us a chance to share stories of our adventures around the campfire.

I've added a few photos to the Photos section of the message board. This link will hopefully take you there directly:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/photos/album/2054124680/pic/list?mode=tn&order=ordinal&start=1&dir=asc

Wisconsin Hiker & Martin Man

P.S. For those that look at our blog, it is now up to date.
