I must say, I've really enjoyed all the tales about this subject...
It has even got my husband laughing, no small feat. I have to say I
never thought of pretending to eat my stamp! But really if you think
about the sport it is really kind of silly (from an outsider looking
in). A bunch of adults roaming around the forest with a baggie full
of ink pads, mumbling to themselves "it should be right here, why
isn't it here?" looking for tupperware. I can't believe THAT doesn't
scare people away. I love the whole secretive aspect of the sport,
the chalange NOT to get cought. This sport lets me go anywhere with
my imagination, something we rarely get to do as adults, and
chalanges me to come up with new inventive ways to add to the fun of
the game. I think it helps me connect with nature, my childhood, and
the artistic side of myself. I don't think I'll ever stop:)
Double Dragon
