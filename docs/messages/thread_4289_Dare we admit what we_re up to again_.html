Dare we admit what we're up to again?

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-26 23:15:06 UTC-06:00
My new boxes (the dozen boxes I hoped to place last spring? ) should be in place before September for you to find. I've found several historical, interesting, scenic locations, now it's time to get busy carving and placing the actual boxes. So this area will be infested with Beetle Boxes soon!

Yes, Team Dutch Elm was on the trails today. We must be mad!!! The sweat was rolling off us...two HOT babes! NOT a beauty picture. However, there is always a mischievousness that occurs when we start talking, brainstorming and thinking that it's time to plan another indoor letterbox gathering. Well, we're at it again!!!

So this is the first time we are announcing what we've been plotting and planning. Fungus Woman and I are planning a 'infamous,' 'roaring good time' letterbox gathering for February 2008. Yes, we have a theme! It's not Chinese New Years/Dress in "RED" (that was a hoot!), but it will be another one of those crazy Kodak memories to remember!!! We know how letterboxers need a reason to play with stamps, sniff ink, and dress up in funny clothes when the weather is too cold outside to be on the trails, so we'll plan another hot time at the Silo....SILO II!

Proposed date: Sometime in early to mid-February. Watch for details to follow. HINT: The date and the month definitely have something to do with our new theme. Any guesses?

Beetle
