From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2019-01-31 21:33:50 UTC-06:00
With today's wild card, two people claimed prizes in the interim games:

Congratulations to Coffee Beans & Nature Scout for claiming their STRAIGHT LINE BINGO!

&

Congratulations to Trail Rider for claiming the 6-PACK BINGO!

This leaves the four corner and 'X' games to go (no 10-spot this year). Also, if you did get a 6-pack or straight line BINGO including all the words up to day, you still have 2 more days to claim it to share in the winnings and bragging rights.

Some fun stats:
So far, the average (mean) is at 6.3 words...putting the finish line around April (which typically seems right).

22% percent of the words have been called. Of those 29% are boxes, 19% are trail names, 6% are events, 45% are general terms, and 10% are wild cards. So, about half the calls have been general terms.

HOWEVER, 22% percent of the words have been called. 20% of the boxes have been called. 20% of the trail names have been called. 13% of the events. 28% of the general terms & 20% of the wild cards have been called. Meaning, that most of the categories are relatively close to being called in a proportional amount relative to the amount of the total words that have been picked.

Happy BINGOing.

A of Atomic Beans
