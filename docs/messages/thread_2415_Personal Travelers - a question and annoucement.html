letterboxes!!

a

everyone is out there enjoying the nice weather and finding some

I don't know about tradition on this topic, but I can tell you what I do.

My 2 oldest PT's have no logbook. My preference. When I get another's
PT, I just stamp it into my regular logbook. My newest PT has a logbook,
because I may turn it into a full-fledged letterbox after the GL
Gathering. I haven't decided yet.

See ya in June with the Answer!
