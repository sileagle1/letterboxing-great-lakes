Holiday Survivor Mini-Meet Update #2

From: lcoil79 (lcoil79@insightbb.com) | Date: 2007-01-12 01:07:29 UTC
I finally went and signed the contract for using the Auditorium
tonight. As such, I now have more details about the space.
Basically, I'll break down the brochure they gave me with the rules
and regs.

-Must be held by a non-profit group and may not charge admission or
take up collections, donations or buy and sell.

-Any person from the general public must be welcome, non-members
cannot be excluded from events.

-Food and beverages may be served as long as you intended to pick up
after yourselves. Fridge and sink are available for use. Red,
orange, and purple drinks are not allowed as they stain carpeting.
Any spills requiring cleaning will be charged to your group.

-There is a handicapped accessible restroom in the hallway behind the
auditorium that is also equipped with a baby changing table.

-The outside door in the hallway is an emergency exit and is locked
and alarmed.


These basics being said here's how it affects us.

*The Raffle - We can't sell tickets inside the library without
violating the rules. So our answer is to designate a person as a
ticket holder (person TBA before the meet). That person will make a
general announcement to the room that they are leaving to "Go Outside"
or "Going to Lincoln Square" and will ask if anyone needs anything.
They will then go wherever they announced and will sell tickets then.
This will be done probably 3 times throughout the afternoon,
currently planned at 1:30, 2:30, and 3:15 with the drawing at 3:30.
Anyone not known to the ticket holder as a letterboxer will be told
that the tickets were pre-sold before the meeting in order to avoid
violating the rules. More details about the Raffle will be announced
later.

Our current list of prizes include:
* Set of LePlume II Brush Markers
* 3 Great Lakes Regional patches
* Telescopic walking stick (courtesy of MRSHAT)
* Centrifuge tubes [great for microboxes] (courtesy of Katy)
* Gas Card (courtesy of Basket Case)

*Food - We aren't thinking of this becoming a potluck. If people wish
to bring snacks or drinks to share, feel free to do so. Just follow
the guidelines above regarding drinks. There is a fridge in the
auditorium if there is something to keep cold. People can e-mail me
with items they wish to bring, and I'll post it on the Atlas Quest
site. I will announce right now what we are bringing. We will be
providing a Dairy Queen Ice Cream cake, as the 21st is my birthday.

*Public - This event will be open to the general public, which means
we will probably have Muggles coming in and out of the event. We will
have information about letterboxing and how to get started so that we
can be welcoming to the public, we might even get a convert out of it.
We are not allowed to tell anyone they have to leave just because
they aren't part of our group.

*Setup - We will be able to start at 1:00 p.m., but will have to set
up our own tables and chairs. This means that while we might want to
start talking and exchanging right off the bat, we'll have to restrain
ourselves for a few minutes and set up some tables and chairs and set
out our info and displays if we have any.

*Parking - Metered spots are available in the library parking lot and
on surrounding streets, they are free on weekends. There are also
some non-metered spots along the roads. The Busey Bank parking lot
behind the library is also available for free.

*Finding the Auditorium - We will be located in the Lewis Auditorium
in the Ground Floor level of the Urbana Free Library. It can be a
little difficult to find, but if you are absolutely lost, you can ask
for help at the circulation desk. If you ask for help, ask where the
Lewis Auditorium is, do not ask where the Letterboxing
Mini-Meet/Gathering is located as they will have no idea what you are
talking about.

To find the Auditorium, enter the library through the doors on the
south side of the building. Go up the stairs in front of you (or take
the elevator to your left if needed. Go through the doors and past
the anti-theft devices and turn right. Go past the circulation desk
and either take the stairs or the elevator on your right and go down
one floor. If taking the stairs, the Auditorium is found to the left,
if taking the elevator it is on the right.

For information on finding the Urbana Free Library, go to
http://www.urbanafreelibrary.org
