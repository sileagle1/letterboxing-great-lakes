We are planning a trip from SE Wisconsin to Cairo, IL. I'm having fun planning the fantasy letterboxing trip - in case we need to do some driveby letterboxes. Feel free to send recommendations.

Our rough intinerary includes: I-90 and I-55 to Springfield; I-55 to Cape Girardeau & Sikeston, MO; Thebes/Cairo/Rock Springs and Fort Defiance in IL; I-57 to Champaign; I-74/39/90/43 home.

One thing I noted is that we go around St. Louis - I've got to find out where Ted Drewes Custard is - I've always wanted to go there - for obvious reasons if you know my name.

My main goals, besides holding to some sanity and taking lots of pics, is to find one LB in IL and one in MO.

- Sunny
