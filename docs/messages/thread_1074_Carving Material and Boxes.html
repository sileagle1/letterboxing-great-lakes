Carving Material and Boxes

From: silboxer61364 (dispatcher_riz@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-15 05:32:40 UTC

As a school secretary, my mom gets a lot of school supply catalogs.
The other day she received one from Dick Blick in Galesburg,
Illinois. They have 1 1/2" x 1 1/2" Staetdler carving material
squares that come in a class pack of 27 squares. These are great for
carving stamps for those of you who haven't used them yet.

Also, Kroger's had some clear plastic containers with lids that had a
rubber gasket seal on them for sale at 69 cents with the Kroger
Card. I picked up about 10 of them for future plantings.
