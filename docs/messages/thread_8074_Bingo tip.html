Bingo tip

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-02-03 13:46:55 UTC-08:00

If you want to easily check to be sure you have crossed off all possible words on your card, here is a quick way to check the words that have been called:


Go to the Yahoo message board

Click on "More" (under the top cover photo)

Click on "Database"

Click on "2015 Bingo Calls"

Click on the "Column" header and all the "B' words will be grouped together, all the "I" words together, etc., making it easy to double-check your card.  (You can actually click on any column header in the table and the sort will toggle between ascending and descending order.)


This link MIGHT get you directly there, but if not, just follow the above instructions.
