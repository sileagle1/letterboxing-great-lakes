Re: [LbGLK] South Bend boxes

From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-03 18:52:46 UTC-07:00
They are all active! The older ones at St. Patrick's
County Park were all found as recently as early June
of this year by Luke and Co. The new ones were found
last Saturday by myself and Ugler. You'll have a great
time at that park, it's a great place to spend a day!

If you're coming from SE Michigan (am I remembering
this right?), I would highly recommend swinging
through Somerset Center for the Intrigue at Aiden Lair
series. 7 boxes, all recently tended to and
active...and seriously fun!

--Armadillo Jo
