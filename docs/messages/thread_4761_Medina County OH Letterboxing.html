From: Drew Family (drewclan@aol.com) | Date: 2008-02-07 03:15:29 UTC
Hi, all! Unfortunately, we are still receiving complaints about
Medina County letterbox placements. If you plan on placing boxes in
county parks, please take the time to familiarize yourself with the
policies of the people who are dedicated to maintaining your local
parks.

Thank you very much,
Jay in CT, one of your LbNA Webmasters

Message to Webmasters,

Message:
Hello,
I have contacted your organization before about a problem we have had
with people placing letterboxes in our parks without permission.
Unfortunately, I have not gotten a response from the placers,
although I have contacted them numerous times through your site. Your
terms of use states clearly that the placer must have permission
before placing letterboxes. Our park district requires that a permit
be applied for and approved for letterbox/ geocache placement.
The following letterboxes have not been approved:
Medina Mystery Box
Princess Ledges Pinecone Bough
Allardale Wildflower
(all placed by traveln turtle)
Kelmark Boxes 1, 3, 5, 6, 6.5, 8 & 11 (placed by Kellie)
Can you please remove these clues from your website and let the
placer know that they must contact the Medina County Park District in
the future for permission to hide letterboxes. Thank you.

Naturally,

Volunteer & Program Coordinator
Medina County Park District
6364 Deerview Lane
Medina, OH 44256
330.722.9364
