Upcoming Gatherings - Calendar

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-10-26 19:44:25 UTC
Both the Twin Cities Area Gathering and the Fall 2006 Northern IL
gathering sound like great ideas!

I imagine during the next few months as our letterboxing activity
subsides a bit for the winter, we will see others begin planning for
more localized events in the Great Lakes states.

I am thinking that to keep everything straight, let's use the group's
calendar feature so everyone is aware of gatherings that are in the
planning stages and can plan their own events and vacations accordingly.

If you don't know the exact date or location for your event yet, that
is fine, just make an entry on the first day of the month you are
planning (you can always moved it later if you want to).

I have added the MI Fall Gathering in October 2006, as Azobox and I
have decided to make this a yearly thing. Date and location are still
unknowns.

SpringChick
