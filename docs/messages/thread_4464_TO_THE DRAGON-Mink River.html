I usually write the name of the box, the location, the date it was placed, and who placed it. So my logbook is full of stamped images and a little bit of journaling too.

I just received an email from a new letterboxing couple who are in Door County this week. They have found 9 letterboxes and they are hooked! He said he found the Mink River box today and the logbook was damp. Someone put the logbook in one of those zipper slide baggies. Even these new letterboxers have quickly realized that those baggies aren't good. He reports that the logbook is full too. Some people have been adding loose slips of paper to the box. He wants to know what to do. He's still in Door County abd writes to me daily.
