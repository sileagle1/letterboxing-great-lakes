kitten's first day out boxing

From: estella_naparella (estella_naparella@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-04-01 00:06:07 UTC
Following the lead of One Love Journeying and Lighthousekeeper, Skootch
and I went boxing on what turned out to be a lovely Easter Sunday. We
even brought the Henry with us (our 7 month old kitten), on his first
boxing adventure. He did pretty well, and dealt quite well with the
tummy carrier we picked up on the spur of the moment. We're hoping to
get him chipped, and more acclimatized to going on adventures with us,
at least on day trips so far.

He's pretty calm and collected in the car, and gave the boxes a really
good sniff. I think he wants his own personal stamp, but I'm going to
hold out on making him a personal traveler, as he's still pretty new at
this. Keep an eye out for the 3 of us on the trail!

cheers!
E.N.
