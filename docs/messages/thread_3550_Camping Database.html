Hey, that was easy. The database is set up, called
GLG 2007 Camping. I included a "# adults," # kids"
and "# pets" so we can see just how many of us there
will be on the campgrounds. If you don't want to
divulge that information, just leave those spaces
blank.

FYI, turns out most of the sites are $27 a night, not
$26 like it said on their front page. There's also an
$8 "reservation fee" which you have to pay no matter
if you book online or by phone. Just so no one's
caught off guard. I just reserved our site, and was
having trouble with their online page (I guess they
just implemented some new changes) so if you're having
trouble booking online, call their number instead.
They're very friendly.
