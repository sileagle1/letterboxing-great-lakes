GLG Update

From: Heather (the_red_cat529@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-09 23:11:35 UTC
It's time to start thinking about food for the gathering! Lunch will
be potluck and my parents (they only letterbox with me, but they have
their own sig stamps, so feel free to ask for an exchange) have
volunteered to bring meat (probably brats and hamburgers) and do the
grilling. If you have a dish you'd like to share, let me know and
I'll add it to the list.

Beverages will be "bring your own". I know this probably isn't an
issue at gatherings with this group, but alcoholic beverages are not
permitted in Minnesota state parks.

In an effort to make this gathering a little greener, I'm going to ask
that attendees bring their own reusable plates, utensils, etc.

And just a reminder on boxes, if you're mailing them to be planted,
they are due to me by May 1st.

Several boxes have already arrived and they are fantastic!
