was lucky enough to find RaqsEnigma and ask her about where she found

unless you really want to see if there's a difference in the stamps!

6" or 7"-square LB NA logo on each end, either quilted or
embroidered. This might be easier to spot me at the Faire, since I'm
not as obvious to spot as the other letterpeople, I'm not usually at
the shop, and my outfit changes depending on the weather and my
activities. Would that help?

Hope to see more boxers this season!
