Box Maintenance Updates

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-08-21 00:51:04 UTC
Hi!

Well we were out today to find 3 boxes in a new series and to do some
box maintenance.

The bad news first: Martini Man's "Figure Eights" box is indeed
missing. His first lost box! He plans to replace it later this fall.

The good news: Two of my boxes had again been logged as "Attempted".
As expected, both were actually still in place. The mystery
box "Milwaukee Neighborhood" is right where it should be and I've now
added a note to the clues asking people to email me to find out if
they were in the right spot before they log it as "Attempted".

As my clues state in "Brookfield Junction", the box blends in VERY
well and some people miss it. I've added a note to my clues that a
flashlight may be helpful.

The REALLY good news: A box I had checked on in the winter and
thought missing, was in fact in place today!!! When I last checked
on it there was snow on the ground, so I thought there was a slight
possibility that it was still in the area, but not where it should
be. We stopped today to confirm that it really was missing, but
HOORAY!, it was there. A group found it on the ground at the end of
March , but returned it to its proper spot in the fallen tree.
So "Winter Fun #4 Ice Fishing" is now an active box again. However
I recommend you wait until fall or winter to look for it. As is the
case for many of the boxes we place, it is MUCH more difficult to
find with all the summer growth.

Wisconsin Hiker
