Hi everyone!


For those that are new to the LBGLk message board, I'd like to provide a brief history of the annual GLG event. SpringChick began the Great Lakes Gathering way back in 2003.  She set the initial guidelines about rotating the event between the GL states and spearheaded the effort to get volunteers from each state. The GLG rotates among the 6 states in the region (MI, WI, OH, IN, MN, IL), but if no one volunteers from a state when its turn comes around, that state is skipped and the rotation continues. The GLG has traditionally been held in late May or June, but can be held any time between May and early September.

A few years ago SpringChick asked me to take over the task of getting volunteers from the appropriate state each year.  After no one from OH volunteered, I was happy to find some willing and eager letterboxers (4 J's, BAMM and jlsd0218) from IN to host GLG 2018. I am equally happy to hear that the experienced crew from MN is willing to host GLG 2019 next year!

If anyone is thinking about hosting a future GLG, please contact me or post to this message board.  Here's a list of the host states for the next few years:
