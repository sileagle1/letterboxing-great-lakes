Oh yeh, hit me in that soft spot... a paper fixation indeed! I don't really even scrapbook all that much, and when I do it is mostly digitally. I just love the paper. Now and then I use a piece for a letterbox logbook, a journal cover or endpapers or in another art project, but mostly I just like to collect the stuff. It is a ridiculous obsession that is rampantly out of control -- papers and unique yarns and fibers. But I guess one could have stranger or more costly obsessions.

It would take a pretty awesome stash of handmade papers to influence my Bingo integrity though.
