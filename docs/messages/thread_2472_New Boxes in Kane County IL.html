New Boxes in Kane County IL

From: Katelyn Burrill (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-04 14:25:58 UTC

Barefoot Ankh and I went out yesterday to place our first two
permanent boxes. I'm so excited!

They are placed along the trail for the annual MS Walk in St. Charles.
So I'm hoping to not only promote letterboxing but to give information
about this odd and debilitating disease. Both my sister and one cousin
have been diagnosed, and I've met many many people in the past 13
years that I've been Walking that either have or know someone affected
by MS.

To read more about MS and my Walk Team, find my new letterboxes or go
