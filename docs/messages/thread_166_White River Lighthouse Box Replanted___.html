White River Lighthouse Box Replanted...

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-04-24 09:18:28 UTC
The White River Lighthouse box (Whitehall/Montague, MI), which I had
to pull due to the fact that the area it was in had been roped off
for dune preservation, has been replanted in a new location and the
clues have been updated accordingly.

If you have not already taken the trip out to this beautifully
restored lighthouse, it is a wonderful spring afternoon drive up to
Whitehall, especially if you take scenic drive up from North
Muskegon along Lake Michigan (hint, hint... there are boxes in
North Muskegon and at Duck Lake).

This is one of my favorite lighthouses!
