Yet another camo tape opinion -- and as usual I'm last in line, LOL!!!

I personally prefer to camo PAINT my boxes: I've had 3 cans of
camo-colored spray paint and although at first the cans are a little
pricey; they'll last forever. Quick and easy with amazing results.
Sticks to plastic containers... dries quickly (geez I sound like a
commercial.)

The stuff really does work good though; and I save my little Jo-Ann's
coupons so it doesn't cost as much. If I recall I think they're like
$6 a can maybe? (Krylon camouflage ultra-flat; bought at Jo-Ann's or
Michael's with 40% off coupon) I've done in full camo for one box,
while others I've used flat brown on one and flat black on another
(depending of course on where the box will be hidden.)

PennyPenny
