Hi all, I just joined the list (I know, I finally got enough arm
twisting :-) Plus I wanted to vote to have the gathering in Ohio so
maybe I could attend! :-) Anyway, I wanted to share info about an
open event I'm hosting. Not letterboxing related, but if you like
or want to learn more about other art forms, this would be a great
way to do it. Since the question has already come up, to attend a
class, you would need to pay the conference fee. Come learn! It
will be fun!

Scoutdogs

Hi all,

I just wanted to let you know I'm hosting an A.R.T. BASH! A
gathering of artists on Sept. 23, 24 and 25.
It will be held at a hotel very near the N. Ky airport near
Cincinnati.

It will include classes taught by very talented instructors
including:
The Magic of Marbling By Pat K. Thomas
It's In the Cards: Mixed Media ATCs By Nancy Curry
Carving your own rubber stamps By Chris Puls (Scoutdogs)
Sumi-e painting By Patricia Wiley Shafer
Cigar box checkers set By Sarah Hodsdon
DEMO- Ruffled Plastic Jewelry By Wanda Hentges
The Amazing Wizard By Cindy Cade

It will also include lots of socialization time to learn from each
other and share/enjoy each other's art. All levels of artists are
welcome! People new to the creating addition are encouraged to come
learn! Due to the size of the room, this event will be limited to
only 20 people. All the forms you need as well as lots
of additional info can be found at this link:

http://www.artistsoftheroundtable.org/news/

If you have any trouble opening them or if you have any questions,
please contact me!

The theme will be a Fall Festival of Art, so come join us and learn
new things, meet new friends and develop your skills!

Don't delay, registrations need to be in by Sept. 1st!

Sincerely,
Chris Puls - Scoutdogs
DogScouts @ hotmail.com

www.PictureTrail.com/scoutdogs
