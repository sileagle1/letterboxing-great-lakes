From: Chris Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2010-09-16 15:57:40 UTC-05:00
Well, all you need to do is sign up on AQ and show up the day of the event
with comfortable walking shoes and your letterboxing logbook, sig stamps,
and markers! (And a potluck dish if you so desire.) All the cluesheets
will be available at the event. There will be some tabletop stamps but most
will be planted out in forest preserve for you to find. And there will be
LOTS of other boxers to meet and do exchanges with. And tons of food!

That's all I can think of right now, email me if you have any questions!

Something else that's fun about gatherings.....personal travelers. They are secret stamps that the boxer carries with them (instead of planting in a box). To get the image, you usually need to answer a question first.

Once you sign up for the event, on the event page above the event description, will be a button that says "personal travelers". Click on that to see all the PT's that will be there (the list is based on the people signed up to attend).

Hope you guys have fun!
