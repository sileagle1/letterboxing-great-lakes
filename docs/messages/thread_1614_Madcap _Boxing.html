From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-07-19 13:55:31 UTC
Whew! Since last Friday, we have gone boxing three times and found 6
boxes! This is definitely a record for us! Besides two beautiful and
fun stamps in the Around the World in 80 Days series, we have
ventured down to Monroe to pick up the Old Frenchtown Island and Lake
Erie Creature Box 1 boxes. The second one was in Sterling State Park
which neither of us had ever been to and we fell in love with it! We
took a 3-mile walk around one of the marshes, where we saw a swan, a
great blue heron, a crane, and a number of rabbits. Talk about a
nature walk! :-) Yesterday, we drove down to Tipton/Tecumseh to the
Hidden Lake Gardens boxes. Both boxes are easy to reach and the
stamps are lovely. We had never been to this park either (for
somebody born and raised in SE Michigan, there is a surprising number
of places I have NOT been to!) and, again, we fell in love with it.
The manicured lawn, extensive flowerbeds, and the hidden lake itself
were wonderful. Plus, we saw two deer! One word of warning about this
park, though, is that they do not close at "dusk," as many parks do,
but at 8:30 on the dot. As we didn't arrive until ten minutes to
eight, it meant for some very hurried boxing for us!

Anywho-- just wanted to share the fact that all 4 of these boxes are
in good shape, and though a little out of the way, the parks are well
worth the trip!

Later,
J&BBooks
