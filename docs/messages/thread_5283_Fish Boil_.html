Yes, I was up in Door County a month ago. I was surprised to discover that The Sandpiper Restaurant in Baileys Harbor was closed on Saturday and Sunday. We are planning to stay at the Wagon Trail Resort in Rowleys Bay, (turn right after Sister Bay, sort of). They have a fish boil that is part of their indoor restaurant dinner buffet with loads of other choices for those who might like to attend but just sample some of the fish boil.

Who is interested in meeting on Friday night or Saturday night for Fish Boil?
