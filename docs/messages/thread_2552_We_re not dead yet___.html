We've made it back! We barely got outta there with our lives! There was blood everywhere and yes, poison ivy is a real and present danger! Be careful where ye tread and DO wear watertight leggings as Brave Dame A.J.(lb) suggests in her clue missive. 'Tis a dreadful, swampy wood with evil stumps everywhere...and beware of the old man (possibly from Scene 24) who lives across the road and watches ye with his evil eye as you dismount from your steeds...

Surviving the Killer Rabbit was cause for much celebration. Not only did we escape with our lives and no effects from poison ivy, but we are now at F100!!!! HURRAH and HUZZAH! Now I shall send away for our much-anticipated and coveted patch to proclaim our deeds to all in the LB land. :-) --Papeseed
