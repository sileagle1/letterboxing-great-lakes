Limited engagement!!!



Catch this box's gravity-defying act at the April 7 and 8 (2006)
performances of ISU's Gamma Phi Circus! Gamma Phi is the oldest collegiate
circus in the US, performing since 1929.



Doors open at 6:00 pm, and the performance ends at 9:00 pm. This is the only
time the box will be available. Tickets are required for admittance ($12
adults, $10 children 5-12, under 5 free; $2 discount if purchased in
advance).



For more info:

http://www.gammaphicircus.ilstu.edu/index.phtml



Clues: Look behind the banner. You may need to read a bit of Greek to find
this letterbox. If you'd like one last clue, check the photos section
(FungusWoman album) or view clues on AQ.



The box may be available at the upcoming April 21-22, 2006 performance in
Peoria. It may also be permanently placed in Normal at a future time. Watch
LbNA or AQ listing for updated information.







This box was commissioned by "The Goddess", one of the Gamma Phi's biggest
fans (and mom of one of the performers).
