Hey Anne, welcome to the group --- glad to have you here!

Your post reminds me of one of the letterboxing related goals I'm
working on for this season.

I'm calling this particular one: "catch a letterboxer in the wild"

Unlike you, and some lucky others, I haven't come across another
boxer while out on the trails. Bindle Bum and I travel a moderate
amount so I decided I'd try to "catch" a letterboxer "in the wild"
(ie: anywhere)

To facilitate my goal, I've been using the LbNA logo as "bait" by
turning a jpg of the logo patch into our luggage tags. So far, no
letterboxers in the airports or train stations we've been through
have taken the bait and talked to me.

I already had it displayed on my trail pack and I'm planning to put
the same "bait" into the back window of my mini-van. Still trying
to decide how to display it on our boat in a way that won't annoy
the Captain (Bindle Bum)too much. LOL ...

Anyone else willing to share any letterboxing related goals you're
working on?

Enjoy!
