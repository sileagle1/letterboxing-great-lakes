From: lesann847 (l-fischer@northwestern.edu) | Date: 2004-05-12 20:19:06 UTC
I haven't stayed in any of the hotels around McHenry, but just a
word of advice about location--be sure to avoid a location that
forces you to travel on Rt 120, from downtown McHenry and
going west (east of downtown/Rt 31 is fine). There is a LOT of
heavy road construction with frustrating delays.

For those of you in a retro mood, there's a great drive in movie
theater in McHenry. It's cheap, clean and orderly--lots of
families as well as teenagers and those of us who like to recall
what it was like to be a teenager. Call (815) 385-0144 for listings
and location. There's always a double feature, so there will be
plenty of time to do a bit of night letterboxing before the late
show.
Acorn
