Those are good for poison ivy, but I don't think any of them will help for
Wild Parsnip. Best thing is to AVOID it! Treatment is mostly cold
compresses and time. My burns finally quit hurting after 1 week. I don't
know how long it will take for my blistered skin to heal. Some sites say
scars could last 1-2 years.
