GR, Mich - Fate of Easter 6 box series PLACED!

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-28 21:21:58 UTC
Wanted to have these placed, clues all written and posted by this
weekend so everyone would have a chance to nab 'em during the nice
weather that was predicted.

Took a little longer to get the clues done than expected, but we
finally have The Fate of Easter series (6 boxes!) placed and posted
on the LbNA clues list.

If any of you are first finders, could you please do me a favor and
put the date 3-28-2004 on the first page of the logbooks? LOL ....
guess what we forgot to do!

BTW - these are the boxes Angela had painted with acrylics and
coated with matte medium. I told her I thought it wouldn't last, but
they held up to the scratch tests she tried and she wanted to
experiment. Guess what? (grin) So, while these boxes started out
wearing camo, but they may not be by the time you get to them.

Enjoy!
Bindle Babe and Enders Angel

You can also find clues to our boxes by going here:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Letterboxing_Bindlestiff/
You will need to join the group in order to be able to open the
files area where the clues are located.
