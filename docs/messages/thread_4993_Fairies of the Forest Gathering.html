T&T (and other interested boxers):

Your comments are very insightful and I'm glad you had a great time. I
now realize that some of the boxes were difficult and perhaps they
needed a bit more direction. Please chalk that up to inexperience with
doing an event. These boxes will remain in place (with the exception
of The Dryad) and I revised a few clues online to make the boxes a bit
easier to find. One of the big goals was to get people out onto the
beautiful trails that Bertha Brock offered, meet some friends, and
have fun. I think that was accomplished.

As for the event stamp. This was a killer to carve and I'm not sure if
I'll ever do one like it again. In fact, it was practically an "I Spy"
type of stamp, with over 20 characters. Wicked. I am now in repenteance.

Thanks everyone. You guys were great. Sorry that I was not feeling
well on Saturday. I would have liked to had more time for meet and
greet, but my illness kept me at bay.
