Tweedle Dee & Tweedle Dum back on the trail!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-08-08 05:13:39 UTC
I'm happy to report that I have finally recarved these "fat boys" and they are back in business at Lapham Peak, rejoining many of the other Wandering in Wonderland boxes. The clues have been revised slightly to reflect their new spot:

http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=23014&boxname=Tweedle_Dee_&_Tweedle_Dum

While we were in the park, we also checked on a few other boxes and are happy to report that "Out & About Box 2 - Kilroy", "The White Knight" and "Humpty Dumpty" are also alive & well and waiting for more visitors. We had to dry out some of the boxes, but since the previous finders had done a great job of sealing the baggies, the contents were in fine shape.

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. We think our bug spray had passed its expiration date, since it didn't seem to faze the swarms of blood-thirsty mosquitoes one iota!
