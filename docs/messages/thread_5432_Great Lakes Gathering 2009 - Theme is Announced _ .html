Great Lakes Gathering 2009 - Theme is Announced & Event Box Request

From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-12-02 03:28:23 UTC
Based upon the poll taken, the majority has spoken. ALL THINGS GREEN will be our Event
Theme.

I made some adjustments to the Database for our event. Here is what I am soliciting at
the moment ...

About 15-20 GREEN boxes to be placed several days in advance along the trails at
Pokagon. These creations should be monochromatic - GREEN will be the inkpad of choice.
If you are concerned that 20 boxes are not that many boxes, keep in mind the 11.0 miles
of trails at Pokagon are rated easy to moderate. Mostly Moderate, so it might take a little
longer to find boxes. They will be available Saturday and Sunday, unless the creator
makes them event-day only.

If you create a Green Box, please list it in the database, giving it a Title.

Not everyone will be able to hit the trails. There will be some event boxes at the shelter.
For these boxes, I submit these themes for a travelling series:

1. Our Favorite Great Lakes State Parks (1 or 2 from each state would be nice)
2. The Conservation Movement
3. The 1920's and 1930's in America

Boxes requiring some sleuthing at the shelter are welcome. If you can create the clues,
great! If you want some help, we will need to know well in advance.

Box Requirements ---

It would be nice to get a standard box size. I used a rectangular LnL for the Box of the
Month project last year and I liked that size. But, due to the temporary nature of these
boxes, I wonder if letterpouches wouldn't be a better option? Definitely less expensive to
mail and return. And logbooks? If we have a standard stamp size, I could make uniform
logbooks, including the official event stamp to return with the logbook as a thank-you to
the stamp carvers. Let us know your thoughts on this.

If you are creating a letterbox for the event, I will need to have them in my hot little hands
by March 15th.

Other activities planned ...

Keep Our Parks Beautiful -- There will be a prize or prizes for the letterboxer who picks
up the most litter along the way. Start saving a couple of plastic grocery bags to bring
along.

I am looking into t-shirts and canvas bags imprinted with the event stamp.

THE OFFICIAL EVENT STAMP -- I want to delegate this to the artistic letterboxers among
us. I envision a green t-shirt with the logo printing on the front. If you'd like to help,
contact me off-list.

Finally - please do not list any event boxes and/or clues on the Atlas Quest page at this
time. The planted event boxes will listed after the event is concluded.

I'm sure I will need to clarify something tomorrow. But this should get you thinking.

~speedsquare
