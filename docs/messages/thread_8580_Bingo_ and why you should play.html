Bingo, and why you should play

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-01-03 22:16:47 UTC-06:00
Just a few days left to join the fun. Maybe you haven't played before, and you are wondering what this is all about.  So, I'll tell you.

1. Find the BINGO 2018 folder in the file section on the Yahoo group's page:

https://groups.yahoo.com/neo/groups/LBGLK/files/Great%20Lakes%20Letterbox%20BINGO%21/2018%20BINGO%20Game/

2. Open the word list file and note all the boxes, events, and boxers you might have found, attended, or met. 

3. Put together a BINGO grid using the boxes, events, and boxers you've met, and then fill in with any general terms.

DO NOT WORRY if you rely heavily on the general terms.  Many past winners have won using a large amount of general terms.

4. Send me your card, log your name in the database section, send in your booty, and then sit back and wait for the game to start.

OK, so now you've entered..NOW WHAT!?!

5. You will find that there is nothing better than crossing off a called word from your list. And if not, the hope that it might be tomorrow is a nice thought to warm you through winter.

6. As people get their first words called, its common for there to be notes of excitement and relief.

7. Then, after what seems like a much too long of a time, others will post disappointment that they still don't have any words crossed off their lists.

8. A word will get called, that you were sure was on your list...or maybe one you changed at the last moment. DOHT!

9. Meanwhile, there may be some nonsensical posts...this year by BaliWho, which you will eventually realize is some sort of code for sidewagers.

10. Tiptoe will mention that she's in the running for the Biggest Loser.

11. Wisconsin Hiker may note some random BINGO stats about words called or how much of the card is typically filled by this point (Oh NO! am I suppose to do that this year?).

12. You quietly realized you might actually win one of the intermediate games.

13. You do not win the intermediate game you thought you might win.

14. Tiptoe assures everyone that she will be Biggest Loser although ArtGekko may refute her claim.

15. Spring approaches and you start realizing that you'd rather be boxing vs just posting about it...but you also note that you will try to find a few more boxes to bulk up your BINGO card for next year.

16. Someone wins..and while you post your congratulations, you secretly are annoyed for those last few words you put on your card, vs the other ones you had considered.

17. For the few winners, a few weeks after the game starts, you get a package of booty and goodies in the mail.

In short, its a fun way to pass winter so fill out a card!

Cards are due Sunday with booty to follow.

Good luck,

A of Atomic Beans
