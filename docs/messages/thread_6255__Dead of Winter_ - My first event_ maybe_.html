Re: [LbGLK] "Dead of Winter" - My first event, maybe?

From: Archaeo Frog (archaeofrog@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-01-14 15:58:46 UTC-06:00
I hope you can come, Sara! I made my mom come with me to Boxacon in
October, because I was feeling the same. We both had a great time and
everyone was very friendly. I'll be carpooling this time with some of my
newly met "strangers."

As for your other questions, I am not too experienced about all the official
protocol, but I am planning to bring a table top event box made of several
stamps that I recently carved for LTCs (letterboxing trading cards). (I'll
also be bringing some LTCs if anyone wants to share, btw.) An event box is
something you bring to an event that sits out somewhere (with clues or not,
like the tabletops). A personal traveler is a stamp that you personally
carry about with you. People ususally have to ask you a question or do
something silly to get the stamp from you. If you are on AQ you can look at
other people's personal traveler clues for this event to get ideas.

WHEN are other people coming? I was confused by the 1-4 pm time and all the
talk of outdoor or earlier boxing ... just curious.

I hope that helps!

- Archaeofrog
