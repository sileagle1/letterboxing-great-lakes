Re: [LbGLK] Grand Rapids Magazine Lb article is out

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-02-26 13:44:12 UTC-05:00
I also received a copy of GR MAG in the mail earlier this week. While I am happy that the reporter seems to have done a pretty good job of getting into the mindset of the game, I felt the focus was off-kilter and did not spotlight the activity itself. I would have liked to see more nuts and bolts, on the trail, in the woods, reverence for nature, "wow! what a great place!", "what a cool stamp!" kind of stuff, or even a narrative on his letterbox hunt would have given a more hands-on feel to the article. It also would have been nice to hear from some of the other people he interviewed. In short, I am disappointed with the things the article left out.

But it is my understanding from talking with him that the article was cut considerably. Who knows how the editing may have changed the tone of his original piece. I doubt there will be much impact from this article... quite frankly, I don't think the magazine distribution is high enough, particularly to outdoorsy kinds of people. I couldn't even find a copy of the magazine anywhere in the greater Muskegon area -- not even Barnes and Noble carries it outside of the Metro Grand Rapids area.

What were your thoughts?

SpringChick
