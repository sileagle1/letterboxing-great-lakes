Ditto to all the earlier messages of praise. SpringChick and Old Shoe
have raised the bar! Kudos to both of them for an outstanding event!
It was so well organized in every respect! The purchase of clues with
ticktets earned in a variety of ways was an ingenious idea and like

for making this weekend wonderful!

Re: West MI Fall Gathering: A County Fair

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-10-20 00:39:15 UTC
I am so glad that everyone had a good time! This one was really fun
to put together and once started, ideas on the theme just kept coming.
The weather yesterday was absolutely gorgeous and I think we hit the
trees in full color. It truly was a perfect Michigan autumn day.

I came home with so many memorable moments, but a real highlight for
me was watching Nature Boy pursue those hidden ticket boxes. His
intensity and enthusiasm were pure joy to watch and I have no doubt
that had he not finally found it at the end of the day, he would still
be there looking for that gold ticket!

I came home with 4 lost and found items -- a pair of sunglasses, a
fleece Dog Scouts headband, a camera case and a coffee tumbler. If
you are missing any of these items, please e-mail me with a
description and I will make arrangements to get them returned to their
owners.

A huge round of thanks goes to those who helped out by contributing
letterboxes and to Milagro for the awesome event stamp. From veteran
carvers to the youngest carvers -- all of the event boxes were unique
and contributed wonderfully to the theme of the event.

Special thanks to Grandvilla and Mobius Klein for spending a day
before the event planting boxes with me and to those who helped in
various capacities at the gathering -- Grandvilla, Mobius Klein,
Familyof6, Doglvrs and two of my daughters, Becky and Lisa -- Becky
and her family already were letterboxers (the Camper Stampers), but I
think we did a good job of intriguing Lisa as she and her hubby went
out looking for their first letterbox today! Most of all thanks to
OldShoe for stepping up, hanging in there and helping to hold it all
together...

Last of all, thanks to everyone who participated for making it a
wonderful day for us with your presence and your enthusiasm! As
always it is great to see old friends and especially nice to meet such
wonderful new ones!

SpringChick




RE: [LbGLK] Re: West MI Fall Gathering: A County Fair

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-10-19 23:04:14 UTC-05:00
What a nice event! So MANY great boxes, nice trails, fantastic weather,
tasty food (who brought that antipasto type salad? Can we get the recipe?
... the apple pie and apple crisp were also wonderful!). We're totally
impressed that SpringChick can host such a high-caliber event every year. So
much creativity... it's amazing how she can carry the theme across all
aspects of the event - the tickets, the clues, the stamps, the group
activity and even the food.

It was fun to see some letterboxers that we only see occasionally and to
meet some new ones. We spent much of the day out on trail with a few people
and saw others in the morning at the lodge. As usual, we were torn between
finding boxes and chit-chatting. We did manage to get quite a few boxes
yesterday and managed to find all the rest today. We even managed to find 7
of the 8 hitchhikers! We'll post some photos tomorrow night and hope to
have our blog updated in the next day or two.

A BIG THANK-YOU to SpringChick and Old Shoe, and to everyone else who
contributed stamps to make this such a fun event!

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
