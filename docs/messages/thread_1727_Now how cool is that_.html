Next weekend, Deedle & I are taking a trip to
Geneva-on-the-Lake (about an hour's drive from
Cleveland). Now, it wasn't supposed to be a
letterboxing weekend, but letterboxes just seem to
follow you, you know? I just saw that the Miniture
Golf box planted there by Chrissy that caused such a

more brand new one, so really, how can we be there and
just ignore them, right?
