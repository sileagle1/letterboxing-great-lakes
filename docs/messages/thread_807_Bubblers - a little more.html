Having been raised in Il and then exported to
Wisconsin (after Marriage) and yes, I do mean Pilot
exported me! :) I have come across an actual old
fashioned bubbler! It was in a funeral parlor
(attending the funeral of a distant family member of
Pilot) and the water did NOT arch like they do in
public parks or department stores. The water bubbled
up out of the middle of the fountian bowl and only
came about two inches above the place where the water
exited. The Funeral Parlor director explained that
part of the reason that these "bubblers" were
discontinued is because too many people put their
mouths right on the spiggot and they spread germs too
easily!

(And it IS Pop and I cross the street, not go acrosst,
and oh yeah, I say melk too!)
