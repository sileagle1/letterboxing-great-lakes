Sound of Music Series

From: cerealcityboxers (cerealcityboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-02 13:11:07 UTC
We did this series on July 23, 2004. Probably not much help to you,
but here is the status that we found the series in at that time. Box
#1 we found, but you had to really look hard up into the stream bank
to find this camo mini. Box #2, no problems. Box #3, did not
locate. Box #4, no problems (the log in #4 said others could not
locate box #3). Box #5, found (the stamp was broken into two pieces).
Box #6, found letterbox but the stamp was missing. Entries in the log
said the stamp was missing for them also. The hiding area was freshly
cut back exposing the hiding place. Box #7, no problems. Box #8,
found box, stamp broken into two pieces. At this box we dropped off a
HH. It sure is a nice location. I hope the owner will check out
these boxes so others can enjoy them.

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-03 07:31:24 UTC-08:00
We found this series in June 2004, so
CerealCityBoxer's report is more current, but I still
wanted to chime in. We had managed to find all 8
stamps, I think there were total, but it was tricky
and took a lot of retraced steps, and several people
after the fact have been amazed that we found them all
in one go. Both broken stamps were also broken in
June '04. I met the placer Fat Larry (or Phat Larry,
as I think she goes by now) at the Lake Eeerie
Gathering back in October, so she's still around, but
I think she's not quite as active as most of us on
this board.

Doodle Bug.
