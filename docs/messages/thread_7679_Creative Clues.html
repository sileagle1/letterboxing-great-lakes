Creative Clues

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2013-03-04 19:03:25 UTC

We were in AZ last month and met some newer boxers at an event (a group
of 5 "kids" in their 20s). They had planted one box last year on a
longer hike and we encouraged them to plant more so we could find them
next year when we are hopefully down in AZ again.

Well, they have been busy planting since then and have developed some
creative clues for their new boxes. I therefore felt inspired to get a
bit more creative for the two boxes I had planted during our recent
trip. If you're planning a visit to AZ, perhaps you can give them a
try! Here are links to our new AZ boxes, one near Phoenix and one in
Tucson:

http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=64107&boxname=Hombre


http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=64117&boxname=Maiden_Pools


(We also have 2 older boxes that we planted in the Tucson area 4 years
ago.)

It would be great if all of the stamps that appeared at the recent Great
Lakes winter events would get planted on some nice hikes and perhaps you
also might enjoy coming up with creative clues. (Like several other
boxers in our region, we really like to get out on the trail for a fun
hunt!)

Wisconsin Hiker
