At 04:24 PM 1/14/2008, you wrote:
>If you could help for some really good ones, we would appreciate it.

There's not a lot in that area, but check on LBNA. There are some in
Mt. Morris & Burton. I haven't found any of them so I'm not sure if
they're good ones.

If you want to travel a little farther, there are a bunch up in
Saginaw, Frankenmuth, Bay City area. Search by Saginaw or Bay
Counties. It seems in this area more boxes are listed on LbNA than
AW, so try there. Looks like Windrose has several up in that
area. I haven't found any of the ones close to where you are, but
the one I did find make me think these would be worth a trip.

There are a lot of great boxes in Oakland County which may not be
that far of a drive for you. Holly & Davison are pretty
close. Auburn Hills, Rochester, Rochester Hills, Pontiac and
Clarkston would be pretty quick access off I-75. Chief Pontiac is a
good one, pretty quick. Letterbox of Piglet has fun clues and it's
in a great place . If you can figure it out, the Michigan Hitchhiker
Hostel is a good one (longish hike). I haven't really found any of
the others in that area so I can't give any other recommendations.

Have fun!

Bali

From: TrailTroll (kanderson@american-community.com) | Date: 2008-01-15 17:38:12 UTC
Kanga in Davison is very close, and it's in a nice park. There are
others in Davison, but I haven't found them yet. An Aussie Pirate's
Treasure in Burton is also close - not quite as nice an area, but
it's a cute box. And ABC:Fish is in Flint proper - not a long walk,
but a very interesting placement! Be sure to check LBNA and
Atlasquest to get a complete list. Good luck!
