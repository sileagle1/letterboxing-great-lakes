This talk of squished pennies reminds me! We found Froggie Went
A-courtin' last Saturday and in spite of the steady stream of muggles
along the way, they were oblivious to our activity from above. This was
one time were we didn't think it wise to carry the letterbox away too far.
It also helped we were disguised as "hi-tech hikers." I had a pair of
collapsible trekking poles. Several adults commented to the young members
... "look, they are serious hikers." If they only knew. We still have a
secret!

I was disappointed to find 55 cents in the letterbox, but I left it
behind. Some geocachers commented they found the letterbox while hunting
for a geocache in the area. This is really amazing that both containers
could be THAT close. I didn't poke around to find the geocache, just
happy to get the letterbox back without detection.

And my better half actually loved the clues (he carried and read them as
we walked), the hike, stamp, and the entire day.

speedsquare

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-27 03:05:20 UTC
#1 - Oh Speedsquare, we are so glad you found our Froggie-Went-A-
Courtin box..We also can't believe it's still going strong..We
placed it a year ago this coming week to commemorate our 25th
wedding anniversary! Seems it should be worth more than 55 cents!
Those silly geocachers...what do they know about value!
We are laughing at the thought of you fetching the box with trekking
poles up that incline!

#2 - We haven't had a chance to officially thank Spring Chick and
azobox for putting together the Cereal City Circus Mystery gathering
last weekend...As usual their hard work was evident and very much
appreciated! But, we can't explain the unusual craving we've been
having for sugared cereal of all sorts!

In that same vein, We must warn future "gatherers," if Spring Chick
gives you that sad, pouty look and tells you that nobody is going
for her boxes just as you are about to search for someone else's---
RUN! Don't let her persuade you with that sob story! We fell for
it! And we fell hard! It took us ALL afternoon to find those darn
boxes of hers! Fortunately, we met up with Doglvrs and between the
five of us we finally got all three boxes! We also met up with at
least three other groups searching for the same boxes! Did she bat
those "feel-sorry-for-me" eyes at anyone else?

O.K. We have to admit that they were great stamps...a great hiking
workout....a great time with Doglvrs...So what's our beef? I guess
we really don't have any other than knowing that another 9 stamps
didn't get a visit! - We had a fabulous time!...thanks SC!
..and azobox, we promise to ignore SC next time and go for your
elusive Snap, Crackle anad Pop!
Tiptoe & Tonto
