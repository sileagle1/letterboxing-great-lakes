Over the past year a lot of my letterboxing friends have asked about
the camp where I work. I wanted to mention that this weekend at Camp
Miniwanca (at Stony Lake, near Shelby, MI) we are having our Fall
Family Festival, which is like an Open House -- a time for folks to
come out and learn about the camp, explore the grounds and participate
in some of the activities and programs we provide in our mission of
positive youth development. The Open House is Saturday afternoon,
Oct. 20 and is free of charge. If you would like more information,
check out our web site at www.ayf.com and follow the link on the home
page to the Miniwana Fall Family Festival.
