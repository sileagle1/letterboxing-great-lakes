Spring Fling Gathering Report

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-05-07 13:01:59 UTC
Just wanted to say a huge thanks to the TJ_Mich Family and everyone
who helped with planning and contributing boxes for putting on a
wonderful gathering in Northville, MI yesterday. The weather was
absolutely perfect -- sunny, on the cool side, and the park was
beautiful with a really nice network of trails and lots of spring
wildflowers blooming.

It was fun to see some old friends again, and great to meet so many
new letterboxers, mostly from eastern MI, but a few from other
areas. In some cases it was finally being able to put a face with a
name that has been familiar on the list, in my letterboxes or in my
e-mail box, in other cases, completely new acquaintances. I always
love hearing the stories of how people got started in letterboxing,
their trail adventures and also seeing new signature stamps.

Azobox and I didn't have time to hunt down all of the boxes before
hitting the road back over to the west side of state, but those we
did find were great fun and I absolutely loved the "May Days" series
idea.

If anyone has pictures, please feel free to post them in the photos
section.

Thanks to everyone for a great time!

SpringChick
