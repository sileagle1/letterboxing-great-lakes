"Gods, Historic Idols and Mountains"

Feel:
The first thing I should say is that even the Kings and Queens: the
Royal Historic contributors to Letterboxing feel. They laugh, cry, and
worry. We as people are never truly free from our Social Constraints
and the desire for loving acceptance.

We want our Letterboxes to please, we long for acceptance, our tender
feelings are sometimes woven into our boxes, our LB Persona and that
can be damaged by others who are not careful and considerate. (Does
This Make Sense To You Readers)?

Just because we are, they are Letterboxers does not exclude us/them
from pain. In that sense the PNW was no different than Spring Lake,
Michigan. (Did I think it would be different)???

Yes, I thought being the crux of Legends, I would find, cutting edge
boxes, places with reckless, loving, artistic abandon. I thought I
would find my heroes infallible to the words and critiques of others.
Or that, no harsh words would/could ever find there way into such a
fine group. This was SO not the case. I am not writing this as a
critic myself, I truly loved what I found, I just thought it was
noteworthy that YES, we all feel even our Gods. So lets be nice to
them, wrap them, swaddle them and nurture there lives. Tell them that
they are appreciated, hold our tongues when we feel the need to
control, play nice and praise hard work, though it may differ from
ours. Let's live with the knowledge that all who walk this Earth feel
pain, and it is our duty as humans to recognize this and try to
alleviate others fears and melancholy.

See:
My main pursuits were to infiltrate Der Mad Stampers life,
vampiracally suck the Genius out of his soul and become a God myself.
Well, he is So worth Vamping, but I left him intact and I can say that
I felt I have found a true friend. He is a fantastical person and
should be given the posh life of Royalty that he deserves! I hope that
we serve him well and carry on his legacy for all of time.

I met the Goddess Pandora and her loving Iron Bear, to me there is no
woman finer than she who planted the most amazing boxes I found! True
tender love resides in Pandora and we must praise her for so
fearlessly making her boxes windows to her soul. Thank you Pandora,
for having the whimsical notion that a person who plants there soul in
a box will not get crushed by fearful finders. I am afraid boxes of
this nature may not last very long. As they are wrought with such fine
feeling, such pure love that others may easily have access to the
maker's heart. It is dangerous and commendable and I am honored to
have found some.

Maiden:
Of all the Letterboxers in the area who were asked to meet me at the
Lucky Lab Brew Pub, only Maiden, Tainted Knight, and Der Mad Stamper
braved the meeting of me. Maiden, who plants like there is NO tomorrow
was in a nutshell totally funny and nice! She is been SO horribly
bitten by the LB bug that she has graced us with a seemingly endless
amount of drive by's! As a traveler I found this quite pleasing, to
say the least! Thank you fine Maiden! May you never tire!

Home?
In the end I missed my Michigan Trails, Spring Chicks boxes and the
sculpting and planting of my own. I did not plant while I was there,
though I did carve there and plan on shipping my creation back to be
planted in a designated place.

What else can I say when those are the only words in my head? Nothing,
but thank you everyone who made me feel at home and took time out of
their busy lives to entertain Lil' Ol' Me!

I was entertained! It was a fantastic adventure for me and many
lessons were learned or reinforced.

The End.
