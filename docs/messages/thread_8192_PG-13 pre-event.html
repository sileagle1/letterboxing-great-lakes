PG-13 pre-event

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2015-05-19 10:54:37 UTC-05:00
Special thanks to TJMich and HowD Girls for hosting the pre-gathering
event! They have posted it on AQ with the clever name PG-13: The
Pre-GLG13 Get-together: Letterboxers Strongly Cautioned! They have
picked a park that is about 1 mile south of the Lowden camping grounds
in a city park that boasts parking, a shelter, bathrooms, and a nice
playground.

As for the actual gathering, we will start to give a bit more
information/reminders about the event next week, after the Memorial day
weekend. Last reminders to send us donations to get the 'Supporter
Series' carved by Coffee Beans (ie, rare!) and clue packets early for
the suggested donation of $10/adult or $25/family.

A few notes about the park. The trails are generally nice and wide,
mostly grass or dirt, and very doable with most any sort of off-roading
stroller. There is 8-10miles of trails adjacent to the gathering
shelters between the north and south trails, and we are going to end up
planting most of the boxes there. However, the trails do seem to be
lined with a decent amount of what looks like poison ivy. We are trying
to avoid planting boxes in those areas, but we can't guarantee it!
Also, all the trails seem to have at least one big hill along the way,
if not a few. On the plus side, we are planting the boxes a bit closer
than originally planned to make up for the ups and downs. Finally,
although we haven't had any run-ins yet, I wouldn't be surprised if
there are ticks around.

almost time for the GLG!
