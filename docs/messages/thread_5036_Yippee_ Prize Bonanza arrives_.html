Yippee! Prize Bonanza arrives!

From: artistampmi (desmene@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-05-12 19:53:19 UTC
A gentle knock on the side door led me to investigate and find a tantalizing brown box... yes!
return address "SpringChick"!
What a bountiful array of prizes inside that included lock'n'locks, carving medium, inks,
logbooks, a neck-cooler cloth, beautifully hand-macramd keyfob with awesome artist's
palette and a package of delicious trailmix.
Thanks to all who played the game this year and provided prizes. I think SC's bingo kept us
smiling in the midst of some long grey days. And now summer stretches out ahead of us--
thank goodness! Enjoy, everyone...
all the best,
artistamp


RE: [LbGLK] Yippee! Prize Bonanza arrives!

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-05-12 19:37:46 UTC-05:00
Yes, I also found a large box waiting on our porch when I got home! I
patiently waited for Martini Man to get home before we opened the "booty"
box. SpringChick had placed two bags of goodies inside, one labeled with my
name (for the perimeter) and the other with Martini Man's name (for full
card).

My bag included a nice assortment of ink pads & logbooks, some small LnL's
and another cylindrical container for a small box. It also included a cool
handmade zipper pull/key ring that included macram twine, beads that
spelled out LBNA and some other colorful beads. (I already put this on my
pack! ) Other useful items included carving material, a small first aid kit,
some "wet wipe" type cloths to clean our inky hands, and some clips that we
are guessing could be used for cooties. Watch out at the next gathering!!!

Thanks to SpringChick for organizing the game and thanks to all the players
who donated such nice prizes!

Wisconsin Hiker

We suppose we have to soften our disappointment in not getting the socks due to the fact
that you had a particularly grueling and disappointing day! We're all for whatever it takes
to get MM out of a funk...But the socks must have catapulted him clear out of the
doldrums and into the euphoric! All we can say is, "TAKE A HIKE!"

Actually, We've been waiting for all the winners to get their booty before posting. Spring
Chick hand delivered our bag of goodies to us at the Forest Fairies:Spring Awakening
Gathering. As she handed over the beautiful gift bag packed with all sorts of wonderful
letterboxing stuff, she carefully explained that the package did not have the SOCKS
included....that it was TRADITION for the full card winner to get the socks, and we were in
fact the second place winners. We all know you don't mess with the Chick and Tradition!

I immediately knew that Martini Man was on my hit list and I was going to have to
challenge him on the coveted socks. But, as I peered into the bag my disappointment
quickly melted with the sight of the cutest handmade knit hat by Maid Marion of the Robin
Hood & Merry Men clan! How easily we change affection!

And as luck would have it, Mrs Doglvrs was on the trail with us and asked if we would be
willing to barter something for another pair of socks...Tiptoe contemplated trading in
Tonto who wasn't doing a very good job finding boxes that day, but reconsidered after
thinking she would have to drive home alone that evening. It was established that Tiptoe
would come up with an art piece for her. Mr. Doglvrs assured us she can whip knit those
babies out pretty quick...Tonto knowing Tiptoe's speed at getting anything
done...remarked under his breath..Take your time!

We thank all of you who contributed such wonderful gifts..who could think that there
would be so many different things...lock n'locks, PZCut, gift card, markers, papers,
logbooks, ink stamps, compass, an Atlas Quest patch, camo tape..Oh boy was it great! We
loved it all! It almost makes one wish for winter again just so we can get back to BINGO!
And, I really want to wear that hat!
Tiptoe....and Tonto
