The snow is starting to melt, so we are getting even more excited for
June. Our pile of donated stamps has grown to six gorgeous carves, and
we still encourage the rest of you to carve a stamp.

In addition, we are going to follow the model developed by last year's
GLK gathering, and we are now welcoming cash donations in advance of the
gathering. While we will still put out a donation jug at the event (to
defray the costs of some of the food, shelter permits, boxes, clues
sheets...), we encourage you to DONATE EARLY! Anyone who donates by May
1 will get the clues early (rather than Saturday morning, you will get
them around Wednesday) and will receive clues to a baker dozen
"supporter series".

The suggested donation is $10/adult or $25/family. You can mail your
donation to the address listed on the stamp submission form (please
include your email address), or contact me to arrange some other
electronic method. Just a reminder, IL parks do not require you to pay
on entry...so kick a few of those dollars our way!

See you in June!
