Great Event!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-06-10 03:29:47 UTC
Many thanks to ArchaeoFrog & Verona Bird Patrol for hosting such a great event today! The weather was perfect, no bugs, lots of trails with a TON of boxes and great food at the shelter. Even though there were probably ~150 people at the event, the trails were uncrowded and we rarely met another group. Martini Man & I enjoyed going out with different boxers throughout the day, and that is one of the especially fun aspects of an event. What is normally a solitary hobby turns into a chance to get to know some of our fellow addicts a bit better!

In addition to chatting with our old friends, we also had a chance to meet several boxers we hadn't seen before and were happy to add several new exchanges to our logbooks.

We managed to find 45 boxes today and are looking forward to getting back out on the trails to hopefully find 34 more boxes tomorrow.

Thanks again to our great hosts this year!

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. We were out with the Pugnasties and PackerBacker on our last loop of the day, Near the end of the loop, Mr. Pugnasty noticed his glasses were no longer in his case. If anyone finds his glasses on the TV loop, please contact him!
