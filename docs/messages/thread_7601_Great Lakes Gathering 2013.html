From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2013-01-15 05:34:39 UTC
We have a gathering host for the 2013 gathering!

The gathering will be hosted in Indiana by the Boston Terrorists. There had been a few bids from Ohio, but nothing came through. Ordinarily this would have moved the gathering to the next state in rotation - Minnesota. But there is a group from MN already working on the 2014 event and it didn't seem fair to mess up their plans for that. So considering that Indiana was the state after that, and we were running out of time, I accepted the offer from the Boston Terrorists to host the gathering when the most recent Ohio candidate didn't work out.

They have selected a date and secured a location and will be communicating that soon!

A HUGE thank you to the Boston Terrorists for stepping up to host!

SpringChick
