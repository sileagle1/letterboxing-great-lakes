From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-17 03:28:41 UTC
I finally purchased my first order of PZKut and I got busy last night carving my stamp for
the June GL Gathering, The Rose Gardeners. With the most intricate design to date and a
good magnifying lamp, and lots of patience, it is amazing what great work one can do. It's
absolutely stunning. Even my silent boxing partner proclaimed it "very good, very cute."
Well, maybe not cute; they are not the Disney version of the Rose Gardeners.

I will make a black ink master for my files and mail it Monday. Do we have the option of
calling it a Permanent or Temporary Stamp? If so, I'll make it a Event Only Stamp, and
bring it back home after the event. I will be living in the Rose City soon and there is a
great Rose Garden not far from my new home.

speedsquare
