Musings on Maintenance

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-11-27 05:03:41 UTC
The Sunday before Thanksgiving was a gorgeous day, so I set out to do
some Box Maintenance on all the Cemetery Stroll boxes and the
Undergound RR one in Lombard. I was pleased to FIND all the boxes I
went out looking for and also pleased that they were all hidden well!
This box maintenance can be some work though--I needed to replace a few
baggies and THREE logbooks. I had some spare logbooks in my bag
(already stamped with the Waltzing Pigs stamp) but I still had to stamp
the box's stamp into the log books and write down when we placed it and
who the FF was, etc. I made a mental note to myself--make 2 logbooks
for each box when first placing it so that maintenance will be easier.
It was fun to go back home and look at all those filled-up logbooks,
though

One of the boxes I checked is a bonus box which I recently discovered
is about 2 feet from a geocache. When someone reported that they
couldn't find this box, I went out and found it IN the geocache. I put
it back where it belonged and all is well. I have noticed several
geocachers who have logged in. Even found (and removed) a few trinkets
when doing the maintenance

There seems to be a rash of HH lately. Found one when I was doing this
maintenance, so that was a nice bonus. It was interesting to see all
of the HH that have gone through our boxes. Even more interesting to
see that some of these HH were ones that we had found in other boxes,
placed in different boxes, and then made their way to OUR boxes.

Had a chance to drop off some HH when we were up in Michigan over
Thanksgiving. One of the boxes we found has been around for a few
years and had a lot of stamps. It was fun to see some of the stamps
from folks who post on this list.
