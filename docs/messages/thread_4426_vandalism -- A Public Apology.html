Big Purple Foot & All,

I was entrusted by Goose Chaser's family to re-hide Lincoln Park Art, the
last box Goose Chaser planted before her death.

This box means so much to so many, and I have let everyone down.

I could have been more careful in my placing the box. I was too busy writing
down notes and taking compass bearings that I neglected to take in my
surroundings and those who may have been watching me.

I lingered a bit at the hiding spot making sure that the box was well
covered and out of sight.

Big Purple Foot, I knew how much this box meant to you. I am deeply sorry
for being careless in my search for a new spot. It was meant to "live" on for
many more years.

To the family of Goose Chaser, I'm sorry I let you down in your trust of me
to take care of this box sufficiently.

To all letterboxers, I hope you get the opportunity to find the rest of
Goose Chaser's boxes. I also hope you have the chance to come to the "Wild Goose
Chase" gathering on Oct. 6th to honor the memory of a letterboxing pioneer.

Hart x6
