Holy Grail Update

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-12-26 22:36:41 UTC
There recently has been some good news on the HG front.

The Sir Robin's Minstrels box (which also had the Migrating Coconuts HH) was found away from its hiding location just before the snow blanketed northern MN. Gooble Gobble is in the process of getting it from the finder (Wallflower), fixing it up and replanting it.

Also, "It's Just a Flesh Wound" in Batvia, IL was also found after many reports that it was missing (including from Team Taco the planters). We're working on getting that one back in action too.

For those who have won the top three prizes, I have NOT forgotten. The problem is that what I intended to have as prizes has become more difficult to obtain than it used to be and I'm still trying to find a new source. Hopefully that will be resolved soon too. Those of you still attempting the series, don't give up! I'm planning that everyone who completes the series will get some prize so they can show off that they found the grail.

Dragon
