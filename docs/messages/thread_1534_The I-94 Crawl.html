The I-94 Crawl

From: m_clan_lbers (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-29 14:57:53 UTC
Alright, so it seems there's enough responses to get this thing going.

First, the facts:
Number of Miles- 1614
* Montana- 249 miles
* North Dakota- 353 miles
* Minnesota- 260 miles
* Wisconsin- 351 miles
* Illinois- 81 miles
* Indiana- 45 miles

Major Cities -
* WEST END: Billings, MT _____
* Bismark, ND _____
* Fargo, ND _____
* Twin Cities, MN (Sqwubb, ____)
* Madison, WI (M Clan, _____)
* Milwaukee, WI _____
* Chicago, IL (Howling Coyote, _____)
* Gary, IN (Tiptoe & Tonto, _____)
* Kalamazoo, MI (Pale Puppy Posse, _____)
* Detroit, MI (Trail Troll, _____)
* EAST END: Port Huron, MI

Let's call the thing "The I-94 Traffic Crawl" and prefix the boxes
accordingly (e.g. "The I-94 Traffic Crawl: Gary, IN". THEME: Just
play off the word "crawl" and think "traffic" - we've all been there,
right? Try and tie it in to your location somehow. Keep things a
mile or two off the freeway and send us somewhere interesting. We
need stimulation after staring at brake lights. (That part makes no
sense for Montana, but oh well...)

Submit ideas and locations to me off-list and I'll make sure we avoid
duplicates.

Let's also try to avoid the Oases since a few people are already
headed in that direction. Get us off the freeway!

It would be great to have all the major cities represented as well as
the start and end points of the freeway. I'll try to find our ND and
MT brothers and sisters.

Even if someone in your area is already signed up, it doesn't hurt to
have more than one. Many of us crawl along this route often.

This should be interesting and make our driving fun. I'm starting to
look forward to my next MI or Minneapolis trip. When you travel this
route, think about adding a box at a rest stop.

Looking forward to your emails and ideas,
M Clan

Re: The I-94 Crawl

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-06-29 16:36:17 UTC
This is a really neat project and I know people are always looking
for organized information on boxes along their travel routes. The
way it is now, most of the easy-access boxes are buried in counties
and you really don't know how far from the expressway the boxes are
without studying the clues for each one.

Might I suggest that you start a Yahoo group specific to this
project? It's not that I don't want it here, just that it spans
geographic regions and as you include some of the states further
West, they may not appreciate having to wade through Great Lakes list
chatter to keep up with project messages.

In many of the art circles I frequent, this is how it is done with
projects -- a separate group is formed and anyone participating or
interested in the project can join. It helps to keep project details
from getting lost in the main list messages.

Just an idea... If you would rather keep it here, that is fine also.

Another suggestion is to create a database in whichever Yahoo group
you end up using so that people can enter information about boxes
they are placing along the route.

BTW -- how many miles are in MI?

SpringChick
