Sorry, guys, Hershey stays! How could I stay mad at those puppydog eyes?
She felt bad, and it was "only" $100 deductible on the insurance (that's a
lot of stampmaking supplies...). I gave her the cold shoulder for a
few hours, but it was killing me to do that more than her. And yes, we're
going to try to head out again Monday...with the windows open before she
gets into the car!!

I did spend the time waiting for the repair shop to call me carving a stamp
for a postal box. Tentatively titled "In the Doghouse", it is for anyone
whose dog did something really bad to land them "in the doghouse". The
concept is to tell the story of what your dear puppy did. A picture of the
pooch would be a welcomed extra touch (actually it ties into the design of
the journal).

So if you've got a qualifying story, drop me an off-list message (unless the
story is too good not to share!) with your address to be included in this
one. (Exclusive pre-release announcement for GLKLb members!!)
