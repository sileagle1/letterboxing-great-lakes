Holiday Survivor Mini-Meet

From: lcoil79 (lcoil79@insightbb.com) | Date: 2007-01-22 01:27:47 UTC
Well, today was the Mini-Meet, and I think we can call it a success.
We unfortunately lost a few letterboxers due to the bad weather that
suddenly blew through last night. We did have the honor of being
little Emily (sp?) Cottontail's first event, and as such put together
a small memory book with all those in attendance stamping into it and
writting her a short note, all done without her parent's knowledge.
We also had some great success with our LBNA fundraiser raffle. We
managed to raise $114 that will now be sent to them to help cover
costs for a service that I'm sure every one of us in attendance use.

With this success, we are looking forward to our Gathering at Forest
Glen Park this summer. Thanks again to all those who came and helped
us to make this happen. We also extend our gratitude to those who
planned to come but couldn't, we're glad you put your safety first and
know you would have come if it had been safe to do so.
