From: thedoctorsb (tana_field@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-08 18:39:44 UTC
Less than a month remains until the Northeastern Iowa Letterboxing
Event, held in Decorah, IA on May 3, 2008!!! We are so excited and
look forward to a great event with lots of neat stamps to be planted
in an area that has yet to see letterboxes!

All are invited and encouraged to attend! Please see the event link
for more details:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=422

And, if the raffle prizes aren't enough to tempt you... I have it on
good authority that there will be a special appearance by a bagpiper! ;-)

A wonderful day in the beautiful Oneota River valley meeting fabulous
people and finding terrific artwork in a fun and casual atmosphere.
What could be better?!
