Hi to all the newbies and those we haven't met yet!

Our trail name, J&BBooks, stands for our names, Jen and Brad, and our
love of reading (we currently have six full bookcases in our house!).
Although there are two of us, we decided to use one signature stamp and
trail name because from the get-go we have treated letterboxing as
something to do together. In fact, neither of us has ever 'boxed
without the other!

We live in Ann Arbor, MI and have done a lot of boxing in the area. We
just got back from a 10-day vacation out east, and picked up
letterboxes in OH, PA, NY, CT, and MA. (More about that on our blog
when we have the time to update it!) We discovered letterboxing in June
of 2005. The LBNA site was a Yahoo link of the day. I read the
description, thought it sounded fun, and forwarded it to B. That night,
we carved our first stamp (out of an eraser) and headed out to our
first box, despite the fact that it was rainy and buggy out. We were
hooked immediately and have been happily boxing ever since! We have
only planted three boxes at this point, but hope to increase that. I
think we have found around 270 or so? Clearly, we like finding more
than planting! But that is one of the beauties of this hobby, you can
really participate in whatever way you most enjoy. And we love
gatherings... and hope to meet some more new people at the MI fall
mystery gathering!
