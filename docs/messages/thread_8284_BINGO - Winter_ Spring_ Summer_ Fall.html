Historical Great Lakes Letterboxing BINGO trivia:


The earliest that a straight-line Bingo was called was on JAN 21 in 2013 and the latest it was called was FEB 10 in 2011, 2012 and 2013.


The earliest that a 10-spot Bingo was called was on JAN 31 in 2011 and the latest it was called was FEB 7 in 2013.


The earliest that a 6-pack Bingo (2x3 rectangle anywhere on the card) was called was on JAN 31 in 2009 and the latest it was called was FEB 23 last year.
