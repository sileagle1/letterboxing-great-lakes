Hi there! I've actually been here all along, but obviously mostly
lurking ;-). I seem to be letterboxing mostly in the U.P. and mostly
when I have nieces and nephews to entertain. (Which raises the
question of how many "mostly's" can this woman fit into two
sentences?). I confess that there are boxes within two miles of me
in Illinois (Forest Park) that I've never gotten, including, alas, the
now departed box by the circus graves. Last summer I carved a stamp,
intending to place it near my apartment, but there's construction
starting and I don't know how accessible the area will be so it's just
been sitting in my bag waiting for the stars to be in alignment, or
something.
I did up my involvement in AtlasQuest after they threatened to
orphan my boxes since I hadn't signed in in a year. I'd also had
requests to put my Wyoming boxes there so it gave me a good excuse to
get that done. It's very gratifying that people are still finding
those boxes I placed four years ago.
So carry on. I'm still here.
