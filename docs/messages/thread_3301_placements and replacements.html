From: Kathi (klkrank@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-11 00:57:17 UTC
Dragon's gathering has gotten me into the holiday spirit so "Holiday
Magic" has been replaced for the 2006 season. It's the same stamp as
last year, but in a new location, and there's no fee involved this time.

The first in a series of letterbox tributes to my Dad has been
placed. "Remembering Boxer: Wolves" has been hidden in Illinois.
While it's listed as a mystery, it really is no big mystery - I'm just
trying to keep it off the Google radar since the location it is hidden
at does not know of its placement. Please be overly discrete if you go
looking for this one.

Finally, a special thanks to The Two Freetimers for adopting and
placing the replacement for my long-lost "SPAM!" box in Austin, MN.
Its new name is "SPAM Town, USA". We had a dickens of a time posting
the clues on LbNA. Choi was on the case trying to determine why both
the Freetimers and I were getting error messages denying us access. He
couldn't figure it out, but the only thing I can guess is somehow the
website did not appreciate me attempting to post spam and was trying to
block me??? I finally tricked it by spelling it "sp*m" and then once
it was accepted I was able to correct the spelling. Anyway, thank you
again to the Two Freetimers, and if you're ever in that region of MN,
please treat yourself to a sample of SPAM! (Just watch out for those
bloody Vikings!)
