We found the 6 boxes in Dragon's PI series today (only 3 days left
for this "limited time" series). Very colorful and amusing - We
especially liked the Moon PI box! There were intermittant showers so
we did get a bit wet retrieving and replacing some of the boxes.
Luckily this is a mostly "urban" series so we could do our stamping
in the comfort of our dry car. We were the 5th couple to stamp in
since "PI Day", March 14.

This brings us to F76, so we're closing in on the F100 milestone.
Unfortunately Martini Man is having foot problems, so that may
curtail searching for boxes on longer hikes, at least for a while.
