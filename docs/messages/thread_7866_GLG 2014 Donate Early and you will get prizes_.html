GLG 2014 Donate Early and you will get prizes!

From: (eshop@blees.com) | Date: 2014-01-19 06:28:48 UTC-08:00

Not everyone gets a prize when they play BINGO, but you will get prizes if you donate to the Great Lakes Gathering before the event.


At Great Lakes Gatherings in the past, there has been a donation bucket available (to help defray expenses for shelter rental, printing, containers and mailing).  For this year's event, the Minnesota Carving Consortium is suggesting a donation of $10 or more per adult letterboxer.  The MCC is offering an incentive to those that send their donations before May 1st.  What kind of incentive, you ask?  (packet available on Friday night and a special WOM clue).  Any money collected beyond 2014 expenses will be donated to the 2015 GLG organizing  group. 


Contact Dartmoor Dreamer for where to send money.  Non-credit card money can be sent via PayPal ("money to family and friends") to DartmoorDreamer@hotmail.com
