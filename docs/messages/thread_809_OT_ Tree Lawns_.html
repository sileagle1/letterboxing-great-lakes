From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-26 09:21:22 UTC-07:00
Kind of ... I wouldn't call it an island, though. In Ohio (Cleveland?) it's the strip of grass between the street and the sidewalk where we put our trash out for collection. Traditionally the city plants a tree in the middle of each one, but not every tree lawn has a tree on it. Also, technically property of the city, but don't expect them to mow it! :)

Here's a Cincinnati-area thing - my cousin's wife was born & bred down there. And here in the northern part of our state, if someone's talking and you don't catch what they're saying we usually say, "What?" Evidently if you're from Cincy, you say, "Please?" At least, Susan does...

Doodle.
