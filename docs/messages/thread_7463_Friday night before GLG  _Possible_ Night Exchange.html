I just called Blue Mound SP and asked about extra visitors in the campground area ... there is a possibility that the Minnesota contingent could host an evening event. (We Minnesotans have a few camping reservations clustered together.)

The Blue Mound policies that would effect a Friday Night event are:
1. Every vehicle entering the park needs a State Park sticker (either annual or a daily sticker)
2. Parking in the campground is limited to 2 vehicles per campsite. Other cars need to park in the overflow area and walk to the campsite. (I cannot tell from the park maps how far the walk would be.)
3. The park closes at 11 pm. All guests need to leave before 11:00.

So, Minnesota, shall we host a Friday Night Exchange Event?
