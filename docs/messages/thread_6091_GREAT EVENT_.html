GREAT EVENT!

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-10-05 09:12:48 UTC-05:00
Aimlesst, LibraryGrrl, all the WONDERFUL carvers, and the rest of the
Boxacon crew:

WOW, A GREAT EVENT! The boxes were plentiful, well spaced, and
beautiful! We had a great time roaming the trails on Saturday finding
boxes and bumping into the occasional group of boxers. However, since
there were SO MANY boxes and a decent amount of trails, it didn't seem
like there were so many people at each box. We managed to get out again
on Saturday, and again had a wonderful day (I think we saw Aimlesst and
her group from a distance, in addition to bumping into the Christmas
Elves, Martini Man, and Wisconsin Hiker). Unfortunately, we had a to
leave a few boxes for next time (doht!)...but we did come away with at
least a few extra boxes other than the ones we were actually actively
hunting (a benefit of: 1. dual 'interpretations' when reading
clues....'you look over there at x steps, and I'll look at x double
steps'...and 2. lots of boxes lurking!) which is always fun!
We had a fun time reconnecting with boxers we already know, as well as,
those we met for the first time on Saturday.

Thank you to everyone for the AWESOME weekend!

Atom118 of Atomic Beans
