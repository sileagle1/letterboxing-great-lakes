From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-11-16 16:53:19 UTC
Oh, this one is great!

Isn't it amazing the things that run through your mind while you are
sleeping? When I was in college, I used to joke that I debugged my
COBOL programs in my sleep... never failed if there was one that was
giving me fits, it would come to me while I was sleeping.

Reminds me of this summer when we were in the U.P. I spent a couple
of hours trying to figure out a LB and was making a really stupid
mistake (really, very stupid). It was 1 of a series of 3 and I had
got the other 2 boxes, so I really wanted to find this one. But,
finally after walking around the entire park a couple of time (a few
miles), I gave up. We were about 45 minutes up the highway en route
to the Porcupine Mountains when I suddenly realized where I went
wrong. I blurted it out, but thought it best not to ask to go back
as we were already way behind schedule due to the time I had already
spent looking for that box. Imagine how thrilled I was when Old Shoe
turned the truck around and headed back down for it! (What a guy!)

SpringChick
