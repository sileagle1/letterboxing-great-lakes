I have absolutely zero Chinese blood too (although my dad has an aunt who is Korean) but I've always been fascinated with the asian zodiac. Katy and I started celebrating Chinese New Year a few years ago as an excuse to host a party. I make these yummy little steamed pot stickers (dumplings, I'm not sure what the Chinese word for them is but the Japanese call them gyoza) and Flying Dragon makes absolutely fantastic pork buns (I believe they are called shu mai in Chinese). We also learned how to make fortune cookies which, although not truly asian in origin, are great for parties. It's a lot of fun and if I'm going to have a bunch of people over who aren't family it's a great incentive for me to clean my house from top to bottom (ok, so the basement is still a mess...but I have to clean that up a bit this year too so that the dogs have someplace to hang out during the party).
