Mr. & Mrs. Smith as letterboxers

From: kermitboxer (kermitboxer@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-07-23 13:06:53 UTC
Funny story.

I went letterboxing on my day off, and my husband went to school and
work. When I came home, I told him about a very unusual part of my
sleuthful day.


Me: "So there I was, at this library, looking in this section for this
false book that holds the LB, and suddenly this woman sidles up to me
and says, quietly, 'I think we're looking for the same thing.'"

My husband (mock horror): "Oh the humanity! The caste of secrecy has
been violated!" (mock seriousness) "So did you have to kill her?"

Me (eyebrow raised): "Gubuh? KILL HER? Are you stoned?"


[interjection here -- my husband is well aware of all the facets of this
hobby, including the stealth and secrecy, and is my frequent companion
on many of my searches. He is also very silly]


Husband: "Sure, it's like the movie 'Mr. & Mrs. Smith,' isn't it? Once
you guys find out you're competing for the same prize, you've gotta get
rid of the competition, right?"

Me (both eyebrows raised): "......you do know that we even have
gatherings, sometimes, right? Where whole bunches of us meet up in a
park and letterbox together?"

Husband (quietly): "My god. How did you make it out of there alive?"


:-)
-mph (Kermit)

PS -- Yes, as a matter of fact, we do look like Angelina Jolie and Brad
Pitt, thankyewverymuch.
