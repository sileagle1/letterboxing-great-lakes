We're from MN and we have some fun ones too.

1. What's that game that you play when you're a child where you run
around and tap people on the head "Duck, Duck ....... Gray Duck" -
We now live in IL and know that Minnesotans are the only ones that
call "Duck, Duck Grayduck.... Duck Duck Goose"

2. We have Hotdish instead of casseroles

3. Pop instead of soda

4. Peppermint Bon Bon instead of Mint Choc. Chip

5. We call that nighttime meal... supper instead of Dinner (At least
on the N. Side of Chicago we call it dinner)

We actually are IN a supper Club now. Just a dinner club that meets
once a month regularly, but our friends from near Rockford, IL named
the group, Supper Club. :)
