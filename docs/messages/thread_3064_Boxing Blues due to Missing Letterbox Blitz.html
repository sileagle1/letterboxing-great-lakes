We placed our first letterbox a little over a year ago and now have 11
boxes out there in the worldand thought we were doing pretty good
because none had gone missing. In the course of just a week, we have
gotten emails telling us that 3 (and possible a fourth) box are
missing. I used to enjoy clicking on my letterboxing email, but I'm
getting a little leery! Two of the boxes are in Florida, so it will
be a quite a while before we can check on them (unless I can convince
my folks to go looking for them when they go down this fall). It
sounds like the home of UGRR #1 got cut downmaybe we'll find it in
the wreckage. Unfortunately, we are in a really busy time right now
so it may be a few days or a week before we can check on the local
ones. Its sad to lose one, but this if just one blow after another :>(
