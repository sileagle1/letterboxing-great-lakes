We stayed there awhile back and found it to be a wonderful experience! They bring a
continental breakfast to you in a basket to enjoy in your room or out on your porch - a little
different than the usual communal breakfast of most B&B's. The grounds are lovely, the inn
(a restored barn & cottages) is top notch! The actual letterbox was placed by a friend of the
owners who has since moved away. BTW ...we needed a little help on the old clues...but then
again, we usually need a lot of help!
Tiptoe & Tonto
