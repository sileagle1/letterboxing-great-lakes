Northern MI Area Box Update

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-09 00:39:46 UTC
OldShoe and I spent the weekend up in the Traverse City area,
checking up on letterboxes and finding a couple of new ones.

The Grand Traverse lighthouse box was reported missing and it is
indeed missing AGAIN. The Old Mission lighthouse box was reported
by a couple of recent finders to be wet and when we went to give it
some TLC, we found the contents scattered. It was very strange as
the little pile of debris and sticks that covered the ground indent
where the box should have been hiding, was undisturbed. We were
able to recover most of the pieces, although the rubber part of the
stamp was separated from the wood block and nowhere to be found. It
appears that a HH had been left in the box, but there was no
logbook, only a little stamp. As the HH was not stamped into the
logbook, I am not sure that is even what it is or who left it and
really have no way of knowing how to contact the owner. If you left
a HH in this box recently, please contact me -- it is a store-bought
stamp, foam backed, the image of a little gremlin type creature.

All of the other boxes we checked on were in good shape and well-
hidden. A couple we were unable to check on because of time
limitations and very busy areas. Seems like half the state of MI
was up there enjoying the gorgeous fall weekend.

SpringChick
