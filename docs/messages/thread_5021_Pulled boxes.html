The following boxes have been pulled from Pigeon Creek Park until
further notice due to the reconstruction (aka *destruction*) of the
horse trails side of the park: Entertainment Series Part II and The
Wise One (Grandvilla), Pteryx (Mobiusklein), Griffin and Waiting in the
Wings (Azobox). Even if the destruction from thinning red pine trees
didn't effect the location of the boxes, the clues were compromised.

could keep saying was "Wow!". It's truly amazing. Grandvilla
