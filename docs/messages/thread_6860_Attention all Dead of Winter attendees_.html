From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2011-01-03 16:46:27 UTC-08:00
I like to carve stamps in one sitting when possible and will stay up late into
the night to finish up, but some are just too large/complex to complete at
once. I do however take little breaks as I work because my neck and shoulders
get sore -- I've tried different positions, table heights, etc. and the ideal
carving position is unfortunately not ideal posture for me.

I usually carve at the dining room table because there is a lot of room, the
table height is good and it allows me to be in the same vicinity as the living
room where OldShoe is generally watching television or on his laptop. I often
carve listening to music on headphones though -- so I guess it doesn't really
matter if I am in the same place as him or not ;-)

SpringChick
