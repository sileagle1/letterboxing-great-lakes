Great Lakes Bingo - I Will Survive

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-02-11 13:25:10 UTC
An ode, to my pathetic Bingo Card:

At first I was afraid, I was petrified
To look at the last ten days of words called and my Bingo card side-by-side [<-- just sing that line really fast]
You see, I haven't had any luck
This year my word choices have sucked
And so I'd given up checking
Thinking I just needed a break

And now I'm back online
Deciding it was finally time
To log into this email account and see what I could find
Two side games already won and ten more words called
And out of all those words, I had only ONE?  That's it, I fold!

Oh wait, this isn't poker.
It's bound to get better, right?  Say I'm not the joker!
I'll hold out hope that this week my card finally fills up
I'm probably just delusional. 
Wi-Hi, you need to shake those words up!

Oh me oh my, I will survive!
The craziness of Great Lakes Bingo, yes I will survive!
Maybe not with my sanity intact
No, not maybe, I think it's a confirmed fact.
And I'll survive!  I will survive!
Hey hey hey!

*dances out of the building*

Doodle Bug ;)

My dear Doodle,

Perhaps your card is barren because you are not giving this game your utmost attention. Are you not aware that you should be leaping out of bed EACH morn to check the hallowed word of the day? Just because the Bingo caller is lolling about in Arizona in the Mountain Time Zone is no excuse for not checking. If the word isn't there when you wake, check again at lunch time. The Bingo gods do not reward those that let ten long days elapse between paying homage to the game!

There is still time to mend your ways and perhaps the fates will smile upon you...

Wisconsin Hiker
