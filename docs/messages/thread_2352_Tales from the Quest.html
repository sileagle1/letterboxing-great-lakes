Tales from the Quest

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-04-01 01:26:49 UTC
Last Saturday we set off on a quest for the Bring Out Your Dead box
to acquire the image and another piece of the Holy Grail code.
During our visit to the area, unlike the mighty Dragon, we were not
aware of anything "pushing on our chest", but something obviously
bewitched us. Was it the spirits or the groups of gamboling maidens
who appeared to be on some type of historical quest themselves?
Whatever the cause, when we departed the dead we had brought forth,
we unknowingly left our personal marks behind yes, our signature
stamps!

We continued on throughput the weekend, shopping at markets, tidying
the hovel and feasting with friends, none the wiser about our tragic
loss. However we were made aware of it Sunday evening when a missive
arrived via our magical mailbox. The gallant band of knights from the
north, Papeseed, Triclops & Escher, had discovered our stamps and
took them with them for safekeeping. Knowing their importance, they
wasted no time in dispatching them southward again and we gratefully
had them in our inky hands by Wednesday. Blessings upon them!

So beware on your quest, there may be unexpected dangers along the
way However there may also be unexpected luck and kindness!

Wisconsin Hiker
