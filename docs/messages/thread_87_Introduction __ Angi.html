Hello Angi, welcome to the group and congrats on finding your first
box! That's great also that you started right out by carving your
stamp!

Sometimes what people do if they have a larger stamp and it doesn't
fit into the log books (as they vary from about 3 x 5 to very tiny
ones in microboxes) is to just stamp a portion of the stamp -- one
corner or maybe a portion of the image that could stand all on its
own. You can use water-based markers to color just parts of the
stamp if you don not want to stamp the entire image.

Happy Boxing!

SpringChick
