Armadillo Jo done lost her stamp!

From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-10 15:14:15 UTC-07:00
So I leave early this AM to go letterboxing in the
Lafayette area, as I know i'm not going to get to it
next weekend because it's homecoming. I get to one of
those cruddy towns on SR 43 (I say cruddy because I
used to get pulled over there a lot in the middle of
the night on my way back to school) for a box, find
the box..and yay i'm the first finder. So, I get out
my stuff...and my sig stamp is nowhere to be found.
*insert primal scream here*. I call my mom, Ugler, and
ask...do you have my stamp in your bag? She
looks...nothing. So now i'm thinking, where is that
stamp? It's in Plainfield of course, the last freakin'
box we got *last* Sunday. GRR. I certainly do NOT want
to go back to Plainfield to get that stamp....so I
guess i'll be making a new one. If anyone finds the
old one, let me know...as not only did I lose my
compass on that trip, but apparently my sig stamp as
well.

As far as the TOTW goes, I am trying a variety of
boxes for my plants to see how they hold up. I have 6
of those blue seal Rubbermaids out there, 4 of those
Gladwear el-cheapo things (which are mostly close to
home), 3 little standard Rubbermaid thingys, and one
plant that I haven't done yet with the Lock & Lock. I
don't have a size preference although I'm trying to
get the boxes as small as possible for the stuff
contained within. I'm hesitant about this Lock & Lock
because it is so freakin' big compared to my other
plants. Then again, the stamp is honkin' big too....

--Armadillo Jo
