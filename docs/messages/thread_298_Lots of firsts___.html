Lots of firsts...

From: luckycharms9595 (merley95@aol.com) | Date: 2004-06-16 23:45:28 UTC
A little late, but I wanted to report a few things about the
gathering... It was loads of fun, wished we could have stayed
longer, but the mosquitoes were BAD. In fact, there was a moment
where Will was carried down the trail about ten feet by a swarm (he
was only about 2 feet off the ground).

We just started letterboxing about a month ago, and attended
(briefly) the gathering this past weekend. Our first event, where we
logged our first exchanges. Our 2 year old nephew, Will, and four
year old niece, Skyler, were with us- their first LB event too. Our
first time overnight babysitting. First time for Will ans Skyler
hiking, first time for poison ivy for Will. Last week we placed our
first box (Trees, Water, Sand in Kalamazoo, MI) and yesterday we
logged our first "first find" (does finding the third placement of a
box count as a first? Azobox?). And this is our first posting on
the group!

Glad to be a part of "the letterboxing club." Thanks for having such
a great event!
-Lucky Charms
