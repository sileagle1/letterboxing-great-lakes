This is scary...did we ALL have cigar boxes of stuff?? And yes, I
still have mine...it too was (correction...IS) a sturdy wooden one.
The goofiest stuff inside. Everything from the multicolored counting
sticks I received in first grade (remember those? Colored balsa wood
sticks...) to pen-and-ink drawing nibs from high school, name tag
from my job at college (dishroom in the dorm), ex-boyfriend's college
football schedule, a little toy from the dentist's prize box (okay,
these are NOT listed in chronological order! The dentist visit came
WAY before college!). I wonder if this is an indication of some
genetic predisposition towards letterboxing. Although I did not hide
and seek the stuff...just packratted it away.

The only treasure hunting I can recall at the moment was when my dad
would collect a red tin can full of pennies. He would then dump and
scatter them and my brother and I would hunt for them. Hey, at least
that activity MADE me some money instead of costing it!
