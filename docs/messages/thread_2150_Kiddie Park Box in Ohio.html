Kiddie Park Box in Ohio

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-09 07:40:25 UTC-08:00
So December 31st I convinced Deedle that we needed to
do one more letterbox hunt for 2005, esp. since there
was a box planted about 4 miles down the road from us
that we hadn't gotten to search for yet (I know, this
post is a little stale now, but last week I was too
busy trying to figure out how much alcohol we need to
buy for our wedding reception). And Letterboxing Law,
just as we found the spot we thought we were supposed
to start searching in, someone else shows up! Holding
what looks like a sheet of paper in their hand...

Well, since we weren't budging (taking photos of the
creek to stall) and he wasn't really leaving, we
decided to relocate and observe from afar. And sure
enough, he ended up going to the spot we had been at.
But then he came back, and went to another spot along
the creek...and then went back to the first part.
Then he pulled out his cell phone and was talking -
which made us wonder, if he was really a letterboxer,
did he know the placer and was calling to complain
that he couldn't find the box? Or was he looking for
something else that just happened to be where we
needed to be?

He finally left, and we went back to our search - and
couldn't find anything. We went to another part (the
same part the mystery man had gone to, also, after he
left our original spot) and again couldn't find
anything, even though we poked in all the hiding spots
that matched the clue's discription. How
disheartening... maybe the box got swept away, since
it was close to the creek? Or maybe it's really just
hidden THAT WELL, since we noticed on LbNA that
someone found it November 12, but someone else tried
and couldn't find it November 11...

Has anyone else searched for Kiddie Park in Brooklyn,
Ohio yet and if so, I'm interested in hearing what you
findings were...

Doodle Bug.
