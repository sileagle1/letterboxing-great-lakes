Boxing Crown Hill Cemetery Event, Indianapolis, IN 8-15-09

From: letterbox_alcalde (letterbox_alcalde@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-08-02 02:42:06 UTC
Come join us for a day of letterboxing fun in a great cemetery August 15th, 2009. Bring along a blanket and/or campchairs if you wish...We will have a potluck lunch and you can sign up for the dish of your choice!

Gates open at 8 AM and we will meet in Section 114 at 9:00 AM. There is a nice grove of trees, we can use (in case of rain).
Get there a little bit earlier and go out and box the 6 boxes that are already planted in the cemetery! Anyone wanting my cell for a contact number please exchange cell numbers by e mail.

We are antipating at planting 35+ traditional boxes for this event.

Tentative schedule
9:00 AM Sign-in and receive clue packet
Noon Return to Section 114 for photos & exchanges
12:30 PM Lunch
1:30 PM Clean-up and Door prize drawing, then back to boxing and socializing
6:30 PM Dinner plans? Meet in Section 114 if you'd like to join us for dinner at Golden Corral, keep in mind you probably won't make it to the Skeletons in the closet tour at 7:30.

7:30 PM Optional Tour of the Cemetery
SKELETONS IN THE CLOSET, Part II - Sensational headlines from the past! See the sunset from the top of the Crown!! Driving tour, participants follow along in their own cars. Meet inside the 34th St. and Boulevard Place stone gate entrance.
The cost for the tour is $5.00 for adults, $4.00 for seniors (age 55 and older), and $3.00 for students. Click here for a coupon for 1 free person: http://crownhillhf.org/docs/FreeTourPass2009.pdf

The Cemetery Grounds are open from 8 am to 8 pm.

Restrooms
Public restrooms are available behind the Gothic Chapel, which can be located from either cemetery entrance by following the white line painted on the road. There are separate restrooms for men and women, each with an outside entrance. There are also a set of restrooms available in the Crown Hill Mausoleum, which is open every day.

Directions
North Entrance to the Funeral Home and Cemetery Office is at 38th St. and Clarendon Road.

To reach the 34th STREET ENTRANCE at 34th Street and Boulevard Place: turn right (east) on 38th St. to Boulevard Place (2nd stop light) and turn right (south). Proceed south on Boulevard Place to 34th Street (stop sign) and turn right (west). Enter Crown Hill through the large stone gate.

Printable cemetery maps can be found here: http://www.crownhillhf.org/docs/Map_April05_CrownHill.pdf

If you're interest in attending and have an Atlasquest membership you can join on there, or if not please e mail us with how many people will be attending and your trail name and if you want to bring anything for the pitch in lunch.

Here's the link for the atlasquest event listing: http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1289
