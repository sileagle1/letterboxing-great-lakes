This easy transfer method uses Avery label backing sheets (labels removed) and an ink jet printer.  You feed the coated paper through the printer.  The ink beads up on the surface of the backing sheet.  Ink transfers to the carving rubber without rubbing.  Let dry.  No chemicals.
