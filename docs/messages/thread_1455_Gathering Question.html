From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-06-17 12:43:44 UTC
WOM means "Word-of-Mouth" letterbox. It refers to a box that is hidden
and does not have published or publicly accessible clues, rather clues
are passed from one person to another via word-of-mouth. Sometimes a
planter will distribute clues for a box to a handful of people via e-
mail or US Mail rather than post them publicaly -- these are often
called WOM boxes also.

There is a very good glossary of terms related to letterboxing here:

http://www.letterboxing.info/glossary/

SpringChick
