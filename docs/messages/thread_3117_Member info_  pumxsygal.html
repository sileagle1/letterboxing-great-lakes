I'm the second of the trio of bookcrossers (www.Bookcossing.com)turned
letterboxers. Aimlesst suggested we go out and try the Miller Park
box at a bookcrossing meeting. So on August 6, 2005, Aimlesst,
freshwater and myself set out to find our first box. I thought I was
just going along to see what it was about--I didn't even take any
equipment with me. We were so excited to find our first box! Not
having a stamp I signed in drawing my favorite doodle. Afterwards I
drove over to Target and got a stamp pad and journal and drove back to
the box to get the stamp in my journal. And I've carved that doodle
into my signature stamp. Now, about 150 boxes later in five states
and 1 plant I'm having a great time. Even went boxing in the snow for
The Cigna Cats' Twelve Days of Christmas series. Also during the
winter I worked out some of the "virtual boxes" that people have
done. Currently I'm mulling over a couple of ideas for some plants.
I have found letterboxers to be quite a varied group of people--with
many diverse interests which seem to add to fun.
