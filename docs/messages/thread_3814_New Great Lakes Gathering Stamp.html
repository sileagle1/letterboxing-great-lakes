From: jose_quiti (jose_quiti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-03-07 13:47:10 UTC
It will be "too late" for a Great Lakes Gathering 2007 stamp on June
3rd. :)
There are basically 3 options...
1. If you are going to the School House Cabin Fever Reliever gathering
in April, you can have your stamp contribution ready and just give it
to me personally.
2. You can have your stamp contribution ready before May 12th and mail
it to me. (E-mail me for the address. Clearly mark it for the
gathering, as I receive MANY PLB's and I don't want there to be any
confusion.)
OR
3. You can bring your stamp contribution with you, plant it yourself
and post the clues on the bulletin board.
Which ever option you choose, please let me know if it is to be a
permanent or temporary (just for the event) plant.
Thanks!
