Ode to the bingo masteer

From: baliddletoo (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2012-01-07 05:15:26 UTC
Oh, dear Mr. Bingo Master,
Won't you please call my words faster?

The words you have called thus far
can be thrown right back in the jar

Obscure boxes from Illinois and Wisconsin,
pretty worthless filling my squares in

If you'd care to to help cure my drought
please shout some general terms out!

Andrew, Andrew, you're the man!
BNL BaliGrl is your number one fan!
