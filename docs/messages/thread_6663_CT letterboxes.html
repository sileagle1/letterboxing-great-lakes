From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-06-22 12:20:49 UTC-05:00
I was in Middleton, CT visiting some friends almost exactly a year ago
and found a few boxes out there. Since my friends aren't boxers
themselves though, I was "limited" on what ones to find, but like WI
Hiker said, there are tons out there to pick from that may be closer to
where you are staying. We did a series of 3 boxes called "Science 101"
(which of course I picked just because they were science-themed stamps),
and it was a straight hike through some nice woods, maybe about a mile
round trip. There was no one else there when we were -- very green and
peaceful with some old rock walls near the last box at least. We also
found one called "Alchemy Symbols: Rubber" at a state park where I
believe the actor (tv? play?) who famously played Sherlock Holmes used
to live, but he donated the castle/land to the state when he died -- we
toured the castle too, and it was neat also.

Have fun!
Coffee Beans
