Here's a bit of insight into when things like this happen -

Your browser probably cached the "old" page with the clues unchanged
and only shows that page when you go back. If you don't see the
changes you expected, refresh the page by clicking your browsers
refresh button, then you'll probably see the changes.

I've recently had a report that my Bristol 2007 clues were still the
only ones available from my website. Once the person refreshed the
page however, they got the new clues. They had been looking at a page
from 6 months or so ago because that was the last time they visited.
Browsers do strange things sometimes.
