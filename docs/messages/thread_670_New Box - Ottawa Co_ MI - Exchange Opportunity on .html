New Box - Ottawa Co, MI - Exchange Opportunity on 9/22

From: aquamarine31577 (karajmcc@aol.com) | Date: 2004-09-13 01:28:56 UTC
Hi, all -
I've posted a new mystery box of sorts in Ottawa Co, Michigan. It's
going to be my last Michigan box for a while. I've gotten a job in
Maine, and am moving in a week.

I wanted to invite anyone who's interested, and has time, to come
find the box on Wednesday (9/15) and to stop in to
the "Establishment" mentioned in the clues for some live
entertainment and to exchange stamps. The activity usually gets
going around 7 pm and goes till 10 pm or so. They've got food (not a
huge menu), refreshments and free, live music. As an added
incentive, I'm going to try to carve a special hitchhiker for my move
to Maine. I can't promise I'll have it done (with packing and all),
but if I do I'll have it with me and will give it to the first person
who asks me for it. How will you know me, you ask? I should have
an instrument to my mouth. Look for one of the younger musicians
playing one of the smallest instruments sitting next to my mom
(fellow boxer Sisu Sue - she'll have her stamp too). We both wear
glasses, and would love to meet any fellow boxers who might be able
to come out before I head out to Maine.

I've had lots of fun searching for everyone's boxes, and hope that my
trips back to Michigan to visit family include visits to everyone's
wonderful letterboxes.

Peace,
Keltic Kara
