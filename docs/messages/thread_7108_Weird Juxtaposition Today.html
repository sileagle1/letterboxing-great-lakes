Weird Juxtaposition Today

From: sunnysideupm (sunnysideupm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-05-16 00:17:19 UTC
So, Mr. Peabody noticed in the logbook that there were some LBers finding the AWESOME Memorial Day LBs before us today. A little later on, we see some likely people, so Fossil Fighter (DS) asks them, "Which way is north?" They point north, but we ask out right if they're LBers - even I can pick up on the signs - clue in hand, compass string around neck, backpack. They are, and when I introduce myself, the woman is so excited and says her goal is to find my boxes. Warm fuzzy moment - and my teenager is impressed and happy for me.

So, as we are following them we start to notice a disturbing pattern of open baggies and uncovered boxes. Buzz killer. And a little fear for my LBs.

So that's my weird juxtaposition today.

I kindly messaged them through LBNA about resealing, rehiding - and told them the story of this stranger (Nam Vet!) startling us at Cave Point by asking us which way north was. We answered - and he demonstrated the RIGHT answer.

- Sunny
