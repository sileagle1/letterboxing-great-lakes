Illinois in CT

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-07-09 04:29:17 UTC
We were in Connecticut over the holiday weekend hiking and hunting for many of the America the Beautiful boxes. This is a new series of 51 boxes (50 states plus Washington DC). Each box is on a hike of at least 1 mile, and we estimate we did 50+ miles over 4 days (and we did NOT find all of the boxes).

Most of the state boxes were created by someone from that state, so we were surprised to see that Lock, Shock & Barrel had done the New Jersey box. However we WEREN'T surprised to see that CherokeeRose2 created the IL box, since she has her own interesting state box series. Since we had brought several hitchhikers out with us, we decided that we'd leave one that had been created by Espresso Beans in the IL box. An IL HH in an IL box in CT. Well it turned out to be even funnier than we thought it would be - the HH was the "White Sox" HH and it turned out that the image CherokeeRose2 had chosen for the IL box was a "Wrigley Field - Home of the Cubs" sign. An interesting team match-up way out in Connecticut!

Wisconsin Hiker
