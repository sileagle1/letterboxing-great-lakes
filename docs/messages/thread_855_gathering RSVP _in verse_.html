If you think you see me there, it will be a mirage,
Since Saturday night I've got tickets for Barrage!

Wish I could be there, it sounds really great,
Alas the show's far from home, so I'll be out quite late.

At least my PLB will act as my proxy.
I hope you aren't insulted by my moxie. (guess I'd better quit while I
can....)
