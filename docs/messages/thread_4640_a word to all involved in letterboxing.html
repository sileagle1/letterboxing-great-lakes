From: gemini6557 (gemini6165@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-30 21:55:18 UTC
As my first year of boxing comes to a close, I'd like to take the time
to thank all of the people involved in making this hobby so wonderful.
I have found my hobby at last!! To all you great folks who carve the
outstanding stamps, put the boxes together and carefully hide them,
then post the great clues - THANK YOU!!!!! You've made this an
outstanding spring, summer and fall for me. To all those who've
answered my tons of questions- I thank you for all your help..
I am anxiously watching for the warm weather , gathering my clues and
mapping out where to go first- another awesome spring, summer and fall
will find me in the wilderness- all thanks to you wonderful people..
THANKS FOR A GREAT SEASON!!! LOOKING FORWARD TO 2008...see you on the
trails!!!!gemgirl65
