When I get to cooking,
I don't let my food hide.
I fill it with spices,
To make eyes pull wide.

But when I cook meat,
For example a roast,
I love the needle leaves,
Of this herb the most.

No matter chicken or beef,
Or even sometime tea,
This evergreen is also known,
As Dew of the Sea.

O- Rosmarinus
