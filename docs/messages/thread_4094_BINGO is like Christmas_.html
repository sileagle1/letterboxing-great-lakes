Wowee!
It's like Christmas and birthday all rolled into one.
Today I received the BINGO prize box from SpringChick (courtesy of the
many BINGO players on the Great Lakes regional list).

Without listing all of the great surprises, let me just say that with
new Lock `n Lock containers, carving material, carving tools and lots
and lots of neat stuff to make letterboxing even more fun, I'm ready
to make some new challenges here in W. Michigan.

The creative and fun things that were contributed by all who watched
the list for the BINGO words each day through the winter are just amazing.

There hardly seems any excuse for there not to be some new azobox
plants pretty soon.
(Although no one included that little slice of *time* that we all
cherish).

So, once again, Thank you to SpringChick who organizes the annual
Letterbox BINGO game that carries us through those short, dark days of
January, February and into March.

And thanks to everyone who sent such cool stuff to be shared among the
several winners.
