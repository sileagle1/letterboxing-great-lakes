From: lesann847 (Acorn@fools-errand.org) | Date: 2006-03-24 23:20:02 UTC
I asked my sister about the Kickapoo River because she spends lots of time there. She
said she's never seen rafting, but there are plenty of canoe and inner tube outfitters.
She said that Titanic Outfitters have relatively new (dependable) equipment.

She also mentioned that last year water levels were VERY low, so conditions were not
optimum for either the canoes or the inner tubes.

Maybe this year will be better.
