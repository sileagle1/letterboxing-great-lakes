Schoolhouse "Cabin Fever" Reliever, 4/14/2007, 11:00 pm

From: (LbGLK@yahoogroups.com) | Date: 2007-04-08 02:56:25 UTC
Reminder from:   LbGLK Yahoo! Group
 
Title:   Schoolhouse "Cabin Fever" Reliever
 
Date:   Saturday April 14, 2007
Time:   11:00 pm - 4:00 am
Next reminder:   The next reminder for this event will be sent in 6 days, 4 minutes.
Location:   http://www.fayettecoagsociety.com/facilities.html
Street:   213 Fairview Ave
City State Zip:   Washington Court House, OH
Notes:   This event will be held in Washington Court House, Ohio, in the Fayette County Fairgrounds Old Schoolhouse from 11 A.M. until 4 P.M. Well have Banquet Tables set up to Meet, Eat and Stamp. Cold cut sandwiches and condiments will be provided along with coffee, hot chocolate, cold drinks. If youd like to bring a dish, that would be greatly appreciated.

Trailtracker is bringing Postcards for the 82nd Airborne". Granny Butterfly is bringing "Honeymoon in Iraq", and I will be bringing a "large" postcard that everyone can stamp and write messages on for my daughter's unit the 22nd Fighter squadron stationed in Spangdahlem, Germany. Feel free to bring your pics and stories of family members and friends serving in the U.S. Armed Forces. There will be one table set up especially for our Patriots.

Plan on having a great time, getting all those PTs, and meeting some great people. Also, anyone attending the 2007 Great Lakes Gathering: Columbus Cluez-in event on June 2nd, 2007 may want to take this opportunity to attend this event and get many stamps that will be there including Mn8xs. Mn8x is hosting the Cluez, and it is definitely going to be a "BIG" one! So, lets all support her any way we can.

UPDATE:

1) To help with the cost of the Cluez-in in June, there will be Donation jars available.

2) 3Buckeyekids will be here if anyone has any ideas for LB shirts

3) Speedsquare will give demo's on bookmaking for anyone interested.

4) You may want to bring your own chair (fold up). The facility only has metal folding chairs only

If you have any suggestions, please let us know, and stay tuned for any updates.

Food list:

3Buckeyekids... dessert
Trailtracker... cheese-n-potato casserole, chips, Diet Mt. Dew and peanutbutter balls
Lindsay's snowman...Chocolate, chocolate Cake.. yum!
Granny Butterfly... pasta salad
Drexel girls..... pasta salad and a lemon poppy seed cake
