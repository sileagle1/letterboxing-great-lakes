Shutterfly---
I checked out a couple web sites on brown recluse spider bites too. I can't get those images out of my head. I had no idea how horrific these bites can be. The doctor must try to get all the venom out or it spreads, and it spreads quickly in fatty tissue areas especially the butt. Not to say that FW has a fat butt, it's just that area tends to have more fleshiness. The tissue in the area dies and those sores open up and look so bloody and sometimes the doctor needs to do skin grafts.
