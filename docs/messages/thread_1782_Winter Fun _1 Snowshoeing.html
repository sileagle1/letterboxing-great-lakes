Thanks! (We actually have replaced whole boxes that have been damaged

stay for a bit longer and some will be permanent. Since you live nearby,
you should be able to find them all within a week or two before some of
them are removed.

I've got an "Attendees" page on the website, so if your OCT 1 schedule
allows, just let me know how you'd like to be listed and the sex/age of any
kids (some families like knowing other kids will be there, so I'm listing
the kids in more detail).
