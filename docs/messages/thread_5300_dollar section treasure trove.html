From: Kathi (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2008-09-19 03:51:20 UTC
Target's dollar section has some of those nice little albums
again...some of you may recall the kind I've used for individual event
logbooks instead of my usual daytimer style. They have eight pages of
cardstock (I'd guess about 90 lb stock) with those screw-post
bindings. I've been able to double that by adding my own pages. They
have stamp pads and other scrapbooking supplies there too, including
the cutest little alphabet stamp set (wood mounted nonetheless!)...all
for a buck apiece!

Now if we could only get them to stock some decent plastic containers
in that part of the store....
