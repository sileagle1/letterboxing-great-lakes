My wife and I just got back from our vacation to Michigan and thought
we would fill you all in on the boxes we went after. From the
Detroit area up to Eastern UP.

Detroit Area:
Pax
Ars Gratia Artis - neat artwork
Round and Round
Cheif Among the Trillium
Desired is under the Elms

Alpena Area:
Blackberry
Water Lily
Lady Slipper
Besser Elementary Nature Trail

Indian River/Topinabee area:
Indian River Railroad Bridge - Artdog I loved the design
Do the Waves
Fishing Mullet Lake #2 & 3 - #1 and #4 not found
(nice designs, FYI walleye stamp was in two pieces)

Eastern Upper Penninsula:
MI CA-T (south of bridge)
Top O' the Pickle
U.P. Waterfalls Series -- Miners Falls (MISSING)
The Pelican (could not find starting place from clues)
Valley Spur
Merry Christmas
Ol' Well
Daughter of the Moon - Incredible design thank you Spring Chick
Song of Hiawatha - loved the design
Halcyon Days - removed geo-stuff left by mistake.
Hiawatha's Portal

Bypassed many others due to time contraints. We also moved some
hitchhikers around, did a couple of exchanges, and talked my parents
into placing their first box, "BIG KNOB" near Engadine.

Thank you so much to all the people who placed these boxes, providing
us many hours of fun.

Lee & Nancy
Apple Valley, CA
