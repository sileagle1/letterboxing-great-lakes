October Twin Cities Gathering

From: Heather (the_red_cat529@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-01 22:11:33 UTC
The location for the Something Wicked This Way Comes gathering has
been set!

It'll be at Como Park in St. Paul on Saturday, October 20, 2007.

Since it'll be after the "picnic season", the shelters are available
on a first come, first served basis. I'm not positive that the
electricity will be on in the shelter. I'm going to try to grab the
Como Shelter on Midway Parkway near Hamline Avenue. Just look for
the girl wearing the hat with cat ears. There might also be
balloons. I'll be there early to snag the shelter and hide last
minute boxes.

The time has also changed. It'll be from about 12/12:30ish until
whenever. Since (hopefully) it'll take up a large portion of the
afternoon, it would be great if people would be willing to bring
snacks/something to munch on.

If you are attending, feel free to hide your own Halloween or Autumn
themed letterboxes in the park. You can either hand out the clues
yourself or email them to me to include in the clue booklet. There
are also going to be boxes donated by several letterboxers from
around the country that will only be available at this event.

If you plan on attending or have any questions, please email me at
the_red_cat529@yahoo.com. There's also an event page at AtlasQuest.

-The Red Cat
