It is always hard to find that balance of chalanging but findable box
planting. My first gathering I had a total of 24 boxes. I thought this
was too little, but everyone only found 8 boxes that day. I guess I
wore them out with the clues I had. I was a little disappointed that
they didn't find more, but the weather was kinda crapy that day too.

The Creepy Crawl was a different format of a gathering but it seems
like the 8 boxes worked out. It took everyone about 3-4 hours to do,
and after being in the rain and cold I think most people were done with
box finding. (Funny how it always seems to rain at my events)
