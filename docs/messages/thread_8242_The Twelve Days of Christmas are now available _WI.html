The Twelve Days of Christmas are now available (WI)

From: (mlcigna@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-11-30 10:09:18 UTC-08:00

After a several year hiatus, the Twelve Days of Christmas are available again in the Milwaukee-metro area. 


This series is available annually December 1 through January 31. (We are so excited to have them out again this year that they are available one day early!) These twelve boxes have colorful stamps, so be sure to allow for 1-2 hours to allow for coloring and stamping in. Dress warm and/or make use of your car when stamping in. Enjoy!
