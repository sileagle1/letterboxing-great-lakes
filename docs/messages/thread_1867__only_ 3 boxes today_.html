I said this would happen! After attending the Wisconsin Gathering and
finding so many boxes, placed close together, I thought it would be
difficult to go back to our regular Wisconsin boxing. This is the
conversation I overheard from Blue Thunder and Hyacinth as they were
getting ready for bed tonight after returning from an afternoon at
Devil's Lake.

Blue Thunder says: "What a nice hiking day!"

Hyacinth says: "yeah, but we didn't do much. We only found 3 boxes!"

At that point I had to chuckle. We just hiked both the East and the
West Bluff and Hyacinth thought we didn't do much? Maybe we have these
kids more conditioned than we thought. Maybe we are ready for that 9
mile Norwegian Woods series!
