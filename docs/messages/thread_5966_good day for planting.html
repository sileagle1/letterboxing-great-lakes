good day for planting

From: Katrina Lustofin (drkaty@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-07-13 22:42:20 UTC-04:00
I set out today to plant the second half of a series I've been working on.
It was a beautiful day to be out, here in SE Ohio - not too terribly hot or
humid, with a bit of a breeze. The dogs enjoyed the fact that the creeks
are not yet dry, even if the red clay turns Yuki an interesting shade of
pink. One of the trails I planned to plant along is one I hadn't visited in
a while and I was pleasantly surprised to realize it cuts right through a
gorgeous blackberry patch, just as the season is turning from raspberry to
blackberry. This made me wonder - am I the only one who forages while
letterboxing?

Katy
whose fingers are stained not with ink but juice!
