Ft. Custer, Battle Creek, MI is burning.

From: cerealcityboxers (cerealcityboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-05 14:39:48 UTC
The last two spring seasons the park staff at Ft. Custer have done
controlled burns in some areas of this park, burning all undergrowth.
I know of at least two boxes that have been turned into ashes. Any
box hidden on the ground covered by dead twigs and leaves is
vulnerable. These piles of wood seem to be favorite targets for the
fire shooting rangers as they traipse all over the park.

Make sure when you are planting boxes in a park that does controlled
burns that they are off the ground, in a stump, under a rock, or
otherwise protected from the fire happy rangers.
