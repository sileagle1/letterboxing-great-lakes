For those going to the faire or for those who are just curious, I've
scanned in and uploaded the current pages of the Spellbook of
Incantrix which is a letterbook at ren faire. You can find it in the
"Files" section of this site.

Finders are not only to stamp into the logbook but should also write
another page to the continuing story.

Those who have found the book in the past can see where the story has
headed since they stamped in. Those seeking the book for the first
time can read ahead of time so they know what to expect.

I scanned all the pages in, so do not feel bad if you didn't
contribute to the story line. We all know that circumstances don't
always allow us to stamp in as we might've liked to. There is no rule
saying one cannot find the book again and add to the story again.
(Only count one find though...)

Enjoy!
