I also am an NIU alumni having done my graduate work and graduating
from there four years ago. My grief has been compounded however as two
of the victims, Gayle Dubowski and Samantha Dehner were my students
when they were in 8th grade. Gayle was killed in the mayhem but
gratefully, Samantha survived.
Gayle was a quiet girl but had a beautiful and gentle spirit about her,
I was priviledged to not only teach her Biology but also directed her
in the school choir. She loved God with all her heart and I find solace
in knowing that she is singing on streets of gold with the One she
loved so very much. What a senseless shame.
