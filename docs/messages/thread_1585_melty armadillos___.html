P.S. We don't leave our boxing stuff in the car in summer or winter, mostly
because of the ink & markers. Starts freezing in winter and we've found
that the markers ooze ink even w/o pressure if they get too warm. We
usually take the pack in to the office with us if we're planning an "after
work" trip.
