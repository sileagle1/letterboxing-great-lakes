I'd be glad to bring Are We There Yet up to MI for the
gathering. I believe that Team Rabbit was looking for
some traveling event boxes...so there ya go, eh?

I actually have found better a better sugar cream pie
than Wicks (I know, this can't be true!). If ever
traveling down US 31 in between Plymouth and
Rochester, IN (Flagbearer is on a planting frenzy, so
you *might* end up this way searching for some of
hers)there's a diner on the west side of US 31 (the 4
lane "bypass" as the locals call it) near the town of
Argos called the Logcabin. Fabulous food, and probably
the best sugar cream pie ever! Amazing! You will
rethink the whole Wicks thing...trust me, I did!!!

A reminder to everyone: There will be a small
gathering in Plymouth IN on May 21st hosted by The
Sisters Act. For more information:
http://www.geocities.com/jes7o/plymouth

--Armadillo Jo
