From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-09-18 23:35:34 UTC-05:00
Yep, same park as 2006. Shorty may be referring to the fact that there are
a lot of older boxes already in the park and if people want to find the
clues they should be sure to check BOTH LBNA and AQ. You can do this simply
by checking the web sites and look for boxes in the Delafield, WI area.
However if you have a Smartphone you could also use Box Radar (free Android
app developed by Maine letterboxer aiphid) which automatically checks both
sites or Clue Tracker ($4.95 iphone app developed by Michigan boxers
MichKathy & TrailMark). Side note: the Box Radar app was extremely useful
to us on our vacation the last 2 weeks. We'll be sending aiphid a
"donation" soon.



And here is another tip for readers of this message board - be sure to check
out the numerous "Eggopolis" clues. You'll need to solve codes for some of
them, but you have a few days to get to work!
