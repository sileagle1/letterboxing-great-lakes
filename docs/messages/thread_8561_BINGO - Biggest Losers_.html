BINGO - Biggest Losers!

From: (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2017-05-04 04:31:26 UTC

According to my calculation, we have a 3-way tie for biggest loser! There are actually 4 people with 8 words not called on their card, but one of those had the wildcard, so technically they only needed 7 more words. The other 3 did not have the wildcard, so truly needed 8 more words... these 3 people have honors of being the 2017 Great Lakes Bingo Biggest Losers!

Biggest Losers (and the words they were waiting on):

PartyOf6Plus1 - First Finder Prize, Dartmoor, Logbook, Clue, Hartx6, Atlasquest, LB Event, Compass, and wildcard Lake Michigan
Angel & Demon - Clue, Dartmoor, Atlasquest, Hartx6, Caesar Cipher, Compass, Postal Letterboxing, WOM Clue, and wildcard Lake Erie
Pastor's Wife - First Finder Prize, Clue, Logbook, The Grove, Atlasquest, Hartx6, Compass, WOM Clue, and wildcard Lake Erie

Congratulations to all of you!

A few fun facts...

The top 3 words being waited on were:

Hartx6 - 29
Compass - 22
Logbook - 22

33 people did not have their wildcard called, 14 of them were waiting for Lake Michgan.

5 people were waiting on 1 more word (or their wildcard).

If anyone feels I've made an error (i.e. you still needed more than 8 words), please email me.

I will post a recap list of all winners within a day or two and will be contacting all winners for addresses. Prizes will be mailed soon!

Thanks for playing everyone!

SpringChick
