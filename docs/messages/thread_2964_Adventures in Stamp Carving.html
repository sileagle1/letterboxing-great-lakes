Well guys, after my second attempt at stamp carving last night, my hat is off to you all. It sure isn't as easy as I thought it would be. My friend Dottie and I are humbled but not discouraged; we look at our failed stamp attempts as learning experiences. The eagle image we tried last night was, in hindsight, too complicated. I guess we'll stick to simpler stuff until we have more experience and confidence.

Does anyone know where I can get a #5 blade for a Speedball handle? I have blades #1, #2, and #3 but I would really like a nice wide #5 for finishing off the edges. I'd rather not buy a complete set if all I need is one blade.

Thanks,
