OCT 1 Haunted Harvest in WI

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-07-12 04:46:01 UTC
I'm pleased to announce that I have been contacted by the renowned
ringmaster Felka Lechsinska. She is a comrade of Phineas Boch and
they have agreed that she will bring a select group of Boch's
Traveling Circus performers to the wilds of Wisconsin for a short
stay. Phineas was notified of the October 1st Harvest Gathering and
he agreed that it would be an appropriate venue with a worthy
audience. (I'm not certain if they know it may be a
somewhat "haunted" gathering, but I'm sure they are brave & strong
performers...)

In this excursion to the Midwest, Felka has informed me that they
will be requiring an admission price - the ability to solve some
clues! Yes, they will be appearing in secluded stages that you will
need to find during your visit to the gathering. As a special
kindness, the troupe has agreed to stay in the area for a few weeks.
This will allow us more time to savor their performances.

I'll forward any additional information I receive from Felka as we
prepare for the gathering. www.hauntedharvest.homestead.com
