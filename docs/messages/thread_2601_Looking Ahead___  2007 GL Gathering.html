Looking Ahead... 2007 GL Gathering

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-06-05 18:29:47 UTC
With this year's event behind us, but still fresh in our minds, it
is time to look ahead to next year and determine a location for the
5th Annual Great Lakes Gathering.

Mark you calendars for Saturday, June 2, 2007.

As for location... OH, IN and MN are all states within the region
that have not yet hosted the event. If there is anyone from one of
these states who would be interested in hosting the gathering on
6/2/07, please speak up -- either post to the list or e-mail me
privately.

I will leave it open until the end of June and at that time we will
vote between the locations that have offered to host. If nobody
from any of the above-mentioned states offers to host, then it will
be opened up to IL, MI and WI.

If you are wondering what is involved in hosting a gathering, there
is a file in the FILES section of the list (in the gatherings
folder) that gives an overview. Also feel free to e-mail myself or
any of the previous gathering hosts with specific questions.
