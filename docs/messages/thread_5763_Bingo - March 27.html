No one is accusing Tonto because we know he's just holding back. He knows this is an addiction and he has a stronger will than the rest of us. When I first started boxing and my husband would tag along. I had a stamp and I told him he needed a stamp too. I even gave him a trail name - Detector. He enjoys metal detecting for jewelry and coins; not inky stamps. And I found a commercially made stamp for him. It was a perfect stamp. I actually carved a Beetle's Family stamp. If any letterboxer ever met both of my family members on the trail, at a gathering, (most likely while dropping me off in the parking lot or picking me up later,) they would get The Beetle's Family stamp. A rare meet and exchange indeed.



One thing I have learned....you either love letterboxing, or you just smile and roll your eyes.
