From: SpringChick (springchick1219@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-02-11 02:33:53 UTC

There are still a few days left to sign up for the group letterbox
exchange project. I am hoping that I didn't miss any e-mails from
folks wanting to participate while my e-mail was under the weather.
If you have already signed up you should have received an e-mail
back from me confirming that I received your message. If you did
not receive an e-mail from me confirming then I did not get your
message about participating in the exchange, in which case I would
ask that you please resend to me at springchick1219 "at" comcast.net.

If you haven't been reading lately and are wondering what this is
about -- we are doing a group letterbox exchange where each
participant makes a letterbox and sends it to another assigned
participant. The theme of the exchange is "Hometown Landmarks."
All the information and details on how the swap works can be found
in the FILES section in the SERIES AND GROUP PROJECTS folder.

Hope you will join us!

SpringChick
