Bingo Booty has arrived!

From: grandvilla02 (grandvilla02@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-07-31 20:20:26 UTC
Twas a hot July 30 and far from my mind
Were letterboxing travels and boxes to find.

Christmas in July was big in the stores
But this is the time for campfires and s'mores!

So what should appear to my wondering eyes
But a box in the garage! Oh joy - a surprise!

A bingo card perched on the top (not behind)
Gave a clue as to what inside I would find.

More rapid than eagles I ran in the house
A mouse WOULD be stirring if I *had* a mouse!

Thank you dear Spring Chick for all that you do.
Now I can enjoy Bingo all the year through!

My "poem" is finished. To list the prizes in rhyme
Would take way too long and we don't have the time!

My wonderful box of booty included: various plastic boxes, master carve, deer fly patches, a very bright flashlight, backpack, log book, camo duct tape, first aid kit, cap lights, small alphabet rubber stamps, index cards, zip lock baggies, and.tah dah SOCKS! Yay! Thanks to everyone who sent in all these wonderful prizes and I again thank Spring Chick for all her hard work!
