I'm assuming that all Target stores have their dollar
section when you first walk into the store, since the
three Targets I live by all have them. Anyway, was in
there last night, and they had some "outdoorsy" type
items that letterboxers might find useful for their
adventures - such as mini first aid kits (for specific
ailments, such as bee & wasp stings, another was for
sunburns, etc), plastic canteens (kids might find
those fun to carry around while traipsing through the
woods), and hand warmers (you know, for when it gets
cold again as it's bound to around these parts).
