One More Week!

From: Heather (the_red_cat529@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-24 21:51:30 UTC
One more week until the Great Lakes Gathering!

I was out at the park today hiding more boxes and it was absolutely
beautiful! The lilacs and wildflowers are starting to bloom and
everything is finally starting to look green. If you like
bird-watching, you should definitely bring your gear. Birds were
constantly flitting across my path and occasionally stopping long
enough for me to get a good look at them. A pheasant even ran across
the trail!

Everything should be set up at the Visitors Center by 9:30 and we have
the center until 5:30. There's a horseshoe pit near the Visitors
Center, so if you have a set of horseshoes, feel free to bring them.

Lunch is potluck and burgers, brats and chicken will be provided
grilled. I'm not sure how many of those attending are vegetarian, so
if you would like a non-meat option, please bring it and we'll be
happy to grill it for you. If there is a side dish or desert that you
would like to bring, please let me know.

And I hope that you guys bring your adventurous appetites for one of
the stamps at the gathering! Mwhaaaa...

Looking forward to seeing everyone in a week!

-The Red Cat
