Special WOM box for the Cleveland area this Saturday only

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-26 12:34:06 UTC-07:00
After almost a year of marriage and home remodeling
and sorting through stuff going, "We really don't need
two of these," and "Do you REALLY want to keep THAT?"
we have enough stuff to stock our own discount super
store. So it's garage sale time this Saturday! Since
this will keep us from letterboxing, borrowing from
The Dragon's original idea a few years ago, we're
going to have a letterbox at our garage sale instead.


We created the Marriage is a Wonderland box for the
2006 Great Lakes Gathering, but haven't had a chance
to plant it locally yet (and don't know when we
will....). So, if you can figure out where we live,
stop by on Saturday for a chance to get this box (no
purchase necessary ;). And bring your markers for the
stamp. Sale hours will be from 9 am to 3 pm.

The clues to find our house are:

Drew Carey hailed from this state, and hey, so do we!

As the Beastie Boys sang, theres No Sleep Till this
city.

In 1692, a massacre happened here. Not _here_, but I
sure hope our street wasnt named after it!

Stay on the east side of the Ridge and youre sure
to see the signs. Look for the doodle and deedle
bug to know youre at the right site.

To get the box, say something to one of the
proprietors about their first year anniversary being
June 10th. Remember that we also have a personal
traveler you can get if you tell us how we originally
met.

Doodle Bug.
