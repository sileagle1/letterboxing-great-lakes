We spent Friday afternoon going after this "local" box... we had saved
it so that when B's parents visited from South Carolina, they could see
what this "letterboxing madness" that has overcome their son and
daughter-in-law was all about! So, despite some late afternoon
thunderstorms, we set out for a lovely (if slightly damp and buggy)
walk in the Arb. The clues were wonderful and really gave us a tour of
the park. And best of all, they offered an alternative way back once we
had found the box. We really appreciated this, as B's parents (and
maybe B, too!) were not looking forward to climbing all those stairs
back out of the park! The stamp and walk were great, and B's parents
were suitably impressed. Even if you are a local or townie and know the
Arb pretty well, I highly recommend this box. :-)

Happily,
