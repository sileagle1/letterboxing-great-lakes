BINGO MARCH 25: Top of the Heap

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-03-25 12:01:08 UTC
How do I love thee?
Let me count the ways.
I love thee in all grade,
For the delicate work made.
I love thee for your feel,
Making carving come real.
I love thee for your transfer,
By the heat which I prefer.
Weather you be A, B or citrus,
I'm glad I got to be a witness.
You may be gone, but not from my breath,
I love thee better now, after your death.

I - PZ Kut
