Just to turn things around and look at things from a glass-half-full
perspective, who thinks they hold the record for most days in a row
GETTING the words? I started off the game early with 3 words in a row
on Jan 8, 9, & 10 and was encouraged that the game was going to be great
for me this year... but then things quickly died off, and I'm now
holding steady with just 10 words total, with only 2 of those in the X.

So does anyone think they hold the record for the most days in a row
with the word? I think 3 in a row is pretty good, but I bet someone can
do better -- come on, fess up!
