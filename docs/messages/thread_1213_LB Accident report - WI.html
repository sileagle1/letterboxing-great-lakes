LB Accident report - WI

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-04-25 03:03:53 UTC

I just wanted to let people know that someone in our area sustained
an injury in February. She's a friend of ours in our local hiking
club and has only gone out letterboxing a few times with her 20-
something year old daughter. I don't think she even has a stamp
(yet).

I just found out recently that she broke her ankle letterboxing with
her daughter on Feb 12. She slipped on some mud in Doctor's Park.
She had a plate, four screws, and one pin inserted in her ankle. She
is now off the crutches but needs to wear a short boot.

I told her that I hoped she hadn't completely given up on
letterboxing after this incident and she replied with this email:

"Right before I broke my ankle, my daughter found the letterbox. She
wanted to drive me to the hospital right away but I made her complete
all the stamping in the letterbox before we left. She still
letterboxes and I'd join her again to find another one. She won't
forget what happened to get that one stamp!"

A real trooper!

Wisconsin Hiker
