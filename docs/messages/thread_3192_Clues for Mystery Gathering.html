From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-18 10:21:12 UTC
There's a map to the Cereal City Circus Gathering
site.
To find it, consult the clues located below
The map's divided into three parts so you'll need to
find all of the stamps to complete your directions.

The map puzzle pieces (stamps) are located along the
Battle Creek Linear Park/Trail.
Note: there are no logbooks with the stamps, so just
stamp the puzzle piece into your logbook and complete the
map.

Since these stamps are located in urban areas, be especially mindful
of who might be watching you. Stealth is the byword.

A good start begins with a good breakfast so begin at
Kellogg's Cereal City, downtown Battle Creek.
Driving Directions:

Take I-94 between Detroit and Chicago
Get off at exit 98B
Now you will be on M66/I-194
This will take you to downtown Battle Creek
Turn left onto Michigan Ave.
Go down Michigan Ave. about four or five longish
blocks, past McCamley. You'll cross the river.
Kellogg's Cereal City USA is the red brick building
located on the left side of the street just across the
river.
Turn left into the parking area.


These boxes (baggies) do not have to be done in any
particular order.
The map pieces will not be in place until Friday
evening, late.


Consult website:
http://www.kelloggscerealcity.com/directions/
