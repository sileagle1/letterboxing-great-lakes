Speedball gouges

From: (stampinpam@aol.com) | Date: 2006-07-04 14:30:27 UTC-04:00
FYI, I was at my local Michael's store yesterday. Noticed there weren't any
individual pkgs. of Speedball gouges in the art section, where I'd found them
previously. Checked out the clearance aisle, and there they were. This
particular store only had pkgs. of No. 2 and No. 4 gouges, and they were marked at
50 cents (normally $2.99). They also had some Speedball Scratch Knives, two
different shapes in a pkg., also marked down to 50 cents (normally $3.99).
Figured these might come in handy for cross-hatch patterns and light texturing.

Thought I'd share the info in case any one gets a chance to check out their
local store.
