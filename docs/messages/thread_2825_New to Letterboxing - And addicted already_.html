New to Letterboxing - And addicted already!

From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-07-26 14:34:11 UTC
Hello!
A few local folks recommended I join this group, and I sit here
already recognizing several names from our finds! (Isnt it funny how
you 'know' people you have never met?)

We already had "Beetle Day" several days ago, and our first find
was "Decaffeinated".

We have been out the past 4 days, and will likely head out for one
local one this afternoon... I can't resist!

Im happy to find a place where everyone can talk, and share
information - I letterbox with my family (My husband and 2 year old
son) and Im really hoping to run across some others of 'our kind'
out on the local trails!

We are getting ready for our first plant soon, and are
just 'scouting' for the right location! So glad to be here - and
can't wait to 'meet' some of the people we have already, well... met
in a way while we were stamping!

Paula
(CinemaBoxers)
