A Little Maintenance...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-26 10:38:15 UTC
Those of you who read messages on the internet may have noticed that
I have replaced the photo on the group home page with the new
Letterboxing Great Lakes stamp image, which now includes MN. Thanks
to those who did contribute photos for the home page, but there
wasn't much interest or participation, so I am planning to go back
to the GL stamp image and leave that there.

I also did a little maintenance in the photos section. If you don't
see your photo album, don't worry your photos are still there. I
renamed a couple of albums from gatherings so that they had the date
in the album name, thus keeping them all together and
chronological. I also consolidated a couple of albums containing
photos of the same gathering into a single album (Spring Fling
photos). I did delete a couple of albums, but only empty ones. If
one of these was yours and you were just leaving it there for future
photos -- sorry, you can just recreate it when you are ready to
upload the pics.

Thanks!

SpringChick
