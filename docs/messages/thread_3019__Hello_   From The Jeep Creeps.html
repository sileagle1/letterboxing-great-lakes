From: thejeepcreeps (thejeepcreeps@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-29 03:28:00 UTC
Hello Friends,

Our family is new to Letterboxing.
We also read about this awesome hobby in Family Fun Magazine.
Believe it or not, it was just recently published in the Sept 2006
issue. I see by reading the threads on the board, that it has been
in a few family magazines.
A few years ago, I went on a few Geocaching adventures with my
brother in law. It was great.
Then I read about Letterboxing and thought .. WOW... this sounds
great for us as a family...
3 kids, ages 10, 7 and 4 years old. We bought a notebook and a
stamp for each kid. The notebooks as journals is an awesome way to
have the kids work on their reading and writing skills.. something
they all need to keep sharp on.
My Fiance, Rob is currently working on making our own "The Jeep
Creep" stamp. I can't wait to see how it turns out...
We have found 3 Letterboxes so far. One of them had an original
carving stamp.. Rob and I thought that was so cool. We get at real
kick out of reading all the journal entries left in the letterboxes
we have found.
We look forward to reading and writing here in the letterbox group.
Hopefully we will get to attend some events too.
Peace,
Bren, Rob & Kids
"The Jeep Creeps"
