Finally... F100

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-04-10 01:44:24 UTC
I spent the day down in Kalamazoo with azobox and finally hit F100
(with his much appreciated shortcuts on a couple of boxes). The new
Celery Flats box, for which the clues were just posted, was box
number 100 and now sports both the "old" SpringChick stamp and the
very first public impression of the "new" SpringChick stamp.

I know F100 was lights years ago for some of you, but it is
challenging finding 100 boxes when everything around you was planted
by you. Speaking of which, today for just a few brief minutes, the
numbers were actually F100 P100. I have been sitting at P100 since
almost a year ago, waiting for the F count to catch up.

THANKS to everyone who has planted boxes in this region (also some
were from the Mid-Atlantic area). And a huge thanks to azobox for
his patience today as I fumbled through the clues and pressed on for
yet "a couple more boxes."

The day ended at F102 P101. And now... I must go plant more boxes.

SpringChick
