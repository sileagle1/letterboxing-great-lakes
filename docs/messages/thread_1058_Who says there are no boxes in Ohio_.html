Who says there are no boxes in Ohio?

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-05 08:17:53 UTC-08:00
Oh wait, that might have been me. As I'm learning,
there are a plenty as soon as you go South of
Columbus. Deedle & I decided that we wanted to do a
3-day getaway weekend sometime in April. Which of
course had to include some letterboxing. So I got out
the calendar, saw which weekends we were free, and
started searching to see what was going on in various
areas of Ohio that weekend, since letterboxing was
supposed to be the secondary activity. I decided that
we should attend the Dogwood Festival in Piketon, I'm
a sucker for town festivals like that. And we'd be
passing by lots of letterboxes there & back.

So I started to compile a list of letterboxes we would
be near to find when we're done in Piketon (which,
according to my friend whose mother was born there,
would be very, very soon after we got there). 106
boxes! And we've only got three days, and we were
going to spend _some_ of that time admiring dogwoods
in bloom and oh, maybe eating & sleeping a little bit.
I'm just floored, because when I came up with boxes
for us to find on our way to and around Adrian,
Michigan, last year, I think I only had 70-some.

Wow. We may need to take an extra day... :)

Doodle Bug.

Re: Who says there are no boxes in Ohio?

From: Brenda Tassava (bktassava@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-03-05 18:15:33 UTC

Just some helpful hints:
The Chillicothe area has a lot of boxes listed. The best areas to
hit with multiple boxes would be the Great Seal State Park and the
Grandview Cemetery. Don't be discouraged as several are missing, but
not listed as such on the website. Traveling a little further south,
stop by Scioto Trails State Park for another area with several
boxes. There are two really great stamps at a Rest Area, just south
of Piketon. And if you go a little further (to Portsmouth), you
should find several nice ones there. I have a plant in Portsmouth
called "The Flood of 1998", so let me know if you find it:)
Artdog
