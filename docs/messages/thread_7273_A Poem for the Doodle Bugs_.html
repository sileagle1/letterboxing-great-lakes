Dear Doodle,

Two words a week is plenty enough....
but it appears that this week is nothing but fluff.
There's still Friday and Saturday, my dear Doodle....
so the chance is still there for you, so don't strain your noodle.
Think of poor Bali, who's less than half way...
from the 10 spot game, and yet, she will play.

you see, her card isn't so crappy.
But....can we believe her? Is it a trick?
Will her bingo magic actually stick?
And what about that Wisconsin Hiker?
Watch out for her skills. She's kind of a spiker!
Plus, we haven't heard much from Tiptoe and Tonto!
We should contact them..like pronto!
Yet, I wonder about Atom 118...
maybe his card is also real mean?
And so many others, like Trail Mark and MichKathy
and yet, we don't hear them being so catty.
So, the moral of this tale, my dear boxing bug...
is to hang tough...you'll get your hug!
Even though your chances seem thin....
There is ALWAYS a very good chance for a win!
