From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-05-11 15:21:25 UTC
The white Speedball stuff isn't good for much. It crumbles when
carving and even if you use it for less detailed stamps, it doesn't
hold up well -- the stamps tend to get dry and cracky.

You should be able to return any unopened material.

SpringChick

Re: HELP!! My new Speedball pink stuff!!!

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-11 17:15:50 UTC
Is the naked nymph checking for ticks?

Anyway, NEVER get the white stuff. Take back whatever you haven't
used. Use the stuff you have used for negative image stamps, or to
have a child practice carving. Remember, if you make a mistake on one
side, you can still begin again on the back side and put a backing on
the mistake side to cover your mistake - as long as you haven't cut
too deeply!

Dragon
