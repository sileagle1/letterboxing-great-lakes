Not sure if this is the right board or not , but I have a report from
Pigeon Creek in West Olive:
I was up there a few days ago , and oh my! They are logging- in a big way!
Most of the trails = especially the horse side and the side by the campground
are closed due to the logging operation, but from what I could see,
ALOT is changing up there..
If you have boxes in those areas, you may want to keep an eye on
when they'll let you back in, so you can [hopefully] locate your box
and/ or update
your clues if you need too.
I was very unhappy with the closures, as this was to be my first trip
there of the season,
couldn't hunt the boxes I was hoping to find!!
Hope this update will help someone out, gemgirl65

Ok --- I just returned from Pigeon Creek...
From what I can tell the lodge side of the park is not
being worked on... I'll say yet --- as I didn't go
through the whole park.
I walked all around the campground and there was no
logging evidence there at all.

The Horse trail side is a different matter all
together. That side has been completely compromised.
: ((
There are giant sweeps of trees cut down and trails
that were heavily wooded are now open fields. Some of
the trails are being re-defined, but we saw some trail
map and trail marker posts lying on the ground.

I will be sending emails to the folks who's boxes we
checked on...
I managed to find one of my boxes that was in the
middle of it --- but obviously I knew exactly where to
look. I didn't have the time to check on all of mine
in that side of the park... the Hitchhiker Hostel is
still there snug and sound.

We live about 20 minutes from here, so we'll be going
back again... probably next week Sunday or Monday.
If you have specific boxes you'd like checked on,
don't hesitate to let me know.

Mrs.Doglvrs
