A Good Friday For Boxing

From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-15 02:14:47 UTC
Really this starts on thursday after work we ran out to do the
Character Carousel placed by Lavender. After findind the first two
boxes we were running out of daylight and therefore were rushing
through the clues without a thorough reading and couldn't locate the
third box. We decided to come back in the morning with more time
and fresh brains.

So onto Friday (today), we returned to the scene of our great defeat
on Thursday night and in about five minutes found the third box
exactly where it was supposed to be, which was nice. In very short
order we then found box 4 and 5, although we did have to be very
careful to avoid drawing the attention of nearby fishermen. (Man
they get up early). On to a different part of the park for the
remaining 2 boxes. After enduring a quick shower we located the
last 2 boxes of the series which we will say is simply terrific, and
to fuel some discussion, starting a weekend with seven first finds
is fabulous! After a couple of must-do errands and some
lunch/breakfast, we were back on the road and unfortunately stuck in
traffic. We were able to find Cemetery Stroll #2, by the Waltzing
Pigs, but because our clue reading skills were left at home after
lunch, we missed Cemetery Stroll #3 due to time constraints. To be
honest we were almost locked in a Cemetery until SUNDAY! We then
went to Fish out of Water #4 Chicago Portage, by Fungaswoman, but
again due to our keen clue reading did not realize that this box was
recently pulled for protection and all we found was a FLAT MUMMIFIED
SQUIRREL which was innocently pulled from the tree under the
impression that it was a piece of bark...AAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHH,
EEEEEWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWWW!

The last two boxes of the day were mystery boxes so we won't say
much except to say that they brought us to F200 (WOO HOO) and we
picked up a hitchhiker to boot!!!!!!! All in all a pretty Good
Friday, even with the problems.

The Cottontails, who are still scrubbing the squirrel hand.

PS: Only 15 eggcelent boxing days left.
