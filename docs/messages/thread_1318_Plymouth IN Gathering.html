Plymouth IN Gathering

From: Flagbearer (the_sisters_act@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-14 04:43:14 UTC-07:00
One week from today!!! Joy in the Journey Letterbox
Gathering May 21st in Plymouth, IN, Centennial Park.
Full of Heart and I have been busy carving and
planting boxes in Marshall
and northern Fulton Counties.

I will have printed clues sheets for all of our boxes
as well as those of Armadillo Jo's and the Weaver
family. There will be a set of the clues for each
group that has said they are coming. Driving tips are
included on these clue pages to help you to find the
best routes to take and the boxes on those routes so
you can make the best use of your time. (We want you
to visist all our boxes!) These clues will be
available at the Neidlinger shelter at 9:00 am for
anyone who wishes to get an early start. If you would
like them earlier than that let me know and
arrangements can be made. There will be at least 45
boxes in the area, 14 of which will be available for
event day only.

When you arrive in Plymouth you will want to stop at
the park shelter and pick up your clue book and the a
bag of goodies for each person in your group.
(Another reason why we want to know how many to
expect.) Full of Hear and I have been busy bees
making these special for you. At this time we have 22
people who have said that they will be coming.

We would like everyone to meet back at the Neidlinger
shelter between 11:30 and 12:00 for stamp exchange and
lunch. Each should bring their own picnic lunch or
purchase lunch at one of the fast food restaurants
near the entrance of the park. If you would like to
bring finger foods to share you may do that. We will
have door prize
drawings at this time also.

Hope to see you on the 21st
Flagbearer and Full of Heart
The Sisters Act
