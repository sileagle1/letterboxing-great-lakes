From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-10 10:12:07 UTC-06:00
Yes, we had a streak for a while this summer where we were consistently
second finders, mostly to Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man on several
Grail boxes... Anyway, this inspired us to make an "Official 2nd
Finder" stamp. :) We stamp this in silver into boxes where we're the
second finders, along with our regular mini-stamps. We also carved an
"Official 1st Finder" stamp on the reverse side as an afterthought,
which we use in gold on FF boxes. :) We haven't used either of them too
many times though.
