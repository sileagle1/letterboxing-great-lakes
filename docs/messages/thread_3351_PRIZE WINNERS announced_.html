From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-24 14:16:38 UTC
It gives me great pleasure to announce our prize winners ...

prizes for the MI counties Quiz:

1st - a Stampeaz gift certificate goes to Bali Grl
2nd - a Staedtler-Mars Grand eraser goes to Star Girl/Amy Hackert
3rd - #11 carving blades - goes to Trail Troll
4th - #1 carving nibs goes to Klustofin/Katy



prizes for the "Cover the counties" contest:

1st - a Stampeaz gift certificate goes to Trail Troll
for covering Arenac county
2nd - a Staedtler-Mars Grand eraser goes to Bali Grl
for covering Jackson county
3rd - #11 carving blades goes to Penny Penny
for covering Montmorency county
4th - #1 carving nibs goes to Spring Chick
for covering Ionia county

Would Star Girl and Bali Grl please contact me with an email address
you'd like to have used for your Stampeaz gift certificate?

I also need each of the other winners to contact me off-list with
their mailing address.

For those of you who have won carving blades and nibs, if you're
preference is for whichever one you did NOT win and you would like to
see if one of the other winners is willing to make a trade, please
include that info as well and we'll see what we can do!

Honorable mention to WI Hiker who inspired this contest and Dragon
who, I think covered the most WI counties since the competition began
(someone correct me if I'm wrong) If each of you would please contact
me off-list as well, (grin) I think I may have something in my goodie
bag for you, too.

Many thanks to everyone who participated with the Quiz and most
especially those of you (in both states!) who got out there and placed
boxes during the contest time.

Enjoy!
---Bindle Babe
