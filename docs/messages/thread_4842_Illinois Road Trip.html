Illinois Road Trip

From: pgranola2002 (p2jd@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-03-13 01:26:05 UTC
Dear Great Lakes Boxers,
The end of a long winter is in sight and spring is just around the
corner. Most of us are more than ready to brush the dust off our
backpacking gear and hit the trails. A couple summers ago a number
of boxers participated in a special letterbox series , "Around the
World in 80 Days" by Azobox and Springchick. It was an adventure
that took us to some great spots around Michigan. That series was the
inspiration for this Illinois Road Trip.
Illinois is rich in history and is just brimming with fun places to
visit. With spring breaks quickly approaching this is the perfect
time to hit the open road in search of some really cool Illinois
attractions. The road trip will take you to museums, restaurants,
cemeteries, historical sites and some really quirky spots.
There are 45 boxes placed all around Illinois that will lead you to
some amazing places. About half of the boxes have been placed just
for the road trip, the others were already existing boxes that were
incorporated into the road trip. Your mission is to collect the
images from each box on a special log map that will be mailed to you.
When you fill in the log with all 45 of the required stamps you will
mail it back to me and you will get the "King of the Illinois Road"
stamp mailed to you. If you are interested, you need to e-mail me
with your home address so I can send you your copies of the Illinois
Road Trip log, then you need to go to: yahoo groups Ilroadtrip.
(that's capital i - lowercase L) and request an invitation. Once I
hear from you, I will send you an invitation and you will have access
to all the road trip information. Once you have your log you can hit
the road!
This is not a race, there is no time limit because of the distances
involved. If you can't get to them all, it's ok.
My thanks to all who have contributed to this series, there are some
great places I can't wait to go and visit myself!
