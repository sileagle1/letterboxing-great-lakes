It seems that there is some desire to be able to find boxes from either
OHare or downtown Chicago. I've responded to a few of the posts on this
site off-list, but I'm posting a few boxes here for general knowledge.
Perhaps people could add to this list if I've left a few off....and I've
not included boxes that I suspect aren't there (based on personal
experience). If you go to www.yourcta.com you can find a trip planner
that will help you find buses and trains.

Boxes In/Near downtown are: Green Tortuga has one in the train station,
"The Grand Hall" / Minimom has one downtown at the "Harold Washington
Library" / Matt The Rat has one downtown, "Chicago Underground"

Boxes a bit further from downtown but that you can find via a short
El/Bus ride and hike: via the El Blue Line north, you can find one of
mine "CTA Blue Line" / Beach Mom has one near downtown in "Humboldt
Park." It can be accessed via the Blue Line and a short hike (~1mile) or
bus. / If you take a bus south (#6) to Hyde Park, Coffee Beans has one
there, "Chicago's Wild Parakeets" and I do too, "CP1." (alas my "White
City Series" has not yet been replanted)

Boxes near downtown but would require a bit more work figuring out the
buses: Beach Mom's "Momentum" box is nearish to a Red Line El Stop /
Pico's "Historic Chicago Box #1: The Clarke House" is just south of
downtown / and you could reach Matt the Rat's "Bubbly Creek or wrgpt9's
"Illinois Centennial Monument" via a buses with a transfer or two.

Those are the ones that are probably easiest to find without a car from
downtown.
