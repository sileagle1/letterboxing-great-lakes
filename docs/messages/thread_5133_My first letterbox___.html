From: Angi (anorsen@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-06-23 02:14:45 UTC
...in nearly two years!! I didn't realize how much I missed
letterboxing until I was talking to my neighbor about it the other
day. Can't say I really missed the mosquitoes though...ouch!! I went
out on Saturday and found a box that we just happened to be going by
on the way to dinner. :) Worked out well.

So, I guess it is my luck that the day I chose to come back to this
wonderful hobby, there was a gathering not too far away. Argh... ;)

Just as an update on how things have been on my end (and why I've
been gone so long), I've been pretty busy. First off, I took a break
from the LB world because I was kind of tired of hearing all the
bickering on the lists online (does that really ever happen? lol). It
kind of put me off for a while. I'm not going to let it get to me
this time. Then Manic Mechanic (Nick) and I bought a house a year ago
and got engaged in November at Devil's Lake. I started teaching
(FINALLY...I never thought this would be such a hard profession to
get into!) last year as well. I have been working like crazy with my
jewelry as well; I plan on getting my business name and tax number at
the end of the summer so I can get my dot com name online. Nick and I
also recently welcomed a new addition to our family...a beagle pup
named Mugsy. :) So...there's my explanation...don't hate me for
leaving!!! ::BIG HUGS::

So people, fill me in on the details. What's been going on in the
wonderful world of letterboxing lately? Anybody have any great new
news they'd like to share? I'd love to hear it!

Shutterfly (who hangs her head in shame for not coming back sooner)
