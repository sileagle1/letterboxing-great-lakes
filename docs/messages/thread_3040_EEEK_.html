From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-05 19:25:27 UTC
When you sign up to join the group, one of the options on the sign-up
page is how you would like to view messages. The default option
is "individual e-mails" so if you do not change it, you will receive a
copy of each message posted to the group sent to you in your e-mail.

If this is not what you want, go to the group home page, click
on "edit my membership" (up near the top on the left-hand side) and
change this to one of the other options.

SpringChick

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-05 19:03:31 UTC-04:00
It is not necessary to receive the e-mail messages from the group into your e-mail. You can turn off e-mails and just read the groups messages on the web whenever you feel like it. To change your membership options to not send you e-mails, go to the group home page, click on 'edit my membership' and then change the e-mail option from 'individual messages' to whatever option works best for you.

If you still wish to unsubscribe -- at the bottom of each e-mail that you receive, there is a link to 'unsubscribe.' Click on this and follow the onscreen instruction to be removed from the list.

SpringChick
