Hi everyone, this is my first post so how you all doin?

As long as Devil's lake and the area is being discussed, I thought I'd
tell my little story of our trip. My family and I were taking a
letterboxing day-trip to central Wisconsin and we hit about 7 or so
sites. Funny enough, every single one was stamped by Wisconsin Hiker
either eariler that day or very recently. We kinda thought that was
neat, but we were curious as to why there were none at Devil's lake
yet. Sure enough when we get home, three more were planted over the
weekend. We just missed being able to get those too, which is too bad
because we don't get to go up there very often.

Anyway, just wanted to say hello. Hopefully you'll be seeing some
boxes from me sometime soon, but work and school keep me pretty busy.
I got some ideas though. :)
