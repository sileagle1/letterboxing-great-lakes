Now that things have settled down...

From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-10 04:40:24 UTC
To start, I would really like to thank everyone for their prayers,
support, and well wishes. It makes me feel good that we belong to a
group of such amazing people. I know that we have only scratched the
surface in terms of meeting the names on this site but you are all
very close to our hearts. I can't wait for some of you to meet
Emily, and she can't wait to get out boxing.

About Bingo...
Well, with the free space and today's word, we have 2! Look out The
Cottontails are on a roll!!

About Christmas Cards...
Not to make ya feel bad, but Mrs. Cottontails and I not only hand
made our cards this year, but we mailed 'em on the way to the
hospital with our Bingo booty. Though I do understand being busy
last year we mailed 'em on December 26th.

About Circular paths...
This makes me laugh because we had a box that did just that. We also
try to send people in a circle so after a 90 minute walk you don't
have another 90 minutes back to the car. Though recently it hasn't
worked out that way. That first box was my favorite, especially
reading the comments in the now missing logbook.

About Registering finds...
I am with the people who like a little note, although checking the
book and seeing lots of names you never heard from is also really
cool.

About the Gathering and Camping...
While I used to do alot of camping, Mrs. Cottontails never has, we
don't even own a tent. I am pretty sure her idea of "roughing" it is
a hotel with a black and white TV. I don't know what Emily thinks
about camping. What I am trying to say is, are there any good
(cheap) hotels nearby? We are definately planning on going in fact
we voted for Ohio because it gets us halfway to CT. Provided there
are no more snow days this will be the beginning of a week long trip.

About the Southern Illinois Gathering...
I think the best way is to try and find out from confirmed attendees
ahead of time so they can send $$ and do the rest outside. We would
like to come but aren't sure yet.

Again, many thanks to all of you.

The Cottontails
