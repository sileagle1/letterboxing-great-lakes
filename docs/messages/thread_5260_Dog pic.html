She was a stray, so we really have no way of knowing, and have no interest
in shelling out $100+ for genetic testing. :-)

We do, however, love to speculate. Our guess is that she's mostly German
Shepherd (her muzzle coloring), Chow Chow (coat color and multi-color
tongue), and Shiba Inu -- a little Japanese hunting dog. Shibas look a LOT
like Akitas, but they're much smaller, usually only 25 lbs or so. They have
the same curled tail, orange-gold coat, and she has a personality very much
like a Shiba -- too smart for her own good, territorial, and absolutely
fanatic about chasing small game. Also, automatically housebroken
(score!). She also has the trademark "Shiba Scream," which she shows off at
the vet when she gets her nails clipped.

she's our baby!
