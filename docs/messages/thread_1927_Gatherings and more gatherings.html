From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-26 01:32:01 UTC
I remember reading some time ago that Indiana would have to wait until 2007 for the
Great Lakes Gathering. That's fine and fair with me. Thanks, Dragon, for pointing us to
the last thread.

In all likelihood we will be moving within the year. My better half is commuting 120 miles
every day, sometimes 6 or 7 days a week. We have many decisions to make and we are
taking them slowly.

I would be willing to help with a big gathering in 2007 or whenever The BIG gathering
comes our way. If I am not totally uprooted at the time of a smaller gathering, I'd be
willing to help too. Right now I just can't make many commitments. I would do my
darnedest to make it to Wisconsin - I have a growing list of reminders and I will try to
bring some friends along. I'm just sorry I have missed at least 4 gatherings since last May,
but who's counting?

As far as Indiana locations ... Pokagon is a nice spot with lots of trails. If anyone knows if
there are heated spaces to rent in Winter, that might be fun. In the summertime, Eagle
Creek Park in Indianapolis is huge. They have Volksmarches there too. I was just in Fort
Harrison SP a couple of weeks ago and there is an entirely new section of the park opening
up, but I don't see indoor facilities yet.

I will be out Friday in the western part of our state hunting for boxes. I'll keep my eyes
open for potential sites for future gatherings.

~speedsquare

PS - Warning: Armadillo Jo has a lead foot! It takes us about 3 hours to get to Steuben
County from Indianapolis.
