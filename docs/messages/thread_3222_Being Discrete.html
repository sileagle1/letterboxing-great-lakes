Being Discrete

From: lininil (letterboxing@mac.com) | Date: 2006-10-26 21:11:58 UTC
I received an email today from the manager of a forest preserve property where I have an
approved and permitted letterbox. The manager was letting me know that people have
been coming into the nature center and asking the employees there "where is the
letterbox that is located in these woods, we can't find it?", and then showing them the
clues.

Just to let the new boxers know; this isn't a discrete way to search for boxes.

Lucky for me it's a box with a permit.
CMoon

I have the funny feeling these people are not a member of the Yahoo

reward for printing out the clues....

Ugh! What a conundrum this is getting to be!!
