Afton State Park

From: Lock, Shock & Barrel (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-20 15:06:00 UTC
We just received word from the Ranger's office at Afton State Park that
our applications were approved, and our three boxes from GLG2008 have
been replaced. The application is very straightforward, and the office
was terrific about providing the coordinates (which are required for
the app). If you have any concerns about one of your boxes from the
event and have questions, I would recommend sending an email to
Gene.Groebner@dnr.state.mn.us. He was very helpful, and asked that we
mention the application process, so that the boxes can get back out
into their spots in the park.

Best Regards,

Lock, Shock and Barrel
