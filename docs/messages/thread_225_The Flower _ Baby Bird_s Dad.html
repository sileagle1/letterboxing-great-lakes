Does anyone know how to reach Baby Bird's Dad a/k/a Little Bird's
Dad? I tried the contact the placer link as well as the e-mail
address in his clue without success.

new, which blocks a trail into the woods. However, the sign didn't

but we didn't find the box anywhere near the tree.

We hoped that Baby Bird's Dad might have an opportunity to check on
The Flower. If its still in place and we just missed it, wed like
to look for it on our next trip to the area.

We enjoyed our trip to Independence Grove and found Pleasant Valley
in good shape.
