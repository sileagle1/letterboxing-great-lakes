The e-mail that was sent to the group (and probably other people in her
address book) is automatically generated by the virus that has been going
around Yahoo! groups (see message posted by BindleBabe yesterday). I saw a
similar post at letterbox-usa earlier today.

Apparently you don't have to actually open the attachment to get infected,
only the message containing the attachment. If your virus definitions are
up-to-date, they should catch it because it plants itself onto your
computer, however if they are not, it will infect your computer and
automatically e-mails itself to randomly selected addresses in your address
book

I deleted the message from the LbGLK group shortly after it was posted, but
anyone who gets e-mail and/or the digest will still get the message. DON'T
OPEN THE MESSAGE titled "New Graphic Site" even though it says it is from
Coffeebeans!

If you accidentally do/did, you should run a virus scan on your computer to
ensure that you were not infected or to clean the virus if you were
infected.

SpringChick
