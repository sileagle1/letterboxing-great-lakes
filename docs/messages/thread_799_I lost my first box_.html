I lost my first box!

From: Gabrielle Coil (gmc81@randomkitty.net) | Date: 2004-10-23 19:06:39 UTC-07:00
I went to check on my Hidden Village boxes this evening after recieving a tip that someone couldn't find the final box in the series (and I was thinking it was about time to check up on them anyway). And, sure enough, the last box in that series has gone missing :( I was afraid that might happen...I was never really satisfied with the location in the first place. It's kind of sad, especially since that particular stamp was the first I had carved after carving my signature stamps.

But it's an easy enough stamp to carve, and I have plenty of erasers left to recarve it. And I already know where I'll be moving it to when I replace the box. Quite frankly, it's a much better location all around...just not quite as close to the landmark. But the best part about it is that I don't have to completely rewrite my clues...just change a few parts.

Kimono Girl
