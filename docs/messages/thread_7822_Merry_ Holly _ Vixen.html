From: (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-11-10 14:44:13 UTC-08:00

A few years back, we placed three holiday-themed pin-up boxes in local shops in Libertyville, IL.  As planned, we pulled them after the new year, and we've been holding them until now.

Earlier today, Merry, Holly and Vixen were given permanent homes in Moraine Hills State Park, just around the bend from Holiday Hills, IL.

Because it's an update to an existing box series, I don't think the update will make its way onto any notification lists from Atlasquest or Letterboxing.org.  So if you were interested in these back in December of 2010, but didn't get to them, we wanted to let you know that they have new locations and new clues.
