WI Hiker,

Thanks for reminding me about all the poison ivy that I may encounter. I seem to forget that itchy stuff is out there just waiting for us.

Over the weekend, an opportunity presented itself and I was off in search of some new places to hide letterboxes. I didn't have my hiking shoes or socks, but that didn't stop me. I was wearing CROCS flip flops; comfortable but certainly no protection from poison ivy. If I didn't have a trail name already, I'm sure people would call me FLIP FLOP. I try to be careful and look where I'm stepping. So far, I'm not itching so I guess I've been able to identify what I'm hiking through or I'm just very lucky.

Hope Martini Man's itching subsides soon. OUCH OUCH OUCH!
