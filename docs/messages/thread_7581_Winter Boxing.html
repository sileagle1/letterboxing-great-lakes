From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2013-01-02 10:46:08 UTC-06:00
As you make your resolutions for your letterboxing year, think about
starting it off with our winter series 'Two is Better Than One.' It is
conveniently located to the highway near Old Orchard mall in Skokie IL.
Since this is our winter series, we will be pulling the boxes once
spring arrives. This year, we are saying Valentines Day or when the
snow melts (whichever comes last) is when we will pull the boxes.

Happy New Year, and may all your adventures be filled with boxes!

Atom 118 of the Atomic Beans
