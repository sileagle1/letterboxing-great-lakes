I see my response has generated a lot of buzz. Absolutely, let the
kids find the boxes and decipher the clues. As always, spot the box
first and rehide exactly as before. We found one box recently that was
not exactly where it was said to be (it was inside the dead tree
instead of in the sticks at the bottom). This frustrated my kids to no
end. Upon further looking, we found it. My wife and I never ever let
the kids rehide the box and we always remind them not to destroy the
locale in any way. But...I stick behind what I posted earlier. Let the
kids find the box, even if you found it first.
