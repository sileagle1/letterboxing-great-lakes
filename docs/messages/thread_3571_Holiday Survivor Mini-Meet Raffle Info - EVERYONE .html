From: lcoil79 (lcoil79@insightbb.com) | Date: 2007-01-13 03:19:14 UTC
Here's how the raffle will be conducted, remember, most of this is in
order to avoid breaking our agreement with the library on taking
collections/donations.

Prizes include:
* Set of LePlume II Brush Markers
* 3 Great Lakes Regional patches
* Telescopic walking stick (courtesy of MRSHAT)
* Centrifuge tubes [great for microboxes] (courtesy of Katy)
* $25 BP Gas Card (courtesy of Basket Case)

- All proceeds will be sent to the administrators of the LBNA website
to help defray their costs.

- One person will be designated the ticket holder.

- At 3 times during the meet (currently 1:30, 2:30, and 3:15), the
ticket holder will announce they are either "Going Outside" or "Going
to Lincoln Square". They will then head over to where they announced
and will sell tickets.

- Tickets will be $1 each and will get you one chance to win a prize.

- Each prize will have a container in front of it for tickets. Place
the tickets in the container for the prize you wish to win, this will
eliminate people possibly winning something they aren't interested in.
On the flip side, if there are two prizes you want to win, you will
need to buy at least 2 tickets, one for each prize.

- You can improve your chances of winning an item by putting more than
1 ticket in it's container.

- The drawing for the raffle will happen around 3:30.


Now, here's an exciting twist to the auction that is open to all
members of the Great Lakes group, and anyone else you know who isn't
on the group. There will be one item up for raffle that is open to
everyone:

**** A copy of Microsoft Streets & Trips 2006 ****

This is a slightly used copy (still in excellent condition). My
stupidity can be someone's gain. I thought I had lost this program so
I went out and bought the 2007 copy, then found my 2006 copy when I
got home from Best Buy. I'd had the intention of returning it, but
have since lost the receipt, so I'm going to keep it. Which means I
now have a copy of MS S&T 2006 that I don't need. Here's the details
for this one.

- One ticket costs $1 and gets your 1 chance to win this prize.

- For those not attending the Mini-Meet, I will be accepting PayPal
payments for the tickets (minimum 3 tickets if purchased by PayPal to
cover the PayPal fees and any shipping costs for the prize). Requests
for PayPal purchase are to be made to me through e-mail at
lcoil79@insightbb.com I will need a valid e-mail address to send you
a transfer request. I will also need your Trail Name to write on the
tickets.

- Once payment is received, your name will be placed on each ticket
purchased and that ticket will be placed into the container for the MS
S&T prize.

- If you are completely against using PayPal I will accept payments by
cash or money order(preferred). But it needs to be in my hands by
Saturday, as such mailing by Monday is advised. I will not be
responsible for cash lost in the mail or for mail that doesn't reach
me before the Saturday deadline. The address for mailed payments is
at the end of this post. If you are sending me payment by mail,
please e-mail me and let me know so that I can keep an eye on the mail
for it, stuff tends to sit for a couple of days if I'm not expecting
it (much to Kimono Girl's dismay).

- Upon request, a picture of your tickets can be provided to prove
your name has been thrown in to the running. After the drawing, all
names for the prize will be logged and signed by at least two other
attendees as proof of who had how many tickets, kind of a backup
verification so you'll know things are fair.

Good luck, and remember it's for a good cause, our continued ability
to find clues to letterboxes across the country. Have fun.
