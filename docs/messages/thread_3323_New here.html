Hi all! I am new to the group....have been lurking for a little bit
to get the feel of the group. Well, since I just went to the database
and entered my name for a patch, I figured that I better introduce
myself. My trail name is Sunny. My 3 daughters and I just started
letterboxing this past summer...well, actually in October. I first
read about LB in my Family Fun magazine and I thought that it would be
something relatively cheap for all of us to do together. It has been
FUN! We've only found 3 boxes together, but my oldest daughter and I
went on a class trip to Mackinac Island and introduced her class to LB
and found 2 on the island (there are 13 total there). AND, last week,
we found our first hitchiker!! It was the "smile" hitchhiker if
anyone is interested in knowing that info.
How does the LB go in the snow? I'm wondering what winter will bring
and if it will dampen our chances of getting out and finding more
boxes.
Oh, we are in Michigan, btw. Near Iron Mountain.
Heather
