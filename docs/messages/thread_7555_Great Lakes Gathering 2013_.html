Great Lakes Gathering 2013!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2012-10-29 11:13:43 UTC
There has been some buzz recently about next year's Great Lakes Gathering, so let's get this thing going!

Ohio is up in the rotation for hosting the 2013 Great Lakes Gathering. As is customary I would like to hear from anyone who is interested in hosting the event. Typically the gathering is held on a Saturday in late May or early June -- exact date is host's choice.

I did have one individual contact me expressing interest, but she has backed out, so if you are interested, please step up!

Please respond to the list or to me directly by Saturday, Nov. 3. If there are multiple parties interested in hosting the event we can try to hook them up or a vote will be taken based on proposed date and location.

Thanks!

SpringChick

Re: Great Lakes Gathering 2013!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2012-11-13 12:07:38 UTC
As there has not been any interest in hosting the 2013 GLG from anyone in Ohio, the opportunity then moves to the next state in the rotation. In the interest of saving time, I am not going to go state by state, rather open it up to anyone who is interested, with preference being given to the next state in the rotation (MN, IN, IL, MI, WI).

If you are thinking about hosting, please don't be discouraged by anything negative you may have seen, as there really is very little politics involved in planning the gathering. Other than this selection process for host, the planning of the gathering is not a group effort unless you want it to be. The host is entirely free to decide location, date, details, etc. I am not really sure what transpired in the previous situation and it is unfortunate.

Please contact me by this weekend if you are interested in hosting.

Thanks!

SpringChick
