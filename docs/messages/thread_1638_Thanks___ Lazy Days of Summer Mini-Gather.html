Thanks!!! Lazy Days of Summer Mini-Gather

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-07-25 18:43:05 UTC
Thanks to everyone who made it out to Muskegon State Park on Saturday
for the Lazy Days Mini-Gather. It was good to see old friends from
far and near and also to meet a few new ones! The day brought visits
from Grandvilla & MobiusKlein, TipToe & Tonto, Team Rabbit, Sisu Sue
& Can Man and Scheherezade & Puss-'n-Boots. Despite the humidity and
late-afternoon rain, we had a great time.

The morning was spent as a group hunting down a little sunshine
(thank goodness Mother Nature spared us the real thing or we would
have surely withered from the heat!) along one of the nicest trails
in Muskegon State Park. We also made a side-trip over to one of
Keltic Kara's boxes for those who hadn't stamped in there before
(thanks to Sisu Sue for bringing along clues to several Keltic Kara
boxes). After the morning trek, the group split and everyone went
their own way, tracking down area letterboxes they had yet to find.
Unfortunately the rain put a damper on the bonfire, but thanks to
Scheherezade and Puss-'n-Boots for stopping by in spite of the
downpour! Team Rabbit, I hope you made it back to your campsite okay
and found everything there safe and dry. Thanks again to all and I
look forward to seeing many of you at the MI Fall Gathering at Yankee
Spring Rec Area on Oct. 22nd.

SpringChick
