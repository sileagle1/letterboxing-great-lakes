From: lcoil79@ameritech.net smokey (ainawgsd@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-12-27 17:03:45 UTC-08:00
It's a little early, but it's too late for Merry Christmas. Speaking of New Year's, would anyone be interested in a "special" exchange? Last year, since I failed to get the Christmas cards out at all, I sent out Chinese New Year's cards instead.

So what I propose is a Chinese New Year's card exchange! After all, Chinese New Year is a celebration of family and this group has shown again and again that they are as close as family, sharing joys and sorrows alike. I would like to have an exchange of hand-made cards (traditional cards, ATC's, postcards, whatever strikes your fancy just as long as they aren't store-bought). They don't have to be stamped, although I expect the majority will probably be...stamp carving being a major artistic outlet for this group...other art mediums are fine too.

The 2008 lunar calendar begins on February 7 and 2008 is the year of the rat. Anyone interested in participating should email me off list at ainawgsd@yahoo.com with their trailname and address by January 10. Real names are optional. I will send the list of participants and addresses along with the mailing deadline.

Kimono Girl
