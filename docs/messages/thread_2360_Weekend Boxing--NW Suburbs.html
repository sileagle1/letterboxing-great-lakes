Weekend Boxing--NW Suburbs

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-04-03 13:46:59 UTC
We decided that Saturday was the day for an early Easter Egg hunt, so
we headed out to Elgin for the Cottontail's Easter Egg Hunt.
Beautiful little stamps (in quite clever containers) and a gorgeous
Easter basket at the end. We had a hard time finding #6 and couldn't
find #7 at first. We decided to try one more time on our way back to
the car and found it! Good thing we saved a spot on our pages for it.
Then it was on to a few more Cottontail stamps: I "Heart" Haring and
Stick a Fork in Me. These works of art are also stamping works of art.

Then it was on to Schaumburg. We tracked down the box to Viva La
Mexico (by Lavender)and then had lunch at that restaurant. Our next
stop was Anniversary Logo--also by Lavender--another beautiful stamp.
We were hoping to be First Finders since it was just placed on
Thursday. However, as we approached the spot we saw that The
Cottontails had just beat us there and were stamping in. Oh well.
Even though we met the Cottontails at the Haunted gathering, we hadn't
done an exchange--so we got the chance here. Then it was on to home
to get ready for our evening activities. But we'll need to head back
to this area as we left a lot of boxes unsearched.
