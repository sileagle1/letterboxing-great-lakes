Cheetah Letterbox?

From: Beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-06-23 05:34:50 UTC
I was asked to share this funny story with others. Here goes.....
A fellow letterboxer found a small plastic cheetah figurine inside one of my letterboxes. She wasn't sure why it was there, and decided to just leave it in the letterbox, but she emailed me and told her about it. Someone has geocaching and letterboxing a bit confused. Personally, when there are trinkets inside a letterbox, it could confuse others and someone might think they can take the stamp as a prize. So, I prefer that people not leave trinkets in my letterboxes. So tonight, I asked my daughter if she wanted to come along for a drive while I did some letterbox maintenance. I told her I was in search of a cheetah inside one of my letterboxes. She was confused. She asked me - "I know about cooties and flees, but what is a cheetah?" Awh, don't you love letterbox terms? She thought a cheetah was some sort of letterbox. ROFLOL!

Beetle
