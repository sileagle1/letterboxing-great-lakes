From: M. Olesen (mdolesen@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-19 14:38:16 UTC
I am Marci of the 3 "MO's" and the other two "mo's" would be my sons
Max and Madison. We live in Northern Illinois, and are gearing up
for a trip to NC where we have already planned our LB day. 3 more
were just placed in the area we are going to be.

We were introduced to letterboxing by good friends of ours about a
year ago and on our very first find we found a HH - we were hooked.
Since then we have found almost 100, and have 2 of 5 stamps carved
for a series we would like to place this fall. Occasionally you will
see when we stamp in that we are the 3 "MO's" plus Dad or plus EM.
My husband, not a huge fan of trapsing around the woods for hours at
a time, actually was disappointed that he couldn't go with us this
weekend. We are slowly but surely bringing him into the LB world.
EM would be short for Emily - our dog. We try to take her along when
the car ride isn't too long. I have found that letterboxing is a
great way to stay connected to the oldest son (about to be a
teenager) I can get him talking about most anything while on the
trail. The younger one just likes keeping up with big brother and
searching for the next stamp.

I don't tend to chime in very often on the posts, but enjoy reading
all about what is going on around the great lakes area. Thanks to
everyone for the wealth of information, otherwise I may have been
afraid to try my hand at carving stamps!!

Marci of the 3 "MO's"
