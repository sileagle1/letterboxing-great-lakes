Re: [LbGLK] BoaF conditions?

From: Mothkovich, Christine (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2008-06-11 09:36:08 UTC-05:00
I was just there last night, and will be back again tonight (hiding event
boxes). The Kishwaukee River is up a little, but isn't out of its banks as
of last night. Tonight I will concentrate on the adjoining Potawatomie
Woods Forest Preserve. I am not concerned about the river, but I AM
wondering if the little creek is up enough to be over the stepping stones
for crossing. I'll check that out tonight.

I have finally learned my lesson NOT to plant letterboxes too close to the
Kishwaukee River because it does flood sometimes up to 12' above normal.
However, almost all of MacQueen, Potawatomie, and Kishwaukee Game & Fish are
high enough not to worry about - it's only the lower sections along the
river that might be iffy.

As a note - IF on the day of the event the creek is high enough that the
stepping stones are under water, Potawatomie can still be accessed by a
short drive, rather than walking the connecting trail.

Let me know if there are any other questions :)

Trail Rider/Chris
