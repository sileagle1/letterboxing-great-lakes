LBNA fundraiser

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-05-07 07:43:51 UTC-07:00

A new fundraising campaign has been started to move the LBNA (letterboxing.org) site to a more reliable server.  As many of you have noticed, there have been problems with the DNS (Domain Name Server) over the past several months.  The Kickstarter campaign will provide funds to move to a better hosting service and fund some updates.


There are some rewards for different levels of giving that you can see on the Kickstarter site.


Martini Man & I will be supporting this effort and hope many others in the Great Lakes regions will also do so.  This is a historic letterboxing site and still remains a NON-PROFIT website run by volunteers who care about letterboxing without receiving a financial income from doing so.
