Fish Boil WI 10th Pre-Gathering Dinner

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-09-26 18:43:06 UTC
Countdown to the WI 10th Letterbox Gathering on Saturday, October 4,
2008 at Whitefish Dunes State Park---see you then! But we will need our
energy and folks gotta eat, so we are planning a pre-gathering meet and
greet Fish Boil the night before on Friday October 3 at The Wagon Trail
Resort, 1041 County Road ZZ in Rowleys Bay, WI. Check out their web
site at www.wagontrail.com or call 1-888-559-2466 for directions. Easy
way to find this quiet gem is to find the Sister Bay Bowl in downtown
Sister Bay. It's located the corner of Hwy. 42 and Hwy. ZZ. Follow
the signs and take ZZ to the Wagon Trail. The pre-gathering will start
at 5:00 pm. Meet in front of the Wagon Trail on by the fish boil pot.
There are some local letterboxes posted on AQ and LbNA, so check out
the clues. And yes, there will be several special temporary boxes that
will be placed for this special pre-gathering as well. Those clues will
be available at the dinner. The theme of the series of boxes for this
pre-gathering will be....Famous Fish! Dinner is a full buffet with
several main entrees. So even if you don't think you are a fish eater,
you can sample the fish boil and choose other items too. Questions:
Contact Beetle. The pre-gathering event will be posted on AQ and LbNA.
There are approximately 22 people who have already contacted me, so the
more the merrier!


Fish Boil WI 10th Pre-Gathering Dinner

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-09-29 19:44:41 UTC
The Wagon Trail Resort has confirmed our reservation for Friday,
October 3, 2008. The Fish Boil "boil over" will be at 4:45 pm. Come
for the blaze--then enjoy the fish.

The Fish Boil Buffet Dinner will be served at 5:00 pm SHARP! Normally,
there is not a fish boil on Friday night; only on Saturday night. But
with advance notice of a group of 24+ guests, they will do a special
Fish Boil for us.

You might even want to arrive in the area early and look for the
permanent local letterboxers. I will be staying at the Wagon Trail
Friday until Monday. I plan to arrive early in the afternoon and hide
the Famous Fish Series. So if you see someone in a Beatles T-shirt
sitting in the lobby or on the front porch of the Wagon Trail on Friday
afternoon, around 4:00 pm. --- that's me! (Unless there are other
Beatles fans in the area too and they are also wearing Beatles t-shirts
at the Wagon Trail - LOL!)

Cost: The complete buffet dinner is $15.99 per person + tax and
gratuity = $19.91 per person. Beverages, not normally included, WILL
be included for us....makes it easier for the waitress and the guests.
However, alcoholic beverages will be extra. So far, no one has
indicated bringing children for the Fish Boil Buffet Dinner, but there
is a lesser charge for children.

MMM...I can almost taste that fish! But there are other entrees on the
buffet for those who want to 'sample' the fish, but enjoy the
additional homecooked foods too.

See everyone on Friday....and again on Saturday at the 10th WI
Gathering at Whitefish Dunes State Park.

Happy Boxing

Beetle
(Sometimes called Carol)
