Re: [LbGLK] BINGO 2008

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-13 05:35:00 UTC-05:00
There is not a suggested $$ amount -- it has worked well to just leave it open to whatever you can do. In the past I have received everything from small blank books to carving blades to pieces of carving material to handmade socks to full sets of Marvy markers... and everything imaginable in between. I don't want anyone to be left out because of a $$ limit. The idea is to build a prize kitty based on everybody contributing one thing and I trust that everyone is best qualified to make the call as to how much they are able to contribute.

SpringChick
