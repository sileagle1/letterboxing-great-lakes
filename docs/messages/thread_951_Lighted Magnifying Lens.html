I LOVE the lighted magnifying lens Martini Man got me for
Christmas!! It clamps onto my desk and has large extendible arms to
postion the magnifying lens wherever I want. It has a bright light
to illuminate the carving media and a large lens with 2.25X
magnification. It really helps me see what I am carving (which is a
BIG help since my "40-something" eyes have deteriorated a lot this
past year!).

good (30s and 40s), we'll probably be out hiding some boxes this
weekend.
