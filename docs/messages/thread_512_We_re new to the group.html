Hi all fellow letterboxers,
We are the Wormy Wanderers from IL and are new to letterboxing. We

Hello Wormy Wanderers (I can't wait to see your signature stamp ;-)!
Welcome to letterboxing and to the Great Lakes group! Sounds like you
have a good start both with finding some boxes and getting the ideas going
for some future plants.

So far there has been one annual Great Lakes gathering each of the past two
years and it is tentatively planned for the second Saturday of June on an
annual basis -- assuming somebody volunteers to host each year. In addition
to that there have been less formal gatherings in individual states and
cities when the mood strikes or if somebody if travelling and a bunch of
people decide to get together and meet up with them, etc. Most of those
types of gatherings in any of the Great Lakes states would be mentioned here
on the list, so stay tuned!

SpringChick
