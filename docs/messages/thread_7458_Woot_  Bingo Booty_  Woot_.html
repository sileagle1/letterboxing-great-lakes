From: (sweaters3@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-05-15 02:47:53 UTC
WOW doesn't begin to cover it, but WOW! I posted a picture in the photos section and will try to list everything here. Navy nylon drawstring backpack with LbGLK logo; Dartmoor Letterboxes paperback book; ziplock freezer bags (PINT size, YAY!); small portable first aid kit; yummy trail mix; cushy Merino hiker socks; insect repellent and sunscreen towel (wish I had that when I was boxing a couple weeks ago!);
whistles (if a boxer blows a whistle in the woods and there is no one to hear it, does it really make a sound?..lol); LED cap light (night boxing here I come); spray pen insect repellent; sketch log book (I signed into one of these a couple weeks ago and thought this was a really nice journal); VERY NICE letter boxing patch; carbiner; take-away business size cards to put inside boxes that explain letter boxing; lock-n-lock container; PZKut (what!?) that's right, honest to goodness 100% real Grade B orange PZKut (woot, woot!); and gift cards for BP gas, Subway, Walgreens AND Stampeaz. WHEW, I can't believe how very generous our BINGO players are, and a big shout out to Andrew for facilitating this year's game, and to those of you with such funny and creative posts that kept me entertained. See you all next January first! Happy Boxing ~ Socks3
