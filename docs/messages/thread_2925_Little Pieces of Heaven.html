Hello all ~ in the mood for a cemetery stroll?

Well ~ good! Just so happens there is a Little Piece of Heaven in
many cemeteries in Ottawa County, MI.

However ~ #7 has been pulled due to some nasty poison ivy in the
area. But ~ if you read the clues, you may still be able to get it.

#9 needed a correction in the driving directions - thanks to
SpringChick, bless her heart - she is a dedicated one! (Sorry it's
taken me so long to fix it...)

That's all for now!

Mrs. Doglvrs

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-09 13:26:57 UTC-04:00
If you are in West Michigan and have a chance to do this series of boxes, I
highly recommend them. I was a little apprehensive about letterboxing in
cemeteries at first, but really enjoyed visiting these places and found some
wonderful history. The boxes are all very respectfully placed and it is a
nice series of stamps.

SpringChick
