10/22 MI Gathering Reminders...

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-10-21 22:46:21 UTC
Just a few last-minute reminders...

- The gathering begins at 8:00 a.m. and the potluck is at 12:00 p.m.
We are planning to wrap up around 6:00 p.m. As the afternoon is
primarily letterboxing time, feel free to stay as long as you want to
keep boxing. The park closes at 10:00 p.m.

- If you placed your own boxes at the park, please bring clues to
share.

- Come with questions, ideas and input for the "Building a Better
Letterbox" forum.

- There is no running water at the shelter so please come prepared
with beverages and if you need water for your potluck item, you'll
need to bring it with you.

- If you have a lantern or other non-electric light you are willing
to bring, please do so.

- Wear bright colors as it is hunting season and land adjacent to
Yankee Springs is open to hunting.


One other thing...

If you are planning to look for the Election '04 boxes by Lucky
Charms, you might want to read through the clues this evening (they
are online at letterboxing.org as there is a little bit of research
required on these).

See you tomorrow!

SpringChick
