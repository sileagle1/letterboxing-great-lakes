Howdy, all -

Thought I'd pass along word that I've planted four new boxes in West
Michigan (Muskegon and Newaygo). The clues for two of them (Music in
Motion) are up on the main website. Eventually this will be a four
stamp series. I've also hidden two boxes at the Coolbough Natural
Area just northeast of Newaygo. Eventually this will be a six box
series, and the clues for these first two boxes will be up soon
(today or tomorrow). Also, keep a look out for a Mystery Box that
will be out soon (I need to hide it yet, and I'm really excited about
this stamp). It wasn't going to be a mystery box - I decided to take
SpringChick's TOTW advice and try to plant one. It should be out
within the week.

Peace, and happy searching!
