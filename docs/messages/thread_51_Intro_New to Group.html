Intro/New to Group

From: lavandarfields (MG02zion@aol.com) | Date: 2004-03-29 19:37:26 UTC
Just wanted to say Hello and what a fun group you all sound like.
I'm new to letterboxing and am sort of here in limbo in the
Stevensville/St. Joseph MI area. I am moving to just out of your
area, on the otherside of the Ohio line to the Pittsburgh PA area.
We've kept a house here in MI for several years but have been hopping
across the country on temp. assignments for my husbands engineering
job. It's never too late to start, but I am a little disappointed
that I'm just learning about letterboxing. In the past five years
we've moved between here, WI, CA and AZ. I could have logged in alot
of stamps. However I kept pretty busy out west with rock hounding.
I found a bottle in AZ that contained an old claim for a gold mining
stake. That was a treasure box! It was an old green 'Bufferin'
bottle from the sixties with a white plastic cap. I was struck by
how well things weather out there as opposed to here in all this damp
green stuff.

Anyway, enough of my rambles. I came across an LBNA site link from
the "ModernGypsy" website, as one of my hobbies is altering books and
mixed media arts. I went looking for a box in Kalamazoo and have
been recieving some helpful insights from Azobox via e-mail. I hope
to locate some more while here and have some places staked out for
searching in PA. Tis heady stuff, all this adventure business :)
Nice to meet you all,
Milly in MI

by the way, my compliments on your logo picture, impressive design!
