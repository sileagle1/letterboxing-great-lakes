From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-11-14 14:47:15 UTC
My husband and I went out of town for the weekend, and the kids spent
the weekend with Grandma and Grandpa. When we picked them up last
night, Do-Re-Mi Gal and Ball Magnet informed us that they had taken
Grandma and Grandpa out letterboxing! They looked up some clues and
went out for a little walk. They looked for "Biker's Path" but the
box wasn't there, which I believe since the kids are good at finding
the boxes--and there are several "attempts" listed on the Web site.
They did find Scott & Lisa's Wedding Box, so Grandma and Grandpa got
to experience the thrill of finding a box. No word as of yet if they
will be joining us in this obsession called letterboxing.
