From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-29 21:15:48 UTC
Wow, I thought the door was pretty much closed on the Bingo game for
new players; I'm glad to see that the reach is widening.

Lest your forget, there is also an under-the-radar game also with a
deadline approaching: Side-Wagers.
Even if you're not in the BIG GAME, you can still play in the
side-wager game. It's kind of like the office pool, but you don't have
to pay to play.

When do you think SpringChick will the pull the item that completes
the winning player's list for Bingo?

Email me (jparkerg at yahoo dot com)with "Bingo" in the subject line.
Just tell me the date when you think SC's Letterbox Bingo will end.
For example, if you think the game will end on February 9, send me
that date. If it ends on the 13th, and no one else has claimed a date
before that (between the 9th and the 13th), you're the winner. In
other words, you "hold" all days from your date until the next
guesser's date comes into play. So there are lots of chances to win.
(And, let me tell you, some of the entries are pretty wild guesses!)

Questions?
Give a shout and I'll try to answer.
Deadline is Saturday night 12/31 at 11:59 p.m. EST.

azobox

Hmmmm......interesting observation and an interesting question. And,
in this case I can't pass up a rhetorical.
The "wildness" was merely an acknowledgment that the dates were all
over the map -- from kind of close to the first possible winning date
(the end of Janaury being the earliest theoretical date that someone
could win) to a more pessimistic date in April.)
Or, Perhaps the April guesser felt optimistic! Hence, "wildness".

I'm undecided about posting a database or other listing of players'
guesses. I kind of like the idea of having each player be the only one
except me who knows whose dates has come -- and gone. Though I'm
thinking about other schemes of announcing who is still in or who may
be out as the days go on. But I'll give it some thought over the next
day or so.

p.s. The example dates I gave were without any regard to dates chosen
or not chosen by anyone. I have just noted the chosen date as they
came in to me and checked for duplicates.
The dates *may* have had significance.
Who knows?
