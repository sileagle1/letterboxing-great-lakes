FYI - Crosswind Marsh letterbox is alive and well. Could not find Bald
Eagle stamp at Crosswind Marsh Park but did see 3 Bald Eagles - one
adult just sitting in the tree with that big white head and two very
LARGE babies. If you go, the nest is visable from the parking lot -
look for a pile of logs in a dead tree.

Has anyone found Mirkwood's Jabberwocky or Bob White this year?
Details are at: http://www.ruthannzaroff.com/letterboxing/index.htm

I really like her art work and would like to find more, but alas I only
found one of four today - but the eagles were worth the walk.
