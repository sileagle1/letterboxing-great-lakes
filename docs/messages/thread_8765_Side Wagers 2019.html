Side Wagers 2019

(thanks to Azobox, Wisconsin Hiker, TJ Mich, Atom 118, and all other previous Side Wager coordinators!)

 

Side Wager: A wager made beyond the normal rules of the game. A hedge bet.

 

If you'd again like to add more excitement to the letterboxing bingo game (or another way to win), enter the Side Wagers Game. When will AtomicBeans pull the term that finally and completely fills in some player's card? Pick the right day, and you WIN! Again, this is the Big Game here, not the interim games. The date will be determined by the claiming of a successful full card by a BINGO player (and its certification by AtomicBeans).

 

To enter the side wagers game, decide what day you think the first full card winner will be crowned.  Send me your guesses.

 

Your guess must be submitted before January 1, 2019.

Email: baliddletoo@yahoo.com

 

PLEASE DO NOT POST TO THE YAHOO BOARD

 

In the subject line, put "Bingo Sidewagers Game"

In the body, please indicate:

1.   the date you have selected as a first choice,

2.   one additional backup date

3.   your trail name.

4.   your email address

 

If multiple people in your household use the same trail name, you can submit one entry for each person.  Please differentiate the entries to clarify each entry.  For example:  Team Rabbit: Brown Rabbit; Team Rabbit: Rainy Day Rabbit; or Hartx6: Becki and Hartx6: Matt, etc.

 

If two or more players choose the same date as a 1st choice, the first entry is given the date and the 2nd choice backup date will be assigned to anyone else who chooses the initial date.   If both of a player’s choices have already been selected by other entrants, they will be assigned a date close to their 1st choice.  This will be done by moving earlier *or* later until the next available date is found.

 

After you submit your entry, I will email you with confirmation of your officially selected date.  Each person will be assigned one date only.

 

Once the bingo game gets underway, you will see me posting some mysterious messages.  These messages are clues to who is currently in the running for side wagers fame.  If you figure out the messages, please do not post answers to the message board in order to keep the mystery alive for others.

 

The winner of the side wagers game is the person who is closest to the day (without going over) the Bingo Game ends or hits the date on the dot.  For example: If you choose February 15th, and someone else chooses the 12th, but the game ends on the 13th…  you will win.

 

The winner gets fame, glory and bragging rights.  As a bonus, a prize will be awarded to the lucky winner!

 

Finally, no purchase necessary to enter, and you do not need to be a player in

the BINGO game to participate.  One entry per person.  Neither BaliWho, nor her aloof calico sidekick Chia, assume any responsibility for the order of words chosen by Atomic Beans! AtomicBeans has no responsibility or knowledge of the dates selected in the side wagers game. Send questions to BaliWho.
