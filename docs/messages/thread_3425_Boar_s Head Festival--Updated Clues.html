From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-12-06 19:47:03 UTC
I've just updated the clues on LbNA and AQ for our Boar's Head Festival
Series. Boxes 1-4 are still out in the woods of River Forest. Box #5
will only be available at the church on the 2 days of the performance--
and that is this weekend (Dec 9 & 10)!! More info on the Boar's Head
Festival in general and the clues to this last box are in the posted
clues.

If you can find time in your busy December schedules to come, I think
you would really enjoy it. We had one of our big rehearsals last
night, and we are really looking forward to the performances--so much
fun and also a wonderful way to personalize the birth of the Savior.

Some of us will be easier to spot than the others. Do-Re-Mi Gal is
the only one of us who is a "solo" performer--she is the Star of the
East. Ball Magnet is one of the many singing waits, although he is
the taller little boy who is front and center during their dance.
Money Man is one of many shepherds--he is also up front during their
scene (green robe with a big furry vest). During the dress
rehearsal, he put on a pair of shades to protect his eyes from the
brilliant light of the angels--the director got a big chuckle out of
that, but I don't think he'll be repeating that little stunt for the
performances :~) We serving wenches all look pretty much the same--
my dress is drab blue with pink sleeve inserts and with a hot pink
shift underneath. You may see me lurking at the back of the center
aisle during the performance as I am the stage manager in charge of
sending people in at the right time. I really think you'll enjoy it!
