From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2009-01-22 04:37:38 UTC
My dear Tiptoe--I hope you're not getting "Dark Shadows" under your
eyes as you think about your Bingo woes. And I pray that you don't
become a "Desperate Housewife" and do something that would put
you "Six Feet Under". Remember, you are a "Survivor" and "Happy
Days" are sure to comesometime??

Granted, you may have been a little "Clueless" when you chose your
Bingo words, and you card needs an "Extreme Makeover".
Unfortunately, "The Facts of Life"and the Bingo factsprevent you
from changing your card. But it is NOT a "Mission Impossible". It
is time to "Get Smart" and become a "Designing Woman" and figure out
your strategy!!

Now, the voodoo doll might be a little harsh; perhaps you should try
a gentler approach (like "Bewitch"-ing Old Shoe) to get one of your
words called. If that fails, you could use some "Dirty Sexy Money"
to bribe Old Shoe and turn him into "The Six Million Dollar Man". I
almost hate to suggest this, but you may need to consider those more
severe methodsa well-aimed kick may turn him into a "Soprano" AND
convince him to call your words. Before doing anything rash, I would
counsel you to think it over carefully for at LEAST "60 Minutes".

If none of these methods work, you may need to do something to take
your mind off your troubles. A "Home Improvement" project? Time
with "Friends" (after all, you shouldn't be a "Lone Ranger" during
such a stressful time). Or maybe a tripI hear that "Hawaii Five-O"
is a real "Fantasy Island".

Just take it "One Day at a Time" and don't lose the audacity of
your "Chicago Hope". Keep thinking that "Today" is the day. One
day, that word will come! Don't get TOO greedy for more words (after
all, I want to win). You may decide that "Eight is Enough".

Or you might go "Zoom"-ing through that card and end up winning
a "Bonanza" of Bingo Bootie. Oryou may realize that "It Takes a
Thief" to get that Bootie. "Diff'rent Strokes" for diff'rent folks,
after all.

Rest assured that "All My Children" will give big "Cheers" when you
get that first word!

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs (who should be cleaning out the basement
instead of writing this)
