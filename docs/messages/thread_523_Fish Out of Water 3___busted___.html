Yes, they gave it back to me. I just happened to stop by to check on who
came to visit it lately, when I noticed it was missing. I saw a guy reading
a magazine nearby and almost started stalking him, thinking he was waiting
for me to leave to replace it. In the meantime, I came to my senses and
went over to where Papillon was standing and told her it wasn't there.
Without going into too many details here, I discovered what I related below.
I just can't figure out how they actually found it. As you know I've been
monitoring my clues by requesting people ask me for them. I did get a
request for FOOW3 & 5 on Monday. I wonder if that was actually a "spy"!?
The hiding place was too obscure without knowledge of the clues...they
wouldn't have just randomly stumbled across it.

Again, I don't want to go into details here, but there is a lesson to be
learned. There was a person looking for the box...that person feels badly
about the situation, but I am not blaming him/her (and I am saying this
publicly!). The topic of searching for a letterbox did come up...an
employee of the establishment had read Time, so was familiar with the
concept, and actually was excited that there was a box on-site. But the
lesson is, don't volunteer more info than needed. Stealth and secrecy! Lie
like a lion...never mind what Mom taught you about being an honest person!
Say you lost a pen, a contact lens, whatever....

And again...you know who you are, we spoke privately. Do not feel bad...I
hold no ill feelings...it's only a game! #3's gonna rise again!
