From: (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2009-12-16 16:18:49 UTC-05:00

Fo6, actually you can send things to other cities to have them post marked there. You would send everything in a larger padded envelope or box addressed to the Post Master General then the city/state/zip.

The Post Master would open and cancel everything inside with their post mark.

This happens A LOT esp. this time of year with towns with names that correspond to Christmas. I know Joy, IL has a special postage cancellation image that is drawn and voted on each year and they get a HUGE amount of mail this time of year -- oh, and Joy, IL is a *little* podunk spot of earth that just so happens to have a P.O.

This is how I believe MM is getting their Post Cards out... sending them to the Post Master General in each town that corresponds to their Post Cards.

hx6
