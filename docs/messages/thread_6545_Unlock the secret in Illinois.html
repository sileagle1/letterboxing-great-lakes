11 months ago I got myself into what has become one of the largest projects I've ever worked on. A project much bigger than myself . . . a project that I alone could not do.

With the help of Pitties & Shorty, we are about to see this project to fruition.

The past year has been amazing!! I can't tell you the thousands of hours spent on-line researching and getting just the right image. Or the hundreds of hours spent carving . . . or the 4 days it took me to type everything up into a nice story clue.

Just so you know, you can multiply the above paragraph three times over!!!! This project, with the exception of the logbooks (shorty & pitties did all of those -- thanks guys!) has been equally carried out by all three of us. I am blessed to have worked with Pitties & Shorty on this project ---- and blessed that we have another LARGE project brewing with the GLG!!!

Now that the end is so close, all my carvings are done, all my story/clues are done. Now all that is left is the big reveal . . . Pitties, Shorty & I have been dreaming of the big reveal for months now!

We pushed ourselves to get this all done, well before GLG -- giving you all something to look forward to -- better yet, giving you a reason to come back to Illinois and our neck of the woods.

This has been our baby for nearly a year, we trust you all to take good care of it. Soon, this project will be just as much yours as it is ours!
