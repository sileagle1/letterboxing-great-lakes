No spooks at the Lake Eerie Gathering Saturday!

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-10 07:33:59 UTC-07:00
Just RAIN!!! The weathermen said that it would clear
up by the afternoon. Weathermen, however, lie. The
rain just got worse! But, despite the weather, it was
another great gathering. Polly/Bibliophile,
Patty/QuiltsNBees & Fran/Cinniboxer did an amazing
job. Each attendee had a hand-made log book - the
time it must have taken to make each of those! There
was bottled water with special "Lake Eerie Gathering"
labels, and a goody bag for everyone that any
letterboxer would have appreciated. As we were told
when we first arrived at the pavilion, "Keep your eyes
open, there are clues everywhere" - including on the
back of our name tags!

We showed up just as exchanges were beginning. As
always, it was great meeting people and being able to
say, "Oh, so you're So-and-So." And finding out how
people got their trailnames and how they got
interested in letterboxing is always fun, too.
Exchanging led up to lunch and our guest speaker
(whose name I don't remember!) but she spoke about
ghosts and energies and things of that nature. Then
the boxing began. It was amusing playing "leap frog"
with SunFleur & her husband (whose trailname of course
I can't remember and I'm here at work so I don't have
his stamp image with me to check) from Dearborn, MI
while looking for the boxes.

We had about 4 or 5 more boxes to go when the rain
decided to make life very difficult for us. We
decided to call it a day and headed back to the
pavilion for hot cider & s'mores. We also got a few
more exchanges with some other later arrivals. I
think this will be our new strategy for gatherings -
get exchanges and event stamps later in the day, when
people are back at the pavilion but just don't want to
leave yet :)

I know that Polly's on this list, so a great big THANK
YOU for such an amazing day. Even with the rain, it
was a hauntingly good time.

Doodle Bug.
