Bad News - No stamping for a while - Post GLG disaster

From: WheretheWildThingsR! (team name) (krysteny24@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-20 02:14:27 UTC
It wasn't even two weeks after I was down for GLG when I went back
down to hit some boxes in Columbus and visit a friend. I went back on
the 16th trying to catch a few of the boxes that were there for one
more day. While dropping my luggage at my friend's apartment, I had to
get my letterboxing pack out of my car to get my clothes out. I sat it
on the ground and dragged my clothes up stairs, when I came back it
was GONE! Someone actually had STOLEN my stamps and inkpads plus a few
granola bars but did not get into my unlocked car! My own stupidity
for leaving it next to my car, but my trunk was open and nothing was
taken out of there. I've decided never to travel alone again, I'm too
much of an accident-magnet to do so.

That's my disaster story, I don't even have prints of the stamps I've
made so I've been searching for those, and then to re-stock on ink and
carving tools, ugh.

Kat
