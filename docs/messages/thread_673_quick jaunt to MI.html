quick jaunt to MI

From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-13 16:54:05 UTC-07:00
Saturday, Ugler and I headed up to Southern MI for yet
another letterboxing adventure. We started with A
Funny Name Sturgis Box...looked and looked and could
not find it. Discouraged, we headed to find First in
the 'Water...looked and looked and could not find it.
(turns out that it is confirmed missing). Even more
discouraged, and itching madly from some evil
plants... we decided to try the Intrigue at Aiden Lair
Series in Somerset Center. SUCCESS AT LAST! What an
amazing park! We only found 4 of seven...but just the
trip alone was worth it! There are some phenominal
works of art on the grounds, in the form of bridges.
Spectacular! Truly a special place...and we enjoyed it
immensely.

--Armadillo Jo, who didn't get poison ivy...but is
suffering from horrible allergies...
