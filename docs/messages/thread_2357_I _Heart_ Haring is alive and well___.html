From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-02 02:05:22 UTC
We finally got out to see what was going on in the park where our
I "Heart" Haring series is. The two poles that we mention in our
clues are gone, but there is no permanent damage done and our boxes
are intact!!!! We have updated the clues so if anyone has not yet
done this series, you may want to check that your clues are the most
current before heading out, otherwise you might be really confused.

We will keep you guys posted on the status of Sternennacht when we get
that series replaced and box #2 recarved.

Only 28 more days left to hunt eggs!

Happy Boxing,
