Copied from msg. #5739 (12/2/05):

In a nutshell, what it is... I've put together a master list of
letterboxing terms, box names, trail names, etc. From this list of
150 items, you will choose 30 items based on the criteria explained
in the instructions. You will keep 1 copy of your list and send 1
copy to me along with 1 item to go into the "Bingo Booty" prize
package. Beginning January 1, 2006, I will call out 1 item from the
master list each day -- if that item is on your list, you cross it
off. The first person to mark off all 30 of the items on their list
is the winner and gets the Bingo Booty.

************

If you go back in the message archives beginning Dec. '05, you will
find several posts about last year's Bingo game.
