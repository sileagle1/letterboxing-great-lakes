From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-19 06:54:45 UTC-08:00
If you want to camp at Alum Creek State Park amongst
fellow letterboxers at Campground 1 (this is the one
that allows dogs), there are still sites available.
As of this morning, it looks like there are 2 sites
left in Row M, and several sites in Row J (J32 - J40)
which are close to Row M.

To reserve a site, visit
http://www.ohiodnr.com/parks/parks/alum.htm

You can reserve your site online, or by calling their
1-866 number. You will have to reserve 2 nights
(either Friday & Saturday or Saturday & Sunday). The
total for the 2 nights will be $62.

Once you reserve your site, add an entry to the GLG
2007 Camping Database (located at our Yahoo Groups
page under databases) to let everyone know where we
can find you at the campground.

Hope to see you there!

Doodle Bug.

You may want to check the maps in the Files section of the message
board (in the > Gatherings > 2007 Great Lakes Gathering folder).
There is a map of the park and the campground. If you want to stay
near many of the other people attending the event, you might want to
reserve a site in the J32-J40 range. You can find out where everyone
else is camping by looking in the Database section of the message
board. Here you can see a list of people who have already reserved a
