A good note was made to me and I thought Id share.

It might be a good idea if some of you bring washcloths, with water only in a baggie to the event. I imagine most of you will either have brown/gray markers/pads, or metallic pads... With the metallic, the ink can build up, especially with so many will be stamping them. So it might be good to have a damp washcloth(water only please, other products can harm stamp, water will do the trick) on the trail just in case the stamp looks a bit filled with ink.
That's all!

See ya in June, so close-24 days!!!
