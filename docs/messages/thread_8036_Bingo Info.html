There is a database of all Bingo words called and it is generally current.  You can find it by clicking on the "More" tab under the message board cover photo, then clicking on "Databases".  Another option for getting the word everyday is to go to the Yahoo group and edit your Membership settings.  Click on "Membership" under the LbGlk logo, and then click on "Edit Membership".  Click on the little pencil next to "Identity" and then enter any email address you would like the message board posts to be sent to.  If you enter an email address that you look at daily, you'll be sure to see the word each day.  You may also want to be sure that your Subscription is set to Individual Email (click pencil next to Subscription, then choose "Individual Email" for the Message Delivery option).

 

There is also a database of all the Bingo players.  We just got back from Florida and I have updated it based on all the mail and packages we received while we were gone.  I've received the $2 entry fee and booty from a bit more than half the players so far.  Please check the database to see if your info looks correct.  Hopefully everyone else will either give their stuff to ggghiker at DoW or have it in the mail to me within the next few days.  We'll be out of town again later this month and I would like to have all the Bingo booty before we leave so we don't have any packages arriving while we are gone.

 

Thanks!
