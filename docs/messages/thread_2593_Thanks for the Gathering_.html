Thanks for the Gathering!

From: the_drama_momma (dee4drama@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-05 14:41:28 UTC
Hi everyone! Have you recovered from this weekend's Gathering yet?
LOL! Lots of fun and a lot of hiking! Thanks go out to Wisconsin Hiker
and Martini Man for organizing such a great time!

So good to see people I recognized from the last gathering and meeting
new people! Big H and Lil' H, my hubby loved the coffeecake!

We may have a new LB'er in my family. My son came with me but didn't
want to go look for boxes. That's fine cuz he's a teen and they seem
to "poo-poo" their parents' ideas. LOL! He helped with cooking (being
a boy scout he has had lots of training) so no complaints from me. But
he went out with me, Shooting star, Mr. Conductor and Madame Butterfly
in the early evening. He had a great time and even lead the way on a
couple of boxes! On the ride home, he was thinking of trail names and
stamp images for himself!

Thanks again for so much fun packed into one day!
Drama Momma
