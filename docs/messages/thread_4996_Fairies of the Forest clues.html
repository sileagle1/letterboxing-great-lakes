It was a great event. It was our first and we had lots of fun and LOVED meeting the people we read about on this group. Opposite of others, we spent too much time having fun meeting everyone and not much time out on the trail! Oops. We didn't end up finding the one box we set out for, but we are planning on coming back on anothercamping trip. We did find one at Ionia State Park later that day (that's where we camped for the weekend).
The stamp was beautiful, the park was absolutely incredible (perfect for the theme)! Thank you for all of your hard work, Familyof6!
We do have one suggestion...have a couple of boxes geared towards the younger crowd. Our kids love to help, but the clues were too hard for them to follow and they ended up getting bored. It would be great to have a couple easier boxes for them to break up their day.
BTW, we spent today at the Public Museum. We didn't get there until 1 hour/45 minutes till closing. We were SOOOOO close to getting all 4, but we were kicked out at 5:05. We were looking for our paycheck!!! How long do we have to get back there before it's gone?
Glad you're feeling better, Familyof6! Thank you to everyone for helping us through our first event! We truly enjoyed meeting you all!
Looking forward to the next one,
The K Crew
