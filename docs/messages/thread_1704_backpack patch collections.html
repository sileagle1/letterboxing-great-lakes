Papeseed, I have a limited number of patches on my backpack. I have
the LbNA patch and three small ones to commemorate what were
significant PFX numbers to me.

If you're interested in seeing the patches I'm talking about, they're
on the letterboxing.org page under regalia. Here's the link:
http://www.letterboxing.org/regalia/orderforms.htm

I have stumbled across enough geocaches while either looking for a
place to put a box or while out doing some boxing, that I joined the
Mich geocache org. They also have patches and I have one of them on my
pack as well.

I'm looking forward to adding the LbGLK patch and think the dark green
one will show up better from a couple of feet away.

I appreciate your thoughts about putting out patches as FF prizes, but
speaking just for myself, I'm not likely to be adding many more
patches. I'm pretty happy with how my pack looks now. (Besides the
fact that it is a killer to hand sew through the plastic-backed nylon
on the flap of my backpack. LOL)

A PFX500 patch would be another nice commemorative, but I don't think
LbNA has one, I think it would have to be a special order.

Thanks again to Wisconsin Hiker for all the effort that's gone into
this patch project so far.

Enjoy!
