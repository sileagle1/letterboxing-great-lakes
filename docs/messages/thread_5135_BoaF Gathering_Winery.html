Re the winery in Genoa - the owners have a wine-tasting every Saturday
afternoon. And in any event, if you happen to be there on a day other than
Saturday, they usually will give you a sample nip of whatever wine you might
be considering. NamVet is quite fond of their Kishwaukee Blue (the bottle
with the blue heron on the label) but my favorite is their Prairie Dog . . .
in fact, we killed a bottle of that last night while munching on some of the
snacks/fruit/desserts we managed to bring home from the gathering.

and for anyone thinking of a visit, you might check out the
clues for the "Cogberry Wine" mystery box :)
