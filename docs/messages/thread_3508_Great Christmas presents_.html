Great Christmas presents!

From: robertsp20 (robertsp20@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-31 16:28:18 UTC
Santa was SO good to me this year! I received a gift certificate from
one son stating that he will accompany me on three letterboxing
searches so that I have a 'body guard' in remote places. I think that
is very cool. The other son gave me a gift certificate for a special
stamp that he will carve. He also wants to design a hitchhiker. He is
quite an artist, so I can't wait to see both. I'll hide very well, as
I know they will be great and I don't want somebody to walk off with
either one--stay tuned!

I am so glad that both 'kids' (22 and 24) have become interested in
letterboxing. We headed to Springfield, IL for a few days of
roadtrip, Route 66, and letterboxing. Springfield only has two, but
they provided a beautiful walk through a beautiful park on a warm and
sunny day. What great weather we've been having for letterboxing!
