From: Barb Funk (mmfunk99@greenapple.com) | Date: 2007-04-19 08:55:41 UTC-04:00
I too chuckled when I read "Whattheheckwerewethinking"! My husband and
I had 3 sons in 4 years and had intended on having 5 children
originally. It was important to me that they be born close together for
their "bonding"! Yeah, then once I realized that if I was holding the
hand of one son in one hand, and the hand of one more son in my other
hand---there would always be one more able to run free with the biggest
smile on his face! That's when that came to a halt.

FungusWoman's FunGal 2nd choice also gave me a big smile! Somehow that
should be on the plates of something RED!!! :) What is that saying???
Live long enough to embarrass your children?? ;)

Okay, Tiptoe and Tonto, this topic has hung over me all week long and
was every bit as difficult as choosing a first trailname?! I have a
couple alternates, perhaps. One I almost chose is AT~bound. I have now
chalked off my list several things I didn't do when I was younger.
Hiking the AT is still on that list. Grandma Gatewood, here from Ohio
was the first female to thru-hike the AT at the age of 63. She used the
beautiful Hocking Hills as her training ground and then after her
husband passed away, she only told her family she was going hiking?!?!
Once news spread that a grandmother was solo-hiking the trail, Reader's
Digest began tracking her....and it was then, that she decided she had
better call home to someone and let them know how big of a hike she was
on?! Anyway, she is my inspiration and ya gotta name it to claim
it---but I haven't exactly put it out there to that extent just yet.

Another name I would consider, now that I have been letterboxing for a
few years and have had my share of challenges, is "Stumped in the woods"
for those moments where I am in the middle of nowhere, clues in hand,
reading out loud (with no one around, of course) turning and looking,
rechecking my compass--and my dog wondering WHY I am tangling his leash
around my legs?!

Last, would simply be Plastic~addict! No explanation needed with that
one. :)

Maybe I'll just make a letterbox with each of these names?!

Whew, assignment turned in late, but complete!
~Trailtracker
