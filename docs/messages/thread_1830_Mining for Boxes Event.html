Mining for Boxes Event

From: Gabrielle Coil (gmc81@randomkitty.net) | Date: 2005-09-19 13:26:58 UTC-07:00
I'ld just like to send out a huge thanks to everyone who could make it to the gathering this past Saturday. We really couldn't have done it without you. It was a great bunch of people and we really couldn't have asked for better weather. Unfortunately, not everyone remembered to stamp into the gathering logbook so I can't be 100% sure about the attendance...but according to the stamp exchanges an my memory, we had 10 adults, 4 children, and 2 dogs from Illinois and Indiana. In addition, Sprite and Highlander from New York were unable to attend but were kind enough to send us a box for this event. And a special thanks to Ruth of Team Red Handed for remembering to bring a camera and being our photographer.

There was one official event stamp, one roaming event stamp, and three absolutely awsome surprise event stamps brought by Team Red Handed. The Blue Dog roaming event stamp raised $40 for hurricane relief at this event. There were boxes placed by 5 different people for this event, all but two of them permenant. Those of you who couldn't make the gathering, be on the lookout for clues to these permenant boxes sometime in the next week or two.

This was my first experience hosting a gathering and I hope that everyone enjoyed attending as much as I enjoyed hosting! Thanks again to all of the people who made this event a success. I will post pictures to the pictures section as soon as Ruth sends me some. See you on the trails!

Kimono Girl
