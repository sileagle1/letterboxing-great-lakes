Well, let's see... I got started in letterboxing way back in April of
2005. My current F count is F187. I found out about letterboxing by
stumbling across a site while looking for clipart on the internet.
As I am always looking for new and interesting things that nobody
else I know is doing, I took on the challenge. I loved it ever
since. My first box was Calatrava in Milwaukee, only a mile away
from my house. I have attended the Haunted Harvest Gathering 2005,
Great Lakes Gathering 2006 and a Micro Meet at the PAW. I have met
so many great friends through the region and can't wait to meet so
many more. I have a huge series in the works (15 boxes in total)
that are finished and waiting to be planted, so that's what I've been
focusing on for some time now.

I can't wait until Ohio GLG2007! Its gonna be a great time! If you
haven't ever been to an event, I urge you to attend. Take the time
to meet people and don't forget to box.

I know that letterboxing has kept me healthy too. I always say, if
you told me to walk 5 miles I would tell you to "bite me", but if you
tell me there is a box at the end of a 10 mile hike, heck, I'd do it
twice.

My signature stamp is an image of a Jigsaw Puzzle piece. It
symbolizes not only my love for logic and clues, but also the game
like atmosphere this hobby affords. Also, if you just look at me,
you might not realize how extremely complex I am. Take a few minutes
to talk to me and you will realize that there are many facets of my
person, just like a multi pieced puzzle, that make up the whole me.

Anyways, I look forward to being a part of this hobby for a very long
time so keep planting those boxes for me to find!! HEhe.

Always, Jigsaw
