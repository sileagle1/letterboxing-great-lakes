Letterbox Bingo!

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-11-18 17:36:51 UTC
Coming soon to the Great Lakes Yahoo! Group -- Letterbox Bingo! Will
you be the first to complete your card and win the Bingo Booty?

Q. What is Letterbox Bingo?
A. In a nutshell... more detailed instructions to follow... I will
post a master list of 125 letterboxing related items. From this
master list, each player will choose 25 items to comprise their
unique Letterbox Bingo "card." Beginning on January 1, 2006, I will
call one item from the master list each day announced in an e-mail
to the Great Lakes Yahoo! List. If that item is on your "card" you
cross it off. The first person to cross out all 25 items on their
card, wins.

Q. Is There a Prize?
A. Of course -- what fun is Bingo without prizes? Each person or
team who is playing will contribute one item to the Bingo Booty --
any item that would be useful to a letterboxer. Winner gets the Bingo
Booty! Depending on how many people are playing, the Booty may be
split up to have 2nd and 3rd place prizes also.

Q. What kinds of things do we give to the Booty?
A. Some examples (but please use your imagination):

Replacement carving nibs
Containers
A hand-made log book
Carving material
A stamp pad
Purple socks?
USE YOUR IMAGINATION!!!

Q. Who can Play?
A. Any member of the Great Lakes Yahoo! List can play. Families and
teams can play either as a group (one list of 25 items is chosen for
the group) or individually (each person chooses their own 25 items),
but each person who is playing needs to send in a separate item for
the Bingo Booty.

Watch for more detailed information to come! I will post the master
list of items during the first week of December. You will need to
choose your list of 25 items and send me your Bingo Booty items by
December 23, 2005.

Letterbox Bingo!

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-12-02 14:29:36 UTC
Okay, we're ready to roll with this! I have posted 2 files in the
FILES section of the list (they are in .PDF format so everyone should
be able to read them -- if you can't, let me know and I will get them
to you in another format):

FILES > BINGO_play.pdf explains how the game is played and how to go
about choosing your unique Letterbox Bingo list.

FILES > BINGO_list.pdf is the master list from which you will choose
items to make up your unique Letterbox Bingo list.

In a nutshell, what it is... I've put together a master list of
letterboxing terms, box names, trail names, etc. From this list of
150 items, you will choose 30 items based on the criteria explained
in the instructions. You will keep 1 copy of your list and send 1
copy to me along with 1 item to go into the "Bingo Booty" prize
package. Beginning January 1, 2006, I will call out 1 item from the
master list each day -- if that item is on your list, you cross it
off. The first person to mark off all 30 of the items on their list
is the winner and gets the Bingo Booty. If you are going to play,
you will need to get your list made up and send me a copy along with
your booty item by the end of December. I know that is a month yet,
but with the holidays and people going out of town, etc., I wanted to
make sure everyone had plenty of time.

Questions? Please read the complete instructions in the
BINGO_play.pdf file first. Feel free to e-mail me or post to the
list with questions (somebody else may be wondering the same thing...)

SpringChick
