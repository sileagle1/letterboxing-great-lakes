From: Angi (ShutterflyIL@aol.com) | Date: 2005-11-11 19:33:04 UTC
After many back and forth emails to a woman from the forest preserve, I
still have gotten nowhere in regards to finding my letterboxes. I do
know, however, that there is a box in an office somewhere that contains
some items. Whether they are geocaches or letterboxes, I'm not sure. I
was told to let her know what my boxes looked like and I explained what
they looked like on the outside as well as what the stamps and logbooks
look like. It has been quite some time since I wrote that email, so I
sent it again today. For those of you who may have boxes placed in
Winnebabo county forest preserves, you might want to get out and check
on them.

I offered to come in and check on what ones they have. If I recognize
any of them, I will definately let the owners know they are there. :)
