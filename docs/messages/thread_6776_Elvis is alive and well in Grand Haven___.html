Congrats and Kudos to SpringChick and Mrs. Doglvrs for a wonderful event yesterday at the West Michigan Fall Gathering 2010: Viva Grand Haven!
Although the day began under cloudy skies with a little precipitation, it turned out to be a great day for Letterboxing!
Once again this event will go down in LB history as a great, fun-filled event with lots of great boxes and many cool challenges along the way.
The photo requirements were a neat twist to the LB challenges of the day.
Team Roustabouts had a great time and want to express our appreciation and gratitude for all the hard work and planning that went into making it a great day for everyone.
It was great to see old LB friends again and to finally meet new LB friends face-to-face!
Happy LBing to all,
JustHappy2CYa &
