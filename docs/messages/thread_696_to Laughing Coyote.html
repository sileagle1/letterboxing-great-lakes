My "spirit animal", or totem if you wish, is the coyote. The coyote is a
trickster in native american lore, so I do love solving riddles and clues to
find boxes. I have a tattoo of a coyote on my right shoulder with a spirit
line (an arrow) on it.
When I started my newest business, a small dog biscuit bakery, I used a
variation of my tattoo as the logo. The spitit line is shorter, and curves
into a smile.
So for my trail name I chose "Laughing Coyote" and my stamp is a variation
between my tattoo and the logo for my bakery business.
