Rebirth, of a sort...

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-20 12:53:06 UTC-07:00
Oh, I remember the day Deedle & I went to visit
Marblehead Lighthouse to see if SpringChick's
letterbox planted there still existed. (It rained.
It poured. It was so bad a tree limb fell on the hood
of a minivan in the parking lot - thankfully no one
was hurt.)

Checking my email just now, I saw a notice from the
letterbox announce list that there was a new box
planted in Ottawa County, Ohio. Anytime Ohio appears
in the subject line I'm on it, even if it's nowhere
near us. Box name, Marblehead Lighthouse. "Did
SpringChick replant her box?" I wondered, knowing that
after our report back to her she officially retired
it. Here, a boxer in the area named antimony is
trying it again. Wonder if they know of the first
box? Anyway, Deedle & I had hoped to make it out the
lighthouse again one day, since it wasn't open the day
we visited. Now we have even more reason :)

Doodle Bug.
