Please read: regarding the security of your boxes.

The White Lake Library is planning a 2-week Spring Break Letterboxing
contest and has pressed me into doing a 45 minute presentation this
coming Thurs evening during Family Night. (Thanks going out to Silent
Doug, for a copy of his class outline!)

They really want this to be a contest and are going to be placing a
number of boxes in the area. They are really stressing the LOCAL
(White Lake area) aspect right now, and I have reluctantly agreed to
include my Wood and Water Nymph box in their contest. (I just don't
know how many participants to anticipate and worry about that
trail/location being damaged/overwhelmed.)

The letterboxing.org address will be included on the class kits that
will be handed out to all participants so it is possible that some of
our other area boxes could get some visitors.

I feel fairly good about this event attracting "good" newbies, since
they first have to come to the Library to obtain the info and/or kits.
However, if you have any concerns for your boxes, please let me know
or maybe disable the clues for a short time.

Thanks, and if you want a box removed temporarily, I could *TRY* to go
pick it for you and save you a drive. (Then replace it after the
contest ends in a couple of weeks.)

Enjoy!
