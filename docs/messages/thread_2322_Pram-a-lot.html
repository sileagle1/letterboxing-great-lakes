My sister, Maple Seed, and I forded Beaver Creek above the falls to discover "Sir
Galahad."

Alack and alas, though we searched for the infant placed under a flat-topped rock by
a silly knight who "Push[ed] the Pram-a-lot" we could not find the child. We did
discover a writing quill possibly left by another knight. We left the quill where we
thought to find the infant, hoping others might have more wisdom or better ears with
which to locate the child.

Your humble servant,
