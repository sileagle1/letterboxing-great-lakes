Hello All,

Do to many different reasons we have not been very active on this
board, or on the trails recently. I was able to comment on the
Museum Boxes because that thread happened to be on the home page
when I was able to find some "online" time. Today at work, I found
some more time (which is very rare) and was able to catch up on
reading some digests. If you will all indulge me for just a moment
I have some comments to add to recent topics.

First to respond to this post:

> So, how are those BabIES coming along?? I don't think I
mentioned that we
> met your relatives (Domedreamers?) while we were in Connecticut in
> mid-September.
>
> Wisconsin Hiker


Everyone is doing very well, our doctor is very happy and Mrs.
Cottontails is very round (hee hee). I don't remember if we let
people know, but we now know the sex of both babies....A Boy AND A
Girl!! Our lives have become very hectic getting our very small
house ready for two more people. We have had to move everything out
of our second bedroom, which meant finding a new home for the
computer, and then the dinning room table, and then the couch, yada
yada yada. We also seem to spend all of our time between doctor
visits and ultrasounds. All of this was compounded two weeks ago
when the doctor decided that Mrs. Cottontails needed to stop working
and take it easy starting tomorrow (Friday) which meant making sure
everything was ready in her classroom.
Yes, the Domedreamers are my sister and brother-in-law, they were
very happy/excited to meet you as they have heard us go on and on
about your gatherings. It is their fault we discovered this
incredible hobby.

On that note... I wish that was the way everyone was introduced to
the hobby. We had someone who took us around and showed us the
right way to box. The articles in magazines and newspapers and the
Girl Scout badge, I am sure are good things that are also well
intentioned. However, I also feel that they fall short in really
explaining things. Many of you know about the problems that have
befallen our boxes, we recently pulled Sternenacht after box 3
disappeared again. Two bonus boxes (on different series) have gone
missing recently. We think that we will recarve box 3 again, but we
are toying with the idea of just hiding those boxes in our yard and
restricting access to the clues. We are also toying with the idea
of doing something similar to FungasWoman with our clues. Only time
will tell.

To Cmoon and Kookaburra...


I am very saddened to hear of the loss of your boxes, many of those
were our first finds, and also our favorites. Your boxes really
helped get us addicted to this hobby. History Tower was one of our
favorite hiding places. I hope that once our team is ready we will
still have Cmoon and Kookaburra boxes to find.

Last...

I don't remember who, but someone was looking for a place to have
their stamps remade for them. If you contact me offlist I can help
you with that, it is what I do (sort of).

Also, if anyone has contacted me about boxes recently, I am sorry
that I have not responded, I just haven't had the time. I am going
to try to catch up on my correspondence in the coming weeks.



Thanks for listening and Keep boxing,


The Cottontails
