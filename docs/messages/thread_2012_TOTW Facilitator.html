I'm still looking for somebody who would be willing to facilitate the
TOTW (Topic of the Week).

Thinking about it, but not sure what you would have to do?

It's pretty simple... Once a week you would post a new TOTW.
Generally to get the ball rolling, it helps to give your own input on
the topic. Topics can be anything that comes to mind in reference to
letterboxing or letterboxers -- use your own ideas, ask for ideas from
the group, bring up topics you've seen discussed at other LB Yahoo!
groups. It is a great way to get involved with the group and learn
more about letterboxing and your fellow letterboxers.

If I don't hear from anyone, I will just drop it and the TOTW will not
be revived at this time.

Thanks!
