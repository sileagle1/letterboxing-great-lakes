GLG 2018 - Indiana

From: (jlsd0218@aol.com) | Date: 2017-08-02 14:48:55 UTC

Hello!  We are very interested in hosting in Southern Indiana.  We have a county park, Scales Lake Park in Boonville, IN that needs lots of letterboxes!  This facility is a campground with a lake electric/water hookups, shower houses,  cabins and a venue to host such get togethers!  There are lots of hotel accommodations just 15 minutes away in Evansville, IN, which, not to mention has over 200 letterboxes hidden throughout the city!!  


We (Jlsd0218, 4J's and BAMM) are three Girl Scout leaders that absolutely love letterboxing together!  We were introduced to this in 2014 with our Brownie Scouts and have been obsessed ever since.  We have attended two events and met wonderful people whom love this hobby as much as we do and some probably more!  Once we heard about the GLG event, our minds started swarming with ideas and really hope that we can host this event in 2018.  Should you have any questions, or would like us to host, please let us know as soon as possible :) I have included some links for you to view:)


https://www.facebook.com/scaleslakepark/

http://www.warrickcountyparks.com/scales-lake-park/


Thank you for considering us up to such a wonderful event!


Lindsey, Jamie and Melissa

jlsd0218, 4J's and BAMM
