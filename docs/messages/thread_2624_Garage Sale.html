Garage Sale

From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-08 03:05:33 UTC
Hello Everyone,

Now that we've almost fully recovered from all the hiking LAST
weekend, we've been getting ourselves organized for the Garage Sale
that we're having THIS weekend.

We have carved a new stamp in honor of this event. Yes, we know of
the Rummage Sale Box, but forgot to request it in time to have it at
our sale, so we've made a new one.

We've posted the clues to the box on AQ and LbNA. We're also
including them in this post. Hope to see a couple of you this weekend.

CLUES:
This letterbox will only be available on Friday, June 9 and Saturday,
June 10 during the hours of 7am and 4pm.

Start from the park where The Cottontails had their Easter Egg Hunt.
Look for a street near the park that an Old Mother would call home.
Between a street that can fly and a more formal Larry, you'll need to
head south to find us. Look for the East-facing house that has only
half of a tree near the driveway.

When you find us, ask us if we have any shopping carts.


GOOD LUCK!!
The Cottontails

I know it's a little last minute but if anyone is going to be in Northern Illinois we will be having our annual garage sale this Friday, June 22 from 9am to 4pm. We had Dragon's garage sale box here last year so this year we thought we'd do something different. In honor of my son's, first day off of school mishap, you will need to find him at the sale and ask if he has a Sharpie to sign his cast. You will not only have that honor but also receive his 'cast' stamp.

As an added bonus, I am currently hosting Doyle the Boxing Buddy. He is really looking forward to the sale (although he will be staying far away from the pile of stuffed animals for sale) and would love an exchange if you visit. If you think you might be in the area, send me a note off list and I'll get you the address. Thanks everyone!
