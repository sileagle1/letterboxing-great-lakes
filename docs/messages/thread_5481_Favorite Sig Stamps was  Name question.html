First, I'd like to say that I love all signature stamps! They represent the
person who holds them. I look back on some with nostalgia such as DMS's,
NamVet's, Kraits & Dogllvrs. Some I get a kick out of such as Big Purple Foots (I
just love the new look with the girl with a big purple foot... very
fitting), Two Free Timers & Gooble Gobble. Some Sig stamps so closely represent a
trail name with an image such as Wisconsin Hiker, Garfan & PackerBackers.

However, the sig. stamp that I always go back to and just stare at is
ArtGekko's new siggy that was introduced at the "Great Lakes Gathering 2008: Land
of 10,000 Letterboxes" Included in the image is her Trail Name & city/state in
one of the smallest fonts I've ever seen carved.

The stamp itself is 2" x 1.5" and is PACKED with details, carved with a
hypodermic needle!!

I'd have to say the incredibly detailed ArtGekko Signature stamp is my
favorite.
