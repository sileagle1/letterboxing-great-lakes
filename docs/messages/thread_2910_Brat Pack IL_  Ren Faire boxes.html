From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-08-07 15:33:12 UTC-05:00
I've been to the Renaissance Faire twice. My first visit was 2 weeks ago. We went on vacation and as soon as we were back home, I wasn't even unpacked, but I went back to the Ren Faire yesterday. Even though it was drizzling a tad bit in the morning, I had the time of my life! But I will warn you, be prepared to walk around with your eyes wandering and your mouth hanging open. There is magic everywhere you look! As for letterboxing at the Ren Faire, it's a challenge in sneakiness. We don't want others in the crowd to observe what we are doing because there are watchful eyes everywhere. I'm not sure if I will be working next weekend or not, but if I'm free, I may stop back at the faire. I'm hooked on having fun!

Beetle

Re: Brat Pack IL: Ren Faire boxes

From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-08-08 00:11:40 UTC
We are lucky when it comes to secrecy - to start, Beetle and
KissmeKate gave us some tips on being annoying to make people go
away ;P and on how to pick things up without looking like an
oddball. Allthough, the Faire is not a place where many are there to
judge Im sure. LOL

We are ALSO lucky in that we letterbox with a 2 year old. Lets us
sit at the trailside and dig through bags without looking like we
are up to something. We can also look like we are searching for
something we dropped... all kinds of fun 2 year old related excuses
for milling around the same spot, or moving to a different one.

When hes with, no one looks twice! (And hes always with! Watch for
us walking - or riding - with a red blue and yellow bike
trailer/stroller. LOL)

Yesterday at the Faire, when the rain was coming down - the handy
umbrella worked wonders on hiding in plain site! Another bonus to
rainy-day boxing!

Paula
