Letterboxing everywhere....

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-10 08:55:58 UTC-07:00
Even when we're not letterboxing. Friday night,
Deedle & I decided to have a tailgate picnic in his
pickup truck at the one nature reserve lake around
here. After we were done eating, we decided to
explore the one trail around the lake, and of course,
as we're walking, we start doing the "Hey, if we were
to place a letterbox here, we could say to look for
this in the clues..." and before we knew it, we had
complete clues to plant a new box...now we just need
to create the box to put there :)

And then, last night we were in Dick's Sporting Goods.
Don't know if any of you are familiar with the "Life
is Good" t-shirts (they have a simple picture on the
front, usually involving the creator's stick figure
Jake, and then a short line of text under the graphic,
usually the phrase "life is good."). They were on
sale, so I started to flip through them, and found one
with hiking boots for the graphic, and the phrase "Not
all who wander are lost" on it. I giddily ran over to
Deedle - "Wouldn't this be a perfect Letterboxing
shirt?" Needless to say, I'm wearing it today....

In 2 weeks we go letterboxing over-board; we're headed
to Southern Ohio and the Chillicothe area to find a
slew of boxes planted out that way. Thanks to those
who personally emailed us with tips for our trip!

Doodle Bug.
