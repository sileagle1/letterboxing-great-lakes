Gino's

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-08-10 19:54:07 UTC
Thanks to Cherokee Rose 2 for organizing the get-together at Gino's last night! It was nice to meet Team BearCat, SZSRocks and her daughter The Tree Hugger.

Our poor waitress was pretty patient as we spent so much time doing exchanges and stamping the great Gino's event stamp that CR2 carved into our logbooks! We eventually did look at the menus and ordered. Team BearCat was quick off the mark in getting some appetizers ordered for the table and then we ordered more to tide us over until the yummy deep dish pizzas arrived. Of course we all spent the time chatting about the hot weekend of boxing and other recent LB adventures.

It's been fun to meet so many visiting boxers this summer! I wonder if we'll see more yet this year???

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. We may have recruited the MN folks into coming down for the Science Fiction event in Waukesha on OCT 3, or they might contribute a box if they can't attend. It looks like the box list for the event is growing FAST!
