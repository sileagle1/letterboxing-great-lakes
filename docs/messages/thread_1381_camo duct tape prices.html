Sam Walton's kids just bought the National Park Service after a very intense

It's just as well. Gates was going to give out free computers to any

Sam's kids hate letterboxing. They still want to make a buck so they're not
going to stop selling the camo tape however. Just up the price a bit.

They've also taken a hit because, for the camo tape, they have to buy the
"Made in America" stuff. Too much suspicion on camo tape coming in from
other countries.

On a serious note tho --

I had mentioned to a friend that the local Walmart here was recently out of
the camo tape. She brought a couple of rolls with her when she flew in for
a visit. She also brought some of those lock 'n' locks. She won't do that
again. She must have looked suspicious because she was one of the ones
whose bag they chose to inspect pretty carefully. She said that they were
asking her a lot of very serious, not-so-nice questions about what the camo
tape and boxes were for. She's the smart-aleck type, but didn't get smart
with them thank heavens. So her time there explaining letterboxing was a
lot shorter than it could have been.

I'll be they wouldn't have challenged Ted Turner if he was walking on a
plane with camo tape and lock 'n' lock boxes.
