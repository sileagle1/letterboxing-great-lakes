From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-02-18 14:24:40 UTC
Just wanted to say a huge thanks to Mrs. Doglvrs for putting this
little event together and providing space for us to hang out all day
and to those who came out in the snowy weather. Although our numbers
were small, just 5 of us (Mrs. Doglvrs, Cindy from DUCA family,
Stargirl, WhereTheWildThingsAre and me), it was an enjoyable, change-
of-pace gathering. We did a lot of idea sharing, Stargirl made her
first Monkey Journals, WhereTheWildThingsAre carved her first stamps
(we wouldn't let her leave until she had stamps to exchange) and we
all found time to collect a few stamps and work on carvings, etc.
Thanks again all! I'm hoping we can make this an annual event!

SpringChick

What a wonderful time I had on Saturday! It was small, yes - but lots got
accomplished!!
I carved only 1 and 1/2 stamps (I was going for 9) - but I got as much
talking done as I possibly could! (always my favorite part of a gathering)

I got to hear the voices of some of my dearest friends from across the lake!
WooHOO!! : ) ("Is this a video phone?")

I would just love to make this an annual event - the snow tends to get me
down when it drags on...we could think of it like "Mid-winter break" -- what
fun! I'm happy to host it too!

Thanks for making an otherwise gloomy Saturday a bright spot in my February!
