Bristol RF update

From: Katelyn Burrill (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-14 15:28:51 UTC
I'm sorry I missed everyone at the Faire this weekend! I did see
Beetle for a moment or two during our Wench Walk, but retired behind
the scenes to overcome a bout of illness (which turned out to be
caused by my outfit not being properly adjusted - oops). Barefoot
Ankh enjoyed meeting and exchanging with the Brat Pack and seeing the
Cottontails, even though he couldn't get out of the shop. I was out
and about all day on Sunday as well, so I've been particularly
difficult to find, although I ran into RaqsEnigma and DerMadStamper
briefly!

After taking nearly 2 weeks to get William Shakespeare replaced, I
was lucky enough to find RaqsEnigma and ask her about where she found
William. Turns out he was lost in the high landscaping in another
nearby area, so Dragon's original is back where he belongs and my
replacement has been pulled unless he's needed again. Huzzah! I will
combine the logbooks as well, so there's no need to make another trip
unless you really want to see if there's a difference in the stamps!

I'm considering an addition to my costume to make me a little more
obvious to visiting boxers. I was thinking of making a sash with a
6" or 7"-square LB NA logo on each end, either quilted or
embroidered. This might be easier to spot me at the Faire, since I'm
not as obvious to spot as the other letterpeople, I'm not usually at
the shop, and my outfit changes depending on the weather and my
activities. Would that help?

Hope to see more boxers this season!

One thing I've learned about looking for people at Faire. You can
chase someone around the entire Faire (which covers ~40 acres) and
never find them. It's better to sit in one place and wait for them to
go by again! Sorry I missed you!

Unlike me, Barefoot Ankh only uses that name for boxing, but if he's
working at the shop (usually only Saturdays), his doublet is either
red or blue. Sylvie-Rose seems to have made the decision to work in
the "normal" world for most weekends, but has announced she will be at
Faire for closing weekend (Sept 2-4). The best chance to find me near
Royal Exchange (I don't work there and usually am wandering about) is
between 12:30 and 2:00. That's when the wenches gather for our Faire
activities.

THREE more weekends to find Dragon's great series! Hope to meet more
LBers soon!
