Hello from SpartyNYC and FJBANI

From: spartynyc (cey3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-12 18:41:51 UTC
Hello,
I just wanted to introduce myself to the board. My wife and I have
been letterboxing for close to a year now in both the Traverse City
Area(parents live on Old Mission Peninsula) and in New York City(we
live here). I found out about letterboxing after reading about it in
Time magazine and was amazed at how many letterboxes were all around
us. Especially the Urban ones. Anyways, we got hooked and we managed
to hook our parents into it as well.

This past week we managed to do most of the ones in the TC area
while on vacation. They all seem to be in pretty good shape. And we
actually planted our first one on www.letterboxing.org. Its
called "Fred's Rock Hunt" and it was posted with the trailname of
fjbani(the parents). But, seeing how they are a little less skilled
at the ways of the internet Iam joining this group for them too.

Anyways, I just wanted to say hi and if you run across the names
SpartyNYC and FJBANI thats who we are....see you guys out on the
trail.

Hello! Welcome to the Great Lakes group to both you and your parents. It is amazing how many boxes have been planted in NYC within the past 18 months. We were there in the fall of 2003 and there were only a handful of boxes in the entire NYC area. And Traverse City/Old Mission... what a beautiful area! It is one of my favorite areas in MI and there are a lot of nice parks/trails there. I would love to hear further details of your boxing conquests in that area if you want to e-mail me offlist. I also look forward to looking for your new box when I am up there.
