NEW in MI - the Hobo signs series

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-09 15:25:10 UTC
Team Bindlestiff announcing the creation of our latest series.

The first few boxes have been placed and clues have been posted. You
can find the clues on letterboxing.org and our yahoogroup. FYI- lots
more interesting stuff at our yahoogroup than what is at the big site.

We anticipate this to be a very long-term project (series) with
constant additions, albeit at irregular intervals.

With the Hobo signs series, I'm limbering up my writing skills so I
can put together a good tale of story-based clues for the series we
are working in cooperation with the DogLvrs family on.

HINT: The plot line of our cooperative series involves the Greatest
Piano Teacher in the World having her Piano kidnapped by an evil
character who not only wants to silence music but also to keep
children from ever being able to create it in the future. The stamps
for this large-ish series are all carved, the location all picked out
with the prelim trips made. We simply need to coordinate our schedules
to get them placed (not an easy feat in the summer!)
