So, I'll throw this out here now -- because I need advice.

I'm planning on hosting another gathering!! As mentioned before, the rain and wind can be scheduled closer to date, however, due to all the red tape I need to get working on the tornados now!!

The gathering will be sometime next year. I was thinking October, however, I'm open to having it during the summer to for more folks could make it.

It will be at Black Hawk State Park in Rock Island, IL with the theme, "Between Two Rivers" as Rock Island is nestled between the Mississippi & Rock Rivers. The stamp images will be anything related to either of the rivers -- Mark Twain, Great River Road, Black Hawk, etc etc.

Black Hawk State Park won an award a few years ago for being one of the "7 wonders of Illinois" and has about 6.5 miles of hiking trails. I have much time to iron out details and will keep you all informed.

Here is where I need advice, the park only has a couple smallish shelters -- not large enough for 30 - 50 folks let alone if more sign up.

I was thinking of renting out the lodge. Here are the rental fees;

Friday - Sunday Rental Fee: $700 - $150 damage deposit
Weekday 9 am - 5 pm Rental Fee: $150 - $100 damage deposit

SO, are weekday gatherings well attended? I've never been to one. If I have it in October, that's in the middle of the school year and might drop attendance . . . if I have it in the summer attendance should be good.

I will be putting a fee on this gathering to help with the costs. The lodge would really make the gathering special, but that's a whole lotta money.

So, here is what I'm asking . . . week day or weekend? I really can't please everyone as far as the when is concerned. I'm well aware of the school year and how that might affect attendance and I may slide the gathering back to the summer months for "optimal" attendance.

If I rent out the lodge, it would be rather empty with only 50 - 75 people there.

Thanks for any words of encouragement/advice.

Weekend or Week day??????

That is a tough one.  On the one hand, Flying Dragon and I both much prefer hiking in October but an October gathering would definitely fare much better on a weekend.  I would also prefer a weekend gathering since Rock Island is 3-4 hours from us and we would have to take two days off to attend a weekday gathering or else go to work exhausted the next day.  A weekday gathering would stand a much better chance during the summer, but it's not as great a time for hiking.  But a $550 difference in rental fees is significant!
