Not sure if this is the right board or not , but I have a report from
Pigeon Creek in West Olive:
I was up there a few days ago , and oh my! They are logging- in a big
way!
Most of the trails = especially the horse side and the side by the
campground
are closed due to the logging operation, but from what I could see,
ALOT is changing up there..
If you have boxes in those areas, you may want to keep an eye on
when they'll let you back in, so you can [hopefully] locate your box
and/ or update
your clues if you need too.
I was very unhappy with the closures, as this was to be my first trip
there of the season,
couldn't hunt the boxes I was hoping to find!!
Hope this update will help someone out, gemgirl65
