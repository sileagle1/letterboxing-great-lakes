Election Results from Wisconsin Gathering

From: Chris Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2008-10-05 17:00:19 UTC-05:00
Here are ALL the election results for the traveling ELECTION DAY stamp:

"Birds of a Feather" event, Kirkland, IL - June 21, 2008:
McCain - 11 votes
Obama - 8 votes
(2 write-ins)

"Mid-Coast Maine Letterboxing" event, Brunswick, ME - August 1, 2008:
McCain - 12
Obama - 10
(20 write-ins for various locals)

"Beautiful Ohio's Edge" event, Woodsfield, OH - August 30, 2008:
McCain - 8
Obama - 2
(1 write-in)

"WI Letterboxing 10th Anniversary" event, Sturgeon Bay, WI - October 4, 2008:
Obama - 13
McCain - 6
(2 write-ins: Bob Barr & Paul McCartney)

As you can see above, the votes were fairly close at the first 2 events. The Ohio event (McCain landslide) was held right after Sarah Palin was chosen as McCain's running mate. And now the economy has everyone concerned (Obama's first victory).

ELECTION DAY isn't scheduled to travel to any more events, so I guess we'll all just have to wait and see what happens next month. Thanks to all who participated, it's been fun :)

Trail Rider ~ Chris
