There are a lot of boxes in Ontario (236 listings, 355 boxes), not as
many in Quebec (13 listings, 17 boxes). If you go to the LBNA site,
click on the map, then the Canadian flag. You may want to contact some
of the placers in the areas you will be in to get their input and
recommendations.

Have a great trip!
