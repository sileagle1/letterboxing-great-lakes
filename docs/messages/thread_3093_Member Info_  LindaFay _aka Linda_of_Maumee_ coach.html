Letterboxing became my naughty hobby since July of 2004 when I
introduced it to myself and 3 of my nieces. My daughter, GretchenF,
was with us, but she thought it was silly then but has 280+ finds
now. Living were I do in Northwest Ohio, I must now travel to far
locations to find them. This last weekend I was in Delaware visiting
friends who now believe I need to be certified - as nuts - when then
followed me around.

My daughter and I go letterboxing every chance we get and I'm getting
better at carving. My daughter, who is a master carver, is currently
into her 6th month of developing a new letterboxer!!!! Yeah.

Oh, yes, I do have to work although I keep thinking if I only win the
lottery, but I've heard you have to buy tickets - hmmmm.

I have three dogs, one husband, 2 adult kids and like to watch birds
when not letterboxing or working. So, why is it a naughty hobby?
Because I spend too much time trying to solve some of mapsurfers
boxes and some of the other mystery boxes out there.
LindaFay
