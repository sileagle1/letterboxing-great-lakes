You know you are addicted when attending a morning out-of-town wedding
where you offer to drive guests to the airport between the ceremony
and the reception because you know that there are lertterboxes close
to the outlying airport. You drop off your passangers and make a
quick costume change right there in the car...a little difficult for
6'3" Tonto, but boxes are calling.
The boxes prove to take more bushwhacking than expected, and after
another quick change back into those stuffy dress clothes, you return
late to the reception. You are greeted with disgust and horror as the
wedding party looks down at the blood dripping from scratches all over
your legs and arms. For the topper they really think you're nuts
because you have that euphoric letterboxing grin plastered on your
face!
Tiptoe & Tonto

You know you are addicted when... You can't wait until after the bridal
shower, and you think you have enough time to stop before the shower, so
you go boxing in your dress/pantyhose/heels.
You show up at the shower with leaves in your hair, mud on your heels,
runs in your pantyhose, and scratches all over your arms and legs.
And, HA HA HA, this was Tiptoe & Tontos Slow Growth Oak box this fall.

Then there are those days when a new box is announced, and it's REALLY
CLOSE BY, and you can't wait until after work, so you run out during lunch
to go find the box, and return to work with leaves in your hair, mud on
your shoes.........

And you think that by now I would have LEARNED to keep an extra pair of
sneakers in the car.

Howling Coyote
