From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-07-10 08:38:56 UTC-05:00
I've only been able to find the camo duck tape at a Wal-Mart store
during the "hunting season" in the hunting supplies aisle. Since stores
seem to be WAY earlier in stocking for other seasons, perhaps they are
already stocking hunting supplies.

Other people have said they found it in the paint section of Wal-Mart,
but this tends to vary by store, since it isn't there at the Wal-Mart
near my office.

Good luck!
