Something a little extra at the WI 10th...

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-10-01 01:47:14 UTC
I'm pleased to announce that those of you attending the Wisconsin
Letterboxing 10th Anniversary event will have the opportunity to
stamp into a logbook that has been at the other two "10th
Anniversary" events earlier this year.

The BABE (Birth of American Boxing Event) gathering was held in April
in North Carolina, near the location of one of the very first boxes
in the United states. This event was hosted by Mountain Scorpia and
she created a replica of the Smithsonian Magazine cover that was the
impetus for American letterboxing. This is a VERY large image,
composed by 11 individual stamps. I'll bring some large sheets of
paper along for those that wish to obtain an image of these stamps.

These stamps and the logbook then made the trip out to Oregon for
the "Live & Breathe" event a few weeks ago, so you'll also see some
stamps in the logbook from that event. In addition I'll have a copy
of a booklet that was created for the L&B event if anyone would like
to take a look at it.

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. I'll also have some Letterboxing Great Lakes patches along if
anyone is interested in purchasing (although I think most of you
already have one!)
