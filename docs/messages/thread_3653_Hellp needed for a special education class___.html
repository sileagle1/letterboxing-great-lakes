Hellp needed for a special education class...

From: silboxer61364 (dispatcher_riz@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-29 06:42:05 UTC
I received this today from a PTO Member at the school my mom works
at. I figured I would post it here as there are letterboxers from
many different states in this group. Thanks. Sil
POST CARDS NEEDED!

As a culminating activity to my States of America Unit, I ask anyone
that can, to send to my class, postcards from any State in the U.S.
If you have friends or relatives in other states could you ask them
if they could do my classroom this favor. I am going to have them put
the postcards on the bulletin board with a string going from the
postcard to the city or state it is from. I don't care if they are
used or new. This will give them the little extra review of the
states every time a postcard comes in the mail.

I am including my address. I would really appreciate it if you would
forward this message to any friend or family member in the US that
you think would help us.

Please try to take the time to do this for me. Everyone knows that
review helps to cement the knowledge into their minds and special
education students need all the review they can get and it really
helps if it is a fun activity.

Thanks to all of you,

Karen Castle

Glen Oak School
809 E Frye Ave.
Peoria, Illinois 61603
