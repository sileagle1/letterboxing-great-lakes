In effort to get everyone involved in discussion, help us become
better acquainted with one another, and provide ideas and insight on
pertinent letterboxing topics, I thought I would throw out a "Topic
of the Week" at the beginning of each week. I can't guarantee it
will be on Monday every week, but I will try for within a day on
either side.

If it is a topic that interests you, please feel free to share your
thoughts and ideas on the subject. If it is not something of
interest to you -- perhaps next week's topic will hit closer to
home. Feel free to e-mail me suggestions for future topics.
