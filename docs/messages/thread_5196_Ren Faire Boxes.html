I JUST got the approval this morning to hide the letterboxes at
Bristol this year. I'll see if I can go out and hide them this week yet.

HOWEVER - as I mentioned a few weeks ago, I will be putting out ALL of
the Bristol letterboxes/stamps for a limited time this year to
celebrate 5+ years of boxing at Bristol. These will be in place for
the weekend of the 26th-27th and I am encouraging letterboxers to go
to Bristol that weekend for an informal gathering. The gathering
location at Bristol will be the large shade tree to the west of the
well near the entrance gate.

Since we will need to share this public area with muggles and we will
all be coming and going from that location, and the fact that we'll be
crawling over each other to get the boxes in a limited area, I suggest
that we each stamp our sig stamp on out left hands so we can identify
each other as a letterboxer. (Use your right hand to get the stamp to
leave/return Bristol.)

I will put this gathering on AtlasQuest so you can sign-up so people
know what day you will be there.
