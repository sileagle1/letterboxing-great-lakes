Explanation for missing box

From: cjosada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-18 17:02:57 UTC
Today, I received an email from a new letterboxing couple. They have
only found 1 box; my Not Licked Yet box in Door County. I retired
that box after the stamp has been replaced twice and it was reported
missing...again. Well, today I found out why. He has the stamp, asked
me what he should do with it now that the box has been retired. I
couldn't believe my eyes when I was reading their email. Needless to
say, people are learning about letterboxing from various media sources
and it's our responsibility to educate newbies.

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-18 23:55:48 UTC-06:00
Update: When I was talking about one of my missing boxes, I thought I had found an explanation. I intentionally didn't post names because hey, mistakes happen. We live and learn. But this time, I need to clarify that they don't have the stamp. They didn't take it. I usually stay up very late, last night till 4 am, and I didn't understand what he was offering to do. He bought some carving supplies, carved a 2 scoop ice cream cone stamp, and was asking if he could mail it to me or replace my missing stamp in Door County with one he carved. He was offering to do a really nice thing, although we usually don't carve replacement stamps for others, but I totally misunderstood him. Just wanted to set the record straight. However, that means, I still don't have an explanation why that box failed twice. Oh well.....
