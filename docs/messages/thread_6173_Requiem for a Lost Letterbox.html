Requiem for a Lost Letterbox

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2009-11-21 14:46:46 UTC
There once was a flapper--the first--
In a letterbox to be immersed.
Her graveyard home was so nice;
At least till it was bulldozed--TWICE!
To replant?--or would that be thrice-cursed?


It appears that Cemetery Stroll #3: Forest Park Flapper is a goner. She disappeared a year or so ago when they bulldozed the small cluster of rocks she was hiding in. I replanted her again in that nice deserted part of the cemetery, but alas, they REALLY went to town with the bulldozer this time. It wasn't just a little stand of rocks they removed this time, but a huge stretch of downed trees and rocks, etc. I tried looking for her yesterday, but she is probably in the middle of one of the big piles of debris :~( I guess the one downside of planting in camo'ed boxes is that it makes them harder to find at a time like this.

Part of me wants to replant (so that ALL of the cemetery strolls are available), but part of me wonders if she is--indeed--cursed.

BirdWoman/WP
