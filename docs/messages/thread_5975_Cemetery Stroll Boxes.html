Cemetery Stroll #3: Forest Park Flapper went missing over a year ago, but I decided to recarve it and bring it out of retirement. I replanted it today and updated the clues (basically just the end part). Cemetery Stroll #2: Rocks and Treestones had very recently gone missing and I replaced that as well.

When I was in the cemeteries today replanting, I noticed alot more visitors than I have ever seen before--more than Memorial Day or Mother's Day. Was a little puzzled about this, but now I wonder if this is the result of the travesty at Burr Oak Cemetery--perhaps people are wanting to be sure the family graves are undisturbed :~(
