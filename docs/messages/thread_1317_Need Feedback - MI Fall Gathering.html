Need Feedback - MI Fall Gathering

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-05-13 19:45:36 UTC
Okay, after checking out various shelters, it is apparent that the
State Park shelters are the best bet -- rental is for the entire day
at a lower cost than 6-hr. blocks at the county and city/township
shelters I checked. Unfortunately, although W/SW MI has some nice
state parks, most of them have very limited trails. The date will
be Saturday, October 22. Here is the scoop on the location options:

Yankee Springs Rec Area: Located just a short distance off U.S. 131
between Grand Rapids and Kalamazoo -- good central location. There
are several miles of prime trails at this park, however there are no
current letterboxes in this area. Park is open year-round and the
shelter is available. Shelter is roof, cement floor w/ half-walls,
includes grills and electricity. Problem: by October the plumbing
is turned off so there is no water and the modern restrooms are
closed. Nearest outhouses are about 1/4 mile from the shelter.

Hoffmaster SP: Located at the south end of Muskegon county along
Lake Michigan -- not as centrally located. There are also several
miles of nice trails at this park with a few letterboxes already in
place and many others in the surrounding area. Park is open year-
round and the shelter is available. Shelter is roof w/ cement
floor, includes 1 large grill, a hand pump for water and a large
stone fireplace in the shelter -- there is NO electricity. No
modern restrooms in this area of the park, but outhouses are located
very near the shelter.

Both places have pros and cons. I have created a poll and will
leave it open through May 22 (next Sunday). If you are planning to
attend the MI fall gathering or even if you are just thinking about
it, please vote. Please don't just vote for the location nearest to
you, rather take everything into consideration when you vote --
amenities, facilities, letterbox availability and location. If you
have specific comments or concerns, feel free to post to the list
for discussion or e-mail me privately.

Thanks!

SpringChick

Thanks for the suggestion. I have Fort Custer on the list if the
others fall through. I really was looking for a location centralized
to West Michigan, with the extremes being Muskegon/Whitehall on the
North, Lansing area on the East and Battle Creek on the South -- --
of course anyone is welcome to attend, this was just my target area
based on those who actively participate on this list.

Yankee Springs fits that bill nicely, being just over an hour from
any of the extremes mentioned, but the lack of water and nearby
restrooms worried me a bit, which is why I included the other
choice. My feeling was that since the big gathering (GL Gather) is
in the south of the state, if there is not a suitable centralized
location for the fall gathering, I would opt for a more northern
location.

But... if you want to host a get-together down in your area at some
point, I would be more than happy to make the drive down there ;-)
