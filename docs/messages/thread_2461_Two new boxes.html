Two new boxes

From: jlizner (jlizner@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-30 23:10:42 UTC
Hey everyone,

I just wanted to let you know that there are two new boxes out there
to find. I'm excited to say I've placed my first boxes this weekend!
Their names are: Old Rugged Cross and Little Mac. (And I really
really hope they don't stink!) :)

Anyway, they are both in the Reed City area (Osceola County) and are
pretty easy (my 9 year old brother was my guinea pig). But here's
my reasoning: Reed City is a place that many people pass or stop in
briefly (for McD's or to fill up the tank) on their way across the
state. So I placed two boxes that are pretty close to highway access
and not too difficult or time consuming so that if you are traveling
through, you might be able to take a little extra time to come see
these two places (which are not jaw dropping spectacular, but pretty
cool anyway).

I posted clues to the LBNA page this afternoon, they appear on the
What's New link, but not the Map (is that normal?).

If you go to find them, I'd appreciate any feedback, since this is
my first try and I want to do more and would like to make them the
best that I can!

Thanks!
