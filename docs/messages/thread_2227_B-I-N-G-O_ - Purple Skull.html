Re: [LbGLK] B-I-N-G-O! - Purple Skull

From: (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-30 21:36:44 UTC
Well, I can actually vouch for the fact that the stamp does indeed exist as I have seen it in personal logbooks at various out-of-area gatherings over the past year, as well as in one Michigan letterbox logbook, so it is not a complete urban legend.

I suppose it is possible that the whole scheme it is a conspiracy amongst a group of boxers, but the images I have seen appear to be done with the same stamp. I've also noticed that it seems to strike in spurts -- at a gathering, in a specific geographic area, etc., which would be more indicative of a single person than a group of people. Unless, of course they are sending the stamp from one person to another?

SpringChick
