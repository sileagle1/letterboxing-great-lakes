Just A Reminder...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-18 18:33:52 UTC
Please limit your use the FILES > CLUES area of the group only for
clues that are not available at one of the public letterboxing clue
sites (i.e. letterboxing.org and/or AtlasQuest). While it is a good
idea to keep a backup copy of your clues for situations when thes
sites are not available, this should be done on your own computer as
there is not enough room in the FILES section of the group for
everyone to keep their backup clues there.

If we ever run into a situation where the site where you have your
clues (letterboxing.org or AtlasQuest) is not available for an
extended period of time, like a couple of weeks ago, you are welcome
to *temporarily* use the FILES section for clues. But provided the
public sites are up and running, this area should be reserved for WOM
clues and other clues not available at a public site.

Thanks!

SpringChick

Re: [LbGLK] Re: Just A Reminder...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-19 10:20:36 UTC-04:00
I keep the actual content of my clues on my own website but for a while I
had the boxes (and links to the clues) listed at both AQ and
letterboxing.org. But as you, I found it a hassle to maintain this at both
sites and removed my boxes from AQ. I also didn't like it that when I had
clues at AQ, people were sending me found reports in AQ mail. Not only is
it another e-mail to keep up with, but I keep folders for each of my
letterboxes in my Outlook Express e-mail with reports and status notes
people send me about them -- it is a nice history to look back on. As AQ
mail does not have the ability to forward out of AQ to another mail program
I wasn't able to file these found reports away with the others.

I use AQ to look for clues sometimes, but for me I find it easier to just
maintain clue listings at letterboxing.org.

SpringChick
