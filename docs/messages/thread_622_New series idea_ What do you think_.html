From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-09-01 13:23:04 UTC-05:00
I'm doing that with the boxes I'm placing at GS camp. There's a song called
Three Chartreuse Buzzards, whose lyrics refer to said birds sitting in a
dead tree. I carved a long skinny stamp of a tree trunk with three branches
coming out of it. There's a buzzard sitting on each branch (and numbers
1,2, and 3). (I haven't the heart to cut them apart yet, but any day now I
have to, since I want to place them soon.) The clues all follow the same
trail, and hiding places are only minutes apart by foot. Each one will of
course be hidden in a dead tree. When the three images are stamped
sequentially below the first, the full image will be seen.

(Others have done this too...I'm not the first....)
