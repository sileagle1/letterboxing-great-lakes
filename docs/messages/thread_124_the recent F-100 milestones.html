We've been out doing Spring Commissioning work on Bindlestiff so
this is my first time back online in a few days.

Couldn't let the recent F100 milestones pass by with adding a great
big CONGRATS from me, to those who've made it though!

I also note that you guys have pretty high "P" numbers too. Thanks
for all of the effort you have put into letterboxing. I'm sure I
speak for more than myself when I say, that those of us who came to
boxing after you, truly appreciate the fruits of your labor.

Enjoy!
