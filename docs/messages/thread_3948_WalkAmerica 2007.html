WalkAmerica 2007

From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-10 23:03:06 UTC
Hello Everyone,

We hope that all of you and your families are doing well. For those
of you who celebrate, we hope that you had a very enjoyable Easter;
for everyone, we hope that you are enjoying your Spring (if you can
call it that with the weather we've been having) so far.

We are writing this to bring your awareness to a very noble cause,
The March of Dimes. We are participating this year in the March of
Dimes' WalkAmerica even on April 29, 2007. This is a 3.1 mile walk
that is taking place in Busse Woods, in Schaumburg, Illinois. We
have a goal to raise $500 as a team and are getting very close to
reaching that goal, we would be ecstatic if we could even go beyond
our goal. Every dollar raised goes towards the well-being of
babies. THIS IS A WALK TO SAVE BABIES!! The March of Dimes uses
this money to fund research in the prevention of premature birth and
the progression of treatment for babies born with birth defects, like
our Joseph was. We are doing our best to help raise money so that no
family has to endure what we did. If we can even help one family,
this will all be worth it. Please help us to achieve our goal, even
if you can only donate $1.00, donate something.

You can donate by visiting our blog and clicking on the link to our
pledge page. If you have a chance, look in our March archive for the
blog entry about WalkAmerica for more info.

www.pinknbluemaketwo.blogspot.com

We appreciate your time and efforts.

Steve, Christa & Emily Bellock
"The Cottontails"
