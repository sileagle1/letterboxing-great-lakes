I have been mulling over ideas for variations on the Bingo game and had planned to post this weekend. But since you asked... yes, I am planning to do Bingo again this year. The game will begin on January 1st. Admission will be one item for the Bingo booty *and* and small monetary sum to help cover the cost of mailing the prizes -- I spent about $70 mailing packages last year thanks to all the great items contributed for the prizes!

I am thinking the deadline for getting the necessary stuff to me (booty item, card, etc.) will be December 28th. Details to follow this weekend... please do not e-mail me to get on the list -- there is room for all who want to play and once the details are announced you can jump on the band wagon. Looking forward to another great year of Letterbox Bingo!

SpringChick
