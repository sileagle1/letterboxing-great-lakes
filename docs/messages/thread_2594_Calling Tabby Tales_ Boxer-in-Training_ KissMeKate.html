My son found the logbook for the "Outlaw Cootie" in his backpack at
the end of the day on Saturday. But there was no stamp with it. I
contacted Kiddy Writer and Private Pilot, who had stamped in last, but
they do not have it. All they received was the logbook and they
thought that was all it was to it.

Tabby Tales, Boxer-in-Training, KissMeKate and Barefoot Ankh were the
4 stamps right before Kiddy Writer. So I'm hoping one of you may have
the stamp for the "Outlaw Cootie". From what I was told, by Wisconsin
Hiker before we left the park, this stamp is attached to a pink
carrabinerand approx. 1" square. Please check your things and let me
know if you have it. If not, I will try to get ahold of the next LB's
on the logbook.

I will be contacting the originator of this cootie to find out what
they wish to do if this has escaped us all.
