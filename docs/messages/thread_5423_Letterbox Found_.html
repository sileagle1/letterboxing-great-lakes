There are many things to be thankful for on Thanksgiving. But here's
something that a letterbox can be thankful for - the return of a
missing letterbox!!! One of my boxes was reported missing by someone
who had previously found it and was returning to the area with a new
letterboxer. So she knew where to look. I replaced the box but not
until after I scoured the area for the missing box. Yep, it was no
where to be found. Today, I receive an email from another letterboxer
that BOTH boxes are in that one location as of yesterday. I'm not sure
what to make of this report, but I'm glad that the missing box has been
found.
Happy Thanksgiving to all from Beetle.
