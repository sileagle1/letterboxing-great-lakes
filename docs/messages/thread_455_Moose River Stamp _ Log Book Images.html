I can feel for the thought of the loss of an altered book/log! It's hard to
even place them when you put that much work into them!

I've taken to making simple AB logs for some of my specimen cup boxes. I
have a large oval punch...maybe 1x1.5"...that I've been using. I punch out
a bunch of cardstock ovals and bind them together with a brad and fancy
fibers. I stamp and emboss the image of the box's stamp (usually glitter
powder) to spiff it up a bit. Not quite the elaboracy of your log book, SC,
but more fun than just an accordion folded sheet!
