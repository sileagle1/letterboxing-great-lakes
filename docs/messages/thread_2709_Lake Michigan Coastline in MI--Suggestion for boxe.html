From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-26 21:58:50 UTC
We just returned from a W. Michigan boxing adventure, and one of the
ones we had a chance to look for was the Fishtown box. Sadly, we
couldn't find it.... but we couldn't really root around for it
because it is in a pretty busy area. Hopefully it hasn't already gone
missing! (And Fishtown is really neat and we were glad we had a
chance to visit it.)

We both really liked SpringChick's Happy Feet and Block House boxes,
which are in a beautiful park. Also, BindleBabe's Fill 'Er Up series
is nearby, which is a lot of fun. And there are a TON of mystery
boxes in the area, if you want to play detective!

Have fun!

J of J&BBooks
