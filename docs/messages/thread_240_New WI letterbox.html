Also, with regards to the gathering on June 12: I don't know now if
I'll be able to make it. I'd love to, but our local hockey team (for
which I am a season ticket holder) made it to the league finals, which

games in either Wilkes-Barre/Scranton, PA or Hartford, CT (depending
on who wins their series this weekend), cheering on the team. I'm
bummed I'll miss meeting you all, but I can't pass up the chance to
see the team in the finals.

I'm torn on who I want them to play against. Hartford means excellent
letterboxing opportunities while on the trip, but W-B/S has my
favorite player in the league on their team. Oh, the conundrums when
hobbies collide! :)

Hope you all have a fabulous Memorial Day weekend.
