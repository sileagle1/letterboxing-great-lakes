From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-11-18 21:22:48 UTC-08:00
Update:11/02/07 -
Our firearm deer seasons are fast approaching. The firearm hunting dates are as follows:
1st Shotgun Season - Nov. 16-18
2nd Shotgun Season - Nov. 29 - Dec. 2
Muzzleloader Season - Dec. 7 - 9
Late Firearm Season - Jan 18 - 20, 2008

The following areas of Matthiessen and Starved Rock State Parks will be closed:
All of Matthiessen State Park - CLOSED
Starved Rock Campground - CLOSED
Starved Rock State Park from Wildcat Canyon to the east end of the park - CLOSED

The Visitor Center and Starved Rock Lodge will remain open. Hiking trails from St. Louis Canyon to Wildcat Canyon will remain open.
