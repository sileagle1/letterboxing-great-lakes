GLG 2008 Attendees Please Read

From: Gooble Gobble (gooble_gobble_3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-16 23:16:06 UTC-07:00
Only two weeks until the 2008 GLG! I am so excited I
can hardly contain myself. I was out earlier today
planting the 4 boxes I made for the big day and had an
idea. If you would like me to email the clues to you
for this series of 4 boxes please let me know. I'm
offering this for a very good reason. The "Loop" I
planted on is 6 miles long. I would guess it would
take someone around 3 hours, maybe more, to complete
the series of four, seriously, wait until you see the
trails, or lack thereof, you will have to travel upon.
I wanted people to be able to go on Friday if they
chose to so they wouldn't have to spend a large chunk
of the gathering looking for only four boxes. I made
the hike so long for several reasons. 1. Afton State
Park is so diverse in it's topography. I wanted to
take people on a tour of all of the different forest
types, etc. 2. Longevity of the boxes I made. When you
spend a lot of time making boxes you want them to last
for a long time to come. 3. it was so much fun hiking
for such a long distance. Some of the "trails" are
completely made up, I hope your orienteering skills
are up to par. 4. Chances of muggles or random others
stumbling across these boxes hopefully is non-existent
due to the long hike and remote locations of the
plants. 5. Last but not least, long challenging hikes
with sometimes difficult clues are very rewarding, at
least to me.

So, if you will be here early on Friday and would like
to go for a nice tour of Afton State Park let me know.
I'll email you the clues.

I look forward to seeing everyone on the 31st.


Gooble Gobble
