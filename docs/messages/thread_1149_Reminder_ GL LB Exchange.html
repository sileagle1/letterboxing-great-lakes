Reminder: GL LB Exchange

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 10:57:01 UTC

Just a reminder that if you signed up to participate in the GL
Letterbox Exchange and have not yet sent out your box, time is closing
in and people are getting anxious. The deadline for sending
your "Hometown Landmark" themed box to your assigned person is April
15! If your circumstances are such that you will not be able to meet
the deadline, please contact me off-list and we will work something
out.

Once you have received your box, please plan to have it planted and
clues posted by June 1st.

Thanks everyone for participating!

SpringChick

Re: Reminder: GL LB Exchange

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 15:56:46 UTC

I don't think that I had actually specified. No need to make it part
of the name of the box, but it would be nice to include at the top of
the clues. Since you will be specifying who the creator of the box
is anyway, that would be a good place to make a note about the
project.

For instance, I received a beautiful stamp relating to a landmark
called "Tuckaway." I plan to simply name the box "Tuckaway." At the
top of the clues, I will make a note that the box was created by
[name of person] as part of the Great Lakes "Hometown Landmarks"
Letterbox Exchange Project. I will probably also include a brief
paragraph of info relating to the landmark itself before diving into
the clues.

SpringChick
