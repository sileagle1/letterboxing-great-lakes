Free Space & reptiles

From: pgranola2002 (p2jd@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-01-05 20:50:24 UTC
I had one of my most memorable letterboxing experiences yesterday. My
husband and I are on vacation in Hilton Head, SC. While out
letterboxing at a forest preserve on the island what should we see
about 15 feet from us but two good sized alligators and 4 rather large
turtles all out enjoying the sun together! How cool is that? Being a
born and raised Chicago area girl this was quite a novel experience!
Then we went down to Savannah to sight see and picked up two boxes
while in the city. Remarkably enough, when I stamped into one box I
saw that Sew-Sew Busy had stamped into that same box earlier that same
day! She being from Virginia and I from Chicago that is pretty neat.
What makes it really fun is that she is on my bingo card because I met
her at a gathering...I took it as an omen(plus her space is
strategically located on my card)and chose her space as my Free Space.
I love this hobby! By the way it is really nice to be out boxing in
74 degree weather in January....not gloating...just sharing.
