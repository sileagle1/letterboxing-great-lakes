From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-12-29 05:13:50 UTC
... but we're happy to report we did our first December boxing today!
We're out in Arizona and of course saw evidence of previous Great Lakes
visitors. We didn't recognize the trail names, but the first few boxes
we found had already been stamped in by boxers from Illinois (Bothman
Famiy from GraysLake) and boxers from Minnesota (Keith & Barb from
Woodbury).

Even after only 1 day of hiking & boxing we know we love this area!
Bright blue sunny skies and some great mountain hiking, what more could
we ask for?? Here's more: we'll be meeting up with TipToe & Tonto in a
few days when they make their annual trek to Tucson. Should be fun to
hike with this duo!

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. We have our BINGO cards with us so we won't miss the first few
calls!
