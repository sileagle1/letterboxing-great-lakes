From: Timbertoes (toomey@genevaonline.com) | Date: 2004-12-29 02:35:08 UTC

My husband, bless his heart, gave me a GPS unit for Christmas
thinking it would help me in my letterboxing searches. When I told
him that there is a whole other hobby similar to letterboxing that
makes use of GPS units, his eyes bugged out and he said, "Another
hobby! You don't need ANOTHER hobby!" After a good laugh, I told
him I had no intention of adding geocaching to my list of pasttimes,
but I AM interested in finding some geocaches that I know are hidden
around here... just to see how they do it. I've never run into any
while letterboxing like some of you have mentioned.

Timbertoes
