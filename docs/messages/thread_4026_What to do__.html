From: Baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-05-03 08:01:13 UTC-07:00
Do you know if the Metro Parks specifically have any rules against boxing? I know there are a ton planted in the parks. But, I wonder if they don't really allow it or if they make you get licenses. I'd do some research on their site to see if you can find out if it's against to rules.

Maybe butter her up some and mention you noticed the email address, ask her about her job, tell her how much you love the parks, etc.

Then maybe email her back and ask some questions about where she's having trouble with the clues. I wouldn't offer any specific details about the boxes at this point.

I think you definately go check on the boxes in the next couple weeks!

Good luck and let us know what happens!
Bali
