Just got an email from the ranger at Rib Mountain. There is a new
Geocaching/Letterboxing policy for the WI State Parks. Doesn't sound
like there is a fee, but there is a compliance form to fill out so
they are sure the boxes are in a "safe location."

It seems that the DNR is going through all LBs listed and determining
whether they are in a SP and emailing people if they are. So far I've
got nothing else - just Rib Mtn.

Looks like the WI geocachers got their wish from the DNR :p
Thanks a bunch!

You know, the same thing happened in some of the parks here near Cincinnati
Ohio. I filled out the forms, add the "registration number" to the outside
of all my boxes and never heard another thing about it. It was supposed to
be renewed each year and that was about two years ago.

My suggestion: Play along and they leave you alone.

Thankfully, when I told them how many letterboxes I had hidden in the park,
they let me fill out one form, list all the boxes on it and use just one
registration number. I have since then pulled some of the boxes to make way
for other placers but no one else has placed in there that I know of.
