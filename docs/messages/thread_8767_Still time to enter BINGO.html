Still time to enter BINGO

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-12-27 19:16:59 UTC-06:00
It is a busy time of year, but there are still a few days left to enter BINGO for this year.

For those of you that have entered, I've updated the database on the Yahoo Group page, and all booty and entries that I've received should be noted.  If you think I've missed you, please let me know.

As for those of you that haven't entered, I'm not sure I can promise a daily poem like last year...but you will get your morning excitement (or let down) as I call a word every day until we have three, full card, winners.

Along the way, you'll have the honor (or dishonor) of taunting the other letterboxers about your progress (or lack thereof).

Fill out a card, send it to me, send some booty, and enjoy.

Good luck,
A of Atomic Beans
