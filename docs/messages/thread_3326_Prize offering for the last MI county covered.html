Prize offering for the last MI county covered

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-11-15 18:39:00 UTC-05:00
Calling all Michiganders and potential out-of-staters who may be
interested in planting letterboxes in Michigan,

The action is heating up! The counties are counting down! Wisconsin
is close on our heels! Do you have a box you want to plant but need
some incentive to do so?

If I've followed the threads here accurately, I believe we have 4
counties left to cover. I also believe that if all goes well this
weekend TrailTroll plans to plant boxes in the uncovered Northeastern
counties (Gladwin, Oscoda & Alcona). So, if that works out, I think
there will be one lonely uncovered county by the end of the
weekend. Poor Mecosta! Do you really want to leave it out in the
cold without a box, all by itself? How fair is that? Come on, plan
a trip to Mecosta! Plant a box!

I've decided to add a little incentive to get that last county
covered. In addition the bindle_babe's ongoing contest, I will offer
up a prize to the first person to cover the last county in
Michigan. Keep in mind, that may or not be Mecosta, depending on who
plants what where and when. (nice sentence!)

Remember bindle_babe's contest runs through midnight on
Thanksgiving. So, for optimum benefits, you may want to plant your
box before then.

I will keep this offer open until: (1) someone plants a box in the
last county in Michigan OR (2) Wisconsin kicks our butt and covers
all the rest of their counties before us OR (3) Midnight on December
3, 2006.... whichever comes first.

If the counties fill up before bindle_babe's contest ends, I'll go
with whoever she shows as the first placer in the last county. If we
don't fill up the counties by Thanksgiving, you can either email me
or you can post a note here with a link to your clues.

So, get out there and plant a box! Let's get the map colored in completely!

GO MICHIGAN! Get Boxin'!


Beth
