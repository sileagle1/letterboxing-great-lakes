Happy Surprises on the Trail

From: Mary (drewes.mary@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-06-13 23:45:37 UTC
Now I know I'm not a true old-timer at this hobby (only 4.8 years), but with 1217 finds, I don't expect to see a lot of surprises when it comes to unique finds and surprising survival tales.

Of course, TJ Mich's Michigan J Frog LB from the GLG was one of those surprises!

And so was today's find, East Twin Boardwalk in Tisch Mills, WI (on the way to Algoma/Door County). I have tried at least two other times for this box, but turned back due to flooding and then snow cover. The way this box (or should I say special pouch) is secured worked wonderfully well for holding it in place even during flooding - and the contents were dry! WH and MM were the last finders nearly two ago - thanks for sealing it well!

If you choose to go for this unique hide, be prepared for tall grass - and keep your eyes open for the yellow blazes of the Ice Age Trail.

What has been a pleasant surprise for you all when it comes to LBing?

- Sunny
