I know this topic has passed through the either recently, but I just
got back from the store and was overwhelmed by the choices. Who can
recommend a specific brand of wipe that can be used to clean off

dollar store? Some say scented and some say unscented and some say

Re: [LbGLK] stamp wipes

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-04-21 12:39:22 UTC-04:00
I have been using unscented baby wipes on rubber stamps for many years per a rubber stamping workshop I attended at one time. But apparently even the unscented wipes are not really too stamp-friendly.

I really liked the idea that Doodle & Deedle shared about placing a wet sponge inside a hinged soap container. Of course I see those hinged soap containers all over the place for a dime when they are of no interest to me, but when I am trying to find one, do you think I can? Nope... not at any price. Just the 2-piece ones...

SpringChick
