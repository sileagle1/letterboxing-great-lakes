If you are an organization geek, dig the whole folksonomy concept
and treat your books like family members, this might be something
you are interested in...

Library Thing (http://www.librarything.com/) is an online book
cataloging app with a lot of flexibility and is really quick and
easy to use. It uses a tagging concept similar to flickr and
del.icio.us, while affording you a simple way to keep a record of
your book collection. It is still in Beta, but I haven't
encountered any programatic bugs or glitches during use. I
especially like that you don't have to provide 17 pages of personal
information and your grandmother's birthdate to create an account --
just sign up and go. The free version only lets you catalog 200
books (believe me, this is easy to whip through in an evening), but
the lifetime unlimited membership is reasonable enough.

Something for a rainy day (actually several rainy days if you have
an extensive library).
