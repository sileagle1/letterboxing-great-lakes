From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-04-20 03:23:02 UTC
Hello again!

We won't have regular access to email next week, so I figured I'd
send another reminder now to those of you who volunteered to make
boxes for the event. I have already received 10 boxes from "out of
towners" and clues for one box planted by a local boxer. I'm
estimating that there are probably about 16 more boxes that will be
sent to us from those that live further away. I just wanted to
remind you that if we will be planting the box, we'd like to have it
in our hands by April 30 (10 days away). That will give us several
weekends in May to get out and hide those boxes. When you're ready
to send the box, just contact me for my mailing address. If for some
reason you will NOT be able to create the box, please send me an
email message so I can remove it from the list.

If you're planting your own box, please email me the clues by May 26
or sooner if you want them included in the clue booklet.

As the event gets closer, let me know if you decide on what you'd
like to bring for the potluck. You can check the website

http://2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com/potluck.html

to see what people are already planning to bring and see some general
suggestions for additonal items.

Happy boxing (and carving)!

Wisconsin Hiker
