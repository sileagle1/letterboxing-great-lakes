Great Lakes Gathering 2008

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-01-02 09:52:26 UTC
I know that a lot of people plan their vacations at the beginning of
the year, so I figured we'd better nail down some of the logistics for
this year's Great Lakes Gathering.

The purpose of this post is to solicit interested volunteers to host
the gathering, not to debate the date of the gathering. As is
customary, the gathering will be held on the first Saturday after
Memorial Day, which is May 31, 2008. The only two states that have
not yet hosted are Indiana and Minnesota so host volunteers from
either of those states have first chance. If you are from IN or MN
and are interested in hosting the GLG Gathering on May 31, 2008, or
may be interested in working together with somebody else to host,
please contact me offlist by January 10. If there is no interest from
either IN or MN by that date, I will open it up to anyone in the
region. If there is interest from more than one person/state, it will
be put up for a vote in a poll.

If you are interested, please let me know where you are from and the
general vicinity where you would plan to host the gathering -- i.e.
Minneapolis area, Indy area, SE IN, et. You do not need to commit to
any specifics at this time (i.e. park, theme, etc.).

If you are wondering what is involved in hosting the gathering, there
are a couple of documents in the files section of the list with an FAQ
and some basic info about hosting the gathering. Additionally, I'm
sure you could post to the list or directly contact any of the
previous hosts (M&8X & Eagle Eyes, WI_Hiker & Martini Man, Dragon,
Team Rabbit, SpringChick & OldShoe) with questions.

Thanks!

SpringChick
