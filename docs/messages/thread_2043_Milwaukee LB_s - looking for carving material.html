Michael's has the Speedball pink stuff and the tan stuff and the
starter kit you spoke of. You will probably not find it with the
rubber stamps though. Michael's typically puts it with the art and
inking supplies - where the multicolored Sharpies are sold. Still,
not all Michael's stock it. The one in LaCrosse doesn't.

Good luck!
