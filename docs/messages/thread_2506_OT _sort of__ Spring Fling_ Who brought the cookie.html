I'll tell ya what ~ I wasn't at this gathering, but the yummy's you've described sound like something I've made a time or two...Magic Cookie Bars ~ here's my recipe:

Magic Cookie Bars





cup butter

1 cups graham cracker crumbs

1 14 oz can Sweetened Condensed Milk

1 6 oz package semi-sweet chocolate morsels

***I used bag chocolate & bag butterscotch

3 oz flaked sweetened coconut (1- 1 cups or so)

1 cup chopped nuts ~ I used pecans





Preheat oven to 350 F. (or 325 F if you use a glass dish)

Melt butter in 9x13 baking pan.

Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs over better.

Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over crumbs.

Top evenly with remaining ingredients; press down gently.

Bake for 25-30 minutes or until lightly browned.

Cool thoroughly before cutting.

Makes 24 bars.
