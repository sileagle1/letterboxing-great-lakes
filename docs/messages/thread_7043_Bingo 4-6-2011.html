It's looking good for Shorty!
Perhaps I can't compete with you,
but at least I'm feeling sporty.

As far as the boxes of Springchick
I just got three in a row
So in this secondary game
That leaves me 2 to go.

But in the main event
I still need 6 more words,
And since there are but 33 left
That percentage seems absurd.

My best hope now is the sidewager game -
I picked this coming Monday.
If somebody beats me to Bingo
At least that could be a fun day!

Oh bingo man, I plead once more
Be kind to your faithful servant
(Unless I missed one of my words
Was I somehow unobservant?)

With grace and style you draw each word
bringing tears or joy to each
If only there were some earnest way
That your cold heart I could reach.

For now I must continue on,
hoping to win myself
And if somebody else should win -
There's always 2012!

I just had to look this up, since the first letterbox we ever planted was about a circus train wreck. The Durand wreck was in 1903 and happened to the Wallace circus: 23 dead along with a number of animals. Our letterbox was about a wreck in 1918 to the Hagenback-Wallace circus (wonder if it was the same Wallace): no animals dead, but 86 people died. No matter what, these were 2 tragedies. But how terrible if it happened to the same circus group.
