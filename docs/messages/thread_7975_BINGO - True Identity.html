The game definitely ended sooner this year than last year, when there weren't any Full Cards called until May.

 

However it's pretty close to when the game ended in other years.  I'll bet you're not surprised that I have the stats on that also!

 

The first year (2006) the game was played a bit differently and there weren't any interim games.  The game didn't end until May 16!

 

After that, the game was played pretty similar each year, with some variation on the interim games or number of wildcards and number of days "bonus words" were called.  Here is when the game ended each year:

 

2007 - APR 23

2008 - APR 24

2009 - APR 23

2010 - MAY 3

2011 - APR 19

2012 - APR 17

2013 - MAY 5

2014 - APR 20

 

Thanks to everyone who played!  With the great warm weather today, it is a good day to end our winter game!
