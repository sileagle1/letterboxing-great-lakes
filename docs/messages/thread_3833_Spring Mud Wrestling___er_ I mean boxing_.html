WooHoo! Sounds great! We went out for our first day of spring
boxing today, too. We were mostly in the woods, so no sun burn for
us. It was 50+ degrees in Michigan. Muddy as heck! We couldn't
find 3 of the 4 boxes we were looking for. The first one we think we
know where it was, but the branches and stuff were still too frozen
to get to. The next two boxes were both really close to each other
and both boxes' last clue hinges on sitting on a bench. Well, the
bench isn't there anymore! All the other benches on the trail are
still there, so I think this one was permanently removed. But, it's
absense makes it really hard to find the boxes! We tried divining
our way to the boxes by playing the "if I were a bench where would I
be situated" game. But, we didn't have much luck. One of the two
boxes we think we know where it is, but again, too frozen to get to
still. The other we don't really have any idea. We search every
logical place and couldn't find any trace of it.

We ended up searching for the fourth box just because we didn't want
to go home completely empty handed. After some initial confusion, we
found it finally and it was accessible! This trail was still really
slushy and muddy, I thought for sure I'd be in the mud or pond before
we finished up,but we managed to escape the trail without
injury. About 50 feet from the car, my friend's daughter took a face
plant right into a huge mud puddle! LOL She survived! Luckily,
super-mom was smart enough to bring a second pair of pants for her
for just this reason!

Anyway, it was a great day outdoors even if we had our worst "% found
record" ever. Hoep you all had as nice of a day as we did!
