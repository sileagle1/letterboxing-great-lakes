I'm an outdoor education trainer for the Girl Scouts. After viewing all those photos, (I must have a strong stomach), I've decided to print out some of those photos and use them as visual illustrations in my training binder. Sometimes one photo is worth a 1000 words!

This also has put an end to my days of hiking around in flip flops.
