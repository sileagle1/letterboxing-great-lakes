Well, I never heard of raw PORK being consumed around here, but I have
definitely eaten numerous raw beef sandwiches in my life. They were
very popular when I was younger (a long, long time ago...). We
usually just referred to them as raw beef sandwiches, but sometimes
they were called "cannibal sandwiches". They were always a high grade
of beef, so similar to eating a very rare steak. We always had them
open-faced on small slices of dark rye bread, topped with raw onions
and salt & pepper. They appeared at almost every festive occasion
when I was a kid - Christmas, New Year's, always at weddings, etc.
You don't see them around much anymore though.

I haven't had one in quite a while, but I'm pretty sure Martini Man
had one or two last year.
