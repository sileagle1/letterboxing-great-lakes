Box Maintenance Season

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-02-10 14:42:04 UTC-06:00
It has been awhile since I think there has been an official 'Box
Maintenance' month. For those of you who have started letterboxing more
recently, Dragon (and later Springchick) organized efforts for everyone
to go do maintenance on their boxes.

The idea is that everyone should go out and check their clues and boxes
to make sure that they are still accurate and in good shape. Do your
part to end 'Attempted Finds!'

As an incentive, I started a database 'Box Maintenance 2012' where you
can list any box that you have recently maintained. For each box you
maintain, you get one entry into a drawing that I will do at the 'Beat
the Winter Blues' gathering (or on April 28th). The winner will get a
great, letterboxing related, prize!

To qualify a box:

1) You (or a member of your 'team') must be the person to visit a box
that you own.
2) You must physically check that the clue is accurate, the stamp and
logbook are intact and in good shape, and that the box container is
intact. If any part is incomplete, you need to fix it by April 27th to
be counted.
3) You must do maintenance between February 1 and April 27th.
4) Boxes planted after November 2012 are ineligible.
5) Like most letterboxing finds, finds are recorded on the honor system

Go find YOUR boxes!

Atom118 of Atomic Beans
