RE: [LbGLK] Question about carry-on luggage (ahem Martini Man)

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-08-30 22:55:20 UTC-05:00
Years ago we did use our packs as our carry-on items, but we packed so much
other stuff inside them that they were so heavy that the seams started
ripping out. Instead we now each have the largest allowable wheeled
carry-on suitcases and we put our backpacks INSIDE the suitcases. We are
also able to fit our boots and other things inside the suitcase without
stressing the pack itself. We always carry-on the REALLY important things -
LB stuff, clues & boots. Everything else, such as clothes or toiletries,
could be replaced fairly easily.



RE: [LbGLK] Question about carry-on luggage (ahem Martini Man)

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-08-30 22:58:05 UTC-05:00
Fee for checked luggage varies by airline. We try to fly Southwest as much as possible - usually low fares and NO fees for up to 2 checked bags.



By the way, you are usually allowed 2 carry-on items if one is a purse or briefcase. You can put a lot of stuff in a briefcase!
