Hi!

We belong to the same hiking group as your mother-in-law (she's still a
member and hikes with us whenever she is back to visit in Wisconsin). Most
of our hikes are within a certain driving radius of Milwaukee. Our club has
5 hikes each week, but most are closer to Milwaukee than Racine. However we
do occasionally hike in Racine and even further south in Illinois. We also
have some hiking weekends planned in Western Wisconsin (Perot State Park at
the end of April and Wyalusing State Park in mid-June). Anyone is welcome
to join us as a guest on any of our club hikes. For more info on the club
and to see our current hikes schedule, check here:
http://www.homestead.com/wisconsingohiking/ We'd certainly welcome any
fellow-letterboxers on these hikes!

I am aware of one hiking club in the Racine/Kenosha area because they asked
permission to copy several of our forms and procedures when they established
their group. It is primarily geared towards retirees, but they followed our
club's policy and welcome anyone as a guest on their hikes. You can contact
Marge Miller for more info on the Racine group: milgeog@execpc.com I
believe they may hike indoors during the winter months, but outdoors on a
weekly basis during the other seasons.
