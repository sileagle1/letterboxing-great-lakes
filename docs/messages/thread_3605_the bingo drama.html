For myself, I've invented the "24 hour code" ... if I feel strongly
about a message I've read and want to reply, I make myself wait for a
day. Then, I'll re-read the message and compose a reply off-line, wait
until later in the day, edit the reply and then finally post. Well,
the first part is really the code, all the rest "more like guidelines,
really" (paraphrased from Pirates of the Caribbean)

I know how much we all think of this as "our list" but, in point of
fact, it belongs to SpringChick. She created it and puts a lot of
effort into making it someplace we want/choose to spend our online
time. It's pretty much her world, and as the list owner, each of us is
only allowed to be here at her pleasure. Like it or not, that's the
way it is.

Now re: Bingo methods ... mine is strictly random. I decided which
items I was qualified to choose from, selected them at random, placed
them on my card in random order and when it was time for our own free
space, (LOL coming next) I closed my eyes, spun my card around and
pointed at a random spot. After I read how SC would've chosen, it made
great sense that I should've chosen a corner spot. However, I hate
math (always have) and my mind just doesn't work in that logical way.
Not only is logical thinking not second-nature to me, it's probably
not even tenth-nature. LOL!

Sail on!
