From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-02 06:49:59 UTC
Depending on the weather, is anyone interested in a get-together in
the Muskegon (MI) area on Sat 10/16/04 ??

There are lots of boxes in the Muskegon and White Lake area.
The Lake Express comes across for any WI folks who'd like to join in.
And best of all ... as Bindle Bum and I are just moving into our new
land-based winter quarters, I could host a campfire at the end of
the day.

Don't get too excited, I'm NOT thinking "gathering" (as in classes,
etc) I'm thinking SIMPLE here.

Simple as in boxers meeting somewhere for brunch then going boxing
together; sharing a BYO picinic lunch somewhere (or hitting a fast
food place -- depending on how cold/wet/hot/sweaty we are), and then
if anyone wants to join me in driving up to Montague, we could share
an end of the day campfire and all contribute to a pizza kitty.

I need to check with the rest of Team Bindlestiff, but it's possible
we could put up a couple of folks overnight if needed.

I'll carve an event stamp if enough people are going to participate.
If there isn't enough interest for a 'real' get together, that's ok.
I'm planning on going boxing that day anyway and anyone who wants to
join me is invited.

Please take note of vauge terms like "meeting somewhere" ... this is
by no means all planned out yet. Help is encouraged, here (grin)

Comments ... RSVP's ... Volunteers to help ... Questions???

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
