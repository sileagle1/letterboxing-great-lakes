Wasp in a Wig - Victory!

From: walesflyermary (drewes@WI.RR.com) | Date: 2008-09-20 00:53:39 UTC
May I share a victory? Today DH and I found a box listed as
unavailable, but found in May. Stubborn me couldn't let it go, as it
was the last unfound one in the state park near us (Lapham Peak in
Delafield, WI). Well, on the second try, with much methodical
searching we, okay "I", found it - wohoo! ;) Earlier in the day I'd
felt frustrated at not finding another box, but I asked DH if he wanted
to run away, so we thought we'd try "one last time" for the last box in
the nearby park. I am so psyched - and hope I have enough time before
work at noon tomorrow to look for box #2 in the series.... And so the
adventuring goes on! - WalesFlyerMary
