No boxing - Dr's orders!

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-01 20:00:33 UTC
Looks like winter carving activity for me can begin early this year!

Due to an exacerbation of an injury, (this goes back to how Bindle
Babe learned she couldn't fly ... by falling about 20 from the top
of our sailboat while it was in winter storage; also when The Foot
joining our team as a permanent member) I've been taken off work
until at least 10/5 with specific Dr's orders of no letterboxing.
(gasp!)

I'm scheduled for a bone scan tomorrow (results back in a week) with
treatment plans based on the results.

Now there is no worry about getting the 9 "quote" boxes for the Holy
Grail series done on time. (grin)

In the meantime, I've been given this great new fashion footwear,
which you can see here: http://tinyurl.com/8apxf which is
especially nice as I have the additional fashion accessory of a cane
that goes with it.

Now I know I've made you all eager to get your own fashion footwear,
but you cant get any for yourself without a Rx from a Dr. so for
now; youll just have to remain envious. (ha-ha to you!)

If you think Im kidding about my new footwear making me feel like a
fashion plate, youd be wrong. Heres how I feel that I look right
now: http://www.afo-lab.com/images/FootBandageCartoon.jpg I might
even post this as my new Bindle Babe photo in the files area on my
yahoo group. LOL!

Back to carving.

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
