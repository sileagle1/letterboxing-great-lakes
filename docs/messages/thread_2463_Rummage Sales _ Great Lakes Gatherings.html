Rummage Sales & Great Lakes Gatherings

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-01 09:24:32 UTC-07:00
So Deedle & I held our latest Yard Sale on Saturday.
Even though we made enough money to buy a good chunk
of drywall (we're finishing the 2nd story of Deedle's
bungalow), alas, no letterboxers appeared on our
doorstep. We would like to hold another yard sale at
some point this summer, but it won't be until the
July/August timeframe, so Dragon, if you have
forwarding instructions as to who The Rummage Sale
Letterbox should go to next, let us know!

Now, during our yard sale, I started work on the stamp
we're still planning on sending for the Great Lakes
Gathering (Wisconisin Hiker, can we also get a
reprive? The box should be ready to send out by this
weekend). And I have a lovely sunburn to show for it
- on my right arm only. I didn't think to put
sunscreen on my arms, but because I was carving and
hunched over our cash register table, only my right
arm got the sun. Up to my sleeve. You can even see
how I had my arm bent the entire time because there's
no sun in my inner elbow. How ever am I going to even
this tan out so I don't look silly in my wedding gown?
I should have sent a bottle of Aloe Vera Gel as my
Bingo Booty contribution... ;)

Doodle Bug.
