Well, if I hadn't already decided I couldn't attend the gathering
Saturday because of work, I would now not be attending because of an
injured dog. Somehow, don't ask me how, Claire rolled on a dead bird and
ended up needing surgery to remove a wing bone that was protruding from
her side. Sitting with woozy dog was a great opportunity to finish the
dog event stamp I had planned and started months ago. I've handed it
over to Kimono Girl and Flying Dragon, so Claire, Yuki, and I will be
there in spirit, if not body. Claire knows from repeated experience
(she's persistent) that Kimono Girl is not particularly fond of having
her face or teeth or tonsils licked, so try to find some other way for
your wonderful pup to ask for it! The stamp is for dogs attending the
gathering and their owners only (although if your dog is like mine and
prone to freak accidents that cannot really be explained and is at home
for that reason, I'm sure KG and FD could be talked into letting you
stamp in sans dog).

I hope every one has fun at the gathering! I'm looking forward to seeing
all the dogs' stamps in my book when KG and FD return.

Katy
