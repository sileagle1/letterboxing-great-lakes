Thanks for the Hart Family

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-05-20 04:05:20 UTC
First of all, I must say a huge THANK YOU for your prayers, thoughts
and notes to Matt & myself. He woke up this morning with a volunteer
giving him a STACK of ecards. The first signature he saw was Leapin
Lizard and he was immediately brightened! He was overwhelmed by the
outpouring from you guys. Thank you thank you thank you.

Here is the latest update. Matt will most likely be coming home
tomorrow. He had a very good day today. The day started with a
visit from our primary dr. He ordered an anti-inflamatory medicine
to work on the inflamed lung. After this injection, Matt immediately
started feeling better. By the afternoon he rated his pain at a 3/4
while yesterday it ranged from 5-9. The GI dr ordered another blood
draw to show the liver enzyme count. That came back this evening and
it had come down drastically. Matt was able to eat dinner too! The
GI dr has signed off on him so now we are waiting for a visit from
our primary dr tomorrow morning to fully release him. Matt will be
on the anti-inflamatory meds as well as the antibiotics for a while
and we don't know how long he will have to stay home before being
able to work...but he will be home and he isn't in pain!

We are still planning on heading to Minnesota in a week. We are so
excited about this trip and we look forward to thanking so many of
you in person! Thanks also for the tip on the care pages. I have a
friend who used these to communicate the struggle of their daughter.
Seeing the words carepages.com actually brought tears to my eyes.

Take care & I am sure that you will be hearing from Matt in the next
few days. I know that he is feeling better because he mentioned
wishing he had a laptop and was bummed that I didn't bring his latest
carving!

May God Bless everyone of you as He has blessed us through you!
Becki (hartx6)
