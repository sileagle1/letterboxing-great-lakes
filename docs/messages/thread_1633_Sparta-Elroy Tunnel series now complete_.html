Sparta-Elroy Tunnel series now complete!

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-23 20:46:50 UTC
Amongst our boxin' yesterday, I was finally able to check up on the
Tunnel #3 box and to place the companion Tunnel #2 and Tunnel #1
boxes. You'll find the clues posted on the LBNA site now.

Thanks to WI Hiker and Martini Man and others that have taken great
care of the Tunnel #3 box so that it was found in great shape when I
arrived! I had expected the worst, but other than the original
logbook being destroyed, everything else was okay. I'm so glad I
didn't have to carve a new stamp while I was there! (For those of
you that have been there, imagine carving for an hour there in the
middle of July!)

There is a bunch of bush-wacking to get to the Tunnel #3 box right
now, and we also saw lots of stinging nettles and poison ivy. As
far as ticks are concerned, we didn't see any there. Although I
braved it with three small kids and their mom, you might want to
wait until fall or spring.

I should also note that all three boxes now require that you bring
inking pens if you want to color them as suggested. Please clean
them when you are done, either way. I removed the pens from the
Tunnel #3 box. The tunnel #2 and #1 boxes don't require bush-
wacking, but all three boxes really require that you go during the
biking season when the tunnels are unlocked so you can walk through
them.

Also, you can always bike the trail to find the boxes instead of
hiking. Just remember you need a trail pass to bike on the trail,
but walking is free.

Off to MN!
