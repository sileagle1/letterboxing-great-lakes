Hi I'm Flotilla the Hun and I live in Brighton and work in Ypsilanti,
Michigan. I've been boxing since July 2005. I read about
Letterboxing in Time magazine in June of '05 right after I returned
from a trip to Connecticut, where I went to visit my nephew. My
nephew took me to Rockyneck State Park during my visit and sure enough
the article in Time refered to that park! Unfortunately, I wasn't
aware of letterboxing at the time we visited the park and I probably
stepped on the box that was planted there (no I didn't count it as a
find!) but once I tried boxing I was hooked! I have not had a lot of
spare time this year so my F count has stalled at around 120 or so,
but I've also planted a some boxes (had a few go missing GRRRRRHHHH!)
replaced a couple. I like both aspects of the hobby, but I like
finding the best (no maintenance). I've turned a few new boxers on to
the hobby but mostly I box alone. I love the secrecy of it! My trail
name comes from years ago, as a young man on canoe trips and rafting
voyages down many of Michigan's fine waterways I "ocassionally" tipped
a few fellow canoeists and rafters - hence my reputation! LOL.

well, thats about all - BOX ON!

FTH
