Veterans can take advantage of several free offers from local businesses
on Veterans Day, Sunday, November 11.


McCormick & Schmick's Seafood Restaurants are
offering veterans a complimentary entree from a special menu on that day.
Veterans are requested to call the restaurant to make reservations and to
present proper identification (VA card, VFW card, discharge papers, DD214
or other Veterans ID) to the server before ordering. McCormick &
Schmick's restaurants are located locally at 41 E. Chestnut Street
(telephone: 312-397-9500) and 1 E. Wacker Drive (telephone:
312-923-7226).



Veterans and active military are also invited to stop by their local
Turtle Wax Car Wash for a free car wash on Veterans Day. Proper military
or veterans' identification is required. Turtle Wax has 14 locations in
the Chicago metropolitan area, including 1550 N. Fremont Avenue in
Chicago.
