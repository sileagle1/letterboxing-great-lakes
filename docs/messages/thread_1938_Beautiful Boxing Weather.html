Beautiful Boxing Weather

From: thecottontails1 (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-31 02:36:14 UTC
What a gorgeous weekend!!! We brought some friends out letterboxing
with us on Saturday to help get them into it. We met Merlin and Ardea
on the trail at one of CMoon's boxes. A first for us, and naturally
for our newbie friends. (We're kind of newbies ourselves, we've only
been boxing since July). It was great meeting them on the trail.

We went out today to plant our first series in Elgin, IL. When we
were done with that, we headed back out to the Haunted Trail series to
get box 1, which had been missing when we were there rain-boxing last
weekend. While we were there, we met the Irish Frogs. We want to
thank CMoon, Kookuburra, and Squeek for this great series that seems
to have everyone meeting on the trail. It's almost like a mini
gathering in of itself, and the stamps are great. Besides the Irish
Frogs, we also saw 5 deer. Last week, we're pretty sure we saw a
wolf, or the world's largest coyote.
