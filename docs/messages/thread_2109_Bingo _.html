Hi Di!

Would love to have you play Bingo with us! The game is set up so
that anyone can play, regardless if they have never been
letterboxing in the Great Lakes region, or even if they just started
letterboxing yesterday and have never found a box or met another
letterboxer.

There are sufficient terms in the general section for you to still
be able to have a 30-item list. While it could be argued that there
may be a slight advantage to having some of the more obscure terms --
i.e. you have it and nobody else does when it is called, when it
comes right down to it -- statistically, each item on the list has
the same chance of being called as the next, so make up your list
and get out the lucky rabbit's foot -- although maybe Team Rabbit
has the edge on luck, after all they have 6 rabbit's feet over there.

Good luck!

SpringChick
