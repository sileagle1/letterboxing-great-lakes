We tried for Center Valley today with same result as you - no box! Searched
a lot of places besides the trees (including the outhouse!) with no success.

Since we were out of town this weekend, we didn't check email and of course
looked for the "Home of the Hamburger" box today. We got to the museum
early Sunday morning and needed to "buy a ticket". We didn't see anywhere
to do this and assumed it was in the museum, which was closed. No hours
were posted for the museum, so we didn't even know when we should return.
We came back mid-afternoon, thinking it might be open then, but it wasn't.
However, Lo & Behold!, there was now a ticket booth!!! Turns out the booth
had been moved to another location for the big "BurgerFest" yesterday (Tire
tracks in grass led us to this conclusion), but it had now been returned.
Luckily the "BurgerFest" was only a 1-day event, not the entire weekend.

We attempted to find all 11 boxes in this series today. As I've already
mentioned, we had no luck at Center Valley School. We also couldn't find
anything for "Play Ball", even though we were pretty sure we were at the
right spot. We did manage to find the other 9, but some were definitely a
challenge!!

We had a busy and very enjoyable boxing weekend. Found 2 boxes in Manitowoc
on Friday night on our way north. On Saturday we found many of the replica
boxes Dragon placed in Door County and Green Bay and also found all the
boxes Papeseed has placed in Green Bay & De Pere. Sunday we did the
Outagamie Historical Series plus a couple of others. Fun. fun, fun! Thanks
to all of you who plant boxes!

Wisconsin Hiker
