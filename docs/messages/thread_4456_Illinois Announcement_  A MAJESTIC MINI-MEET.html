A MAJESTIC MINI MEET
WHEN: SUN, SEPT 30TH, 2007, Time TBA.
WHERE: STREATOR, ILLINOIS

WHAT: It's a majestic mini-meet, come join fellow letterboxers as

Disney's "The Game Plan" at The Majestic Theater, 119 N Vermillion
St, Streator, Illinois (show times for the week have not been put out
yet, but I am guessing roughly somewhere between 12:30 and 2pm) Cost
for the movie is $5.25 plus any concessions. Everything working
out how it should, we will then proceed across the street to Scoops N
More Ice Cream Parlor for some letterboxing fun. If the ice cream
parlor is unavailable, we will be meeting at the community hall.
