What a cute story Leapin Lizards.

We are Spartanfans, comprised of Mike, Heather and Matthew. Our
trainame comes from our love of Michigan State Univ., where we met
and fell in love, Awwwwww.

Heather saw a link to letterboxing.org on a board for parents with
babies born in March 2005. This site has hundreds of hits a day.
We feel it was divine intervention that we came across this post. LOL

We found a LB under a mile from our house, and thanks to StampinPam,
we were hooked. This was April 8th of this year. Three weeks later
we found our 2nd box and our 1st hitchhiker. May 6th found us at
our first gathering and we were well on our way.

We have boxed in 3 different states, in downpours and over 100
degree weather. We have converted Heather's parents (Hoot Owl and
Grey Owl) and an Aunt and Uncle who live in Maine. After 75 boxes
and a trip around the world we feel there is no end in sight. We
love meeting other boxers, and the riddle aspect of letterboxing.
We can't wait until our 17 month old, Matthew, can figure out the
clues by himself, and we eagerly await the delivery of our newest
boxer due April, 2007.
