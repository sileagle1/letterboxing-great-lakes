From: Jason (lewwdawg_840@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-02 14:39:42 UTC
I just wanted to send out a "Thanks" to everyone who organized this
wonderful gathering. Jason & I absolutely loved the park. We came up
early on Friday to get Gooble's series so we got to enjoy two days
exploring the trails. And although my legs are crying now from
climbing up those hills - I am so very glad that we made the trek out
to MN for this one.

We got so involved with the actual boxing this year that we missed out
on the group lunch & picture and only got to do a couple exchanges, but
we found almost every box in the clue packet in addition to the Snow
Friends series and Gooble's series. We probably could have found them
all, but at about 5:30pm on Saturday we decided to call it quits. We
were both too exhausted and just wanted to hit the hot tub at the
hotel :)

But it was really nice to get back on the trails with all you wonderful
folk! We got to box alongside a couple groups - the Papeseed &
Triclops group, TJMich & company, the Leapin Lizards/Dragon crew -
which is always a fun experience. Usually Jason & I box alone so it's
a nice treat for us to box with a group.

Thanks again to all involved with the gathering. We really enjoyed
ourselves and hope to see you all on the trails soon.

Julie (Wormy Wanderers)
