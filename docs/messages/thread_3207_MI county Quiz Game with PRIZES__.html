Ok, I'm finally in the area with some free-time and bumming that I
missed the Mystery Gathering. So I just put together a contest for MI
boxers which I hope will spark some carving ideas, get us excited and
encouraged about getting our counties all "covered" with an active
letterbox. (Competative? Who, me?! LOL!!)

WHO CAN PLAY:
1. Players must either be residents of MI or ABLE (not just willing)
to place a box here BEFORE Thanksgiving (yes, that would be Thursday,
November 23 of 2006 for you nit-pickers)

2. You can play as an individual, a family, or a team but please
don't enter yourself in more than one category (ie: individual answers
as well as team and/or family answers) because it wouldn't really be
fair and MI is know for being picky about what's right and wrong

PRIZES for the Quiz Game:
1st prize a gift certificate from Stampeaz, the PZ Kut folks
2nd prize a Staedtler-Mars Grand eraser
3rd prize #11 carving blades
4th prize #1 carving nibs


RULES:
1. Go to http://tinyurl.com/tzr6g where you will find a file called,
" Unboxed MI counties QUIZ GAME" (if the link doesn't work, you can
just go to our group homepage and click on files in the left-side menu)

2. Find the answers to the quiz questions and email them to me.
DO NOT POST YOUR ANSWERS TO THE LIST!!

3. There is a question for each "unboxed" MI county plus at least one
bonus question and even a few extra credit points available. For each
correct answer you will be awarded a "point" which will translate into
your name on a slip of paper (ie: the more points, the more slips of
paper with your name) All slips of paper with names will go into the
drawing. Winners will be selected at random by my Grandson in two
weeks ... on Saturday, November 4th (2006) and announced on the list
by 11pm that night.

Sail on!
