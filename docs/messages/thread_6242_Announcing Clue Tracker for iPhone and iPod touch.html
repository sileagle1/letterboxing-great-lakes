Announcing Clue Tracker for iPhone and iPod touch

From: kathleenbrade (brade@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-01-11 14:43:33 UTC
[Please excuse the commercial announcement but I think this will be of interest to many people on this list.]

Hello!

I am happy to announce the availability of Clue Tracker for the iPhone and iPod touch. Clue Tracker is a location-aware mobile app that makes it easy to find and use letterbox clues. Learn more here: http://cluetracker.pearlcrescent.com/

Clue Tracker's features include:
* Find nearby boxes based on your current location.
* Find boxes near a location that you enter
(partial address or zip code).
* Access letterbox listings from both Atlas Quest and LbNA.
* For boxes listed on LbNA, view clues within Clue Tracker
and optionally save them for offline use (in this version,
Atlas Quest search results are opened in Safari).

The cost for the app is $4.99 which includes upgrades. The ability to view Atlas Quest box information and clues in the app is coming soon.

Also, we love to receive feedback and suggestions so please send them to us via this page: http://cluetracker.pearlcrescent.com/feedback/

-- MichKathy
