Hmm, snow on Easter? Reminds me of last year on Easter weekend (which
was a week or two into April) when Atom and I went to find the last
couple of grail boxes we needed in OH. We did a weird driving loop and
were driving north after finding "Camelot: it's only a Model" near
Dayton. Things were fine (mostly clear skies, lower 30s probably) until
all of a sudden we reached Cleveland and it was a blizzard!! Of course
we still went to the park and found the "The Old Man" under all of the
day's fresh snow just as it got dark. It was the very last box we
needed to finish the quest too. Ahhh, good memories! :) I posted an
album with some pictures from that fateful day in the photos section --
enjoy!

Here it's been snowing all day too -- big, wet flakes. Luckily it's not
accumulating too much though. But I think it was a good idea for us to
postpone our weekend in Milwaukee until next weekend...
