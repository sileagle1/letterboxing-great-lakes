Doodle Bug here, aka Diane. My husband Jeff would be
the Deedle Bug part of our couple. We live in
Cleveland, Ohio, and have been letterboxing since
March 2004. We actually found out about letterboxing
in the fall of 2003 while searching for information on
Squire's Castle online. One of our hits was for clues
to a letterbox hidden in the area. As excited as we
were reading all about letterboxing, it took us a
while to finally carve our signature stamp and get out
on the trails (and that Cleveland winter also deterred
us some). We weren't able to find the Squire's Castle
box on our first try, but that didn't deter us from
become the addicts that we now are (ok, me a little
more than Deedle). We finally did find the box and we
even got to meet the woman responsible for this
afflication of ours, Blaze, at the unofficial
Brandywine Falls mini-meet that The Cat's Meow
mentioned.

Also, The Cat's Meow mentioned a stamp that Gamecock
had carved for us in honor of our engagement at that
Mini-Meet. We use that stamp as our personal
traveller. If you meet us on the trails or at a
gathering, tell us how we first met to add the image
to your log book.

Looking forward to seeing some of you in 2007 at the
Great Lakes Gathering, if not sooner!

Doodle Bug.
