From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-10-26 20:50:46 UTC-05:00
Tim Allen?? Jamie Lee Curtis??? Ha! They've got nothing on us. Heck,
I've been celebrating Christmas with the Kranks since 1980 when I met Tom
and his family, and now I'm one of the Kranks myself!

Anyway, in honor of the impending release of this new movie, I have created
a new postal box I'd like to share with you, my LbGLK friends, this holiday
season. And I hope that even if you are not a PLB kind of person, you'll
make an exception and share the holidays with me!

I'd like this box to travel to as many of you as it can by Christmas. To
facilitate that, I'm hoping we can send it out by state, perhaps starting in
Michigan, letting it tour the area a while, passing it across to Wisconsin
for the mini-meet, and then take it from there. I'd like it to be home for
Christmas (and to keep it from getting lost in the holiday mailing rush),
and then it can go out again to hit more of you.

SpringChick, do you mind if I send it to you first, not only because I have
your address, but also because I want to send you that HH I talked to you
about?

If there are clusters of you who can get together to stamp in, that would be
great.

Please send me your address off-list if you'd like to participate. I'll
send it out ASAP, pending SpringChick's response, with as many Michigan
addresses as I have to that point. When the last person on that list
receives it, email me for more addresses.

Priority mail with as fast a turnaround time as possible would be
appreciated, as would delivery confirmation and email notification of its
whereabouts. I know I should have started this sooner, but I just got the
idea a few days ago, and just finished the stamp and book.

Happy holidays...and God bless us, everyone!!

--klk

A copy of this will also be kept in the FungusWoman clues folder in the
Clues and more Clues file.
