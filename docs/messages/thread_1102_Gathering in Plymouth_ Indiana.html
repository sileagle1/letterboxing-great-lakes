I have been eavesdropping on your conversations for a while now, but this is the first
time that I have written. My name is Flagbearer and along with my sister, Full of
Heart, make up "the sisters act". We wish to extent the following invitation.

Date: May 21, 2005
Place: Plymouth, IN Centennial Park
Marshall County

Marshall County has a growing number of letterboxes and, I hope, letterboxers. If
you have noticed this and have been wanting to come to this area, plan a trip on May
21 and join us for a fun day of letterboxing. This is an opportunity for us to get to
know each other, exchange stamps, and find the boxes in the area. There will be a
number of stamps that will be placed for just the one day, including a set of of 8
stamps that will make a completed object when all are found.

Hope you will plan to join us. We would like to know ahead of time if you will be
attending so that we will have enough handouts and freebies. We have a number of
surprises planned to make the day special. Contact flagbearer at
the_sisters_act@yahoo.com for more information.
