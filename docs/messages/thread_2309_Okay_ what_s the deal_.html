Sil,

I was in another town yesterday and I went to Hobby Lobby to get some
logbook making supplies. One of the clerks asked me if I needed help. I
said I was looking for a number of things and that I hadn't been able to
find carving medium for quite a while in Indy. She said the store was in
the middle of reorganizing and she found some in a bushel basket on top of
the shelves in the painting supplies area. Who would have known? Even
though I just purchased 3 pieces of PZKut last week, I picked up on of the
thick pieces of master-carve at 40% off. They had at least one more and a
couple of large pieces of pink stuff. She said she would place another
order for either as I needed it.

If you want my mastercarve or want me to go and get the rest of their
supply, let me know. If the weather is nice on Saturday I will be back in
that town.
