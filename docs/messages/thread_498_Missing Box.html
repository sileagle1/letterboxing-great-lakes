Missing Box

From: Timbertoes (toomey@genevaonline.com) | Date: 2004-08-08 03:51:39 UTC
I am so bummed! My very first box with one of my best stamps has
gone missing. I stopped by today with my non-boxing husband to show
him how cool the placement was and, once I realized it really was
gone, searched all over to no avail. On the bright side, my husband
(yes, the same non-boxing one!) found an excellent replacement spot
that I never even saw the first time around! With a few minor
changes to the clues it should work out just fine. Now I just have
to redo the stamp and get a new box out there. I guess this
unfortunate experience just makes me a more seasoned boxer...

Timbertoes


Re: Missing Box

From: Kathi (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-08-08 06:14:39 UTC

Timbertoes, I know EXACTLY how you feel!! I was so proud of my first
carving attempt and first box placement. It really upset me when
FOOW1 disappeared...the only thing left behind was the pencil that I
had put into the box (which, BTW, was not even hot pink or
anything...just a plain white rubbermaid lid). I must have gone back
a half a dozen times thinking someone would replace it. After
my "period of mourning" I decided I wouldn't let a box thief have the
upper hand, so I went back and replaced it. So far, so good. I
still feel bad, though.
