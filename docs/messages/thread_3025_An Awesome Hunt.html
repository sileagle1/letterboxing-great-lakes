An Awesome Hunt

From: pinkie21299 (stjohn116@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-09-01 00:07:02 UTC
We had a great letterboxing day today! Isn't it convenient that our
daughter now has therapy in Dupage Co? What better excuse to go for
the boxes there!
Thanks to Midnight Visitor for a wonderful clue, awesome hunt and
great stamp. I highly recommend "Untitled".
We saw several parks that we would never have known were even there.
On one trail, we saw a very well preserved (and very clean) skeleton
of a small mammal. We haven't ID it yet, but know it is bigger than a
squirrel, but not as big as a racoon. Our 7yo daughter declared that
it was totally worth becoming a burr-ball to see it.
Yeah!
