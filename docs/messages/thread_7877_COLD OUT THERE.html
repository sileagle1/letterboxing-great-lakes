From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2014-01-27 09:55:52 UTC-06:00

Yes, this was the COLDEST Dead of Winter yet, but we were very impressed with the number of people that still went out on the trails to do some fossil hunting and to take in the Night Flight sights!  We appreciated the carvers and the planters (TrailRider & NamVet) that made this possible. 

 

Thanks to ggghiker for hosting another fun event and to Hartx6 for carving a nice event stamp.  We were also happy that a hardy bunch of letterboxers braved the weather with tasty treats in hand to provide another delicious Great Lakes potluck.  When we weren't on the trail, we enjoyed chatting with people in the comfy lodge - with the sun streaming in and a fire to keep things cozy. 

 

Nonetheless, I hope this winter is on its way OUT - we have had ENOUGH ALREADY!
