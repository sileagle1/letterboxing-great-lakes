Hi Everyone,

Just dropping a quick note to let you all know that on Wednesday
(2/15) we found out that box # 2 of our Sternennacht Series was
missing. We went out yesterday to check, and sure enough, the rangers
had done some brush clearing with our mild winte weather. The
location of the box no longer existed. We have recarved and replaced
box # 2 today. We also checked on the other 2 boxes and all is well
with them. The clues on the LbNA website are up-to-date.

Also, stay tuned later this week, early next week for some Easter news.
