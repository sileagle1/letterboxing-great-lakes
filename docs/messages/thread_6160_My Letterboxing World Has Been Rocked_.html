I'm rather persnickety over my counts as well.

However, when I first started I counted HH's in with my F-count. Once I got
to about F300 I reconsidered and went through and deleted out all the HH's
I counted as finds. So, ALL my F-counts changed and I lost about 22 off
the total number.

I log my PFX counts in my logbook alongside the stamp image, so I have some
overlap and double numbers in my logbooks. However, my spread sheet is now
spot on (as far as I can tell) :-)
