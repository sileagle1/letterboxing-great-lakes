From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-10-27 09:48:48 UTC-07:00

Just a reminder that I will be at the Fall Into Fun event in Rockford, IL on Saturday NOV 7 and will be able to collect your Bingo Booty then if you want to play the slowest Bingo game in the world.  The game will start on JAN 1, 2016 and I should have the word list out by the end of November.


Bringing your booty to the event will let you save some mailing costs AND, more importantly, allow you to cross this off your list before the hectic holiday season!


If you do bring your booty, please also include the $2 fee (which goes towards postage to mail prizes and any excess goes toward gift cards).  PLEASE put your NAME with the booty & fee so I can keep my records straight.  I've already created the database to keep track of players based on who has already given me booty at the recent WI and MI events. You can find the database on the Yahoo message board under "More", then "Database".  Feel free to add a record with your trail name if you plan to play.  


It's not too late to send me suggestions for any new twists for the 2016 game.


Don't miss the thrilling game that helps you get out of bed in the morning on those cold winter days!!
