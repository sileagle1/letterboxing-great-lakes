--- In LbGLK@yahoogroups.com, "Debbie Kotlarek" wrote:

> So where did everyone box this past weekend?

Happily, we were up in Door County for a long weekend. Letterboxing was at a minimum as we had people and places to see that didn't have a letterboxing component! A good analogy would be in the playing of the game "Password" where you were under a timed limit and had to pass on words (or in our cases letterboxes) that gave us any hick-up. As a result, due to awaiting ferries, rain, scheduled dinners, sore hips or sleeping friends in cars, we had to give up sooner than we'd like on some boxes which proved to be a little frustrating. However, over all, the time in Door County was wonderful! We can thank Dragon, Leapin Lizards, Verona Bird Patrol, Merlin & Ardea, and Beetle to name a few for the 26 boxes that we did manage to find and enjoy.

We hid a box in Door County 3 years ago, but forgot the clues...(must be a Wisconsin thing!) Fortunately, we found "her" and incorporated her into a new series that we placed for all of you Wisconsinites to find. It is newly listed on LBNA as "Tortoise and the Silly Hare". We didn't have time to retrace our clues..so we're hoping someone will be able to give them a test run soon and let us know how we did at writing clues from our scrawled notes!
Tiptoe & Tonto
