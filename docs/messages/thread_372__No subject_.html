From: Heather M. Fieldhouse (bunnyhugger@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-01-29 18:32:44 UTC-05:00
Hi folks! I started a new job last August and this resulted in my
being very busy. As a result, I didn't do any boxing for a long
while. Recently, though, my schedule has gotten a little more
manageable so I have tried to get back into boxing. Unfortunately,
my husband and I have had a run of bad luck -- three times in a row
we've failed to find a box and had to go home empty-handed. :(

Anyway, the main point of this post is to make a couple of
announcements. First, at long last, my original box, "Say Yes to
Michigan" has been replanted. I'm sure everyone had given up on it,
but today we found a new spot for it in downtown Lansing and are
crossing our fingers that it won't go missing for a third time.

Second, I have placed a box at Central Michigan University in Mt.
Pleasant to commemorate my job there. It's called "The Owl of
Athena." My stamp carving skills are improving and I'm rather proud
of it (though it doesn't compare to the elaborate stamps that I have
seen in other people's boxes!).

Both boxes are listed on LbNA. Hope you like them!

Heather F.
