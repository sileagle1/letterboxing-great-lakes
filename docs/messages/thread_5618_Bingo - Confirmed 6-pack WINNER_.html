Bingo - Confirmed 6-pack WINNER!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-02-02 11:12:23 UTC
Not to rain on the misery parade or flaunt luck at anyone, but we have
a confirmed winner in the 6-pack game! MobiusKlein completed a 6-pack
with 2nd and 3rd row words in the I, N and G columns. Winning words
were I-Ink Pad, I-WOM Clue, N-Muggle, N-Backpack, G-Pace and
G-Compass. His win was with Saturday's word, Ink Pad.

Just a note of interest... for those who were disappointed that they
only could select from the general terms to fill in their cards,
please note that all of these were general terms ;-)

Congratulation to MobiusKlein! Well, folks the game is on! The first
week of February is where we have traditionally seen the straight line
Bingo winner also, so we'll see if this year follows suit.

This means that the 6-pack game is now out of play. In addition to
the full card game, remaining interim games are:

Straight Line
4 Corners
"X"
10 Spot

Good luck to everyone playing!

SpringChick
