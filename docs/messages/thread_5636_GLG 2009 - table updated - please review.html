From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-06 16:44:26 UTC
I finally took the time this morning to gather much information from here and AQ to see
where we stand regarding event letterboxes. Please accept my apologies if I overlooked a
post, or did not respond. I think I've found all of them now.

Several letterboxers offered to carve stamps that are still needed. Thank you, thank you.

It looks like we have enough green trail stamps/letterboxes. You can start mailing them
to me if they are ready. I think I sent my mailing address to everyone who needs it.

Here's where we need additional help/coverage:

Shelter boxes. I added listings in the table where I will fill in your name after you confirm
your submission. I envisioned a series of Favorite State Parks - 2 per state. The series
could, but certainly does not have to, remain a group series available for future
gatherings. I have a couple of donations pending. If you want to help out here, let me
know which state park you'd like to represent. If the series remains intact, I'll have a
logbook made after receiving them. If you want to keep your stamp, we will just have
individual logbooks at the shelter.

I haven't really had any takers on the other shelter series ideas. The conservation
movement and the 1920-30's. I updated the table and you will see "Our resourceful
ancestors." I thought this would make a nice series - ideas generated an put into action in
the 1930's. My folks were Depression children and I learned much from my grandparents.
My grandmother never tossed a food container -- might be useful! What a letterboxer
she could have been.

The other idea I had was to have some of the shelter boxes remain PT's - as long as they
fall into one of the major themes of the day. That way, those of you who want to create
puzzle boxes in advance, will be able to post clues ahead of time. Please add your
thoughts here.

There will be a few LTC's with a Green theme at the event. I'm not heavily into the major
group swaps by mail, but I will have one for every attendee as you sign in. I think it was
Dartmoor Dreamer that has a Tracker at AQ - you will have a deadline for that LTC swap.


Thanks again!

~speedsquare

From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-08 17:15:07 UTC
1. All add MN - Split Rock Lighthouse to the database, along with your potluck items.
2. The shelter boxes will be on the tables. Some folks will carve special PT's for the event
using one or more of the themes. Some of us like puzzle boxes, and due to the
organization involved it is easier to create a personal traveller/shelter box for this
purpose. Creators can make the clues easy to mind-bending. Some might involve
research prior to the event day and to get the clues listed, PT's are a simple way to
disseminate these clues. Other shelter boxes (series) will have not have extra work to
solve. They are just there for the stamping.

I'm planning on having a event specific LTC, my very favorite PT, and my signature stamp
with me at the event. I haven't signed up for a specific event box at this point as I am
waiting for others to fill in their requests.

If you have any other specific questions or want ideas, let me know.

~speedsquare
