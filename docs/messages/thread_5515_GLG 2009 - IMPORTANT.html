GLG 2009 - IMPORTANT

From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-07 17:40:17 UTC
Thank you, Wisconsin Hiker! I am presently getting ready to post more information. Yes,
folks, if you haven't made lodging reservations, do so as fast as you can. In some parks,
reservations are snapped up one year in advance. If you haven't signed up to attend the
event, please do that soon before we hit the maximum of 150.

I planned to post most of the information here, but I worry that my posts would get lost
with the Bingo activity. Plus, the sign-up list at Atlas Quest is nearly 100 already. I think
it makes sense to post there.

I cannot vouch for local lodging options and their suitability. If you need additional
assistance, please let me know. Due to other obligations, I just didn't have the resources
to create a website for this event. I am doing my best to see that this event is very low-
stress for me, and not competing for the blast-of-the-century award.

LOCATION REVEALED - We will be using the Apple Orchard Shelter. Pale Puppy Posse
contacted me and offered to spearhead the Potluck. THANK YOU SO MUCH! Look for
those details at Atlas Quest.

I haven't delegated the "OFFICIAL EVENT STAMP" yet. No takers? I have a simple design in
my head that I will work on later today.

WE NEED TRAIL BOXES!!
We have permission to place 12-15 temporary boxes for our event. I'm going to create 15
listings at AQ.

Guidelines for trail boxes -

1. All Things Green (15 boxes max) - please submit your idea to me for listing in the
series posted at AQ. For this series, please design your stamp to use GREEN ink only (no
metallics, unless you provide the ink with the box). Any GREEN IDEA is acceptable. Since
we are emphasizing "green" I'm suggesting the design not exceed the size of an LTC
trading card (2.5x3.5?). Why? Simple! After the event, these stamps can be returned to
the owners to be recycled!!! An added bonus - we can create uniform logbooks and have
each container look more official.

Deadline for mailing - March 15, 2009.
Include in mailing - stamp; green inkpad (optional). In return you will receive the logbook
created for your stamp, including the event stamp, and all the finders during GLG 2009.
And of course, your stamp. If the stamp is lost you agree not to hold speedsquare et al
responsible.

Shelter Boxes (series) --

1. The Conservation Movement
2. The 1920's & 1930's
3. Our State Parks (favorites from each GL state)

These will be listed as Travelling Event Boxes at Atlas Quest. No restriction on size and
color. It would be nice to see at least 2 submissions from each of the Great Lakes States.
Contact me with your requests/reservations. No limit set at this time.

Deadline for Mailing - April 15, 2009
I will send the mailing address under separate cover.

Letterbox trading cards (LTC') - will be welcome, especially if they incorporate any of the
themes listed above.

If you are available Tues-Thurs before the event and want to help plant, contact me off-
list. I may be able to offer lodging at Clear Lake.

---- That's it for now ----

~speedsquare
