MN State Parks Experience

From: Scott Farley (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-14 12:12:06 UTC-07:00
For what it's worth, we've had some communication with Gene Groebner,from the Minnesota DNR. He works at Afton State Park, and seems to have tracked down several boxes, including the three that we planted for the event. He forwarded coordinates to us, along with a link to the application, and has offered to replace the boxes once the applications are approved. If you're looking for more information about boxes in Afton, I would recommend reaching out to him at: Gene.Groebner@dnr.state.mn.us.
Best Regards,
