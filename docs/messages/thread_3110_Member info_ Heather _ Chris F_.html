I'm Heather, of "Heather & Chris F." (we don't have trail names).
Chris is my husband. We are based in Lansing, Michigan. We started
letterboxing in February '05 after I accidentally Googled a clue
sheet while looking for information about the Catoosa Blue Whale (a
Route 66 landmark). We have planted four boxes plus two hitchhikers,
and we have found somewhere around 55 boxes including hitchhikers.

We always go letterboxing together -- neither has ever gotten a box
without the other. Nevertheless, we have two separate (and
thematically unrelated) signature stamps, and we keep separate
logbooks. This means that for the most part our logs are the same,
but we have the freedom to choose different colors of ink, and
sometimes where one person failed to get a good impression, the other
succeeded. Thus it provides useful redundancy.

Heather F.
