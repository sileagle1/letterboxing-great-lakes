Hi Kathy,

In answer to some of your gathering questions... Not sure if there
really is a rule as to how you count the boxes that you create and
then send to someone else to hide. I have contributed several boxes
to events and series around the country in this way, and I have
never counted them at all. But it is up to you -- some people count
them as a plant, some count as a 1/2 -- not a big deal, just however
you feel comfortable doing it.

It is up the the creator of the box to decide it's fate. If you
want the box back after the gathering, just take it home with you or
let Tim know and perhaps it can be sent to you at a later date,
etc. If you decide to leave it, it would be your responsibility to
maintain though unless you make an arrangements with somebody else
in the area.

As for personal travellers... yes -- keep your eyes and ears open.
Another good idea is to search through the archives at the big list -
- you don't have to read them all, just do some searches on the
words personal traveller (both spellings as some use only one "l")
and the posting names of people... Also sometimes people who have
personal LB'ing web sites will have hints on their web sites, etc.

I like the idea of a "what to expect at a gathering" file. I will
spend some time this weekend pulling info from the various messages
that have been posted here and at the other groups and add them into
one place in the files section.

SpringChick
