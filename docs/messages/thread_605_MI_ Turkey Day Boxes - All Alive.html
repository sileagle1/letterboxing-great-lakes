MI: Turkey Day Boxes - All Alive

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-08-28 02:15:45 UTC
I have had a few reports over the past month about the possibility
that 1 or more of the Turkey Day boxes along the Buck Creek Trail in
Grandville may be missing. Seems one box in particular has been
eluding many people.

So today on my way home from hunting down a few G.R. boxes I stopped
to check on the Turkey Day boxes and am happy to report that all 5
boxes are safe and sound and located as they should be. Did a bit
of cleaning up and even grabbed a HH out of one of them.

I will try to e-mail the folks who contacted me about them, as I'm
not sure they are on this list, but just wanted to post it in case
if I missed anyone.

This is the first time I have been there when the area was in full
foliage and I can understand why some of the clues are a bit
tricky. If you are having problems with some of the boxes, go back
after the leaves are gone and the bush dies back.

SpringChick
