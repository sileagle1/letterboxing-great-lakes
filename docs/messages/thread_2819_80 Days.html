From: pgranola2002 (p2jd@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-07-26 02:33:30 UTC
I AM DONE! I went up and got that box #8 today. I am as happy as
Dragon on his 1000 find. Congrats to you Dragon!
What made my last find even more special is that as I was stamping in,
another car pulled in and out jumped someone who looked suspiciously
like a boxer. I asked her if she was looking for the stamp and she
said she was, her mother was with her as well and we did an exchange
and wouldn't you know it, she was finishing up the series and that was
the last stamp she needed too. So we rehid the stamp so she could
find it and we finished at the same time. How cool! Congratulations
to Spartanfans and Hoot Owl.
Well I am sad to say that it looks like I will be the only one who
finished outside of the 80 day time period, I was counting up the days
and I think it turned out to be 380 days...do you have a stamp for the
one who finishes dead last per chance? How about a perseverance stamp?
(or obsessed stamp? Anyway, thanks Azobox and Spring Chick, it was a
great journey and I can honestly say that I saw more of Michigan than
I ever dreamed possible!

P.Granola
