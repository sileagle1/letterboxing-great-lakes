Michigan or Bust

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2010-07-13 06:19:22 UTC
Finally!!!! A trip to Michigan -- I used to head up to Michigan several times a year growing up. Now its been nearly 5 years (we were pregnant with the twins) since our last visit.

This will be the first trip to MI since we started boxing and look forward to finding boxes from the folks of Michigan -- I also hope to meet up with some of you while we are there.

Please forgive us, as this will be a relatively short trip and it will be packed to the gills with family activities opportunities to meet all you wonderful folks will be either on-the-fly or later in the evening.

Day 1: Wed. August 11 -- drive from Moline to Grand Rapids staying at the Fairfield Inn. We plan on stopping at Warren Dunes and possibly Holland en-route.

Day 2: Turs. August 12 -- drive from Grand Rapids to Stanton. Will be spending much of Thurs. in Grand Rapids.

Day 3: Fri. August 13 -- drive from Stanton to Battle Creek staying at the Holiday Inn.

I will be with family all weekend in Portage. We will be returning home on Monday.

There wont be time for any "gathering" but if you are willing/able to meet us "after-hours" we would be thrilled!!!

If you live along our route and want to meet up during the day for a quick exchange please contact me for my cell phone #.

Looking forward to visiting the wonderful state of Michigan.

hx6

P.S. If there are any drive-by type boxes along our route that we just *have* to get -- please let me know what they are. I will be spending a good amount of time entering them in the GPS over the next month.
