Here in West Michigan, Dollar Tree carries a decent clone of the LnL that holds up well and stays shut. They are sandwich size. The thing to test with the imitation LnL styles is to make sure they stay shut -- I bought some online at American Science a couple of years ago and they don't catch good and therefore don't stay closed.

We found a few boxes in California that were in those sport valuables
cases. They are waterproof and have a clamp down lid. They were
probably 3.5 x 5 inches and maybe 1.5-2 inches thick. About half the

place to buy them. No luck yet, but I haven't really started looking.
I think they would be much easier to hide and hold up to the elements.
