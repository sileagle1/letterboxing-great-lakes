From: Sara (fivestmc5@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-11-03 17:30:44 UTC
I have been a carving fool since I got some speedycarve last weekend.
I carved 4 stamps in 5 days and only have a small chunk left waiting
until for my stampeaz order arrives.

I had to carve a new team stamp cause my first one broke but then I
decided to carve another team stamp for when just my daughter (Baby
Apple Bloom) and I go out together and I love how it turned out,
specally with fairly small text. It measures 1 1/4 X 2 1/4 inches

I got my first two boxes completed and one already hidden, just
working on the clues for the second one.

I think this link will work for my one team stamp
http://ph.groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/photos/view/8be6?b=1

Sorry I'm just so excited, I realize others don't post like this but
this one I think deserves a little pat on the back.

Thanks
