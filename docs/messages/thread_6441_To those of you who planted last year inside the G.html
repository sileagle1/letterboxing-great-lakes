To those of you who planted last year inside the GR Public Museum...a question

From: cpjenks2001 (cpjenks2001@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-17 18:53:50 UTC
I saw clues for 3-4 (?) boxes planted last year during the MAPS exhibit. That gave me the inspiration to plant some this year for the Big Bugs exhibit. Lulu and I were there this past weekend scouting out good hiding spots and coming up with ideas for clues.

When you planted yours, did you include your contact info somewhere in the logbook or the box? I normally include that in my plants, but I'm such a fraidy cat and I worry that if the boxes are found by museum staff, they'll somehow figure out who I am by my contact info (mainly my email), and they'll revoke my membership or something stupid ;o)

I hope to get these carved and planted next week. Thanks!

~pjynx
