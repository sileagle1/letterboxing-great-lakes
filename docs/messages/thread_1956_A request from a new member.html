A request from a new member

From: marisa1of9 (marisafink@bsu.edu) | Date: 2005-11-04 21:38:29 UTC
I am an avid letterboxer and have several boxes along Lake Michigan
listed on LBNA as 2bentriders.

Currently I am a doctoral student at Ball State University working on
my dissertation - a descriptive study of letterboxing. So...You are
invited to participate in a web-based survey looking at the profiles
and behaviors of individuals who participate in letterboxing. This
research is being conducted as part of a doctoral dissertation about
the activity of letterboxing. Any visitor 18 years of age and older
may complete the survey, and all responses are anonymous. To
voluntarily participate, go to
http://portal.bsu.edu/inqsit/inqsit.cgi/fink and select
`letterboxing'and 'continue'. Use your trail name or a pseudonym to
sign in. For more information about this research project, please
contact the Primary Investigator - Marisa Fink, Ball State University,
AC 414, 2000 W. University Avenue, Muncie, IN 47306, 765.285.1962,
marisafink@bsu.edu. Thank you for your participation. (Note: If you
participated in the pilot study for this research in February 2005,
you may also participate in this study).
