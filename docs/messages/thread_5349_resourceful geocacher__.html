From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2008-10-09 22:34:07 UTC-07:00
Edited to disguise the location, I received the following message from a geocacher who found one of my letterboxes that shares a hiding place with a cache:

"Yes, It is fine. It was slightly damp but not to worry with the paper in the little plastic bag. We had no Stamp Pad, but made several copies of your wonderful rendition using my wife's eye-liner. I had read on the Geo-Cache site that there was a Letterbox there, and was greatly tickled to find it. We found three total that day, so it was a rewarding Friday. We still have not located the Geo-Cache there at XXXXXX."

Got a good chuckle out of that one!
