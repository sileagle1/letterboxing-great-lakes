Good news in the 'Zoo

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-15 22:10:54 UTC
"Celery Flats" letterbox was reported missing quite awhile back
(including reports from highly reliable boxers from states to the
west.) Those reports had me convinced that it was very likely MIA.
Today, with a rare glimpse of blue skies and reasonable temps outside,
Artistamp and I headed out to make a definitive assessment......Was
it there or was it missing?
YEA! It was still in place!
So to those who tried in vain, my apologies....and my invitation to
return to MI and give it a go.

There had abeen a great deal of brush-clearing in the area of the box
and I suspect that newly created obstacles obscured the placement from
even resourceful seekers. Artistamp rolled a log away from atop the
box place and there it was.

I'll repost the clues with a minor change.
BTW, the last recorded visitor was in May 2005. There had been quite a
few prior to that.
It's a favorite box of mine for many reasons, but most of all because
it was SpringChick's F-100 and I *think* it has both of her sig stamps
(pre F-100 and post F-100).

Perhaps it's a hint of good things to come.
