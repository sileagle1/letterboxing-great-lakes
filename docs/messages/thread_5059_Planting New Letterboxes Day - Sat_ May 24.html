Planting New Letterboxes Day - Sat. May 24

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-24 05:08:18 UTC
Atlas Quest Webmaster Ryan has declared that Sat. May 24 is Planting
New Letterboxes Day. Not a Hallmark card sending holiday, but for
letterboxers, it's a day to remind us that new letterboxes are always
needed. So, I was up till 5 am last night (Why do I stay up that
late?), and I'm up tonight putting the finishing touches of several new
letterboxes to be placed in the Lake County area. I'm actually proud
of these newly carved stamps. PZ Kut has made all the difference...and
lots of practicing, and tips from Fungus Woman too. Clues will be
posted on Saturday evening. That means Sunday and Monday will be two
days for letterboxers to hit the trails and find some new boxes. I
filled my gas tank (ouch!) and I'll be out looking for boxes too. Hope
to meet others on the trails.

Beetle
