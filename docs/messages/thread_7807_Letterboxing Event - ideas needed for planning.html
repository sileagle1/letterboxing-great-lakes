From: Mrs. Robbins (robbins4th@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-08-30 08:01:39 UTC-07:00
Hello! I am new to this group and quite new to letterboxing as well. I asked my library to allow an Introduction to Letterboxing Evening with an experienced letterboxer to present. All was good to go until the presenter took a new job and is unable to make it. The library I volunteer at is hoping for me to present and go through with this presentation/discussion which will be held on September 16 in Central Illinois. If any of you have set something like this up or have attended an event such as this, I would appreciate your ideas and help for the agenda, etc.

I have contacted the local Girl Scout Council to put a note in their newsletter for leaders, placed posters at a few of the Forest Preserves in the area, and am spreading the word as best I can. Is this something I should post on atlasquest? I am hoping that both experienced letterboxers and those wanting to learn about letterboxing will attend for a good discussion. I have one letterbox planted inside the library and am thinking about adding one or two more. I wondered if something like this invites an event stamp-- I have never attended an event and am not sure.

Your ideas would be appreciated and welcomed! If any of you live near theEast Central Illinois or West Central Indianaarea and are interested in attending, I will be glad to share more information. It is a very small library so 20 people would be the limit and it is on a Monday evening.

Thank you!
