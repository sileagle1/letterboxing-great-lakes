From: olsentrio (hlo281@aol.com) | Date: 2007-06-15 18:41:50 UTC
Hello all,

I just wanted to share an ad I saw today in my new issue of "Family
Fun". It was a two sided Chevy ad, which was based on letterboxing. I
posted the pictures in the "Files" section. Sorry, I don't know how to
put 2 pictures in one file.
I thought they did a great job of describing letterboxing. And I was
happy to see that they mentioned "leave no trace" and "re-hide better
than you find" a couple of times.
I was just amazed to see a huge company like that using letterboxing in
their advertising. Just wanted to share it with the group : )

Olsentrio
