Hi everyone,

I don't often chime in here, although I do enjoy reading everyones
comments, but I recently found a Hitch Hiker for the second time. I
found it last July in Dexter, Michigan and then this July in Lansing,
Michigan (at least 50 miles apart). In the HH log book there were
about a dozen boxes it had visited since I last found it (several
right before or after I hit those same boxes.) My question is should
I have taken it? Or leave it for someone else to find? I ended up
taking it so I could move it further up north when I go later this
month. Any thoughts?
