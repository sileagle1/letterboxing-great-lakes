MI Fall Gathering Update

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-08-16 11:05:20 UTC
I've finally gotten through my e-mail and have updated the Gathering
pages with attendees and Clue Stamps. If you are planning to attend
the MI Fall Gathering at Yankee Springs SP on Oct. 22 and aren't on
the list, please drop me an e-mail so I can include you.

There have been some really neat ideas for the Clue Exchange Game and
I'm looking forward to seeing some of these stamps (The Stinky Cheese
Man?). If you want to play the Clue Exchange Game at the gathering,
or just want to contribute a stamp for the game, please see this page
for further details.

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/051022_game.asp

Also, we are still looking for more letterboxes to plant at Yankee
Springs SP in time for the gathering. We encourage you to head over
there within the next couple of months to plant a box or two, or if
you can't make it to the park but would like to contribute a
letterbox, we will plant it for you. See the Gathering web page for
more details on this.

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/051022_main.asp

I have received some great suggestions for the workshop and will be
putting up a poll within the next few days to see what people are
interested in.

I am going to try and get some information up on the web page about
possible lodging if you are coming from far away and would like to
stay over. I did have a question about camping and thought I would
mention that the Yankee Springs State Park campground is open at the
time of the gathering. You probably don't need a reservation, but can
make one at the park's web site if you would like.

http://www.michigandnr.com/parksandtrails/ParksandTrailsInfo.aspx?
id=511

If you have any questions, please feel free to e-mail either Azobox or
myself. Looking forward to seeing everyone at the Gathering!

SpringChick
