From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-10-14 00:34:10 UTC
Hey Mrs. Doglvrs, which one did you go back and find to make #12? I
think you guys were tops, unless JBBooks went back out after we ate
dinner. Way to go!

THANKS to everyone who came out for the MI Fall Gathering and to
everyone who contributed excellent boxes and time and food to make
this year's fall gathering one of the best yet!!! It was so great to
see old friends -- has it really been a year already?!? And just as
good to meet new letterboxers from near and far -- we truly enjoyed
chatting with all of you and all the wonderful trail stories we heard.

The boxes that were planted for the event are intended to be permanent
at Pigeon Creek Park and the clues will remain in the files section
here at this list. I will be uploading a new version of clues
tomorrow with a few corrections (what kind of a trail map puts north
on the bottom anyway?)

Again thanks to everyone! I am exhausted but it is a good kind of tired!

SpringChick
