From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-12-03 07:04:15 UTC
Many thanks to Dragon for organizing yet another event! Although it
got a bit nippy at times, plodding through the deep snow warmed us up,
as did the cider & chili. Of course we also enjoyed the many other
tasty treats people brought, especially those Atom 118 bars!

There were some very nice stamps in the boxes we found, so thanks to
all of you who created them. Once again at a WI "fall" event, it ended
up that two boxes were unknowingly hidden in the very same spot. Very
funny! It was nice to get together to wrap up the day over an evening
meal. Chatting in the comfort of a warm restaurant after being out on
the trails was great.

I didn't take a ton of photos, but you can view the few I have at this
site: http://tinyurl.com/yxdlyk
