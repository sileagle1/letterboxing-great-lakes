OK, it looks like we might be looking at a group of 10-20+ people on Friday night. 6:30 pm seems to be the preference, although at either place listed below it would NOT be a problem if some people showed up later. Here are two restaurant options:

1. Fuddruckers
Appetizers, burgers, chicken sandwiches, salads, shakes, etc. Also serve beer & wine. Very informal, order at the counter. No reservations, but a fairly large dining area where we could arrange tables to suit. Located near Brookfield Square shopping mall at intersection of Moorland Rd and Brookfield Rd. More details on menu here:

http://www.fuddruckers.com/locations.php

2. Champps
Large sports bar with a separate side area frequently used by groups. Wider variety of food & beverage offerings. They DO take reservations and DO allow separate checks. Located on Moorland Rd in Brookfield, just south of I-94. More details on menu here:

http://champps.com/Menu/tabid/156/Default.aspx

=================================
It looks like fewer people are available to get together Saturday night. This is probably the best restaurant choice:

Matteo's Italian
A few blocks from the park. They DO take reservations and DO allow separate checks. They have pizza and other Italian sandwiches, pastas, entrees. More info here:

http://www.foodspot.com/Clients/WI/Waukesha/MatteosItalianRistorante/default.aspx?accid=13754

====================================

I have created a .pdf file that shows a map of these locations, including the park that the event is at. You should have already seen a post on this, or perhaps this might also get you to the map:

http://tinyurl.com/yasrx2s


I revised the database table so you vote on FRI location and to get firmer numbers for reservations. Please edit (or add) info if you plan to attend either/both nights. This link might take you directly there:
