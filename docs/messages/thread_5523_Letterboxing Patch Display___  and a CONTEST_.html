Letterboxing Patch Display... and a CONTEST!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-01-09 01:32:44 UTC
I know a lot of folks here don't read the big list on a regular basis,
so I thought I'd pass along this item of interest. This past
September at the "We Live and Breath" gathering in Oregon there was a
display of letterboxing patches throughout the years -- LbNA patches,
Dartmoor patches and of course regional and state patches, including
the two version of our own Great Lakes Letterboxing patch. Here is a
link to the display online if you are interested in seeing it.

http://www.weliveandbreathe.com/history.htm

Anyway, after taking a look at the variety of patches displayed, I
noticed that a couple of states within the GL region have their own
patch (Ohio and Indiana) and I thought it would be fun if all 6 states
within the region had individual patches. So what does everyone
think? How about a contest for patch designs for the other 4 states
(Wisconsin, Illinois, Michigan and Minnesota)?

I would be happy to coordinate the entries and post them online for
voting. Once the final designs are selected, I would solicit a
volunteer from each state to be the point person for getting the
patches made and taking orders, etc. I am sure Wisconsin Hiker could
share her experience with this as she currently does this for our
regional patch. If we time it right, maybe we could have them in time
for this year's Great Lakes Gathering!

Is there interest? Give me your thoughts and I will put together a
timeline and instructions for submitting your design(s) for consideration.

SpringChick
