The debate goes like this:

I say plastic is animal, because it's made from oil, which is, in turn, made from the gooey remains of dinosaurs and such.

D.H. says it's mineral, because if I'm going to break things down to their genesis to name them, then I have to go back to the source, which is mineral, because we're all just a heap of minerals (he's very romantic).

I then tell him that he's not made of minerals, he's made of something that rhymes with "loop".

He tells me then there's something fundimentally wrong with someone who would choose to spend time with a person who really WAS composed entirely of said substance, and then we're off and bickering.

And so, the answer is, ANIMAL! Plastic is an ANIMAL!

Cheers,
