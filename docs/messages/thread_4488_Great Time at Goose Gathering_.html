Great Time at Goose Gathering!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-10-07 04:19:33 UTC
We had a wonderful time at the "A Wild Goose Chase" gathering today
in Iowa! It took place in our "sister region", the Midwest, but we
were not the only Great Lakes representatives. It was "Great" to see
our "old" friends The Cottontails, the full contingent of Hart x6,
Atom118 & CoffeeBeans, and Prairie Dog again. We also met some newer
Wisconsin boxers, the Olsentrio, for the first time. They have a
very artistic daughter and a very good carver (mom), so we are
looking forward to finding any boxes they may place in the future.

We also had an opportunity to chat & exchange with 16 new
boxers/teams that we hadn't met before. It was a nice low key
gathering, starting with lunch at 1:00 pm, followed by a group photo
and lots of exchanges. We also continued our lucky streak this year,
since Martini Man was the winner of the big prize basket!! Later in
the afternoon we struck out to find some boxes, which were nicely
scattered throughout the park.

At the end of the day, we shared a nice meal with Hartx6 (dad only)
and A118 & CB. A great way to end a great day!

Wisconsin Hiker

A round of applause for Big Purple Foot, Mr. Duck, and Scout Angel.
Coffee Beans and I had a great time at this gathering, and we had the
chance to meet many boxers for the first time. We enjoyed chatting with
Scout Angel and her mom. Although we didn't know Goose Chaser, we had
found a few of her boxes on our last trip to the quad cities. It was

from Emily Cottontail.

We did manage to get out on the trail and find a few of the nearby
boxes...(our enthusiasm for the hunt was obvious since we managed to get
to several FFs) One of Mr. Duck's boxes caused us a brief amount of
trouble, and just as we were about to jump into the woods, Mrs Dr B and
her sister, Urban Cowgirl, drove up. As they scratched their heads with
us, Martini Man and Wisconsin Hiker pulled up. Well, we soon figured it
out and were on our way, but its always a bit fun/hectic to run into
others while out on the trail at a gathering. Alas, the sun was
starting to set and we wanted to stop by an thank our hosts before they
left...so we had to leave a few boxes for our next visit....

We are looking forward to the next Goose Chase since this one seemed to
be a grand success.
