Member Info - Keith and Autumn

From: bunnyslippers217 (bunnyslippers@columbus.rr.com) | Date: 2006-09-19 18:36:46 UTC
Hello to all! I am Autumn of Keith and Autumn. We have been
letterboxing since May of 2006 - I read a Family Fun article about
getting your family excited about hiking (some members of our clan are
not all about nature...) and knew that it would be a great hobby for
us. Our son, Micah, is 4 and loves going letterboxing. We are
currently using bought stamps as our signatures, but we will be
carving and choosing actual trail names after our first log books are
full. We also have a daughter, Charlotte, who is 18 months old. She
is just starting to be interested in the ink and stamps, so I'm sure
in another year she will be stamping in, too. We have found 30
letterboxes and planted one on our recent trip to the UP of Michigan.
We have found boxes in 4 states so far. Our favorite find was Berry
Patch in Paradise, Michigan - anytime I get to eat pie and letterbox
at the same time, I am all about it. We are planning on attending the
October gathering in Chillicothe, so hopefully we will meet some of
you there!
