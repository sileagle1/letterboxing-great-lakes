From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-26 23:28:51 UTC-06:00
I never thought I'd say this, but it's getting easier, emotionally, to loose a letterbox. It's never pleasant, but 'all things must pass' and that holds true for letterboxes too. They can always be replaced. But when the area is cleared of ALL natural growth, it's a bit difficult to replace a box. So, I'm in the process of creating all new letterboxes. I've placed several new boxes within the past month.
The Lake Bluff Furnace Death Mystery is a series of three boxes, and the written clue probably holds a record for longest written clue. But a good story! Then, I placed Diamonds Are A Girls Best Friend. And just a couple days ago, I placed Polly Wanna Cracker. I have another series of 2 boxes to place as soon as I have a day off. See you at the Lb Wedding!
