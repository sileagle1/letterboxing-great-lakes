Just a quick note to those of you who may not have seen this on CNN or
the Weather Channel after Labor Day. Rockford had some pretty serious
storms run through...tornado spottings and everything. Well, we
basically got 10 inches of rain within 3 hours...worse than a
hurricane, the weatherman said. Some areas of Rockford and Cherry
Valley were under several feet of water (check rrstar.com for some
crazy photos...search for labor day flood). If you are planning on
coming to the area to find any boxes, you might want to check with the
owners to see if the area was flooded or not. I'm hoping to find time
to run out to Bauman Park to check on my CV boxes and then doing some
others as well. Hopefully all are there! :)

How are the other Rockfordians? Are you all okay out there?
