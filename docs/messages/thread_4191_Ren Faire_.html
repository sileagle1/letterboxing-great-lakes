From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-22 02:04:48 UTC
Yes, there will be boxes at the Ren Faire. What they will be is still
to be determined. The idea that was planned might not be done in time
due to the move to our new house, so I'm thinking of doing something
simpler or maybe going retro with some of the first year stamps so
those of you who didn't get those can. (The stamps are intended to be
rotated every so often anyway.)

I'm open to hearing any ideas for themes for this year. We've done
food, shops and performers in the recent past. The "What Not to Wear
at the Bristol Ren Faire" theme will probably have to wait for next
year... Any suggestions in the meantime?

A reminder that I have special permission to plant boxes on the Ren
Faire grounds. I have to follow some specific rules in order to have
their permission. If you are considering planting a box, let me know
and I can clear it for you with my regular boxes. Do NOT plant a box
without permission!!! If you are going to have a special box carried
on your person or you are a shop-owner, you do not need their permission.

Dragon
