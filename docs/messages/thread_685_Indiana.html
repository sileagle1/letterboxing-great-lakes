From: Delirium (donaldson_home@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-19 11:03:28 UTC-07:00
If you go to www.letterboxing.org, and click on "Clues" (or the big picture of the book) it'll give you a map of America. Click on Indiana, and when the state map comes up, click on the southern portion. You'll then get a list of all the boxes in the area, along with clues.

Happy hunting
