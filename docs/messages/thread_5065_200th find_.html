200th find!

From: junk4joann (junk4joann@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-26 22:10:52 UTC
I had my 200th find today! *celebrate!* In the expansive and lovely
Greenfield Park.

Thank you to everyone who plants boxes. I've been to Ohio, Michigan,
Illinois, Indiana, Iowa and Minnesota, not to mention all over my home
state of Wisconsin. If not for all the carving that goes on, I
wouldn't have had so many fun boxes to find. And I've only scratched
the surface!

I hope to add to my numbers at the Bird and GL gatherings. However,
Aimlesst is very militant about only counting boxes that I go out and
find... those clever gathering stamps that I find in cookies and
Christmas trees don't count! ;)

Thanks everyone!
LG.
