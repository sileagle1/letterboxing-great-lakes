Welcome to the list -- from one sailor to another, Ginny.

Our big regional Gathering for the Great Lakes area is always the
weekend after Memorial Day. There was a discussion about this some
time ago. (You can check the message archives to bring you up to speed
on some of what has gone on here)

If I remember correctly, it was decided to keep the dated as a
permanent thing so it would make things easier for vacation planning.
Some members are employed where schedules are done by the quarter or
even annually, and of course not everyone works just Mon-Fri so
weekends are an issue when it comes to time off requests.

Not to worry though, there are often other smaller events held and
Michigan seems to have a history of having those kind of events fairly
regularly.
