Saturday Letterboxing

From: Pale Puppy Posse (advantagek9@voyager.net) | Date: 2005-07-18 11:05:35 UTC-04:00
Well we managed to have a few house inspections to do on Saturday that just
happened to take us "close enough" to the Van Buren State Park in South
Haven. We got the opportunity to hunt down Azobox's Whose Shoes and I Spy
boxes.
What a hot day and to make matters worse, when you see the size of the
hill you have to climb in the sifting sand I thought I was going to die!
But we managed and the stamps are pretty awesome. We found the Wonderful
Surprise HH and will be moving him on.
Azobox, the boxes were wet inside but no damage to stamp or logbook. We
dried them out before replacing them in their hiding spots. Wish Lucky Day
was up and running but hey you can't have everything. I love it when we get
"paid" to go letterboxing!
