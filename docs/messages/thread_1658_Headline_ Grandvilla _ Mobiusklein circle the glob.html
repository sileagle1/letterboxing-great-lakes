Headline: Grandvilla & Mobiusklein circle the globe!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-30 20:51:45 UTC
Attention: World Travelers and those following the exploits of
"Around the World in 80 Days" adventurers; and arm-chair
travelers everywhere.

From: Travel Documents Division, "80 Days"
Department of Passports
Itinerary Special Section

The Department of Passports and the Itinerary Special Section have
been notified, and have certified, that the team of Grandvilla &
Mobiusklein have circumnavigated the globe in a manner sufficient to
meet all requirements specified in the challenge presented by
"Around the World in 80 Days" letterbox series.

Not only did the aforementioned Grandvilla & Mobiusklein successfully
traverse the fifteen secret locations, and remit the requisite proof,
they accomplished their feat in barely a fortnight!

The "80 Days" Travel Staff is astounded by this stupendous
act of clue solving.
The names "Grandvilla" and "Mobiusklein" will be entered in the
appropriate logbooks to commemorate their accomplishment.

Other travelers have reported incremental success and so the
Department of Passports expects to have others soon qualifying for not
only the "World Traveler" bonus stamp, but the highly
prized, "Around the World in 80 Days" bonus as well.

The D of P has asked the Itinerary Special Section staff to remind
other potential travelers that Passports are still available and that
the accomplishment of Grandvilla & Mobiusklein (their superb skills
notwithstanding) indicates that success is certainly possible for
others who wish to travel "Around the World in 80 Days".

To learn more about the potential this adventure offers, click on:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days.asp

For those already underway, we remind you that a status page is
available:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_status.asp

You may contact the travel staff at:
80days@letterbox-mi.com

Respectfully at your service,
Travel staff

azobox
