MI - Keltic Kara Muskegon Boxes

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-08-08 23:12:53 UTC
You may have noticed the proliferation of new Muskegon letterboxes
in the past couple of weeks placed by Keltic Kara. I found 4 of her
new boxes on Friday afternoon and went back out for another
yesterday afternoon with Old Shoe, only to come home to see that
additional clues had been posted. So this afternoon I went out for
a couple of hours and nabbed 2 more. Have a couple left to do to
finish off the Lumbertown Series.

Since those first boxes on Friday afternoon, I have been selfishly
keeping this little secret to myself -- well, okay I spilled to two
other MI 'boxers...

Of course it is exciting for me because theses are the first boxes
placed in Muskegon by somebody other than me with the exception of 1-
2 placed by vacationers. Yup that's right folks -- after 2 years
of 'boxing, I am finally able to look for a letterbox in my own
city! It is true... if you plant them, they will come (it just may
take a while).

Anyway, if you are at all near Muskegon or your travel plans can be
modified to include a stop here, you should check out these boxes.
I thoroughly enjoyed the ones I have found -- all with very nice
stamps and logbooks and a thoughtful variety of clues with some nice
bits of area history thrown in for good measure. Of course, I want
you to look for my boxes while you are in town also, but I am
resilient -- if your time is limited, at least go for a few of
these. You won't be disappointed and hopefully seeing some stamps
in the logbooks will encourage her to plant more!

A big thanks to Keltic Kara (whom I don't believe is a member of
this group... yet?)

SpringChick
