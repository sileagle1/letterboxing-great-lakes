Hello all,
I am fairly new to this group - I do more reading and listing in than
adding messages and thoughts of my own, but I thought I would share
the latest news about Price Nature Center here in Bridgeport,
Michigan. I thought about Windrose who planted "Cottontail in the
Cottonwoods" The newspaper notes that there was a body of a 27 year
old woman found there recently. The article gave me the creeps
because not only is this close to home, but more times than not it is
myself and my young children Letterboxing together, and that not only
would be a scary "find" but now I worry about my own protection.
Either I have been watching too much TV to know this is where creepy
people lurk, or I am just getting myself worried for nothing.
Needless to say keep this in mind next time you go searching through
the fall leaves on the lookout for a box, you never want to flip over
a piece of bark covered with leaves to discover a body. Who carries a
form of protection while Letterboxing? Is that absurd or what? I
didn't think I would ever have to ask that question!
