It's an incredible look at the facts about global warming presented by
former Vice President Al Gore. I know this sounds a little crazy: you could
go see Tom Cruise or Tom Hanks, and I'm thinking you'd rather watch Al
Gore?! Trust me, this is a movie that will leave you more stunned, with more

I thought I knew a lot about global warming, and frankly, that I had watched

island nation getting submerged or endangered species in Africa dying off.

will affect you.
