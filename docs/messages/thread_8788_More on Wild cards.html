More on Wild cards

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2019-01-14 18:10:10 UTC-06:00
Based on some of the emails I've gotten lately,  there seems to be some confusion over the Wild cards. 

So...If I call the wild card you picked on your card, you have up to 3 days to claim it (including the day that it's called).

Within those three days,  email me to claim the spot you will be using for your wild card. 

You can use your wild card to replace any word you've picked on your card. However, you have to tell me the exact word you want to use your wildcard on. Once you pick your word,  you can't change it (even if I call it the very next day).

If you email me after the 3 day window,  you cannot claim your wild card. It is USE IT OR LOSE IT.

For example,  I called "GLG11: Wonders of the World" on Saturday. If you had put GLG11 on your card as your wild card,  you have until the end of the day, tonday, to email me and say,  for example, "I'm going to use my wild card for O-Hike" because O-Hike is on my card, but it's not been called. However if you email me about it on Friday, I won't count it since it's past the window to claim the wild card.  

I hope that clears things up.

Thanks, 
A of Atomic Beans
