Bingo and 10 for 10

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2017-01-28 19:13:37 UTC-06:00
I must admit, this year BINGO started well for me.  I was feeling pretty good a week or two ago. But then, I've hit a bit of a dry spell of late. But, I guess that is how BINGO goes.

On a more positive note, I'm happy to announce that we managed to get our 10 for 10 series completed.  We've spent the last year planting 10 sets of boxes in honor of our first decade of boxing, representing some of the things we've enjoyed while letterboxing. So, it took us a few days past our 11th anniversary, but I still think we did pretty well considering we didn't plant the first box of the series until about 6 months into the year and series encompasses about 20 boxes. Most of the boxes are around Chicago (in some of our favorite haunts), but we did plant parts of this series on in MI, IN, and WI as well.  Search "10 for 10" under our trailname on AQ to find them, and we hope you enjoy them.

I'm looking forward to tomorrows BINGO call with hopes of starting another hot streak!

See you on the trail,
A of Atomic Beans
