Martini Man & I recently started participating in Quest Scouts which is billed as "Scouting for Adults".  There aren't any actual meetings; it is more like a personal quest to meet certain objectives in a variety of categories. 

According to the web site, Quests are designed to help you:

  • Learn new things
  • Explore your creative side
  • Connect with interesting people
  • Discover more of the world around you

 

For more information, check out the website (www.questscouts.com)

 

You can get more info if you sign up for the free weekly newsletter.  The "Welcome" newsletter articles provide more info and solicit feedback.  

 

You can participate for free, but various membership options are also available.  It is a new start-up business, but Martini Man & I definitely had fun doing our first "quest" which had a history theme. Some of our letterboxing friends in other parts of the country are also having fun with it. Check it out if it sounds interesting to you!
