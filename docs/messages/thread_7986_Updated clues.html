From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2014-06-09 11:51:28 UTC-07:00

Martin Man & I were out the past two Sundays doing some box maintenance.  His Night Sky box was lying out in the open and the logbook was a bit damp, but otherwise  was fine. He has made a slight modification to the clues, so if you haven't ever found it, be sure to get the current clues from the LBNA site.


We also did the long 9.2 mile hike to check on our Norwegian Woods boxes.  Here we were only able to find the first 3.  All were varying levels of wetness and one box had the lid chewed and was now a major ant farm.  Luckily stamps were fine and we either added or replaced logbooks where necessary.  We couldn't find the 4th box and totally missed the bench mentioned in the clues for the last box.  The box may still be there, but since the landmarks have changed, the clues aren't very helpful.  We may replace these two boxes later in the summer.  We also updated the clues for the first three boxes to better reflect current trail conditions, so once again, check LBNA for current clues.


All of these boxes are almost 10 years old, so we're happy that at least most were in place.


We also checked on Half Empty's Rexlettob #1 box and did some much needed maintenance.  It has been overrun by geocachers and the stamp was gone.  We relocated the box and added the new logbook and stamp he sent me.  He should be updating the clues soon, but if you are heading out that way and need the update, please contact me.


Hopefully many of you have also been able to get out on the trails in this nice weather.  We look forward to seeing many folks at the GLG later this month!
