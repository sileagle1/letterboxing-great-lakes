Kudos to MI State Parks - Porcupine Mtns and Lake Gogebic

From: sunnysideupm (sunnysideupm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-07-19 01:22:41 UTC
WOW - Kudos to the staff at the Porcupine Mountain and Lake Gogebix State Parks for encouraging letterboxing in their parks - and planting boxes themselves! Their boxes are simple images and easy finds, but really feature wonderful aspects of the park. And when I asked for the box in the Visitors Center, I had a nice chat with Sunshine about LBing. She seemed impressed by the Jubilee images, BTW!

I just spent two days finding all 20 boxes in these two parks - ink me (color me?) happy! It did rain briefly here today - but it wasn't a Jubilee rain!

I am planting nearby some boxes with personal stories and one of my best images yet. Two of the hikes might be rugged enough and long enough even for Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man! Now to write the clues before I lose my notes...

- Sunny
