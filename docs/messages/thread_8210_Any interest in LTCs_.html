Any interest in LTCs?

From: Katie McEnaney (archaeofrog@gmail.com) | Date: 2015-07-08 11:36:25 UTC-05:00
I realized that I have accumulated quite a backlog of LTCs (Letterbox Trading Cards) that I would be happy to pass along to anyone interested. They are all numbered, limited-editions, but I often ended up with a few extras of each after a specific trading ring. 

You can check out the specifics in my Atlas Quest logbook: http://www.atlasquest.com/people/logbook.html?memberId=33618&action=plants&type=ltc&sortBy=alpha  (You may need to be logged in to view.)

For the curious, an LTC or Letterbox Trading Card is a small card with an original stamped image. Many of the stamps for my LTCs have now been planted out in the wild but some still have not. LTCs arose as another way to share stamped images based on the tradition of ATCs or Artist Trading Cards. 

If you are interested, please let me know if there are specific cards and your mailing address. (When replying, double-check that you are sending your email to me, archaeofrog@gmail.com , and not to the whole group, which is the default.)

Enjoy!
