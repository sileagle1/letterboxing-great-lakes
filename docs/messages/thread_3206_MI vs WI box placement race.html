This morning, I thought I'd trying to catch up with the mountain of
Digests in my email by reading the latest and going backward. LOL I
soon realized the folly of that you folks are way to prolific at
posting!

However, I did catch that the gauntlet has been thrown down for a race
between MI and WI to see who can fill all of their counties with at
least one active letterbox.

Since I couldn't have made the Mystery Gathering today, even if I had
known about it in advance, and in that same spirit of friendly
competition, I've created a new Album in the photos section showing
which MI counties still need a box. Here's the link (I hope it works,
but if not just go to the last page of the albums in the photos area)
http://tinyurl.com/ygstj3

Whenever anyone gets a box placed in one of the "empty" MI counties,
just email me or SpringChick and we'll get that county filled in on
the map.

Now then, here is a list of the MI counties and a link for information
on each of them. (HINT: might be important information real soon!)

Branch http://www.infomi.com/county/branch/

Montmorency http://www.infomi.com/county/montmorency/

Oscoda http://www.infomi.com/county/oscoda/

Sanilac http://www.infomi.com/county/sanilac/

Tuscola http://www.infomi.com/county/tuscola/

Alright Michigan dwellers ... let's get out there and show those
Badgers just how aggressive a Wolverine can be, shall we? (I found
this quote online while checking the aggressive nature of both
animals: "Except for the wolverine, badgers are probably the most
fearless and aggressive animal in North America for it's size.")
Let's go MI team ... RAH, RAH, RAH!!

Oh, and if anyone in MI has the inclination to place some boxes, but
not the time to carve, I have some hand-carved stamps that could be
sent out for adoption to help our placing efforts! Just email me off
the list and please promise you will place the box ASAP, and we can
make the arrangements.

Sail on!
---Bindle Babe
