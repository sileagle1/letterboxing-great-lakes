Hi everybody!
Sorry it has taken me so long to send out a thanks for choosing Ohio
for the next gathering. School started back about a month ago and
things are just now starting to settle down.
Anyway...my name is M&8X (Stacey) and I'll be the host, along with my
husband EagleEyes (Jim). We started letterboxing in the Spring of
2003. The first gathering we attended was the Winter Barbeque in
Louisville, KY last year. That made us want to try our own. So was
hosted a gathering this year in central Ohio. It was very informal,
with about 25 people attending. The thought of hosting the GL
gathering is quite intimidating. Since we haven't attended a GL
gathering, we are unsure of how things have been done in the past.
Any help/advise would be greatly appreciated. One thing I know I will
need help with is coming up with a theme. Any ideas?
At this point the only information I can give is the intended
location.
We are planning on holding the gathering at Alum Creek State Park.
Here is the web site for more information about the park.
http://www.dnr.state.oh.us/parks/parks/alum.htm
It is located about 20 miles north of downtown Columbus.
If you have any questions, or want to offer help/suggestions
(hint,hint) just let me know!

See ya' soon.

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-28 12:26:20 UTC
The Great Lakes Gathering is held every year on the Saturday after
Memorial Day, which I think is generally the first Saturday in June.
The 2007 event will be on June 2, 2007.

There is a short FAQ in the FILES section (GATHERINGS folder) of the
list that gives some very general information about the annual GL
Gathering as well as a document that offers guidelines, hints, helps
for hosting a gathering.

Stacey, feel free to ask any of the former gathering hosts if you have
questions, but keep in mind -- you don't have to do it *exactly* like
it has been done in the past. Variety is nice too ;-)

SpringChick
