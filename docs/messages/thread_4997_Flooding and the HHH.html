Flooding and the HHH

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-04-27 15:27:46 UTC-05:00
Hope everyone is enjoying the weekend! We took the opportunity to
double check our HHH boxes. We are happy to report that we were able to
find two of the boxes that had been underwater just a few weeks ago.
Unfortunately, we decided to remove 'Defender of the HHH' since it had
floated ~40ft from its hiding place. Since we now have a better feel
for how often the area floods, we decided not to press our luck any
further (nor force finders to squish through the mud or potentially
knee-high water), and we retired the box. The image of the backside of
the stamp is posted with the clue if you desire to search for the HHH
(which we were happy to find safe in its hiding spot).

The remaining HHH boxes will be available through the summer after which
we will likely retire the series. For those of you who won't have a
chance to find them (or those of you that have), we will likely be
hiding a second HHH series sometime later in the summer or fall. These
will be an entirely new set of stamps, but they will be grouped like the
current HHH series with the bonus box to find after finding the other
clues. This time we will be sure to plant them somewhere that will stay
drier throughout the year!
