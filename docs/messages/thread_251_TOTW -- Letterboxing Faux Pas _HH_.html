Kaycee,

Don't feel bad; you certainly have not done any damage. The
owner would probably appreciate hearing about the hitchhiker
(as the owner of the box would, too).

While the philosophy is to keep hitchhikers moving, a letterboxer
will sometimes decide to leave a hitchhiker in the box for various
reasons, among them might be that she won't be going
letterboxing again soon.

If you will be letterboxing soon, and you are so inclined, it would
be a favor to go back, pick up the hitchhiker, and move it along.
