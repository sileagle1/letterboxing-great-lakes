From: (katy@dsa132.org) | Date: 2005-07-07 03:24:12 UTC
I think you mean ticks. To my knowledge, Lyme disease is only carried by
two species of ticks (Ixodes scapularis and I. pacificus). Not to make
any one paranoid, but there are plenty of other diseases vectored by
ticks, so one should always perform a tick check just to be sure, on
yourself and your dog

On the plus side, we haven't had any reports of humans with West Nile in
Illinois yet this year (knock on wood) and only 13 counties (including
Champaign and DuPage, unfortunately) have had mosquitoes that tested
positive for WNV.

Katy
