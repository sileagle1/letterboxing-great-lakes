Ok, ok. So, I initiated a few rule changes this year,  in part, to try and speed up the game.  These changes included modifications to the wild card rules,  and expansion of terms to try and include more terms that people would pick.

I think the wild card change did help in the short term games,  so far.  However, the changes I made to picking terms seemed to be a double edged sword.  At the start of the game,  the zero terms led to a bunch of early wild card calls and a free fast starts. However,  due to our geographic diversity,  there seemed to be a bunch of terms that were picked by only one person that has had an unintended effect. In general,  the singletons should be good since it should help minimize duplicate winners...but it  seems to have slowed the pace of the game (or at least that is my impressions). I'll have to review some historical data to confirm. 

We are nearing mid April,  and by my records, there is a trio at the top who still have a few boxes left to complete.  Since we are getting closer, I'll not specify totals for the home stretch (to keep everyone in suspense). 

In short, it seems that not all my changes had the desired effects. I guess this is still a bit of a work in progress.  

Please note,  I know I'm slightly behind updating the Yahoo site, but I'll do it tonight. 

I will also note that the intermediate 'X' game is still up for grabs. 

On the plus side,  most everyone is bunched in a relatively tight group. I've not yet worked out who might be the winner,  but I think the game is still very much too close to call. 

Good luck everyone!

A of Atomic Beans
