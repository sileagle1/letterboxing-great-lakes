I took the hint for Wi Hiker's post, and Fungus Woman also asked I post
the recipe....so here it is! These were the oatmeal bars I made for
Saturday's Gathering. Actually, they are a 30 year old recipe from the
Wisconsin State Journal's Cookbook. I leave the nuts out.

Cookie part: 1 cup butter or margarine, 2 cups brown sugar, 2 eggs, 2
tsp vanilla, 2 1/2 cup flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp salt (optional),
and 3 cups quick rolled oats

Chocolate filling: 1 can (15 oz) sweetened condensed milk, 1 pkg (12 oz)
semi-sweet chocolate chips, 2 Tbl butter or margarine, 1/2 tsp salt, 2
tsp vanilla, and (1 cup chopped walnuts, optional)

In a large bowl, cream 1 cup butter and the brown sugar. Beat in eggs
and 2 tsp vanilla. In a separate bowl, mix flour, soda, and salt. Stir
in oats. Stir dry ingredients into creamed mixture until blended. Pat
2/3 of mixture into the bottom of a greased 13x9x2 inch baking pan.

In a heavy saucepan over low heat, melt milk, chocolate, 2 Tbl butter
and salt. Stir until smooth. Stir in remaining vanilla (and nuts).
Pour mixture into pan over oat layer, and then dot chocolate with
remaining oat mixture. Bake at 350 for 25-30 min. Cool and cut into bars.
