Damski i Dzentelmenska!

I am pleased to announce that a select group from Phineas Boch's Traveling
Circus has arrived in Wisconsin! I've been busy getting them settled in
their new temporary quarters, although we had some difficulties in the park.
Many of the areas we explored seemed to have an eerie feel and tended to
spook the animals. And given the nature of circus performers, the humans
were similarly uneasy and complained of a "haunted" feeling. We eventually
found an area that seemed safe and I'm sure they'll be practicing their acts
soon.

Our opening performance will be on October 1 at Menomonee Park and we expect
to remain in the area for two full weeks. A program outlining the various
venues and stages that you will need to search out to witness these amazing
artists will be available at the local Gathering. If you're curious as to
what the circus will include, I've created a poster to publicize our show.
You can view it here: http://hauntedharvest.homestead.com/circus.html

To be sure you have a ticket for the performance, please RSVP by September
28. I'm looking forward to the day when I meet you, our esteemed audience!
