From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-12-03 15:19:35 UTC
When I uploaded the photos from yesterday's gathering, I also
discovered some photos I had taken during our Illinois jaunt on NOV 4-
5. One of them shows the possible cause of the disappearance of
the "What a Scream" stamp - drowning! I was made aware of this by
numerous signs in the IL forest preserves. If you'd like to see the
sign yourself, just click here:

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/TheScream.html

One other photo turned out quite well. It is a scene taken from a
bridge when we were hunting the Brutus and Wolverine boxes in the Doxie
Doos "Big 10" series. You can see the photo here:

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/Big10Scene.html

We're looking forward to another trip or two to Illinois this winter.

Wisconsin Hiker
