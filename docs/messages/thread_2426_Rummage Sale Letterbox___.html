From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-21 11:39:44 UTC-07:00
Just a reminder that this box is still with Doodle &
Deedle. We did quite nicely at our yard sale on April
8th, but alas, no letterboxers came a huntin' :(
(Although we did have some Jehovah's Witnesses stop
by...)

As of right now (2:30 on Friday) we're still planning
on holding our yard sale scheduled for tomorrow,
despite the threats of rain in the area. If you
figure out where we are, and if it's not raining, stop
by!

Since we can't set up our sale in the garage thanks to
Deedle's Charger taking up all the room, if it does
start to rain we might have to shut down shop early.
If it is raining and you've made the effort to track
us down but don't see a yard sale sign, look for the
house with the "welcome" cats sign in the front flower
bed, and the "Kitty Kitty" sign on the front door (one
of our three cats may even be peeking through a
window!).

We also have one last garage sale scheduled for next
Saturday, April 29th, too, so if you're in the area,
don't pass up your chance to get this great stamp! ;)

Doodle Bug.
