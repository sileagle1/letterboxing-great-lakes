Well, it doesn't sound like anyone is going to take over for Sidewagers
this year....so I'll give it one more go.

I'll use the same set of rules for last year, so please check the files
section and read the sidewagers 2011.pdf file if you are unfamiliar with
this game.

Side Wager: A wager made beyond the normal rules of the game. A hedge bet.

Basically, if you'd like to add more excitement to the letterboxing bingo
game (or another way to win), enter the Side Wagers Game. When will
FamilyOf6 pull the item that finally and completely fills in some player's
card? Pick the right day, and you WIN! Again, this is the Big Game here,
not the interim games. The date will be determined by the claiming of a
successful card by a BINGO player (and its certification by FamilyOf6).

Your guess must be submitted before January 1, 2012.

PLEASE PUT, 'SIDEWAGER' in the subject line, and send your guess to me at
aatom118@gmail.com

Given the lateness of this email, I may not reply to your date request as
promptly as last year (and my first post to this game might not come right
away on Jan 1). However, I should hopefully confirm your date by the first
week of January.

As of now, the game is OPEN.

GOOD LUCK!

Atom118 of Atomic Beans

(and if you want to know what kind of prize you might have in store...talk
to last year's winner, Prairie Dog)
