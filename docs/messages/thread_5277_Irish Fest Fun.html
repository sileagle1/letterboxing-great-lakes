Irish Fest Fun

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-09-02 06:12:01 UTC
It was a sunny Saturday to meet and greet letterboxers at the Long
Grove Irish Fest. The Brat Pack was the first group I happened to meet
up with. They were busy searching for the local boxes. Mischief was
casually sitting near the new box she hid that morning, and getting a
good sunburn too. Finding boxes was the priority. We wandered throught
woods and meadows, crossed over and under bridges, and tip toed in flip
flops from rock to rock to cross Buffalo Creek. And later in the
afternoon, we wandered over to Village Tavern Restaurant where we were
joined by Amy, my new fun filled buddy - Wheels, and Captain Muffin.
Super J arrived after he got off work. Then, it was time for the best
legs in kilts contest. Did you know that Super J was a past contestant
of the best legs in kilts contest? I saw the photos saved on his
phone - hubba hubba!!! Thanks to all who turned out for this mini-meet.
Beetle
