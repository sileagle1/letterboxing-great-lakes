We Need Your Help!

From: Steve & Christa (sbellock322@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-08 02:45:30 UTC
Every year, more that half a million babies are born too soon. More
than 120,000 are born with serious birth defects, like our Joseph was.

It doesn't have to be that way. You can do something about it.

We (Steve, Christa & Emily) are walking in the March For Babies with
the March of Dimes. We are walking in Joseph's memory and we need
your help. We have a goal to raise as much money as we can to help
babies have a better chance at survival.

We aren't asking for much, whatever you can afford to give would be
greatly appreciated, even if it's only $1.00--just give something.
You can make a donation using a credit card by visiting our blog
www.pinknbluemaketwo.blogspot.com (there are 2 links for donations in
the sidebar) or you can contact us through AQ to find out where to
mail a check. If your company has an employee donation match program,
you can even double your contribution at no extra cost to you. All
donations are tax deductible.

We want to thank everyone who donated to our team last year. You all
helped us to raise over $1700 to help babies and we're hoping that
maybe, just maybe, we can get close to that amount again this year.
Who knows, maybe we can beat it!!! Be assured that $.90 of each $1.00
raised by the March of Dimes is used to carry out their mission.

Every donation helps, no matter how big or small it is. Please donate.

You'll be helping incredibly adorable, totally lovable, absolutely
wonderful little babies.

One day, every baby will be born healthy. But we have to walk to get
there.

Thanks so much for your time,
The Cottontails
