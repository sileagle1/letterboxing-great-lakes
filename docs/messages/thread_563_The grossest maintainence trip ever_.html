The grossest maintainence trip ever?

From: Delirium (donaldson_home@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-18 16:37:47 UTC
Just got back from a quick checkup on my boxes. Thought I'd start at
Kensington, since there are three there and it was the farthest
away. It also ended up being the single most disgusting trip I've
ever taken.

Went to check on "Freebird" first, which was located by an area where
some Detroit Edison guys were doing tree trimming (why I have no
idea, since there are no lines anywhere to be seen). Got to see the
one guy in the cherry picker picking his nose, then spitting on the
ground far below. Nice.

At the box itself, I ended up with a slug on my hands that I didn't
know was there, and only discovered it after I'd smushed it. Slug
slime and blood. Nice.

On to "GOTCHA"! Which hadn't been hidden well, and was consequently
ten feet away, in plain sight, with animal teeth marks all over it.
The same animal (I think) liked the box so much it peed all over it,
marking it as its own. Nice.

At least the plastic bags held up. So now, here I am, slug guts and
animal pee on my hands, getting a bit grumpy. But then, what do I
see in the trees but a small gorup of Cedar Waxwings- so pretty! The
baby and I stop to observe them for a little while. Then one of them
pooped on the baby's leg. Nice.

I no longer find waxwings so agreeable, but the baby didn't seem to
mind. She's two, what does she know? At this point, I realize I
have no baby wipes and now have to cut the check up trip short, if
only to throw the baby in the tub when we get home. However, always
the model toddler, she gave herself her own bath in one of the oilier
puddles back at the parking lot. Nice.

So at least it's good to know the three Kensington boxes are still in
place.
Cheers,
Delirium
