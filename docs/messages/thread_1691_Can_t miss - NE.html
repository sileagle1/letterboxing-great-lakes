Hi!

If you haven't done so already, you may want to join some of the
regional message boards for that area. The Connecticut group and the
Northeast group would seem to match your plans well.

We've always done this when we travel with boxing in our plans. We've
gotten tips on recommended boxes/areas, warnings about boxes that are
missing, tips on additional clues that aren't widely published and
suggestions on where to eat. We've also made plans with some of the
local boxers to get together with them while we're in the area. Lots
of fun!
