From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2009-03-20 02:12:20 UTC
Last night I had a dream. I was on a field trip with my junior high class to a national park. I stumbled across a tupperware container.

I was thrilled and brought the classes attention to the plastic box I had found. As soon as I picked it up I realized it was not a letterbox, rather a geocache.

I was bummed, but opened it anyway and was surprised. Instead of junk (oops, did I just say that?) I found 6 stamps . . 3 store bought and 3 hand carved.

At once a ranger was standing over us and demanded the cache and its contents. I tucked the hand carved stamps into my jacket and handed over the box & store bought stamps.

Once the ranger left I turned to my classmates with a smile on my face . . . then I revealed the hand carved stamps hidden within my coat.

It was a fantastic dream!!
