I found this stuff called "Fix-All Adhesive" (made by Super Glue Corp.) at
Dollar Tree (you can guess the price). It's a contact cement, and has been
working for me...again, following the apply-to-both-sides-and-allow-to-dry
method. It is flammable, contains xylene, naptha, and ethylbenzene, so
leave the blow torches off while playing with it and no getting high on the
fumes, kiddies, or we'll take it away from you!
