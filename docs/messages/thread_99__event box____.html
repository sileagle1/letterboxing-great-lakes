If the event box is delivered to me before the event, yes, that
would be an apt description. If, however, you want to hide the box
in the area of the gathering, then give me the clues, or distribute
the clues yourself, that would also fit the definition.
Essentially, an event box is a letterbox that is done at a
gathering, some how, some way. Some event boxes are only available
during the event, some become a permament fixture at the event site,
others travel to other events, and still other go home with the
creator to be hidden in their area as regular letterboxes.

The artwork is SpringChick's. It is the gathering stamop image from
last year's gathering.

And, bringing up a subject from the "big board"....

I wonder whether WE can do any "night letterboxing" at our
event...hmmm.
