Ways to win at BINGO

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-01-19 12:10:39 UTC-08:00

As more words are getting crossed off, I just want to remind everyone about the 7 ways to win at Bingo. 


THE 7 WAYS TO WIN A PRIZE ARE: Squares Needed
1 4 corners: first person to have ALL 4 corners of their card filled in 4
2 Straight line: first person to cross off 5 items in a straight line anywhere on their card (across, down or diagonal) 5
3 6-Pack: first person to cross a 2x3 (or 3x2) block of items, anywhere on their card 6
4 "+": first person to cross off the items in an "+" configuration (N column and middle row) 9
5 10 Spot: first person to mark off 10 items on their card – no special configuration, just any 10 items in the 5 x 5 grid 10
6 Full card 3 winners 25 (can include wildcard)
7 BIGGEST LOSER: person with most spaces left at end of game ?


In past years we have had some winners in January: in 2009 - 6-Pack, in 2011 - 10-spot, and in 2013 - Straight Line.  Will there be any JAN winners this year?????


Q. What do I do when I get a Bingo?

A. Please e-mail me (kotlarek@wi.rr.com). I will then confirm against the copy of your card that you mailed me and post notice of confirmed Bingos to the group. You are encouraged to share your excitement with the group, but remember that it is not an official Bingo until I have confirmed against your card.


 Q. Do I have to let you know on the exact day that I win? What if I don't check the list that day and then somebody else wins in the meantime?

A. I suspect that once you get close to a Bingo, you will be making a point to check for the word each day, however, because life can sometimes get in the way... you have 3 days from the time of your win day to claim your Bingo and still have it be effective on the day you actually won. If you miss this, you can still claim it, but it is then effective on the day I receive your e-mail and it is possible that somebody else may have claimed the win in the meantime. Do you really want that to happen?


Q. What if more than one person has a Bingo on the same day?

A. In the event of a tie in the interim games, each person will win.  In the case of a tie in the big game, each will take one of the three possible winning slots.  For instance if two people tie for 1st place in the big game, they will take the 1st and 2nd place slots (I will draw tie breaking names from a bag to determine 1st & 2nd places) and we will continue to play for a 3rd place winner.  If there is a tie for 3rd place, then there will be multiple 3rd place winners.


Q. Can I win more than once?

A. Yes, multiple wins are allowed. For instance, if you are the winner of one of the Interim games you are still in the running for all of the other games. PLUS, you can go onto the Yahoo group and gloat about it! J
