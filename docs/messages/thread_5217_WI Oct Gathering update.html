WI Oct Gathering update

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-27 23:02:25 UTC
I was up at the park last week and the shelter is fantastic!
Hopefully we'll have great weather anyway.

We are still looking for someone to make the event stamp, so let me
know if you are interested.

If you want to contribute a box that does not fit the theme, that is
also okay. The park has its own Mt. Baldy sand dune and there are any
number of nature themed boxes that would also fit the area. Remember,
the more boxes, the more the fun!

I scouted out 22 hiding locations on the yellow trail and I also made
note of a number of locations near the shelter. There is still the
whole black trail and the red trail along the water to scout out yet.
If you bring a "last minute" box to the gathering, you could use the
clues to one of the spots I already staked out for your convenience.

I hope to have info on where to mail the completed boxes in the near
future. In the meantime, just make them and hold on to them.

As a side note, I was able to drive to the park from my campsite at
Potowanami SP in about a half hour. I think it would be less than 20
minutes from the Chanticleer B&B.
