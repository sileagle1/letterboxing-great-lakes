Yahoo! Groups will be continuing our database maintenance on Monday
night, January 9, at 9:00 pm Pacific Time (5:00 am on the 10th (GMT)).
For approximately 30 minutes, the Groups site will be down for all
groups. In addition, 25% of groups will be unavailable for an
additional 30-45 minutes. Messages sent to affected groups during
that time will be queued and delivered once the groups are back online.

We appreciate everyone's patience and apologize for the unusually
frequent series of maintenance downtimes we have had to schedule
recently. The Holiday season gifted us with an unfortunate combination
of hardware failures and data corruption issues. Fortunately, we're
seeing the light at the end of the tunnel, although folks should
expect at least one more period of downtime in the coming weeks.
