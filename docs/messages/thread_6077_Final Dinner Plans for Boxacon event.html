From: wi_letterboxer (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-10-01 21:36:48 UTC
The weekend is almost here! It's time to meet some letterboxers & find some boxes. Here's the info for the pre- and post-event dinner get-togethers for those of you who expressed an interest:

We'll meet at Champps on Friday night at 6:30 pm. If you're a bit later, no big deal - I'm sure we'll be there for a while. We will be in the patio room (don't worry, it is INDOORS). Just ask for Debbie with the hiking group if you need help finding that area. They DO allow separate checks, so be sure to tell your server who should be included on your check when you first order. Champps is located on Moorland Rd in Brookfield, just south of I-94. The address is:

Champps
1240 S Moorland Rd
Brookfield, WI

Details on menu here:

http://champps.com/Menu/tabid/156/Default.aspx

===============================================
We also have reservations at Matteo's Italian Ristorante on Saturday at 6:30 pm. It is just a few blocks west of the park. They also allow separate checks. The address is:

Matteo's
1608 E. Sunset Drive
Waukesha, WI

They have pizza and other Italian sandwiches, pastas, entrees. More info here:

http://www.foodspot.com/Clients/WI/Waukesha/MatteosItalianRistorante/default.asp\x?accid=13754
