From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-21 23:01:42 UTC-04:00
Yeah! More travelers! Glad you found the passport -- good luck!

And, speaking of finding the boxes before the end of September... just a
reminder to anyone else who was thinking about doing the 80 Days series --
we will not be maintaining the boxes after September 30, 2006. We are not
planning to pick them up, but if one goes missing, it will not be replaced.
After September, as long as all of the boxes are still in place, anyone who
finds all 15 of them is still eligible for the World Traveler Bonus Stamp
(and also the Around the World in 80 Days bonus if they are found within 80
days), but if a box is missing and you are not able to get all 15 because of
this, the bonus stamps will not be available.

As one of the locations closes for the season at the end of September, if
you want to ensure yourself as much time as possible while the boxes are
still being maintained, you should request a passport soon as there are only
about 72 days left before the end of September.

SpringChick
