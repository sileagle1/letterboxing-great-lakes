Get a Clue Gathering Was Great

From: golfgalnopar (golfgalnopar@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-25 04:17:02 UTC

Just wanted to say a big thank you to everyone at Yankee Springs for
making the WONDERBROADS feel so welcome. It was our first time and we
had a terrific day. And now I have faces to go with the names in the
Yahoo group. It was clear that Spring Chick and Azobox put in many
hours organizing the details for the event. The folders, the workshop,
the clue game and of course the GREAT hot dogs and fixings made for a
wonderful event. And I'm not forgetting all the dishes to pass that
everyone brought. You just can't beat a good potluck!!
Although I was at first reluctant to give up the stamp that I carved
since it was my very first one, Its good to know that the clue game
will be traveling around for other boxers to enjoy. In fact, I think
there might be an old Clue game in the basement that I might have to
resurrect. I'd forgotten how much fun it was.

Again, our thanks to Spring Chick and Azobox for putting together a
great day, and everyone for making us feel so welcome.

Golfgalnopar (sometimes called The Tea Lady)
and the Wonderbroads.
