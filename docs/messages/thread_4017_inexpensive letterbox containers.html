inexpensive letterbox containers

From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-04-28 21:08:06 UTC-07:00
The dollar section at Target currently has some decent containers for letterboxes. They are small, but still a nice size (about 4" diameter lid). The plastic is not the sturdiest, but of better quality than the disposable containers I've noticed being used a lot lately. Unfortunately, the lids are bright green, but nothing a little camo tape can't disguise. There are six in a package for a buck. They come in both square and round, but while I found the seal on the square is less than optimal, the seal on the round ones is quite good.

They also have some unlined notebooks (scrapbooks) in the dollar section amongst the graduation themed items. Of course the covers say "2007"...which would be perfect if you need a new logbook for the 2007 Gathering!!!
