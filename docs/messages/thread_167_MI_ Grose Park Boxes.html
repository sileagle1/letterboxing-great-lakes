MI: Grose Park Boxes

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-04-24 18:29:32 UTC
Got out for a bit this morning and found the Grose Park 5-box series
near Conklin, MI. I was FF at these boxes -- it has been a while
since that has happened!

For anyone who may be heading to these boxes in the near future --
in about a week the spring wildflowers will be gorgeous. The woods
are covered with them and a few trillium and voilets were just
beginning to peek out. Also, there is much poison ivy, so be
watchful for that (especially near box #3).

All in all, a very nice Saturday morning walk in the park. Thanks
to JayBee and M.O.E. for placing this series!
