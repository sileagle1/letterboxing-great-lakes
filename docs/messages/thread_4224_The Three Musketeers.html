The Three Musketeers

From: runciblespn (runciblespn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-06 17:10:13 UTC
I finally got a chance to check up on the Three Musketeers series in
Mequon, WI. I've received numerous e-mails about the third box, so I
knew it was missing, and it seemed that the first box had gone
walkabout as well. I a bit concerned about the location now, even
though none of the boxes are in an especially open area.

I've giving the series a second chance in the current location (if the
boxes disappear again, I'm going to have to re-think things), so I re-
carved Athos and Aramis, and as of yesterday, 7/5/07, the boxes were
all in place.
