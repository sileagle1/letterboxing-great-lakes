Re: Cinemaboxers at the Ren Faire

From: Katelyn Burrill (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-08 17:29:42 UTC
The outside entertainment starts up around 9:30 or so. There's jokes,
an intro to Robin Hood's adventures, Jane the Queen's Phoole (make a
note of her outfit so you can find her later!), and the entrance of
the Queen's courtiers!

The kids, depending on age and interest, might like to follow the
Robin Hood story throughout the day and/or watch the Queen on her
daily activities (look on the 11x17 schedule when you come in the
gate for times and locations). If you'd like to show off your "bun
in the oven", might I suggest a short shirt and a visit to the face
painter near the candied almonds. He does a wonderful job on bellys
(one of my friends had a full underwater ocean scene when she came to
Faire when she was about 7 months along). There's also a Kid's
Kingdom, where there's kid-appropriate activities all day.

Barefoot Ankh and I will be at the Faire both days, although Barefoot
will be working at Mayhawke Armory all day (he'll have his stamp) and
I'll have some official wenchly activities between 1 and 3pm. We
hope to meet you there!

-- KissMeKate
