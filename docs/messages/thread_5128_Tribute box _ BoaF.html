Tribute box @ BoaF

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-06-20 05:26:16 UTC
My Tribute to my Dad box will be at the BoaF gathering.

It contains 3 stamps, one an image of my Dad straight out of boot
camp in 1967, then my "Thank You!!" stamp and "on bended knee" by
mudflingingfools of Maine.

There is only one logbook, a nice one that I will present to my Dad
for his 60th birthday this November.

I've had this box going around as a postal, and making some
appearances at gatherings as it travels. I've been asking people to
write something more than just, "thanks for the nice stamp."

I want people to write a tribute to those who have fought or served
during the Vietnam War, as so many were spit upong (including my Dad).

I know that at gatherings people just want to log in and move on. I
understand this and will have a header page with the BoaF Event Stamp
and I just ask you to stamp in and write your trailname/city/state.

If you wish to write more, please feel free to. Several have pre-
written expressions of gratitude that they have taped inside the
book. If you have any military themed stamps you would like to stamp
into the log book, feel free too.

I'm amazed at the stories, stamps and trinkets that have been
included in this book. I know my Dad will love it!!

Thanks,
