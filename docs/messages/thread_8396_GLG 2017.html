We've been busy moving from WI to AZ, so I've been a bit distracted.  I've been meaning to post that the GLG host state for 2017 should be the great state of WISCONSIN!


Hopefully someone will volunteer to host this long-standing regional event - the 2017 event will be the 15th year for the Great Lakes Gathering.

Here is some info posted by SpringChick a while ago:

=============================
 In 2010 the group voted and it was decided that the date would be at the discretion of the
event host but must fall somewhere within the May - September time fame.


Q.  How is it determined where the event will be held each year?
A.  The event rotates from state to state throughout the region. The goal is to move the gathering
from one state to another to give all areas a chance to host and to enable people in the further 
reaches of the region an opportunity to attend an event near them. During the first six years, a 
schedule is being set and after that the rotation will continue based on the order that was
established during those first six years.


Q.  Who hosts the event?
A.  Each fall, the moderators of the LB Great Lakes Yahoo! Group will solicit the list for volunteers from
the eligible state(s) to host the event for the following year.  During the first six years of the event, 
an eligible state is any state that has not previously hosted the event. After all states have had a chance
to host and a schedule is set, the next state in the rotation is the eligible state.  In the event there 
are no willing hosts from an eligible state, it will move to the next state in the rotation.  If more than
one person/state volunteers, a poll will be used to allow group members to choose their preference of
location.


Q.  Who can attend the Great Lakes Gathering?
A.  As with the majority of other letterbox events and gatherings, the annual Great Lakes Gathering is open 
to anyone who wishes to attend, regardless of where they live and whether they are active letterboxers or not.
============================

We've had many great hosts, themes and locations the past 14 years.  If you'd like to see a list of the previous events, you can find it here:  file:///C:/Users/Debbie/Downloads/GLG%20list%20(1).pdf
