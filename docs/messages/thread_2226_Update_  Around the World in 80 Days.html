Update: Around the World in 80 Days

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-30 18:39:00 UTC
We are happy to announce that the Around the World in 80 Days
Letterbox Tour will once again be available to interested travelers
during 2006. Last year several people successfully completed the 15-
stop whirlwind tour within 80 days and several others are well on
their way to becoming world travelers.

We apologize for a few unexpected travel delays and black-outs that
were experienced last season. We have made the necessary
arrangements to accommodate these travel-related inconveniences
during the coming season, including rerouting one leg of the trip.
As one of the tour destinations is closed to tourists from September
through April, the 2006 travel season is set to begin on May 1,
2006. As of this date all boxes will be in place and available for
tourists. During February, travelers who began the tour last year
will be sent information specific to their itinerary, including an
adjusted passport expiration date, allowances having been made for
destinations that became unavailable during the 2005 tourist season.

New travelers are encouraged to request a passport and make plans for
this exciting adventure. All passport requests received prior to the
end of April, will be mailed out on May 1st, unless you specifically
request it be mailed earlier. Passport requests received after May
1, will be mailed within 10 days after receipt of application.

This is the last year that we will be maintaining this series of
letterboxes. As of September 1, 2006, the clues will be posted at
letterboxing.org and the boxes will remain available, but the "80
Days" and "World Traveler" bonus stamps will no longer be available
and boxes will not be replaced if they go missing. So if you are
hoping to do the entire 15-stop tour within 80 days to qualify for
the bonus stamps, be sure to request your passport by June 3, 2006 to
allow yourself sufficient travel time.

For details and information about requesting your passport:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days.asp

Bon Voyage!

Azobox and SpringChick
