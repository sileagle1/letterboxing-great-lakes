A review of how it works:

There are 34 players/teams playing Bingo! Entry is officially
closed -- if you are in the players database at the group, you are
in.

Beginning today, every day I will draw out one of the
items/terms/etc. from the master list of 150 items. Between 9:00
a.m. and 10:00 a.m. EST I will post the chosen item to the group.
If that item is on your personal 30-item Bingo list, cross it off --
1 down, 29 to go!

Once all 30 items on your list have been called out, you have to
notify me within 3 days of your last item being called out. I will
verify my copy of your list with the list of things that have been
called out. If two or more people finish up their list on the same
day, there will be a tie-breaker to determine the winner.

I have divied up the booty a little so there are also 2nd and 3rd
prizes -- but the first prize is definitely the grand-daddy of
booties and anyone would be thrilled to win all of these cool LB
things!

If you miss a day, there will be a record of all the items that have
been called out in a database at the group.

Questions? Please post to the list or e-mail me.

Good luck everyone!
