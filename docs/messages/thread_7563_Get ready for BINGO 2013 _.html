From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-11-23 00:57:26 UTC

I hope everyone had a great Thanksgiving. Martini Man & I have much to
be thankful for and one of those things is knowing so many fun
letterboxers in the Great Lakes region!

As most of you know, part of our winter fun is playing the Great Lakes
Letterboxing BINGO game started by SpringChick back in 2006. The
illustrious Mr. Familyof6 took the reins for the last two years and now
I'm honored to be hosting the 2013 BINGO game. We're hoping to have a
lot of participants this year, so spread the word. You sure wouldn't
want to miss experiencing the slow motion thrill of victory and agony of
defeat that this game is famous for!

The necessary files are ready for your use in the "Files" section of the
message board (just scroll down to the "Great Lakes Letterbox BINGO!

rather be out letterboxing this weekend than venturing to a store filled
with those crazed "Black Friday" shoppers!

If you have any questions, just shoot me an email, or post the question
to this board.
