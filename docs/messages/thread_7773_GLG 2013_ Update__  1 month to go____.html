So here we are, just over a month until GLG:2013. We are hard at work
making final preparations for the event. And thought it best to send out
a quick blast to all. We are having some difficulty locating all the
GLG yearly standards (GLG standard event Boxes & whatnot), if you know
of their whereabouts please let us know.
The itinerary for GLG will be as follows (All times are Central):

9:00 AM Event Begins: Pick up Clue Packets
11:30 AM to 1:00 PM Lunch Time
1:00 PM Group Photo
6:00 PM Tear Down (Please have all personal effects & food removed from
the pavilion by 6:30, we will not be responsible for personal belongings
after this time)

Most stamps for the event have been accounted for, but if you still have
an itch to carve please let us know what you'd like to carve and we can
quickly let you know if its still available. Time is of the essence
here.

If you have Traveling Event Stamps that you will be bringing, please add
them to the Event on AQ.

If you would like a GLG T-shirt please contact rnfrog She will be happy
to provide you with any/all details. I'm not sure if she is actually a
member of this group so AQ mail is best for her.

Lastly, please take a look at Potluck Details and update your
contributions. We are in the neighborhood of 200 attendees and, that's
alot of people to feed!
