"Election Day" letterbox

From: Mothkovich, Christine (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2008-09-09 09:58:13 UTC-05:00
As some of you know, the "Election Day" box made its debut at the Birds of a Feather gathering June 21 in Kirkland, IL. To get the image, people were asked to vote. Discounting the local write-ins, the results were McCain 11 votes, Obama 8 votes.

The box then travelled to Maine for the Mid-Coast Maine Letterboxing Event in early August. Again discounting write-ins, results were McCain 12, Obama 10. And then the box went to the Beautiful Ohio's Edge gathering.

I just received the box back and here are the results: McCain 8 votes, Obama 2 votes. For what it's worth, this gathering took place the weekend AFTER McCain announced Sarah Palin as his running mate.

Two months to go til the REAL election and the box hasn't been invited to any other gatherings. Guess we'll have to wait 2 months to see if the general electorate follows the letterboxers' trend :)
