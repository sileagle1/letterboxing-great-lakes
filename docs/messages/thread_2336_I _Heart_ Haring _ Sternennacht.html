Just an update our 2 of our series. . .

We have decided to not only replace box 2 of Sternennacht, but to
pull the whole series from its current park and replace all 3 boxes
in a new park. We have learned from some people who frequent this
park that the work they're doing will be extensive and on-going and
don't want to keep losing our boxes. So we're pulling them this
weekend and we're going to find a "safer" place soon. We'll let you
all know when they are all intact again.

We also learned today that the park where our Haring boxes are is
under going some improvements as well (Just our luck!). The boxes
seem to still be intact(so far), but the landmarks to box 2 are
missing. It seems as though they're burying all of the power lines
and so the electrical poles that we reference in our clues are all
AWOL. We'll have to get out there this weekend to investigate
things a little further and rewrite our clues a bit.

Elgin, they just keep improving things. It's great in a way because
we live in Elgin and they have really improved the city a lot since
we moved here, but can't they leave the parks alone?!! What a
nightmare for some letterboxers who are just trying to keep their
boxes close so that they can maintain them more easily.

We'll keep you all posted!!
The Cottontails
