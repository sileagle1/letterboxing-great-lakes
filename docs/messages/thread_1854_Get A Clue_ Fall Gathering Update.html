Get A Clue! Fall Gathering Update

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-09-28 09:52:52 UTC
Wow, it's nearly October already! The MI Fall Gathering is quickly
approaching and we are really geeked about this get-together on
October 22nd at Yankee Springs Rec Area!

There is a great list of clue stamps in the works -- some have
arrived already. If you haven't already sent me your stamp, please
have in the mail by October 10th. Looking forward to seeing
everyone's carvings for this. I had the opportunity to watch the
Clue exchange game in action at the Virginia Mystery gathering last
a couple of weeks ago. This is going to be fun!

Aside from the Clue stamps, we could still use some more letterbox
contributions -- boxes to be planted out on the trail. Some have
been planted already and others are on the way. If you can get over
there to plant a box or two yourself, please bring copies of your
clues to the gathering to distribute. If you can't make it there to
plant, but would like to send a box, I am planning to go out there
and place boxes the weekend of the 15th, so if you can have it hear
by then, I will place it for you and write up clues to hand out at
the gathering.

There is a delicious list of food brewing for the potluck and it
promises to be a day filled with good fun, good boxes and good
people!

If you are planning to attend (or contribute a box) and I don't have
you on the list, drop me an e-mail and I will add you.
