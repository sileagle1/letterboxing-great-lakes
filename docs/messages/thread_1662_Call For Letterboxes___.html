Call For Letterboxes...

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-07-31 17:37:44 UTC
We are looking for people willing to create letterboxes for the Get
A Clue! MI Fall Gathering at Yankee Springs Rec Area.

There are a handful of existing letterboxes already in the park and
we are hoping you will help with some additional boxes before the
gathering. There are a few ways you can do this:

* Ideally we would like to see more permanently planted boxes in
this awesome park. So if you are able, why not plant a box or two
here sometime before the fall gathering? If you do, feel free to
bring copies of your clues to distribute to gathering attendees who
may not have had a chance to print clues before coming.

* As not everyone lives close enough to head over to Yankee Springs
and plant a letterbox, we welcome box contributions specifically for
the gathering. We are not setting a specific theme for boxes, so let
your imagination run wild -- feel free to play off the fairy tales
theme being used for the clue exchange game, anything related to
fall, perhaps a Halloween themed box, or maybe something relating to
the park name or history... Whatever appeals to you! If you will be
contributing a letterbox for the gathering and would like to have us
plant it for you, you will need to have it mail it to us so that we
receive it no later than October 5, 2005, to allow time for planting
and writing clues. Clues for these boxes will be distributed at the
gathering.

* Alternately you can create a box and bring it with you to plant
when you arrive at the gathering. In this case you will need to plan
on distributing clues either via word of mouth or by writing them up
for people to copy, etc.


Of course, we really want you to come to the gathering, but if for
some reason, you aren't able to attend, we would be thrilled if you
would still like to send us a letterbox!
