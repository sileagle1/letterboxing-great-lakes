OT (Michigan)-The Getty 4 is OPEN!

From: rick dannenberg (doglvr895@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-22 15:19:27 UTC-07:00
Many of you know that we (the Doglvrs family) make the
trip to Muskegon, MI many times each summer to visit
the Getty 4 Drive-in. I just wanted to let everyone
know that it's OPEN for business! There is a really
neat write-up in the local Advance newspaper about it
this week.
All four screens are now in operation - the main
screen (the biggest) needed to be replaced last year -
and even though it will eat most of the profit for
this season - they replaced it and now it is better
than ever!
Let's all go to the drive-in this summer and show our
support for a little piece of history right here in
West Michigan! ~Mrs. Doglvrs
