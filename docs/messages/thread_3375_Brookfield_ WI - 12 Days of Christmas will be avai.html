From: the_cignacats (mlcigna@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-30 14:34:28 UTC
We are finishing up the hiding of the 12 Days of Christmas this
afternoon. (One of the boxes needed a new spot and we couldn't find
a good one in the dark! Imagine us the other night, both in our
business wear, me in heels, trying to find a hiding spot by cell
phone light!) The boxes will be available starting tomorrow and
will be out until the end of January. St. Nick's Gathering folks,
these boxes are less than 5 minutes away from the gathering
location. The clues out on LBNA are not correct right now, since
they do not include the new spot for the one box, so don't print out
copies of the clues until tomorrow AM! There will be a message at
the top of the clues once they are updated for 2006-2007.

We will only be at the gathering on Saturday for an hour or two
since we need to drive down to Chicago, but we will print out some
copies of the clues and leave them there for anyone interested that
doesn't get the chance to print them out before hand. We will also
be leaving clues laying around for our newest box, The Three Little
Kittens Who Lost Their Mittens, which will be available at the
gathering.

Looking forward to seeing the early folks, and we will be sorry to
miss most of the day! There are a lot of new boxers that we were
hoping to get to meet! Stay warm and happy boxing!
