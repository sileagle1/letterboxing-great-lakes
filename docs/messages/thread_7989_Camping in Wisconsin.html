We'll  be camping in Pinewoods Campground in Kettle Moraine Forest southern unit in Wisconsin.  Any suggestions for boxes near there?

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2014-06-16 16:48:42 UTC-05:00

The closest to your campground is probably the "Animals of the Forest" series.  You can check both LBNA and AQ for boxes located in the cities of Ottawa, Eagle and Dousman.

 

If you haven't already been to Lapham Peak (which is a nearby KMSF area), there are a TON of letterboxes there.  Many are from the "Wandering in Wonderland" 2006 GLG, there are also numerous "Eggopolis" themed boxes, many boxes from the "Boxacon II" event in 2012 and several other individual boxes.  Be sure to check BOTH LBNA and AQ because some boxes are only listed on one site.  You can search for boxes in the city of Delafield and for boxes with the word "Eggopolis".
