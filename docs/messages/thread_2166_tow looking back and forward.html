We loved LB in hawaii on our honeymoon! The best box we did was Puako
Petroglyph by Wisconsin Hiker. It was the creepiest forest we have
ever been in. We even video taped the find it was so surreal. And we
went to the southern most point in the u.s.a to see the girlguides
volcanoes national park. We walk to see hot lava over the lava field
and went in the lava caves. I will treasure that vacation forever!

Looking forward: I would love to go to the U.P. and camp around for a
week up there going to different LB. I would like to place a LB on
Isle Royal. I am also looking forward to the gathering I am hosting
(hoping it is not a crazy mess).
