Where is Hollywood, anyway?

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-06-14 23:36:14 UTC-05:00
The Paw is located in the western suburbs (Brookfield, IL, home of
Brookfield Zoo), about 15 miles due west of downtown Chicago, about halfway
between I290 (Eisenhower) and I55 (Stevenson), just a few blocks west of
First Avenue (which is an exit on both expressways).



Hollywood has an interesting history - actually it consists of parts of
three towns, Riverside, Brookfield, and Lyons. It was the original
Hollywood that Hollywood, California was named after.



--klk
