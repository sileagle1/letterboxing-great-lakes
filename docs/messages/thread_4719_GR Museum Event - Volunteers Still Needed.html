GR Museum Event - Volunteers Still Needed

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-01-27 14:11:06 UTC
We are still looking for a few volunteers to help out with the carving
activity at the Grand Rapids Public Museum event being held on
Saturday, Feb. 2. It was decided to forego the formal carving
workshop due to potential for injury and cost of providing carving
tools. Instead there will just be an informal activity area where
children (and adults) can make stamps using fun foam. It was also
decided to move this activity down to the main galleria area next to
the information table instead of having it off in a separate room,
which will make it much more accessible to people.

A few volunteers are still needed to help oversee this area, just to
be available to assist or answer questions as needed. Shifts run for
blocks of two hours each (9-11, 11-1, 1-3). Volunteers will receive
free admission to the museum for the day and free parking. Come for
the day -- volunteer for a couple of hours and then spend the rest of
the day enjoying the wonderful exhibits the GR Public Museum has to
offer and hunting down the letterboxes that have been placed
throughout the museum for this event!

If you can help out, please e-mail me offlist to let me know which
time works best for you.

Thanks!

SpringChick
