From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-11-30 20:57:55 UTC-06:00
So, no one has voiced said, "NO" so I'm doing the SIDEWAGERS this year.
Here are the details.


*/Side Wager: /*///A wager made beyond the normal rules of the game. A
hedge bet.///

If you'd like to add more excitement to the letterboxing bingo game (or
another way to win), enter the Side Wagers Game. When will FamilyOf6
pull the item that finally and completely fills in some player's
card?Pick the right day, and you WIN! Again, this is the Big Game here,
not the interim games. The date will be determined by the claiming of a
successful card by a BINGO player (and its certification by FamilyOf6).

Your guess must be submitted before January 1, 2011.

Email me at aatom118@gmail.com
In the subject line, put "*Sidewagers*."
In the body, please indicate the date you have chosen and your trailname.

The winner is the person who is closest *before* the day the Bingo Game
ends or actually hits the date on the dot. If you choose February 15th,
for example, and someone else chooses the 20th, and the game ends on the
19th, you win. In other words, you'll hold all of the days until the
next guesser hits the calendar. (But you don't know who has chosen which
days or how far apart they've fallen -- and neither does FamilyOf6!).

If two or more players choose the same date, the first guesser is given
the date and another available date will be assigned to anyone else who
chooses the date already taken. This will be done by moving earlier *or*
later until the date, roughly, between your choice and the next one
chosen is available.So, if you choose Feb 20 and it's already taken your
date could be: the 22^nd if the next date chosen is the 24^th ; or you'd
get the 25^th if 4, 21, 22, and 28^th are taken; or you'd get the 18^th
if 16, 21, 22, and 28^th are taken.

As a bonus, a prize will be awarded to the lucky winner!

Finally, no purchase necessary to enter (ie, you do not need to be a
player in the BINGO game to participate).One entry per person.Atom118
and the Atomic Beans assume no responsibility for the order or words
chosen by FamilyOf6.

Send me any questions.

Thanks!

Atom118 of the Atomic Beans
