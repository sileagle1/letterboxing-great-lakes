From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-12-26 20:42:20 UTC

It's Boxing Day and even if you can't get out to hunt for a letterbox,
you CAN get your 2013 Bingo card filled out if you haven't already done
so!

There are only 5 days left to get your card submitted. New players are
always welcome and I hope more of the experienced players will be
playing again this year.

I have received a card from someone who claims it will be the "winning
card". Do you want to prove this person wrong? Then get your card in
soon! You can send it to me via email (kotlarek@wi.rr.com
) or pop it in an envelope and mail it to
me. I must have your CARD by DEC 31 if you want to be part of the fun.

Your booty contribution and $2 fee (to cover costs of mailing out
prizes) isn't due until JAN 26. You can give it to me in person (I'll
be at the Dead of Winter event) or mail it to me by that date. Booty
can be anything related to letterboxing. If you want to save on mailing
costs, gift cards for gas, craft stores or restaurant chains can easily
be sent in an envelope with your card and $2.

Wisconsin Hiker
