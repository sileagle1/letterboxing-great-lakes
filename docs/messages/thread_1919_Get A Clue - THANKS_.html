Get A Clue - THANKS!

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-10-24 13:35:39 UTC
I just wanted to pass along a huge THANKS! to everyone who took a
chance on the weather and came out to Yankee Springs for the Get A
Clue! Gathering on Saturday. It was a bit chilly in the morning, but
it didn't take long for the sun to peek through and the chill to burn
off. It is such a pleasure for me to see the planning come together
and everyone having a great time acquainting, exchanging and sharing
stories.

You guys were all so busy playing the Clue Exchange game that you
probably didn't step back just to observe -- what a riot! Everyone
frantically collecting clues from the other "detectives" and
secreting scouring the area for hidden clues laying around the "scene
of the crime." Congratulations to the Four Clovers (Munchkin Stamper,
Stampin' Pam, Scooter Lady and One-Cent Stamper), who were the first
to successfully solve the crime, and also to Pale Puppy Posse and the
AW half of Doglvrs who were the 2nd and 3rd place sleuthing teams.
And the food -- WOW! What a wonderful selection and I simply must get
the recipe for those Rice Krispie things from TJ_Mich.

I enjoyed meeting each and every one of you whom I had not previously
met, and of course seeing old friends again! What a great bunch of
letterboxers! I didn't get a chance to head out onto the trail
myself so I will be back down within the next month or so to look for
the Amazing Series and Cranky Things (and hopefully the Lucky
HorseShoe). I also have some pictures from the event -- I will try to
get them uploaded this evening.

Many thanks to Azobox for all of his help and input putting this
thing together and also to my OldShoe for being my backbone and
helping to keep things running smoothly throughout the day. You guys
are the best!

Looking forward to the next gathering... spring?

SpringChick
