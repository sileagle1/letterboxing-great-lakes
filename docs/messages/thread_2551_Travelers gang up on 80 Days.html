The "Around the World in 80 Days" staff has reports of near-record
crowds at certain passport checkpoints.

the elusive 15 city tour stamps and attemnpt to qualify for the bonus.

We tried our best but alas, like Tiptoe and Tonto and Star Girl and
El Cajador, we were stuck at one location. We visited all 15
locations, but only found 14 of the stamps (after 2 visits to one of
them). We're tired, sore, biten, and scratched, but we still managed
to have a good time!

It was great to meet Tiptoe and Tonto on the trail (although we both
snuck by each other at one location and then met up at another!) and
would have been great to meet other boxers! We thought we might
have found another group from Minnesota, as their van had the same
park stickers ours had, but alas, they had left just before we
reached the parking lot.
