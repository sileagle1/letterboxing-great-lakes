Oh, I've bought a sheet of SpeedyCut myself. It has been working well. I
think it just depends on what you're trying to do with it. My first sig
stamp was made from MasterCarve, and it wore out, so nothing is perfect.
Granted, I have used the SpeedyCut stuff mostly for either limited use
carvings or those which are going postal, so that they will endure less
weather-related stress. I've had none go bad on me. Whatever works for you
is the best stuff to use!
