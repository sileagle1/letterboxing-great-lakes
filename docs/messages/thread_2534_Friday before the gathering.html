I've tentatively reserved an area at the Delafield Brewhaus for Friday
evening, starting at 8 pm. Let me know if you think you might make it, and
what time you might arrive. We'll probably be there for part of the time.
The Brewhaus is open until midnight.

If anyone is interested in getting together Saturday night after the event,
one possible meeting place is Marty's Pizza in Delafield. Perhaps an 8:30
or 9:00 pm time? Again, let me know if you might be interested in this....

Either or both nights would provide another opportunity to talk letterboxing
and do some "exchanges".

Both places are off of exit 287 in Delafield (~2 miles from Lapham Peak).
Here are links for more info re: menu, etc. No need to order food Friday
night, there is full bar in addition to their specialty beers. However if
you do want food, both a regular menu and a "late night" menu are available.
Marty's of course offers pizza, in addition to other Italian items.

Friday night Pre-event: http://www.delafield-brewhaus.com/interior.html

Saturday night Post-event: http://www.foodspot.com/martys/delafield/

I'll hopefully also post this info on the website tomorrow or Thursday
night.
