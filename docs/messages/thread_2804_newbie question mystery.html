From: doubledragonletterboxer (M8KC5008@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-21 13:05:57 UTC
I guess it depends on what meet you are talking about. If you go to
the files section on this site there is some info on gatherings. There
are micro meets that are orchestrated by individuals, and you just
have to ask them. As far as I know it usually isn't a secret where the
meets are. If you want the right info on the meet it would help to
know which meet you are talking about so someone can point you in the
right direction. I hope this helps you!
