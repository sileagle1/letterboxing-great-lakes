T&T, likewise it was great to see your smiling faces at the museum!
What a wonderful surprise!

I'm sure the look on my face was priceless when I looked up (and up,
and up) and there stood Tonto! Before I could screech "what are you
doing here?" Tiptoe came running over, big smile, arms flailing...
Hugs were exchanged all around and after catching up on where
everyone's life was at, T&T set out in search of the letterboxes at
the musuem. It was great fun watching them strategize, running to and
fro, from floor to floor, collecting clues and bits of information. I
am happy to hear all 4 boxes were found!

Thanks also to everyone else who made it out to the museum yesterday.
We enjoyed meeting everyone and the laid-back atmosphere of the day
made conversations very easy.

The boxes will be at the GR Public Museum through May 11, so if you
haven't been there yet, there is still time to make the drive for
these limited edition treasures.

SpringChick
