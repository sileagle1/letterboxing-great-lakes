"John J Foerch" wrote:
Bindle Babe, what material are your stamps made of?

Almost all my stamps are on Speedball Speedycut; the "cuts like
butter" stuff, John. The Speedycut is easy to cut on, but it is true
that it can be crumbly; especially if your carving nib is even
slightly dull.

The other drawback to the Speedcut material is that it is less
durable than rubber-type carving materials. That's why I mount all
my stamps.

I just updated the carving tutorial at Picture Trail. If anyone
wants to take a look this is the link:
http://www.picturetrail.com/bindle_boxers

Enjoy!
