TOTALLY BUSTED

From: estella_naparella (estella_naparella@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-02-14 02:59:44 UTC
Skootching Dog and I were out boxing this morning and got totally busted
logging in while sitting in a residential alley (right where the box
was) by a morning power walker. Crap. What to do?? Giving away the
location of the hide is NOT an option.... so what?

Answer: long, boring tutorial. we talked to this poor woman and showed
her our (entire) logbook until she was squirming to get away. we were
THE most BORING people on the face of the PLANET for about 10 minutes
today. She finally escaped us, knowing we were not ill-doers, sitting
about with markers and notebooks, doodling in the early Sunday morning
hours...

Sometimes, being able to extemporize on the spur of the moment really
pays off!
...and the hide was protected!
