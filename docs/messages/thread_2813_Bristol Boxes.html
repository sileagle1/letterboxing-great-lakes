From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-25 03:37:34 UTC
I think that having more boxes and a gathering and all at the faire
is a great idea, however, please keep in mind that it is a privately
operated business and proper permissions are needed before anything
is done. Although I have never had a problem with getting permission
and they enjoy having us do the boxes each year, I do HAVE to make a
formal request each year and provide detailed information about where
the boxes are and stuff.

I would suggest that if you wish to plant any more boxes at Britol
and/or have a gathering there that maybe Kate could talk to Sue (the
Bristol manager) about it as I am too far away right now. I'm sure
that as long as the boxes are placed so they don't interfere with the
activities of the faire, and the proper channels are gone through,
they will be allowed. I also would think that the faire may work a
special deal for letterboxers coming for a gathering. If you intend
to make yourself into a letterperson, I don't think you would need
any prior permission for that, unless you work at the faire in a role
and it might interfere with your role.

Glad everyone is having fun with the boxes again this year! Let's
keep the Bristol management happy with us!

Thanks!
Dragon
