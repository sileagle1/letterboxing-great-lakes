Great Lakes Gathering 2011: A Day in the Life

From: zilzilwat (papadelunagirl@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-12-31 18:10:15 UTC


Lunagirl and Papa and the Southeast Michigan Stampers are proud to present Great Lakes Gathering 2011: A Day in the Life, celebrating the musical genius of The Beatles. Join us on June 10th and 11th at Proud Lake Recreation Area in Milford MI. Camping reservations and park information is available at: (http://www.dnr.state.mi.us/parksandtrails/Details.aspx?id=487&type=SPRK

Featured Events are to include (subject to change):

A Hard Day's Night

Night of June 10th

6:00: Evening Gathering, Potluck Dinner, Bonfire, Beatles Rock Band Non-Competitive Competition

10:00: Showing of "Yellow Submarine" and "A Hard Day's Night"

11:00: Night boxing for park stamps


Good Day Sunshine

8:00: Potluck breakfast 9:30: Group photo. 10:00: Boxing begins.


Penny Lane

Vendors with booths and table-top displays featuring letterboxing-related items. Interested vendors please contact Lunagirl and Papa.


Yellow Submarine

A family-friendly letterbox quest to collect all the major characters from the Yellow Submarine Movie (John, Paul, George, Ringo, Chief Blue Meanie, The Dreadful Flying Glove, Jeremy Hilary Boob, and Captain Fred.)


Here, There, and Everywhere

Stamps of characters from Beatles' songs will be hidden and sought for around the ground of Proud Lake. Can you find Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds? The Fool on the Hill? Chairman Mao? Lovely Rita? Maxwell and his Silver Hammer? How many characters can you think of? Guest submissions are welcome. Anyone wishing to carve a stamp, please contact Lunagirl and Papa via AQ.


The Magical Mystery Tour

An advanced letterboxing quest for those experienced in puzzles and ciphers. Various Beatles-related images will be hidden throughout the area. Find them to decode the cipher and be the first to uncover the four-part solution stamp.

All letterboxers are more than welcome. We look forward to seeing you there.

Lunagirl and Papa
