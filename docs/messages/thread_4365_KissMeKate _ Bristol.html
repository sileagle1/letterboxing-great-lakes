Thanks to everyone who has been out at the Faire and looking for
me! I'm sorry I haven't run into to anyone yet this year, as I've
been so busy with other activities! I'll be there for the next (and
last) three weekends of the Faire, so keep an eye out for me!

This year I'm involved in the charity for Renaissance Faire workers
and have been selected as one of the champions for my group (the
Wenches Guild and other affiliated guilds).

The charity is R.E.S.C.U. and they are a 501(c)3 organization. Their
website is http://www.rescufoundation.com/ and they provide funds to
help cover medical and other costs incurred by people who work at
Renaissance Faires when they are ill or injured. Unlike working for
businesses or corporations, many Faire workers are one-man
operations and generally are able to make ends meet, so when illness
or injury strike, there's usually no insurance or rainy-day funds to
pay for clinic and hospital costs.

Please help me raise money for R.E.S.C.U. over the next two weeks
and to crown me and my fellow champions as Bristol Best In Show!
I am accepting funds through my PayPal account (kburrall [at]
trcsolutions [dot] com, and will be making a stamp image to send to
everyone who makes a donation.

Thanks again and I hope to see a few more 'boxers at Faire this
season!

-- KissMeKate
