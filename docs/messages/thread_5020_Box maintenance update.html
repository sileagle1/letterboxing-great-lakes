Box maintenance update

From: Chris Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2008-05-04 18:42:25 UTC-05:00
OK, now that I have replaced the two missing boxes from my "River Fauna" series, I was able to do some checking elsewhere today and found that "Signs of the Zodiac: Capricorn" is gone, apparently the victim of an overzealous burn-and-clear campaign by the county conservation department. I will get it recarved soon and hidden in a hopefully safer location.

Also, "Signs of the Zodiac: Aquarius" had been washed away by the early-April flooding in this area. You'd think I would have learned my lesson since it was FIRST washed away last August, and I went right ahead and replaced it in the very same place last fall. But now I do believe I may just have finally wised up, and today NamVet and I rehid Aquarius III in a higher, dryer place and updated the clues.

We also checked on "Signs of the Zodiac: Scorpio" and found the box safely in place, even though (due to the April flooding) there is a lot of debris strewn about the area.

Trail Rider
