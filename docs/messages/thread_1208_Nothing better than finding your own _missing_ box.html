From: Leapin' Lizards (emptyenergy1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-23 17:38:19 UTC-07:00
Hi everyone!

We had received an update on our Circus Tent box in
our Greatest Show on Earth Series. It sounded like
the letterboxer had the correct place but couldn't
find the box. We were finally able to get over there
today and check for sure. When we first looked, we
were confused. Seemed right, but seemed wrong. We
searched around. Then I saw that the downed tree had
been moved a foot or two down the hill. We then
figured that the tree hadn't been pushed but flipped.
When we checked the branch that would have been in the
air(but was now cracked on the ground) we found the
box intact inside the branch. It hadn't been found
since December. So anyone missing the Circus Tent
letterbox in the series can check the updated clues
and complete the first five in The Greatest Show on
Earth.

Leapin' Lizards
