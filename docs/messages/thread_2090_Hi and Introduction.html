From: cap (chancey@cablespeed.com) | Date: 2005-12-11 20:51:31 UTC-05:00
Thank you, Spring Chick, for the invite to join this group!

I'm PennyPenny from South Lyon, MI ... and I'm relatively new to
letterboxing!

I found the hobby too irresistible to pass up when I read the little
article in Time Magazine. That was early in the summer of '04, I
believe.

After reading Randy Hall's book I quickly carved my first stamp (my
signature stamp, "PennyPenny," which I created from a photo of my cat,
Penny.)

The kids and I hunted down a couple of local boxes soon after. I've
placed two boxes myself recently... and I'm finding I really, really
like placing better than finding -- at least for the time being! I've
monitored the lists since last summer, and am soaking up the info and
nuances of the sport (can I call it that?!) I don't post too much
really... as I feel I'm such a "newbie" that I don't have much to offer
yet!

I've got 3 sons; one of whom is not in school yet, so my busy life
curtails the time I spend doing letterboxing. I'm counting on doing
much more of it in the future! I'll eventually pull my hubby into it
too -- somehow -- although his work keeps him occupied all-too-many
hours of the day to bring him onboard right now. (I do know he's
intrigued, though... he really liked watching me create my first two
boxes!)

So for now I'm doing this alone... although I almost met some local
boxers a few weeks back... (namely, The 7 Huckaby's!) but alas,
Schedules ultimately nixed our meeting. I'm sure we'll get together
another time... there aren't a whole lot in our area!

So a big HELLO to all and I look forward to meeting some of you in the
future! Have fun boxing!!

PennyPenny

Hi PennyPenny! Welcome to the Great Lakes group! I can really
