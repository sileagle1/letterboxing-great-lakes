Oh, dear Family of Many...I will have to contradict..I have heard that there is a fine place in CA
called Tip Toe Falls (also Tiptoe Falls) it is a tiny 5-8 foot cascade (though most sources say
5 or 6 feet) Very fitting for the exact size of the REAL Tiptoe...and Tonto.

This falls is located in Portola Redwoods State Park, California. They claim it is on either Fall
Creek or Mill Creek, however, no one knows for sure. (Again, the similarity is amazing as we
usually have no idea where we are either!) Nevertheless, it is located on a tributary of
Pescadero Creek and can be reached by the Iverson Trail, which is accessed by the visitor
center.

Also stated in the description is that, "There are banana slugs and wild turkeys on the trail."

With that description, no one can refute that the REAL Tiptoe & Tonto are adequately
memorialized at this little known spot regarding their letterboxing trail behavior!
Regards,
The Real Tiptoe
