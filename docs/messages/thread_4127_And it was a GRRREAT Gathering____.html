Horse Trader and I had a great time at the Cluez-In Gathering and want
to thank Stacey and Jim for putting on a top notch Event. Their
organization and effort were noticeable everywhere with the facilities,

prize for furthest travel.

From: Barb Funk (mmfunk99@greenapple.com) | Date: 2007-06-03 13:36:55 UTC-04:00
I will second Horse Trader and Safari Man's motion in recognizing the
incredible effort our hosts put forth to insure a fantastic event! Many
thanks to you both and to your two children, who no doubt, helped in
many ways as well!

I also want to tack on a thank you to Rosmarinus for putting together
the Friday night Sock Hop and reserving the church for the several
pre-event-event. Your organizational efforts were very noticed there as
well.

Personally, I very much enjoyed the camping part and having a chance to
chat in a very relaxed fashion. I had the best camping neighbors (both
previously known and new) anyone could ask for.

I think the Great Lakes group has some stunningly talented and creative
carvers too! What a wonderful bunch of stamps we all got to add to our
collections!

Last, some of us commented later in the day on Saturday how wonderful it
was to take note that all these kids had a fantastic time playing,
finding all the many wonderful boxes Hales Angels made for them,
noticing them getting along so very well together having just met and
for being so polite around adults. All of us adults had such a great
time meeting each other or reuniting and even all the dogs had a great
time together as well! It just seemed like life got suspended for one
wonderful day!

My thanks to all who contributed in so many ways to the Columbus
Cluez-In Event!
Trailtracker

Hi everyone!

I agree with Safari Man (whom I was very glad to meet, by the way) that the gathering was a
blast! We had never been to one before, having just started boxing in November, and frankly, we were a bit nervous...not knowing exactly what we had gotten ourselves into! :)

Let me tell you, I have been bragging about you folks to anyone who'll listen for the past two days! I've never met a nicer group of people...or seen a BIGGER potluck! (and I come from a BIG potluck family!) :) The kids had a great time as well, especially my friend's youngest, who kept proclaiming the Disney Series as "so easy"! She's 8 and feeling pretty proud of herself!

Speaking of potluck, does anyone know who made the awesome salsa with all of the corn and tomatoes in it? I'd love to have the recipe!
We are excited to start placing some boxes of our own soon, so be on the lookout for Huntin' Dogs and Busy Bees! See you on the trail...and hopefully at another gathering!
