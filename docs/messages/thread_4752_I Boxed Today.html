I Boxed Today

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-02-06 01:59:39 UTC
Today for work I had a stop in Oregon, Illinois. The last time I had
a stop in Oregon was November 16th and I found Pastor Wife's "John
Deere Plow II" after dark during hunting season!!

Today with a forcast of 9-14 inches of snow I raced out there hoping
to beat the snow... I got there 5 minutes after the snow started
falling in HUGE flakes so thick it was hard to see anything.

I couldn't park at the correct picnic shelter as the road was closed
off... so I had to walk an extra 3/10's of a mile (just a guess on
that number).

The snow on the ground from the previous storm was still 3-5 inches
deep and I clammered and clawed my way down the path.

We had rain fall this afternoon before the snow so it was all a
sloppy, slushy mess with some puddles and ponds of collected water on
the pathway.

I was able to find my prize with a bonus of a HH inside!! This is my
first Trail Rider stamp and its a beauty!! Thanks TR for the
wonderful find in a great park.

As I walked back, re-tracing my footsteps back to my car became
difficult as they were already filling back up with the snow that was
falling.

My socks are still drying out, but it was worth it! Chalk this up to
my ideal lunch break.

I did drive past White Pines State Park but by now the snow was even
worse . . . not to mention I already took my lunch break, so
Wisconsin Hiker, your stamp will have to wait :-)

Thanks Trail Rider for the great excursion!!

hx6
