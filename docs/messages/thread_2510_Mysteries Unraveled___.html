From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-05-15 02:11:02 UTC
I'm really excited because this weekend two of my boxes that have both
given numerous people fits and both produced countless "the box is
missing" reports were found, safe and sound, right where they should
be -- Tulip Time Twist and Copper Country Autumn.

Both are boxes that I have not personally been able to check on and I
had begun to be concerned that perhaps they had actually gone
missing. Thanks to the finders, J&BBooks and Martha from the U.P. for
updating me on these boxes and terrain changes, etc. A few clues
changes are in order due to slight terrain and landmark changes on
both of these.

Congratulations on finding these boxes!
