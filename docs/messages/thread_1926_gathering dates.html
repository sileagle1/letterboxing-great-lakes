From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-05-10 18:30:53 UTC-04:00
Per the Great Lakes Gathering FAQ (found in the files section of this list), the annual gathering is held the first Saturday after Memorial Day each year. Typically this would be the first Saturday in June, but in a few cases, it is actually the last Saturday of May as will happen in 2008, in which case the Gathering will be held on Saturday, May 31, 2008.
