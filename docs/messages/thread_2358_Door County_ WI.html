From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-04-03 02:06:55 UTC
Spring is vacation planning time for a lot of people and if you are a
letterboxer, chances are you're looking for a vacation spot that
offers great scenery and lots of great letterboxes.

I thought I'd put in a word for Door County, WI. If you've never
visited this little peninsula that juts out between Lake Michigan and
Green Bay, you're in for a real treat. You will find quaint towns,
art galleries, antique shops, parks, lighthouses and lots of hiking
opportunities. Not only is the area packed with things to do and see
and some of the best scenery around, but there are several fantastic
letterboxes to be found here.

I just finished posting my photos from our Door County trip last fall
to my Flickr account. You can view them here (you do not need to
create an account there to view photos):

http://www.flickr.com/photos/springchick/sets/72057594077618161/
