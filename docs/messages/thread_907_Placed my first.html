Hi Great Lakers.
I've been lurking (reading and listening) since September. I got
started doing letterboxing in July when I wanted something to do with
my Great Niece who is 11 and I only see once a year on average. So,
I'd heard about letterboxing on NPR, went to the site and found one
in the community where her grandma is. It was a success and she has
since Placed 4 (to in CA where she lives and one in Hilton Head where
another grandma lives and 1 in NY where her Dad lives) and found 27.
We email lots and she and her Dad came to my house in Maumee for
Thanksgiving. We found 2 letterboxes and placed one. She has
challenged me to have as many as she does by June when she will be
back. She did say I could add mine and my daughters together -
daughter lives in RI - so that is what I'll be doing at Christmas.

My box is at: http://letterboxing.org/BoxByRegion.asp?region=North%
20Western%20Ohio and it is very near the intersection of I475 and I75
near Toledo OH.

Thanks for being around.
