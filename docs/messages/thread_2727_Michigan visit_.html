From: TrailTroll (kanderson@american-community.com) | Date: 2006-07-05 16:09:23 UTC
Ann Arbor has more great locations than you'll cover in a day - I
love 'boxing there! If you're going for the Little Buckeye, I know
Flying Saucer is not too far and it was one of my favorite walks.
Kind of long, but interesting trail and fun clues. I had several
others I was going to recommend, but I see that the clues are gone
now. And there are a lot I haven't done, so I can't really give you
any advice. I guess letterboxing is a dynamic hobby!

If you happen to take Pontiac Trail on the way from Ann Arbor to
Troy, you might check out the Wetlands Wanderer (3 boxes) and Seasons
(4 boxes) series. I haven't heard anything about the Seasons boxes
in a while (they're not mine), but I know the Wetlands series is all
there.

Unfortunately I have plans for Friday night, or I'd love to do an
exchange. Hey, what time are you coming? I work right off the
highway in Livonia! Good luck, happy anniversary, and enjoy your
southeastern Michigan experience!

TrailTroll
