What once was lost, but now is found!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-09-30 03:23:59 UTC
Woo Hoo! I have to compliment some geocachers once again. We had
reports that my All Fool's Day box was missing, and when we checked a
month or two or ago, could find no sign of it. We therefore indicated
in the clues that it was missing.

However last Friday I received a message from a geocacher who had
found it out in the open in the general area, and who put it back in a
nearby tree. They gave me the GPS coordinates (which didn't do much
for me), and also provided a description of where they put it (that
DID give us something to go on).

Since we were out of town for a 3-day weekend last weekend, and then I
was out of town on business during the week, today was the first
chance we had to look for the missing box. We managed to find it
fairly quickly based on the info from the geocachers. Box is a bit
nibbled on, but stamp & logbook are in great shape. Hooray!! We
brought it home to put in a new box and will be replacing it shortly.

Wisconsin Hiker
