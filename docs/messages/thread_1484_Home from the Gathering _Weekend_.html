Home from the Gathering [Weekend]

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-06-21 00:39:24 UTC
We are finally home from the weekend. OldShoe and I went back today
to grab a couple of boxes that I didn't have time for on Saturday
and then made a stop for Dragon's the "Go to Hell" box on our way
back to the highway.

For anyone who is still looking for boxes in the next couple of
weeks -- if you were hoping to get the "Perfect vision from the 1st"
box... sorry, I took it home today. Actually I went back for it on
Saturday evening on my way out (yes, at 9:45 p.m. in the dark), but
it had moved a bit from where I had originally placed it and I felt
around a bit in the dark (it was really dark back there in that
grove of trees), but didn't find it. So picture this -- here I am
back in the weeds and trees in the dark (I saw later that there was
a flashlight in the truck), on my hands and knees feeling blindly
under some logs for this box, all the while singing because I didn't
want to startle any critters by my presence and I had seen those two
very large holes (dens?) only a few feet from where that box was
hidden, not to mention this was the place where I saw the Massasauga
rattler a couple years ago. I didn't find the box, however the
singing was a hit and successfully scared away all critters and
snakes.

Anyway... the GL Gathering was great fun -- thanks Team Rabbit for
all of the work you put into this event! Wow, talk about
overwhelmed and overloaded! There was so much going on, from the
many new faces to the circus, to meeting Mark and Sue, to the
wonderfully created event boxes and "secret" boxes. I know I didn't
even get a chance to meet everyone trying to fit everything into the
day. Hopefully at the next gathering! I was thrilled though to see
some old friends and meet so many new letterboxers -- from near and
far!

All of the stamps were awesome and it was nice to see the work of
first-time carvers (way to go Kody!), creative ingenuity (Dragon,
the Mo' Mustangs series was great!) and skillfully carved works of
art (Team Rabbit, the landmarks stamps were gorgeous!)

Mark & Sue... it was awesome to finally meet the two of you and
have you at our event here in Michigan! Thanks for bringing the
circus to town -- some incredible carvings in there! Mark, thanks
for the informative and inspiring presentation on Dartmoor -- where
do I sign up?

Moma Bear -- So nice to meet you and have you at the Gathering from
New York -- and thanks for the present you left us over in Grand
Rapids. I am hoping to get over there while it is still safe and
sound.

Dragon -- thanks also for your efforts with another successful event
(and BTW -- loved the pi so much had to go back today for another
hit).

Tom & Jill -- so glad you took pictures and thanks for posting them!

Again, thank you to EVERYONE and so glad to have had the chance to
spend some time with you all for the day!

'Til the next one...

SpringChick
