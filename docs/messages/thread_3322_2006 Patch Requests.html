I'm very close to having the patch finalized (they are taking one
more pass to get the colors closer to last year's patch).

As I indicated before, I was thinking of only ordering 100 patches
this time, because I'm assuming that some people that purchased
patches last year wouldn't necessarily purchase another one. With a
smaller order quantity and a slightly larger patch size, the price
will probably be about $2.50 per patch.

Before I put the final order in, I thought I'd try to find out how
many people might want to order them. I therefore created a simple
database within this Yahoo group. Simply go to the database section,
then click on the New 2006 Patch Requests database (near the bottom
of the database list). After you open the database, near the top &
center will be the words "Add Record". Click on this then enter your
Trailname and the number of patches you might want. This isn't a
binding obligation, but helps me determine an initial order quantity.

Please put your request in ASAP, since I'll need to finalize this
order in the next day or two.
