From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-06-18 22:06:29 UTC-05:00

Sunny,



The same decision making challenges face all of us from time to time. That's because we are 'artists' and we're not always satisfied with how our conceptual ideas translate to a finished project. If this helps you, and others, the only rule is.....do your best! We won't know where the mistakes are.



Sometimes, it's best to keep it simple. Once, I was asked to participate in a Beatles LTC exchange based on album art and our favorite song. I choose.....the white album. The LTC was a plain white card. ROFLOL!!! Of course there was one stamp hand carved stamp...the name "The Beatles" stamped in the lover corner in light gray ink. It was very affective.



Sunny, your best effort is all that is necessary. We were there at the GLG 2010. We all know what a muddy boot represents.....good times on the trails looking for letterboxes.
