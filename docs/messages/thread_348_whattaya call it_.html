From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-27 01:43:34 UTC
So when you meet another letterboxer out in the wild, but you've
already done an exchange........whattaya call it?

A nice opportunity to renew the acquaintance, I guess.

Artistamp and I were setting out to hunt the new Celtic series at
Grand River Park in the Jenison, MI area (awesome stamps, BTW!). As
we drove through the parking area she said, "Hey, there's 'Lucky
Charms' -- back up the car".
Sure enough, it was the Charms sitting at a picnic table mapping
strategies for their next hunt. We had a nice visit. They had just
finished the Celtic series and were about to begin the Sphinx and
Serpentine box hikes.
Since our previous exchange was during the busy time of the McHenry
Gathering, it was nice to have a chance to chat a bit (and they
didn't have the added responsibility of young neices and nephews this
time).

Keep your eyes open.
Letterboxers are everywhere.
