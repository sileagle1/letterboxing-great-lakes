From: Althea Gill (althea_gill@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-21 00:17:30 UTC
The Neighborhood Boxers Mini Meet drew a small but distinguished group
of boxers. We had visitors stop by unexpected and a core group for the
entire time. The TESS box lived up to the hype; the stamps are amazing
as well as the coolest box I've ever seen!

It ended up being perfect weather to sit in a beautiful park stamping
and chatting. Lots of boxes were found before and after and even new
boxers were run into and exchanges gathered. Lots of fun. Cooties even
reared their sneaky little heads... I'm sure they'll be at Columbus
too! :o)

Thanks everyone who attended. I loved meeting all the new boxers and
seeing all my old friends. May more gather at the next Who are
the 'boxers in your neighborhood? gathering!

Watch for photos which will be coming soon.
