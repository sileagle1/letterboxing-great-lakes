From: Doglvrs (doglvr895@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-30 13:32:33 UTC
Hey all...after a little prodding, I've posted a picture of the pumpkin I carved last night.
My first try doing the skin peel technique. (I couldn't get a good picture of it lite in the
dark...but trust me, it looks totally sweet!)

I used a #2 Speedball, and a couple of cheapy gouges made for wood carving.

I transfered the image onto the pumpkin using tracing paper I sent through my inkjet printer,
except for the lettering. That I had to do free hand due to goofing up on the punching.

Anyway, it turned out pretty good for my first try. If you want to see it, it's in the "misc."
folder here on the group photo's page.

Mrs Doglvrs
