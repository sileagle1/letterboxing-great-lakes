From: brownrabbit_teamrabbit (teamrabbit@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-04-28 16:46:22 UTC
A pleasant day, yesterday (and a prompting from Dragon) sent me to
Hudson Mills Metropark, site of the 2005 gathering. I'm happy to
report that Mo' Mustangs (Dragon's crazy assemble-your-own-car
letterbox) was still all there and is now prepped for more visitors.
Also ready for a new season is "Tranquil Moment", the Doglvrs box
we "adopted".

Further travels in the area also yielded these results:
Dragon's "Go to Hell" - doing great (especially considering its age).
"Kensington Haiku" and "So Long Michigan '75-'05" both planted by
Delirium before she left the region are each thriving in their
respective locations. For all you bird freaks out there, there is a
nesting white swan just off the trail betreen these two boxes (at the
edge of the lake).

That's all the news from the trail today!
