As some of you know, I've planted a few urban boxes. They present
their own special challenges, for sure.
If you're at all interested in getting involved with planting/finding
boxes in non-rural settings, you might want to check out a Yahoo!
group just for those boxers.
Here's a link:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingurbanstyle

There have been some interesting threads on camouflage techniques,
containers, etc.

It seems like a friendly group.
