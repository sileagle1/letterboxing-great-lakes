From: Familyof6 (familyof6portland@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-01-18 22:08:38 UTC
The International Olympic Committee recently has been informed of some speculative irregularities in Olympic Training and are thoroughly investigating the teams of several countries. Each country in question is being asked to submit to extensive testing to negate the allegations of doping. When asked of these allegations, President Jacques Rogges responded with these words:

"We are saddened to hear again that the 2010 Olympics has received allegations of inappropriate performance enhancing medication and we are currently conducting testing to determine the honesty and integrity of all countries and teams representing them."

When questioned further, Mr. Rogges responded with "no comment", but sources have revealed that Australia, Sweden, the Bahamas, and possibly Switzerland, may have violated Olympic rules. Damning evidence can be found at the following;

Sweden: Allegations that Nordic Heroes, Grunhild and Ivor, have relied on "special" food from a beloved Swedish chef, seen here: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/message/21398
Now, our sources say that with this supposed broken English and snappy song, this chef has, in the past, has had questionable recipes. As for Grunhild and Ivor, only time will tell. perhaps they should rely on a more distinguished chef: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HwCZbeV96yc

Australia: Apparently, there are some special "AtomicBeans" involved. Australia, of course, have denied all allegations, yet, we question these "so called" beans and their "magical" powers. I guess down under means just that. You be the judge: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/message/21398

Which, leads us to the Bahamas. It has long been suspected that these Rastafarian's have indulged in "herbal brownies" and such, but apparently there's more. Upon investigation, we have found that their body weight appears to be extremely low, as proven in their self proclaimed dance video (seen here at: http://bahamachampions.homestead.com) Champions? we question you on that. What? with all that, ya mon, non-distinguishable language of theirs...who really understands them anyway?

Furthermore, there has been some speculation with Switzerland as they have been requested a place to purchase thermal lederhosen. Why? What are you hiding?

So, we question you. Only your urine tests will set you free. Thank goodness that other countries have stayed honest. God Save the Queen with the British!

So, prove yourselves! We, of course, have suspected doping all along! In the meanwhile, we shall keep you posted with updates.
