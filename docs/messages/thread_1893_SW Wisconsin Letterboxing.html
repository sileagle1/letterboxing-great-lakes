SW Wisconsin Letterboxing

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-10-14 14:29:13 UTC
The kids had a 4-day weekend this past weekend, so we made a trip
(mainly to go to the Dells) but also got a few letterboxes. We were
pleased that we found all the boxes we were looking for and they
were all in good shape. On Sunday afternoon we went to Madison and
went to the zoo, where we then went on to find "Lions" (placed by
Ili and Lil). We then went on to find another box by them: "Peas in
a Pod" as we walked around the State Capital and Campus Areas (this
was Find 100 for the kids and me!).

The next day we drove through Merrimac and found the "I Believe in
Ferries!" box placed by the Puddlejumpers.

Then we rode the car ferry and went on to Devil's Lake State Park.
We hiked for about 3 hours and found "Vertical Ascent" and "Snake
Story", both placed by Wisconsin Hiker. There were tons of people
on the trails that day, including a school group of about 100 kids.
It was a challenge getting to the boxes in between the various
groups, but we managed--patience isn't always one of our virtues.
Then trying to stamp in without looking like we were stamping in.
For Snake Story, we were perched on some rocks a little further down
the trail. The kids pretended like they were taking notes in their
journals and sketching plants. One lady walking by said we looked
very studious writing in our books, so I guess the plan worked. It
was a very foggy morning as we hiked and we feared that we would
never see any of the pretty views. Finally, about noon, as we were
on the final leg of our hike, the fog finally lifted and we had some
beautiful views of the lake and the bluffs!

Then it was on to the Waterpark at the Dells, so no more
letterboxing. I had a few more boxes that I wanted to get but
unfortunately my family isn't as obsessed with getting boxes as I
am. There's always another trip I guess.

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs

I recently went through Sw Wisc. on a two day boxing binge and found many great boxes by Leapin' Lizards, Puddlejumpers and The Dragon. If anyone hasn't been to the Dragons tunnel series yet I HIGHLY recommend them. My favorite boxes so far. Once you go you'll know why. I can only say this about them. Go alone and don't bring a flashlight!!!!! The creepiest and most fun I've had boxing to date. There are other great boxes in Sw Wisc. as well, I hit so many in two days I can't remember them all without looking at my logbook. Thank you to all the placers in SW Wisc. I thoroughly enjoyed myself.

Gooble Gobble (Burnsville, Minnesota)
