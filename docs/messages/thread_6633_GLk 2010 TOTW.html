Good morning!
We found a business on the outskirts of Kickapoo with a loading dock and backed the van down into it so we were mostly below ground level. Then we all crouched down in the back of the van covered under blankets and pillows...unfortunately we didn't have any chocolate cream pie, but I was probably too hysterical to eat at that point! Quincy (our dog), thought we had come up with a new game and rode out the storm with no worries. We wanted to have the Wizard of Oz series adventure, but not quite that literally! I'm glad to hear everyone weathered the storm. BTW, there are several videos posted on YouTube of the tornado...here's a link to one of them. Thanks for an awesome event!
