New Boxers In Lake County Illinois

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-08-28 07:09:45 UTC
I am pleased to report that there are several new letterboxers in Lake
County, Illinois who have recently found some of my boxes and contacted
me about their finds. They are very much interested in this hobby.
They have a lot of questions, so I don't mind filling them in on the
do's and don'ts. One claims to have accidentally found a letterbox in
the forest preserve, read the label and went home to learn more about
letterboxing. That's how he learned about the hobby. Also, he rehid
the box properly. Bravo! Another newbie found one of my boxes and we
met by accident. She was trying to be so secretive. Today, we met
again - I showed her my LTC binder and gave her a LTC and she gave me
her entire collection of 172 Beatles bubblegum trading cards from the
mid 1960s. WOW! That's what I call card swapping!!! Thanks Dreams
With Hobbits! It's nice to meet new letterboxers who remind me of the
excitement of that first find. They're hooked! ---Beetle
