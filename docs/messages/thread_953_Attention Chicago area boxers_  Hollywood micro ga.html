Hey, it's been a long time since we've heard from the Tacettes!! How are
you guys doing?? Good to hear from you.

Okay, so here's the scoop for anyone interested...(sorry it took me so long
to figure out, but I was waiting to hear about the time of another
appointment I have that day).

Saturday, January 8th, 2:00 pm at the Paw. The Paw and Cyberdog Cafs are
located at 8420 Brookfield Avenue in Brookfield, Illinois, across the street
from the Hollywood/Zoo Stop on the Burlington Northern Santa Fe line (the
one that runs Union Station to Aurora). 708-485-8448. I believe they are
only open until 4:00 that day.

If you get there early before I do, be sure to ask for the Red Bulldog
(letterbox). It is located in the Cyberdog, which is the smaller internet
cafe located in back, off the west side of the building. If the snow melts
by then (!!!), Hurray for Hollywood is also nearby.

I'm participating in ScoutDogs' Mutts Art Schwap, and I'll bring along my
5-piece (including "accessory" stamps) Ozzie and Earl stamps if anyone wants
to stamp them.
