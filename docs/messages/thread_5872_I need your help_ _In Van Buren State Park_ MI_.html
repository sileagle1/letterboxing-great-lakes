I need your help! (In Van Buren State Park, MI)

From: Jeni Fuerstenberg (jeners@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-15 19:21:18 UTC-05:00
Hello Friends!! I was recently out letterboxing at Van Buren State Park,
along lake Michigan in downstate MI... azobox has got a couple of great,
really fun stamps out there!

At any rate, i'm back in Milwaukee, WI, and I was getting ready to do more
letterboxing when i realized- I'M MISSING MY STAMP AND MY OLD FULL LOGBOOK!
*cries* I've looked everywhere for them and they are nowhere to be found!
That book means a lot to me... i just got back from travelling cross country
since January and i have stamps from nearly every state i went through! I
live more than 4 hours from VanBuren park, and I am literally out of money.
I have like $10 to my name, which means that driving out there to look for
it is not really an option for me right now. So I'm wondering if there is
any POSSIBLE way, out of the kindness of your fellow letter-boxing hearts
that you could make your way out there to see if i left it by azobox's
"Whose shoes?" box? I asked azobox directly, but unfortunately (s)he is not
in Michigan right now and can't help me!

Is there any way you could look for it for me? I know that's asking a lot,
but it would mean the world to me! :( I'd gladly send you like $20 for the
effort as soon as i get paid for starting work again... in about 2 weeks. i
know that's not much... but it's the best i can do

The logbook is purple and rather small, not much bigger than a deck of
cards, and the stamp is about half the size of those pink rubber make-ur-own
stamp sheets. I kept them together in a small black polyester bag with two
drawstrings. If i left it out there by the log under which whose shoes is
hid, it should be pretty easy to find. my friend and i sat on the log close
to the maple tree sapling when we stamped up.


Please let me know ASAP if you might be able to help?

Thanks so much,

Jeni (One love journeying)
