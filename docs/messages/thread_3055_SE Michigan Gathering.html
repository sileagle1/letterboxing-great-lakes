From: LindaFay (coachlindafay@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-09 23:39:33 UTC
Thank you so much to double dragon for putting on such a wonderful,
well thought out letterboxing adventure. So many boxes - So little
time! And in spite of the rain, we all finished. Although my team
finished first we double faulted and couldn't take first prize.
Seems we suffered a slight direction impairment, but did find the
hidden treasure and the flag! Sorry, TrailTroll - but maybe that was
part of the directions that the rain washed away.

We also had the fun of meeting Miles and His Tiki Party - hmmmm this
might be an idea for a postal series - interesting behavior at a
park. The stamps were beautiful and some people (rabbits) have just
awesome carving skills - I'm just glad letterboxing includes other
skills, too like walking and talking at the same time - that I can do.

I enjoyed meeting new people and familiar friends and soooo glad I
could get ride of that cootie that had been pestering me since my RI
daughter dropped it in my bag.

Thanks again DD for a great day. Oh, yes - even though I was a Girl
Scout (always prepared) I didn't read the weather report and it did
rain a lot - so I was the one in the stylish trash bag - next time
I'll remember the day glo duct tape and then I'll truly be styling.

Have a great boxing fall!
LindaFay AKA MaumeeMuse
