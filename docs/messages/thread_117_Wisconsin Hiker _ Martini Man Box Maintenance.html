Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man Box Maintenance

From: wi_letterboxer (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2004-04-11 19:00:36 UTC
Today we decided to check on the boxes we've placed to see how they
were doing and take care of any necessary maintenance. Since we've
only placed 5 so far, and they're fairly near each other, it wasn't a
huge undertaking.

Unfortunately we discovered that one is GONE! Of course this was a
disappointment, but it is one of the risks of the "game". When we
initially placed "Brookfield Junction" we were worried that it might
not survive spring flooding. However the water levels are fairly low
this year so we weren't concerned about it as we traipsed out to
check it. But when we got to the trestle all that we found was the
outer ziploc bag (secured in place). We searched around the area,
but saw no sign of box or contents. We'll check again in a few weeks
to see if by some REMOTE chance someone returns it. I've added a
note to the clues in the database accordingly. We'll delete the box
if it doesn't reappear. (There's no other viable spot to hide a box
out there, and since the first was taken we don't want to risk
another.) FOr those of you who did find it, you're now in possession
of a "limited edition" stamp!

The other four boxes were just fine:
- Wirth Park
- Foxbrook Park
- Lucky Leprechaun
- Capone's Cargo

We both agreed it was a shame we couldn't check on our adopted box
today - we're sure it was MUCH nicer on the beach in Hawaii than
braving the nippy winds here in Wisconsin!
