From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-14 22:48:19 UTC
Acorn sent me clues to "Run Away!" and "The Holy Hand Grenade of
Antioch" and I've already posted them up on the site. Only 13 more
boxes to plant!

Also, I discovered that I had not replaced the Boyhood Adventures
boxes in April (they were buried in my letterboxing stuff). So I
got out today and replanted them and modified the clues
accordingly. Hopefully they won't fall prey to delinquents
anymore! The BA box #2 has a new hand-carved stamp if anyone that
found it previously wants to go get a new stamp image!

As a side note, I think I made my first "fatal" carving mistake
today :-(. After spending umpteen hours on one carving, I
mistakenly ripped off a couple of fine parts on it while cleaning it
up. Unfortunately, it looks like I get to spend another umteen
hours recarving it as I don't think it can be saved. As those who
recently traveled to the Buffy box said - "Grrrr Arrrrgh!"
