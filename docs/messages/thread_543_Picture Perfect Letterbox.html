From: Agent Ink (agentink_and_zfan@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-14 13:40:24 UTC-07:00
Hello great lakes boxers.

In response to the question in this forum about the
Picture Perfect Letterbox:

1. The box was checked today. It is intact and
in-place.
2. The box is hid in a very wooded area and is
difficult to bushwack to.
3. The final paces count is based on a man with
around a 2.5 to 3 foot stride. If you think you've
gone far enough, try just a little bit farther in case
your paces aren't as big.
4. We checked all of our boxes in Southeast Wisconsin
today. All are intact and in-place.

Happy Boxing,

Agent Ink and Z-fan
