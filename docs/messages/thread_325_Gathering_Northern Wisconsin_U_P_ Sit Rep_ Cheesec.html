Crescent Roll Cheese Cake
2 pkg crescent rolls

2 pkg 8oz cream cheese

1 cup sugar

1 tsp vanilla

1 egg (separated)

1 tsp cinnamon

Preheat oven to 350. Spread 1 pkg of crescent rolls in a 9x13 pan.
Mix cream cheese, egg yolk, 1 cup sugar, and vanilla. Spread over
crescent rolls. Top with pkg #2 crescent rolls. Brush top with egg
whites. Mix cup sugar with cinnamon and sprinkle over top. Bake
at 350 for 30 minutes.

Enjoy!
