For many of us, one of the wonderful aspects of letterboxing is the
science that it presents...Personally, one of our favorite is
spotting and photographing fungi. Those at the Haunted Harvest
Gathering this fall will remember the incredible Giant Puff Balls
near some of the boxes! Biggest ones we've ever seen!...I think
some got murdered...too bad... they make for great eating!

Thinking about recent posts asking about talk lists that others
visit, we thought it would be fun to hear about websites that others
use to enhance their letterboxing forays!

A fun, yet sometimes technical, website that we thought we would
pass on is Tom Volk's Fungi page...

http://tomvolkfungi.net/

He'll prove that there is more to Wisconsin than cheese!..Our bet is
that his favorite is blue!
