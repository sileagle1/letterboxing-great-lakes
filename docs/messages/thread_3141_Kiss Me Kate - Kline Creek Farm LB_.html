Hi Kate! Now that the Ren Faire is closed, I don't see you as often. The solution to finding that box is easy....just bushwack the entire area along the back of service road! Stick your hand in every dark, musty, fungus invested crack and crevice (no offense Fungus Woman), and look high and low. You'll find it! Or die trying....LOL!

Sorry that I don't have an old clue sheet for a Kline Creek Farm Letterbox. I started with all the boxes in Lake County and I've only recently branched out to other Chicagoland area counties.

I know you will find that box. It takes some real investigating. Start calling the farm and find out who found the box. You'll track it down.

Beetle

Does anyone remember a box placed at Kline Creek Farm in DuPage
County, Illinois? I think I saw clues for it when we starting boxing
a couple of years ago, but I can't find any information for it.

Strangely enough, the staff at Kline Creek Farm remembers that one of
their volunteers brought "something like that" back to the farmhouse
to show everyone and then put it back. But the woman we spoke to
didn't go with them, so she only knows it's somewhere near the back of
the service road.

I'm hoping to get more information from here or from the farm to go
looking for it. I'm willing to adopt it if I find it and no one else
wants to claim it.

-- KissMeKate
