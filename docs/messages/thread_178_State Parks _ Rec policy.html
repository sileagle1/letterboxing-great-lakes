State Parks & Rec policy

From: John J Foerch (jjfoerch@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-04-28 21:30:40 UTC
Sandrich has posted the current policy recommendation in the Migo
forums. (
http://www.mi-geocaching.org/modules.php?name=Forums&file=viewtopic&t=1575
) You will need an account on mi-geocaching.org to read and post to
the thread, and please do.

Some things that I would like to see in this policy are:
* Recognition of letterboxing.
* License the _person_ not the _container_.
* License should allow five boxes, rather than three.
* License should apply to all Parks & Rec land, rather than a single
park. Each park manager would get a copy of the license.
* A box should be able to stay in place for one year, conditional on
the formation of use trails.
* Eliminate the text about virtual caches. They are not geocaches and
the policy does not apply to them.

-John
