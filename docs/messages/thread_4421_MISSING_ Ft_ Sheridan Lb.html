MISSING: Ft. Sheridan Lb

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-11 01:33:06 UTC-06:00
Today, I followed up on a tip from a letterboxer that my Ft. Sheridan Lb might be missing. I am sad to say, it IS gone! This area along the beach has been remote and unused for decades. Now that the Lake Country Forest Preserve has purchased many of the acres in this area, the beach has become populated with 'partiers'....which is obvious by the large amount of debris, alcoholic beverage containers, cook out leftovers, and piles of burned wood. What a mess that beach has become in one short year!

But the Ft. Sheridan area is truly beautiful with many hiking trails and prairie style plant life. I chose this location for it's historical beauty too. Today, I was looking for a new, safe place to hide a new box, but it started to rain, again, this afternoon. But I'd like to replace a box in this area.

Beetle
