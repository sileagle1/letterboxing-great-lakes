From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-12-01 06:37:42 UTC-05:00
I decided to let the TOTW lie for a bit. Participation had dwindled to just
a few folks each week and list chatter is carrying itself well with topics
presented for discussion by various members without presenting specific TOTW
posts.

Perhaps once we get a bit past the first of the year and life calms down a
bit, I will revive the TOTW. We continue to grow and some of the previously
discussed topics would be helpful discussion for new members and good
refreshers for list veterans alike as winter wanes and thoughts once again
turn to letterboxing.

Of course, anyone can post a topic for discussion at any time so if there is
something that you feel would make a good TOTW and you'd like some input, by
all means, pose the question to the list.

SpringChick
(who hasn't disappeared, just has gotten really, really busy with some
projects at home and holiday preparations, etc.)
