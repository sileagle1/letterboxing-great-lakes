GLG 2008

From: Heather (the_red_cat529@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-02-02 19:27:44 UTC
Looks like the winning theme is Celebrate Minnesota/Loon-y
Letterboxing. I like the name that someone (I forget who) suggested:
Land of 10,000 Letterboxes. Now, I know there aren't really 10,000
letterboxes in Minnesota (yikes-can you imagine!), but I think it's a
catchy title, so I'm going to go with it. I'll post the event on AQ
for those who want to sign up over there. If you are bringing kids,
please let me know how many and what their ages are.

Boxes can be on anything Minnesota, which is a lot! Please let me know
if you plan on carving a stamp and what you'd like to carve. I know
there is already a lutefisk stamp in the works and a Peanuts stamp,
but there's so much more! Prince is a MN native, Post-Its were
invented by 3M (Minnesota Manufacturing & Mining), "The State of
Hockey", etc, etc. If you get stuck and really want to send a box,
just let me know and we can brainstorm. I'm going to ask that if you
live in the Twin Cities area that you plant your own boxes, but if
you live outside of the area, you can contact me
(the_red_cat529@yahoo.com) for an address to send them to. The
deadline for getting them to me is May 1st.

If you live in the area and want to help, please let me know! I've
had several people offer already (thank you!) and I'll get in touch
about planning and stuff like that.

Look for more information soon!

-The Red Cat
