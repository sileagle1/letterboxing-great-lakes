Bristol Renaissance Faire

From: Jason (lewwdawg_840@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-10 22:53:15 UTC
We just returned from an eventful day of letterboxing at the Bristol
Rennaisance Faire. It was a great day to be out there, even though it
was pretty hot. We successfully found the 10 letterboxes (the 9 Sir
Timothy's Journey boxes & the Bristol Ren Faire box), but we weren't so
lucky with the letterpeople. We found 3 or 4 of the letterpeople, but
only one of them had their stamp on them (Laughing Coyote). The others
explained that they did not have their stamp yet or something to that
effect. But its only opening weekend so we kind of expected that. We
will definitely return later in the season to pick up those
letterpeople boxes.

We want to let everyone know that the stamps we did find were
beautifully carved and definitely worth the trip (and the cost to enter
the faire). It was a lot of fun reading through Sir Timothy's Journey
and following it around the Faire. We also really enjoyed the joust and
the whole audience participation factor. The staff there is very
friendly and they really make an effort to get everyone involved in the
atmosphere.

So all in all, we had a great experience and look forward to making
another trip up there soon :)

Julie & Jason (Wormy Wanderers)
