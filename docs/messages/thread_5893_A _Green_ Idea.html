I would certainly be interested in old stamps.

At the end of last school year I did a questing/letterboxing event for the 1st & 2nd graders which had them following clues to find stamps. This year we did a passport program in the library and I created passports for the kids and then carved stamps relating to each country we learned about.

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-27 10:27:46 UTC-05:00
Ok then, there seems to be a few folks on board with our idea so we will
be bringing a box of our old stamps to recycle, and I'd encourage you to
take as many as you want. They will either be out in a box marked
'Recycled Stamps', or you can come ask us and we'll direct you to them.
We ask, however, that you don't just sit and stamp them! If you want
them, please just take them. You can give us credit if you use them
later, but please feel no pressure to do so. We will just be happy that
they have found a second home!

Just a heads up...among the choices we have a few fun Fraggle stamps,
X-Files and Buffy stamps, a beautiful Brad Paisley stamp, and a small
variety of baked goods!....come early for the most options! ;-)

Atom 118
