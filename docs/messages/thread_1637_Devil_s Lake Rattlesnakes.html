We'd agree w/Dragon - didn't see any poison ivy tonight at the Ferry box (it
was 9:45 pm but we DID have a flashlight). This was our niece & nephew's
first experience w/"night boxing" and Jake the Snake did a great job of
finding the path. Super Frog was a little anxious in the grass in the dark
and decided to retreat to the asphalt. The kids also enjoyed the ferry and
Jake had a significant event while stamping in which we failed to mention in
the logbook - he lost one of his teeth! He had told us it was loose, and
after stamping in, pulled it from his mouth. Now he's hoping for a visit
from a different type of fairy....

Also agree w/Dragon on Phantom Lake poison ivy. We were there last week w/2
friends and one was paranoid about coming down with a rash after she spotted
it on the trail. Luckily none of us did.

Wisconsin Hiker
