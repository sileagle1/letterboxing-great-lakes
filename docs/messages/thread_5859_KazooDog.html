I regret to inform the Great Lakes group that KazooDog has passed over the rainbow bridge peacefully at home on Friday.

The last time we spoke she had agreed to let me take over her boxes. Regretfully I didn't act upon it while she was still alive.
I have sent the webmasters a request to adopt the boxes. I will keep you informed of the outcome.

I know some of you have had some issues with her boxes and I hope to correct them for future finders.

She was a wonderful woman and she will be missed by many.
