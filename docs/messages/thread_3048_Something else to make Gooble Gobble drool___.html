I was thinking I *wanted* to go to the gathering, but shouldn't be making
any plans since who even knows where I'll be that time next year, but now
I'm thinking I absolutely have to go!!! The only time I was at Cedar
Point, my grandmother wouldn't let me ride the rollercoasters, all
because my cousin was afraid. I called him a scaredy cat and all the
worst epitaphs my 9 year old brain could come up with, but nothing would
convince him to ride and Grandma wasn't going to let her granddaughter on
something that frightened her grandson.

Hmm, apparently I still have some latent anger over this experience and
need to work it out on that 95 degree hill.

But the big question is, who likes to ride in the front and who rides in
the back? I like the whippy action of the last car myself, and the
anticipation built by hearing the front half scream.

I'm so there.
Katy
