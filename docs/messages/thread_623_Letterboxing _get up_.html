OMGosh! I am crying tears of laughter! I nearly had a Dilly Bar moment
laughing so hard at the "get up" notes. That was great! I needed that good
laugh. We haven't taken that much with us when we have gone out on a hunt, but the

when I might need to take that stuff with me.

Lipstick Chick and I (Dumpster Diva) would be the ones giggling all along the
trail, sharing funny stories, catching up on what we hadn't already discussed
on the phone 6 times a day and remarking about how far we had come since our
first letterbox hunt! in the rain, mud, good sandals on, no umbrella, huge
tote bags plus our purses! (what were we thinking! who takes a 10# purse with
them - duh!) no water, of course all we had to do was drink from the sky if we
needed to, no compass, no tissues incase we had to potty or "loop", no nothing
but this huge tote with the worlds largest, heaviest journal to use as our
logbook..... duh! no power bars for nourishment, no plastic sleeve for our clues
which got very soggy and difficult to read, and then to finish us off....we
couldn't find the box! Ugh! But it's one of our best memories!~

With LC living in MI and me in IL and trying not to letterbox except together
we seem to live vicariously thru all of you and chat everyday about the
thread and what's going on.... thanks for that great laugh - and I'm laughing with
you, not at you....
