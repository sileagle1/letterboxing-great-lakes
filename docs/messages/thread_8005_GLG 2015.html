From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2014-10-21 22:22:46 UTC-05:00

Atomic Beans are formally volunteering to host the 2015 Great Lakes Gathering in Illinois.


To celebrate the 13th Annual Great Lakes Gathering, we propose having the event on June 13th near Oregon IL in Castle Rock State Park (with boxes also hidden in Lowden and Lowden-Miller State Parks).  Oregon is nearly halfway between Rockford and Peoria and ~100 miles west of Chicago, which should make it relatively accessible to many in the region. Lowden State Park has tent and RV camping (and a few more rustic, walk-up sites) and Oregon is centrally located to Rockford, Rochelle, and Dixon (~20-30mins in the different directions) for those who hotel camp.


The dual themes for this 13th event are ‘rites of passage’ and ‘superstitions,’ and we are still working on the event title.

We'd like to get some of the initial plans ironed out over the next few weeks and reserve the site, so if anyone else is conceiving another plan, please voice it.

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2015-01-17 21:47:22 UTC-06:00
Although it is cold outside, we know many of you are thinking of warmer times.  We are too.  This is the first, of perhaps several emails leading up to 2015 Great lakes gathering on June 13.  We want YOU to carve a stamp for the event. This year's dual themes are "rites of passage" and "superstitions," both of which are related to this being the GLG's thirteenth event.    
      
While we want a stamp from you, we only want 1 or 2 so we can get a nice variety of stamps by not be overwhelmed.  (If the initial response is low, we may accept more from those of you who love to carve.)     If you are newer to events, feel free to interpret the theme however you want.  In fact, creativity and originality usually make for wonderful stamps!   Even if you are still new to carving, don't worry, we'd still love a stamp.

If you want to "reserve" your idea, we have started a database section on the GLK Yahoo board.  If you don't know the exact idea, we still encourage you to sign up as "TBD" so we can estimate numbers. If you don't have access to the board, and don't want to join, we can add you box if you email us the info.

In accordance with the park's director, these boxes will not be permanent.   However, if you would like to have your box replanted someplace else in Illinois after the gathering, you can note that, and we will work with the state's boxers to do that. (IL boxers, let us know if you are interested.)

We have posted a 'submission' form in the yahoo group in the files section in the gatherings folder under 2015 (https://groups.yahoo.com/neo/groups/LBGLK/files/Gatherings/2015%20Great%20Lakes%20Gathering/).  It is so we can keep track of stamps, but also we'd also like to plant some of your boxes as mystery clues and bonus boxe at the event. If planted this way, your box might not be posted, directly, in the clue packet.  If you are ok with that, please indicate that on the submission form posted in the GLG files.  We have included our address at the top of this form.

We will post all the boxes to both LbNA and AQ just prior to the event.  Please do not post them directly if you are sending them to us.

Finally, and perhaps most importantly, we ask that you send us your box by May 1 (earlier is better!).

We will post this message on AQ and FB, but we intend to post most of our communications on the Yahoo group.  More information concerning suggested accommodations, maps, and other shenanigans will follow over the next while.

Atomic Beans
