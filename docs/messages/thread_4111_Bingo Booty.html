Well, Azobox said he won't be hosting the Side Wagers game this year.
However since I'd hate to see this "tradition" fall by the wayside, I've
decided to step in to host it instead. I've taken the liberty of copying
his rules from last year and setting up the game for this year. Here's the
scoop:

Side Wagers 2009



Side Wager: A wager on a particular team, or side, to win a contest



If you'd again like to add some excitement to the Letterboxing Bingo game,
enter the Side Wagers Game. Email the date you think the game will end to
kotlarek@wi.rr.com

When will SpringChick pull the item that finally and completely fills in
some player's card? Again, this is the Big Game here, not the five interim
games. The date will be determined by the claiming of a successful card by a
BINGO player (and its certification by SpringChick).



Your guess must be submitted before January 1, 2009.

Mail to kotlarek@wi.rr.com



In the subject line, put "Sidewager".



The winner's prize goes to the person who is closest *before* the day the
Bingo Game ends or actually hits the date on the dot. If you choose February
15th, for example, and someone else chooses the 20th, and the game ends on
the 19th, you win. In other words, you'll hold all of the days until the
next guesser hits the calendar. (But you don't know who has chosen which
days or how far apart they've fallen -- and neither does SpringChick!).



If two or more players choose the same date the first guesser is given the
date and another available date will be assigned to anyone else who chooses
the date already taken. This will be done by moving out in the calendar
until a date between your choice and the next one chosen is available. So,
if you choose Feb 20 and it's already taken, and the next date chosen is the
24th, you'll be assigned the 22nd. If there is another player already on
that date and it is not available, you'll move to the next available date.



A prize will be awarded to the lucky winner.

You do not need to be a player in the big game to participate.



Send questions to Wisconsin Hiker.



====================



This info is also posted in the FILES section of the message board.



Wisconsin Hiker
