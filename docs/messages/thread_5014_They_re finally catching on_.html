Re: They're finally catching on!

From: momofask97 (momofone@iwon.com) | Date: 2008-05-20 01:51:05 UTC
I wanted to thank you for posting this. I've only used store bought
stamps in our boxes because I didn't know where to get supplies to
make stamps, and thought myself too incompetent to carve my own.
When I saw your post, I decided to go to Hobby Lobby as soon as
possible to get a kit to give it a try.

We live in the UP of Michigan & weren't able to go to Hobby Lobby
until we took a quick trip to Green Bay last week. With the very
easy instructions, I've already carved 3 stamps! It's so much
easier than I thought! I'll be updating my boxes with hand-carved
stamps very soon.

Thanks again!
Keweenaw Kimballs
