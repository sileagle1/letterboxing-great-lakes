Just a friendly reminder..if I called your wild card this weekend,  you need to claim it by emailing me your pick of how you'll use it within 3 days from when I called it.

This is a new option for 2019. You've not needed to do this for past years, but you do this year.

I will reply to your note to verify I got it. If you don't get a reply from me, I didn't get it, and it won't count.
