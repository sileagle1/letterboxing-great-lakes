From: allaboutrain (allaboutrain@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-21 17:05:10 UTC
Hello everyone,

I'm new to the group. Thought I'd come out and introduce myself.

I heard about the Dartmoor letterboxes a long, long time ago and
longed for the idea to come to the U.S. I finally got around to
checking on letterboxing again in 2001. That was way back when there
was only one lonely letterbox in the northern Illinois area.

My first adventure in letterboxing was a mixed bag. A few hits
peppered with a lot of frustrating misses made my interest fade a bit
(I started out with searching for most of the old missing Door County
letterboxes in 2001 and a bunch of now missing Mount Desert Island,
ME letterboxes in 2002).

Luckily, I found myself a wonderful trail partner who is very
intrigued with the concept of letterboxing. Rebrab is one of those
wonderfully cheerful, patient people that you just love having on the
trail with you.

So, just this last weekend we went down to Starved Rock and found
eleven stamps including our very first hitchhiker!

We found five of the six Starved Rock Lodge boxes. Posts and Posts
is definitely missing. We found the correct site, searched around
and came up with nothing. Someone at the lodge confirmed the missing
status of the box.

Illinois Canyon is alive and well.

I told Rebrab to finally stop poking around for Starved Rock Buffet.
We can't really confirm if that box is still there or not. I was
more concerned about not brushing up against all the poison ivy in
the area.

Three of the letterboxes we found were Funguswoman's. Those
definitely brought us to parks that we might not have thought to
visit but were well worth the trip!

Our last box was A Step Above the Rest. We were ALMOST first. We
tried for it on Friday, but it was getting dark and I asked Rebrab to
stop and try for it again the next day. If only ;).

Wellhow was that for a long-winded intro? :)

I can't wait for our next letterboxing outing. You'll be seeing more
of our stamps out there soon.

~Rain (who letterboxes with Rebrab)
