SNAKES!

Well, my son and I just watched an entire special on them. I'm not afraid
of them because I grew up with them in CA, but it's always good to know your
stuff. Basically, there is No way to know which ones are poisonous. They can
mimic each other. There are several that even have a rattler (oh that's
what we all wanted to hear, right)?
GOOD NEWS!!!!
They cannot chase you:) They are not very fast. Good idea to jump over it,
Timbertoes! Unless it sees you first and you startle it, you can just run
and it can't keep up, and just gives up. Also if you talk with your LB friend
as you walk you should frighten the ones you are coming up upon and they
should slither away...should...

Happy Snake news from Lipstick Chick (Team Jo Jo)

Actually it was a tidbit that stuck in my head from watching king of the
jungle on animal planet. One of the girls on the show misrepresented a
snake by saying it was not venomous because it's head was shaped like a
triangle.. they really beat her up for that mistake! So it seems to have
stuck in my head. Blue Racers bite but have no venom.. but they are fast
and are found in our area. Most other snakes will avoid you at all costs,
unless you step on them.. High ankle boots are a good idea to have on
hiking in these kinds of areas. My brother almost sat on a baby rattler
curled up on a tree stump sunning itself.. he was going to sit down when he
noticed it.. wouldn't that have been awful! We were camping in the middle
of lower Michigan at the time..
