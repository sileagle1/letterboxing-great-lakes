For those going to the Columbus Cluez-In by way of
Cleveland so that they can spend a day at the Rock
Hall, you may want to visit this website on Wednesday,
May 9th, around 10:30 am to buy tickets:

http://wkyc3.mediawebconnect.com/

A lot of the Cleveland-area radio and TV stations have
weekly "half-price deals," usually where you can get a
$50 gift certificate to someplace for only $25, plus
shipping (usually $3 to $5). They have limited
supplies, though, so it's a good idea to visit the
website when the sale opens to ensure you're able to
purchase one of the certificates you're interested in.


This week, our TV station Channel 3's offer is 2
tickets to the Rock Hall for $20 (plus shipping) - the
normal price is $20 per ticket. Again, if you're
interested, visit the link above on May 9th around
10:30 am. Deedle & I have bought several certificates
this way before and never had any problems. The
certificates usually arrive in 1 to 2 weeks of us
ordering them, so you should get your order in time
for the Gathering.

Just trying to help our fellow 'boxers and music
lovers save a little money!

Doodle Bug.
