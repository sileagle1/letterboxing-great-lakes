I will be thinking about all the fun I'm missing tomorrow, but I just could not get the stars
to align this year.

Which brings me to the earlier question about GLG 2009. It's time for INDIANA to host
one. I've been looking and looking for a spot that will accommodate, more or less, 150
folks. And if I'm going to be super involved in planning, I would need to make the
location convenient for me to visit several times before the big day. Pokagon State Park
really has the best facilities and location for Great Lakes Folks.

The shelters could not be reserved until today. There are 2 shelters that accommodate
150 and one of them was already booked when I went online. I was lucky and made the
reservation for the other large shelter. If this location is not picked for the event by the
Great Lakes group, I will only be out $30.

I will need some help organizing this event! I would like to make a website (learn
something new this year beyond my own blog) and get others to handle various aspects.
My folks have a summer home nearby the park where I could host a planning party any
time after Labor Day.

Hopefully I have not stepped on any toes by getting an early start for May 30, 2009. I was
afraid to wait another day to make a reservation. If anyone else has planned and paid for
another location, I will happily step aside.

I would like to keep any more details under wraps until a small committee is formed. If
you think you want to help, please contact me off-list.

I need to log off now. The tornado warning sirens just began.

Have a great day in Minnesota!
