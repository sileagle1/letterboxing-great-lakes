From: Gooble Gobble (gooble_gobble_3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-21 01:24:21 UTC-07:00
It was AWESOME!!! We are all doing fine here in MN.
The gathering was a huge success. The Red Cat put
together a great time for everyone. There were enough
boxes, event stamps and exchanges to keep us all busy.
I finished the day with 13 boxes, 7 event stamps, 11
exchanges, 4 personal travelers, 4 hitchhikers, and
one cootie. It was so nice to have a gathering where
we could all get together and box the day away. The
last time MN. had a gathering was last November. Way
too long. In fact, we were all just talking earlier
about how jealous we were of the many many many many
many gatherings our friends who live a few hours to
our east have (just one more rivalry between our
states, but you guys win this one). We will definetely
have more gatherings in the future. It would be nice
to have more of our fellow MN boxers come out to
gatherings. Of the 45 people I contacted only 9 signed
up for the gathering. We still had a blast though, I'm
not sure what the final count was for attendance. We
even had a small surprise contingent from WI. Optic,
out of Chippewwa Falls and Kayakin' Monkey, who hails
from Madison made the drive SPECIFICALLY for the
gathering. Thank you to them for driving over to enjoy
the beautiful fall weather with us.

Speaking of which, Red Cat and I were talking (like we
did last year around this time) it would be great to
have the 2008 GLK gathering here in MN. We already
have a fantastic idea of where to have it. I would
like to say this is a request to have it here. If my
memory serves me right, I do believe MN and IN have
not hosted gatherings yet. We would love to see
everyone here in '08. We'll see what happens.

As far as pictures go, I did not take any. But I'm
sure they will surface before long.


Repeat after me: MN '08 MN '08 MN'08 MN'08 MN'08

Have a great day everyone, I missed seeing everyone at
the GLK gathering this year.


Gooble Gobble
