"Horsin' Around" event

From: Christine Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2010-10-18 12:39:33 UTC-05:00
Well I'm finally starting to recover from Saturday's event - although I still do have to get back out on the forest preserve trails and pull 40+ stamps. I intend to start doing that tomorrow afternoon. To all who sent stamps for the event, they will be in the mail to you hopefully within a week or less.

Where to start? Well first of all, thanks to everyone who came! It was great seeing so many old acquaintances plus lots of new faces. As usual I missed getting some exchanges. Hopefully at the next event ("Dead of Winter II"?).

Secondly, a TREMENDOUS thank you to the following boxers (in no particular order) who provided so many wonderful stamps for the event:
Beetle
Funguswoman
Mars888 (traveling event stamp)
Maude
Nitrocat
Prairie Rose
Pugnasties
Archaeofrog (Black Beauty postal)
samwashere
Ferrit-It-Out
SunnySideUp
SweetEllGee
Sarcasmo
Wisconsin Hiker
Martini Man
PackerBackers
Pink Unicorn Girl
Hartx6
Funrunner

I hope I haven't missed anyone, if I have I apologize.

And an EXTRA BIG thank you to Nitrocat who:
1. Carved 19 stamps
2. Provided over a dozen containers
3. Supplied about 15 logbooks, all hand-made
4. Drove up to deliver all this personally, and
5. Put on a mini-workshop at the event on pouch-making

Another big thank-you for all the wonderful dishes brought to the pot luck. Russ and I are still talking about the soups, desserts, pasta dishes, etc. (The bees loved it all too.) So much food, I wasn't able to sample more than half of it. And the bathroom scale is now my bitter enemy.

Thanks to all who donated so generously to help cover expenses. And finally, thanks to Pastor's Wife and Steering Lives. PW is a great administrator, did all the printing, remembers all the important stuff that I forget, and is a an all-around great event-planning partner :)

And - I only found two cooties . . . so far.

Trail Rider & Nam Vet
