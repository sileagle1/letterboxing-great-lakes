Beware the Ides of March, you say -
Although it's March 15
I got a straight line Bingo
With Smithsonian Magazine!

I win no prize, too late I know,
Yet still my heart it flew
For also I see on my card -
SpringChick's Stinky Lepew.

And so, for now, as words are called,
I remain your trusty scribe,
Happy that the bingo chief
Did not demand a bribe!

Lady TrailTroll
Court Poet and Royal Sucker Upper
