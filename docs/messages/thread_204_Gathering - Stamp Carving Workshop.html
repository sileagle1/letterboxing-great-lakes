Gathering - Stamp Carving Workshop

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-05-15 10:59:04 UTC
Hi Everyone, I wanted to pass along some info on the Stamp Carving
Workshop at the GL Gathering next month and get a rough idea of how
many folks are planning to sit in on the workshop.

There will be a $1 charge per person to cover materials. You will
need to bring with you your own carving instrument(s). I have a few
extra Exacto knives in case somebody forgets or shows up last
minute, etc. but not enough for the whole group and I do not have
extra Speedball carving tools. If you like to carve with magnifiers
or a mat, please feel free to bring that with you also. I would
assume everyone will have with them an inkpad in their letterboxing
bag, but just in case I will mention that you will want to have an
inkpad with you to do test stamps of your image. I will supply the
carving material, images, transfer medium and a multi-page printed
tutorial that you will be able to take home with you. If you have a
specific image you would like to work on, feel free to bring that
with you -- the size of the carving blocks is 2" x 2". The image
should be printed with a toner-based ink so that you will be able to
transfer it.

Thanks everyone for posting carving tips -- I will be including
these in the handout. I would also like to include a page or two of
carved images and would prefer they are not all my carvings. If you
have a carving (or a couple) that you would be willing to let me
include, please send me a clear scanned image via private e-mail.
The handout will be B/W so keep that in mind when choosing an image.

If you are planning to attend the workshop, please send me an e-mail
offlist. This is not an official count and you can opt in or out
later, but I do need to know an approximate count so I can have
enough supplies with me.

Thanks!

SpringChick
