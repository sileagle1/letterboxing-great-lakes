being mindful makes for cool adventures!

From: estella_naparella (estella_naparella@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-22 00:17:51 UTC
Yes, I'm rather a newbie on the LB front, but am having the greatest
adventures!

Today (and yesterday), Skootching Dog and I went seeking out places to
plant letterboxes. I remember a tutorial about planting letterboxes,
and there was a note in there about not planting in the fall where it
might be under 4' of water in the spring. WELL, as MN is in flood stage
all over the place, we've been out exploring all kinds of parks, trails
and places to make sure that as bad as it gets in the Mississippi
Valley, we can still plant boxes!

This has also helped with walking in a fitness challenge for my
workplace, too! (55 miles in a week to honor the Civil Rights Marchers
who walked from Selma to Montgomery in 1965) AND we've found some
really great places with no bum scat!

This awesome hobby is getting us outside and active! so cool!

E.N.
