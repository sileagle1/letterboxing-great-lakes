For those of you in the Milwaukee area (or those that might want to travel here), there are two free outdoor movies that might be a fun place to get together:

Friday SEP 10 - Darkside of Oz (in honor of the big series planted in IL this year by Hartx6, Pitties & Shorty) But not your traditional soundtrack...

Friday SEP 24 - The Rocky Horror Picture Show (this was one of Jigsaw's favorites, but he's not in the area anymore...)

Movies are free, food & beverages are available for purchase. More details on full schedule, menu, bazaar, etc can be found here:

http://www.pointfishfryandaflick.com/

Let us know if you'd be interested in meeting us there.
