Howdy!


We've been traveling, but are now back home and I have processed all the outstanding Bingo emails & regular mail.  So far we only have 29 players signed up, but I hope there will be many more entries flying in soon!  Typically we have had at least 50 players, with a high of 73 players in 2010!


I have updated the database - please check it and let me know if I missed anything.  It seems many of you might be hoping to find a few more boxes or do a few more exchanges before you send your cards in.  


NOTE:  If you will be mailing a package with booty, you need to send it early enough that I will receive it by DEC 30.  We will be traveling again and packages will NOT be able to receive them after that date. However if you send a gift card as booty in a regular envelope, that can be received on any date.
