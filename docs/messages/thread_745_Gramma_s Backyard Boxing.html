Re: Gramma's Backyard Boxing

From: dasprite222 (dasprite222@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-09 16:05:08 UTC

I don't have little ones of my own yet, so I've been letting my
elementary students box instead...they really love it! I go to 4
different schools and I hide my personal traveler each place I go.
They actually come in during recess to find them, even after school!

This week, I'm placing a real box in their honor called "Keeping Up
The PACE" (PACE is our school district's gifted program). They wanted
to make their own boxing group (even made their own symbol, a racing
letter "p" with a little brain in the middle), so when they find next
week's traveler location, they'll get the clues to this new box.
Inside, I've left a bunch of the stamps I created with their symbol
on them, so they'll finally be official.

Planning for kids make just about EVERY hobby just that much more
fun! ;-)
