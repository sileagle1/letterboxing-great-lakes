Woo-hoo! I'm thrilled at the success of the series and that so many
of you were able to enjoy it this weekend!

It was a real treat to run into Spring Chick and "Butterfly" doing
the series. Now I truly have met my goal of catching a letterboxer
in the wild and have the stamp images to prove it! When Deb said
they had seen azobox, Grandvilla and Mobiusklien I was a little bit
bummed that I didn't have time to find them and exchange with them
too, but we were on the way to get Bindlestiff ready for launching
next week.

(Background: When Enders Angel and I met Grandvilla and Mobiusklien
on the trail, I didn't have my logbook or stamp with me (but she
did) so she won't let me count them as meeting my goal, saying, "No
stamp means no proof.")

Thanks again for all the nice notes about our series.

Enjoy!
