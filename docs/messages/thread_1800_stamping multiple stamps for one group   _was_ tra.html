Something to consider when you have multiple stamps - sometimes you will
come across a microbox or other small box with a likewise small logbook
(scroll of paper, etc.). You might find that if everyone stamps in, you
take up much of the capacity of the log, not leaving a lot for others to
stamp in. It may not be convenient for the placer to promptly replace it,
either. In such a case, suggest that each member stamps in, but just with a
fragment of their image (ie, if it is an animal, just stamp in the head).
Enough to be recognizable, but to not take up as much room. And stamp
images close together, for the same reason, to conserve space.



--klk/FungusWoman, who speaks from experience, as the placer of many
centrifuge tube boxes (BTW, Dragon ---)
