From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-14 10:57:49 UTC-05:00
To celebrate their upcoming nuptials,
Coffee Beans and Atom 118
invite you to spend an afternoon with friends
(while getting your fingers all inky of course)
at the Letterboxing Wedding event on
Saturday, November 10th, 2007
from 12 o'clock until 4 o'clock in the afternoon
at Pulaski Park in Chicago.

RSVP on the event's page on AQ.

This "reception" is a potluck, so let us know what (if any)
dish/beverage you could bring to share and we'll add a list to the AQ
page. Also, feel free to sign up for a wedding-related stamp that you
wish to carve and share with the rest of us. This event is going to be
indoors, but we're also thinking about hiding a few outdoor urban boxes
for people to find that day.

Hope to see you there,
(the-soon-to-be) Atomic Beans
