From: veronabirdpatrol (rselzer@tds.net) | Date: 2012-05-20 18:25:10 UTC
We were out planting boxes both Saturday and Sunday morning. It was 85 degrees both days by the time we were in from the trails. I wonder what another few weeks will bring. I think it will be a hot one folks!

A few things to note, the trails are all damp in places, even though we have not had rain in about 10 days. I predict muddy conditions if it is rainy the week before the event. There is not much in the way of poison ivy so far on the trails we have planted on. Just a little, but I did run into stinging nettles. No mosquito sightings so far.

See you in a few weeks!

Verona Bird Patrol

We were also out at the park planting our boxes yesterday. Nice trails! We
also didn't see any mosquitoes or ticks - hope that will be true a few weeks
from now.



Even though we will be staying at a hotel, we drove through the campgrounds
to see what the sites were like. They were all in nicely wooded areas and
very private, not jammed together.



Remember, if it is hot the weekend of the GLG, Blue Mound is the only state
park in WI that has a swimming pool. Fee is $1 for kids and $2 for teens &
adults, so don't forget to bring your suits if you might want to cool off
during the day. http://dnr.wi.gov/topic/parks/name/bluemound/pool.html



Only 20 days until JUNE 9!
