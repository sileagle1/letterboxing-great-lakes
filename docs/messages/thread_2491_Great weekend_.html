Great weekend!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-05-09 01:29:59 UTC
Well, we weren't the BINGO winners (and aren't likely to take 2nd or
3rd place either) but we aren't complaining - we had a fabulous
weekend!

While we were waiting for the last few Wandering in Wonderland
gathering boxes to arrive, we headed out to the La Crosse, WI area to
join our hiking club for some hiking along the Mississippi River. On
Saturday we did a challenging 10-mile hike up on the bluffs and on
Sunday a 5-mile hike on a river dike, nice and close to the water.
Fabulous weather in the low to mid-70s, sunny skies, sparkling water,
lots of birds (including herons, egrets, hawks and an eagle), and
newly leafed out trees, including many flowering trees. Quite the
spring spectacle!

Of course we incorporated some boxing on our weekend trip. We
checked on two of our boxes. Heron's Habitat is in great shape, but
hasn't had a visitor since OCT 2004. You can easily bike to this box
on the river dike. Wildcat Mound Wolf needed a new box, due to some
cracking, but contents were in perfect shape. Very few visitors to
this box also.

We also placed two boxes. One of my own and one for someone else who
is doing a national series.

We managed to find 12 boxes. Amazingly we were first finders at 7 of
the boxes, even though some had been placed last fall. Two other
notable events for us were finding our first Iowa boxes and finding
our 1,300th box (Y's Mac, with very cute clues). Unfortunately we
weren't able to find Dragon's Tunnel #1 box and we have notified him
accordingly.

Wisconsin Hiker
