What we did last weekend...

From: Doglvrs (doglvr895@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-26 18:15:33 UTC
I meant to post this earlier, but dang! Time does get away from ya...

3 of the Doglvrs went letterboxing this past Sunday, and I wanted to
share this experience with you, because it was so unique.

We had 3 sets of clues printed up, with no idea where one of them
started. Off went went to one of the other boxes, and after
sucessfully hunting and rehiding...we headed back to the car. On the
way there, "Daddy's Girl" says, "Hey Mom, can I see those clues for
that one box?" Sure thing -- I handed them to her. She says "I think
I know where this is! I'm pretty sure I saw it on the way here."
Alrighty then...back we went. Sure enough - she had seen it, and after
much cheering and hand slapping - off we wnet on our hunt. It was
awesome! I highly reccomend "The Green Mile" if you're ever in the
area.
Way to go Stripey and gang -- keep up the great work!

Mrs. Doglvrs
