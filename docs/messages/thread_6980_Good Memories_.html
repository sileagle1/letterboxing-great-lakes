I'm in the process of uploading all our CDs to a hard drive via ITunes. I had many good memories of several past letterboxing events since today's CDs included:

- Fifty "50's" songs on the CD that Safari Man created for a box he planted in conjunction with the 2007 Great Lakes Gathering in Ohio.

- All the "Bird" themed songs on the CD that Trail Rider, Pastor's Wife and GGGHiker created for the Birds of a Feather event they hosted in 2008.

- 3 different "Olympics" themed CDs created by Familyof6 as a prize for the Winter Olympics event they hosted in 2010.

Lots of fun times at the events and another THANK YOU to the creators of these music mixes!
