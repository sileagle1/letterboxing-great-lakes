From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-01-16 22:39:09 UTC-06:00
We'd also like to chime in with thanks! It was fun to have a chance to
socialize mid-winter AND be able to get out on a nice loop trail for some
winter scenery. ggghiker had everything covered, including a way for us to
print clues for boxes we had just hidden that morning. Definitely a "full
service" location!! She was everywhere making sure everything went smoothly.
The nature center was great - roomy enough for the "mini" group of 50+
boxers in the main room, with the added attraction of the outdoor porch and
fireplace and the other various rooms that allowed people to spread out &
explore. This was especially popular with the kids in attendance. As at
all other LB events, the potluck food was great and we had the bonus
opportunity to purchase some farm-fresh eggs from Terrio. It was also nice
to meet a few people we hadn't met before - they all seemed like fellow
addicts (... I mean enthusiasts!).



Oh, and that chicken dance was a new initiation rite for Great Lakes
letterboxers, although next time I think it will be the ROOSTERS who need to
perform instead of the hens!



Thanks gghiker for a fun day and we're glad to hear you're already planning
a sequel!
