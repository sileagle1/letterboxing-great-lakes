WOW! Fun Stuff!

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-06-03 21:49:08 UTC-04:00
WOW! What a blast! We had a lot of fun at the gathering! We never
made it out to do any real boxin' until about 5pm! I had such high
aspirations of things we would find. I printed about 20 boxes in the
area, not to mention the tons and tons that were planted specifically
for this even. I printed another 20 or so to find to & from the
park on the drive down. I had spreadsheets, I had flowcharts, I had
maps, I had clues and I apparently had pipe-dreams from here to the
moon! I figured we'd find 4-5 on Friday night, lots and lots on
Saturday and even more today. In reality? Not even close! We found
absolutely zero on Friday.... the 5 Disney stamps, 2 Lucy Stamps and
1 Lego stamp on Saturday, and 3 other boxes on our way home today in
Bowling Green and Perrysburg. Grand total 10! Boy, was I way off in
my estimated count!

I did manage to fill up 34 pages in my log, though! So, I still put
in a lot of effort and no carpal tunnel pains in my wrist
either! The stamps are all fabulous! It's so fun to go back and
look at them all together. Yesterday was such a whirlwind of
activity and it all went by way too fast! It's hard to even remember
everyone we talked to and met, but looking at the stamps helps bring it back.

Many, many thanks to Jim & Stacy for the awesome event. You did a
fantastic job! That would have stressed me out to no end and I don't
think I could have done it. But, you pulled it off marvelously with
not a glitch in sight! Oh, and I'm not bitter that I missed the
grand prize drawing by 3 numbers... really, I'm not. Really, I
swear! Where do you live again? ;-)

Thanks to Mr. The Doctors B for the wonderful bagpiping a the end of
the night, too. It was fantastic!

I had a blast meeting everyone and putting names to faces! There are
so many people I never did meet up with, though. I'm glad I had a
couple boxes there so I could see their stamps even if I didn't see them!

Our only real mishap of the weekend was that we forgot to put the
garbage in the van last night and we had a raccoon fight/picnic at
our site at around 1am! They didn't do much damage, but they sure
put up quite a ruckus! RedRoxxieRaccoon was at the camp site next
door with WhereTheWildThingsR. She swears she wasn't involved in the
squirmish, but you never can tell!

Oh, BTW, do you Ohio-people germinate and grow Poison Ivy in your
spare time or for extra cash or as a social experiment or
something? I've never seen so much in my life! If I don't break out
with it, it may just be a minor miracle. PoPoBullyGirl was chicken
(or would that be smart??) and she wouldn't go sauntering off the
trail for some of those elusive boxes. But, I kept up my mantra of
"don't touch me, don't touch me, don't touch me" while I was trying
to pick my way through patches of it! Let's hope that Ivy listened
to my demands!

I've got a mild sunburn, several sore muscles, moderate dehydration
and literally ink from head to toe (anyone know how to get permanent
ink out of clothes?)... but I had the best time! I can't wait for
the next one!

Oh, I only managed to take one picture! I see some are posted on AQ
already, but those of you who did manage to take pictures, please,
please share!


BNL BaliGrl
