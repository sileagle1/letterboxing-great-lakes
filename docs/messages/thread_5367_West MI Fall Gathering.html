From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-10-20 21:08:04 UTC
It seems that starting a few hours after a gathering, posts begin to pop up filled with
expressions of gratitude toward the event planners, the amazing stamps, clues, food and
fun. I want everyone who plans these events to know we don't take these gatherings for
granted by the repetition of our thanks. We all know that the gatherings take a ton of time
and effort to pull them off, not to mention the cost. (BTW - I hope everyone remembered
to slip a few green ones to the grillmeister on Saturday to cover some of the cost!) THANK
YOU! THANK YOU! THANK YOU for providing such great fun for all of us!

Spring Chick & Old Shoe sure made the annual West MI Fall Gathering one to remember.
We really appreciated the detail and organization of the clues, the great stamps, and we
are still walking heel-to -heel just for the "heel" of it!

We actually found 18 letterboxes on Saturday!...Hmm, lets see...that is about 16 more than
we found last year! Before any of you think that we finally "got it," be reminded that
Grandvilla and Mobius Klein graciously agreed to lead us throughout the park for the
afternoon - after all, they did help hide some of the boxes! We even nabbed two more
from last year. Leaving 4,678 combined boxes to find in that park when we return! Just
kidding! Only like 4,676... We also once again failed to find our own box hidden in there!
Spring Chick and Mobius Klein assured us it was still there.

Tonto and I didn't have any arguments all day (a first!) thanks in part to our valiant
letterboxing partners, Grandvilla and Mobius Klein, who kept us pleasantly occupied.
Tiptoe...and Tonto

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-10-21 22:25:36 UTC
All of the event boxes will be in place through the end of November
2008. After that they will be pulled and returned to the people who
created them, who may or may not choose to plant them at a location
closer to where they live.

The 5-box carousel series at Pine Bend park will stay in place as
permanent boxes (although I may move one or two to different hiding
spots).

All of the box clues are currently available in the files section of
this list.

SpringChick

From: springchick1219 (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-09-10 09:33:38 UTC
No problem! The fall gathering is traditionally a very laid back affair -- come and go as meets your needs... the schedule for the day is *very* loose -- early morning socializing, sig stamp exchanges... followed by letterboxing. The only thing that is pretty "on schedule" is the potluck, just so people know when to be back at the shelter for food.

The clues will be available in the files section of this list the night before the gathering. Printed clues will also be available at the shelter at the event.


Hope to see you there!

SpringChick
