Re: Sort of OT: Treasure's Trove

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-22 17:19:56 UTC
So I was intrigued by this conversation and picked up a copy of both
the original Treasure's Trove book and the solution book. OMG...
this is incredible! I am in awe by this guy's creative ingenuity
and how he has embedded clues and pieces of code into this wonderful
story and its illustrations.

It has been great reading through the finders' stories and following
the thought process for solving each of these.

I was even more pleasantly surprised to find that there is still a
14th treasure at large (and possibly more after that?). I had
thought that once the Crystal Pook was found in Newaygo State Park
that they were done. Not that I think I have a chance in a million
of finding it, but it is still much more fun trying to work through
clues that are unsolved than those that have been figured out
already.

I haven't been able to put this down since I picked it up on Friday.

SpringChick
