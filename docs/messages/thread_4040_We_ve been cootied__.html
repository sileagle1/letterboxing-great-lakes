We've been cootied!!

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-09 06:19:02 UTC-07:00
So last night, I had a meeting with the local Red
Cross, which I volunteer for. When I came home around
8, Deedle was sitting at the kitchen table, working on
our stamp for our June gathering box. I kissed him
hello, grabbed something to drink, and went into the
living room to sit on the couch and watch some TV.
After a few minutes, I looked up and noticed something
on our front door... we have a tin cat sign hanging on
the door, and there were two small plastic bags
hanging from the sign. As I took them down, I
realized...

We'd been cootied! In our own home! Twice! I feel
so violated... ;)

Watch out for that sneaky Turtle.... :)

Doodle Bug.
