Got My Goodies Today...

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-04-27 22:44:58 UTC
Last week's T-shirt discussion prompted me to place an order of a
few Silent-Doug designed goodies from CafePress, including a T-
shirt, a coffee mug and a license plate frame. Today the goodies
arrived... I love them and can't wait to show them off!

One thing I did want to pass along that took me by surprise, and
frankly was a bit of a disappointment... The URL printed on the
items is not www.letterboxing.org (the LbNA site), rather it is
www.letterboxing.info (Silent Doug's site). I wasn't sure how I
could have missed that so I went back to look at the items I ordered
and the pictures are so small that although you can see there is a
URL, and you can deduce the word "letterboxing" it would be
difficult to see that the domain is .info instead of .org unless you
know it is. The one item that you can see the text clearly -- the
box stickers, which I did not order, but did view at the time I was
ordering, actually does say letterboxing.org. So my own fault, I
made an assumption that was the URL on all of the items.

It is a little disappointing because I would hope to catch some
people's attention and possibly lead them to check out the web site
for more information. While it is true that Doug's site does give a
lot of great information, I would have preferred to direct people to
the official LbNA web site. And being honest, I guess as the web
person behind my own letterboxing web site, it seems a bit strange
to be walking around with the URL to somebody's personal
letterboxing site that is not my own.

Anyway, Doug please don't be offended -- you know I love you dearly
and I think your site is certainly one of the best 'boxing sites out
there. I was just surprised... But hey after all, you designed the
merchandise, you are free to put whatever you want on there,
including promoting your own site. The stuff is cool anyway and I
will use it proudly. Thanks!

SpringChick

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@letterboxing.info) | Date: 2004-04-28 12:13:59 UTC-04:00
Deb wrote:
>One thing I did want to pass along that took me by surprise, and
>frankly was a bit of a disappointment... The URL printed on the
>items is not www.letterboxing.org (the LbNA site), rather it is
>www.letterboxing.info (Silent Doug's site).

I am sorry about your disappointment, but completely understand. I was
concerned that people would be offended if I used the letterboxing.org URL,
since some folks on the big list are sensitive about the appropriation of
anything related to LBNA and letterboxing.org (not to mention the flak I
get if I ever mention my site on the list). But your point is good, and I
think I will make some changes based on your comments to make it clear
about what's on the items (such as in the description text), or maybe add
some LBNA logo items (at cost, of course, as their guidelines do allow) to
give people a choice if they'd rather not have the .info domain on items.

(And personally I'm grateful for the graceful way that you wrote your
message, which is in stark contrast to a lot of the a bit
less-than-pleasant tone of way too many posts on the big list, IMHO. On a
related note, I'm finding that a lot of the conversations in the regional
letterboxing groups are much more interesting and friendlier than the main
LBNA list, and so I'm pleased that you set up this group....)

Doug
