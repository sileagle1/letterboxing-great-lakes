For those who didn't see the post on AQ.

I recently realized that the June 2nd Event is on the same weekend as
the annual Memorial golf tournament in nearby Dublin Ohio. I'm not
worried about the influx of people in the area. I'm worried about the
weather.
If you've never heard of the Memorial Tournament, here's the scoop.
According to legend, the golf course (Muirfield) was built on an
ancient Indian burial ground. And the chief of the disturbed Indians
(Chief Leatherlips) has cursed the tournament every year with rain. It
is a given that it will rain at some point during the tournament.
This year the tournament runs from May 28-June 3.
Just something to keep in mind when packing.
