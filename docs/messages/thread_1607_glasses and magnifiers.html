From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-07-15 20:58:53 UTC-05:00
Oh, they laugh at me all the time at work, because I wear a pair of
prescription reading glasses, with my distance glasses on the top of my
head, AND I use my "big giant eyeball" (as they call the Professional Grade
magnifying lamps I made them buy me - and it is kinda scary to look at me
from the other side as I'm peering through it!)) to read the cultures.
(Progressives or bifocals just don't cut it - my neck gets too sore tilting
up to see through the bottom.) Anyway, my carving setup is pretty much the
same. I bought myself a nice light-up magnifier with my doubled Discover
cash-back bonus from Sharper Image. As they say, "it pays to Discover" but
"sucks to grow old" (but it's better than the alternative...).



--klk/FungusWoman
