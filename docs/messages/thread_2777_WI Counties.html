WI Counties

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-07-17 03:55:52 UTC
I'm happy to report that Martini Man and I did manage to get boxes
planted in two new counties (Waupaca and Oconto) in WI recently. I
also noticed that The Dragon Clan planted a box in Juneau county so
that means there are now letterboxes in 45 Wisconsin counties. That
also means there are 27 counties remaining that still need boxes. If
you'd like to see a map of the counties that have boxes, and those
that don't, you can see a map here:

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

I think it would be great to have a box in every county in WI, so
please consider planting a box in one of these counties if you are
traveling through the state. Thanks!

Wisconsin Hiker







WI Counties

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-09-12 04:47:22 UTC
Hooray! In the last few weeks, 2 more Wisconsin counties now have
letterboxes. Thanks to a traveling Arizonian (I think), the far
northwest county now has a box and the Y's Guys planted one in
Richland county in their recent planting spree. That means there are
now boxes in 51 counties, with only 21 still waiting for a box. I'm
hoping to plant one in Marquette county before the end of the month
and Martini Man might also get something planted somewhere in the
middle of the state.

The updated map can be seen at:
http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

I was very pleasantly surprised to see that the LBNA website has
recently been updated to show the counties that received boxes this
year also colored in on the map there. Thanks to the webmasters
(Choi?)!!!

Wisconsin Hiker
