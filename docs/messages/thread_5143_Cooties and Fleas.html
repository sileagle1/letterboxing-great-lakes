Just when you thought that all you had to worry about were cooties,
check this out! I read about 'Fleas' on AQ.

"A flea is a non-restricted form of a cootie. You can plant it in
letterboxes as if it was a hitchhiker, or on a person like a
traditional cootie. You can log in with a thumbprint signature or use
your signature stamps."

So now, there's the possibility of a non-restrictive flea epidemic too.

I'm not 100% sure but if you find a flea in a letterbox, do you have to
take it and move it like a HH? Or, I guess you could leave it there for
future unsuspecting victims. LOL! As always it would depend on the
labeling instructions.
