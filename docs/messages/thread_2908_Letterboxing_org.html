As most of you are aware, the letterboxing.org site has been down for
several days. Here is a note that was posted on Choi's blog:

LbNA Will Be Back Soon
My apologies to all those who have tried to access Letterboxing North
America to access clues but are locked out. Unfortunately, our web
hosting service has suspended our account because of the increasing
level of demand on the database. We're in the process of upgrading
our service from a shared server to a dedicated service. The downside
is that this increased level of service is significantly more
expensive, at least $45 compared to $8 per month. I've received many
offers to help and would be happy to accept donations via check. You
may send checks to John Chapman, LbNA Server Fund, 28 Birch Road,
Andover, MA 01810. If you'd like to use Paypal, please send the
donation to john@johnsblog.com from Paypal.
