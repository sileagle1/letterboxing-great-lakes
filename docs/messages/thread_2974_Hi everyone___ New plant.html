From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-08-19 15:24:48 UTC
Hello!
We planted a box today - its a very personal one, VERY near our
home - and, our home is the 'theme' as well. We live in an old train
station that has TONS of history - so.. we thought, what better idea
than to plant a box where we can SEE people coming to it, and where
they can learn a little something about the place we live! (Hint -
check out our personal traveler hints before trying - in case we are
home!!) Obviously, if we saw someone, we would pop out and say Hello!

There are also other boxes in the area, so would make a
nice 'social' find for someone coming up to do the others!

I DID give it an F count to start... if Im sending people to my
yard, I felt a little better about that! LOL If you don't use
Atlasquest to log finds - just email me for the clues - I would be
HAPPY to send them.

BTW - I LOVE this stamp. VERY pleased.

Paula
