BINGO-Wrapping Up

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-05-10 22:03:58 UTC-05:00

Congrats to the winners,

The game is now done.

Winter has ended,

And so has our fun.

Congrats to the Party,

Of Six plus one more.

But I know some of you want,

To see what was in store.

I'll tell you the sequence,

Of words I did not call,

Count the days it would've taken,

To cover your card with all.

I won't name any names,

Or point any fingers,

But if you had 6 to go,

You are the opposite of the winners.

I'll add just one more note,

To help soften the pain.

If you didn't have the wild card,

Don't feel that pick lost the game.

Mr. Elf, he himself,

Who was the first to have a say,

Would have still won the game,

If wild cards weren't in play.

Ok, one more verse,

With a personal word.

It's been fun writing poems,

A thought once absurd!
