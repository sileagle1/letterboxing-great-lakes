From: Mothkovich, Christine (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2009-03-23 08:52:29 UTC-05:00
I haven't had the heart to break the news to "Krait" yet (my friend who does
the carving) that he'll need to recarve 2 stamps. Plus, I did a status
check on the remaining Birds of a Feather boxes at McQueen and Potawatomie
yesterday, and Box #1 of the Songbirds series is gone. Everything else was
OK, although there were so many muggles out I didn't get a chance to check
on ALL the boxes.

When we drove past McQueen on our way home from church yesterday morning
around 11, we could see that the parking area had a fair number of cars.
When we went back 2 hours later, the cars were gone. We checked on Nest
Eggs first and discovered that it had been found that very morning by a Boy
Scout troop from Woodstock. They didn't have a stamp, but signed their
names and wrote "We left you a pen and a quarter!" which indeed they had :)

Anyhoo, PB, you've already found the River Fauna series, remember?

Trail Rider/Chris
