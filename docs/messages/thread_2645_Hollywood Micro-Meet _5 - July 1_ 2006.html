Hollywood Micro-Meet #5 - July 1, 2006

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-06-09 21:35:08 UTC-05:00
I spoke with one of the managers of the Paw Caf today. She loves having us
letterboxers stop by for our micro-meets, and told me she would be willing
to open up on a Saturday if we were interested in meeting there.



Unfortunately, my weekend schedule totally sucks from now until August. The
only Saturday I am not working or otherwise committed is July 1st.



Ive created a poll please indicate if you can attend and how many in your
party. Since shed be opening especially for us, Id like to be able to
commit to tell her the number so she can plan her staffing and stocking
needs. If you say you can make it but later change your mind, please change
your vote(s). If there isnt enough interest (I realize this is leading
into the holiday), I will reschedule for later in the summer.



The Paw and Cyberdog Cafs are located at 8420 Brookfield Avenue in
Brookfield, Illinois, across the street from the Hollywood/Zoo Stop on
Metras Burlington Northern-Santa Fe line. As Ive been known to mention
before, the Metra runs a $5 unlimited riding program on weekends (heck, you
can ride in from Kenosha (but must get from one station in the city to the
other) for five bucks such a deal in these days of $3+/gallon gas!). Make
a day of it and visit the Brookfield Zoo afterwards its walking distance
and see the new carousel. We can even go as a group.



Anyway, let the voting begin I hope to see you guys on the 1st!



--klk/FW
