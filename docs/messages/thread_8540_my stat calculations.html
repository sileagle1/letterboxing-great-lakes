I was wondering how things were going so far and missing WiHi's stat reports.  So I did some counting of my own. 
My statistical reporting is far inferior to what WiHi has done in the past, but if I counted correctly, there are 29 out of 140 words still remaining that have not been called. 

This means 79% of the words have been called.

If you have 5 words remaining on your card (as I do), you are exactly on par with the totals that have been called.  Any more, you're doing worse.  Any fewer and you're doing awesome!


The N column is being shy this year, there are 9 words remaining in that column.  Only 5 words remaining in each of the others.


All the events have been called in all columns.  All of the trial names have been called in B and O.  All of the letterboxes have been called in O.  All of the places have been called in G and O.  All of the general terms have been called in B.


All of that information is based on the assumption that I accurately crossed off the words on my master sheet as they were called.  Historically speaking, I tend to miss a word or two, so take that with a grain of salt or two.
