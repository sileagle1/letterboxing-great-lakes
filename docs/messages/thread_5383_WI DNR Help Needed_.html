WI DNR Help Needed!

From: (sarabyron@att.net) | Date: 2008-10-28 16:56:55 UTC
I need some advice, again! In July, I planted my first LB in WI at a boat landing that is state land managed by the WI DNR. (I can hear y'all saying, OMG, what a lost cause!) I really liked the spot so I persevered. I downloaded the DNR Geocache Placement Notification form, estimated the Cache coordinates from GoogleMap and sent it in. Lo and Behold! I received a reply yesterday that the Geocache had been approved, but I needed to email exact GPS coordinates, as mine do not match the box! FYI there is a new updated form also. I had used a 6/06 form and the new one is dated 4/07. The DNR "real estate program coordinator" had checked the box, approved the location and said that it was nicely hidden, just wrong coordinates! (Our taxpayer money at work!)
I do not have a GPS - I don't even know the format usage. I also don't want to buy one. And my cell phone is not GPS enabled.
So, I don't know if I just entered the coordinates incorrectly, or if google maps just didn't get close enough to the exact location of the tree stump. And I don't remember how I got these coordinates off of google! I entered: Latitude N45/87/2118, Longitude W -89/67/3082. The DNR wrote on my form: S.18, T39N-R7E NH-AL State Forest.
I'd love to just ASK her what I should do, but I remember that some of you warned me not to tell her that this was a LetterBox and not a real geocache .... The address of the boat landing is 8500 Brunswick Road, Minocqua, WI.
Would anyone be willing to help me figure this out somehow? - or do you think I should just let it go? They must not be very busy up there, if she actually went to check these coordinates! I planned on putting one on an island - wonder if she'd canoe out there to check it! I'll be sure to get my next cell phne with GPS but my 2 year contract is not done until February.
Thanks in advance for reading this far. Good news is that they seem to be open to stuff hidden on thier lands!
Sara of JeffersClan
