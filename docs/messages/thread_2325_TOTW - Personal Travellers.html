From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-27 00:32:10 UTC
I have 2 PT's and another that is acting like a personal traveller for the moment. With the
upcoming gathering, I've been getting a bit nervous about all the people I want to meet in
person for the first time and how harried exchanges can be when there is a set-time for
this activity. Trying to solve and obtain all the PT's is difficult enough, making sure I don't
lose my sig stamp and favorite PT in the process is an added burden.

I am considering retiring one of my PT's now, but I wanted to hear from the group. Do you
carry more than one? Do you retire them ever? How stingy are you if someone attempts
to acquire your PT and doesn't quite hit the mark? I have been pretty generous with mine.

~speedsquare
