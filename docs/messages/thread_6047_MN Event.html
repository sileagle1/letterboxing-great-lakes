From: wi_letterboxer (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-09-09 03:57:34 UTC
Woo-hoo! Gooble Gobble just emailed me to let me know that a fall event was planned in MN and it JUST SO HAPPENED to be the same weekend we will be in MN for a wedding! We then double-lucked-out since the wedding isn't until 3 pm. That means we can make it to the event for at least a few hours.

I hadn't seen anything mentioned on this board about the event, so I figured I'd post in case there were others that might be able to make it. It will be held at Cottage Grove Ravine Park on Saturday OCT 10. The theme is "Harvest and Haunts".

From: Gooble Gobble (gooble_gobble_3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-09-08 21:53:42 UTC-07:00
Please come one and all, the more the merrier. Red Cat usually plans a fall gathering around Halloween time of year. It's always a great time and we'd be more than happy to have you. The park it's at is beautiful, it really is a ravine and there are plenty of ups and downs to be hiked (nothing as severe as Afton State Park, where the 2008 GLG was, but some good leg-burners nonetheless). I hope a lot of you can find the time in your busy schedules to come to Mn. to see us on Oct. 10th. Sign up on AQ or just surprise us and show up. Hope to see you there.

Gooble Gobble
