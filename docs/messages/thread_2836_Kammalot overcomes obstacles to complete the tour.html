From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-27 10:46:10 UTC
The Battle Creek, MI letterboxing team, Kammalot, quietly waited
through park closings, boxes missing and clues changing, and finally
overcame the many obstacles and completed the 80 Days series. Like
many other seekers, Kammalot's persistence was the key to success.
They may have been overheard muttering, "What's the big hurry,
anyway?" the Travel Team cannot confirm that rumor.

Kammalot will join the Honor Roll at
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_travelers.asp
when the Travel Team updates the site.


Congratulations!
