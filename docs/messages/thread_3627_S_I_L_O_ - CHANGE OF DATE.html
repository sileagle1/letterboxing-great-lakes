Some Chinese New Year celebratory tidbits I learned from a Chinese coworker - the oldest and youngest attendees should dress entirely in red for good luck.... The proper greeting for the day is Congratulations or Happy new year, the former because tradition says a beast (the name is something like "Nyah" - does anyone know the anglicized spelling?) is known for snatching people away overnight, so you congratulate everyone for surviving the beast.... The traditional gift is money. (I'll be happy to accept any gifts you guys would like to give me!!!!)

Good thing we aren't meeting on the actual day - tradition calls for EVERY article of clothing (from undies to coats) be brand new...could get expensive!

Assuming we have no dissention, are we going with this theme??
