From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-01-11 06:55:40 UTC-06:00
On this, the fourth day,
I have more to tell.
I want to say it nicely,
I don't want to yell.

A reminder seems needed,
A note about booty.
You must send me some,
Or else I get moody.

Ship me your stuff,
For I'd hate to pronounce,
You could've been a winner,
But your card, I RENOUNCE!

Gift cards are great,
A recent addition.
Carve, tools, or ink,
Also fit, per tradition.

Details, as usual,
Can be found in the files,
So please send me things,
Though the mail, many miles.

I can cut you some slack,
Due to our late start.
But please do it soon,
And do the rest, of your part.
