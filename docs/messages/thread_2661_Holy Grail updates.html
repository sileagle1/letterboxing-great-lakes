The quest to place Holy Grail boxes is filled with peril. Even the kindest friends will tell
you that I am not known for grace or twinkle toes. It was with great trepidation that I
followed my faithful squire's lead over no fewer than three shifting and unstable bridges of
death on our quest to place Witch & Duck.

It was on the third rolling ford that Golden Boy came to grief while chivalrously extending
a hand. While I crossed unscathed, dry socks and a picnic lunch went a long way to
console him.

Don't worry, faithful knights, you need not attempt these bridges to fulfill this portion of
your quest, and the limestone outcroppings in the area are well worth seeing.

Acorn
