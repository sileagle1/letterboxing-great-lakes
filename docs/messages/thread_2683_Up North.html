GretchenF, YooperGal and I got together in the TC area and had a
wonderful time.



Did three of SpringChick's letterboxes - she is everywhere! I think
of her clues often as suggestions - put on your thinking cap when going
after hers. As usual, she shows us new places we have not been before
which is amazing since I spent every summer up in the TC area from age
10 on and I went to a Nature Preserve that I didn't know existed.



Now, we always liked the hiker's creed "take only pictures, leave only
footprints" - but we left letterboxes, too. If you like pictures,
check out http://tinyurl.com/r2ook



We added two boxes - Lost boat at Suttons Bay
and
Fishtown .
Set a hitchhiker free and released another.



If you haven't been to the Lake Leelanau Peninsula, it is worth a day or
two. What always amazes me is you can forget all your troubles and
worries - the wind and water just take them away.



Thanks for the fun SpringChick - and when I grow up (I know I'm older
than you already) I want to carve like her.
