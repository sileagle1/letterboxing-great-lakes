From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-12-31 17:40:34 UTC
It may be too late to get into the Letterbox BINGO game for 2008, but
there is still time to take a guess about when that game will end.
It's another way to put a little glimmer into the darker days of
winter for those of us in the upper Midwest.
It's called The Side-Wagers game.

As SpingChick's BINGO game moves through its daily draw and the
players anxiously check their cards to see if "we got one!",
eventually someone will call "Bingo" for the grand prize.

All you have to do is take a guess as to when that date will come.

With 25 spots to fill on the card, it's theoretically possible for
someone to call "Bingo" on January 25. But we all know that's not likely.
But when will someone fill his/her card and claim the top prize?
January 25? Valentine's Day? Groundhog Day? The Ides of March? April
Fool's Day? Tax Day? May Day? Memorial Day? Or someday in between?

Look in the Files section of the Great Lakes list for the Side-Wager
rules and give me your best guess. If you want to play entries must be
in by midnight tonight, December 31, 2007.
I supply the prize so you don't have to do anything except pick a date.
The more the merrier!


Good luck,
