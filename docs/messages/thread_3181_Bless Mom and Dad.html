Bless Mom and Dad

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-10-16 20:32:15 UTC
My folks don't understand our (especially my) interest in boxing, even
though we've dragged Dad along on a search or two. They think
it's "nice" but not their cup of tea. Bless their souls, though,
because they went out looking for our missing letterboxes in Florida.
They're down in Ft. Myers for a few weeks and headed out to Lakes Park
this morning. They were along when we placed one box and poked around
in every pile of rocks looking for it--but no box. They went to the
site of the other one--couldn't quite figure out what a "rock that
looks like a castle" was but poked around in that area without luck.
So, it's a bummer that both of these boxes are missing, but it's nice
to know my folks will go out of their way to indulge us in our/my
obsession. Guess you never outgrow that parenting thing.
