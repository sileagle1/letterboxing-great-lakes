Well, since Dragon has now posted the clues to the FOUR boxes he hid
recently to fill in the "foot" of the boot, WI now officially has
boxes in 61 counties, with only 11 to go. You can see an updated map
here:

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

If you can plant something in one of these counties in the near
future, please let me know at: kotlarek@wi.rr.com It will be hard
to beat the MI mob, but let's give it a good try. All help is
appreciated!

Those crafty Woverines have been hard at work planting in MI while
Martini Man & I were gallivanting about finding boxes in Illinois.

We had much better weather today and were very happy that we were
able to find all 11 boxes in the Big Ten series (but I still don't
know why they call it Big TEN if there are 11 teams??? I guess I
could Google it for more info. Martini Man told me they added one
along the way, but I don't know why). We enjoyed many IL Forest
Preserves (the supply of Forest Preserves seems ENDLESS in Northern
IL) while hunting these down. Also found some very nice P. Granola
boxes, and the excellent "Untitled" box, and the historic Quilt #2 in
the Waltzing Pigs' series. On the way home we stopped to check on my
Sweet Prairieland box because I had heard a Girl Scout troop visited
recently. It turns out the box had been rehidden well and they only
used 2 pages in the logbook. While in the area, we also found the
other Jelly Belly related box placed by Cinemaboxers. We were able
to find a total of 16 boxes today AND we found THREE Hitchhikers -
woo hoo, what a great day to be outside "hunting" with lots of woods,
rivers & lakes as scenery!

I guess now we'd better start planning a "planting trip" to try to
catch up with MI...

Thanks to all of you who have planted boxes for us to enjoy!

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
