a new adventure for us

From: stargirlboxer (abf@i2k.com) | Date: 2008-05-25 21:44:47 UTC
El Cajador and I will be leaving Michigan to head into a brand new
adventure. We're moving to the Dominican Republic for the next few
years. We'll both be teaching at Santiago Christian School. I'll be
the library media specialist there. El Cajador will teach some Spanish
classes and some business classes. We're really excited about the
whole experience but are also overwhelmed with details about all the
preparations.

Since we're leaving the area I'm thinking about the boxes I've
planted. I've planted two boxes in Wyoming, Michigan in Palmer Park:
Crazy Golf Day and Big Bite. I'm wondering if anyone might be
interested in adopting them.

We're planning to have a blog (which we're going to start creating
tomorrow before we had to a friend's cottage) so you can follow our
adventures if you're interested.

Stargirl
