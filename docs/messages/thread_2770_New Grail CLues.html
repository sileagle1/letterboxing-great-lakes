Doglvrs has planted the Sir Robin's Shield box. Since I can't upload
them to my website on vacation, here they are in case you want to get
this MI box:

SIR ROBIN'S SHIELD



Location: Seidman Park Ada, Michigan (East of Grand Rapids off M-21)



Directions: Seidman Park has two entrances...one at 8155 Conservation
in Ada township. The second is on Honey Creek about 1 1/4 miles North
of Conservation. This box will be found at the Honey Creek entrance.
It can be reached off M-21. From M-21 turn North on Pettis Ave. to
Honey Creek. Turn right on Honey Creek, travel past Conservation
about 1 1/4 miles...entrance will be on your right (East)



Clues: Take the trail directly East of the big brown sign. Follow
the trail down and aroundrocking and rooting all the way. At your
first choice you must keep straight ahead. Head into the woods for a
short way. Just before leaving the woods you will see a blazed flexi-
post Investigate a small trail on the South side of the main trail.
You will come upon Nessy ~ she holds the treasure in her Y.
