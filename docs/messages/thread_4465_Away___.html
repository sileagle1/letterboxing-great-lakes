The event she is talking about it our "Letterboxing Wedding." Coffee
Beans and I are hosting the event. It is posted on AQ

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=381

We've not done much to publicize it other than a few posts here and
there. It will be an indoor event not far from downtown Chicago. Some
folks (mainly Fungus Woman) have signed up to carve some event boxes,
and others have volunteered to bring some food or festive supplies (I
mean you, Nitrocat!). If you'd like to carve a box, please do! And if
you want to bring any food, please let me know so I can list it on the
event page. The event will mainly be a chance to exchange travelers,
sig stamps, and event stamps...and will be a chance for all of the new
boxers in Chicago to get together. As a bonus, Coffee Beans and I will
be planting a few temporary urban boxes in the surrounding
neighborhoods. There are also a few permanent boxes nearby.

Hopefully it will be a fun afternoon!
