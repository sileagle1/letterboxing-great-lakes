From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-03 06:52:56 UTC-08:00
A fellow Ohio letterboxer who's not a member of this
list (Kelmark, for those who do know her) sent us an
email about _another_ letterbox gathering planned for
this summer on June 24th - the 6th Annual Ohio
Letterbox Gathering (6th? I didn't know there ever
was a first! :)

The group hosting it is referred to as SWOGO (South
Western Ohio Geocachers) (don't let the word
Geocachers scare you). From looking at their main
website, they seem to work fairly independently of
AtlasQuest or the yahoo talk lists (but if anyone on
this talk list is familiar with SWOGO and this
gathering, please tell us more!).

Their website's main page is http://www.swogo.com

This link should take you directly to the post at
their page about the gathering:

http://swogo.org/cachebb/viewtopic.php?t=689

If no one objects, I'll place a notice of this
gathering in our Yahoo Groups calendar, with a link to
the SWOGO page for more information.

Doodle Bug.
