Alternate box exchange

From: kycoondog70 (kycoondog70@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-04 12:21:20 UTC

I don't want to make any hard feelings, but I don't care for the top
two themes and some of the rules for playing in the offical box
exchange so I made up a database and will host an alternate exchange.

Anyone who wants to play can sign up in the database by clicking on
the left hand side menu. Then when you reach the database, click on
add record.

Sign up available until Monday morning only. Then I will assign you
a trading partner and add their name to the database under your name.

You will be responsible for using edit record to add the dates you
do your mailing, planting, etc.

There is no specific theme for this exchange, you choose your own.
You can make it a regular boxe, virtual, or postal. Hand carving a
stamp is a option but store bought are also allowed and they can be
placed anywhere.

When you accept a box you agree to plant it, post the clues, and do
the maintance on it.

One last thing. When you exchange with your trading partner, you
must include a print of your personal stamp for the partner to
include in their own logbook.

KY coon dog
