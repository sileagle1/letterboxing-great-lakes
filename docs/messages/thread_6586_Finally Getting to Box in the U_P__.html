From: sunnysideupm (drewes@WI.RR.com) | Date: 2010-05-06 18:04:01 UTC
I am SO psyched to be getting up to the U.P. to box for the first time ever. I started boxing after my last trip to the U.P. in July '07 - my countS since then are P63(+2) F563. We'll be in Happy Rock for my uncle's 90th birthday - let's hope for good weather - no white or wet stuff (yeah, right). I'll be really excited whenever I can get to the west end of the U.P., as the Porkies are "the ancestral homeland" on my mother's side. I may have been born a suburban Chicagoan, may love living in SE WI, but I am a YOOPER by blood!

- Sunny
