Singing and Boxing Series Adventures

From: beachmom11 (MegPLouthan@cs.com) | Date: 2006-07-30 02:09:12 UTC
Okay, for those of you near Chicago, I'm sure you've all seen the new
12 box series by Atom 118 and Coffee Beans. A huge thanks to both of
them for creating such a massive letterboxing series with stamps well
worth the investment of both energy and time to find all 12 -- you did
a great job!
Mini Mom, Frogman, Big Boy and I headed out this morning to tackle the
series and we had quite the adventure! We thought we were very
clever, deciding to ride our bikes since the hike was a bit further
than Frogman and Big Boy usually enjoy and we thought it'd be a bit
hot (heat index over 100!) for a four-hour letterboxing episode. We
geared up four bicycles and hit the road.
When we arrived and started unloading, we noticed that Mini Mom's tire
was flat, despite the fact that she'd just put air in it before we
left. We inflated it again, only to be met with the hissing noise of
air flying furiously out of the valve. Bummer! Undeterred, and
wanting to keep our happy group together, we decided to bike with Mini
Mom walking.
We quickly realized that there were some long stretches, so we pulled
a move from our teenage days and I rode her on my bike -- she sat and
I stood up pedaling while she balanced the clip board with the clues
on my back and read them while we rode. Yes, I supplied the power (I
was a retired marathon runner, but am now thinking of making a
comeback!) and she supplied the unprecedented coordination and
multi-tasking skills. So much for being discreet and unnoticed by
others!! Everyone who saw us surely thought we were crazy, and I have
to say, we even knew we'd gone over the letterboxing deep end!
One by one, we found the boxes... one had been attacked by an animal
and we did the best repair job we could (good thing Mini Mom is always
prepared with extra ziplocs)... one had us riding in a circle for a
bit... and one caused Big Boy to wipe out on his bicycle, giving him a
nasty scrape on his elbow (luckily it was quickly cured with wet
wipes, sanitizer, and a lollipop), but we triumphed in the end!!
