Lake Michigan Circle Tour Update

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-07-13 16:27:58 UTC
Thought I would post an update on this project that never really got
off the ground. Thanks!!! to those of you who did send me box
information for the circle tour project, but I don't feel as though
there are enough boxes along the route to go through with the
project. I've held out for a bit and did get a straggler, but it is
still sparse -- not that any one of your boxes in itself is not
worthy, just that the project really needed a better distribution of
boxes to take off.

But, all is not in vain -- obviously, your boxes stand on their own
for Great Lakes letterbox seekers, and I have not given up on this
entirely, just for this year. I will try again next spring in hopes
that most of these boxes will still be available and that there will
be others to help make this a go. I am leaving the information in
the files section, although the dates are wrong...

So keep that in mind when winter comes around and you are sitting in
front of a roaring fire adding to your pile of carvings with no home.

Thanks again to everyone who responded!

SpringChick
