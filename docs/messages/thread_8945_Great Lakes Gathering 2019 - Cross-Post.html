Great Lakes Gathering 2019 - Cross-Post

From: (schuppner@uwalumni.com) | Date: 2019-07-14 13:05:54 UTC-07:00


The group has been really quiet and we just realized that our AQ Great Lakes post may have missed some folks who don't follow that board and might have been curious about the gathering, so we ask your patience with our updated cross-post.


Thanks to the Minnesota Carving Consortium, Crow Wing State Park, and other contributors for the excellent 2019 gathering filled with Minnesota Myths and Legends! We enjoyed the trails and other boxing opportunities, found humor (and rewards) in dealing with the numerous ticks and mosquitoes, and admired the carvings and creative logs. As usual, we caught up with longtime fellow adventurers and met new ones. The weather was great and a huge improvement over what was forecast even a day before. Tie dye sue’s Letterboxing 101 class was well attended, too.

We also appreciated the opportunity to list the trails we planned to hike on Sunday, so we could collect a few more images before the boxes were collected.


The Christmas Elves


P.S.  7 frogs (probably more), 3 snakes, and 1 skunk were in our wildlife count.  Happily, the skunk rustling in the bushes wasn't troubled by our presence on the trail.


P.P.S.  Does anyone else have boxing updates to share - gathering related or not?

Gosh...your post seemed like a refreshing blast from the past!  We miss the banter that once filled this group. BINGO comes about as close to filling that void in this present era. 

We too enjoyed the clever extra games at this years GLG..especially the ones that included the dreaded tick!  It is fun to see how each event has something unique to share...sometimes it’s very clever themes and clues, spectacular scenery, meeting new people and seeing the old boxers...and then there are the mishaps..or weather issues (snow, rain, tornadoes etc.) that make a specific event stand out as a true adventure!  
MANY happy memories of letterboxing for us over the years!  Now looking forward to yet another Homerdoodle event next weekend!
