From: corvisia (bunnyhugger@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-06-08 14:58:14 UTC
I found "Historical House" on May 15, three days after it was
placed (we were the first finders). To be honest, I was
concerned about its safety. When we found it, there were some
teenagers sort of skulking around, and we found some beer
cans and other signs that people like to "hang out" at that spot.
The box was actually right out in plain sight, although I'm sure
the placers hadn't left it that way, so I think it might have already
been disturbed. We tried to hide it as well as possible (covering
with leaves and such), but the spot wasn't really very secure. I
wouldn't be surprised if it is missing already (which would be
sad because it has a great stamp).

I see on LbNA that the placers checked on and found the box a
few days after my visit (May 18), so it lasted at least until then.
