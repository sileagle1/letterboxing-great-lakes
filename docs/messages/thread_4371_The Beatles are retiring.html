The Beatles are retiring

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-08-15 23:37:13 UTC-06:00
This is a notice to my LbGLK friends,

The Beatles Series of 4 boxes in Lake Forest, IL is retiring after just over 2 years. Paul was reported missing. He may have been washed away due to unusually heavy rains and flooding. I contacted the Open Lands Association to inquire if a box was found; and it wasn't. But they informed me that they 'highly discourage' any treasure hunting games on their properties. They found a letterbox once before and contacted LbNA. LbNA contacted me because I'm the only one in this are who is one of those crazy letterboxers. Hey, don't knock it till you've tried it. Sitting on the sofa isn't my idea a having fun in life. That found box belonged to another letterboxer who I recognized and I mailed it back to her.

Anyway, John, George, and Ringo bid farewell to Paul...where ever he drifted off too. And this series will be retiring in the morning.

Thought...It's getting harder and harder to find a good place to hide a letterbox. I guess 2 years was a good length of time for this series, but I would have hoped it would have lasted longer. Maybe I should recarve Paul and relocate the series.

Beetle
(Sometimes called Carol)
