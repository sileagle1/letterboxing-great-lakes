From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2010-08-20 06:36:10 UTC
Got a status report on one of our Cemetery Strolls--logbook swimming in water (bummer). But at least it didn't float away in all these floods!! The boxer said he/she dried things out as best they could and tidied up. What really impressed me is that this is a brand-new boxer. Only 5 recorded finds--all this week. And they are already savvy enough to be doing first aid. Wow.
