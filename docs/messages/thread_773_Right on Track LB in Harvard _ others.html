Right on Track LB in Harvard & others

From: Shutterfly (angi0105@aol.com) | Date: 2004-10-17 19:25:19 UTC

Has anyone found the Right on Track letterbox recently? My friend and
I went out boxing the other day and that was one on our stop. We
looked all over the place we thought it would be located in with no
luck. If you have found it, can I e-mail you off the list to pick
your brain to see if we did it right?

On a more positive note, we did manage to find three boxes that day.
Red Horse Bend, Belvidere IL
Humming Letterbox, ??? IL
All the World's a Stage #1: Kindergarten, Wauconda IL

On another, well, not so negative, but not so positive note...
We went looking for the Dead River box, a little late in the day, and
didn't find it. What we did find was Lake Michigan and the wonderful
beach there. On the way back over the sandy hill from the beach we
hear this over the loudspeaker: "Would the owner of the [my car]
please come back to your car? We would like to go home sometime
tonight." Oops! Well, I guess it was later than we thought.
So...which way did we come in at? That way took a half hour to get
here...maybe there is a faster route. Of course there was! We found
the park ranger shortly after his second comment about how "everyone
else figured out that it was time to go, so why couldn't you?" and
told him that we had gotten lost. He was nice after that. We then
traveled the hour and a half road back home to Rockford, completely
worn out from our day of letterboxing.
