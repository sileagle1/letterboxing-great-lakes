Milwaukee - Happy Days Boxes

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-30 16:34:52 UTC
These boxes ARE active (last I knew). Although I have emailed Canadian
Goose a number of times with the updated clues to these boxes, she has
never changed the clues on LBNA. In case someone wishes to try to
find these boxes here's the clues I sent to Canadian Goose to use:

Clues

So a last minute change in my Business trip brought me
to Milwaukee with a weekend to spend. So I went to
Wal-Mart bought some supplies and carved two stamps
with a Jack Knife in my hotel in Connecticut.

So the stamps are not my best work but a worth the
walk.

To begin find Back Bay Park. "Woodstock" fans will
find it quickly.

Once in the park find the lamppost that has a clear
view of the lake and the playground. Take 14 steps to
the north. There you will see a path leading
downhill. From the edge of the park "lawn" count
approximately 20 paces down the trail. Stop. On your
right deep inside a Pisa log is letterbox #1. Be sure
to recover it with leaves when you are done, but make
it look natural.

Back at the lamppost stand on the left and walk
toward the next closest lamppost. From here head to
yet another post approximately south. Now grab a seat
at the nearest bench to enjoy the view. From the top
of the nearby stairs, one should take a bearing of 259
degrees and walk 48 paces (steps) to the tree line.
