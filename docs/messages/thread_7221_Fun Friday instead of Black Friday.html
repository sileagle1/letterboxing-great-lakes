Fun Friday instead of Black Friday

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-11-26 04:04:55 UTC
We ignored the stores today and instead headed outside to do some letterboxing in the enjoyable 50+ degree weather. We found another great new Lock, Shock & Barrel box at Devil's Lake (and checked on two of my old 2005 boxes on the same trail). Later we found 2 lonely boxes planted by Verona Bird Patrol at Redstone Lake over 2 years ago. The boxes only had 1 or 2 other finders before us. Both were nice carvings and had neat logbooks with covers made from bark. Also found 3 other boxes, including one by ggghiker that had a great comment about deer overpopulation in the clues.

Hopefully many of you were also able to get out before the rain/snow arrives later this weekend!

Wisconsin Hiker
