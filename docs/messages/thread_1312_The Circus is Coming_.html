The Circus is Coming!

From: Mark Pepe (mjpepe1@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-05-13 01:28:32 UTC
To all residents of Michigonia and nearby districts:

Please let me introduce myself. I am Marco Peperini, owner of the
world famous Pinecone Extravaganza Circo of Venezia, Italia. I will be
on holiday in your district during the month of July and have a most
wonderful surprise for you all!

A fellow circus owner from the US, Phineas Boch from the southern
regions of America, has given me permission to take his troop of
oddities, animals and performers along with us when we come to your
area.

Look for Boch's Traveling Circus on Saturday, June 18th at the
Waterloo Recreation Center. We are most excited to bring you this
wonderful assemblage of acts from around the world for your
entertainment pleasure.

We look forward to performing for you all,

Ciao for now!

Marco Peperini
