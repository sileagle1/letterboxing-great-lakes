From: Gooble Gobble (gooble_gobble_3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-02-11 04:57:55 UTC
My wife is so great, earlier this week she said" Why don't you get an enclosed shelf thingy for all your letterboxing stuff"? I thought it was a great idea and promptly found an AWESOME enclosed computer armoire/desk/whatchamacalit on Craigslist. I had previously been storing all my boxing stuff in various drawers and cabinets. Now I have everything in the same spot. No more searching for the right tool or piece of carving material. I no longer have to dig through layers of stuff to find what I need. I created a photo album called "Creation Station" for everyone to post the area/setup they carve with. So everyone...where do you do your creating?

P.S. My daughter couldn't help "modeling" for me. Enjoy.
