From: blueskyinkazoo (blueskyinkazoo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-04 16:33:29 UTC
Just wanted to let those of you concerned with posting clues publicly
let you know that Atlasquest has an option of limiting the viewing of
clues posted to those with accounts with a certain found count that is
specified by the lister. I think it would be safe to assume those who
take the time to create an account and log their finds are fairly
serious letterboxers. I don't know if LBNA also has this option - it
may, I just haven't noticed it!
Blue Sky in Kazoo

From: blueskyinkazoo (blueskyinkazoo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-06 18:18:09 UTC
Didn't mean to open a can of worms regarding the option to limit clues
by found counts, as I said in an earlier post, I don't think the answer
is to have all clues restricted in some way. Just wanted to point out
one option that may (or may not) discourage some less scrupulous
hunters. As some LBers have pointed out, I think a variety of LB
options is great - the easier ones will appeal to beginners and those
limited for other reasons (physical ability, children, etc), the more
challenging ones will appeal to another set and some to both. That's
part of what makes it so appealing.
Blue Sky
