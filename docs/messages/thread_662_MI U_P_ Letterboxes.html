Sounds like some of my U.P. boxes have been seeing some activity
this summer. I was wondering why I didn't hear much on them from
finders and think I figured that out -- because the LbNA clue list
was immediately redirecting to my web page for the clues,
the "Contact the Placer" option was not available. I changed that
and just within the last week have heard on a couple of them.

I appreciate the reports from finders especially on these boxes
since they are so far away from me and I do not have contacts in the
U.P. to just run over and check on a box for me. We will be heading
up to the U.P. later this month and I feel an obligation to check on
my boxes, but am anxious to find the new boxes that have been
planted since we were last there. We're planning trips to both
Mackinac Island and Grand Island and a bunch of boxes along the way
between Copper Harbor and Mackinac. But as most of you who have
been up there realize, things are quite spread out and the condition
of some of the roads makes getting from one place to another
sometimes very time-consuming. Even though we will be there several
days, there is no way we can check on all of our boxes and find all
the new boxes along our route (and still leave some time to eat and
sleep). So I would really appreciate hearing from anyone who has
been up there and found, or not found, any of my boxes within the
past 4-6 weeks (if you did not already contact me). The more box
check-up stops I can cross off the list, the more box find stops we
can make.

A couple I am especially hoping to hear on are the Big Two-Hearted
River box in Seney and the Marquette Harbor Light box. I received a
couple of messages early in the summer from 'boxers who were
successful at the Big Two-Hearted River, but just received one in
late August from a group who were unable to find the box. This one
is way off our course and if anyone can confirm it was there after
8/27/04, it would save us a lot of time. I've received conflicting
reports on the Marquette Harbor Light box just within the past two
weeks -- both found and not found and I am not sure who was there
first. This one is along our route though so if I have to stop and
check, it's not a big deal.

Please e-mail me offlist if you have any reports you can offer on
these or any of my other U.P. boxes. Thanks!

SpringChick
