From: the_cignacats (mlcigna@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-04-15 21:45:02 UTC
I spent the day out checking about half of our active letterboxes. I am happy to report that the following boxes are alive and well:

Smelly Cat (WI mystery)
The Three Little Kittens Who Lost Their Mittens (Brookfield, WI)
It Also Rises (Waukesha, WI)

I also rewrote the clues for and rehid Retzer Nature Center box (Waukesha, WI) which had been pulled a few years ago after its hiding spot was demolished.

In looking through the logbooks, I had forgotten how Martini Man and Wisconsin Hiker had an uncanny ability to be first finders on all of our boxes!

Now, off to nap....
