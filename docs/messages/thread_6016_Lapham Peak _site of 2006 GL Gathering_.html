Lapham Peak (site of 2006 GL Gathering)

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-08-17 01:54:43 UTC
Hi!

We were out boxing in Waukesha County (our home county) today and in addition to finding some newer boxes, also checked on several boxes at Lapham Peak.

For those of you who didn't attend the Great Lakes Gathering in 2006, Lapham Peak is home to nearly 30 letterboxes. In March we checked on some of them and today we checked on a few more.

I am very sad to report that we could not locate MY box, Tweedle Dee & Tweedle Dum. Since there are so many of the Alice in Wonderland boxes remaining, I will recarve & replant so these characters are not missing. I'll post again when I've replaced the box.

On the bright side, we did find these other boxes in good shape:

White Knight
Humpty Dumpty
DRINK ME bottle
Mad Hatter
March Hare
Cheshire Cat

When we hosted the event, a few of the boxes were only in the park on a temporary basis. If anyone still has their temporary boxes and hasn't planted them anywhere else, we'd be happy to replant them again in Lapham Peak so they can rejoin their friends in Wonderland. You could bring them to the OCT event in Waukesha or mail them to me if you're interested in having them back at Lapham Peak.

Wisconsin Hiker
