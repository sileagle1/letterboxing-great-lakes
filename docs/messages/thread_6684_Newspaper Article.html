Best advice that was once shared with me was this....



When a newspaper article causes newbies to rush out and find letterboxes, sometimes without reading all the do's and don'ts, when they log their 'finds' and contact us, it's up to us to welcome them to the hobby and coach them.

Remember, we were all newbies at one time.
