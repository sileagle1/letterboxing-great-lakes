Whoo-hoo! A visitor!

Lake Elmo and Woodbury are about 20/30 minutes away from
Minneapolis/St. Paul. If you get the time, I highly recommend
the "Minnesota History series" by ToT and HK Wolf. It's a beautiful
walk and the stamps are great. Another one is "A Grand Excursion" by
Deb Rocks. It was my first one and is still one of my favorites.
There is another series by ToT in Afton (south of Lake
Elmo/Woodbury). As much as I complain about the roads in Minnesota,
all of the suburbs are connected and you can (usually) get to them
quickly.

I would definitely be up for a meet/exchange if our schedules work
out. My schedule is pretty flexible except for the fact that I have to
work during the day. Too bad you aren't coming up next weekend when
I'm hosting a mini-meet. Let me know if you would like to meet up!

-The Red Cat
