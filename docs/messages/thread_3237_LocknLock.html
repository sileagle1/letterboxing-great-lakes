I just wanted to say that we put a locknlock box and 10 days later 2.5 inches of rain fell, flooding the area. After a week, the water finally dried up enough that I could finally get back there to check if the box washed away. It managed to stay wedged under the branches and aside from mud on the outside of the box, it was in good shape, completely dry on the inside. So to The Cats Meow and all else, the lock n locks seem to be water tight. My only concern is the hinges and whether or not they will wear out.
