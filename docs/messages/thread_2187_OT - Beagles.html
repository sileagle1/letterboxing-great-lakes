From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-18 15:10:02 UTC
One of the conditions of relocating to a new home was that I would
finally be able to have a beagle. This reality will be here very
soon. I will do my homework and be prepared to parent a new puppy. I
have some ideas for her name, but I'd like to hear some feedback. I
didn't want to reuse some of the great beagle names of my friends and
family, so I think I have found something that might fit the bill.

I would like to name her Pandora, or Dora for short. It seems
appropriate on a couple of levels. Pandora, the goddess of mischief;
Dora the Beagle Explorer, and the most obvious, Pandora's (letter)box.

I didn't want to recycle these names: Hoover, Loki, Daisy, Snoopy
(dh's choice, even for a girl beagle), or Schatzi.

Let me know what you think!

speedsquare
