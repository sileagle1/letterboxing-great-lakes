From: Mark & Dottie Campbell (Campbell@Barnabas.org) | Date: 2012-10-27 07:19:41 UTC-05:00
Hi Shorty . I've got some boxes for your trip out . I'm assuming you're
taking I-80 out.

Of course Hartx6 has a couple boxes in LeClaire, IA if you're
coming through there.

There are a lot of boxes in the Council Bluffs/Omaha area . we
mainly boxed in Council Bluffs.

Memorial Day Letterbox, Bloomer Box (very hard to
find), Photo Op, Tractor Trails.

On I-80: Rose Garden (Giltner, NE), The Huskies Husker
(westbound I-80 Cozad, NE), Huskers (Emerald, NE), Big Truck (Brady, NE
westbound), BL Stomps & Stamps in NE (Sutherland, NE), several in Sidney,
NE: A Royal Challenge (at the Cabela store), & 4 boxes in Fun at Grandma's.



I just used the trip planner guide in AQ to plan our boxes as we went to
Estes Park a couple of times. It sounds like you're making a fast trip out,
however, and may not have time . but these make for good leg stretchers as
you're driving!!! We were surprised that we didn't recognize any names in
the logbooks . so, Shorty, join us!!!



Pastor's Wife
