From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-29 22:54:48 UTC
There will be an informal workshop for kids conducted by Bindle Babe
during the Haunted Harvest Gathering.

The Foot and I probably won't be able to get out and do much boxing,
so I thought I'd put together a quickie "LOG BOOKS for KIDS" workshop.

We'll be making two kinds of logbooks if there's enough interest and
enough time. NEEDLES are NOT needed for these bindings -- even young
kids CAN play!

Since I'm doing this last minute and taking the ferry, I won't be
bringing all the supplies along with me. (sorry)

Here is the list of supplies each participant will need to bring:
* 4-8 sheets construction paper (any color)
* 10-20 sheets plain printer/copier paper
* 2-4 regular size rubber bands (about the size of a hair-twist tie)
Kids can go out and find their own sticks for the binding while we're
at the Gathering Park

Additional supplies that would be REALLY helpful if anyone
could bring for sharing:
* Paper hole punch (single one, the hand held kind)
* Scissors (Kid safe ones would be great)

Supplies to be provided:
Cotton thread for the bindings on one of the logbook styles

Send questions or an email to let me know if you'll be able to bring
supplies for sharing to me at bindlestiff_boxer at yahoo dot com
(don't forget the underscore mark)

The storm caused power problems in the marina last night which remain
unresolved, so I'm going to be without internet access from now (Thurs
7pm) until Mrs DogLvrs and I get checked into our hotel room tomorrow
afternoon. We have wi-fi in the room and I'll bring my laptop, so I
should be able to answer email Fri after 2pm.

Enjoy!
