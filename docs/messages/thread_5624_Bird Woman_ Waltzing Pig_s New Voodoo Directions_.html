OH dear! I see that the voodoo chants for you must go to the next
level. So, follow these steps exactly:

1. Chant three times: "Springchick WILL call my word todaY!" Actually,
scream it from your back porch loud enough for her to hear in West
Michigan, OK?
2. Thing sandals DO NOT count! There are no sandals involved.
3. It's a RUBBER chicken you MUST have.
And here is where you bump it up a notch:
4.Take some red ink (yes, it must be red) and smear it across your
forehead. Repeat: "By the ink-stained fingers, I summon the voodoo
letterbox gods to intervene on this poor, poor soul." then, do twenty
jumping jacks, cross your eyes, and recite 10 times, "Springchick WILL
do my bidding, or a pin she'll get" (At this point, you know what to
do with the pin...or at least should.)

IF this fails, summon the Tiptoe TV BINGO gods, or.....go shopping at
the mall. (At least put some clothes on before you go.!)

Hope this helps....and please...update us on your status. :)
