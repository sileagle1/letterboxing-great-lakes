Hi, Sir Sil...

Those boxes I placed at Matthiessen and Buffalo Rock (Lil Bit of Flutter,
Shuffle off to Bluffalo, Dog out of Water) were placed this summer after the
tornado. Matt The Rat's Effigy Tumuli (at BR) is still there, though I was
unable to find it this summer when I went looking for it. The only SRL box
(The Point) I had a chance to find is also there as of early August (which
was the last time I checked on all of my boxes. I also have two boxes on Rt
71 near Norway which are still there as of that date. The clues to all my
boxes are in this group's file section, so you don't have to use the Contact
the Placer to get them from me.
