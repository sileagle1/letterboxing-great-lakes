Went out today and checked on the boxes at Hoffmaster State Park in
Muskegon. My 3 boxes there (Spirit of the Dunes, Walk-A-Mile,
Dutchman's Breeches) are all doing good -- well one of them had to be
relocated because a tree had fallen and crushed the stump where it was
hidden, but at least I found it in one piece.

I also checked in on PJ'S Pleasure, planted by Hummingbird, which was
in good shape.

For anyone who was hoping to still do Scheherezade's boxes at
Hoffmaster SP, looks like she has pulled them as I wasn't able to find
any of them even though I knew where they were as I had previously
found them.

Gorgeous day for a walk along Lake Michigan!
