From: walesflyermary (drewes@WI.RR.com) | Date: 2009-04-29 23:23:39 UTC
Trying to find a mystery LB after much work at home on it, I experienced major frustration today. So, to soothe my LBing soul, I drove across two (technically three) county lines to find some boxes. When I got home, I realized the last one was my 300th find! It was the "Have a cookie...or two!" LB by one our newest LBers here in SE WI - Cuppie! I highly recommend looking for this indoor LB in Rochester. Frank and Nancy are very chatty. Next time I want to find out why the code phrase (no secrets revealed here) is "I'm here to meet Zulu Lavendar".
