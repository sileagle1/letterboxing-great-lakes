I did a Google search and found this: "University of Wisconsin-Waukesha
alumna Pamm Kasper, Greendale, has agreed to join the campus' Friends &
Alumni board, filling a vacancy." Other Internet references indicate
she is a Soil Scientist and avid knitter. Not only is she teaching
the "College for Kids" about letterboxing, there is a similar class
being offered for "Middle School University".

I'm hoping she hides a separate box for them to look for, not one (or
more) of the existing boxes. Not sure how large the classes will be,
but if they look for some of the existing boxes we may need to replace
our logbooks soon.

To answer Cigna Cat's question re: my Retzer box - I haven't checked it
recently but plan to do so next week since someone reported they
couldn't find it. I also need to check on our Toboggoning box. I hope
they're both still in place!
