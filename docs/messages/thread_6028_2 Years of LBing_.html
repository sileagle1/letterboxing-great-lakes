2 Years of LBing!

From: sunnysideupm (sunnysideupm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-08-26 01:30:36 UTC
Today is my 2 year anniversary of letterboxing! We found four boxes on that day - all from the LbGLK Alice in Wonderland series - Why the Rabbit Was Late, Hookah Smoking Caterpillar, Alice's Naughty Kitten and Pool of Tears. Despite a busy day today, I grabbed a drive-by LB to celebrate the day - the Glacial Drumlin LB was 384th find! Thank you to all you planters and fellow letterboxers - this is SO much fun!

Sunny/Mary
