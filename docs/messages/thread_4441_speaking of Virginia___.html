speaking of Virginia...

From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-09-17 12:38:19 UTC-05:00
Atom and I were in Virginia this past weekend for a wedding, and we
managed to schedule our return flight yesterday so we could do a little
hiking and boxing on our way back to the airport. :) We went to the
Bull Run Mountain Conservancy and found all of the boxes hidden in that
park. If anyone gets a chance to get out there, we recommend it. I'm
guessing Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man would agree, since it turned
out they were the last people logged into the boxes we found! :)
Imagine that -- hundreds of miles away from home, and *still* they've
already logged in right before us... except we did manage to find one
that they missed. But anyway, it was definitely a different hiking
experience than we're used to here in the midwest -- incredibly rocky
terrain and an 1000 ft elevation gain! But we managed, and the view
from the top was incredible! Thanks to BullDawg for planting one of the
boxes near the top!

Coffee Beans
