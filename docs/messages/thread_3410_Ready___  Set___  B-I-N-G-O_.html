From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-12-05 17:42:18 UTC
Don't feel that people who can choose from several different boxes,
gatherings, etc. have an advantage in the game. The purpose of that
really is more so that people are choosing from unique subsets of the
words and there is less chance of duplicate cards. This was a common
misconception last year too and in the end, several people who did
have a lot of the extra stuff to choose from and thus used less of
the general words, did not come out nearly as well as some who used
primarily the general terms.

Also when you consider that we are a 6-state region and there are
only 5-10 boxes from any one state in the game, taken somewhat
proportionately from across the different areas of the state, there
will be many people who will not be able to claim any of them for
their list.

When it comes down to it, every word is equal and has an equal chance
of being called, no matter what category it is in.

SpringChick
