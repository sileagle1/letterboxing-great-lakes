From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-05-25 20:36:56 UTC-06:00
P.S. For those who aren't sure where to register for the Friday night
Pre-event get-together and/or the Saturday Post-event get-together, go to:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/database?method=reportRows&tbl=21

(This can also be found by going to the LbGLK message board, clicking on
"Database" in the upper left corner section, then choosing "GLG 2006
Mini-meets". You should now be able to see who has already registered their
plans. To put in your own info, click on "Add Record" near the bottom of
the list. Then enter the number of people, what nights you might attend and
what time you might arrive.
