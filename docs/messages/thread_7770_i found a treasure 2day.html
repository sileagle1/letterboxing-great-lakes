Hi!

The Demon's Dwelling letterbox always stays at Devil's Lake, but people from
all over the country (& world) follow clues to look for it. When someone
finds it they stamp their "signature stamp" into the box logbook. They also
put an image of the box stamp into their personal logbook and then put
everything back where they found it. My husband and I have found over 6,000
boxes all over the world.

It sounds like there may also have been a "hitchhiker" in the box. This is a
special stamp & logbook that DOES travel around. People move this special
stamp from one letterbox to another.

There are several other boxes hidden at Devil's Lake if you want to look for
them. Clues can be found at letterboxing.org.

Thanks for the report & photos!!
