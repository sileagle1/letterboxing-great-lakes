Placed my first box!

From: corvisia (bunnyhugger@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-04-11 16:03:48 UTC

Hello, I have just joined this group. My name is Heather and my husband Chris and I
started letterboxing in February. So far we have found nine boxes and a hitchhiker.
Yesterday we placed our first box -- the first in our hometown of Lansing, Michigan. I
drew the stamp design and my husband carved it (he's much better at that kind of
thing). It had to be a microbox because it's in a well-travelled area with manicured
grounds that don't lend themselves to hiding boxes.

The box is called "Say Yes to Michigan" and you can find the clue at the LbNA site if
you want to look for it.

Heather
