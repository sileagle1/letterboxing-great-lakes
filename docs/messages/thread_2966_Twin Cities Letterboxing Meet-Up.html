Twin Cities Letterboxing Meet-Up

From: cahillcowboy (cahillcowboy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-17 21:25:11 UTC
In an attempt to meet other letterboxers in the southern MN area, and
to measure interest in having reqular get togethers to share
knowledge, stories and promote letterboxing, I am forming a
Letterboxing Meet-Up.
If anyone is interested, please go to www.meetup.com and enter
Minneapolis, then search for "Letterboxing". The first Meetup is
scheduled for Saturday, 16 September at a location to be determined.
I would appreciate it if people would contact me via this group, but
better yet either via Meetup or directly to janmtomj@juno.com.
Look forward to hearing from my fellow letterboxers!
Happy Trails
Cahillcowboy
