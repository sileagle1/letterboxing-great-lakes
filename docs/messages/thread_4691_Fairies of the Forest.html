From: heather (heatherstreets@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-04-29 17:11:08 UTC
We had a great time at the Fairies of the Forest event.
We have never gotten all the boxes at an event, which is why
we decided to make a weekend out of it. At past events we spent a lot
of time exchanging and chatting. Then we never got many boxes, so
this time we concentrated on boxes at the expense of getting to know
our fellow letterboxers.
We still owe Condo our Personal Traveler.

We didn't find Beechnut, Dryad or The Gatekeeper and HH.
We rechecked the clues for Beechnut and the Gatekeeper and feel like
we could find them now. We think the Dryad was already gone by the
time we looked Sunday night.

It was fun boxing with TrailTroll, SpringChick, LunaGirl and Papa and
without them we couldn't have found Cyclops or Indigo Fairy. We
really owe them thanks as we are quite a bit slower with Spartanfans
Junior Varsity in tow.

On Sunday we spent the day boxing with Team Rabbit, together we were
able to get almost all the rest.

Family of 6 shouldn't feel bad about the clues as this was their first
time hosting an event and it was obvious they put a GREAT deal of
effort into it. We felt like we letterboxers were the only ones at
the park and appreciated the mostly stroller friendly trails.

The Fairies of the Forest gathering kicked off the season wonderfully
and we can't wait until the next event. Maybe we will learn how to
balance getting to know our fellow boxers and spending time on the
trails.

Spartanfans
