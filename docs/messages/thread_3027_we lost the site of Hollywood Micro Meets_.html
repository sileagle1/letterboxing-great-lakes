As far as the Matheissen gathering goes, I am still
talking with IDNR to secure the necessary permits.
Let me give you a brief synopsis of working with a
state agency:

Another civic group I belong to held a Bike Ride along
the I & M canal for Habitat for Humanity. Well, the
paperwork was filed and we got approved. The day of
the race a Dept. of Conservation pulled up and asked
one of our checkpoints what they were doing. The CP

the permit. We told them we had the permits. Well
after researching, Dept. of Conservation sent the
permits to the Morris office and not the Utica office,
who cared for the I & M section we were using.

So I am trying to make sure all the ducks are in a row
before I put out an actual gathering announcement.
