Some Bingo Questions...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-22 15:56:40 UTC
I've received a few e-mails with Bingo questions that I thought I
would share with the group because they are good questions and there
are several new players this year.

Q: What if I can't check the group every day, will I miss the words?
A: You can always look back in the group messages to the previous
day, but if you are playing Bingo, you might want to turn on e-mails
or daily digest if you are not already getting group messages. There
is also a database on the group site that lists all of the words. It
is to your advantage to keep up when we are playing as there may be
times when action is required on your part within a limited time window.

Q: What do I do when I get a Bingo?
A: Please e-mail me (letterbox@comcast.net). I will then confirm
against the copy of your card that you mailed me and post notice of
confirmed Bingos to the group. You can certainly gloat and share your
excitement with the group if you want to, but remember that it is not
an official Bingo until I have confirmed against your card... there
have been instances of *false* Bingos in the past.

Q: Do I have to let you know on the exact day that I win? What if I
don't check the list that day and then somebody else wins in the meantime?
A: I suspect that once you get close to a Bingo, you will be making a
point to check for the word each day, however, because life can
sometimes get in the way... you have 3 days from the time of your win
day to claim your Bingo and still have it be effective on the day you
actually won. If you miss this, you can still claim it, but it is
then effective on the day I receive your e-mail.

Q: What if more than one person has a Bingo on the same day?
A: The first year we played, I did a tie-breaker, but last year there
was enough stuff in the Bingo Booty to award all winners in a tie
situation. The prize box is looking great already, so this year I
will do the same thing as last year -- in the case of a tie, all
confirmed bingos on the same day will be winners.

Q. Can I win more than once?
A. Yes, multiple wins are allowed. For instance, if you are the
winner of one of the Interim games (i.e. 4 corners, perimeter, etc.),
you are still in the running for the other games.

I have opened up all of the packages I have received so far (as of
yesterday's mail) and updated the database with status. Thanks for
all the great prize items so far -- this is going to be fun!

Also a big thank you to those who sent holiday wishes and goodies for
the host and her OldShoe... it is much appreciated and I trust these
were solely in the spirit of the season with no intent to bribe ;-)

SpringChick
