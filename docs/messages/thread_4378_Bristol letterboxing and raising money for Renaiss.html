Bristol letterboxing and raising money for Renaissance charity

From: Katelyn Burrill (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-20 19:47:09 UTC
I finally met a group of 'boxers down at the Noble's glade at Bristol
last Saturday, although my new outfit limits me from carrying my
stamp with me! :-)

The Wenches Guild (including myself) are trying to raise money for
the RESCU foundation, a not-for-profit charity that focuses on
helping Renaissance Faire workers when they are injured or very ill.
More information at www.rescufoundation.com. We are competing with
other groups to raise the most money for RESCU in two weeks, which
ends on Saturday, August 25.

I am asking all of my friends, family, and groups to help me raise
money for this terrific charity. To sweeten the pot for my
fellow 'boxers, I will be creating a special stamp for everyone who
helps me by giving a donation.

The easiest way to donate is by sending funds to my PayPal account
(kburrall [at] trcsolutions [dot] com. The account was empty when I
started this last week, so all funds will be withdrawn and given to
RESCU as a check. I do have a premier account, so it will take credit
cards. :-) I will send the stamp image to everyone who donates and
gives me a mailing address!

If you will be at Faire on Saturday, Aug. 25, I will working at
Camelot Fans and will have the stamp with me there and accepting
donations all day!

Every dollar helps out in so many ways. Please give a buck or two if
you can. Thanks!
-- Kate
