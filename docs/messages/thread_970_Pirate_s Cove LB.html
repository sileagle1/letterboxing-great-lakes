For those of you that may be going to search for Pirate's Cove in
the next couple of days, I would strongly suggest against it. The
reason is that with the heavy rain, not only is the trail very soft
and slick, there is also the chance of there being parts of it
underwater. I didn't get a chance to grab it before the rain
storm, so the box may have decided to set sail for somewhere else.
When I placed it, I believed it was up high enough to where there
shouldn't be a problem, but this was a out of ordinary rain storm.

As soon as the trail opens back up, I will go and check on the box
and let everyone know the status.

Sil Boxer

PS: If anyone does happen to be searching for a letterbox and comes
across the pirate, please email me and let me know you have him. I
