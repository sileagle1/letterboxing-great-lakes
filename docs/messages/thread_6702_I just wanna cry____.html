Well, we woke up this morning to 13 inches of rain in our finished basement. As we were hauling things out of the water, I noticed that my husband's logbook and my son's logbook had fallen into the water.
:~( Apparently, they had been on top of something else in the basement and slipped off when THAT object tipped with the rising waters. Lots of abstract art instead of stamp images. I also lost the very first big counted cross stitch projects that I had ever done. I'm trying to soak it now, but it is not looking good.

And to add insult to injury, we were going to do the Harry Potter Scavenger Hunt at the Art Institute this afternoon. That certainly wasn't going to happen. Fortunatly, when I called them and gave them my sad story, they waived the no refund policy, and gave us a credit for another hunt in the next 12 months.
