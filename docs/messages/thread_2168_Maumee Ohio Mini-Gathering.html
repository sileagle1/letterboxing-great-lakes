Maumee Ohio Mini-Gathering

From: LindaFay (coachlindafay@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-13 03:43:25 UTC
A RI Celebrity Boxer is coming to Ohio! Yes, GretchenF featured in
the RI East Bay News
http://www.eastbayri.com/story/285089066837355.php will be visiting
Ohio on January 21.

Okay, so she is my daughter and I think she is the grandest, but she
and I will be hosting a mini-gathering for exchanges (she has already
carved an event stamp).

This gathering will take place at the location of the "Is it time for
Tea?" letterbox. Now, if you are unable to solve the location, email
me off-list and I will help you.

I have reserved 8 places for "Tea and Sweets" at 3:15pm. In the last
year, many more letterboxes have arrived in Lucas County, so if the
weather is fine, come early and look for some boxes. Aunt Phoebe's
purse will be added as a bonus box to the current Aunt Phoebe's
Letterbox. Details to follow.

3:15 PM January 21, Saturday Mini Gathering Maumee, OH!

LindaFay
Linda_of_Maumee
