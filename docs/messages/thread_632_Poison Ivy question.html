Re: Poison Ivy question

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-09-02 16:40:00 UTC
I always just toss everything in the washer and wash with regular
detergent and hot water. Since having got PI a couple of time, I
make it a habit to:

1) Cover the vehicle seat with a sheet when I am headed out to LB in
a wooded or beachy area
2) Wipe down exposed skin with baby wipes as soon as I am out of the
woods and before getting into the car (hand, face, neck)
3) Toss all clothing and the sheet from the vehicle into the washer
as soon as I get home
4) Shower immediately after getting home
5) Wipe down shoes/boots with soapy cloth and put out in the sun to
dry

Sometimes I throw my pack in the washer too if I know I have been in
PI that was high enough to have touched my pack.

Haven't had another outbreak and I know I have been in some areas
with a lot of it around.

SpringChick
