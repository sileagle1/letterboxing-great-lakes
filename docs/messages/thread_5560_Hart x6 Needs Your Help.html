Hart x6 Needs Your Help

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2009-01-19 03:30:20 UTC
I need your creativity (who better to ask than letterboxers??).

I've had this idea for a series of boxes floating around in my head
for a year and a half now and its time!

Letterboxes are not allowed in certain parks and places, some owned
by the state and other spaces owned by the nation.

Clues on LBNA are looked at by such authorities and now and then we
get messages from Rangers and other "powers that be".

I've always had a rebellious streak in me, so I've wanted to carve a
series of stamps titled "Pesky Park Ranger," to be placed in said
areas.

Here's where you come in. I would like pictures of you, my fellow
boxers, taking on your most menacing pose (arms crossed, finger
pointed, billy club in hand, etc) if you have some clothing
resembling the uniforms worn by our trusty rangers that would be
GREAT (and helpful).

Email me a picture of yourself and I will (eventually) carve a stamp
of your efforts. These stamps will be placed somewhere . . . here or
there . . . who knows.

I am sending this call out to you my fellow Great Lakers first and
see what response I get back, then I will probably open this call out
to other message boards around the nation (my ultimate goal).

Thanks,
hx6
