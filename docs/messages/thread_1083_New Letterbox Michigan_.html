New Letterbox Michigan!

From: clickermom3 (clickermom3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-20 13:00:37 UTC

Hi
I decided to welcome spring with a letterbox!
FYI for letterbox "purists". The stamps, there are two, are not hand
carved. The log book IS hand made. No room for a pencil so bring your
own. The name of the letterbox is "Just us Chicks!" I would call it
an easy box, placed in a picnic area with flat terrain and less than
a half mile total walking. The ground may be a bit damp in wet
weather.
It was planted 3/19/05 at Willow Metropark, which is southeast of
Bellville and northwest of Flat Rock. To get to the park, take I275
to exit #13 (Sibley Road/New Boston)Take Sibley rd east 3.1 miles to
Vining Rd and turn right. Go 3.2 miles to the T-intersection and veer
right onto Willow rd. Drive 1.2 miles to the park entrance on the
right.

Here's the clues:
After entering the park, turn right past the toll booth and then
turn left into the parking lot for the Flint Woods picnic areas C.
Begin your quest facing east.
South drinking fountain
North on the trail
Stop for a snack if you're hungry
NE corner of your stopping place
Look for the victory sign at 110 degrees
Go there
The victory sign's behind you
Look 110 degrees
45 footfalls to your goal
Look low
You've found it!

good luck

Clickermom3
