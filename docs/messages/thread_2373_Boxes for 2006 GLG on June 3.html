Hello everyone!

I figured I'd send another reminder to those of you who volunteered
to make boxes for the event. I have already received 9 boxes
from "out of towners" and wanted to remind the rest of you that we'd
like to have all the boxes in our hands by April 30 (about 3 weeks
away). That will give us several weekends in May to get out and hide
those boxes. When you're ready to send the box, just contact me for
my mailing address.

We really appreciate the local boxers placing their own boxes since
that will lighten the load of planting AND it gives us a few to look
for ourselves! If you're planting your own box, please email me the
clues by May 26 or sooner if you want them included in the clue
booklet.

In case you haven't looked at the event website

http://www.2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com

in a while, we will probably have 40+ boxes and are approaching 100
expected attendees.

As the event gets closer, let me know if you decide on what you'd
like to bring for the potluck. You can check the website to see what
people are already planning to bring and see some general suggestions
for additonal items.

I'll be posting some more info about the three workshops within the
next week.

Happy boxing (and carving)!
