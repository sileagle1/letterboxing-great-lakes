Michigan Geo-Caching

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-03-22 11:23:52 UTC
Hi everyone! I am not really an active geo-cacher, although since
there are many more caches than letterboxes near me, I have
considered embracing them also, however I do lurk at the MiGO
(Michigan Geo-Caching Organization) web site from time to time
because they have a finger of the pulse of environmental and land
usage issues throughout the state.

So, even if you are a plain vanilla letterboxer, you should check
out the site. There is a link over in the left-hand sidebar that
deals with Land Usage -- where it is okay to places caches (i.e.
same rules generally would apply to letterboxes) and where there has
been a specific ban on caches/letterboxes. Very helpful when
planning your next box placement if you want to avoid the
disappointment of having the box confiscated.

http://www.mi-geocaching.org/index.php
