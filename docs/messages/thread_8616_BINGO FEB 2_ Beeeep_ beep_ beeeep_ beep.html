From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2018-02-02 06:54:49 UTC-06:00
Dad said to mom the other day,
A baby's language is hard to say,
Should we try and translate the verse?
Her answer to him was a little terse.

"Does it really bother you so?
Out the door, please just go!"

The point was missed,
Dropped, per say.
And left for another day.

She looked at Morse,
Her son,
Dreaming of his message.
Of thinking of the sounds,
That must be hard to passage.
