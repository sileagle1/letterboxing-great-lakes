Get ready: GLG this Saturday!

From: Archaeo Frog (archaeofrog@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-06-04 21:19:56 UTC-05:00
Dear all,

I am sending this out to the list as well as posting on AQ. Sorry for the
repeat (and for those unable to join us)!

We are so excited to have you join us this Saturday for the 2012 Great
Lakes Gathering: Drawn to Letterboxing at Blue Mound State Park outside of
Madison! Clue packets will be available at the shelter starting at 9 am.
There are bigger parking lots near both the west and east towers of the
park, and the main shelter is located near the east tower, right when you
come up the hill. Every car will need a Wisconsin State Park annual or
daily pass, available from the Ranger Station when you enter. (Annual is
$25 WI or $35 non-resident plates. Daily is $7 WI or $10 non-resident).

Lunch is potluck style, and thank you in advance to all of you who have
already signed up for something on the AQ Event Listing. We will be
providing brats and hotdogs and ask for your help in filling out the menu.
Please also set your watches, as we would like to take a group picture of
everyone at NOON at the shelter.

Otherwise, the day is yours. There will be food, exchanges, and likely a
few table tops at the shelter and more than 80 boxes to keep you busy in
the park! (Make sure you have enough space in your logbooks for all these
incredible images.)

Only a few mosquitoes have been spotted so far, but you will want to keep
your eyes out for poison ivy in some areas. Trails can stay muddy for days
after rain too. If you really want to get wet, though, be sure to bring
your suit, because the pool will be open! ($1-2 fee, children and adults).
If plans are made for dinner after, details will be left at the shelter.

Also, if you are in town early, the Minnesota Camping Contingent is kindly
planning a Friday evening get-together, details at
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2530 .

See you on Saturday!

Archaeofrog and the Verona Bird Patrol
