We've enjoyed the sunny warm FL sunshine for 2 days so far. We didn't see any folks from our region stamped into the boxes we found yesterday, but today was a different story. It seems the Verona Bird Patrol was at the beach on Treasure Island less than 2 weeks ago! We only hunted 2 boxes today while we were visiting friends, but the VBP were logged into both. We also saw the HowD Girls logged into one of them last year.

It's always fun to see some familiar stamps while we are traveling!
