A Whole Day of Letterboxing!!

From: jenm2kk (jenm2kk@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-08-18 11:02:06 UTC
Hi everyone!! I'm new-ish to the group, but I thought you, of all people might be able to appreciate my excitement.

I started letterboxing 2 years ago, and probably have around 50 finds. I usually tote my 4 year old along with me. So, when all the stars align (weather is good, toddler mood is good, time permits, and there's a box we haven't found within close driving distance) I'm lucky if he is patient enough for 1 find, sometimes super lucky and we get two in one day.

Well, as for today, I have a sitter, and the day off work, and my sister and I have plotted a course a bit further away than I would go with my boy. I am very excited to get out there! I love my boy, and I love that he likes to go with me, but I also love the fact that today - for the whole day - I get to go with someone who doesn't start whining about going back to the playground we passed on the way in!! (At least I hope she doesn't!)

Happy boxing!!

jenm2k
