RE: [LbGLK] Re: Create & Carve One week away!

From: Doglvrs RASW (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2007-02-15 20:12:36 UTC
Well, you need to bring $$ for pizza lunch, and your own beverage.
If you want to carve a stamp -- go to Michaels or Hobby Lobby and pick up a
Speedball stamp carving kit -- it comes with a pink slab of carving medium,
a carving handle and 3 (or 4) carving tips. This is a great way to get
started with short notice...

If you'd like -- you can bring along some black & white images to share with
others...otherwise, there should be some there for you to start out with.
