Chris and I went to Kings Island for the fourth of July weekend, and
we decided to get a letterbox near our motel before we left town.
This box, StampawayUSAbox (Sharonville, OH), was unfortunately not in
very good condition. Because we didn't come prepared for repairs and
we don't live in the area, we sadly had to leave it as is. When we
got back, I sent a status report to the placer (franzsolo) through the
LbNA site, but haven't heard back, so I don't know if it was
received. Given the condition of the box, I am going to guess it is
not being maintained much anymore.

So, I thought I would write a note to this group in case there are any
Cincinnati-area boxers here who might be able to give the box some
TLC, and so people who intend to find the box can come prepared.

The main problem is that the container is rather beat up and obviously
no longer watertight. The contents were very damp when we found
them. As a result, the wood backing on the stamp has warped, making
it difficult to get a good impression. Also, the log is full. There
are various loose pages in the box that finders have added.

So, in my opinion, StampawayUSAbox is in need of:
1) a new log
2) a new container
3) a new backing for the stamp
4) drying out of the existing log


Heather F.
