From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-08-11 14:38:12 UTC-05:00
We'll be there! Unlike some other downtown concerts, you ARE allowed to
bring your own picnic food & beverages (including alcoholic beverages) to
this concert site. There are also places to purchase food & beverages at
the concert if you don't bring anything, so it's your choice! Just bring a
blanket or some lawn chairs along and you'll be ready for the concert.

There is street parking available near the park and I believe after 6 pm it
is free. In addition, here's a site that shows some other parking options
near the park:
http://www.parkmilwaukee.com/destinations/7-pere-marquette-park


We'll probably be at a concert there next week, so we'll scope out a place
that might be good to designate as a meeting place and post it next
Thursday. If anyone that will be meeting up with us on AUG 25 wants our
cell phone numbers in case they can't find us, send me a private email.

Here's some info on the group that is playing:

August 25 - Five Card Studs http://www.fivecardstuds.com

The Five Card Studs returns to River Rhythms for a night of fun to prove
they are "the hardest working lounge band in the Midwest"

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QKwNoVwg5EA


Wisconsin Hiker
