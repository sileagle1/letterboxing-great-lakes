DUDE! (sorry) For some reason, we always say "I hate white rabbits" whenever the smoke is blowing at us. People not in the family always look at us like we're crazy, but then the wind shifts, and the smoke is no longer in our eyes- ha ha!! who looks so crazy THEN? (I know, still me). But I never knew where or why that phrase came into family vernacular. You say it's from the Girl Scouts? Curiouser and curiouser, considering no one in the family's ever been a girl scout.

Now I'm going to have to research this one deeper!
