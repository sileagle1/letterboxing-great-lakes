Sounds like we missed a wonderful time at the Gathering this weekend.

We were busy winterizing for my mother-in-law up North and when The
Foot demanded my attention, I went indoors and put on an icepack while
I took my old (filled up) logbook apart and sort of scrapbooked the
entries.

I discovered that I reached X100 at the 2005 GLK in Hudson Mills Metro
Park! Yeah .. now I'm at 100 (at least) on all 3 milestones (PFX)!!

Our CT visiting dignitaries Mark and Sue Peppe were close at x99 but
Tiptoe and Tonto are the lucky ones to be my exact X100.

I stopped counting my P and F numbers after they reached 100 so I
guess I'll stop with the X now, too.

Thanks to everyone who helped me reach this milestone (as well as the
others)

Enjoy!
---PJ
