From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-03-25 22:49:02 UTC-06:00
OK, here are a few suggestions for the WI portion of your trip, roughly
south to north:

Burke Park
Mounds of the Midwest Series: Man Mound (a bit further off I94, but perhaps
you could stop on your way home if you have time)
Echo Rock
Mirror Lake
Wildcat Mound Wolf (not too far off I94, but a bit more of a hike -
approximately 1 hour)
Bell Mound (a must do since it actually is in a rest stop - can't get
easier/closer than that!)
Elk Mound

Have a great time!

Wisconsin Hiker
