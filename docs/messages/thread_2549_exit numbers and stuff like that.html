Hi!

Yes, Trecker Lodge in Homestead Hollow is #9 on the park map. There are
signs in the park directing you to Homestead Hollow, so it shouldn't be too
difficult. To get to the park, take Exit 285 off I-94, then head south on
Co. Highway "C" for 3/4 miles to the park.

Yes, Delafield Brewhaus is Exit 287 off of I-94. This is Hwy 83.

We'll be out to the park again today. After that I'll post detailed
directions for getting to the pre- and post- meeting places.
