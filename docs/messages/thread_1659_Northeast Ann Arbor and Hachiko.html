We went letterboxing for the first time in a while on Friday. Since
we've exhausted the local (Lansing) letterbox population, we went to
Ann Arbor to get a couple of boxes, "Northeast Ann Arbor"
and "Hachiko." The former involved a fun hike, but I would heartily
recommend that future finders remember to bring insect repellant --
don't make the same mistake we did and get eaten alive!

The latter has an amazing stamp, but unfortunately the logbook was
missing. I wonder what happened?
