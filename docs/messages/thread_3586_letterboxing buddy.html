From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-01-16 19:33:42 UTC
I have created a Boxing Buddies database. If anyone has suggestions
for format, additional info needed, etc. please let me know. This is
just intended to get you hooked up with people in your area -- you
can talk specifics with people you contact when you try to arrange
boxing dates.

Columns:

Name - real name or trail name, whichever you are comfortable with
Where You Live - preferably city, state
Travel Radius - the distance you would be willing to travel to meet
up with a boxing partner (this is one way distance -- in other words,
the distance you would travel get to the place where you will meet to
letterbox).
Best Time to Box -- Generally speaking, the best time for you to
letterbox.
Other Info - anything else that may be important, such as if you box
with children, pets or have limitations, preferences, etc.
Contact Info - whatever you prefer, e-mail, phone, cell phone, etc.

SpringChick
