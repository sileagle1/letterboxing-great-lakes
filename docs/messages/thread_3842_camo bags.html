I looked at Wal-Mart a few months ago during hunting season and the guy told
me they didn't stock anything like that and told me to look at Gander
Mountain or some outdoor type store.

I asked Diamonds in the Rough where they found there's and they wrote me

shoelace as a drawstring.

tape. I'm not sure what that is but maybe an alternative to the mesh bags.

Though the bags are nice for hanging in trees.
