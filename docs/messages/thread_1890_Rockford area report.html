It seems the "Contact the Placer" probably isn't working on LBNA, so I'm
posting a general note re: our boxing last Sunday (OCT 9) in the Rockford,
IL area. Here's a list of the boxes we attempted:


8th Illinois Cavalry 1 Laughing Coyote Cherry Valley IL Winnebago Box
in good shape
Huron Lightship 1 Laughing Coyote Cherry Valley IL Winnebago Box in
good shape
The Howling 1 Laughing Coyote Cherry Valley IL Winnebago Box in good
shape
Cartoons of the 80's 5 RaqsEnigma Rockford IL Winnebago Found all 5,
emailed placer
On the Waterfront 1 RaqsEnigma Rockford IL Winnebago Box a bit damp
but OK
Just Face It Series 7 Shutterfly Loves Park IL Winnebago Couldn't find
#1, so decided to skip the rest this trip
Creatures of Blackhawk #1 2 Shutterfly Rockford IL Winnebago Couldn't
find either box
A-Maze-Ing 1 Shutterfly Rockford IL Winnebago Couldn't find
Kinnickinnick 1 Trail Rider Caledonia IL Boone Closed due to hunting
Oak Ridge #1 1 Trail Rider Cherry Valley IL Winnebago Box in good
shape
Sgt. Snuffy 1 Trail Rider Rockford IL Winnebago Box in good shape
FISH TANK: Fish 1 Wavecutter Belvidere IL Boone Box in good shape
FISH TANK: Castle 1 Wavecutter Cherry Valley IL Winnebago Box in good
shape
Cardinal feeder 1 Wavecutter Rockford IL Winnebago Couldn't find
FISH TANK: Fish bowl 1 Wavecutter Rockford IL Winnebago Box in good
shape


Thanks to everyone who is planting boxes!
