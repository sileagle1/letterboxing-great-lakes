You are inspirational! The Beatles Letterbox Series is really good series. I like nice easy hikes along rivers. I really don't want to retire this series. Maybe what the Open Lands Association doesn't know won't hurt them. Actually, they told me they 'highly discourage games" because of sensitive planting areas but they didn't absolutely prohibit letterboxing under penalty of fine or conviction. Don't ask/don't tell, out of sight/out of mind...etc. And my boxes aren't in sensitive planting areas. I know better. Be friendly to mother nature - the letterboxer's motto. Maybe I should just replace the Paul box and let the fab four and that great musical clue (which took me a long time to create) live on........yeah yeah yeah yeah!

It was overcast and looked like rain so I didn't go out to retrieve the three remaining boxes. Maybe I won't. Thanks for the alternative idea!
