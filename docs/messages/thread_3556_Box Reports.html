Hey All! Haven't posted in quite some time but I have been trying to
keep up with reading the board. Gotta know the newest gathering info :)

After receiving a couple reports on some of my boxes, I went out today
to check on a couple of them.

Adult Swim Series: Futurama is doing fine. Had to replace the logbook
though since it was completely filled up.

Adult Swim Series: Family Guy had to be pulled for some MAJOR box
maintenance. The stamp is MIA and the logbook has been reduced to
loose pages. A special thanks goes out to Atom 118 & Coffeebeans for
salvaging what was left of the box. I will recarve and replace this
one as soon as I can.

Catapulted Cow had to be pulled for maintenance as well. This poor
letterbox has the worst luck ever! This time is was discovered by
forest critters. When we got to the tree that houses the box, we just
started pulling out random pieces. First came a chewed baggie with the
logbook inside. The logbook was wet and dirty but it should be
salvagable. Then came the stamp in another chewed baggie. The stamp
is completely ok - just had to peel the semi-frozen papertowel off of
it. Then came the chewed bottom of the box - filled with slushy
water. And we never even found the top to the box. But never fear -
this one doesn't need too much work so I should be able to get it back
out rather quickly.

Hope this finds you all well and I'll see ya on the trails :)
