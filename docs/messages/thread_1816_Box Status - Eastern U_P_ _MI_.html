Box Status - Eastern U.P. (MI)

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-09-13 00:38:07 UTC
OldShoe and I spent a wonderful weekend doing a bit of boxing in the
Eastern Upper Peninsula this past weekend. Here is a run-down of
boxes found -- all were in good condition, unless otherwise noted:

*Demond Fire Tower (Raco)
*Brimley's Whitefish Point (Brimley) -- This box was found laying
uncovered alongside the trail. We attempted to conceal it with bark
and sticks when we left.
*Ursus Americanus (Newberry) -- this box was laying out in the open
and I couldn't really find a way to disguise it much when putting it
back -- not sure exactly where it is supposed to be "hidden" as
there didn't appear to be any actual hiding spots there.
*Big Pine (Paradise)
*The Seney Stretch (Seney) -- This box was also found laying in the
open. The people who were stamped in last also stamped in a HH and
appear to have left it, however, there was no HH in the box. Not
sure if they stamped it and then forgot to put it in the box, or if
somebody came along afterward and took the HH, stamped into it's
logbook and forgot to stamp into the actual box log.
*Big Knob (Engadine)
*Cut River Bridge (Epoufette)
*Under the Northern Lights (St. Ignace)
*Sail Away (Mackinaw City) - logbook is full
*MI CA-T (Mackinaw City) - logbook is full


In addition, the following boxes were replaced and updated clues
have been posted:

Hiawatha Series: Gitche Gumee (Bay Mills)
Point Iroquois Lighthouse (Bay Mills)


Thanks to the placers for some fun boxes! On to the Western U.P. in
a couple of weeks!

SpringChick
