From: junk4joann (junk4joann@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-07 23:21:34 UTC
I get really nervous when (even careful) letterboxers plant boxes in a
wildlife nature preserve. I just can't live with my own guilty
feelings involved with stepping on plants or flowers in a preserve in
order to track down a box. And, yes, I'm aware that occasional
footsteps on plants don't really harm the ecosystem. However, I've
seen areas trampled quite thoroughly by people searching for caches
and boxes that probably shouldn't be there to begin with. So, I guess
I'm not completely hooked... just sayin'. :)

LG.
