Hi Angela,

The May 1st deadline was for getting boxes in place, but I am not really
holding to that too tightly, I just needed some kind of a timeframe so I
would have the information necessary to put the web page together. If you
have additional boxes you would like included, please e-mail me the
information for them and I will add them.

I too am really tied up with job-related things and haven't made as much
progress on the Letterboxing 'Round the Lake web page as I would have liked.
I am hoping to get a few hours this weekend to finish it up. In that case I
would still be able to get the link out the the tourist associations before
the first of June.

I am open to adding more boxes along the route on and ongoing basis, as long
as they meet the criteria and are kept maintainted, etc. It really takes
minimal work on my part to put in additional boxes once the initial page is
made. I think the more boxes there are along the route, the more people
will be interested. So, the more the merrier!

SpringChick
