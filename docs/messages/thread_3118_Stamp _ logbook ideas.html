From: (kevjean@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-21 23:32:08 UTC
In our "Tribe" LOL there are 8 of us that go on the hunt for boxes. I decided to let each of the kids pick out their own stamp, and each of them has their own logbook. My parents each have a stamp of their own, but they share their logbook. I figure if they ever keep this addiction...I mean hobby going when they get older, then they are ready to go. =) We have one trail name for us all, but they each use their individual nicknames when we do the postal letterboxing rings with the family trail name.
