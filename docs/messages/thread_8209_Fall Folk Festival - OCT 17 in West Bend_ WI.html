Fall Folk Festival - OCT 17 in West Bend, WI

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-07-06 19:49:50 UTC-07:00

Well, now that the GLG and the 4th of July holiday has come and gone, I'm starting to get things more organized for the Fall Folk Festival event.


Later in July I'll post a link to a web site, but before that YOU can choose a country to represent at the event! We would like all stamps and all potluck food items to be related to a specific country.  The stamp images could be a person from that country, a flag, a building, a symbol, a food, a tradition or ANYTHING that you feel is related to the country.  It can be one box or a series of boxes.


If you live near West Bend (or will be making a visit to the area), you are welcome to plant your own box and then provide the clues to me for inclusion in the clue packet.  Otherwise we'd be happy to plant any boxes you give/mail to us by SEPTEMBER 12.


IMPORTANT NOTE: In order to get a "passport" to the event, at least one person in your group needs to create a box to be planted on the trails. But to make it even more fun, why doesn't EACH person carve something?  We know you can do it!


I've created a database on the Great Lakes Yahoo message board to keep track of the countries that will be represented.  It is OK if more than one person chooses the same country (but you might want to coordinate with them to ensure you have unique stamp images).  Just add a row to the database to enter your trail name, county, stamp image/title and potluck food contribution.  When deciding on your food item, keep in mind that the park lodge has a serving kitchen with a refrigerator, sink and microwave (NO stovetop or oven). Plenty of electrical outlets should be available.
