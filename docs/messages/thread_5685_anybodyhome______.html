Anybodyhome, you ask? Answer: We're ALL at home, of course. Where
else would we be? Certainly not out on the trail. Cabin fever hit us
hard this year with early snow, heavy snowfalls, ice, and now rain!

This weekend, I'm ready to do something totally foolish and I don't
even care what the weather will be. I have new boxes to place. I
have clues in hand for boxes that were places right before the snow
that hit in early December. I just couldn't get to them. I predict
that the first warm and sunny weekend, we will ALL be on the trails
tripping over each other.

Yes, SILO helped to lift our spirits and get us ready for spring
letterboxing. Thank you to everyone who came and especially Lock for
an inspirtational workshop that resulted in many light bulb moments.
Spring will be here soon. I feel stiff; a symptom of cabin fever
also associated with coach potato syndrome. My legs need to walk and
wander. I want to see spring flowers peeking up through the soil.
And letterboxes....Stand back and let me at 'em! Boxes! I need to
find a box! It's not an addiction and I can stop at any time.

Sing a long....I'm boxing in the rain. Just boxing in the rain. What
a glorious feeling I'm boxing again.
Beetle
