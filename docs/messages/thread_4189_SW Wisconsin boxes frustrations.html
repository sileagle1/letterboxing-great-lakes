SW Wisconsin boxes frustrations

From: mkeaims (aimlesst@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-06-21 12:44:21 UTC
Greetings all,

I was able to visit my boxes out in Grant County over the weekend. I
had planted two boxes last summer during the race to plant in every
county. I got emails from first finders on both boxes and then they
were both promptly reported missing! (Very discouraging.) I had heard
from an uncle that the location of one box was gone for sure, the town
had gone in and cleared out the deadfall last fall. So I recarved my
two stamps and got their boxes ready. Plus I had two new boxes to
plant. I'm all set for the trip out there.
We get out to my aunt and uncle's home and get settled in. We stopped
and replaced Channel Cat. We head over to Bald Eagle Days, and the box
is exactly where it is supposed to be! I had thought that this was a
very straight forward box, so when it was reported missing, I figured
it really was gone and recarved it. So now I have a reserve stamp in
case it ever does go missing. I planted one of my two new boxes, but
there was a pretty intense thunderstorm that went through, so I ended
up not getting the second one planted.
So my score for the trip, I went out with 4 boxes to plant and brought
2 home with me!

From: twila75 (twila75@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-25 15:35:32 UTC
I've worried about being "that LBer" who contacts the placer and
reports the box missing, only to find out I'm just a dolt who
couldn't figure out some simple clues. So far I haven't been
though, fortunately, even after being the one to report a couple
boxes missing. My mom and I looked for one box within probably a
200-yard radius of where we thought it was supposed to be, just in
case we were looking in the wrong tree stump. I've found that I can
pretty easily convince myself that I must be completely blind and
dumb while following some LBing clues. Although, I sincerely hope
I'm not the only LBer who is so easily convinced. :/

Glad to hear that at least one of your boxes was still there! I'm
sure I'll be experiencing the same worries/frustrations after I
start placing my first boxes. :)
