new Lb crawl box coming soon

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-11-05 23:53:01 UTC-06:00
I'm sorry to have to tease you guys this way, but I'll be placing a new box
on the Burlington Northern Santa Fe line -- but not until Monday. It is
already listed in the database for the Metra Letterbox Crawl, and the clues
will be in my FungusWoman clues folder in the files (I do take a chance and
post these clues on LbNA in their entirety).

Also take a look in the files if you are new and aren't familiar with what
I'm talking about. The Crawl is a series of boxes being placed in the
Chicago area along the various Metra commuter railroad lines. (I started,
but am not sole "proprietor" of the concept...everyone is welcome to place
boxes in the series.) I've modified the original concept ( and updated the
file) of only "password-protected" indoor boxes, since not every station has
a suitable indoor facility. So conventional "clue-driven" boxes are also
welcome to be considered part of the series. If you have one that fits the
bill (ie, CMoon's "History Tower" or my "Hurray for Hollywood"), you might
want to list it in the database.

To view or download a train schedule, go to
http://metrarail.com/schedule.html. Metra offers an unlimited weekend pass
good for all the boxing you can do on Saturday and Sunday for $5.00 (well,
they dont really market it as a boxing pass.). With the cost of gas these
days, that's a pretty good deal. Even you Wisconsinians can pick up the
train in Kenosha and come on down.

(I have no affiliation with Metra, BTW, except that I live right along the
tracks!)

--klk/FungusWoman
