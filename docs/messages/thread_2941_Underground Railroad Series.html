Underground Railroad Series

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-08-13 16:41:13 UTC
Saturday was a beautiful day for boxing--we found 3 boxes (and a HH)
and had the time to plant 2 boxes. We decided to do a series on the
Underground Railroad, which I have always been interested in. I read
a book about the Underground RR in Illinois and was surprised by all
the nearby sites. So we decided to plant some boxes by some authentic
sites. The stamps will all be quilt patterns that are supposedly
connected with the UGRR (I say supposedly because some historians
think this quilt code is real and others think it is fiction). Either
way, it is interesting and gives us ideas for stamp images to carve.
I like quilts, but since I can't sew I guess I'm left with carving
quilt pieces.

Anyway, we hope people will enjoy visiting these historic sites--and
finding a letterbox to boot. The first 2 boxes are in the
Maywood/River Forest area and in Lombard. Hopefully it won't take us
too long to explore some other sites and plant some boxes.
