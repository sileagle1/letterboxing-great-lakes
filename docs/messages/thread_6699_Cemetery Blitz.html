Cemetery Blitz

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2010-07-20 04:23:58 UTC
Since my husband and kids are off at New Orleans without me (they are at a huge youth gathering for our church denomination), I decided to indulge myself with some boxing. Since I enjoy cemeteries, I decided to tackle the Egyptian Hieroglyph Series (by Control_Freq)--each one is planted in a different cemetery. The carves are awesome, and the variety of cemeteries was fun to see--my favorites are the older ones, and there were lots of them.

If I posted the following "stats" on Facebook, my friends would think I had been in the sun too long, but I figured that you would understand:
**13 hours
**12 towns
**19 cemeteries
**13 stamps
**2 HH
**1 very fun day

I also planted a box--clues yet to come. It is in a cemetery (so it could be a Cemetery Stroll), but it is also about an underground railroad site (so it could be in the UGRR/Quilt Series). Which one to choose???

BirdWoman/WP
