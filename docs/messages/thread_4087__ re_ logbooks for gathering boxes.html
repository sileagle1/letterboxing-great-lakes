From: ruhlette (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-23 16:50:03 UTC-07:00
I have two handmade logbooks for my boxes. For Rosa I have 96 pages (8
signatures with 6 sheets each); if space is an issue, letterboxers can
stamp both sides. I won't mind. Fun with Dick and Jane is a larger book
with 72 pages. My personal book better be big enough .. it has 192
pages!!

I've already calculated there are more than 166! Some of the attendees
with boxes listed in our database have not signed up at AQ, but they have
camping spots. My not-so-little spreadsheet will be printed in the next
couple of days. So anyone getting ideas for last minute PT's, I just
might miss'm this round. I have 42 with answers!

prepared for the excursion); we'll go back this fall.
