remembering Boxer (in TRAINing)

From: Kathi (klkrank@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-03 05:26:15 UTC
I just saw this message Ryan posted on my Dad's tribute page on AQ:

"I never had the pleasure of meeting Boxer in TRAINing, but I loved
reading about the stories everyone who has met him had to tell.
Funguswoman asked that contributes be sent to AQ in his memory--a
rather unexpected and touching request--and I wanted to save it for
something useful for the continued development of this site. I
scratched my head quite a bit wondering what worthy purpose I could
put the donations towards, and finally decided to upgrade my decade-
old version of Paint Shop Pro 5 to the recently released Paint Shop
Pro 11. Next time you see a new theme on Atlas Quest, or new photos
used in a tutorial or on a blog--remember Boxer in TRAINing. The
contributions in his memory helped make them possible. Thanks to
all, and especially to Boxer and Funguswoman."

Pretty cool, eh? BTW, if any of you would like to see the tribute
page, it's located at
http://www.atlasquest.com/people/tribute/obit.html?gMemberId=4086

I placed the first of a series of three letterboxes in memory of him
today in Illinois (clues coming shortly). A second box is being
sent to WI Hiker and Martini Man for hiding in Wisconsin (sorry,
Michigan!) and a third is being sent to my cousins ZEPE in Texas,
since WI and TX were two places he rather extensively hunted down
boxes.

--klk/FungusWoman
