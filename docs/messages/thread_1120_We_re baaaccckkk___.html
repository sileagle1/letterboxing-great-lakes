Talk about rotten luck! A gorgeous 70 degree day in the Twin Cities on

boxes. Thursday's weather was gorgeous again (for our drive home). We got

there as a simple drive-though. I am looking for someone to adopt that box
however, since I doubt I will be passing that way again. Any takers??
