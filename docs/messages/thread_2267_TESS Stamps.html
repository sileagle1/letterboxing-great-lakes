TESS Stamps

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-22 13:50:06 UTC
As many of you might remember, a while ago this group put together a
collection of carvings for the Great Lakes Travelling Event Stamp
Showcase -- or TESS. There are a total of 15 wonderful carvings, done
by members of this group, based on the theme "Made in My State." The
stamps are accompanied by a unique handmade log book, which also
serves as the box.

This collection of event stamps made its debut at the 2006 Great Lakes
Gathering and was also in attendance at the MI Fall Mystery Gathering
yesterday. It is available for any event in the region. If you are
hosting a gathering and are interested, please e-mail me and I will be
happy to send them out to you.
