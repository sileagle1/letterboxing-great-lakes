From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-01-18 11:54:07 UTC-06:00
I believe the main reason Team Taco went to the "trivia question" approach was that they believed someone was intentionally vandalizing their boxes. The trivia question then let them know who had access to the clues, and that the only ones to have the clues would be valid letterboxers, not the general public.
If someone has a different email address for them, perhaps they can try to contact them to confirm that it is OK to release their clues to other letterboxers. If they still don't answer within a week or two, or if no one has any other contact info, I think it would be OK to share the clues with other boxers.
Wisconsin Hiker
