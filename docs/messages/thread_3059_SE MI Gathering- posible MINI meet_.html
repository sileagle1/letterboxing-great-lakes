Better late than never - What a fun gathering this was!! What
organization it must take to get together three quests complete with
maps, scrolls, hidden treasure (seriously creative props). Hey, if
you're willing to do this every year, we're definitely there! Of
course, a team had to come in "last", but SpartanFans teamwork made
for a blast of a time (not to mention the late addition of a
Cichlid)! The weather tried to spoil it, Miles tried to spoil it,
but nothing could spoil the fun that everyone had! A perfect amount
of 'boxers, and I couldn't stop eating TrailTroll's cookies - Yum yum
yum! DD, we're in for the "recovery" meet too, if our schedules can
work - please don't put yourselves out trying to accomodate.

Did anyone get some good photos that day? Most of ours are of
people's backs and folks eating. Not too flattering... Hope to see
you all at the mystery place (if we're not dumb bunnies)!

Team Rabbit
