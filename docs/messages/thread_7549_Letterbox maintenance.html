From: mthkvch (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2012-10-21 00:34:00 UTC
Just wanted to let everyone know that (FINALLY) I have gotten around to replacing two of my boxes boxes that were either missing or pulled.

First of all, my 2-box series FEED ME is complete again and has a new logbook.

Second, 4 years ago I pulled CASTLE ROCK because our beloved governor Blago had decided to close that state park. But instead he went to prison, and now the original stamp and logbook are back in place. This stamp was carved by my friend "krait" who, as many of you already know, was a wonderful carver; and this image is one of my favorites of his.

One more newly-recarved stamp to replace and then I think I'm done for awhile.

Trail Rider (Chris)
