According to letterboxing.org, there are two
letterboxes hidden in Parma:

Trash to Treasure, hidden by Wee Walkers

send him an email to see if this is the one they
found, since I think I might have contact information
for the Wee Walkers (both The Pirates and Wee Walkers

can tell; we had emailed the Wee Walkers about one of
their boxes a month or so ago).
