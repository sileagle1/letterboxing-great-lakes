Ok, I did a little more checking....

This is where we stand...

Moraine Hills SP (just to the south of Chain 'o Lakes) has ALL of
their picnic shelters available June 12! They also have more trails
and the woods seem to be just as old with hiding places. I
currently have one of the shelters on reserve, and intend to go down
tomorrow on my way to MI to check the place out for final commitment
and finalizing reservations.

So (unless something else goes wrong) it looks like it will be June
12 for the gathering at a picnic shelter at Moraine Hills SP. (AND
I think you can drive there w/o paying tolls!)

There are a number of camping spots available at Chain o' Lakes for
the weekend of June 12 yet. I personally have site #15 in the
Turner Lake South area reserved. Since at most I'll have one tent
and two people, I'd be willing to share the campsite and the costs
with another group of two with one tent if anyone is interested.

Dragon

into this gathering -- I'm pumped!
