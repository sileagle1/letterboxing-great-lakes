Nitrocat will be presenting a 20-minute workshop on making planting pouches, right after the lunch break and group photo at the "Horsin' Around" event. Anyone interested in making a pouch, please bring the following supplies:

Ziplock bag - double zipper works best
Scissors or exacto
Ruler or tape measure
Black or camo duct tape, 2" wide (1 roll is enough for several bags)
Cutting board or mat (optional)

The "Horsin' Around" event is 2 weeks from today!
