And we're back, too! Our GLKLB 2005 recap

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-20 08:37:16 UTC-07:00
What an awesome weekend we had. My head is still
swimming with all the people we met, all the boxes we
found, all the neat hidden surprises that were around.
And I must say, I was taken aback a few times when
Deedle & I introduced ourselves and were greeted with,
"YOU'RE Doodle & Deedle Bug!" Wasn't expecting that,
made us feel so special! :)

Alas, we headed out to box during Mark & Sue's
Dartmore talk, so we're not in the group photo - but
we were captured on the photo titled "More Stamping"
(I'm in the bandana, Deedle's in the flannel) so there
is proof that we were there!

Too exhausted to find all the boxes on Saturday, since
we were camping in the area we went back to the park
Sunday morning to find a few more. And wouldn't you
know it, we found a cuckoo clue in the one box, but
because the clue specified that it was for the
gathering, and given where we determined the box would
have been we're pretty sure that it wasn't there for
us to find any longer. Ah well....

Can't wait to go over all the stamp images we gathered
this weekend again! But alas, it's Monday morning,
and here I am back at work...reading emails about
letterboxing ;)

Doodle Bug.
