Secret signal on the trail?

From: carol_beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-09-29 22:44:32 UTC
I was reading one of the question in an AQ profile and this was it -

Do you think there should be a 'secret signal' to identify other letterboxers on the trail, and if so, what?

This made me stop and think...How would I answer that question? Should we have some sort of secret signal or do we just linger around an area, glancing at the other person, wondering when they are going to leave the area so we can palm the box we seek?

Sometimes, I go to a mini meet and I wonder how I will find the letterboxers that I have never met before and they have never met me.
We usually find each other. Inky fingers are a dead give away.

My leg is still on the mend. For now, I'm reduced to short hikes and drive bys. Several new boxes in my area and I just can't reach them. Oh, the pain of it all.

Beetle
