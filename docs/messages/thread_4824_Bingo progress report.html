I'm Maid Marion, scribe for RobinHoodandMerryMen, reporting progress on
the cute game we call bingo. Apparently I should have let Friar Tuck

get out and start searching again! Thanks Spring Chick for setting
this all up. I maintain a quiet presence here on LbGLK, but I have
really enjoyed reading and getting to know you all!
