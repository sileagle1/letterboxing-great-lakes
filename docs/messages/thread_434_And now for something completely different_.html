Oh wow. This is kind of scary, because without knowing there was an organized organization for visiting these historical markers, a few months ago I actually came up with the independant thought that I'd like to visit as many Ohio historical markers as I could. I was trying to find a list on the Ohio Historical Society website of exactly how many markers there are in Ohio, and their locations so I could tick them off as I visited them...on my way to visit my friend who lives in Lisbon, Ohio (future site of a letterbox, if we ever get it carved ;) I pass a historical marker on Route 14 and purposely stopped one day just to read it. Then, after I got back in my car, turned back around and went back to the sign because I realized I didn't bother reading the other side, assuming that it had the same information as the front. :)

Doodle aka Diane, happy that others are as crazy -- I mean, eccentric, as she.
