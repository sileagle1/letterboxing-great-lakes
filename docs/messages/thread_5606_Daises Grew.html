Side Wager: A wager made beyond the normal rules of the game. A hedge bet.

Every year there is a 'Sidewagers' game that runs with the BINGO game.
Here, people have picked a day that they think will be the day the BINGO
game ends (you can also see the sidewager info in the LbGLK files
section). As has been the tradition since Azobox started the game,
things are posted on specific days, and they are posted in a cryptic
fashion (consider the subject line). So, as Wisconsin Hiker points out,
it is a mystery to figure out! For those of us with little (or no)
chance of winning BINGO, it keeps things interesting.

Unfortunately for you, you didn't join the game. I think many of the
clues become more clear when the day you pick gets called! (hint, hint)
