The Mt Baldy boxes are INDEED a challenge.
We,too, went up and down those stairs a couple of times last summer
in the heat before finally figuring where we went wrong.
Like others, we took a long walk towards the lake -- no luck.
Then carefully re-read the clues and picked up one significant piece
that we'd missed so with renewed energy we tried again. But still the
Mt Baldy evaded us. Then while I was climbing down a steep dune
looking for who-knows-what, Artistamp saw a bit of an edge from a
familiar Tupperware container peeking out from a root, and we had it.

Read SpringChick's recounting of HER search for the Baldys for yet
another tale.
http://www.confessionsofdecember.com/lbna/lbfound.asp

BYW, the placer of Mt Baldy is an out-of-stater who summers in the
Saugatuck area, I believe.

Glad to have a place to discuss the Great Lakes area boxes!
