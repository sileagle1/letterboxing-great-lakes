New Great Lakes patch

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-11-06 17:37:54 UTC
Well after all our fall trips, I finally contacted the patch company a
week or two ago about the new patch design. Today I received a
sample, but I think it has way too much blue in it.

You can see the preliminary sample in the Photos section of this
board, in the "Patches" photo album:

http://ph.groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/photos/browse/54cf

The original round patch is also shown there. I'd welcome comments on
the colors on both the "tree" portion of the new design and the
outside border color. I liked what we had on the round patch and am
tending to ask them to try to duplicate those colors. Let me know
what you think.

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. I was amazed they could get the detail of the little LBNA logo.
I did need to fill in the outlined letters, but am still quite
impressed.
