Mobiusklein and I had to remove our boxes from Palmer Park in
Wyoming. The State Dept. of Agriculture will be cutting down
HUNDREDS of Ash trees from the park! Some of them were in our clues
and many are around the boxes. Since the park does not officially
open until May 1st, we hope to have them replaced by then. The ones
affected are The Dragon's Lair, Present from the Pines, and Dragon
Fyre. Will let you all know when they are back in place.
