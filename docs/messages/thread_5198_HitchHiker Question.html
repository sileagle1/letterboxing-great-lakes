One of the western suburbs of Chicago, Western Springs, has a HH Hostel. (Traveler's Rest Hitchhiker Hostel) I haven't been there lately, but it is supposed to have 3 HH's "resting" in there. The idea is to drop off a HH & pick one up. I might be a good place to view a few before you launch yours. I currently have 3 HH's that are traveling with me. I've not been able to drop them because all of the boxes that I've found lately are very small. They will depart soon though. It's a great impetus to go boxing if you have a HH to get rid of.
