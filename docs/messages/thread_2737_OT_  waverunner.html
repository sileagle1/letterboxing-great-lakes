From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-03 22:48:55 UTC-04:00
I am not aware of any such permit, unless perhaps you are referring to a
Boating Safety Certificate, which is only required for persons (and their
accompanying parent/legal guardians) between the ages of 12 and 14 operating
a PWC. But this is not just a form to fill out, it is a class that must be
taken and I would suspect this is only available within the state of
Michigan. Most of us, particularly in the Lake Michigan bordering counties,
took it in school and it is offered by local Sheriff's Departments.

See this site for regulations regarding operation of personal watercraft and
boats in Michigan:

http://boat-ed.com/mi/handbook/

SpringChick
