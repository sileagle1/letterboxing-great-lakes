From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-16 16:01:17 UTC-04:00
My personal preference for detailed images is PZKut from Stampeaz. The A grade is the orange stuff, which is not very appealing to me, but the B grade is still white. I have always used the B grade since I began using it 3 1/2 years ago and have never had an issue with it being so irregular that I couldn't use it. You can order PZKut at http://www.stampeaz.com/. They are really good about mailing ASAP and I usually have my order within 2 days. The pink stuff would be my second choice for detail -- it is a lot like PZKut, but a little softer. This they have at many Michael's stores.

If you aren't able to find either of these, I have a piece (of either) I can mail out to you tomorrow.

SpringChick

Hi DD...

Personally, I like the pink SpeedyStamp best. MasterCarve is my
second choice. I like the MasterCarve mostly because it is thick (in
the 3/4" size,) and then doesn't need a block or backing mount of any
sort. I tried the orange PZ kut... and I really didn't care for it;
it seemed a bit more brittle and plastic-y than the pink stuff, which
for me was important in carving the very fine, small details in a lot
of my stamps. I've also liked how the pink stuff has held up so far.

For what it's worth!!!
