From: cjosada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-11-30 05:11:11 UTC
Fungus Woman and Beetle are putting their heads together again, and
planning a second Special Indoor Letterboxing Outing (S.I.L.O.) at
the Silo Restaurant in Lake Bluff, Illinois. Tonight, I asked the
manager for a reservation for Sat. February 16 and she said that
would be fine. She asked what type of group, so I started to try to
explain to her that we were 'hikers' from Northern IL and SE WI
and....she suddenly blurted out - "You mean like letterboxing?"
ROFLOL! Sandy, the manager of the SILO is a letterboxer! Well,
needless to say, we must have left a huge impression on the staff
last year, because the manager told me she heard all about our group
from the staff for weeks after we left. Hmmm...I guess all those
red clothes left an impression. She's gone out letterboxing too.

So, mark your calendars. Saturday, February 16, 2008 from 11 am to
4 pm. There is a cure for winter cabin fever. Fungus Woman will be
handling all the high tech postings and she will be posting the
information on AQ and LbNA soon. Beetle will handle the reservation,
decorations, and entertaining details for the afternoon.

So, what will the theme be this year? Well, WI Hiker already
guessed when we gave a tiny little clue a few months ago. The event
will be held in February. No, we're not celebrating Chinese New
Years again. This time, the theme has a little bit to do with
Valentines Day, but not love and kisses.

The theme will be: SILO II - "Crime Doesn't Pay-The St. Valentines
Day Massacre." Think about those days back in the 1920s. Everyone
is invited to dress up in a disguise and come as cops and robbers,
crooks, outlaws, mobsters, flappers, prisoners, underworld figures,
FBI agents, etc. Think back to those days of crime, prohibition,
bootlegging, the roaring 20s, etc. Now that should give you plenty
of ideas for creating indoor stamps!!!

Insider information has it that Al Capone will stop by for the group
photo. It will be a roaring good time!

More information to follow soon.

Beetle
and
Fungus Woman
