Never too old.....

From: Beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-07-26 06:18:59 UTC
You're never too old to go letterboxing. Today, I was trying to explain to my mother-in-law that I was going to look for some letterboxes after my morning visit with her. She asked me to explain about this hobby of finding boxes. She is 92 years old. She has Alzheimer's Disease. She is confined to a wheel chair. And she is legally deaf. She can still read, so I explain letterboxing to her using a dry erase marker board. After 50 minutes, she smiled and asked me - "How can I find some of these letterboxes?" I may have to create and plant a special box for her to find. The look on her face was priceless when she said, "Oh, it's just for FUN!" She gets it!

---Beetle
