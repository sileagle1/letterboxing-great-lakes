the captian of team bindlestiff has proposed a MAJOR change in our
trip for this weekend because some of our team have never been to the
UP, he's putting on his road trip captian hat (oh boy) since i'm in
charge of the boxing part i'm struggling to catch up the new plans and
could use some help from all of you.

here's what he is suggesting for the new trip plan:
tonight (wed) after work - head to chicago (4 hrs drive) and stay
overnight somewhere
thurs - do the sailboat show at navy pier, meet moms art friend and
her family then leave for milwaukee.(2 hrs drive) stay overnight in
milwaukee.
fri - drive to the menominee area (3 hrs drive) and stay overnight
sat - drive all the way across the UP (5 hrs drive) and stay in mackinaw
sun - reach traverse city (2.5 hrs drive)and visit with our family

there are some long drive times in there so i'm looking for
suggestions for quick boxes that we could do and it would also be nice
to get in some exchanges as we go zooming through this new plan if
thats possible. mom carved a new personal traveler for bindle bum -
just ask who his favorite president was to get it. plus we've got 4
boxes we were hoping to place during the trip, but i think we're going
to run out of time.

thanks for the help! back to the letterboxing dot org site for me.
