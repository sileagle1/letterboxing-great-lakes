From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-16 15:28:34 UTC
Those of you sending me boxes to place for the gathering - please
get them to me by the first week of June so I have time to plant
them. Thanks!

We can always use more boxes for the gathering! If we don't have
anymore workshops, we'll need something to fill our time! How about
a ton of more boxes? (Just a wish...)

Here's a workshop suggestion - a "newbie" workshop. Learn all the
tricks of the trade to get you started, including making your first
stamp (in conjunction with SpringChick's workshop). You'll learn
how to find and place your own boxes, write clues, etc. If we have
enough interest, I think I could give the workshop a shot. Any
takers? Let me know.

T-shirts - still waiting for info on cost. Hopefully very soon. So
far I have an $8 to $15 range depending whether you get your name on
the back or not. I'd ESTIMATE $9 for no-name and $11 with name.
