Re: Wisconsin Letterboxing

From: M Clan (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-07-29 17:43:58 UTC
Mosquitoes can be bad, but welcome to the Midwest. We've never seen
any rattlesnakes and managed to avoid the poison ivy.

Certainly caution needs to be taken wherever you are, but welcome to
hiking/outdoor recreation, I guess. There's a number of really
great boxes in the Madison area. And the one in Spring Green is a
spectacular box to find (Mercy at Mill Creek). If you're in the
Dodgeville/Spring Green area, I'd definitely recommend that one as
well as "Get the Lead Out."

Have fun,
M Clan
