I'm looking for recommendations for an Android app that can be used to track distance and elevation when planting letterboxes. I know there are several apps out there - what do you use and how do you like it?

From: rei5kids (5reikids@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-10-23 20:19:04 UTC
I use MapMyFitness for my running app...it does track distance and elevation. It is available on Android in both a free & paid version. I'm cheap and use the free version because it does all that I need it to.

You can edit your recorded workouts online through their website as well, so if something recorded wrong and you know it did, then you can go in and change it. Or if you wanted to go back and rename the workout something specific after it's been recorded & uploaded you can do that too.

It must work off satelite because the ap will work in locations without phone or 3G service. Only issue we've found is if it loses satelite signal, it fills in your route as the crow flies.
