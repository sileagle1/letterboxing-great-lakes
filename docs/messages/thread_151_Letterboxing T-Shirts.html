From: Angela (missangi1210@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-19 20:07:14 UTC
The poll about the gathering shirts got this idea into my head. :)
I am a t-shirt girl, so I am always looking for something new. I went
to cafepress.com the other day and saw that if you send your graphics
to them, they will sell and distribute t-shirts to whoever wants to
buy them. If you want to make a profit from them, you can hike the
price up to whatever you want. Otherwise the money goes directly to
cafepress for making the items.
Has anyone ever thought about using them, or have you used them
before? I thought it would be neat to have a letterboxing shirt to
show off the idea and get others interested. I know some people don't
like to spend the time getting them made and dealing with the money
and shipping, so I thought the cafepress thing would be a good idea.

Just a quick idea I thought I'd share. :)
Angi
