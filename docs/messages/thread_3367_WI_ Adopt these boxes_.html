Hi everyone! We just got a note at LbNA concerning some wonderful
sounding boxes in Wisconsin:

Sleuthing Outagamie County Letterboxes
Appleton, Wisconsin

<< I write with an update on the letter boxes placed by the Outagamie
County Historical Society identified as "Sleuthing Outagamie County"
LbNA #9095 on your website. Unfortunately our organization can no
longer commit to maintenence of the letter boxes. Vandalisim and
unintentional destruction took its toll. We left the boxes in the field
so people may continue to use their passport. However we no longer sell
the passports and will not replace missing boxes.
Thank you for this great resource.>>>

Is anyone interested in caring for and feeding these little treasures?
If so, please write us at webmasters-lbna at yahoogroups dot com.
