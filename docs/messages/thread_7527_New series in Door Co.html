We planted a new 5 box series in Door County, WI this weekend. It is called Snooping Around Door County. Please be aware they are all in different locations. They celebrate all things we love to do there. The Clues are posted to Atlas Quest.



RE: [LbGLK] New series in Door Co

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-09-03 23:35:33 UTC-05:00
Wow! There have been a LOT of new WI boxes posted this weekend!



A month ago I realized that there were over 300 boxes in WI that we hadn't
found yet, so we have spent the last 3 weekends out & about in the state
(Eau Claire area, LaCrosse area and Rhinelander area). We've had lots of
adventures & pretty sights and were successful in finding most of the boxes
we looked for. We even "found" a letterboxER yesterday - we ran into
Pastor's Wife on a trail in Saint Germain, WI!



Now that we've been whittling away on our list of boxes to find, at least 10
new ones were planted this weekend. Many thanks to all of you who have been
planting!



Wisconsin Hiker
