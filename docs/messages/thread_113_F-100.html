F-100

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-10 23:47:17 UTC
After watching SpringChick reach her F-100 mark yesterday, I HAD to
get out today. When I realized that *I* was within striking distance
myself, the deal was sealed. So off I headed to the Grand Rapids (MI)
area to join with Grandvilla and Mobiusklein. First on the agenda
were Bindle Babe's Fate of Easter Series and then the Bonus Easter
Eggs.
An exchange on the trail was another bonus during that hunt.
SpringChick's letterbox partner for the day, Taylor, shared her stamp
with us.
Then we moved on to two very cool hunts at Grand River County Park.
Great clues and stamps were the way to close in the 100 mark. While I
didn't need a slide rule, trig tables or algebraic formulae, a
calculator would have come in handy. Wow, I did more math than I've
done for years! "Sphinx" and "Serpentine" were challenging hikes
through a wonderful park -- with good clues to boot.
They finished me with an easier one at Hager Park for F-100!
If you go to Grand River, study the clues in advance.
Oh yeah, I ended the day with an even F-100.
Now, for tomorrow.

azobox
