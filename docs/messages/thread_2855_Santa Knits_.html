Santa Knits!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-30 22:45:00 UTC
Who would have known that Santa was a knitter? I suppose sitting up
there at the North Pole, one would have to have some hobbies other
than letterboxing since the snow no doubt forces much time inside.
So knitting seems a logical past-time since the elves pretty much
have that toymaking stuff under control.

Well, it appears that Santa has had a great knitting teacher
(perhaps he has attended one of Doglvrs knitting workshops) as my
Secret Santa has brought me not one, but two pair of the most
awesome hand-knit socks!

Of course I don't know the real identity of my north-pole knitter,
and there are no doubt several knitters on the list of
participants. Although one person comes immediately to mind, the
postmark does not match... however the postmark looks suspiciously
like that of an individual who could have conceivably teamed up to
help disguise my Secret Santa's true identity... Regardless of
Santa's name, the socks are gorgeous and I love them!

You can see a picture of them in the photos section in the album
Speedsquare created.

THANKS!!!
