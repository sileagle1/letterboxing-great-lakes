Wow! We go away for a short vacation and come back to all sorts of GLK activitiy!

We had a great time with many of the New England letterboxers attending the Bed & Boxing III - A Green Mountain Journey Event in Killington, VT this weekend. It was really a dilemma for us having two events on the same day...as we haven't missed a fall Michigan event in awhile - we did think of you on Saturday and hoped that you all had a wonderful time together - sounds like that was the end result...accept for Mr. Family of Many! Do you think Spring Chick has that voodoo doll up and running already?
