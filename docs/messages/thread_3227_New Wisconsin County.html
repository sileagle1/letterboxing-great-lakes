New Wisconsin County

From: meanderingmelee (meanderingmelee@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-27 13:17:29 UTC
Although my roots and my heart belong to the U.P., I must pledge my
allegiance to my home in Wisconsin, the birthplace of my wife and
children, (although I don't hold that against them), to aide in our
mission of planting every Wisconsin county with a letterbox. I had to
forgo some extra time in Michigan's Dickinson County, a county well
planted, (we did still manage to stop for a couple boxes), to spend
some time in Wisconsin's Florence County and plant 2 new boxes!!

Wisconsin will win this race, even if it takes us Michigan natives to
make it happen.

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-10-27 15:03:29 UTC-05:00
Woo hoo! The Meandering Melee have the honor of filling in Florence,
the last northern border county! (perhaps also known as the rock
kicked up by the boot, if you see the imagery on our "boxless map"...)
http://www.letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html
I'll update the map sometime this weekend.

I appreciate a MI native helping out. I have also helped out in MI.
Last year I noticed there were only 2 boxless counties in the U.P., so
in July 2005 I decided to plant boxes in those locations when we
traveled up there. I thought it was fun to "complete" that area and it
is what got me interested in trying to get a box in all the WI counties
(a much more daunting task). I've been very happy with my decision to
publicize the mission, because as a group project we have made great
progress!

Only 15 counties left.....
