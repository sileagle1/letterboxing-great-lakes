Okay, as I'm sure you've read here and elsewhere, in this game there
are "no rules".
That said, here's some history:
In the late Winter/early Spring of '03, SpringChick floated the idea
of a Michigan letterbox gathering. Since get togethers were much
rarer in those days, interest from boxers outside the state quickly
surfaced. So an event was planned and many came from far and wide.
Our neighbors in Wisconsin, lead by the intrepid Dragon, offered to
host an event in '04. The successful "Cooler by the Lake" (McHenry,
IL) Gathering took place in June. Organization for The Great
Lakes '05 Gathering (something like "Mo' boxing in Motown"? -- help
me, Team Rabbit and Delirium) was assumed by dedicated SE Michigan
folks -- the aformentioned Delirium and Team Rabbit.

So that's the status as I know it.
Of course, we've since learned that Delirium has had to beg off her
commitment due to family priorities.

That's a long answer to the question. A short version is that to my
knowledge there are no plans for a Michigan event prior to the Great
Lakes Gathering which will be held next Spring/early summer.

There are informal get togethers of course as boxers coordinate
schedules with others to meet for meals, coffee, etc.

To get an idea of how things have changed, it appears that perhaps
300 people may show up for the Monster Mash in the East.
