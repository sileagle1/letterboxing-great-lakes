From: ruhlette (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-07 17:46:55 UTC-08:00
I'm sorry you weren't successful in your first attempt. Keep the Faith!

The good news is you will learn something from this experience. I just
did a quick clue search for the Antrim boxes. Hmmm. This box was placed
in 2003. While that isn't ancient history, it doesn't take a lot of time
for terrain and nature to change. If the box hasn't been checked by the
placer in a while, he/she may not be aware of these natural changes.
Animals could have moved them. Were any of the locations near water?
Maybe washed away? I'm suggesting there are a number of reasons why
someone might not find a particular box. If you haven't noticed, some
boxes will indicate the last date found and status. I haven't searched
for these boxes, so I cannot speak with any authority about them. I would
just suggest trying another box.

Good luck!
