Well, if you solved my last puzzle, you know that no one officially won the side wagers game.  For the game, everyone who participated picked a day they thought the bingo game would receive it's first full card winner.  Hypothetically, the winner would be the person who picked the actual day the game was won... without 'going over' so to speak. 


Example:

Person XX selected 04/12

Full card called 4/13

Person YY selected 4/16.


In this scenario, person YY would have won the side wagers game.  Although XX was actually closer to the winning day, the bingo was called after the fact, so the next in line wins.


In our game this year, we had people pick dates as early as March 3, then scattered through 4/30. No one selected any dates in May.  Since the full card win was not claimed until after the last chosen date, there is no official winner under the rules of the game.   


I didn't have a contingency plan in place for what would happen if the actual win was after the face.  I didn't know if I should just declare the game void and say "house wins" or have NorseGnome be the winner since she was the closest.  Neither of those options seems exactly right to me, so I decided to go with a random number generator to declare a winner.   My number generator spewed out #18.... the 18th name on this list is Socks3!  So, congrats Socks3!  Send me your mailing address to baliddle @ sbcglobal.net and I will mail out a prize!


Did anyone solve all of my ciphers?  :)  I will post the solutions in the coming days.

I hope you had fun playing along!
