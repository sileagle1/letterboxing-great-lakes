Welcome!



The Great Lakes regional group has a tradition of a BINGO game to get us through the winter. This is our 7th annual game and it started JAN 1 and will probably continue into April or May. More details can be found in the FILES section of this message board.



In addition to the BINGO game, someone else usually hosts a "Sidewagers" game where we guess the date that someone will have the first full BINGO card.



You're encouraged to explore the various files, photos, databases, links and old posts on this board to get familiar with the rest of us crazies. Who knows, you might even find a clue or two, or some info on how to decode some clues....
