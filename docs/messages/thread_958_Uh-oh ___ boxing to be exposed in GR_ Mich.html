Must be a slow time of year for reporters or something, because I was
just contacted by our local paper (Champaign-Urbana, IL) about doing an
article on letterboxing, too! I'm going to do the interview, but only
because I know and trust the reporter who's writing the article. It's
kind of funny - I've known her for years because our dogs play together,
and she got my name from a friend of hers who emailed me about one of my
boxes (it's missing, again). The article will be published sometime this
month, so I will let you know how it goes. I'm hoping that it will get
more people in the area interested in boxing and I can collect more
stamps in my logbooks...
