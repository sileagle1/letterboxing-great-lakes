From: M Clan (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-09-02 17:17:07 UTC
As a former Portlander, I congratulate you on your travel location
selection. The Pacific NW is the most beautiful part of this great
country and you'll have an amazing time! It also is an apparent
hotbed for letterboxing, which we unfortunately had not heard of
when we lived there. Alas, one day, we too will take this sort of
trip.

Spend some time in the Columbia River Gorge. The waterfalls there
are amazing - and there's a ton of letterboxes at Multnomah,
Horsetail, Bridal Veil, and all the other waterfalls.

Downtown Portland is a blast! Make sure you visit the Saturday
Market on Burnside some Sat. morning. I'm sure there's some boxes
around there. The Mt. Hood area has some great hiking as well,
although if you wait too long, snow will be falling. The coast is
also great.

Seattle is Portland's big brother, with all the growing pains
included - traffic, more sprawl, etc. - as well as some of the
benefits.

I've longed to take this sort of trip. I'm really jealous. In a
few years, perhaps my son will be old enough to take a week during
the summer for some boxing, etc.

Enjoy! And make sure to keep us up-to-date.

(I think WI Hiker and Martini Man just went to the Pac NW to box.
You may want to contact them for particular information. I know
there's also a Pac NW Boxing group so they'll certainly help you
out.)

Good boxing,
Father M Clan
