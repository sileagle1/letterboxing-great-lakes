annual fall service project

From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2009-09-04 15:27:28 UTC-07:00
Those of you attending the WI10 gathering last year may remember the "Project Pajama Pants" letterbox I had with me, to coincide with Operation Support Our Troops - Illinois' pajama pants drive for the wounded soldiers in military hospitals.

I'd like to do another drive this year at the Boxacon gathering.

One of the things OSOTIL does this time of year is collect items to stuff into holiday stockings for the troops. Their website lists the items they're collecting: http://www.osotil.org/christmasstockings.html, but I'd like to concentrate on collecting AA and AAA batteries. Bring a package of batteries to Boxacon and you can receive a special "I get a charge out of Letterboxing!" stamp.

For those of you attending the Western Michigan gathering, I've got an offer for you too. Rather than sending batteries (too heavy to mail, and probably a bit hazardous), donate a dollar and I'll buy batteries for you. (Can I have a volunteer to coordinate this for me??)

If any other gathering (I see there are quite a fewthis fall in our area) wants to participate, please let me know. However I must have everything collected by October 15th.

Last, if you look at their website, you'll see they need volunteers to sew the stockings themselves. If anyone wants to sew some and bring them to Boxacon, that would be great! (You'll have to contact them to get the official pattern.) As to the other gatherings, I'll leave that to you if anyone wants to go that route...again, because of the expense of shipping them.

And of course, thank you letters and holiday cards for the troops are always welcome.

Thanks in advance!

--klk/fw
