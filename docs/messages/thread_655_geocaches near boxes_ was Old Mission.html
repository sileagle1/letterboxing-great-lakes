From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-08 11:36:34 UTC
The first geocache I ever found, I stumbled on while looking for a
place to put one of my boxes. The spot was PERFECT ... I was already
writing the clues in my head, until I found the camo covered box.

It never occured to me to "spoil" his location by putting my box
nearby ... wonder how come so many caches end up next to boxes, then.
I suppose it may be because the cache placer didn't discover the
letterbox when the cache originally went in.

The day we found the Old Mission box, there was no geocache nearby.
However, as there was a bit of poison ivy along the trail, Bindle Bum
waited while I did the box and what he described sounded like people
who were probably on their way to return the cache but I was too
close so they were lingering about. He thought it was one of the
funniest things he'd seen since we started boxing. All of us trying
to be discreet. Kinda sounds like as much fun as Kara and her mom
had watching Bindle Bum and I look for the East Meets West box.

Enjoy!
---Bindle Babe
