SILO #4 Letterbox Gathering!!!

From: beetle_carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-09-07 17:40:27 UTC
Since letterboxers are already starting to ask - "When is the next SILO Letterbox Gathering?", Lock and Beetle are proud to announce plans for SILO #4. It will be held on Sunday, February 28, 2010 at the Silo Restaurant in Lake Bluff, IL. Theme: Comic Book Characters There's a wide open theme certain to inspire many creative event stamps. And we all know that letterboxers are a bunch of creative 'characters', so we hope yo see you then.

The event is not posted yet. Lock will take care of that later in the year. However, we thought it might be a good time of the year to post the theme, before Halloween, so families could keep their eyes open for comic book character masks, costumes, jammmies, capes, etc. and plan ahead.

SILO #4 will surely be a Kodak moment. Boxers in disguise. See you then!

Your SILO co-hosts

Beetle (aka Carol)
Lock (aka Scott) of Lock Shock and Barrel
