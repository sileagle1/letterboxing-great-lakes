Logbook Sale

From: Timbertoes (toomey@genevaonline.com) | Date: 2004-11-10 19:40:34 UTC

Well, they don't actually advertise it that way, but for those of you
who use artist sketchpads for logbooks, they're on sale at Hobby
Lobby. All sizes are 40% off making the smallest size pretty
affordable. I like the 100-page 3"x5" size because they fit well in
the boxes I use (don't look for any at the Round Lake, IL store
because I just bought them out). The sale is good through Saturday,
November 13.

On my way to the store I thought of a fun idea - I bought my 9-year-
old son a compass for Christmas and I'm going to put it in his
stocking along with a clue sheet. I'll hide another small present
somewhere in the house for him to find using his new compass. He
still believes, so I'll have to tell him I stuck it in his stocking
early in the morning. Ahhh, childhood!

Timbertoes
