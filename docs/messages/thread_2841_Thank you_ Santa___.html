A big public "Thank You" to my Secret Santa!!!

I think I know who you are, but until I verify that w/ a personal
email, I'll take a moment to thank you here first and let everyone
else have a cyber-peek at my goodies:

Before my goodies arrived, my Santa had sent me about 1-2 postcards a
week from various locations in the US... all mentioning good
letterboxes to find in the area. Very neat! (At last count, I think
there were 6 postcards.) All with the snowflake stamp (see below) on
it... so I knew immediately who it was from :-)

Then, after all that anticipation....

My package arrived Wednesday afternoon, and had some of the most
awesome things in it:

The contents of the entire package were all S*N*O*W*F*L*A*K*E
themed!!! There was an awesome carved stamp of a snowflake, with a
great silver ink pad!

Also, the coolest little hitch-hiker kit that I've ever seen. (Did
you make that yourself, Santa??? You should sell those online
somewhere if so, I'm sure you'd get takers :-)

Then there was the cutest little white christmas letterboxer bear
tree ornament... complete with back-pack, tiny rolled up clues
sticking out from the back-pack (and thank you for THAT clue,
hehehe!) and the bear also holds a stamp and logbook in his hands!
Absolutely so cute.

Next was a winter mini - scrapbook/logbook... I'll surely use that!

And a box of yummy hot chocolate (I've already hid that in the back
of the pantry; with 3 growing boys at home, that won't last otherwise!)

And (if I'm not missing anything else,) the really, really special
gift: A beautiful hand-knit dark red scarf, with a beautiful
snowflake pattern throughout! It is so, SO nice!!! I just love it!
I'll be wearing that while I trek the trails this coming Fall.

Santa, you really out-did yourself! And you also gave me WONDERFUL
ideas for next year's Santa exchange! I've never participated in this
before, and the idea of a whole set of "themed" gifts was so
wonderful to discover in the great box of goodies you sent me :-)

Thanks again,

PennyPenny
