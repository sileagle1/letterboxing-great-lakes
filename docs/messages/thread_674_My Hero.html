My Hero

From: Shutterfly (missangi1210@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-14 15:31:00 UTC
So I get this e-mail on Sunday from Agent Ink and Z-fan saying that
my Creatures of Blackhawk #1 boxes have gone missing. Taking their
place, scattered around the area, are beer bottles. Well, it was my
first box gone missing, so I was a little upset, but at the same time
I realized that it would have happened eventually. Isn't that how it
usually works? You make a few successful ones, and some go missing?
Anyway, I told my boyfriend (who recently became a letterboxer
too...Manic Mechanic) about it that night when I talked to him on the
phone. Well, yesterday I get a call from him telling me that he has
good news. Guess what!? He found one of the boxes! He found the first
of the two boxes thrown quite a ways from where it was supposed to
be. The second of the two, which was a micro-box, is still missing.
So now, for finding one of my boxes, he is my letterboxing hero. :)
(insert Awwwwww... here) Because of the telltale clothing items left
in the area, I'm not too sure if I want the micro-box back. ;)
Whoever has that one, can keep it. I can make a new one.

Shutterfly
