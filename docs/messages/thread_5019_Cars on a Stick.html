From: Kathi Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2008-05-04 12:03:26 UTC-07:00
Yeah, but have you seen the state of the Spindle lately? It was really disgusting. The cars were rusting, and the bird poop all over it was gross (I'm currently taking a little break right now from revising mycology lectures, and I can't help but think "Cryptococcosis!!" whenever I see pigeon poop.). Like that other "sculpture" they used to have in Cermak Plaza with all the junk welded together.

I have nothing against modern definitions of art, and I'm just as sentimental as the next person (sometimes WAY too sentimental for my own good!). Quite honestly, I'm disappointed that the landmark for the long-gone letterbox is gone, (I remember when it was one of the handful in this area, and alas MIA before I started in '04), but I won't miss the Spindle itself.

There's always the yellow Pinto splayed on the wall by the old Walgreens (less than a block away in the same shopping center)............
