Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man Around the World in 80 HOURS!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-24 12:12:43 UTC
The dynamic duo from Wisconsin left records in the dust during a
whirlwind weekend in Michigan. Yes, it was less than 80 HOURS for
their search for the elusive "Around the World in 80 Days" series. The
travel staff was aware that an attempt on the record was afoot and so
the telegram-like reports received throughout the search were
carefully studied and the documents of authentication were scrutinized
for accuracy. In the end there was no question: a record had been set!
While speed was a factor for the series (after all, it is named "80
DAYS"), the travel staff could not have imagined the warp-speed
demonstrated by Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man.

Though obstacles of all types were thrown before the travelers (ferry
canceled, grave diggers very close to an important site, etc) plus the
usual clue interpretation challenges, WiHi and MM persevered and
brought home the prize!

The Travel Staff does not recommend that others attempt to better this
record.
Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man are professionals.
(you know the drill:"professional drivers, closed course..do not try
this at home.")

Congratulations to the latest winners of the "World Traveler" and
"Around the World in 80 Days" letterbox/bonus box.

Reminder: the series will not be maintained after Fall 2006.

Box safely,

Travel staff
