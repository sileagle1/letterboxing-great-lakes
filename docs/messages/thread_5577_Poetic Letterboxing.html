Poetic Letterboxing

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-23 01:45:54 UTC
Today, MamaMir was out looking for some of my letterboxes hidden along
the Metra Train Line. These boxes are not difficult to find, even in
the winter, although she said she is being rxtra careful to create
extra tracks in the snow to disguise her path and to protect the
letterboxes. Here is the poem she wrote:

Frozen fingers, frozen toes
Winter letterboxing woes
Nonetheless, I found your train
And so my world is right again!

Receiving a letterbox poem - how heartwarming. Just thought I'd share
it with you.
