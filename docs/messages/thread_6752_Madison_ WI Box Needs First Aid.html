Madison, WI Box Needs First Aid

From: sunnysideupm (sunnysideupm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-10-03 14:15:41 UTC
The Walnut Grove Park letterbox in Madison needs major first aid - box, logbook and baggies. The stamp is fine - whew! I have emailed the placer.

The Madison area has many fine boxes - local history, lots of beautiful natural areas, library boxes, clever clues and an early Wisconsin Hiker carving.

Thank you to all of you plant letterboxes - you take me to gems of places I didn't know were there, teach me things, make me think outside the box, and leave me the gifts of a (usually!) pleasant memory and unique piece of art!

Now, I'm off to heal my scratches and pick off some burrs from my shoes before the Horsin' Around Gathering. The last gathering I went to had torrential rain and a nearby tornado - what adventures await us in Cherry Valley?

- Sunny
