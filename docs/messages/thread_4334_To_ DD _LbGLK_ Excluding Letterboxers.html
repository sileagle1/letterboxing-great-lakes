Usually the gathering event planners will post the boxes as event boxes rather than traditional boxes. You can always ask around at a gathering. "FIND" counts aren't necessary; some people count and some don't. Just have fun. I once thought that clues with a find count minimum was the way to go to restrict newbies, but I've rethought that idea. Boxes need to be challenging and the placement needs to be more carefully thought out. Then, there still needs to be some fast and easy boxes for beginners, or experienced letterboxers who just 'need' a quick fix....so to speak. LOL!
