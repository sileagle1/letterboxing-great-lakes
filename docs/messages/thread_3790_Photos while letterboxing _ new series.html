Thanks Atom 118 and Doodle & Deedle....
Your pictures were a refreshing visual distraction from the damp,
gray sky and white ground that we are presently peering at!
It was fun to see images of places we've visited along the way with
other letterboxers. Kind of like sharing visual DNA with you all!
...Dragon's "45-90" box covered in snow?...For sure not the same shot
we have taken during an August rain storm! But, I think we have the
exact shot of the famous French icon!
But our favorite has to be Doodle & Deedle's picture from Marian Park
in Ohio of the blooming redbud tree (a sure sign of spring!)... No
doubt pointing the way up those awesome wooden steps to letterbox
heaven!
It has been so long since we've been able to letterbox that it is
certain we will be more rusty and pathetic than usual at decoding
easy clues...things are so bad that Tiptoe has even resorted to
carving stamps! Some may have seen the introduction of our new
series...but others may not have...

In tribute to those who made the journey of us reaching "500 boxes
found" so delightful, we have introduced a series called "Tiptoe &
Tonto's Favorite Letterboxers." Needless to say, this could include
ALL of you...but, at the current carving rate we will have moved into
a letterboxing-assisted living environment before even a few get
finished AND planted!(BTW, our answer to SC's TOTW "What we want to
accomplish"...)

But, we are going to try our best to plant a few this spring...Be on
the lookout!...Our placement in no way shows favoritism!... It is
strictly dictated by our travel schedule coinciding with the chosen
letterboxers completed stamp...Our #1 and #2 have been planted in
Arizona...#3 and #4???? ...your guess is as good as ours! All chosen
letterboxers will remain a mystery until you actually stamp
them...Please don't spoil it for anyone by telling who exactly is
depicted.
Tiptoe....and Tonto
