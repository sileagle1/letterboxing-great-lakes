I have moved our Nutty Plunge Mystery box. I had gotten some reports that it was not being found. Upon trying last fall, I couldn't find it either, all was too overgrown. After searching for 30 min (and I had visited this box often), I gave up and decided it would have to be replaced. Being lazy, I did not get around to recarving. So yesterday, went to check and there is was. Amazing what a winter kill will do to uncover secret hiding places. Anyway, I moved it just a short distance from it's original spot, where hopefully it can be accessed year round.
