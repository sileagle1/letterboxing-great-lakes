Okay, now for something a little different...

Remember how your BINGO card started without the free space? So
today, instead of me calling a word, you get to choose any one word
to make your free space and thus eliminate it from your Bingo card!

HERE IS THE IMPORTANT PART -- you must e-mail me to let me know what
word you are choosing to eliminate. In order to be fair, all
players must choose the word they are eliminating before we move
forward with the game. To give everyone time to get in touch with
me, there will be no new words called on Saturday or Sunday this
week -- the next new word will be Monday morning. You have until
midnight on Sunday night (1/7/07) to e-mail me with the word you are
eliminating. Send your e-mail to me at letterbox@comcast.net and
please include your trailname to help me in matching up with the
correct card.

If I do not receive an e-mail from you by midnight on Sunday, the
center space on your card will automatically become your free space
(*), just like on a traditional BINGO card.

Due to the position of the words already called, there is no
possibility that this free space could give anyone a win in any of
the interim games, but you can definitely use it to your advantage
in conjunction with previously called words, if you have had any of
those on your card.

So yes, Gooble Gobble, you actually do get to pick one word off your
card to scratch through ;-)

SpringChick


(*) In the event that I do not hear from you and the middle space on
your card is a word that has already been called, then your free
space would be the one directly above the center space, etc.
