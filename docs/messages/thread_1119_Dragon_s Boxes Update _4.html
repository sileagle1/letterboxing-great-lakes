Dragon's Boxes Update #4

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-01 02:52:41 UTC

Again, for those interested (esp. Speedsquare):

Triangles! Triangles! Everywhere! is now fully operational. Check out
the updated webpage now! For those of you thinking you might never be
able to do this series - here's your best chance! The box to box
(letterboxer) clues are the easiest they've ever been! Even LL John
could do them ;-)

Weapons of Math Instruction - now all cleaned up and in much better
shape than the mushy trails you have to walk on to get them!

County Cross Country - ditto (I wonder why?)

Environmental Classroom - the rock that was frozen in place so you
couldn't get to the box has now thawed! Go for it!

One very surprising item worth mentioning was that I was finally able
to check on the report that Mathman 11 was missing and I actually
found everything in place!!! The only problem is that it relies on
some park features that the park changed right after the box was
placed, so plans are underway to compensate for the changes in the
park so this great box can be activated again. In the meantime, if
you think you know where it is located, or tried to find it in the
past and thought it was lost, the box itself, now camouflaged taped,
is right where it should be.

Oh, and I changed my log-in name on the LBNA site from Tim to The
Dragon so you can search for my boxes without wondering which Tim I am
(apparently the one with P103).

Get out and box!
Dragon
(who's wishing he had another week off of school like this one!)
