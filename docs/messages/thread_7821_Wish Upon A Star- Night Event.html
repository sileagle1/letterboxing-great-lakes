Wish Upon A Star- Night Event

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2013-11-09 22:30:32 UTC-08:00

In case you don't already have plans for next Saturday (NOV 16), you might want to attend the event in Wisconsin hosted by Seermesh.  It will be held in a suburb north of Milwaukee and should be a lot of fun.  She has listed it on AtlasQuest and sent out this info today:


*Most of the boxes are relatively easy because of the night aspect. Some will be moved after 2 weeks. There are roughly 30-40 hidden boxes on 4 loops plus bonus hidden boxes and several nearby boxes.
*There will be a drawing. You get a free entry per person at sign in, any additional entries are $1 to help cover the cost of the rental of the building.
Because of the parks rules, all boxers must be back to the beach house by no later than 10:30pm. We have the building a little later so anyone wanting to chill afterwards for a bit are welcome to, but they don't want people wandering trails. They think we are going on a family scavenger hunt party, so as long as we are respectful, we should be fine.

We're looking forward to it and hope to see many of our LB friends there!
