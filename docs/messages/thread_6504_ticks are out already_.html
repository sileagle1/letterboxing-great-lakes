From: Katrina Lustofin (drkaty@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-04-06 13:07:00 UTC-04:00
I was reading the blog of a friend of mine over the weekend, describing her
tick bite and subsequent bulls eye rash. Apparently, this is the second
time she's gotten Lyme disease and she was baffled by the difference in tick
size and coloring between her two exposures. As I was writing a comment
explaining the difference between male and female deer ticks, I felt this
tickling on my neck and immediately freaked out. Sure enough, it was a tick
-but not a deer tick.

I hate those suckers! Years of grad school in entomology taught me to
appreciate most insects and even spiders and house centipedes, but ticks are
just gross and nasty.

Thankfully, I recently applied Frontline to the dogs, so I was the only one
bringing a live tick into the house.

Katy
