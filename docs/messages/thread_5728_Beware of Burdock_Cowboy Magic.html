"Cowboy Magic" is a product for detangling hair and adding shine. Can be
used on horses, dogs, or humans. Not sure exactly what the ingredients are,
but it's a slippery, non-sticky gel and you put the proverbial "dab" in your
palms, rub your hands together, and then comb your fingers through the mane
or tail. The burrs will then easily slide out, and any snarls or tangles
are also easy to fix.

http://www.cowboymagic.com/index.html
