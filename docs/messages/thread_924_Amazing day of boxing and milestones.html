Amazing day of boxing and milestones

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-05 03:14:00 UTC

After all the rainy weather we endured last weekend while
placing boxes for our new "The Hauntings" series, I decided
to take advantage of today being a beautiful, brisk day and
went out to find some letterboxes.

On the way down to the first park, I was gifted with being
able to observe a mature Bald Eagle soaring above the
freeway for at least five minutes. He had an amazingly large
wingspan and had fully developed his white head feathers.

Great way to start the day and it only got better.
I found all of the boxes (placed by DogLvrs wow, some great
clues and stamps!) that I had planned on getting today, plus
a HH and a bonus box; which took me to a wonderful little
restaurant with unbelievable hand painted murals on all the walls.

The over-all theme is Hiking the Appalachia Trail. If you should find
the box that contains the clue to this bonus box and then go to
retrieve it, please take the time to closely examine the animal life
in the mural. The birds are so life-like, you almost expect them to
begin singing!

Then, at the end of the day, as I was adding the new images
from today's boxes to my logbook, I discovered that I had
reached F100 I knew I was getting close, but had not realized
that today would actually put me there. I hope to have my "P" number
up to 100 before we move back aboard our boat this spring.

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
P75 F100 X55
