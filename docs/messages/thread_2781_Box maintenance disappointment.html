Box maintenance disappointment

From: TrailTroll (kanderson@american-community.com) | Date: 2006-07-17 16:01:30 UTC
I checked on my Chief Pontiac this weekend, and found that the stamp
and inkpad were there, but the logbook was gone. Oh well - that
happens. What disappointed me was that at least three people have been
there since it disappeared, and nobody contacted me. I could tell
there had been visitors because one left a post-it-note and two wrote
directly on the tupperware. I know lots of people don't contact the
placer with regular finds, but it is sad that nobody could let me know
it needed maintenance. I imagine many of you have had worse
experiences, so thanks for letting me vent!

TrailTroll

I understand your venting! I have lost 2 log books and a stamp in the
past two weeks! After I heard from a boxer that she found one of my
boxes that had a bonus and she didn't mention the bonus, I checked it

stamp! Another box had the stamp and the remains of a chewed paper,

letterboxing experience. I too wish more people would "contact the
placer"!
