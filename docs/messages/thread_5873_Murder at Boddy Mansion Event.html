From: doubledragonletterboxer (M8KC5008@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-05-17 12:16:09 UTC
Wow! That was quite a challenging event, but we loved every minute of it (even the mud soaked shoes ;) ). Luckily we had some of the best brains in the LB bizz to help us crack codes (Spartan Fans and Village Idiots)! The only thing that made us sad was that we couldn't finish the whole thing due to dinner plans. We had brought some newbies (the Village Idiots) with us that took up the challenge of crime solving and they proved to be quite the sleuths. Thanks for the fun day guys!

Trailtroll, Lunagirl, and Papa you really outdid yourselves!! This was the most complicated LB event I have seen and it looks like it was a hoot to put together!! This was definitely advanced LBing and we enjoyed the challenge!! It really took the "ho-hum" out of the typical event. It definitely rekindled my love affair with LBing, and gave me some new ideas of my own... so look out >:)
I'm glad that you thought of me to do a carving for this event, it was my pleasure. Everyone did a fantastic job carving, the stamps were meticulous!! Haha, I'm glad I didn't get a Room to carve... those looked hard!!
Great stamps, great clues, and great people ...what more could you ask for? Thanks for hosting this event!!

P.S Sorry we didn't really get to talk to you Team Rabbit :(, but we wanted to hit the trails asap before the lil dragon started shooting fire out her nose, lol! We hope to get together soon :) Much Luv,

Double Dragon
