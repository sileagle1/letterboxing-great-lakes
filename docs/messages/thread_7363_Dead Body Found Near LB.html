Dead Body Found Near LB

From: Beetle (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2012-03-13 21:31:39 UTC
In 2006, I hid a letterbox entitled Three Eyed Ax Murderer. Now in 2012, a woman's dead body was found on Sunday not far from the location of my letterbox. The body was found approximately 20 yards 'off the trail.' People frequently jog, bike, and walk their dogs along this wooden stretch, but why was she 20 yards off the trail? This police story is giving me the willies! I have updated that letterbox and asked people to avoid the area which is now a police investigation site. Who do I contact for information about the Letterboxer's Bail Fund should I need to do some explaining?
