When you're out at happy hours or at dinner tonight, ask your bartender or
wait staff for "one less beer." It might raise some eyebrows until you
explain that you'd like a glass of water and that you'd like to place the
price of your beer (or martini or glass of wine) into the donation canister.
And what self-respecting establishment isn't going to have a canister for
flood relief donations? - if they don't, ask them to start one. Tell your
friends, tell your bartender - heck, tell people you don't know - about the
one-less-beer campaign.



As you're enjoying your glass of water, think about all of the people who
can't get clean water and who have no homes. "One less beer" by just a few
people might mean one more blanket, one more meal, one more shot of insulin,
one more glimmer of hope.



We can do it. Again and again.



For those of you who don't drink alcohol, try the one-less technique on
bagels, coffee, dessert, cosmetics, pet treats, etc.
