Just a reminder that with the fall season comes a better chance of
getting a nasty "hitch hiker" of a different kind! TICKS!
I had the unfortuante luck of picking up such a critter this past
weekend while out boxing...and I really didn't do that much
bushwhacking to merit such a beast!

Fortunately, Tonto noticed it before I was sucked dry! But, he makes
a much better letterbox finder than a tick extractor...as a result of
his botched attempt at surgery, I had to go to the doctor to get it
professionally excised! Not a fun experience!

So, as you enjoy those crisp, autumn days of boxing beware the
tiny "spoilers"! Give yourself a good examination once you return
from the trails...also check those letterbox dogs too! These creepy
crawlers are a real "cootie" you don't want to take home with you!
