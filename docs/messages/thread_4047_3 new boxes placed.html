From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-05-12 06:08:28 UTC
We've got our clues posted for the 3 boxes we placed last weekend. Our
new goal is to get a box in every county that borders WI. We therefore
planted boxes in two MN counties on MAY 5 & 6. We're planning to get
to the other 2 "boxless" MN counties that border WI in early July, as
well as the one remaining Iowa "boxless" county that borders WI.

It will be interesting to see how long it takes for someone to find
these boxes, since they obviously aren't a hotbed of letterboxing
action. We still haven't heard anything about 3 of the boxes we placed
in northwestern WI in November.

On the other hand, my latest WI box is right along I-90/94 for those of
you who might travel that route this summer and are looking for a
diversion....
