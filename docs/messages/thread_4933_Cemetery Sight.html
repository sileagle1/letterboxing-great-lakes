Cemetery Sight

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-04-14 13:26:33 UTC
Late yesterday afternoon we went by the place where we just planted
Cemetery Stroll #4. There were a number of cars in the area, with a
group of people gathered in the cemetery. We thought it was odd to
have a burial on a Sunday afternoon. As we got closer, we saw several
people standing amongst the gravestones wearing capes and greenish-
blackish makeup on their faces. And another person or two with
camcorders. Guess someone is making a little movie. And I thought it
might look odd/suspicious if people were letterboxing in a
cemetery!!:~).

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs
