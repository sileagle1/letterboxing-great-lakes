Hi everyone. I've recently received several "can't find it" emails
regarding the 'Laika' and 'Strongheart' boxes in Maybury Park in
Northville MI.
I'm happy to report that a local 'boxer found both boxes safe and
sound in their hiding places last week. I had removed the clues for
Strongheart because based on the emails it had seemed to be MIA. I
will repost the clues in case those who gave up trying to find the
boxes get the urge to try again.

We have a graco travel system.

to cause further damage to the stroller.

be a good alternative.

From: TJ_Mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2007-03-20 15:01:14 UTC-07:00
Still, though, I'd have to say that the Laika, Strongheart, and Lovebirds boxes at Maybury are among the more stroller-friendly in the area, since most of the trail is a paved biking path. (The clues mention this, so I don't feel I'm giving away any secrets here.) Other boxes in the park are on the hiking trails, so a more trail-ready stroller or a carrier would definitely be preferable for those. Your 9 year old will surely enjoy any of them, though!!
