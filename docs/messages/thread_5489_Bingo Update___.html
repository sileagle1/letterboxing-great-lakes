Bingo Update...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-12-30 14:30:14 UTC
Only a couple more days until the first Bingo word is called -- do you
have your dauber ready? Have you read the FAQ? If you have any
questions about the game, please feel free to throw them out to the
list -- somebody else may be wondering the same thing.

I currently have 59 Bingo cards in my hands. Since this is everyone
who is showing in the database and today is the deadline, I am
assuming nothing is floating around in the mail and this is the final
group. However, if you did mail last minute and have not gotten
around to adding yourself to the database, please drop me an e-mail to
let me know your card is still on the way.

If there is anyone else who has just been so busy with the holidays
and has all of a sudden realized the Bingo due date is upon us and
still wants to join in the fun, please e-mail me TODAY and we will see
what we can work out.

Thanks again for all the cool stuff sent in -- it's going to be a
great round of Great Lakes LB Bingo!

SpringChick
