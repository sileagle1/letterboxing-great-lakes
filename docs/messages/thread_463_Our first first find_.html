Just had to share the excitement. Deedle & I were out
letterboxing today, and we had our first First Find!
The box is a mystery box, so I don't want to say too
much about it, but it was placed in honor of someone's
wedding, which made our finding it even more apropos,
because one year ago today was when we first met, at
our friends' rehersal dinner. The bride & I were had
literally known each other forever (our parents went
to high school together); the groom & Jeff were
college buddies. And they paired Jeff & me together
in the bridal party, never thinking that we'd start
dating :)

Hope everyone's day's been as good as ours...

Doodle & Deedle, aka Diane & Jeff.
