The Rattle In The Woods - Has been removed from it's hiding place.

Sad to say, I went out on a routine maintenance tour of the trail,
and found that someone had trampled to pieces the letterboxes hiding
place... Sheesh.. Little further investigation showed me that it was
some local kids due to conditions of other items along the trail.
Luckily I found the box and contents thrown to the bottom of the
hill, all in good shape, but strewn about. Which leads me to believe
this was a very recent incident.
The last folks into the logbook were on Sept. 1st.
I plan on replacing the box in the very near future to a location
closeby the original location, and will post updated clues, etc. when
that is accomplished.

Spring Chick - You may want to confirm the locations and conditions
of your boxes in "Duncan's Woods" as well.
