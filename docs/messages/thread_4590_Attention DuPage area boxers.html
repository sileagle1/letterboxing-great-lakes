Hey Folks,
The following (edited) message was received regarding letterboxes in
DuPage County Preserve.

---------------------
(snips)
"We want to get as many people out to the preserves, enjoying
the beauty of nature. Letterboxing is a great way to get people
started
on visiting the preserves or keeping them coming out. When we noticed
how many people were coming out for geo-caching we went through this
same process. They have one main website that we worked with and got
on
the same page with the people placing caches. They went though the
Forest Preserve District to get all caches and locations approved (they
all have our stickers on them) and they are registered with us in case
we have any issues with their caches. I was hoping to get all of the
people placing Letterboxes in DuPage County do go through the same
approval process.

Your LbNA website seems to be the biggest
organization posting locations and clues for boxes.

(He refers to LbNA, not the Great Lakes Yahoo list)

If you would
contact the people posting at the site so they could get our approval
to
place letterboxes I would really appreciate it.

(snips)

we would really like to
get all of these formally approved and make sure they are following all
regulations & guidelines we have in place. Thanks again for all of
your
assistance!

Dan Hebreard
Forest Preserve District of DuPage County
Senior Ranger
East Sector

------------------------------------


So, there you have it.

Questions? Contact me or the Senior Ranger.

Thanks,

azobox
