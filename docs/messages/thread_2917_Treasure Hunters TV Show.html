Treasure Hunters TV Show

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-08 13:40:54 UTC-07:00
OK, so someone on this list had posted about this show
when it first started airing. Has anyone else gotten
as hooked on it as I am? I keep telling Deedle it's
like Extreme Letterboxing. As tempted as I've been at
times, we've never spent 5 hours looking for a box's
hiding place.

Anyway, the letterboxing similarities got even closer
when the one team (The Geniuses, for those who watch)
started bickering in the car as they're driving to
their next destination. Stuff like, "OK, turn
northeast at this next intersection." "What direction
did you say to turn in? Northwest?" "No, that's not
what I said." "Will you just tell me what direction
to turn?" (Voices start to raise) "Northeast."
"That's what I needed to know!" "I told you that in
the beginning!" (and then they still got lost ;)

I turned to Deedle and said, "Hey, that sounds just
like us when we letterbox!!"

We have never gotten as bad as The Southie Boys,
though, who I thought really were going to start
throwing punches instead of going inside the building
to look for their clue.

Doodle Bug.
