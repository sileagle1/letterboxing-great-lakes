Re: [LbGLK] Textures

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-11-19 07:20:43 UTC-05:00
I have used needles and paperclips to do texture/shading. Just poke the needle in over and over in a small space. It takes a little experimenting for needle size and depth, but works well. Another thing I have done is to drag the Speedball "V" tool across the surface with just a little pressure in a cross-hatch pattern (i.e. parallel line and then parallel swipes at ninety degrees to the first set. Again a little experimenting is needed to get the pressure down, so you're leaving some marks but not removing chunks of carving material.

SpringChick
