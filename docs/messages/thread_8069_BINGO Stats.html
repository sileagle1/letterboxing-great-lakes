Yes!  I love cryptic crosswords too... The monthly ones in WSJ Puzzles are about the only ones I have time to do lately.

 

Here's a great explanation of how cryptic crosswords work, for anyone interested:

 

https://www.pennydellpuzzles.com/upload/documents/How%20to%20Solve%20Cryptic%20XWD.pdf 

 

This guide helped me get started a few years ago, because the way cryptic crosswords work is not immediately obvious when you first see them. Each clue actually has 2 parts, but you don't know what the 2 parts are.  And the WSJ puzzles are variety cryptics - meaning they each have an extra twist, in addition to being cryptic crosswords.  Lots of head-scratching but fun!
