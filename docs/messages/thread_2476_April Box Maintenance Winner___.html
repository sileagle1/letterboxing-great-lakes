April Box Maintenance Winner...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-05-07 12:48:40 UTC
Yesterday at the Spring Fling gathering a name was drawn from all of
those who made entries in the April box maintenance database for
checking in on their letterboxes during the month of April.

There were over 200 entries, nearly half of which were Dragon -- so a
huge pat of the back goes to Dragon for getting out and checking on
all of those letterboxes! But despite the fact that he dominated the
entries, it was not his name drawn out.

The winning entry was Howling Coyote for checking on the Stockman
House box in Mason City, Iowa on 4/15/06.

Howling Coyote, please send me an e-mail offlist with your mailing
address and I will get the handmade journal out in the mail to you.

Thanks everyone for the great job of checking on boxes!
