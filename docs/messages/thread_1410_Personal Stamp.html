From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-06-10 14:47:37 UTC-04:00
My very first stamp was store-bought (can you believe I actually found a stamp that said "SpringChick"?). I replaced that after only a one or so with a hand-carved one once I realized that carving wasn't all that difficult. I used that one until 100 at which time I began using the stamp I am using now. My F100 box has both the old one and the new one stamped in. I meant to switch every 100 finds, but my F200 was up in the U.P. and I don't keep very close track of numbers and didn't even realize I was getting close, so didn't have a new stamp ready for the occasion. Since then I have stopped keeping track of numbers all together and don't really have any idea what F# I am even at. I like the stamp I have now and will probably continue using it for a while yet.

I did carve up a tiny stamp a while back that I carry with me and use in micro-boxes because mine is too large for some of the very tiny logs/scrolls.

It is simply a matter of preference... If you're ready for a change, go for it!

SpringChick
