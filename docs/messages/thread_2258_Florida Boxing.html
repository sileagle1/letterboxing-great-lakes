Florida Boxing

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-02-24 03:33:38 UTC
We just got back from sunny Ft. Myers. We've always enjoyed going to
a local park there--Lakes Park--and were excited to see that they had
several boxes placed there by StarSaels: Walk in the Park and Light
as a Feather (which we found). Were a little bummed to discover that
we will just miss being able to go to a gathering there this coming
weekend. We left a few boxes there for the event, though.

Went to Sanibel and found "You Might See an Eagle" by LindaFay. It
was a little challenging to get to the box since there was a group of
birders standing right by it watching the eagles (adult and juvenile)
and 3 pileated woodpeckers. They finally left and we were able to get
to the box. Have been a lot of HH in the box. One note said that one
particular HH had been carved by Azobox--can't remember the exact name
but it had Rider in the title. No HH waiting for us though.

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs
