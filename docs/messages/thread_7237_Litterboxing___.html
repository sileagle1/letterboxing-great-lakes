Litterboxing???

From: Chris Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2011-12-28 14:40:36 UTC-06:00
No snow, 30-degree temperature, and minimal wind today, so I decided to take Harry for a walk in the woods and check on one of my low-traffic Zodiac boxes. I found it right where it should have been, but completely uncovered. Upon checking the logbook, I found the last entry (from 2010) as follows: "Found this by accident and removed it. Replaced reluctantly, as I DO NOT LITTER."

Well whoop de do. I don't litter either!

I slipped the box back into its hiding place and covered it up with bark and sticks.

Trail Rider (Chris)
