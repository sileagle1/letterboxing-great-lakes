From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-13 14:17:50 UTC-06:00
Certainly not amusing, but definitely a memorial experience...finding dead animals! I just wasn't expecting to look in the end of a hollow log where I had previously hidden a letterbox and find an old, chewed up, decaying pile of fur and flesh. Sorry if that's too graphic. I'm lookig forward to reading the comments of others. Wisconsin Hiker's question is a good way of getting us to focus back on the fun things about letterboxing....those unexpected, amusing experiences that are the bonus to seeking a box.
