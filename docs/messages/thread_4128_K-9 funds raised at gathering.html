K-9 funds raised at gathering

From: Scoutdogs (DogScouts@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-06-03 15:35:28 UTC-04:00
I just wanted to thank the generous folks at the gathering this past weekend. $68 dollars was raised with the help of the K-9 fundraiser letterbox. This will be added to the funds already collected to get a custom fit bullet proof vest for a police K-9 near Cincinnati Ohio. The vests are $1800 each! And we only need a little over $100 more to be able to purchase the vest!

I'll be updating the amount raised on the website very soon. If you'd like to learn more about it or see a photo of the recipient, or want to make a donation... please visit my Dog Scout Troop's website: www.DogScoutTroop107.com

Donations of $20 or more still can get a custom patch (photo of the patch is on the website.) Just be sure to include your return address and a stamp to cover the postage.

Thank you all again! We had a GREAT time!
