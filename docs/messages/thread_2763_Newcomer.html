From: brat_pack07 (kevjean@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-11 15:29:32 UTC
My family and I just started letterboxing this past Sunday. We had
such a good day!!! On our first "quest" (as the kids put it), we got
to meet Wormy Wander!!! It was so cool!!! We did 5 letterboxes that
day, and found a hitchhiker as well!!! This was so awesome!!! By the
4th one we did, we finally agreed on a trail name... "Brat Pack". My
mom has been printed stacks and stacks of clues for anywhere and
everywhere and we plan on doing several on the weekends. The kids love
it so much, and each have their own stamp. I thank Wormy Wanderer for
letting us know about this group, and we are looking forward to meeting
you all either through gatherings or even out and about!! =) Happy
letterboxing!! =)
