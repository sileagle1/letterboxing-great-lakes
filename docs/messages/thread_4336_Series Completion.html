From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2007-08-07 01:58:15 UTC
After placing my first stamp in this series on April 24th and dragging
my heels and what-not I've finally planted the last two stamps in the
Channel Cat series!!

Posted on both AQ and LBNA for your enjoyment.

If you just find my Channel Cat series (in its entirety) you will find
9 stamps. If you find the boxes within walking distance from other
letterboxers you will find another 6 more stamps and if you bring your
bike you will find another 2 boxes.

So you will enjoy the Quad Cities from the Mississippi River. You will
stop in 5 cities and possibly find 17 boxes!! Sounds like fun (I just
had someone from New Mexico find all but the two I planted today and
got great feedback).

Also, you can get to all of them by car in the off season, however, I
plan on pulling the one from the Channel Cat itself by Labor Day.

Hart x6
