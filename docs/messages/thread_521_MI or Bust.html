From: funhog1 (funhog1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 16:13:45 UTC
Greetings all! I'm making my appearance here to clear up some possible confusion.
Last Spring ('03,) I sent a guest letterbox to the Upper Great Lakes Gathering. It was a
joy to see all the stamps in the log when it returned home. Thinking that this box
should find a new home here in my home state of Oregon, I rehid it in Milo McIver
State Park. I carved a little (LO) stamp and glued it to the back of a shiny penny for
folks to add to the image. The history of the box's travels was included in the clue:
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=4803&boxname=MI(LO)_or_Bust!

Well, last week it came to my attention that one of our local boxers here created a
letterbox and sent it to MI to be hidden. Lo and behold... What name did she give it?
MI or Bust! Needless to say, I am quite taken aback. I want anyone who got the first
image in their journals to know that this is not the same letterbox that they saw at
the gathering. I knew nothing about this second box and am very sorry that it bears
the name of my event box.

I also want to say that you guys are so lucky to be able to go after Spring Chicks's
letterboxes! I am sooooo.... jealous! Anyone want to take up a collection and buy me
a plane ticket? Funhog
