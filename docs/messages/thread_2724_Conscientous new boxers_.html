Conscientous new boxers!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-06-29 20:10:56 UTC
I have been VERY pleased in the last week to receive reports on 3
different boxes that we placed very far from our home. I really
appreciate it when we receive messages via the "Contact the Placer"
feature on LBNA and am excited that many new boxers are taking the
time to send a report and that they are sending some good info. I
wanted to share the 3 I received recently, so you could see the
enthusiasm of some new boxers in the region:

Biking Bear Minocqua, WI
My wife and I and three kids found this letterbox today. The walk
over the old train trestle was beautiful and the history added to the
interest for the kids (7,5, and 3). The box is still in good
condition and it appears that about three others have found it since
it was placed. We come up this direction once a year and this is the
first year that we have looked for letterboxes. The area could use a
few more so we are considering adding some. It was fun, thank you.

Goat Prairie Trempeleau, WI
Hello-Since this is the first letterbox we searched for and found we
wanted to let you know how much fun we had! We are two sisters who
decided to start this up with our children (Ages 8,3, and 5 months).
This was quite a hike but finding your box and the HitchHiker made it
all worth it. We will definitely be searching for letterboxes in the
very near future. Thanks for the fun and the exercise!!!!

Highest Bluff McGregor, Iowa
I visited your letterbox on June 24th (Saturday). It was in
excellent condition. The last stamp before me was a year ago. The
park was a lovely sight. I enjoyed my afternoon there. I only
heard about letterboxing a couple of weeks ago and went on a couple
hunts and caught the bug. It is so much fun to figure out the clues.


Reports like this encourage us to continue placing boxes, even in
areas that are far from our home.
