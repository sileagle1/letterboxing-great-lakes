Gathering Potluck

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-05-29 16:04:23 UTC
So far, the weather forecast looks pretty good for next Saturday - low
to mid-70s and no rain.

We're therefore planning to use the big grill at the lodge. We'll
bring some hotdogs/sausages & buns and Shooting Star has also signed
up to bring hotdogs and buns. If you haven't already decided on
something to bring, it might be good to have a few more people
bringing hot dogs/sausages & buns since our expected attendee count is
now 130 people!

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. Don't forget your lawn chairs if you can fit them in your car.
We'll need some extra seating due to the size of the group.

Link to potluck page:
http://www.2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com/potluck.html
