Take a trip down south, 
Towards the middle of the state. 
Don't forget to bring Maggie,
And try not to be late. 

Please stop the scribblings, 
The drawings and scratches,
Marge's husband is busy, 
Sketching a plan as it hatches.

Well not really anymore, 
This year was out in the wild,
Still, lots of fun was in store.
