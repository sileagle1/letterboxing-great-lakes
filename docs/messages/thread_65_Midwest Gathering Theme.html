Midwest Gathering Theme

From: Duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-01 03:54:40 UTC
Although the specific date and location are still being determined,
I think I'm ready to start taking suggestions for a "theme" for the
gathering. Last year the theme was "Visit Michigan!" I suppose we
could go with "Visit Illinois!" for this year, but I'm open to other
suggestions. Please post your ideas to the group.

Remember, the theme will help give direction to other letterboxers
that will provide stamps and boxes for the event.

Also, John from the Leapin' Lizards has agreed to make the event
stamp. This does not mean that there can't be more than one. You
can also make and hide boxes for the event yourself as well.

Thanks for your input!
Dragon
