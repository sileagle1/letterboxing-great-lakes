I-94 Update

From: m_clan_lbers (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-30 13:49:34 UTC
I'm pretty excited about the results so far. As of now, boxes in the
I-94 Series will pop up in Detroit (Trail Troll), Ann Arbor (The
Wulfes), SW Mich (Pale Puppy Posse), S. Chi/NW IN (Tiptoe & Tonto),
Chicago (Howling Coyote), Madison (M Clan), MaustonWI (M Clan), Twin
Cities (Sqwubb), and Fargo (Dragonfly). Thanks to all the soon-to-be
contributors.

It'd be great to have a Port Huron box. I may be there in Aug, but
can't promise a plant. We could also use a Milwaukee box. Other than
that we'll have frequent stops between Port Huron, MI and Minneapolis.

The discussion for this series will move to a primary location of
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/freewayletterboxing from now on. I've
set it up to eventually allow for other Freeway Series - something
along I-75 would be very cool!

Thanks for all the future plants,
M Clan
