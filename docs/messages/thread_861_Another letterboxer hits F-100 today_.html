From: Karen Ruhl (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-08 14:46:00 UTC-08:00
I am sorry for the cross-posting, but I just had to share my enthusiasm.
I'm actually at F-101 today. My first find was April 16th of this year,
and most have been found in Indiana - a state that currently lists about
150 boxes. My farthest find was "Don't Mess with Texas" in July, but I
also have some from Ohio. Big thanks to the faraway finders of My Dora
series, Connecticut? Since the hobby is just growing fast here now, I
have had the privilege of many First Finders.
