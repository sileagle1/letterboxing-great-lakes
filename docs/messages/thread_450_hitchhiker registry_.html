From: lesann847 (l-fischer@northwestern.edu) | Date: 2004-07-26 20:33:23 UTC
This is good information for everyone to have, so I'll post to the
list. Wanda and Pete's site for tracking HH activity is:

http://alum.wpi.edu/~p_miner/travelers

Also, you can subscribe to a yahoo group. Not much chat, more
informative than anything:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox_travellers/

I always send a note to the letterboxer who launched the HH and
an email to Pete so that he can update the website. I like to track
the HH's I've found and those I've launched myself.
