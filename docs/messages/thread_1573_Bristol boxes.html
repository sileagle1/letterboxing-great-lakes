From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-06 02:42:29 UTC
Alas, another sucessful season of the Bristol Ren Faire came to a
close today. Thanks to all of you that came to find the letterboxes
and letterpeople. A preliminary count shows me that over 40
letterboxers found some or all of the boxes! We had local people as
well as letterboxers from the east coast! It would seem I'll need to
put THREE sheets of paper in the boxes for next season, as the two
sheets were barely enough! It was also great to see that my friends
the >>-CC-> Family have finally started letterboxing too! Another
great thing is that, although some boxes were not quite in their
original hiding spots (although close), none of the boxes went missing
all season! Sorry that today was the only day I could get to the
faire to meet anyone, and only one family found me today. Hopefully
my car won't break down next year!

Hip Hip Huzzah!
Dragon

The letterpeople hang on to their own stamps. If they don't have
them, they must've either lost them and didn't tell me, or they forgot
them. Sorry, but both happens. Not much I can do about it.

what make the Bristol boxes fun! Here's your chance to work in
stealth mode - or as shutterfly found out, to heck with stealth, just

From: jigsaw_tom (jigsaw_tom@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-20 12:42:24 UTC
Beetle, the clues can be found via Dragon's website. All are in
place and easily findable IF you read the clues carefully. I was at
the faire last weekend and I was able to nab them all. Jane the
Phoole and the Knight of Swords are participating, but didn't have
thier stamps on them. They said they would have them this weekend.
(I did ask, but alas). The stamp images are amazing and the faire in
and of itself was absolutely fun. I too was a Faire Virgin until
then. It took me a bit to get the guts to go to these areas and get
the boxes as people were always "looking". Shutterfly's advice is
correct. NO ONE CARES nor really is watching. Use caution but not
so much that you are a wreck about it. Happy Boxing!!! Jigsaw
