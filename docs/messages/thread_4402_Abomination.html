I was reading "Book Page"--a book review magazine given away at our
library. The tagline for a book called "Abomination" read: Somewhere
in a secret geocache lies the unspeakable clue. Apparently, the book
is about a serial killer who posts clues about his next victim on a
geocaching website. From reading a review, it also seems that the
killer uploads the GPS coordinates of the dead body on the geocaching
website as well. Certainly wouldn't want to be looking for that
geocache! I don't like creepy/scary serial killer books, so I don't
plan on looking for the book, but thought it had an interesting twist.
