"Around the World in 80 Days" status/prep for 2006

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-15 15:12:51 UTC
The Travel Staff at "Around the World in 80 Days" is finalizing tour
plans for the 2006 travel season.
Ports of call at about half the fifteen destinations have been
reviewed and have been certified for spring and summer tourists. Staff
is planning site visits at the remaining locations.

Previous and successful travelers are advised that their comments left
at the boxes thus far certified, have been read and noted. The Travel
Staff is impressed with the number of visitors' stamps observed.

Comments, both affirmative and disparaging, about a certain "crossing
point" will be studied. However staff who conducted this week's site
visits did agree with some visitor comments. Inspectors did not agree
wth some others. Staff was certainly amused.

More to come in the days/weeks ahead.
Website updates are planned.
