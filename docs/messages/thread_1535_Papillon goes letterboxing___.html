Hey, Papillon actually went letterboxing with me today - and it only cost me
about $57 worth of bribery ("If I take you shopping, will you go

regularly $79 and $99, for $13.77 and $17.77!!!

Howling Coyote beat us again! She must prowl the neighborhoods like a real
coyote to find all the new boxes. We were there at about 1:30 today. What
time were you there, HC?



I took her to

Gephyrophobia and we both walked both ways. That traffic is so freaky!
