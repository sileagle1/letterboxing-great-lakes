2007 GL Gathering Location Poll

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-30 22:18:28 UTC
Of the states that have not previously hosted, there have been two
volunteers for hosting the 2007 GL Gathering to be held on Saturday,
June 2, 2007.

I have created a poll for group members to choose between these two
locations. To access the poll, go to the group online and click on
*polls* in the left-hand navigation menu. Each group member may
vote one time. You can change your vote at any time before the poll
closes on August 31, 2006. At the end of the month, the location
that receives the most votes will be chosen as the host location.

A huge thanks goes to both of the letterboxers who have stepped
forward and volunteered to host the gathering. This is a big
undertaking, but surely a rewarding one. I know I am not alone when
I say that past gathering hosts are more than happy to answer
questions as you plan the event. We have been fortunate to have had
several outstanding events over the past few years and it is
certainly something we look forward to each June.

Good luck to Minnesota and Ohio!

SpringChick
