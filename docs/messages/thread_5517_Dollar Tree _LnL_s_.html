Re: [LbGLK] Dollar Tree "LnL's"

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-07 22:45:44 UTC-06:00
The first batch of look alike LnL boxes I bought had green seals, and the later ones I bought last fall had blue seals. Yes, they have ridges and the "wings" snap over all four sides of the box. The lid fits firmly in place because the bottom of the box fits into a groove in the lid where the seal is located.

I have a basic method for 'testing' boxes before I buy them. I seal them and then squeeze them and listen if any air escapes. Some food storage containers don't seal well. I did not hear any air escaping from these boxes, so I bought some....well, actually I bought a dozen. The boxes are crystal clear and the top of the lid is relatively flat - good for attaching identification labels.

Beetle

We'll all be watching the Dollar Tree stores for these boxes. I think they are called Seal N Lock. I have on favorite Dollar Tree in Wauconda/Island Lake area in Illinois because it's always well stocked to the ceiling. I haven't been there recently. The best thing about these boxes is the design. It's virtually impossible to leave a corner open for water to seep in. Some boxes are difficult to open and therefore the boxes don't always get sealed properly. Not the fault of the finders, as a placer I feel it's in my own best interest to use a securely sealing container.
