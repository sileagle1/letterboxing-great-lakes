Bristol update

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-28 01:00:10 UTC
I just returned from Bristol (Friday, 7:30pm) having checked up on the
boxes there and to place new ones.

I replaced the missing shoes and striped tights and placed them in
better hiding locations. I also checked on all the other boxes and
they are doing just fine.

At the request of the faire, I have also made a series of boxes for
patrons to try. The clues to these boxes will not only be up on my
webpage, but Bristol will also be printing and providing them to the
patrons. (Only the clues to the three new boxes, not the ones there
before.) The handout will have info about letterboxing (what to do,
what not to do) and the clues and a place to stamp the box stamp, and
me for an email contact.

The three new boxes are themed "Here Be Dragons" and feature stamps of
the three dragons on the Bristol map. They are regular letterboxes
with a stamp pad inside for the patrons to use. I don't know how long
they will last, so get 'em while they are there!

Bristol update

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-22 03:59:09 UTC
I spent most of the day planting the 50 boxes at Bristol for the
weekend. I was hoping to have time to update the clues Monday night
before leaving for Door Cty but that won't happen. I will be bringing
my laptop and working on them at night. I'll upload them here and/or
on my website when I can, so check for updates.

As a side note, Bristol is VERY excited about the letterboxes being
there again - I believe to the point of me being able to leave all the
boxes there for more than one weekend. Therefore I am planning to
leave all the boxes in place until further notice, maybe for the rest
of the season. So those of you who cannot make it this weekend,
here's your chance to plan another weekend.

Bristol update

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-07 19:59:44 UTC
Ok, I got a reply from the Bristol management, and it didn't say one
way or another about leaving all the boxes there or not, so I guess
I'll just leave them all there for the foreseeable future. So if you
haven't gotten there yet, you still have a chance.

I also got the following from Barefoot Ankh:

"I just signed my company up for the Bristol
Renaissance Faire Premium Partners Ticket Program.
While I was signing us up I asked if an account could
be set up for groups (i.e. letterboxers) or only
companies. They said that one could be set up for
letterboxers! It would mean LbIL or LbGLK could have
a discount code for buying tickets online at the
faire's website. The prices would be $12 adult/$7.50
child instead of $18.95/$9.50. The code would be good
for the rest of this season and should also be good
for future seasons."

I've asked him for more info, but there may be a good deal in the
future for us!
