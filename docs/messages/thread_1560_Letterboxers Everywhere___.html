Letterboxers Everywhere...

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-05 06:44:29 UTC-07:00
So this past weekend, Deedle & I took a break from
letterboxing to participate in the 2nd annual Rails n'
Trails Bike Tour. It's held in the Cuyahoga Valley
here in Northeastern Ohio. You & your bike get loaded
onto the Cuyahoga Valley Scenic Railroad train, and
you're taken to Akron. From there, you leave the
train & bike the 27 miles back to your car along the
towpath, stopping about halfway through for a
spaghetti lunch served by the bike club that sponsors
this tour.

That morning as we were waiting to board the train, I
saw someone out of the corner of my eye who looked
really familiar. "Hey, isn't that Blaze?" I asked
Deedle. Sure enough, one of the other participants in
the tour was Blaze from Ohio, who we'd just met a few
weeks ago saying good-bye to area letterboxer
Gamecock. So of course, as we boarded the train we
said hello to her and talked letterboxing on the way
to Akron.

Letterboxing - it follows you everywhere! :)

Doodle Bug.
