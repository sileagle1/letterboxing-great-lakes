WHO CAN PLAY:
1. Players must be members of the Great Lakes yahoogroup and there
is no longer a requirement to get a box placed in MI by Thanksgiving.

2. You can play as an individual, a family, or a team but please
don't enter yourself in more than one category (ie: individual
answers as well as team and/or family answers)

PRIZES for the Quiz Game:
1st prize a gift certificate from Stampeaz, the PZ Kut folks
2nd prize a Staedtler-Mars Grand eraser
3rd prize #11 carving blades
4th prize #1 carving nibs


RULES:
1. Go to http://tinyurl.com/yn38r2 where you will find a file
called, " Unboxed MI counties QUIZ GAME" (if the link doesn't work,
you can just go to our group homepage and click on files in the left-
side menu)HINT: there was another message posted that you might find
helpful.

2. Find the answers to the quiz questions and email them to me
privately. DO NOT POST YOUR ANSWERS TO THE LIST!!

3. There is a question for each "unboxed" MI county plus at least one
bonus question and even a few extra credit points available. For
each correct answer you will be awarded a "point" which will
translate into your name on a slip of paper (ie: the more points,
the more slips of paper with your name) All slips of paper with
names will go into the drawing. Winners will be selected at random
by my Grandson in two weeks ... on Saturday, November 4th (2006)
and announced on the list by 11pm that night.
