Hi all,

We had a blast this weekend! It was so great to finally meet the
people behind the stamps we've seen around the GLK. Not only were the

and there were several creative logbooks as well (we especially liked
the deck of cards).

Big thanks to Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man for all of their efforts
in organizing the event!
