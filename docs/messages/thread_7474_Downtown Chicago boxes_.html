From: Kate J (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-06-04 00:22:31 UTC
There are many great boxes in and around Chicago, some are more difficult than others. There should be some in walking distance, but check the last find date as many go missing.
Another idea would be some of the Metra line boxes (FungusWoman and Beetle have done some, plus many others. On weekends, you can get an unlimited ticket, so you can ride the train, pop off for some quick boxes in walking distance, then hop on the next train going out or back.
Many of us will be up near Madison for the GLG, but have fun in Chicago and good hunting!
