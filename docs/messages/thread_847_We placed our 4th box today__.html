We've gotten into a placing groove.

Sometimes it is just as challenging placing boxes as finding them.
Especially with four kids hanging off of you. If you ever see me
smile and wave. I'll be the one with one in the sling, one skipping
on ahead, and two more trying to fit into a one person stroller. I'll
even exchange provided no one has unpacked our bag and lost all the
ink and stamps under the bed. (sigh)

You can check out clues on the LBNA site, under Buffalo Creek. My 5yo
daughter helped with the carving.

From: Timbertoes (toomey@genevaonline.com) | Date: 2004-11-07 00:02:45 UTC

You're a brave woman. I'm sure most things are more challenging with
four kids hanging off you!

Yesterday my two kids and I went to check on some of our boxes that
they had helped to place. We took along our 8th grade neighbor boy
and let him follow the clues to the boxes. I practically had to hang
on to the kids' jackets and put my hand over their mouths to keep
them from running ahead or giving away clues. They were jumping up
and down and giggling - it was hysterical! Our neighbor was a real
trooper and even said he'd like to help us look for some others in
the area next time we go out. Nice kid.
