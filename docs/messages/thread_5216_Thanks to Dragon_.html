Re: [LbGLK] Re: Thanks to Dragon!

From: Kermit (kermitboxer@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-07-27 21:37:38 UTC-05:00
I add a hearty third to this sentiment! Thank you, Dragon, so much, for
creating and maintaining such a wonderful set of stamps.

We found 12 boxes today (my personal one-day best, ha!), only had one miss
(attempt but no find), got lightly sunburned, watched the joust, watched
Dirk & Guido, had my husband pulled on stage by Dirk & Guido (and then he
had to learn to flex, stand and strut in a manly way, and then kiss me
rather publicly, which I found terribly funny and embarrassing at the same
time), shot bows and arrows (I have a welt on my arm from the bow to prove
it!), ate WAY too much food, including the largest turkey leg I have ever
seen and two pickles on a stick, and generally had a complete blast.

It's been several years since I've been to the Ren Faire; won't let that
mistake happen again! We plan to go again and try for more boxes during our
next free weekend (which is in August). We stopped by the Wishing Well on
the way out; there were at least seven or eight boxers at the Faire
yesterday, and seven today! Sorry I didn't see any of you, but I hope you
all had as much fun as we did.

As Calvin (of he and Hobbs) says, "I consider this day seized! Tomorrow,
let's seize the day and throttle it!"

-mph (Kermit)

From: Wendy Brem (wendybrem@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-28 04:51:09 UTC-07:00
What a BLAST!!! We found 24! And yes, I'm going back next Saturday (this time with my hubby & daughters) for more :) I think the most fun was introducing it to my friend's nephew. He was so into it that even after the Faire was done he wanted to keep looking for more :)

Kermit, your day sounded much like mine...sunburn, Dirk & Guido, Jousting, eating...and it had been a few years since our last visit, too. (Won't let that happen again!)

Dragon, I have to tell you, that the Sir Timothy's stories were a joy to read. Like I said, it had been a few years since we had gone to the Faire, and reading Sir Timothy's travels thru the years REALLY got me in the spirit. Our last box was the Spell Book and I thought my friend's nephew was gonna explode with delight at how cool this treasure hunting had turned out to be :) (I think he's 8).

Thank you Thank you Thank you for making my return to the Faire so much fun!

For those of you possibly going next Saturday, I'll watch for your handstamps! :)

Wendy

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-07-28 09:48:31 UTC-05:00
We too had a WONDERFUL time on Saturday. We managed to find many of the
boxes, but there were FAR to many to get in one day! We had a great
time meeting MamaMir and RocketMan, and we thank them for not minding us
continually bumping into them! They always seemed to be stamping in at a
box for which we were searching....granted we did help with some
reciprocity at a box or two. We saw the marks of a few others at the
Faire, but we only managed to bump into Jigsaw and Dragon. (how did
we miss the Brat Pack?! We figured they would have been easy to find)
The stamps are beautiful with fun clues.

The Faire was a bunch of fun (although we might have seen a bit more if
we weren't so focused on the boxing!), and we would recommend it to
everyone.
