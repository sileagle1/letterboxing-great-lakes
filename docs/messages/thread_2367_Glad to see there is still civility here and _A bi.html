From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-06 01:54:39 UTC
Forgive me for using this forum for expressing some thoughts after
seeing what has been going down on other lists...

Ever since the beginning of this particular group, we have always
taken great strides to be a very civil list. In fact, one could say
this is one reason this particular group was started, as many of us
were disenchanted with the "flaming" going on on the "big list".

I am sure I am speaking for many, if not all, of the older and newer
members of this group when I say "thank you" to all of you wonderful
people, including BindleBabe. You have remained steadfast in
keeping this group free of anything even remotely close to what may
be considered "flaming", even with a wonderful BINGO April Fools'
joke fooling many of us. We got a great thing going here - let's
all give ourselves pats on our backs and continue to do whatever we
can to build this group up and never tear anyone or anything down.
I'm proud to be a member of one of the greatest groups to be
involved in!!!

To BindleBabe, you are always welcome here. I hope that someday
your ship finds safe harbor here once again. Sail on!

It's spring! Let's all get out and box!
Dragon

P.S. If you disagree with anything I said, please "flame me" via
private email. Except Leapin' Lizards, you jerks! Your last email
was the LAST STRAW!!!!! ;-)
