From: Laurie Marselle (ltmarselle@msn.com) | Date: 2010-03-29 18:35:45 UTC-05:00

You can use any kind of logbook. For gatherings, some letterboxers like to use a separate book just for that event, so Shorty decided to make special GLG 2010 coin-themed logbooks. The books are available to everyone and are totally, completely optional.



As far as shaded camping, the camping areas are wide open spaces surrounded by trees, so I'm sure you could probably find a shady spot. Check out the camping pictures on the great lakes group for more perspective of the area.



We look forward to seeing you in June!
