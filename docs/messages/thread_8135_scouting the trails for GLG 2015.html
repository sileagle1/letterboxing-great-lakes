scouting the trails for GLG 2015

From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-03-26 14:21:24 UTC
We hiked some of the trail loops for GLG and started planning out box locations this past weekend.  There are plenty of great trails that will lend themselves nicely to a range of box types and hikes, so we need more of you to sign up for stamps to carve in order to make this a box-filled event!  There are a lot of ways the themes of "superstitions" and "rites of passage" can be interpreted, so be creative!  We seem to have more superstition-themed stamps so far, but some ideas for "rites of passage" can be in terms of certain letterboxing shenanigans that many of us have gone through (getting your car gets stuck at the trailhead, animal encounters while boxing, a big find or plant milestone like F500 or P100, or your first lost box), or else there are many more common rites of passage like getting your driver's license, voting for the first time, etc.  You can check out the database in the LBGLK yahoo group for ideas that others have already signed up for to give you some more inspiration, and when you come up with your idea, be sure to enter it into the database to "reserve" your stamp.

We'd like to have everyone's stamps by the beginning of May, so we have enough time to prep the boxes and then start planting them later that month.  We will be attending the Video Games event that SeerMeesh is hosting in Waukesha, WI on May 3rd, so we will gladly accept stamps/boxes then to save you the trouble of mailing them.  There is a submission form in the files section of the yahoo group that we ask that you fill out and give to us along with your stamp.  We will also gladly take any donations (to help with the costs of hosting) prior to the event as well, and early donations mean (slightly) earlier access to the event clues, as well as clues to a special "baker's dozen" bonus series!

Any questions, please let us know!
Coffee Beans (of Atomic Beans)
