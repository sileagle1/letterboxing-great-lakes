In reference to finding carving stuff at Michaels - it is indeed pretty hit
or miss to find it. The store by us only carries the Speedball starter kit,
and usually only has one or two of them on hand at any time, and only
restocks very infrequently. If they have it, it is with the fine art
supplies. Hobby Lobby usually carries more, including a wider variety of
carving tips, and lately I've noticed if you have a Joann's nearby that they
have been stocking MasterCarve and starter kits. Although I think their
prices run a little on the high side, if you register with their online
mailing list, they will inundate you with 40-50% off coupons.
