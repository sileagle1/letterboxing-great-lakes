From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-11 04:22:20 UTC
Well, I have to relate this story....

I had a student braid me an LBNA car attenna ribbon that I proudly
flew from my car ever since our WI gathering in March. I even have
a few to give out as first finders prizes. Anyway, I took my carin
for a simple oil change, and when they gave me my complementary car
wash, the ribbons got caught in the whirling brushes and it pulled
out my car attenna! They replaced it for free, but it took a while.

So be warned - remove LBNA ribbons before going through car washes!
