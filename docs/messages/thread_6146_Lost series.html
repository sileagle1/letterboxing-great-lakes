For those of you trying to squeeze in the Lost series before it gets pulled, I have some bad news. Addison and I were searching the island for Jin last night when, upon finding his hiding spot, Jin was nowhere to be found. After some time we gave up and then discovered his logbook further down the trail covered in teeth marks. We did not find the rest of his remains.

Sorry to Leapin' Lizards for the loss of their friend. Looking at the last entry in the logbook, someone had made his acquaintance earlier the same day, and by the time we arrived the damage had been done.

Leapin', if you would like us to mail you his personal effects, send me an email at riiyak@gmail.com with a mailing address.

-RiiyaK
