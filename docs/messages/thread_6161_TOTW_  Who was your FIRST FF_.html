From: sunnysideupm (drewes@WI.RR.com) | Date: 2009-11-17 16:00:30 UTC
Just checked my first plant - my first finder was from out of the region - Front Range Hiker from Colorado. I met her at my first meet-n-greet, a dinner out in honor of her visit to Milwuakee's Irish Fest. Meeting her and many local boxers really reeled me into this hobby hardcore!

This first plant - Magic School Bus Hops Home (Parade of Books) - was also my first carve - it's um, shall we say "crude" or "primitive"? I thought about replacing it, but it is such a contrast to my later carves that it shows how we improve in our skills rather quickly. In the same town (tee-hee) is the stamp I get the most compliments on. The pair are a nice contrast!
