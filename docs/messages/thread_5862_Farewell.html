Dear Members of the Great Lakes Group,

My time with you has been well spent; I have enjoyed your company and your wit. However, all good things must come to and end. I do have a few parting suggestions for your consideration.

Keep your cell phones away from Wisconsin Hiker; she has a well-known aversion to their usage.

Invite Fungus Woman to weddings. She really knows how to celebrate.

Encourage WFMary to plant a Frankie Furter. Maybe she will split the check with you.

Send Familyof6 more mittens and bacon. They can never have enough of either.

Teach HX6 fungi. It is a marvelous language.

Someone give TJ Mich directions to a real brewery. Tequila will drown misery faster than root beer.

Encourage Socks3 to count to at least 5. There must be some conformity.

Get that Mrs. Doglvrs to knit Barefoot Lucy some purple socks. Maybe then she will stop whining.

Kick off a fund drive for the purchase of neon blinking arrows. These are to be placed over difficult letterboxes for Tiptoe & Tonto.

Keep the shining knight away from Dragon. Every group needs a good riddler.

Till we meet again.

Sincerely,

Investigator Francis Bacon
