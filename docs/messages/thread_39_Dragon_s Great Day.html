RE: [LbGLK] Dragon's Great Day

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2004-03-27 20:17:20 UTC-06:00
The Dragon is the only person we've met out on the trail and we recognized
him immediately - clues in hand & Kettle Moraine High School jacket. We
immediately greeted him and we chatted and did an exchange. We have put a
LBNA patch on our pack to hopefully alert other LBers that may be on the
trail and thereby prompt an exchange.

Speaking of thinking everyone you meet might be a LBer...

One of our friends has only been out letterboxing once (last summer), but
she told us a hilarious story about the occasion. She came to the box
location (The Green One) but a couple with a bike was lingering at the spot.
It is a very busy public spot. She waited patiently for them to leave, but
they stuck around. She therefore concluded that THEY must also be looking
for the box and waiting for HER to leave. So she said to to the guy, "Are
you looking for something SPECIAL?". They looked at her in horror, said
NO!, and quickly departed. She couldn't believe that she thought she was
propositioning him/them but was so surprised (and not wanting to divulge too
much) that she couldn't reasonably respond. She hoped they weren't going to
report her to any nearby police for soliciting (or drugs) as she was busy
retrieving the box. Our hiking club has gotten quite a laugh over this!

Wisconsin Hiker
