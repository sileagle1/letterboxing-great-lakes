Just got back from wedding reception, so only a short note. It was FREEZING
on the trails today!! We were out from about 7:30 to noon on the trails,
then another hour and half at the shelter trying to warm up with hot
chocolate, holding our hands over a propane warmer and hot dog coals while
we ate lunch and did a few exchanges! My toes were almost totally frozen,
but luckily finally thawed at the church later in the afternoon. It wasn't
deep snow, but there was sme on all the trails and the paved trail was icy
in spots.

It was a nice event and we're hoping to return to the park tomorrow to find
more of the boxes. Today we only had time for the series Gooble Gobble
planted and 2 others (plus a few right near the shelter). Unfortunately we
missed seeing a lot of people since they were out on the trails during
lunch.

There was a cool "severed finger" dish at the potluck and Gooble Gobble had
assembled an impressive haunted house that contained new hitchhikers for the
first 14 people/groups that finished his series. I'll post photos later.

Many thanks to Red Cat for organizing the event!

Wisconsin Hiker
