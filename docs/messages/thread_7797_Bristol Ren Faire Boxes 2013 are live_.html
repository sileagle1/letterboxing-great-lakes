Bristol Ren Faire Boxes 2013 are live!

From: Kate J (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-07-07 15:00:45 UTC
Thank you BristolStitch for taking the lead on getting letterboxing
rolling at the Bristol Ren Faire this year, as both Dragon and I are
going on long vacations this summer.
Boxes are planted and clues are live! There are 7 new boxes in the 2013
series, and the boxes from 2012 and 2011 have also been replanted. The
6 "permanent" boxes are back in place and there are many letterpeople
wandering about as well.
Bristol Ren Faire Boxes

Wenches of Bristol Boxes


Most of the boxes can be stamped in black or another single color. Two
of the new boxes require specific colors. NO EXCEPTIONS!! One is all
blue, the other needs red, green, yellow, and black markers. If you
don't have or didn't bring markers, stop by Mockingbird Studios and ask
BristolStitch to borrow some.
Enjoy your day at the Faire! Get some letterboxes, watch a show, see the
Queen and her Court, do some shopping!
