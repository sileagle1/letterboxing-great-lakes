Mastercarve.....ahhhhh........

From: clickermom3 (clickermom3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-05 01:28:19 UTC

I'd call myself a very casual letterboxer...I don't count my finds,
haven't ever gotten all my found stamp images together in one place,
and I've only placed a handful of boxes myself. I started out carving
dollar store erasers with an exacto knife, and eventually moved up to
PZcut and actual carving tools. Well...I was at the local Joann's last
week, and, on impulse, purchased a block of Mastercarve.
Finally got around to carving it yesterday, and all I can say is, WOW!
It does 'carve like butter'. I'm sure there are some drawbacks,but it
sure was nice to try the good stuff!!

Just thought I'd share
