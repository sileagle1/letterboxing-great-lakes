So, you still are at zero,

My words don't amuse.

You look every day,

But the picks you refuse.

 

We are at word number five,

There many more yet.

Of one forty seven,

Surely, several you’ll get.

 

So take a deep breath,

Don't be disappointed,

It's only a game,

Don't get disjointed.

 

I'll try to do better,

So come back tomorrow.

To see different words,

To lighten your sorrow.
