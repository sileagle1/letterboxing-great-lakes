From: Pale Puppy Posse (bleubreac@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-23 20:34:12 UTC
I attempted this on Sunday and I input the information to the poll but
thought I would do an additional message to make sure everyone got the
word.

The Dog Days mini gathering is at the Ft. Custer Recreational Area in
Augusta, MI between Kalamzoo and Battle Creek. We are meeting at the
Whitford lake picnic shelter. There will be signs to help you navigate.
Meeting time is 1:00 pm. There are 58 miles of trails and several nice
boxes already planted by some of our members. So be sure and bring your
clues to these boxes. I have also planted several boxes in honor of
this event plus an event stamp just for spending the day with us. I
will bring the clues to these boxes.

There is camping available here if you wish to stay and spend the
weekend letterboxing or just visiting Battle Creek or Kzoo.

Hope to see some new faces along with some regulars!
