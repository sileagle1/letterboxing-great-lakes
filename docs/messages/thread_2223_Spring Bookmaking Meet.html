Spring Bookmaking Meet

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-30 12:21:14 UTC
Okay it is official! The details are worked out for this exciting
event! Thanks to those who provided feedback and offered to lead
workshops, and of course to Speedsquare for providing the meeting
place!

The first Great Lakes Bookmaking Meet will be held on March 18, 2006
in Clear Lake, IN. This is in the extreme NE corner of Indiana,
just off the tollway before the Ohio state line. This is a private
residence that was generously offered for our use for this event.
Specific directions will be given to attendees once they are
registered. While it would be impossible to find a location
convenient to everyone in our region, this location is centrally
located among the majority of those who had expressed interest in
the bookmaking event.

This is an all-day event consisting of 4 hands-on bookmaking
techniques, including:

Stab Binding (by Acorn)
Buttonhole Stitch Binding (by Boot Prints)
Single Needle Coptic Stitch (by SpringChick)
Intro to Altered Books (by Doglvrs)

There will also be a Bookmaking 101 session in the morning to get
everyone familiar with terms and general information.

To keep class size manageable, it is necessary to limit
participation to 10 attendees. Through February 5, 2006, sign-up
will be open only to members of the Great Lakes Yahoo! group on a
first-come, first-served basis. After that date, sign-up will be
open to all letterboxers until seats are filled.

There is a cost of $10 per attendee for the day. This covers the
cost of project specific materials.

To register, please e-mail springchick@letterbox-mi.com. As seats
are limited, please sign up only if you are sure of your commitment
to attend for the full day.

More info:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/060318_main.asp

Hope to see you there!

SpringChick




Spring Bookmaking Meet

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-02-07 10:31:45 UTC
Come join us for a fun-filled day learning some great bookmaking
techniques! There are still some spots open for the bookmaking meet
being held on Saturday, March 18, 2006 in Clear Lake, Indiana.

This is an all-day event consisting of four workshops teaching various
bookmaking/bookbinding techniques suitable for use with letterbox
logbooks. This is geared for beginners, so even if you have no
previous experience with any type of bookbinding, we'd love to have
you join us.

There will be an event stamp and a couple of theme-related
letterboxes. Lunch is potluck. For additional information and
details about signing up:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/events/060318_main.asp
