From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-19 19:14:34 UTC
I'm so happy for your good news! I do understand your confusion, anger
and frustration during the recent time of terrible uncertainty. It
must have been very difficult for all of you.

Two things come to mind as I read your message. First, maybe it wasn't
"bad film" at all; maybe it was all the prayers, thoughts, and good
vibes being sent into the universe on her behalf. Miracles do happen;
I've seen them occur.

Second, as a nurse, I sometimes have my own frustrations with
Physicians, but I am so very thankful that I live in a part of the
world that allows me, and those I care for, to have access to those
Physicians with relative ease. I've been to parts of the world where
this is not case at all.

Yes, it is the "practice" of medicine, but remember, only humans need
to practice, and what Physicians practice is the gift of healing that
they have been given.

Celebrate the good fortune, count the blessings and be thankful,
because I've seen that when people focus on the opposite aspect, their
world is a much darker place to dwell.

Enjoy!
---PJ
