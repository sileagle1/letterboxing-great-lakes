LbNA State Maps

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-04-05 02:54:10 UTC

Along with the functionality updates to letterboxing, the state maps
are being revised. They will be brought up to date as far as the
colored in counties (counties that have boxes) and where requested,
regional divisions will be added or modified.

The revised Michigan map was loaded up to the site today. The main
change is a few extra regions because the lists of boxes on a few of
the pages were getting really long. The U.P. is now 2 regions and the
L.P. has been further split from 3 regions into 6 regions.

I have received input for WI and IN and they are in the process of
being revised. If anyone has suggestions for any of the other states
represented in this group, please feel free to post to the list or e-
mail me off list.

Thanks!
