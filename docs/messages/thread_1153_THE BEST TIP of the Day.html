> We have a sponge that we keep in a dollar-store soap container (had
to cut the sponge a little so it would fit). Before we head out, we
wet the sponge (just plain water), and then we can clean stamps on the
trail, and since it's a hinged soap container, it's easier for us to
use than a sponge in a plastic baggie. We also bring paper towels to
then blot the stamp dry after we've pressed in into the sponge a few
times.
