From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-03-31 14:08:57 UTC

Oh bummer! It is sad when they go missing -- they are like babies.
Look on the bright side though -- at least you didn't lose a log book
full of a year's stamp-ins.

It does sound like somebody may have seen you, or perhaps the placement
was a critter home. I had a box that had been placed inside a hollow
log and every time I went to check on it I found the box gone and the
contents scattered a ways away from the log. Finally I wised up and
moved it to another log in the same area and it was fine.

A smaller box is never a bad idea and given that the hiding place may
have been compromised, I would move it at least a little bit away from
the original placement too.

SpringChick
