It wasn't stolen from Team Armadillo....

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-01 13:49:26 UTC-08:00
Just inspired by him! On the postal letterboxing
list, he posted a link to a map he set up through this
site called Frappar where people can post a "tack" to
show where they're from. Now, some of you may
remember that about a year ago, I created a file in
the files section of our Yahoo Group called
"Who_Where_Dec_04," which was an unofficial list of
where various members of this talk list were from. It
was just fun to see how many people represented each
state, and was also meant as a tool if, say, you were
looking for someone to go letterboxing with, or maybe
carpool to a gathering with. However, I've kind of
let keeping the file go. Even though I made
corrections and additions that were sent to me, I
never got around to posting a revised list. So when I
added my entry to the PLB map that Team Armadillo
created, it struck me - the new version of the "Who
Where" list for our group! So if you'd like to add
yourself (or transfer yourself over), the link is:

http://www.frappr.com/letterboxinggreatlakes

I've also added the link to the links section of this
group. Have fun!

Doodle Bug.
