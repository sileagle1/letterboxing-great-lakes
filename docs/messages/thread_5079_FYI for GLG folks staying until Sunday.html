Hello fellow GLG attendees,

I was just checking out the Afton State Park website again and saw this
posted on the homepage:


Visitor Alert!
Join us for the Great Minnesota Picnic on Open House Sunday, June 1:
Admission to all Minnesota State Parks and Recreation Areas is FREE
that day - no vehicle permit is required. So pack your picnic basket,
come on out, and enjoy a great summer tradition - a picnic in the park!
Camping fees, however, remain in effect.


So for those of you who are staying through Sunday to find more
boxes...you won't have to pay to enter the park again! Yea!! Just
thought I'd spread the word =) See you all on Saturday.
