Welcome New Members!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-17 13:47:58 UTC
Our membership continues to grow by leaps and bounds and we just
wanted to take a minute to welcome all of the new members to the
group. We hope that you will feel at home here.

Given all of the new members, we thought it would be beneficial in
lieu of a TOTW (Topic of the Week) for people to give a brief
introduction -- we'd like to get to know you! If you've been around
here for a few months or even a few years, don't feel left out --
even if we already know you, the newer folks don't, so feel free to
participate in the intros.

If you could, please use a subject line "Member Into:
[name/trailname]" as this will make it easier to find these posts in
the pile of group messages once we are a few months down the road.

We also want to make sure that all of the newer letterboxers are
aware of the Newboxers Yahoo! group. If you have not already joined
this group, you may find it beneficial. Of course your questions are
always welcome here and don't feel anything it too basic or stupid
to ask, but the newboxers list tends to have some very informative
threads of particular interest to new letterboxers. You will find
the group here:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers/

Again, welcome to letterboxing and to the GL group! We're glad
you're here and look forward to meeting up with you on the trail
someday.

SpringChick
