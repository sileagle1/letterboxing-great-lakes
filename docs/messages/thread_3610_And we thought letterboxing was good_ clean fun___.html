From: cupcakewithacherryontop (wickedcuban@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-01-19 04:57:41 UTC
A quick introduction... I'm With A Cherry on Top. As I became more
and more obsessed with letterboxing, I also became more and more in
love with trees. That's about it. I'm a baby boxer, about ready to
go to college, but mature for my age? Nature Nerd might suggest
otherwise. Anyways, I am heading down to the mini-meet sunday, so I
hope to meet many of you, and everyone else in the future.

Anyways... the joys of Thursday have brought my little sister (Music
Nerd) and I closer to our first plant... coming soon in a library
near Chicago. We ruined the house with a book's inside as we
hollowed it out to stash the stamp. It looked like the most boring
book ever, but I found this not so true. Each page we chose as the
bottom for the faux book would have some innappropriate word, and
also some weird reference to political figures, perverted preists
and mutant homicidal lesbian worms?? I am not lying... how could I
make that up?!?

We did find a profanity-less page, so letterboxers can once again
claim they have clean mouths, if not clean shoes.

-Christie/With A Cherry on Top
