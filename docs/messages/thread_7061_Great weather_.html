Great weather!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-04-11 02:50:21 UTC
It was fantastic to get out today in the summer-like temperatures! We found two quick boxes in the Milwaukee area (strangely, one did not contain a stamp, but instead sheets of little commercial stickers. What's up w/that?!?!?).

Then we headed up to the northern Kettle Moraine to replant the 3 boxes we had created for Dead of Winter II. We chose a nice 5-mile loop trail which included views of some ponds and a marsh. The marsh was a noisy area with spring peepers, geese and sandhill cranes. Even though the temperatures were in the low 80s, intermittant sections of the trail were still covered with snow/ice. We will be glad to see all of that melt in the near future!
