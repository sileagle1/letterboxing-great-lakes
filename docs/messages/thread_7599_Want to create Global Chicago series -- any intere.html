Want to create Global Chicago series -- any interest in helping?

From: ashleyandcorey@att.net (ashleyandcorey@att.net) | Date: 2013-01-14 15:25:36 UTC
I work in study abroad programming, both sending students out of the US for a term, and welcoming incoming exchange students to the United States.

I would like to create a series of stamps celebrating Chicago's global status, highlighting our diverse neighborhoods and the immigrant communities that made us what we are today. Pilsen, Greektown, Little Italy, Devon Avenue, Albany Park -- these are just a few gems I would love to feature!

Here is some background on how immigrants have shaped this city:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demographics_of_Chicago
http://chicagogreeter.com/greeter-visits/sample-greeter-visits/
http://worldchicago.org/
http://chicagostories.org/immigrant-chicago/

These will be planted over the summer 2013 and used to help welcome and acclimate our new students in August 2013.

I'd welcome any and all feedback on the idea, not limited to locations that you think would be good points for placement (or awful ones, for that matter).

In addition, if you have any interest in helping to carve, plant, or anything else, I'd be most grateful!

Best,

Ashley, of The Reindeer Family
