I just wanted to post here, in case anyone had clues printed out for
this box, that it has indeed come up missing. I had checked on it June
23rd and it was fine. July 3rd I received a report that it was
missing. Come to find out from my local newspaper, a boy scout troop
and volunteered to clean the cemetery up. Go figure, I would come
accross the ONE scouting troop that doesn't know what letterboxing is,
and pitched the box in spite of notes on the outside/inside asking it
stay in place. Talk about one of life's little ironies.

I'm not sure if I'll replace the box or not. In the process of
cleaning up, they moved a large rock that was part of my hiding spot.
So I'll have to look to see if I can find a new spot.

Both web pages have been updated with the new status!
