If anyone happens to be in the Chicago burbs this weekend, and would
like to experience a bit of local history, Hoffman Tower, the subject
of the exchange box sent to Artdog, and locally commemorated by Fish
Out of Water 9 (and near FOOW5), will be open to the public for tours
from 5 to 8 pm this Friday, July 22nd. All five levels of displays
will be available. For more info, see "Hoffman info" in the files
section under "FungusWoman clues" (under "clues and more clues"). The
tower will also be open Sunday, August 21 and Sunday, October 16th.
I'll be out of town this Friday evening, so I won't be able to meet
anyone for exchanges (but I will be placing a new box in Kendall
County that day!!!).

On another note, I recently found out that the site of Happy Apple
will be closed for vacation until September 12th. But at least when
it reopens it will be caramel apple season -- yummmm!!!!
