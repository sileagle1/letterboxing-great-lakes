Fall WI Gathering - OCT 1

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-04-29 20:29:06 UTC
OK, all the talk of mini-meets, the big GL annual gathering, summer
gatherings, etc. got me motivated to start firming things up for the
fall. The first place I was thinking of was already booked up, so I
hustled and found another spot which should also be very nice.

So for those of you (like me) who like to plan ahead, please mark OCT
1 on your calendar if you're interested in attending a gathering in
Menomonee Falls, WI on Saturday OCT 1. (Menomonee Falls is about 15
miles northwest of Milwaukee) The gathering will be held at
Menomonee Park which "has 394 acres of rolling field, high quality
maple woods, cattail marsh, wetlands, and a 16-acre quarry lake".
I've rented the beach house right on the shore of the quarry so we
will have an enclosed shelter with kitchen facilities. There is also
an attached covered patio with picnic tables and a nearby playground
for the younger LBers. There are a number of trails in the park
which should be suitable for hiding boxes.

A limited number of camping sites are also available in the park.
Apparently the group sites are reservable and the 33 individual sites
are "first-come, first-serve". Here's a link to the park web site if
you want more info right away:

http://www.waukeshacounty.gov/parks/parks/men_main.asp

I'll create a web site for the event within the next week or two, but
just wanted to give you all an early notice that things are finalized.

I hope many of you can make it!

Wisconsin Hiker
