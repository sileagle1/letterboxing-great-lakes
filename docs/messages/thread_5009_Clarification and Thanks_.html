Clarification and Thanks!

From: Familyof6 (familyof6portland@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-29 18:41:08 UTC
First of all, I would like to thank all of those out there that helped
with the Fairies of the Forest Event. First and foremost, a HUGE thank
you goes to Springchick, who guided me through my first event ever,
kept me informed, and helped to plant and clue boxes. Also, a BIG
thank you goes out to JustHappy2Cya (Bill and Patti) who equally
offered to help with planting and cluing of boxes, and to Derby Dude
as well. This was overwhelming, and I can honestly say I am drained,
drained, drained!

Also, an additional thanks goes out to the following boxers who carved
a stamp/box for the event: Springchick, JustHappy2Cya, Doglvrs, Derby
Dude, Midlandtrailblazer, Wild Dreams, Littlmoon, Celtic Clan,
JBanana, and Double Dragon. Excellent stamps and great hiding spots!

Furthermore, everyone that came were just wonderful and I can tell had
a good time (despite the cold). Many of you had wonderful comments and
suggestions, and it was GREAT to meet so many of you.

As a clarification goes, I was not disappointed with comments or
suggestions. To set the record straight, I had one small group of
boxers at the event who thought it just to personally attack and
criticize me through email, basically just to be mean. I was saddened
by the lack of etiquette this one group posed as they pounced me all
over, chewed me up, and then ripped again. Disheartening? Yes. Cruel?
It was. Will it turn me off to this great sport? No. Some people are
just happier when they tear others down.

Let the boxing begin! Thanks for a great event everyone!!!!

Familyof6

Oh dear, I hope Familyof6 didn't take our post about the TOTW as a personal one! We
actually wrote that post to clarify that we thought they did a GREAT job with the clues! We
had heard/read a few comments that seemed to indicate that the clues were too difficult.
We were wanting to shine the light on the fact that difficult clues are part of the game and
welcomed by many. You did an awesome job!

That led us to wonder if there might be ways to make gatherings in general easier to enjoy
all aspects - boxing with children, exchanges & finding boxes in a limited time. We felt it
was a good topic to see how others felt. Please don't think that we meant to attack you or
the Fairy event in ANY way. We loved the event and all that you did! It just so happened
that it was this gathering that triggered the topic and some of the examples were made as
references because they were so fresh in our minds. We should have posted the TOTW in
a separate post as to not associate a negative feeling toward you. Actually the only
negative we felt was toward ourselves for not being able to find all of the wonderful
stamps!
Please accept our apologies if our message came through as an attack. It certainly wasn't
meant that way at all. We will have to work on our communication style!
Tiptoe & Tonto
