Anyone want a dog...cheap???

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-09-16 12:21:56 UTC-05:00
Scenario: A beautiful Indian summer day, clear blue sky, no rain in
forecast, temp in the 70's. Husband Tom left 4:00 this morning for a
weeklong fishing trip in Canada. Daughter Michelle is in school and has
practice afterwards until 5:00. I bought groceries yesterday, so I have
nothing pressing to do (well, there's always housecleaning, but who wants to
do that?). "Hey, Hershey, want to go bye-bye in the car? Want to go
letterboxing?" Needless to say, she's right there. I pack up the gear, put
her in the back seat, rev up the Jeep, power down the back window for her
and CRASH! My back passenger window is shattered.

Let me explain...she has this goofy habit that when the window goes down she
growls and bites at the window. We used to think it was funny until she
grabbed at Tom's Explorer's window and it shattered in her mouth. (no
injury to her that time or this time, thankfully) You'd think she'd learn a
lesson, right...? So now we have broken window #2 to deal with. The car
gets unpacked, the insurance company called. I'm stuck at home on a
gorgeous day cleaning up broken glass waiting for the repair place to call
that the replacement window has arrived so I can go have it installed.

On the plus side of this all, at least I have an inspiration for a new
letterbox! "Bad Dog"...or how about "Dog in the Doghouse"..."Jaws (just
when you thought it was safe to sit in the back seat...)"...hmmm, the
possibilities are endless!

--klk/FungusWoman
"If you place them, they will come...."
