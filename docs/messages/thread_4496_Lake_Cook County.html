From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-10-11 01:19:01 UTC-06:00
Greetings from Beetle!

Yes, I have placed several boxes and a few series of boxes in the Lake Bluff area. Others in Lake Forest, Rondout, Mundelein, Deerfield, and most are easy to find. Some are drive bys...for when you just 'need' to find a box on a rain day and you don't want to get drenched. Even drive bys have a purpose. Flying J has some boxes in the Mundelein and Libertyville area too. And of course, if you are anywhere near the Brookfield Zoo area, you have to find as many of Fungus Woman's boxes as possible. There are lots of boxes in Lake County. Come on over and look around!

Beetle
