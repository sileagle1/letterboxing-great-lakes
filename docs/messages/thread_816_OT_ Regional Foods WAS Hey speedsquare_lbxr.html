Everything in moderation, I say! I lived in Kenosha 3 years and I probably can count on
one hand how many times I ate kringle, and each piece was never more than 2 inches
wide. I passed the O&H Bakery many times. I wouldn't try the cheese topped. Almond is
such a treat, I never tried any of the fruit varieties. But you're probably right, nostalgia
sometimes makes foods taste better than they really are. I'm sure it wasn't the healthiest
thing to have.

Here's my last contribution on regional foods, folks. How many of you have ever tried or
heard of sugar-cream pie? It seems like a central Indiana pie. Not really a custard, not
really a cream pie. But yummy in small pieces. Wick's Pies ships them everywhere.
Childhood memory.
