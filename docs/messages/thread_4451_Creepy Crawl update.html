Creepy Crawl update

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-09-20 22:37:25 UTC-04:00
Things are coming along for the Creepy Crawl to be held 10/27/07 in
SE Michigan.
Check out the blog for more information! http://creepycrawl2007.blogspot.com/

The "destination clues" for the first 7 boxes have been
posted! You'll want to get started on solving these as you need
these names to complete the hunt successfully. I'll get the 8th clue
posted as soon as we figure out a "movie" and a "placement site".

There have been some minor rule changes, notably the age of kids who
count as a "team member" has been raised to 8. Teams can have a max
of 4 people, and please keep your total group limited to 6 people,
including the younger kids.

If you're planning on attending, please RSVP on AQ by 10/1, so I can
get an idea of what to do about
food. http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=449

Let me know if you have any questions or comments. Hope to see you there!
