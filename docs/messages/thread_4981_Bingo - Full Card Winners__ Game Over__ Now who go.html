> I know there was some debate over whether they would be considered the
> last two winners or if it would be a tie for 2nd and the game would
> continue for a 3rd. I do allow ties where there is no other choice --
> i.e. when there is a tie for an interim game or if there is a tie for
> 3rd place in the big game, or if say 12 people would have called bingo
> for full card. Nobody would be denied their win, even if it meant
> increasing the expected number of winners. However, in a situation
> where there are open winning slots when the tie is called -- i.e. if
> two people would call Bingo for 1st or 2nd place on the full card, it
> just uses up the available winning spots in order to try and keep the
> number of winners as close as possible to the predetermined number of 7.
>
> Hope that makes sense... and if you were one of those people sitting
> there waiting for "exchange", "degree" or "trail map", you are not
> alone. Each day as I pulled the word, I breathed a sigh of relief
