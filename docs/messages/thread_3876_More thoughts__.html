PS....A big thank you to JBBK2006 clan for hosting the Hoosier Heart-
St. Pat's Day Gathering at their home in Indianapolis this weekend!
They put a lot of effort into making sure we all were taken care of!
After hours of stamping madness, the room was an explosion of stamp
junkies! You know the look? Blurred vision, muddy brown fingertips
from the marker colors stamped together, and hunched lifeless bodies
only moving their hands to color yet another stamp. In the future, we
will only be able to recognize fellow letterboxers by the top of their
heads!

We enjoyed meeting more boxers from IN, IL and Ohio!..We finally got to
meet Hoosier Honey Bee one of the first boxers in Indiana. When we
started letterboxing,there were only a few boxes in Indy..almost all
were hers.

Happy to report that letterboxing is alive and well in the
Midwest!..and some awesome carvers too!
