In response...yes, we could plan SILO #4 for a Saturday. Actually, the
Silo isn't open for lunch on Sundays; they were only opening early
because we were a large group. I think FW had difficulties getting off
on Saturdays and she was a co-host for SILO #1 and #2. I work
alternating weekends. Also, I think the Silo is usually booked up on
Saturdays way in advance (it's a very popular local restaurant!), and
Sundays were available only for large groups. They require a room fee,
a deposit, and a party menu. We prefer to come and order from the menu
which makes more sense for our type of group, and they make more money
too. But it takes some coordination with the table checks and the
table captains, and Sandy, the owner, agrees that she admires the way
we handle the food bills (only 6-7 checks) for this large group.
Alcoholic drinks are paid for at the bar. It just works for everyone!
So that might be the reasons we've been holding it on Sundays.

The Silo Restaurant is located at 625 Rockland Road, Lake Bluff,
Illinois 60044. The owners are Larry and Sandy Pike (rhymes with HIKE!)
I know that several letterboxers have come back to the Silo when they
are in the area because it truly is a great place to eat. Very popular
with the locals but their famous prize winning pan pizza is well
documented in the newspapers. Feel free to drop Sandy a hand written,
hand stamped letter and thank her for accommodating our group. I know
she's get a kick out of receiving thank you letters from letterboxers.
She knows about the hobby, but seldom gets out on the trails because
she's managing a business.
