Ann,
Have you kept impressions of the various versions of that stamp?
A la the infamous K-Martha, I wonder which version of "Cars-on-a-
Stick" *I* have .
FYI SpringChick: I only C-O-A-S via the postal. It was carried away
by a previous boxer who couldn't replace it due to local crowds. I
searched and searched the right spot but came up empty. When I read
her report of "..boxing in the Chicago area.." right before my hunt
for that box, I began to wonder if maybe I had *just* missed it.
Turns out, Yup....following the failed attempt to return it to the
hiding place, it had gone south a ways. Thanks to Yooperann's help
and intervention, I was a stop along the way for the box's return to
Chicago.
Lots of history with that one.

azobox
