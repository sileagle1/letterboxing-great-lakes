From: The FM Girl (mishiekins@prodigy.net) | Date: 2006-10-18 07:57:29 UTC-07:00
That's a GREAT idea. Museums are non profits that exist to be places
for members of the larger community to visit AND that museums receive
most of their funding based on their annual traffic -- a small museum
would love this idea. All you have to do is point out it will bring
people to the museum who would never have known it was there, and that
many of these people have children. Especially if you are giving clues
that involve people actually visitng the museum itself. Small museums
typically have low visitor counts so anything that gets more people
visiting would be a real boon to them.
