More Info on Stamps for Great Lakes Regional Traveling Package...

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-11-04 05:29:15 UTC
I spoke earlier today with She_Who_Plants about the Great Lakes
Regional Traveling Event Stamps and have a little more info to pass
along for those who are considering...

She is not setting a theme for us, but would like the package to
have a theme. This is something we can decide as a group once a
group has been put together. I'd like it to be something that is
representative of our area but yet allows enough room for creativity
so everyone is free to produce some awesome carvings. Also, the
complete package is not due to her until early spring, so this is
not something you would have to get done within the next few weeks
or even by the end of the year. I will probably set a deadline of
mid-February. She would also like us to make a logbook to go with
the package and I have approached an individual about making that.

Thanks to those who have already e-mailed me. I will be getting
back with everyone during this weekend.
