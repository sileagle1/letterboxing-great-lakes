The following was received at LbNA.

=======================

From: Senior Ranger:

Message:
The FPDDC is beginning a letterbox program and I have placed two boxes
to get things started. I recently placed the clues on the
letterboxing.org website, but would appreciate some assistance. We
are trying to
catalog all of the current letterboxes on our property and have their
owners apply for approval. We are doing this to help ensure they are
following all of our rules and regulations, while not disturbing any
sensitive natural areas. Towards that I was hoping to get our official
Letterboxing Rules and Regulations information posted on letterboxing.org
and contact all owners who have placed boxes in DuPage County, IL to
ensure compliance. Please let me know if you can help me with either
of these matters. I hope that we can continue to help letterboxing
grow in DuPage County and give people another way to enjoy visiting
our beautiful forest preserves. Thanks for your time and
consideration, it is appreciated.

From: Senior Ranger, E-mail: dhebreard@dupageforest.com

===================================================

LbNA will include a link to the rules and regulations but there is a
connection here for Great Lakes area boxers, too.
While placers who have boxes in these areas cannot be compelled by
LbNA to contact the DuPage officials, fostering good relationships
will benefit all of us.

Thanks for helping,

azobox
