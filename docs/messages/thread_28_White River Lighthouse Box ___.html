White River Lighthouse Box ...

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-03-26 01:18:01 UTC
I stopped after work this afternoon and picked up the White River
Lighthouse box. I received report from a recent finder that the
area where the box was hidden has been posted as a Dune Preservation
Area and is now off-limits to foot traffic. The box was in its
hiding spot safe and sound. I think more than a few fisherman
wondered what on earth the crazy woman was doing walking out on the
beach/up into the dunes in work clothing in the fog (I really need
to remember to pack other clothes).

I was so surprised to see that only 7 letterboxers/letterboxing
groups had logged in during the 18 months it was in place -- the
first finders being Leapin' Lizards last May -- 8 months after the
box was planted! Anyway, I will clean the box up and plant it in a
new location. Should go back out sometime next week with a little
extra surprise to sweeten the pot.

Thanks to those who found it for all the nice comments!

SpringChick
