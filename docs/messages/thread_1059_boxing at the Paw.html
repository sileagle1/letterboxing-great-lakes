Funny you should mention next time. We noticed last time we met was early
January, now early March. Astutely we noticed the pattern and decided our
next get-together will be May, so I have just made the executive decision
that our next meeting at the Paw will be May 7th. Everyone's invited! Time
TBA, so feel free to add your two cents on that issue. Being Mother's Day,
I will carve a themed "event stamp", "I love my Mommy". But now comes the
question: should it be "I love my Mommy more than Letterboxing" or "I love
my Mommy almost as much as Letterboxing"? As you've probably noticed, I've
entered a poll.please respond with your vote!



We had a good turnout today! Nuts4Mice and her sister, new boxer HopsBaby;
CMoon and Kookaburra, Buckaneer, and SquEEK!. Buckaneer's mom has been
christened "The Reluctant Boxer". Her mission is to have a stamp carved by
May. Also joining us today were P.Granola and Nature Nerd, KissMeKate and
BarefootAnkh. And just after everyone left, I heard from one of the
managers that there were more boxers in the Cyberdog, Laura and her three
sons. I just sent them a message with the address to join this group. The
managers of the Paw are just tickled to have us over there. They think it's
so cool and love having us there.
