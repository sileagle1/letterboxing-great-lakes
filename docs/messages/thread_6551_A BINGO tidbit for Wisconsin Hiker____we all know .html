So, as of 4-23-10, 84% of all bingo words have been called.

89% of boxes
88% of trailnames
80% of events
80% of general terms

93% of B
81% of I
74% of N
85% of G
89% of O

Which means, 99% of the time, Sprinchick CANNOT be bought to call your words (unless you through in some wicked craft paper and plane tickets to see her grandkids, which, I'm pretty sure, will get you the FULL card bingo!)

So, my guess is this.....90% of the Illinois and Wisconsin people are all waiting for the same words...7% have given up long ago...and 3% are dipping into Tiptoe and Tonto's famous spa and don't give a darn about BINGO now (We can include Spartanfans here too!)

So, your odds of Sprinchick working her magic fingers in your favor for what's left is this:

You have a 9% chance she will call a B word, 24% chance for I, 33% chance for N, 19% chance for G, and a 14% chance for O (THIS, of course, will all change after 4-24-10!)

And, to add more fun, you have a 24% chance that the next word will be a letterbox, a 14% chance that it will be either a letterboxer or an event, and a 48% chance that it will be be a general term...this too shall change after 4-24-10.

So, what does this mean for you? What are your odds of winning? Why don't you check out these odds and see if yours are better:

Odds of being murdered: 18,000 to 1

Odds of getting away with murder: 2 to 1

Odds that a celebrity marriage will last a lifetime: 3 to 1

Odds of getting hemorrhoids: 25 to 1

Chance of winning a bingo game where 100 players manage four cards each: 1 in 100

Chance of having a stroke: 1 in 6

Chance of dying from heart disease: 1 in 3

Chance of getting arthritis: 1 in 7

Chance of suffering from asthma or allergy diseases: 1 in 6

Chance of getting the flu this year: 1 in 10

Odds of being killed on a 5-mile bus trip: 500,000,000 to 1


I don't know about your chances at wining the bingo, but I would say the bus trip looks pretty good! (want more? http://www.funny2.com/odds.htm )

Anyhow...anyone want to take a guess on who is going to take it all? and from what state?
