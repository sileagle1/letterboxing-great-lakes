From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-11-29 00:45:30 UTC-06:00
Correct! From Barker Road, turn right (east) on River Road just after you
cross the railroad tracks, then left (north) into the park. Shelters will
be on your right near the dog exercise area. There's only one main road
through the park, so it will be fairly easy to find the shelters. Here's a
link to a map: http://letterboxing.homestead.com/StNickmap.html
