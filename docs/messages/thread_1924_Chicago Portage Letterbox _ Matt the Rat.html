I found the Chicago Portage box today.
I didn't see the "mysterious iron hook" listed in the clues, but found the
letterbox because it was laying on the side of the trail in pieces.
Luckily, the plastic bag everything was in held up fairly well. It looks
like it had been ravaged by hungry critters. The tupperware container
also has a crack in the bottom of it. I camo duct taped the bottom of the
box, inside and out, and I triple bagged the stamp and log when I put it
back together. It is now hidden under some sturdy rocks next to the tree
with the long roots.
Also, the log book is full.
