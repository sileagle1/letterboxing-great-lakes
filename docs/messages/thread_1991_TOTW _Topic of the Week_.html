I've had a few people e-mail me wondering about the feasibility of
reviving the TOTW. This was a fun thing we did for several months (a
year?) when we first got the list going. It kind of fizzled out
because we ran out of topics and we were all so busy with
letterboxing... But a lot has changed in letterboxing since then and
we have so many new members that it might be fun to revisit some of
these topics, particularly as we head into winter and outdoor 'boxing
trips kind of slow down for many people.

I personally do not have the time right now to facilitate the TOTW --
we have two weddings in planning in my family for 2006, but if there is
interest in starting this back up again and there is somebody who would
be willing to facilitate it, please e-mail me.
