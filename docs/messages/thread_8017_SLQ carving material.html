I ordered some SQL carving material in September and am finally carving a new stamp using it.  I like it quite a bit, but not as much as the old PZKut.  SLQ is softer than the PZ, but definitely firmer than the pink stuff.  I am still able to carve fairly fine lines and pop out the excess material pretty easily on the SLQ.


Tonight I carved the first stamp in an 8-box series that I hope to get planted within the next month or so. Following in the footsteps of the Atomic Beans, it will be a winter series (but they will remain as year-round boxes).
