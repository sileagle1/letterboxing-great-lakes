GLG 2011 Stamp Request

From: zilzilwat (papadelunagirl@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-01-22 21:11:21 UTC
Hello all,

There's a new sign up page available for anyone who is interested in carving for the 2011 Great Lakes Gathering: A Day in the Life. We are looking for stamps of characters from Beatles' songs--Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds, Rocky Raccoon, Chairman Mao, etc.

If you are interested in carving, please do the following:

1. Please go to www.signupgenius.com
2. Near the top, click the green panel that says "FIND A SIGN UP".
3. Enter my email: zilzilwat@gmail.com
4. Click on the "Characters from Beatles' songs" link.
5. Sign up using your first name, trail name (instead of last name), email, and the character you would like to carve.

Deadline for submission is May 6th. If you have any questions, please contact me via AQ. Thanks!

papa
