Grail box update: "Oh Lord bless this..."

From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-17 11:25:52 UTC-05:00
While I was in MI over the weekend, I went to check on our grail box
("Oh Lord bless this thy hand grenade") to make sure things were still
okay with it. Well, it turns out the city is doing construction on the
road to get to the park. :( The road is torn up and closed right
around the park entrance. So take into consideration the extra
directions below:

As of 4/15/07, Osterhout Ave is closed to thru traffic between Oakland
Dr. and Westnedge Ave. while the city puts in new sewers. However, the
road is still partially accessible, so we suggest that you approach from
Oakland and drive east on Osterhout as far as you can get before the
construction starts (this should be just before you reach the Lakes Area
Conservation Club gun range). Find a place to street park and carefully
walk around the construction area and continue walking east until you
get to the Schrier Park entrance, not more than 0.25mile further, and
follow the rest of the clue from there.

Keep up the good work with the grail quest, all you valiant knights!
