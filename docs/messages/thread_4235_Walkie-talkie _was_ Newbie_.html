Love it - a very pratical solution!

While I haven't done this, I have remotely participated in a find. A
letterboxing friend called me from over 900 miles away (I was at
work) and said "Hey - remember that mystery box we were looking at?
I think I just stumbled on the starting point! Look it up and read
me the clues." So I did, and she found the box! I really thought I
should count it as a find, but I waited until my next visit to the
area and made her take me to the spot. It was a good one, and a fun
find for both of us!

~rozebud

> When he found the box he stamped in for the both of us.
> We continued doing this for many of the letterboxes on our trip,
> alternating who had to stay in the car.
