I think I've mentioned this before, but if you want to be sure you see the posts in this group, you have the option of having them sent to an alternate email address.  Simply click on the word "Membership" just under the message board title photo (currently some daffodils), near the right hand side.  Then click on "Edit Membership".  Next click on the little pencil icon by "Identity".  You can add one or more other email addresses and then choose which email address you want the message board posts sent to.  So if you check another email account more regularly, you may want to do this, especially as we near the exciting conclusion of the 2015 BINGO game!
