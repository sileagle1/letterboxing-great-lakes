From: Katie McEnaney (archaeofrog@gmail.com) | Date: 2014-07-09 09:50:22 UTC-05:00
Blue Mound State Park west of Madison has a camp site with both drive-up and walk-up sites and has a significant number of boxes (70? 80? I forget exactly.) from our Great Lake Gathering a few years back. I can't remember precisely about showers, but if there aren't any at the campground I am sure they have them at the pool at another part of the park. It isn't a very large park, but it is letterbox-packed!
