MI Fall Frightfest Gathering!

From: (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2015-10-20 03:44:18 UTC-07:00

Only a few days until Frightfest! I hope you are all as excited as we are! We were at the park this past weekend finishing up the planting and it is gorgeous on the trails! The weather has warmed up a bit this week and the forecast is looking nice for the weekend! A few things to pass along:

1) If you are planning to play Great Lakes Bingo this year, Wisconsin Hiker will be at Frightfest and you can bring her your booty item to save on postage.
2) In addition to the traditional box hunting, there will be a scavenger hunt running simultaneously... there is nothing special you need to do to prepare for this, but you may want to be thinking of teams -- a family unit, or 3-4 unrelated people... also, it would be a disadvantage if you were coming to the event with a brand new logbook.
3) Some of the event boxes have a puzzle component to them (after all, this is a SpringChick event)... so as you don't have to spend half of the day figuring out puzzles, the puzzle piece of these clues will be posted on Wednesday night... and if you really are not a puzzle person, some of the solutions (not all) will be available at the gathering, although asking for them may cost you scavenger hunt points.
4) Don't forget to check for personal travelers of your fellow Frightfesters...
5) There is still time to jump on board for the Friday night haunted house and dinner being hosted by 4eyesMcGee and PoofMagicEvilKitty!

See you on Saturday!
