Hi!

I have completed the basic pages of the website with the 2006 Great
Lakes Gathering information. You can check to see who is coming,
what boxes are planned, and what food. As you finalize your plans,
please be sure to send me an email so I can add the info to the
website.

I've been EXTREMELY impressed by how many of you volunteered to carve
stamps! If anyone else is thinking about carving a stamp, and want
to do something related to the theme, I've put a link on the Boxes
page to a list of some additional ideas that haven't already been
claimed. If you'd like to do a stamp unrelated to the theme, that is
fine also. Just send me an email if you'd like to carve something
and I'll add you to the list.

As you can tell from looking at the website, we've reserved an
enclosed building just in case the weather is bad. However it does
also have several outdoor picnic tables where we will hopefully be
enjoying some pleasant weather!

We'll get a more detailed agenda put together in the upcoming
months. It sounds like we will have two interesting workshops that
day, in addition to the boxing, exchanging and eating.

If you have any comments, suggestions, or additional questions that
aren't addressed on the website, please feel free to send me an email.

We're looking forward to the big day!
