put
in the pttp box. Also, for anyone out there who may be looking for the
Outagamie County Historical society boxes, The Shiocton box was stolen
too
many times and is no longer there, Also, the Seymour box needs added
instructions as it had to be moved after a theft, " You did not find
the
letterbox because someone took it. Now, turn around & check the time.
Proceed on a heading of 350 degrees taking one step for every hour on
the
clock. Due west of your current position is the new location of the
box.
Please follow previous instructions 9 & 10" Good luck and have fun.
