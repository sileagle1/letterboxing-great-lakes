Permission to hide in a library

From: (sobarbiejo@aol.com) | Date: 2005-06-08 18:30:47 UTC-04:00
Can someone please help me? Dumpster Diva and I are going to plant some
boxes in FL. We inquired last year at a library if we could plant a box there.
The librarian told us that we would need a formal letter from the "Letter
Boxing Organization" verifying that it was indeed a legitimate hobby.

Has anyone else had to do this? I have seen on the thread that others have
planted and found boxes in libraries. Did you get permission, or did you
just do it on the sly? We want to comply with his wishes, but where the heck do
we get such a letter?
