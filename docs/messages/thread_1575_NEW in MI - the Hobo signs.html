Just a note on Around the World in 80 Days...

The first mailing is going out on Monday for those who have requested passports. Sorry for the delay -- beauacracy you know... things got caught up in the red tape between the Kalamazoo office and the Muskegon office, but all is back in order and final processing and paperwork was completed yesterday. We trust that those who are anxiously awaiting with their carpet bags packed will find the adventure to have been worth the wait. We intend to keep this series up and running for at least a couple of seasons so you should be okay for next year if you make it back to MI then.

Just as a reminder... in order to qualify for the bonus "Around the World in 80 Days" stamp, you have to complete the 15 boxes in 80 days -- 80 calendar days as measured from the day the passport in mailed to you until you return to us the "postcard" of your travels (this is explained in the passport), so if you are hoping to get the "80 Days" bonus stamp, don't request the passport until your schedule will allow you to do some traveling. If you just want to find the letterboxes, but don't care so much about the bonus "80 Days" stamp, feel free to request the passport at any time. There is also a bonus "World Traveler" stamp for anyone who completes the entire 15-box series, no matter how long it takes. To give you a little idea as to the travel requirements -- the boxes are located throughout the lower peninsula of Michigan.

For anyone who wants additional information, please visit my web site or feel free to e-mail either Azobox or me with questions.

http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days.asp


Happy Travels!

SpringChick
