Hi there,
I'm looking for some boxes in South Oakland County Michigan,
specifically in Southfield, Lathrup Village, Farmington, Farmington
Hills, Franklin, Beverly Hills, Berkeley, Huntington Woods, Royal
Oak, Ferndale, Pleasant Ridge or Oak Park. I haven't had any luck
finding any on letterboxing.org or atlasquest.com. I did a google
search, but all I came up with geochaches, which I have no clue
about. I can't believe South Oakland County is letterbox bare. I
found a bunch of listings in Clarkston, West Bloomfield and farther
north and west.

Does anyone know of any sites that have clues to boxes in this area
that aren't published on the 2 main sites? Anyone want to go hide a
box in one of these cities really quick before the weekend? LOL

Some friends and I are going to be in Southfield this weekend for a
scrapbooking crop. We thought it might be a nice break to go out and
find a box or two while we're there just to stretch a little and get
out of the room. But, we don't want to have to venture too many miles away.

Any suggestions?

From: TJ_Mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-09-18 16:56:40 UTC-07:00
Hi Bali,

Sorry, I know that I have not heard of any boxes in the vicinity, other than what's on letterboxing.org. At one time there was a box in Royal Oak and one in Farmington Hills but I think they are long gone.

You might try Northville and Milford, instead. There are a lot of boxes at Maybury State Park, Island Lake State Park, Kensington Metropark, and so on, that are not all that far from Southfield. Also - West Bloomfield is really quite close to where you'll be, and there are several boxes there. In fact there are 2 series of 4 boxes each, practically across the street from each other.

Have fun!
--TJ_Mich
