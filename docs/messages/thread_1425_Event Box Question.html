No problem with the questions.

If it is a "Mo' Boxin'" themed event box, you should hide it when you
get there (or before you get there) and distribute the clues yourself
at the gathering.

How can you do this with so little time?!? Well, find a hiding spot
that makes the clues short and quick to write (that doesn't
necessarily mean easy, but they can be). Then either write a bunch of
copies of the clues to distribute, write a few or one copy of the
clues and have people exchange them, make a quick trip to a Kinkos, or
make it a WOM box, or any comnination of the above. You won't be the
only one!
