From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-06-19 19:08:25 UTC-04:00
Ginny, many people place letterboxes while they are travelling or on
vacation. Some never go back to that spot again, so in your case, once a
year is at least better than that ;-) In some cases, you may be able to
find a local letterboxer who can check in on the box for you between your
visits, in other cases, it may not be near any other letterboxers. I have
several boxes in the U.P. and northern MI that I only get to on a yearly
basis but they get lots of visitors (more than most of my down-state boxes).
If you do decide to place a box that you cannot check on often, it is nice
to indicate this in the clues so that people know they may be taking more of
a chance in looking for the box and can weigh their decision with that in
mind.

Go for it! I've always thought Bois Blanc Island would be a great place for
a letterbox! This will give me a reason to go there ;-)

SpringChick
