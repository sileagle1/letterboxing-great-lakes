ARGH! WHY can't it be snowing HERE?! I LOVE snow. I like to play in it, I derive some sick pleasure from shoveling it, I even enjoy driving in it when there aren't a bunch of idiots on the road (we won't mention that the snow seems to bring out all the truly stupid drivers). It's not good for letterboxing in, but we don't do much of that this time of year anyway because if it isn't snowy it's muddy and I HATE the mud. We got several inches of snow last Thursday, and Sunday the dogs and I went hiking in the snow. But the last two days were nothing but fog (yesterday the fog just kept getting worse!) and today we had torrential downpours all afternoon. Flying Dragon came home from work and asked me how the basement was. I told him it sounded like it was raining down there and that I had been afraid to look all afternoon. He went to go see how bad it was...and informed me that we had a small river running through the basement. And to top it all off, we have been
trying to get in touch with the landlord since Saturday with no luck!

Kimono Girl
