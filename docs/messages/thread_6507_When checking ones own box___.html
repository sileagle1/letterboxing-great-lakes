What is the proper etiquette when you find a hitchhiker? We were just checking out a box we planted recently and found a hitchhiker in it. We decided it was only fair to leave it, even though we wanted to take it, or at least log in and leave it. But we did neither. Anyone else ran into this?

RE: [LbGLK] When checking ones own box...

From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-04-07 23:08:24 UTC-05:00
Everyone logs things differently, but if someone gives us a HH in a
person-to-person transfer or just to stamp it into our logbook, we don't
actually record it as a HH find in our logs. We count it as a
"miscellaneous" stamp. We only count HH's if they are found as a surprise
in a traditional letterbox (and that's why we don't go to HH hostels - they
aren't a surprise).
