GLG 2012 - my one and only PT **spoiler alert** ??

From: ruhlette (ruhlette@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-05-25 11:42:43 UTC
For those who are interested, my personal traveller, Lutjanus apodus indianensis, was originally carved to be DH's signature stamp. He has never adopted it and refuses to stamp in boxes. He claims he's not a letterboxer, but I know better. After 5 years of hunting, he's starting to appreciate this stamp, but still won't stamp in found letterboxes.

I am hoping he'll actually hold and carry the stamp during the weekend. So it's really an opportunity to exchange with him. When I created the stamp I wanted it to reflect him and I thought I found the perfect trail name. Little did I know that it was an obscure reference for so many people.

If you think you know the answer, you should cross-check your work. All good students do that.

~speedsquare
