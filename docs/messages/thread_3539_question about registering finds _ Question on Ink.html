Side note to Dragon's response. The green flags next to the clues on
the LBNA website only appear if you become a member. Membership is
free you just have to give your Email address, Trail name and
password -- then you will see the green flags.

Also, I'm kinda new as well, started Nov. 25, 2006. My black ink is
already running a little low on my ink pad.

All of the ink pads I looked at said to buy ONLY the same brand ink
as the brand of ink pad to "re-fill it." Is this the case? Or will
any brand ink do. Or is it just a ploy to get us to buy more of the
same brand?

From: trekkiegal1701d (kjnohr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-08 03:57:23 UTC
Yes, I believe it is just a ploy to get you to buy the same brand.
As long as you use the same type of ink (pigment, dye, etc.), you
should be ok, especially because black is an easy color to match up.
For other colors, I would probably buy the same brank re-inker just
to be sure it was the same color.
