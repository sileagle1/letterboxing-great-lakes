From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-08-10 18:27:44 UTC-04:00
Anyone can request to join the Great Lakes Yahoo! group. It is not an
invitation only group. They simply need to go to the following link and
click on "join this group"

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbGLK/

Membership is on approval, so it won't happen immediately, but between
Azobox and myself, we usually get people approved within a few hours of
their request, and so far we haven't turned anyone away as long as they
mention the reason they are here is because of letterboxing. It is more
just to ward off potential spammers.

Sounds like you are having a great time in WI!

SpringChick
