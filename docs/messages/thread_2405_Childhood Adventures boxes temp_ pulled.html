Childhood Adventures boxes temp. pulled

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-17 02:17:38 UTC
While out on my box-checking rounds today, I found both of BirkieBob's
Childhood Adventures (1 and 2) boxes open with their contents strewn
about. I picked them both up for major repairs. I don't know when
they will be back in action or in what condition.

Although I suspect animals or delinquents to be at fault, I would like
to put in a friendly reminder to be sure to re-seal everything back
into their baggies, if not double bag them! In both cases I found the
logbooks outside their bags with other contents in their bags and
sealed. As a result, the logbooks are extremely wet and may not be
salvageable. Considering the age of the boxes, these logbooks are a
huge piece of WI letterboxing history, and now may be lost to us all.
Remember - the next box you go may be of historical significance to
future letterboxers. Treat all boxes better than if they were your
own!
