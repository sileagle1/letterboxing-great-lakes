We had a very successful day of letterboxing on Saturday and just had to
post a big "WOW" for the people whose boxes we found. We were up along
the Des Plaines River Trail in northern IL to find boxes by
CinemaBoxers, Cherokee Rose2 & Hawkeye, and Beetle. We thoroughly
enjoyed the "Trip Across America" boxes 1-10 by CR&H -- very fun to see
what images were chosen to signify each state. We look forward to the
remaining states and are betting amongst each other as to which ones
will be next. ;) And for being new to letterboxing, CB have carved some
amazing Hollywood-themed stamps. We enjoyed the new "Hollywood
Blockbusters" series (which we happily report being FF on) and are great
fans of Harry Potter too. I personally think Johnny Depp is a bit
scary, but apparently CB are big fans and did a fine job with those
stamps as well. :) We ended the day with a couple of boxes by Beetle,
thankfully surviving a visit to the three-eyed ax murderer and avoiding
the cops at the site of the Rondout train robbery. Unfortunately it
grew dark by this time, and we weren't able to visit the Fatman without
causing all his friends to wonder what was going on with the flashlight
party around back, so we will have to visit him another time.

All in all, after about 8hrs of boxing and 10-11 miles of hiking, we
managed 21 boxes for the day. :) Thanks for the fun!

Coffee Beans (and Atom 118)
