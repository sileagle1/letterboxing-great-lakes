Holy Grail Bridge of Death Clues


Poison ivy in the area!!!
Follow the signs to the Chief Shabbona Historical marker off of Route
30 in Shabbona, Illinois.
Park by the shelter near the merry-go round and begin walking down the
road into the woods. Take the orange trail until it opens into a
clearing, look to your left and walk into the oval bordered by wooden
posts. The number 1848 is significant. From this number there will
be a trail to your left at 200 degrees, walk down this trail until you
see the bridge that will lead you to "The Bridge of Death". Cross the
bridge and follow a winding path until just before it makes a
noticeable ascent and curves to the left. Look low and to the left to
find a rather large rock, from this rock, walk about 38 steps up the
curving hill. These steps should take you to a point where there is a
sawed off double trunked tree to your right and a pile of logs to your
left. Turn off the trail to your left at about 40 degrees and walk
forward about 18 steps to a large fallen tree. Your quest will end in
the roots of the old fallen tree.
