Paranoid, with good reason.

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-04-18 23:22:36 UTC-04:00
Ok, here's the most bizarre follow up to my paranoia story from
yesterday. I received a status update today from someone who
actually went out today to find the box that I planted yesterday.

That's not the bizarre part. The bizarre part is that this first
finder just so happens to be friends with the neighbors of my
cemetery. They went over to talk to them, and the lady WAS watching
me and DID think I was being suspicious! I guess I wasn't being as
attentive to the stones as I thought I was. I guess I need to work
on my acting skills. I gotta remember to bring my camera tomorrow
for my next plant. Thanks for the pointers and advice that Mosey
and others offered.

Still... What are the odds of them actually knowing the neighbors? I
can't get over it. LOL It's a small world, after all. ;)


BNL Bali Grl

Or maybe since they (perhaps?) already knew about letterboxing from
their friend, that's partially what kept their attention on you. Those
ignorant of letterboxing/geocaching might not notice or pay as close of

that we know about letterboxing, Atom and I are constantly watching
"suspicious" looking people because we're trying to figure out if they
are boxers or not! That's what happened when we stumbled upon With a
