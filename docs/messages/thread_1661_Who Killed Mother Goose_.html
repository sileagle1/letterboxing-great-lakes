Who killed Mother Goose?

Horror of horrors, the beloved Mother of rhymes, along with her
flying goose, has been found dead in the vicinity of Gun Lake, near
Middleville, MI. Preliminary investigation at the scene indicates
the body was moved to this location from the original location of
the crime. Detectives S. Chick and A. Zobox have been called in
from regional offices to help investigate and they are asking the MI
letterboxing community for assistance in solving this case...

If you think you may have any ideas or information regarding
possible suspects, the murder weapon or actual location of the
crime, please do not hesitate to submit all possible leads in the
form of a rubber stamp carving.

A meeting of detectives and arm-chair mystery solvers has been set
for Saturday, October 22, 2005 at 11:00 a.m. at the Picnic Shelter
located at the Yankee Springs Recreation Area to review the list of
suspects. At that time, we are hoping to successfully identify the
guilty character, the murder weapon and pinpoint the actual location
of the crime.

Respectfully,

Sgt. Grimm
