From: trailtroll1 (kanderson2000@aol.com) | Date: 2012-04-10 12:20:51 UTC
The Bingo game is nearly o'er
excitement fills the air
How many words do we each need -
is one already there?

Our faithful bingo leader calls
a random word each day,
delighting or frustrating all
for that is e'er his way

The winter months were mild this year
yet boredom faced us still
and bingo skipper rescued us
when we had time to kill

The game unfolded, day by day,
with highs and lows for all
Sometimes two words came in a row
and then a week-long stall

Interim games, one by one,
were called out loud and clear -
X, 10-spot, perimeter,
the winners shout with cheer

The final game, so close now,
just sixteen words remain,
with We Dig Sears and Sarges Wide
to stimulate the brain

So please be kind, dear Bingo chief,
and call my word apace
And finally put an end to this
exhilarating race!
