TOT(L)W Finding locations
Everyone else who has posted seems much more thoughtful and organized
about finding locations than I am. Sometimes it's the park -- I just
think there oughtta be a box here somewhere; where can I put one?
Sometimes I have an idea that cries out to be carved into a stamp
hey, that's a cool image, I bet I can make a letterbox to go with it.
And sometimes it's a theme I can't shake okay, what other versions
of "Whose Shoes?" can I make? And finally, sometimes the location
calls for a box and so I search for the right fit "The Breakfast
Elves" (Snap, Crackle & Pop) *had* to be in the Battle Creek area,
right?
I certainly admit that not all of my boxes `make sense' where they
are, but heck, they're there for searchers to find.
Now when it comes to actually putting them in a spot, I've learned
through my own errors not to just put them on the ground with a
covering of leaves and twigs. SpringChick led the way in W. Mi with
placements so we learned a lot by seeking hers. While a dry location
is preferred, I'll happily take a hollow stump since the critters
don't seem to drag boxes up and out. Under a log with natural sticks
and leaves for cover works okay around here, too.
Micros, urban boxes and hitchers are their own special breeds with
their own special needs.
Perhaps that's another TOTW?
Now, time to think about *this week's* TOTW.
