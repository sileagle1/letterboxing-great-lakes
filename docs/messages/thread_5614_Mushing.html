Mushing

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-01 03:15:45 UTC
We could hardly believe the beautiful above freezing weather today...So, Tiptoe took her top
lead "dog', teammate, soul mate - aka Tonto - out for a bit of mushing practice at the
Dunes. Up and over about 4 miles on top of a 35 inch snow base on frozen sand gave us
both a pretty good workout this afternoon. Watch out all you mushing teams...Tiptoe &
Tonto are getting in shape for the Iditarod next weekend.
(If all that sounds intimidating, rest assured we came home exhausted and so sweaty that we
worried about hypothermia setting in....But, we did manage a nice glass of wine and a good
martini with dinner...it will take us all week just to recoup for the big race!)
Tiptoe & Tonto - Mush!
