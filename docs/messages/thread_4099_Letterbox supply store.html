Nobody received a reprimand of any kind and messages by this poster
were not moderated any differently than messages by any other first-
time poster. You would be surprised at the amount of SPAM that comes
through to Yahoo groups and the amount we are able to head off before
it hits the list by moderating first posts. If you would like us to
stop moderating and start letting all this crap come through, just
say the word -- it would certainly make moderating the group easier.

Other than first-post moderation and a few problem situations we do
not moderate posts here and are very lenient with chit-chat and
promotional messages, etc. It is my feeling that even though this is
a letterbox list, it is a community and people form relationships and
there are bound to be things that are not specifically letterbox-
related and even the occasional promotional heads-up on letterboxing
supplies, etc. On the other hand, as moderators of the list, it is
our responsibility to try and keep conversations pertinent to
letterboxing and to try to catch SPAM, gimmicks and self-promotional
messages.

I guess it is another of those darned if you do and darned if you
don't situations... there is always going to be somebody who isn't
happy or thinks it wasn't done the right way.
