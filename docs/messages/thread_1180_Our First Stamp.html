Our First Stamp

From: Jason (lewwdawg_840@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-17 18:34:42 UTC

Well after months of procrastination, we have FINALLY carved our first
stamp. It's a simple one but it came out pretty darn good for a first
try. Hopefully within the next few weeks, we can find it an
appropriate hiding place and get our first plant out there. I was
wondering if someone could tell me where to find some camo-tape for our
box. I looked at Walmart and Meijer but they don't seem to carry it.

Another first this weekend was finding our first geocache. I got a
handheld GPS for Xmas and have been dying to try it out. We were
successful in our find but . . . I still enjoy letterboxing more :)

I will definitely post here once our first plant is ready. Can't wait
to get one out there!!

Julie (Wormy Wanderers)
