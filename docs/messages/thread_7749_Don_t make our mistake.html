Don't make our mistake

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2013-04-22 08:28:37 UTC-05:00
So, some of the rain from Thursday ended up in our basement. Yuck!
Fortunately, other than the pain of all the cleaning, we didn't seem to
lose much (combination of only a few inches of water, rubber totes, and
less than awesome stuff in our basement). However, I'd suggest everyone
of you to go check where you store your letterboxing journals! If you
store them in the basement...put them on a high shelf or have them
otherwise protected.

Unfortunately for us, we had our old logbooks stored on the bottom shelf
of our letterboxing cabinet, and now I'm hoping that they are
salvageable after being partially submerged in water. Some of them are
better than others, but I did notice many pages of smeared rainbows of
ink...I know it is about the journey and the memories, but I like some
of the mementos too!

On a happier note, I was double checking the bingo calls against my
list, and I found a word I had missed!...one call closer!

Have a happy week (and hopefully dry),

A of Atomic Beans
