Get a Clue!

From: grandvilla02 (grandvilla02@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-24 12:35:27 UTC
Can't believe we are the first to let you all know what a great
gathering we had at Yankee Springs on Sat.! Great thanks to Spring
Chick and Azobox for putting it together! It is always such fun to
meet known and new letterboxing friends. The weather held...we even
had some sunshine! And we surely did NOT lack for anything to eat.
The "clue" game was an added feature that seemed to be enjoyed by all
as well. For those of you who couldn't come...try to make the next
one! :-)

Grandvilla and Mobiusklein




Re: [LbGLK] Get a Clue!

From: TJ_Mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-10-24 05:47:42 UTC-07:00
Agreed! The location was beautiful... a shelter surrounded by views of Gun Lake, ringed by trees in all colors from green to orange to red. The event was fun... plenty of time to mingle with letterboxers new and old, and share stories of hunts all over Michigan. The Clue game was great and I learned how "ruthless" some of our competitors are... they will stop at nothing to be the first to learn the identity of Mother Goose's murderer!!! :-)

Our only regret was not having more time to seek out the many other boxes which are now hidden all across the park. Grandvilla's series was indeed Amazing! We highly recommend it.

Thanks to SpringChick and Azobox for putting on a great day for all of us.

Group photo to be uploaded soon....

--TJ_Mich and The Nickerboxer

From: Doglvrs RASW (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2005-10-24 13:09:35 UTC
I think I was still trying to recover from all the great boxing we did! : )
Plus, I had to put some time in at the sewing machine making costumes.

WE HAD A BLAST!!!! As much as I love the large gatherings with lots of
people to talk to - I like the small ones just as much because then you get
to really know some people.

Early on Mr. took the kids and hunted up Fungus Woman's Jack Pumpkinhead -
Neat-O stamp!! (I'm certain I was still talking during this hunt....)

We went boxing in the AM with JBBooks - you guys are great! Thanks for
putting up with us and the wee ones. We were able to hunt up more than half
of SpringChicks boxes. (very cool!!)

The Clue game was - well, frantic?! LoL! "the skull" had so much fun
though (we were a team) and we even ended up being one of the 3 winners!
Boy is he happy!!!

In the afternoon we set out for the "Amazing" series! And guess what?!
They are AMAZING!!!! I have one thing to say about these great boxes...if
you have kids, you must hunt them up!!! : ) Thanks Grandvilla!

Mr. & I left the kids in the van, and ran out to get "Lucky Horseshoe" as
the final box of the day - what a cool stamp!! And I love the logbook! : )
Thanks Cereal City Boxers!

We were all pretty tired when we got home, but we all agreed that it was a
great day!!
Thanks for the hard work and good food!!

Mrs. Doglvrs
