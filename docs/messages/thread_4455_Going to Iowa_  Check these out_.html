Seeing the recent posts about Iowa gatherings reminded me that we are
in need of someone to check out the Iowa boxes that are in the
letterboxing basement. A number of us found them years ago, but
don't live nearby, so haven't been able to check on them. So, if you
head over that way, please consider giving them a try and let us know
if they are still active. If you do live nearby, please consider
adopting them. If any are missing, and you'd like to replace and
adopt, a number of us do have the stamp images to re-create the stamp
from.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-basement/msearch?
query=iowa&charset=windows-1252

(or go to the letterboxing underground yahoo group and seach the
messages for Iowa)

IA Hosper's Furniture Store - I went to check on this in the summer
of 2005, but the store had just closed by the time I go there. I
left a note on the door, but haven't got a reply. Although the town
is still small, it is bigger now, so one WOULD notice a person
driving backward down Main St. !!! :-)
