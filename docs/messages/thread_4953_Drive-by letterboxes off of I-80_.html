From: (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-04-19 14:08:52 UTC-04:00
Amy,

The ones at rest areas I could point you too are "Underground RR" by Goose
Chaser (Cedar County, IA) that is Eastbound. Westbound you have Goose Chaser's
"Old Man River Series: Paddle Wheeler" in Scott County.

Eastbound in IL you have my "Welcome to Illinois" stamp in Rock Island, Co.

Boxes you could get going either west or eastbound are, my "Welcome to Iowa"
stamp in Scott County, IA & Goose Chaser's "Stray Dog" stamp in Rock Island,
County.

There are many many more boxes to get if you are willing to go up to 5 miles
off the interstate, however, all the boxes I just listed would be very easy
and quick finds right off the interstate.

Please drop me a line as to when you are coming through the area and perhaps
we could meet up for a quick exchange and "how ya do".

hope you have a great trip,
