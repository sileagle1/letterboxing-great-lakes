Gather in Nashville, TN on December 3

From: she_who_plants (she_who_plants@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-21 14:24:28 UTC
Hey there! I thought since I'm just about as close to y'all in the
Great Lakes region as I am to the LBSE, I'd send a quick note your
way to let you know that there's a Gather in Nashville in just a
couple weeks. There's more information on AtlasQuest's event planner,
but here's the general information:


Saturday, December 3, 2 p.m. until 9 p.m.

Meet in the Opry Mills Mall at 2 p.m.
Indoor boxing from 2:30 - 5:30.
Regroup at 5:30 to move to a nearby restaurant. (Trying to reserve a
room to ourselves this week).
6:00 - 8:00ish supper, exchanges, event stamps, etc.
8:00ish - Head back over to the Opryland Hotel to see the lights
(which really are amazing and worth the trip in and of themselves).
9:00 and on - Those who wish to can stay over and box into the night.
There will be several boxes planted inside the Opryland
Hotel...again, lots of lights, Christmas decorations, poinsettias,
waterfalls, etc, etc, etc...

If you are interested, there are a few more details, hotel
information, etc. on the AtlasQuest site...or just email me!

She Who Plants
