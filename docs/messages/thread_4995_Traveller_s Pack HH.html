Hello, I will be cross posting this with LB Travellers. I found a HH
today. The name on the bag says "Traveller's Pack HH", but I don't
know if this was the original name for this little guy. The initial
entry on the journal says that it was "found at Bristol RenFaire on
9/7/2005 w/o a logbook". The stamp is a handcarved image of a "hobo
pack". You know, the little bundle on the end of a stick....
The log is so stuffed full that I would love to get in touch with
either the original owner or the one that started the new journal. The
journal is simply a folded up piece of 8 1/2 x 11 sheet of paper
inside
of a cute little blue envelope. If this is your baby and you would
like to give me instructions as to what you would like me to do with
it, I would be more than happy.
Thanks,
Bethany
