Winn. Co. FP Final update

From: Angi (ShutterflyIL@aol.com) | Date: 2005-11-22 04:53:44 UTC
I went out to the forest preserve office with Laughing Coyote and
Manic Mechanic today to see what we would find. I spoke with the
woman I had been emailing and she gave me three of my boxes back. I
got the Spider, Japanese Beetle and Bee, which were all placed in
Blackhawk Springs in Rockford. I was really happy to get the Bee back
because it was my favorite stamp carved so far. When we asked about
others they might have, she said that they were the only three. I
guess the ones that were found before then were thrown away because
the people who found them didn't know what a letterbox was. We are
just too stealthy for our own good. ;) So, if you happen to have a
box that was placed in a Winnebago County forest preserve go missing,
it could be in the trash or gone missing by some other means.

As for me, I'm going to take a break from placing boxes. Call me
cheap, but right now I don't feel like spending the $10 to place a
measly two letterboxes. I know I can find other places besides forest
preserves (which I have), but it has put quite a damper on my
letterboxing spirit. Hopefully I'll get a chance to attend the GL
gathering this summer and it will put me back into the mood to be an
avid letterboxer. :)

Shutterfly
