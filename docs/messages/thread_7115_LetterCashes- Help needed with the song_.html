Hello LB world! This is the KID, not the adult. Ha ha.

OK. I came up with a new letterboxer lingo word, (I'm not sure if a word like
this exsists already,) LetterCaches. It is a Hijacked letterbox. I bet you
probably ran into some box that a geocacher took the stamp and left stuff in.
UGGG!


So, I am making a song to the tune "The 12 days of Christmas". The song is
titled "The 12 days of Letterboxing". Lets sing the 1st verse!

On the first day of letterboxing,
I found a lettercash,
with
1 missing stamp!

I am gonna post a letterbox each month (12 days of letterboxing and 12 months a
year. Get the connection?) about something that you may find in a LetterCache.
Thats where I need YOUR help. What have YOU found in a letter cache? Ex:

2 chewed on tennis balls
1 watch
5 dollar bills
1 bic ligter
6 Pokemon cards

ETC ETC ETC.

Please email me back with YOUR ideas. Look for more e-mails coming out soon, and
be sure to look on atlasquest (maybe even on lbna.org)!

Untill then,
B e aware of geocachers
Y ou are awesome
E at a Culvers!

SamWasHere
