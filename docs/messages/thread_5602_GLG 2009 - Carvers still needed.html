If you've been thinking about carving an event box stamp and haven't contacted me,
please do so as soon as possible. I will be out of town Fri-Mon (and may not have internet
access). On Monday or Tuesday, I will open up my request for Green Stamps, Favorite
Great Lakes State Parks, and 1930's-1930's themed boxes at Atlas Quest. I'd like to get
the stamps (and whatever else you'd like to contribute towards the letterbox) by March
15th. That'll give us time to complete any box details.

Originally I thought I would only ask for stamps so postage costs would be less for
contributors. I also thought a standard box size would be nice, but I discovered that is
too limiting for some of you. SO ... If you want to create a complete letterbox (stamp,
logbook, container, optional ink), please feel free to do so.

When you contact me with your donation, please let me know if you want it to be a SAT
ONLY/SAT&SUN box. Also, do you want me to keep the name of your box secret until the
event.

I have a volunteer for the Official event stamp!

I have deliberately not spent much money at this point. I am super-conscious about
keeping the costs to a minimum this year. Once the official stamp design is made, I may
look into having shirts or canvas bags commissioned.
