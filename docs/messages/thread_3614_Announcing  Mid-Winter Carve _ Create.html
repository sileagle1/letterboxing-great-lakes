Doglvrs RASW wrote:
Where: Borculo, Michigan (near Zeeland/Holland) **6204 96th Ave. Zeeland, MI 49426**
when: February 17, 2007 -- 10 am - 5 or 6ish pm
What: kind of like a "crop"
meal: pizza
Bring?: $$ for pizza, your own beverage and Black & White images to swap

The Mid-Winter Carve & Create is a chance for poor snow bound letterboxer's to get together and work on projects in preparation for spring planting!

*Have an idea percolating? Bring it and bounce it off other letterboxers.
*Stuck on a technique? Bring it and someone else can help you.
*Have projects sitting on the back burner? Bring 'em and finally get 'em finished.
*Have way cool stuff you're dieing to show other boxers? Bring it on.
*Curious about Postal Letterboxing and how it works? Your questions will be answered.
*Need clue writing help? Someone will be able to talk you through something.
*Want to sit around and talk letterboxing? You're not alone.

There will be time for meet and greet and exchanges and even a couple of letterboxes to hunt up (a little piece of heaven is very near) or stumble upon.

There is a sign-up on Atlas Quest if you so desire - or just show up and plan to have a good time.
One nice thing about AQ sign up is the personal traveler option....hint, hint.

Couple more things:
*Snacks/goodies are welcome -- this is not a potluck. Our noon meal will be ordering pizza in - so if you don't like pizza, just bring your own lunch. The restaurants are not close to this location, however there is a convenience store right next door. Bring your own beverages please. (There is a refrigerator and microwave available.)
*There will be no specific activities available for children. I do not want to discourage you from bringing them along, however - you will be responsible for entertaining them. I'm sure I can set up a movie for them to watch -- but that's about it.

Looking forward to spending the day with other letterboxers, and hoping to meet a few new faces!!

Mrs. Doglvrs

Announcing Mid-Winter Carve & Create

From: Doglvrs RASW (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2007-02-21 13:15:27 UTC-05:00
Where: Borculo, Michigan (near Zeeland/Holland) **6204 96th Ave. Zeeland, MI 49426**
when: February 17, 2007 -- 10 am - 5 or 6ish pm
What: kind of like a "crop"
meal: pizza
Bring?: $$ for pizza, your own beverage and Black & White images to swap

The Mid-Winter Carve & Create is a chance for poor snow bound letterboxer's to get together and work on projects in preparation for spring planting!

*Have an idea percolating? Bring it and bounce it off other letterboxers.
*Stuck on a technique? Bring it and someone else can help you.
*Have projects sitting on the back burner? Bring 'em and finally get 'em finished.
*Have way cool stuff you're dieing to show other boxers? Bring it on.
*Curious about Postal Letterboxing and how it works? Your questions will be answered.
*Need clue writing help? Someone will be able to talk you through something.
*Want to sit around and talk letterboxing? You're not alone.

There will be time for meet and greet and exchanges and even a couple of letterboxes to hunt up (a little piece of heaven is very near) or stumble upon.

There is a sign-up on Atlas Quest if you so desire - or just show up and plan to have a good time.
One nice thing about AQ sign up is the personal traveler option....hint, hint.

Couple more things:
