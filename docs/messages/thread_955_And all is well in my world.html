And all is well in my world

From: Gabrielle Coil (gmc81@randomkitty.net) | Date: 2005-01-01 16:18:10 UTC-08:00
Monday I finally got around to re-carving my missing stamp. So, today I took my brother (who is home visiting from Iraq) out to show him what I'm talking about when I write to him about letterboxing. We replaced my missing box and I had him follow all my clues to check up on my other boxes. I am glad to say that all of them are in good condition, in spite of having been chewed on by critters. One box has a tiny puncture in the lid, so I'll be keeping a close eye on that one come spring but I'm hoping the log it's hidden under will protect it. We saw a LOT of deer this time. I'm so proud of Aina for not turning her brain off when she saw all the deer tails dissapearing into the woods.
