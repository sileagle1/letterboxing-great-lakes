Re: [LbGLK] A Brand New Compass!!!

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-05-09 19:55:43 UTC-04:00
A couple of the most common general purpose orienteering compasses are a Silva and Suunto, but I'm sure there are others that are good also. Most of the major sporting goods stores carry either Silva or Suunto or both. I got my Silva Explorer at Dunham's a couple years ago for about $20. You don't need a fancy model -- look for a fluid-filled baseplate compass -- which means it has a rectangular baseplate (preferably transparent for ease in using on a map) -- with a transparent housing (the round part with the numbers inside), preferably with gripping ridges. Other nice to have features -- ruler edge, magnifying lens and stencil holes.

SpringChick
