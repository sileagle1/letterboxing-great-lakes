Buckeyes Racing in under the wire!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-21 11:30:31 UTC

The Columbus OH team of Flag Bearer and Full of Heart, along with
DogBone beat the clock and survived the "80 Days" tour. As the Travel
Staff was locking the office and retreating to new autumn endeavors in
W. Michigan, the Ohio group slipped their documents under the office
door and quietly qualified for the bonus stamp as well.

Earlier claims that the series was finished and the trails were empty
were again proven wrong as the intrepid Buckeyes ducked in under the
wire and neatly beat their 80 Days timeframe.

While the Travel Staff had already moved on to corral a group of
wayward circus acts, the "Around the World in 80 Days" trails were
surprisingly active.

We *think* that Flag Bearer and Full of Heart, along with DogBone are
the final finishers. But, we've been wrong....lots of times.
