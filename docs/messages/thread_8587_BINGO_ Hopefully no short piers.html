Another day with a verse for you to read,
I'll take a moment, that's all that I'll need.

If you notice a minor typo, a mistake, or omission,
Please correct them (by thought), without my revision.

For example, just yesterday I failed to say,
Another way to 'win' the game, we all play.

The biggest loser, alas, that's a thing too,
But hope, for pride, that winner isn't you.

I also feel compelled, nay I really must announce,
It was 'Have I' not 'I Have,' so please don't denounce.

And finally, if my lines, you deem a bit long,
Just skip to the bottom, no worries, not wrong.
