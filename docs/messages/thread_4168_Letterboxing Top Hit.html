From: Pats (votremerci@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-15 07:09:27 UTC-07:00
The shorter the response the better, otherwise people's eyes start to glaze over, they tune you out and that's less likely for a permission to plant situation. My spiel is:

"It's a treasure hunt. People from all over the country get online and find the clues, then come look for them. Let me show you what's in the box."

While I'm showing them, I'm throwing in the fact that there are over 20,000 of these things hidden in parks and neat places all over the country and that it started in England about 150 years ago. The number, all over the country, and history adds credibility that this isn't a one-off kooky weird thing. If they seem interested, I show them some of the stamps in my logbook to reinforce that its not just me doing this.

MoWizLiz
