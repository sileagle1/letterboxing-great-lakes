I think the hardest thing to remember when we first started out was
that the landscape in the forest DOES change from time to time. I have
two boxes that have clues I have to fix because of A large tree
falling over and a small path being swallowed up again by under
growth. I remember getting frusterated looking for older boxes because
the clues weren't just right. Now I realize that if the older boxes
aren't really maintained the chances of the landscape varying from the
clues are good.
