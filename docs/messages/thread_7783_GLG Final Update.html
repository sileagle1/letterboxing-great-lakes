Now there is plenty to do for those noxers of yours:

Dune Park Events
Friday June 14
10am Aspiring Artists Adventure. Did you know that the dunes were once
inspiration for famous painter Frank Dudley? Join us for a morning hike
to learn about Dudley and channel our inner artist as we spend time
sketching and drawing at the site of his old studio near the lakeshore.
Aspiring artists of all abilities welcome! Simple supplies provided.

3 pm Naturalist Beach Break! Watch for the naturalist near the beach
pavilion with some cool critters of the dunes.

8pm Things that Go Bump in the Night! You do on this creature of
the night hike! Grab a friend to hold on to, and join us at the
Campground Gate for a special evening stroll exploring the animals of
the dunes and the special adaptations needed to survive in a world of
darkness.

Saturday June 15
8:30pm Singing Sands Star Show! Join us for our annual event. Meet local
astronomers from the Calumet Astronomical Society for a night of star
gazing. Special telescopes will be set up as well as periodic
constellation talks. Bring a blanket and join us on the main beach, near
the pavilion. Activities going on through 11pm. Optimal star gazing
after 9:30pm.

Sunday June 16
8:30am Informal Church Service at the Campground Shelter.

mudbugs today. Youâ€Ÿll be surprised to learn about the
"high-tech" methods researchers use to study them. Meet at the
Beach Pavilion and prepare for some creek walking as we hike upstream.

3pm Critter Feeding Time! Scoot on over to the Nature Center for some
critter feeding fun! Learn what some of the Nature Center animals eat
and maybe even get to feed some yourself!
