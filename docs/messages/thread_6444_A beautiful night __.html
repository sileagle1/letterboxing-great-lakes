A beautiful night :)

From: Angela (justhave2teach@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-18 23:35:48 UTC
It was a beautiful night so we headed out to do some investigative letterboxing. We headed back to the olympic site to search for the elusive doping box. The only box left that i was aware of to have not found. The only thing we knew was what was on the extra hints page, but by golly we found it!!! I am curious if ANYONE found this box while at the event or afterwards, because it seems that the clue to find this particular box was IN the doping box...now how is someone supposed to find a box if the only clue to find it is in the box itself???? was our host the one doping????

After that funny discovery we ventured to place one more box in the area based on something interesting that we had seen on the trails while at the park---new box is posted :)....BUT while we were investigating for the perfect hiding spot we couldn't believe our eyes when what did we find, but our own missing letterbox!!! Back in January, we were alerted that a box in our series was missing, and we had since recarved it and planted it in a more secure spot in the park....but now it has been found!!! Looks like an animal must have taken it for a walk and been very dissapointed when they couldn't open that trusty lock and lock!!!

What a great ending to a beautiful day!!
BB
