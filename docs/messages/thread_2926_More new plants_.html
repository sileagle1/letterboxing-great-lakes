From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-08-09 22:09:12 UTC
We planted a new series today... Hollywood Hike in Kenosha WI.

Clues posted on Atlasquest, but we DID start it with a higher F
count because I wanted to be sure those who did this one were
familiar with the idea of being VERY discreet, and being very
conscious of the environment where you tread.

The series is in an AMAZING place, but of course, the caretakers do
not know the boxes are there. While I think they would respect the
way most Letterboxers feel about nature, I think they would also
imagine a whole buttload of people heading out to trample their
woods. LOL

So... incognito is the word folks... and if you DO head out, and are
concerned about a box's location, I have NO problems with you moving
it, or removing it for us to replace somewhere else. I don't want to
see these found by the wrong people! We took alot of time and
planning to get this series out there, and we certainly think we
found a VERY special place to send you to find them... not to
mention the lovely stamps you will find.
