I think I have indirectly addicted a new family to letterboxing. They found Potter's Bridge 2

stamps that Swiper took. I love the way the leaves hide the boxes in the fall. The group:

successful day and the 4-year-old is convinced that Dora put those boxes there. They are
"Dora's boxes". I was told the story was very clever. I could not have been told a more
heartwarming story about something I created (with help from Dora).

I am still smiling,
