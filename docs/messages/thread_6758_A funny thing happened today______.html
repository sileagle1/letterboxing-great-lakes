A funny thing happened today......

From: Toni Murray (tonikmmay@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-10-09 12:13:48 UTC-07:00
My letterbox, Pick-A-Bird, was missing from its spot due to the tree it was in
being part a removal area at the Wetland Preserve it was in. Walking out there
today with friends, we stumbled across the box w/logbook and stamp pad but no
stamp. It was about a mile from its plant site, in good condition. It was
under one of the signposts. Apparently several non-letterboxers have found it
and jotted notes about their visits in the logbook. A couple of rude notes were
there as well, easily removed. I will re-carve the stamp and find a new, safer
spot for it soon! I'm so excited!

In the meantime, a new box was planted in the same preserve and will be listed
soon. Hopefully no loss with this one!
