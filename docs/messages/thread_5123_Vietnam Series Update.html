Vietnam Series Update

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-06-18 03:54:21 UTC
The last two people/groups of people who attempted my Vietnam Series
reported back that they couldn't find 3 of the 4 boxes!! Only number
3 was found by both.

So, today I ventured out to assess if these boxes are missing or just
placed very very well.

For those of you who have not found these boxes, they are located on
a major creek that, with every other creek, stream and river in Iowa,
has had major flooding.

My heart sank as I walked the paved path back to box #1, sand and
sediment were all over the place, plowed from the pavement in mounds
several feet tall.

I haven't been to box #1 since I planted it last July -- almost a
year. Did I really remember where it was??

BINGO, found it right off... right where it should be! My spirits
were on their way up, perhaps they are all still there.

Then, I came to where box #2 should have been . . . I have no words
really, there was smelly river mud packed into the log where it was
hidden, and the force of the flow of the water tore the log to
pieces - glued together with this smelly river mud.

I found a stick and pocked and proded. . . nothing :-(

Box #3 I found on the ground, outside of its supposed hidding spot --
I returned it.

Box #4 I was confident was still there (5 feet off the ground in a
tree). Boy the walk was muddy muddy. The creek had washed sticks and
debris all over the place. Fianally I reached the tree . . . gone!!
Gone!! How could this be???

I will replace boxes 2 & 4 next month with my parents when they are
here to visit.

I'll keep you updated.

hx6
