When I woke up on Sunday morning, I woke up laughing. It was going to
be 60 degrees and sunny all day. It was perfect weather for us to
gather up all the stamps from Saturdays gathering. Bali did all the
wonderful clues for the gathering and we got to do them on Sunday to

I wanted to give a special thank you to Bali, without her attention

As always it was so great to see new faces along with some old
friends! We hope to see all of you in our box's log books sometime in
the future! We also want to thank you all for making a monetary
contribution to the gathering which allowed us to pay for the food,
prizes, gas, and all the rest that goes on with putting one of these
events together. Thank you all again for coming, and we hope you had
a great time!!
