GR Public Museum - Tomorrow!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-03-28 10:33:01 UTC
We will have a letterboxing info table set up at the Grand Rapids
Public Museum again tomorrow (Saturday, March 29) in conjunction with
the National Geographic MAPS exhibit. If you were unable to make the
February date and have not yet made it to the museum to experience
this awesome exhibit and to search for the limited-edition letterboxes
planted in the museum, come on down tomorrow. There is an admission
charge for entry into the museum and the MAPS exhibit; letterbox clues
will be available at the desk or at our info table.

Azobox and myself will be there between 11:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m. so
stop and see us while you are there. We will have the Public
Museum/MAPS event stamp available at the info table in case you missed
it the first time around.

SpringChick

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-03-30 02:56:12 UTC
"Hey Tonto, how do you feel about bribery?" Tiptoe asked.

Huh?

"Bribery, arm twisting...you know, providing a little influence for our well being! We're
from Chicago...we know how things are done!"

"What on earth are you talking about?"

"Spring Chick has sent out an invitation to visit her and Azobox at the Grand Rapids Public
Museum today where they will be manning a letterbox information table. I was thinking
maybe we could pay them a little visit and personally persuade Spring Chick to pick our
remaining BINGO words....if ya know watt-a-mean!"

"Grand Rapids is two and a half hours away...and not to mention the price of gas.."

"PLEASEEEEEEE.....After all, it is BINGO and we WANT those Doglvr socks!

So went the conversation....until Tiptoe finally came out the victor!

We drove up and surprised the GLK letterboxing duo...(aka the King and Queen as noted
by their little crowns after their names as "moderator"!)

We spent the day at the museum, which by the way, is wonderful...found four
letterboxes...had a great dinner at Charlie's Crab...stopped in South Haven at our favorite
ice cream shop..Sherman's (double chocolate malt!!!!)....now open for the season...(A
definite sign that the WARM weather is coming!) A great day...EXCEPT...Spring Chick
wouldn't agree to "fix" our call...If we happen to win those socks, it will be wholly on our
own luck! She was not to be bought, bribed or cajoled into any under the table deals! So
those of you reading and thinking of the same...forget it! BUT, if you can get up to the
museum to get those boxes...Go for it...they are so worth the trip!
Thanks to those fine carvers of stamps hidden at the museum...And as usual, thanks to
Azobox and Spring Chick for making our day with your bright smiles! It warmed our cold
little hearts more than you know!
Tiptoe & Tonto
