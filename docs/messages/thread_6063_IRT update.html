IRT update

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2009-09-24 02:53:12 UTC
Welcome to Illinois is unavailable for now. They have the Welcome Center closed while I-80 is being worked on. I'll let you know when it re-opens.

Pinus Strobus, is still snug in its place and dry as a bell. I was able to check on this Wisconsin Hiker beauty last week at work. I got to the location and there was a group of people there (I think they may have been Geocachers) they kept running into the woods and giggling. High school kids you could expect, but these were middle aged people not in the best of shape.

I waited them out for about 10 minutes -- I was beginning to wonder if I would get to it or not.... I don't have the longest of lunch breaks -- they finally went their way and I was able to grab it.

Its ready to go for the next finders.

hx6
