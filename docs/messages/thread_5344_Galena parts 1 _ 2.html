Galena parts 1 & 2

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-10-09 03:54:45 UTC
On September 20th we went up to Galena with a few of our very closest
friends (albeit non-boxers).

I re-placed 3 boxes, hid a new box and checked on many others. To my
knowledge ALL of my boxes in Galena are now in place and ready to be
found.

I have updated some of the clues. All of my clues can now be found on
my personal website (have you found it yet??) except for
the "Memories of a Life Time" for now, you have to Email me for those
4 boxes.

There are a few boxes I have clues to on AQ & LBNA.

The very next week, we decided to head north again. We hit Mount
Carroll, and boy oh boy are these boxes worth finding!!! Garfan is
one of the best carvers I know, and so much fun to just hang out with.

Then we headed up to Woodbine to find 3 boxes, Apple River Fort in
Elizabeth and then on to Galena for the second time in as many weeks.

We found the last couple of boxes in Jo Davies county.

On our way out of town towards Dubuque I decided to run into Stoney
Creek Inn. My Wandering Moose stamp hadn't been found in a very long
time and I wondered if it was still in place. If it were in place I
was going to pull it and re-hide it.

Once I found the box, still in place, I found many many people who
had logged into the box without logging it on either AQ or LBNA.

On top of that, there were two stamped images in the logbook
from "The Stoney Creek Gang" who wrote in the log, "we wont tell a
soul."

I was so excited, and with this I decided to leave the box in place.

So, to all interested in visiting the Galena area, all boxes are in
place and ready to be found!!
