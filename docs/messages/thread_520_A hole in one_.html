A hole in one!

From: Pale Puppy Posse (advantagek9@voyager.net) | Date: 2004-08-09 10:52:28 UTC-04:00
I have to tell you all that I know we are fairly new to letterboxing but
our Dog Scout troop had it's annual picnic and I did a commemorative stamp
for the occasion. I located the park I wanted to plant it in and set out
for the perfect hiding place. I meandered past a couple walking the trail
and then an older man who wanted to chat with me as he saw me scoping
things out. I am just thinking "ok you can leave now". Finally they all
left and I had spotted my ideal location. Looking around to make sure
everyone was gone, I set off into the woods just off the trail to hide my
box. When low and behold, another letterbox was already hidden there!! Now
what is the chances of that? My husband likens it to a hole in one for
golf. Me, I feel more like I found a needle in a haystack... I guess great
minds think a like. Wouldn't you know it.. I didn't have any of my stamping
stuff with me..
