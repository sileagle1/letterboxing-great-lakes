How long will the BINGO game last? The Side-Wagers Game is back!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-16 01:02:48 UTC
When I saw SpringChick's announcement about re-running Letterboxing
BINGO, I again wondered how long the game might go. Last year we were
all a bit surprised that it lasted until mid-summer.
Okay, okay, it only lasted 'til mid-summer for *some* of us.

So anyway, how long might it take for your BINGO card to fill? Or,
more specifically, for *anyone's* card to fill?
Now, I'm talking about the Big Game here, not the four interim games.

Maybe early to mid-February? How about March? Could be April or even
May. Who knows?

If you'd again like to add some excitement to the Letterboxing Bingo
game, enter the Side-Wagers Game.
Email me the date you think the game will end. In other words, when
will she pull the item that finally and completely fills in some
player's card?
Again, I'm talking about the Big Game here, not the four interim games.
The date will be determined by the claiming of a successful card by a
BINGO player (and its certification by SpringChick).

I must have your guess before January 1, 2007.
Mail to jparkerg at Yahoo dot com
(my usual address for this group)
In the subject line, put "Bingo".

I'll award the winner's prize to the person who comes closest to the
actual day or is closest before the day the Bingo Game ends. If you
choose February 15th, for example, and someone else chooses the 20th,
and the game ends on the 19th, you win. In other words, you'll hold
all of the days until the next guesser hits the calendar. But you
don't know who has chosen which days or how far apart they've fallen
(and neither does SC!).

Last year there were a couple of players who chose identical dates. I
accepted the first one I received (and offered another shot to the one
who'd doubled up on the date.)
This time around, I will assign the another available date to anyone
who chooses a date already taken. I'll do this by moving out in the
calendar until I find a date between your choice and the next one chosen.
So, if you choose Feb 20 and it's already taken, and I look and see
that the next date chosen is the 24th, I'll assign you the 22nd. If
there is another player already on the adjacent date and it is not
available, I'll move you to the next available date.
I anticipate a few more players this time and since I *do* have a life
besides shepherding the BINGO Side-Wagers game, let's just say that
I'll do my best to keep you informed if I need to alter your chosen
Side-Wagers date.

I'll select a prize for the lucky winner.
You do not need to be a player in the big game to participate.


For more about SpringChick's game, check the files section of our
Great Lakes Letterboxing Group. Go towards the bottom of
the files list.
The word list, the grid and the rules are included.
There is still time to get into the game(s).


Good luck,
