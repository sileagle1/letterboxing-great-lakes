Boxer in TRAINing and I took the second Cemetery Stroll today. We couldn't
believe we were second finders after Team Taco (good to see you guys back on
the trails!), who beat us only by a few days!



In all my years of living in the area, I'd never entered any of those
cemeteries. Pretty interesting! Good clues and location. Lots of workers
around, and even a few picnickers. (Kind of funny - we heard this animated
conversation coming from a woman eating a Whopper while sitting on a
tombstone. Looked like she was talking to herself (or a dead relative??)
until we eventually saw her companion lying on the ground nearby.)
