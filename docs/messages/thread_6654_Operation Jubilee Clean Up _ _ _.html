Operation Jubilee Clean Up . . .

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2010-06-17 04:42:24 UTC
Is under way. Today I went to start pulling boxes from the park. You all have very limited time to collect what you can before all the boxes are pulled.

I pulled Wisconsin Hikers Coin-Operated Series Box #'s 2, 3, 4, 5 & 6.

I know, pitiful, right?

Well, I hiked the still muddy trails around the perimeter of the park and managed to find #4 -- a box that elluded Pitties, Shorty, Tiptoe & Tonto & myself not even a couple weeks ago.

After two hours in the mud -- I can honestly say, I don't care if I ever hike those trails again . . . after hiking most of them 3 - 5 times apiece and ALL but one of those times they were wet, soggy & muddy.

The BSM (Boot Sucking Mud) needs to be reclassified as FSM (Footwear sucking mud) as it also takes a liking to shoes!

So, after two hours in the mud, my shirt was 100% soaked through with sweat and I drug myself back out to the road where Becki picked me up -- sorry sap that I was.

Oh, I also met & exchanged with a couple of boxers from the Racine area. Cuppie & #5!! I was thrilled to see someone gathering the coin series while it's still available.

I'm happier yet, that I met the two before I grabbed my first WiHi box and BEFORE I broke a sweat!! Boy, that would've repelled them!!

One more thing, I was swarmed by horse flies the entire 2 hours -- they just circled and buzzed around my head the whole time and I had 3 bite me!!

I can honestly say, I'd rather be swarmed by mosquitos than these spruce goose's (spruce geese???) of flying insects!!!!

hx6
