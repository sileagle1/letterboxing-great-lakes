Is Bad Grandma lurking in southern Wisconsin?

From: lesann847 (l-fischer@northwestern.edu) | Date: 2004-11-01 14:26:43 UTC

Do Leapin' Lizards place their boxes near where others practice their secret
vices, or is that Acorn and Golden Boy have an affinity for secret stashes of all
sorts?

Two weeks ago, searching for the second Chocolate City series, we
happened upon a stash of "adult reading" hidden in the woods. Yesterday, as
were finishing up the Greatest Show on Earth series, Golden Boy found a
leather box. He opened it, and found it had once held a watch. Being a
curious sort, he lifted the interior structure and found two cigarettes stashed
inside.

It was clear that neither the reading or smoking malefactors were letterboxers,
as both magazines and cigarettes were soaked and unusable. I'd hate to
think what we might find on the trail if Bad Grandma learns to store her vices
in the woods in little plastic containers.

Acorn
