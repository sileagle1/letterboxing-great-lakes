creepy letterboxing find

From: tat2bob (tat2bob@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2012-01-08 21:27:29 UTC-06:00
Tat2Bob and I went to check up on a few boxes a month or so back and found... a pair of Santa candle holders. No sign of the box at all. Robert thought I was joking when I can out of the “hole” the box was in with them. They seem fairly old. Look good in my kitchen. So the really gross part. A pink grocery style bag with another pink bag in it. With a white garbage bag in it. And it that bag was a dead rooster. Even with the other things in the bag I know it was a real dead rooster. The cop I spoke with did the “really wide eyed” look before asking “really?” And then said “Eeww.” Some days it isn’t worth it. And some days it is.

Kimberly

Mine was only a little creepy. It was late spring/early summer, and I was
looking for a box that was in a small pond area. It was a really pretty
spot, and easy to get to the box w/o having to hide. I wasn't sure EXACTLY
where the box was, but I thought it would be under 1 of the rocks. So,
silly me, I started feeling under the rocks (not smart in a different part
of the world, but in Central IL, it's not TOO stupid ;^) I felt something
soft, so I thought, maybe this is a letterBAG instead of a letterBOX. I
pulled out what I thought was a bag, to find a very surprised toad in my
hand!

I have to say, I'm very proud of myself. I jumped and screamed a little,
but I did NOT throw the toad! After a good laugh, I gently put the toad
back under his rock. Oh, and I DID find the box, too!

RCHORR'
