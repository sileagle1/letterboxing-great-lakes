Jan 6 - A popular fella

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2019-01-06 08:51:13 UTC-06:00
Many have met him,
At the shelter or on the trail.
So today's word choice, 
should make everyone hail.

The most popular term,
This year's, many picked pick,
Makes me happy to call it,
Since a late one had me worried sick. 

Turn up the Walkman,
And cross off his name,
His flip phone won't hinder,
The joy he'll bring to the game.

G - Hartx6
