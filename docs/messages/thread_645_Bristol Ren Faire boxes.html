For all of you that have been wondering, here's the scoop...

I have gotten a late start on these boxes. I am currently waiting for
permission to do these boxes again this year at Bristol and I expect
no problem getting permission. Since the Faire opens on July 9th
(next Sat), I hope all the planning goes quickly...

I am currently also in contact with a number of "letterpoeple" both
new and old to work out clues and details with them.

If allowed, this year's letterboxes theme will be "The Shoppes of
Bristol". For those of you that missed getting the Gamemaster stamp
in 2003 and/or the Ale and Sassafrass stamp in 2004 (as these went
missing) these will be placed this year in tribute to their respective
shoppes. There will also be new boxes to find for other shoppe
themes. The "Letterboxing BRF" box will also be available again this
year.

The clue page will probably not be updated until the night before the
faire opens. If you are planning to go to the faire on opening day,
let me know, so I can make a point of letting you know the clues are
ready. I will also be a "letterperson" available on opening day at
least, if not other days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
