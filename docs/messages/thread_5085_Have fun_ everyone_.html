Deedle & I just couldn't swing the drive from Cleveland, Ohio to Minnesota for this year's gathering. And usually if we can't make it in person I try to send a box on our behalf, but I just lost track of time this year and completely forgot about creating a box until it was too late. And I so wanted to do one on behalf of us English majors and the Partnership of English Majors (check out some Garrison Keillor if you're unfamiliar with the organization ;). What I found most amusing about the Partnership of English Majors is that in college, my friends (also English majors and minors) and I decided we would create The Organization for the Preservation of English Majors and Minors, where the plan was that one of our fellow English majors would go on to a career making lots of money and would therefore be able to support the rest of us. However, 10+ years later, no one has gotten that high enough paying job to support the rest of us, too...

Anyway. You all have tons of fun, and take lots and lots and lots of pictures to share with the rest of us!

Doodle Bug.
