Temporary Message Moderation

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-06-12 22:53:39 UTC
I have turned on message moderation for all group members. A couple
more of the "New Graphics Site" messages have come through and I
suspect this problem is only going to snowball as more people open
the message that have come through.

Since these messages are coming to the list from senders who we
know, people are opening them without realizing it is a virus.
Anyone who opens the message and does not have up-to-date virus
definitions on their computer gets infected and the virus e-mails
itself to people in their address book, which often includes this
list, so it just keeps coming back.

What the message moderation means is that Azobox or I have to
approve each message before it will actually post through to the
list. As we both have other things we do besides moderate this
group, there may be a bit of a lag at times. We will try to do our
best to keep up with it. Hopefully this is only a temporary thing
and Yahoo! will put something in place to filter out these messages.

In the meantime...

1. Don't open any message with the subject line "New Graphics Site"
even if it is from somebody you know.
2. Make sure the anti-virus definitions on your computer are up-to-
date as most of the major anti-virus programs are on top of this and
will stop it before it infects your computer.
3. If you already opened one of these messages, you may have been
infected and should do a virus scan on your computer immediately --
this is especially true if you are one of the people who has
[unknowingly] propogated the message.

Thanks for bearing with us!

SpringChick
