Mark Pepe's Now is the Time!

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-09 18:19:02 UTC
After reading Mark Pepe's newest interview and seeing the "gift", I
realize I'm getting too emotional over letterboxing....Maybe I need
letterboxing therapy!

I actually had little tears in my eyes thinking how awesome it is
that our wonderful hobby has grown into a national pastime shared by
so many. Heart felt thanks are due to such visionaries as portrayed
in the video who had the gumption to step out and be a little daring
and plant those first boxes of Tupperware in the great outdoors!
How crazy was that?

Also, a big thanks to those who have spent (and still are spending!)
time, money and technical know-how to make the clues available to
all of us on the Internet. Without them where would we be spending
all of our discretionary time? I shudder to think!

Thank you, Mark, for bringing this all full circle to us through
your blog...
Maybe letterboxing IS all the therapy any of us really need!

I'm trying to convince Tonto of this fact as I am begging to go out
letterboxing today after a good 6 inch snowfall last night....He
claims he is a little tuckered out from shoveling....But, I'm
desperate for some LB therapy!
Tiptoe & Tonto
