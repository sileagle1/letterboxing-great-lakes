Ok, I thought it was bad yesterday when I practically started having DT's when SpringChick didn't post the bingo word by 7ish, like normal.

But, this morning, I actually dreamt that I logged into the yahoo group on my phone to check for the bingo post. I dreamt that there was a post and that SpringChick picked two words for today. I thought it was odd that she'd post two words on a Saturday! They "words" were names of boxes which isn't all that unusual. But, I should have realized it wasn't real when one of the box names was supposedly planted at a local mall about 5 miles from me! I don't think she ever posts the actual city the boxes are planted in. But, I thought, "Hey, I never heard of that box, maybe I should go find it today!"

The worst part is (obviously, since they're fake words), they didn't even help me at all with my card! You'd think that if I was going to dream about bingo, the least I could do was dream about words I acually have!



When's Old Shoe going to wake up? I need my bingo fix for the day!
