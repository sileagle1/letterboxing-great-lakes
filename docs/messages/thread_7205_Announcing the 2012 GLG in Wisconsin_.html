Announcing the 2012 GLG in Wisconsin!

From: Archaeo Frog (archaeofrog@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-10-23 13:18:14 UTC-05:00
(Sorry if everyone is receiving this twice, but given that no one commented
on the thread in days, I am thinking the first version of this email didn't
go through on the list ... )


Thank you for all the excited thoughts and comments, already. The Verona
Bird Patrol and I are proud to announce the 2012 Great Lakes Gathering :
Drawn to Letterboxing!
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2290

Although reservations cannot be made until January, we are planning to host
the event at Blue Mounds State Park, a half an hour outside of Madison, WI,
on Saturday, June 9th, 2012. Camping is available at the park, and there
are many hotels and even other campgrounds in the surrounding area.

Our theme for the gathering is Cartoons! While the 'Bird Patrol already has
the lock down on the Smurfs, there are near endless possibilities, and we
hope it will be a lot of fun for all.

More details will emerge as we get closer, but for now, mark your calendars
and start planning for your Wisconsin Road Trip!

- Archaeofrog & the Verona Bird Patrol
