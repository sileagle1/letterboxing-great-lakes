From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-09-11 09:30:11 UTC-05:00
Just wanted to share the news that letterboxers in the Great Lakes
region are featured in the 2008 AQ calendars!

Atom had a photo selected for the "silly" calendar for May. It's a
photo he took last year over labor day weekend when we were camping in
southern Illinois -- the photo was taken in Mattoon, Illinois actually,
right near CMoon's box "The Green Wave." :)

The Waltzing Pigs also had a photo selected, although it doesn't
represent the midwest. ;) It is a beautiful shot from their recent trip
to Alaska, selected for December in the "original" calendar.

Here's a link to the post on AQ, which has links to the calendars:
http://www.atlasquest.com/boards/message.html?gMsgId=127811;gBoardId=314

Congrats Atom and Waltzing Pigs! :)

Coffee Beans
