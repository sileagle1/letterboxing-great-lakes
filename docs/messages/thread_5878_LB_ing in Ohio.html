I would suggest that you contact, franzsolo, Mama Stork, Team Mad Dawg
or Lindsays Snowman for guidance. If you like cemeteries, Spring Grove
is a HUGE and stunningly gorgeous cemetery in the area. In fact, if I
remember correctly, it's the second largest in the nation and was
designed to be a gathering place for the community. There are many great
boxes there and you would get tons of walking in while exploring and
boxing. (Just one suggestion---but for the parks, the other folks would
be your best bet.)
