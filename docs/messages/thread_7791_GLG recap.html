From: klk-fw (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2013-06-15 23:18:45 UTC
Conversation in car driving home:

Mr. FW: That was a nice park. Too bad they didn't allow beer.
Me: Well, most of us are too busy letterboxing to care.
Mr. FW: Actually I did see someone in the shelter carrying a bottle of beer.
Me: In a cozy?
Mr. FW: Yeah.
Me: That was a letterbox.

end of conversation

Thanks to all those who contributed stamps and food, but especially to the BT's, rnfrog, and Joe Boxer for all their hard work. See you all in MN if not sooner!

--klk/fw

PS: May the best team win!
