From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-01-03 01:11:46 UTC-05:00
It is a type of handmade logbook that is easy to make and customize to any size. It is hand-stitched with a simplified variation of the coptic stitch, but the covers are done much easier than stitched journals. I started making it soon after I began letterboxing based on the desire to make my own logbooks -- that looked nice, could be made any size I needed to fit the container and didn't take forever to make. At the first Great Lakes Gathering I did a workshop on making it and since then several other people have led workshops on making them at subsequent gatherings.

I called it the Monkey Journal based on my nickname as a kid.

You can download the instructions here: http://www.stampinthebox.com/files/monkey.pdf

SpringChick
