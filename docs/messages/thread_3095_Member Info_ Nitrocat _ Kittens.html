Even though we are "pinkie" on the yahoo groups, we are Nitrocat and
Kittens everywhere else. Except for where people call us by our real
names of Stacy, Jurene and Megan. We started letterboxing in July
2006 and immediately jumped in with both feet. We just recently
started the postals because one of the kittens hates hiking. Plus
winter is coming. The strangest thing we have seen while boxing is a
broom-propped up against a cliff face in the middle of nowhere! Our
favorite box so far is "Untitled" by midnight visitor.
We live in rural northern Illinois and do all of that farming, stay-at-
home, homeschool stuff. That means we can letterbox whenever we want,
not just when school/work is done!
