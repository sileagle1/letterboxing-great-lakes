> You know you're addicted when...
* You plan one of your yearly vacations around new places to letterbox - like this past June's trip to Michigan - and you change the week to coincide with the gathering! And plan to visit Oregon next June to - that's right - letterbox!
* You convince your friends that letterboxing is the best hobby ever so when we get together with them we can letterbox instead of all those stupid things like playing cards or going to the movies - what a waste of daylight!
* You spend every free moment from work reading (and responding!) to the talklists!
* You are constantly thinking of new and innovative ways to hide, carve and clue boxes.
* Everyone is work knows about your hobby and refer to it as "that cult that walks in the woods."
* You become a ringmaster for a traveling stamp circus and change your name to Marco Peperini as well!

You know you're addicted when you think up this limerick as you are
exercising:

There once was a boxer from County Cook
Who longed to hunt stamps for her book!
Her search for more clues
Caused her family to lose
Her role as a cleaner and cook.


That's only a partial exaggeration. I don't mind cooking, but I'll
look for any excuse not to clean. I'd much rather be letterboxing or
planning our next letterboxing excursion than to tackle our basement--
which currently looks like it has been ransacked by a gang of thieves.

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs
