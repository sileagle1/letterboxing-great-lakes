From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-12-09 18:16:44 UTC
How is everyone doing with the snow? I'd say we got about 6-8 inches of wet heavy snow. Martini Man had a business dinner last to celebrate the conclusion of the big SAP project, so he didn't get home until after 9 pm. By that time the roads were pretty bad and it was also impossible for him to get up our hilly driveway. He ended up driving the car back to a nearby hospital parking lot and then walking back home. Our driveway was plowed during the night and this morning Martini Man walked back to get his car and headed off to work again.

The trees were loaded with the snow and we were worried about a lot of branches breaking off. Luckily we haven't noticed anything too serious. Our power flickered off & on a few times last night, so I gave up working on the computer for a while. It's snowing lightly now. We'll have to see how things are tonight since we have theater tickets. Will we head downtown.....?

The first big snowstorm made me think of the winter letterboxing Bingo. Curious to see what the word lists will look like this year! We also still need to decide on some Booty to send.

Wisconsin Hiker
