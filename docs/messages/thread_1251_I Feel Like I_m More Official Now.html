HavyBeaks and I started letterboxing in January. Even in 6" of snow
we were out looking for our first boxes. We've since become hooked.
But a pretty busy life has hampered placing our own boxes. Until now
that is. Yesterday I planted our first box. It's a HH though so
there aren't any clues. :P

I also have a few stamps carved and more ideas in my head. I'm hoping
that in May our hectic life will slow down enough to have time to get
out and plant some boxes. We've already scouted out a few places and
just need to write clues and carve a few more stamps. There's a need
for more boxes on the east side of Grand Rapids. ;)

Letterboxing combines many of the things that we're interested in so
I'm really happy we discovered it!
