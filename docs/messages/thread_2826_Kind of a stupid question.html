From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-07-26 23:26:44 UTC
Hello,
I wanted to let everyone know - Hobby Lobby carries MasterCarve, and
Speedy Stamp now - but NOT in the Stamping Aisle! Its over near screen
printing, and similar items. (Was near the derby cars!) They also have
the carvers. (I bought a kit with SpeedyStamp, and several blocks of
Master Carve.)

Found it yesterday - Prices werent bad either. (Beats paying shipping,
and instant gratification!

I hope this was helpful to someone - OH! Go to the Hobby Lobby Website
and sign up for their weekly ad to come into your mailbox and about
every other week get a 40% off one item COUPON! Whoo Hoo!

Paula
