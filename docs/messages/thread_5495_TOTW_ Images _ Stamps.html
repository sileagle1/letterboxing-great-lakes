Great, here is how it works.

I am going to use March 1st as the deadline to get your box to the
person you are swapping with. I will assign each person another
person to whom they will be sending their box to. When you receive
your new box in the mail you are responsible for planting it and
putting the clues on the internet for all the find. Please be sure
and give credit to the creator of the box.
