Time for Spring Cleaning

From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-23 03:04:54 UTC

If memory serves me....

A reminder that the month of April has been declared the official
LbGLK letterbox "spring cleaning" month. During the month of April,
we should check all of our boxes and do any maintenance on them,
including updating our clues to say "Last checked - April, 2005".

The thought was to have all our boxes in the upper midwest ready not
only for us local boxers to find, but also for any boxers that might
be planning trips our way!

If anyone has boxes far away from you, I'd suggest you ask another
boxer that is closer to check on your box for you. For example, I
just received word that my "Go to Hell" box has just been found and is
in good condition and ready for the summer from a boxer in MI. They
had emailed me before going, and I asked them to do any "spring
cleaning" on the box if needed.

If there is anyone in the UP that could check on my boxes up there,
please let me know. I already have plans to lots of checking and
fixing of my boxes around here during my spring break next week. Let
me know if I should check on any of yours while I'm at it. Hopefully,
the weather will cooperate...

Dragon
