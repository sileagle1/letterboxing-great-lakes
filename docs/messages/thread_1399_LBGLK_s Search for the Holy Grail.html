Papeseed - to bring you up to speed, the concept that
has developed with this "search for the holy grail" is
that all the participants from all over would carve a
stamp pertaining to the movie, but when we list the
clues they'd just be listed as "Search For The Holy
Grail Box 1" or something like that, so that the
searcher wouldn't know what the actual box's theme was
until they found it, and then whoever found the Holy
Grail box first could gloat....

Kimono Girl - I was thinking, too, about how could
this series be created without duplicates, etc. One
thought I had was at the gathering, we could right
down characters/scenes/ etc. on slips of paper, and
everyone who wanted to participate could draw one
(keeping the element of surprise, no one will know who
got what or where it will be planted). But since not
everyone who's voiced desire to participate will be at
the gathering, how would THEY then get to pick their
character/scene/etc? Unless we didn't look at the
remaining slips after everyone pulled one and put them
in envelopes right away and mailed them out after the
gathering.... *shrug*

Anyone have any other thoughts on this yet?

Doodle Bug.
