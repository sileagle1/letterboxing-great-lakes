Great Lakes patches now available!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-08-25 02:04:15 UTC
Hi!

I received the patches in the mail today and they did turn out pretty
nice! I'll hold in reserve until OCT 1 patches for anyone who
already emailed me that they wanted some. They'll be available for
purchaseat the Haunted Harvest Gathering on OCT 1. If you won't be
able to attend the gathering, or if you want them sooner, you'll need
to send me a self-addressed stamped envelope and a check for the
appropriate amount. The patches are $1.50 each. I don't think
you'll need any extra postage for 6 patches or less. I also ordered
some extra ones, so you can still email me to let me know if you want
some. If you want to see what they look like, go to the Photos
section of this group and click on the Patches folder.

You can send checks and self-addressed, stamped envelopes to:

Debbie Kotlarek
19425 Buckingham Place
Brookfield, WI 53045

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. I won't be able to get them out in the mail to you until after
Labor Day.
