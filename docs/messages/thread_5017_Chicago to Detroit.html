From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-02 13:16:17 UTC-05:00
Atlas Quest allows you to do a trip planner between cities along
interstate highways. If you go to AQ and click on "Letterboxes" then
"Simple Search," there is an option on the upper right for "Trip
Search." You can specify I-94 between Detroit and Chicago and then
choose if you want to look for boxes 5miles, 10miles, etc off the
highway. This search will give you 43 options for boxes within 5miles
of the interstate along that route.

Good luck and have fun on your trip! :)
Coffee Beans

From: baliddle (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-05-02 14:16:33 UTC-04:00
Hi!
I thought that there was a listing of boxes along i-94/i-80 in our
database or files section, but I can't seem to find it now. But,
poke around in there on Yahoo, there may be something I missed.

On Atlasquest, you can do a "trip planner" search and select highways
and map them out. It's a cool feature. But, it'll only show up
boxes that are on AQ, which only seems to be about 1/2 in this area
(SE Michigan). To do this, click on Lettterboxes on the main menu,
then advance search. On that page over on the top right, use the
little drop down thing to select trip planner. Then you just go from there.

I know there are several boxes in the Dearborn/Allen
Park/Wyandotte/Taylor/Trenton/Livonia/Redford areas that should be
pretty accessible to you when you're here.

I have a box in Jackson County, MI that's not too far off i-94.

Good luck to your daughter and have fun!

Bali
