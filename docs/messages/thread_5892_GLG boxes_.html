To: lbglk@yahoogroups.com
From: krank@ameritech.net
Date: Mon, 25 May 2009 16:40:30 -0700
Subject: [LbGLK] "Project Pajama Pants" to be available at GLG







I know this is kind of late notice, but I found this letterbox (created for the WI-10 gathering last year) amongst my collection of stuff here at home and decided to bring it along to the GLG. However instead of pajama pants, to receive this stamp, please bring a box of cold drink mix packets for bottled water (such as Propel, Gatorade and Crystal Lite To Go), as requested on the Operation Support Our Troops - Illinois website. Please do not bring any out-of-date or soon-to-be-expiring products.

I AM NOT COLLECTING PAJAMA PANTS OR SUNDAY COMICS, SO DISREGARD THAT PART OF THE MESSAGE IN THE AQ CLUES.

Letters to the troops are still welcome.

See http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=91695 for more information.

--klk/fw
