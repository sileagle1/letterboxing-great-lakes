From: amyscrapdiva (amyscrapdiva@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-06 14:00:24 UTC

Hi all,

My name is Amy McIntire and I live in Columbus, Ohio. I am very
interested in getting started with this neat thing called
Letterboxing! I would love to meet some of you at a gathering
sometime. Please e-mail me at amyscrapdiva@yahoo.com, I would love to
get to know all of you and chat about all sorts of newbie stuff. If
any of you live in Ohio, I would love to meet up and go box hunting.
I am really thinking about placing boxes too, but I kind of want to
get me "feet wet first".

I am a stamp designer and scrapbook/rubber stamp artist...that is how
I found out about Letterboxing! I am looking forward in creating
stamp designs for this and would love to do a stamp swap if anyone is
interested.

Anyhow, hope all of you have a great day and I look forward into
chatting with everyone soon!

Amy
