Wisconsin Hiker...I'm giving Fungus Woman a run for her money...I
LOVE the cicadas..she only has them on her arm...You probably don't
want to see the pictures of them scampering on my face and
hair...And, I won't even mention in my ....uh....mouth!

We have them so thick here that I am guiding field trips around my
back yard to watch the mayhem! It is so noisy that you can hear them
over the roar of the lawn mower.

It is nothing short of a cicada orgy!

In matter of fact, Tonto went out to his car this morning to put
something in the trunk. He returned inside to kiss the old girl
goodbye...He nonchalantly said in the midst of the kiss, "Is there
one on my neck?"
Sure enough, as I opened my eyes, (Yes, I close my eyes when he
kisses me...)there was one coming around the corner, so to speak,
from his ear to his chin....A male to be sure as it was making a
whole lot of racket...Probably realized Tonto wasn't exactly his type!
I'm getting sad that they will be gone by the fourth of July...not to
be seen again for seventeen more years!
