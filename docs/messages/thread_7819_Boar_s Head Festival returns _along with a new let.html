Once again, our church is presenting the Boar's Head Festival--a wonderful way to celebrate the Christmas season. And also, once again, we will be hiding a letterbox that can be found on the days of the performances. Those days are Saturday, 12/14 and Sunday, 12/15.

See our church website for a full description of the festival: http://www.stjohnforestpark.org/upcoming_events

Clues for the letterbox will be posted closer to the event. It will have the words "Boar's Head Festival" somewhere in the box name and will be listed in Forest Park, IL.

We look forward to seeing you there!
