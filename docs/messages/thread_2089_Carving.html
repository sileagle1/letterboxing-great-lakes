From: ncginger2000 (ncginger2000@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-12 01:42:15 UTC
We have two Michael's in town (Asheville, NC): one carries carving
material, the other doesn't. The materials are near the Calligraphy
supplies. (Speedball writing nibs and carving nibs go together, right?
Hmmph... Doesn't make sense to me...)

IF your Michael's has them, look for them in the Art section with
paints, calligraphy inks, etc. Not all Michael's have them and DON'T
expect the Michael's employees to actually know where they are. Even
the ones that are responsible for the section often don't know what
the stuff is used for.

Good luck,
