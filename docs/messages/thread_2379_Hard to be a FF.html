Hello to you too! We're looking forward to meeting you at the
gathering in June.

We have 2 FF stories:

It was a Saturday afternoon and we had just finished making dessert for
our gourmet dinner group that night. We checked our emails and saw
that a 3-box series had just been posted! Without any hesitation we
decided we had enough time to head to the lakefront to hunt them before
the dinner group met. As we were on our way to the 3rd box of the
series (logging in as FF), we passed the 2nd box's location and saw
someone with sheets of paper in his hand. And so it was that we met
our first boxer out on the trail - The Dragon himself! (who had ALSO
obviously jumped in his car when he saw the new clues). I think your
chance of meeting other boxers out on the trail increases if you're out
right away for a new box.

Our other story: Clues to a new box only a mile or so from our house
were posted late Friday night. Martini Man was already sleeping, so I
printed them off and figured we could head out on the hunt the first
thing in the morning. Well, MM is usually a very early riser, but that
day he slept in. I didn't want to wake him (well, actually I DID, but
figured he might not appreciate it), so waited for him to wake up. As
soon as he did (still well before 8 am), we were out the door. We got
to the park and saw a car just pulling out of the lot. It COULDN'T
have been another boxer, could it? Well, when we got to the box we
discovered it HAD been. Acorn, all the way up from Illinois, had
stamped in before us! Unfortunately we missed doing an exchange at the
time, but had the good fortune to meet her later at some events.

Wisconsin Hiker
