I'm sorry I haven't had a chance to reply sooner (hopefully you receive
this in time):

Definitely look for mystery boxes in Washtenaw County.
If you are at Rolling Hills, be sure to look for Blue Diamond boxes in
the area (Potluck Picnic is one of my favorites).

If you are going to the hands-on museum, be aware that it is very crazy
if it is a rainy morning. When we were hiding the boxes, it took us
almost an **HOUR** to hide the one box.

Also, at the museum, #3 is missing its stamp so it has been pulled until
Mysterina can recarve it. The other 4 are still in place.

Have fun wherever you go!

-- MichKathy
