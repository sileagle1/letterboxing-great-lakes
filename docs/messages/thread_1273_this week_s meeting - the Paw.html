From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-05-03 00:05:48 UTC-05:00
Everyone is welcome to join us at noon Saturday at the Paw in Brookfield
(IL). We have some guest postals stopping by for a visit, including "Happy
Birthday to Me!" (in honor of my impending milestone coming up in October).
Nuts4Mice has a surprise too. I will be bringing the LbWO for any Girl
Scout letterboxers in attendance, and the limited edition "I love my Mommy"
in honor of Mother's Day. On the "regular" box front, FOOW9 is waiting for
visitors (don't know if anyone has claimed first finder yet). I'm going to
bring stuff to make "explosion books" (if you're on the Postal list, you may
have seen the instructions posted by Janice of The Sugar Beats) if anyone
wants to try making a fun logbook.
