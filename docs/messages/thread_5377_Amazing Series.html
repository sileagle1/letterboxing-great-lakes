Amazing Series

From: grandvilla02 (grandvilla02@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-10-24 00:46:32 UTC
If any of you have tried and failed to find the Amazing series at
Yankee Springs...I can only say I now know why! The park has added and
taken away many signs and posts used in the clues! In fact we were
there yesterday revising the clues, and in the hour and a half we were
there, the new clues were altered! When we were about ready to leave,
we met a (very nice) ranger who was adding to and removing signs and
posts that we had referenced! Aarrgh! A trip back today confirmed what
we had finalized yesterday (no new changes). So, print the new clues
and rush right out to find the series before they change again! :-)
Grandvilla
