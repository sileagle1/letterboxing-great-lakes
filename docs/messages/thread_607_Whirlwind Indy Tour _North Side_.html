Whirlwind Indy Tour (North Side)

From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-28 19:00:43 UTC-07:00
Did you ever dream about letterboxing? I did...so I
took a ride to Indy today to find some boxes...

I found:
36th in Kokomo
Potter's Bridge in Noblesville

Picnic in the Park
Papillon
Cicada Land
Cross Country Crossroads
Morning Glory
Independence Day #1

all in Fort Harrison State Park. More Than Corn seems
to be kinda missing (found the tree, looked under the
rock, no box). All had nice hand-carved stamps. Boxing
under the nose of 50 million cyclists and the DNR was
pretty fun. No one seemed to notice me poking around
in my "letterboxing getup" while they were having some
big bicycle race around Harrison Trace. It was an
ungodly humid day, so I pooped out pretty early.
Didn't meet my goal of getting to Broad Ripple and
Eagle Creek...but oh well. I'll get back down there
soon, i'm sure.

Does anyone else have a "letterboxing getup" or outfit
that they wear exclusively to letterbox? I'm finding
that I am partial to an obnoxious tie dyed t-shirt,
olive cargo pants that roll up, hiking shoes, and now
i've added a tacky visor from work. I'm sure the
walkers on most of the trails I took today thought I
was certifiable...

Time to gear up for Leland, MI trip next weekend! My
host is dying to go boxing so....it should be fun!
