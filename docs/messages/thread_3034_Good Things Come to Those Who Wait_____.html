Good Things Come to Those Who Wait.....

From: karen grage (jkgrage@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-02 22:49:01 UTC-05:00
Well, I must have been a very good girl this year because my Secret Santa delivered the most amazing package to my doorstep yesterday. I opened up my box and was greeted by four beautifully wrapped packages tied together by elegant strings.

My first present contained much needed stamping markers. They were in fact so needed that they were opened up for use the day the package arrived.

My second present contained a hand macramd keychain with LBNA beads. I can't wait to add it to my keys.

My third and forth presents contained the most amazing handmade log books I've ever seen. I can't say anything other than they are truly works of art. I can't imagine ever using them other than to show them to everyone I know.

Thanks so much to my Secret Santa for making me feel so special. These gifts will be treasured.
