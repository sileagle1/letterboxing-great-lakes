PLAYING TIME:

WINNERS DRAWN ON:
Friday, November 24, 2006 and posted to the list before 11pm

HOW IT WORKS:
Each time you are the first one to place a box in one of the 14
"unboxed" MI counties and email me with the URL for the clues, you
will get your name added to another drawing ... twice! NOTE: You
must email me the URL with the clues for it to count in the drawing

places in Jackson county AFTER it has already been covered, they would
get their name in the drawing once for each box placed)

I have prizes, but more could be possible if others would like to
