Wow, I didn't realize there would be so much interest in Polish
Christmas foods--I guess VeggieTales knew what they were doing when
they wrote that song!!

Here is the list of Polish foods included in the song, which is sung
by "Oscar, the Polish caterer" (along with some of the commentary
that the other people in the song give):

1. The first Polish Christmas dish I bring to the party--a boiled
potato topped with dill weed.
2. Two steamed pierogies (What's a pierogi? It's a dough, wrapped
around meat)
3. Three simmered gwumpkies (What's a gwumpky? It's a cabbage
wrapped around meat)
4. Four baked paprikas (What is a paprika? It's a bell pepper
stuffed with meat)
5. Five smoked kielbasas (What is a kielbasa? It's pretty much
just meat)
6. Six fried hoosecheekies (Let me guess--something in the meat
family? Actually it's a delightful pastry with a thin flaky crust)
7. Seven pitted prunes (I don't like prunes! With this food, you'll
need 'em, son)
8. Eight poppy seed cakes (Poppies--Poppies--Poppies. I'm feeling
rather sleepy--there's no place like home...)

So there you have the Eight Polish Foods of Christmas!!
