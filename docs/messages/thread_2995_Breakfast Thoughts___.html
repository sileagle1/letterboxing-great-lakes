was actually developed as a health food and for several years was
served exclusively to patrons of the Sanitarium, a spa-resort run by

uses enough sugar each year to cover every single American with

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-02 11:45:38 UTC
The word "breakfast" literally means to break fast. As we know,
fasting is the act of not eating over a period of time. We may not
think of it this way, but we virtually fast while we sleep even if
we dream about food. The act of eating breakfast is literally breaking
the fast!

Cereal is the number one food bought in supermarkets today. The first
breakfast cereal was created in 1863.
