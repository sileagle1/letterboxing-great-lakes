Today I went to downtown Milwaukee to replace a box of mine that was
reported missing. Wouldn't you know it, but I ran into a fellow
letterboxer who happened to be looking for that very same box! He
helped me replace and rehide the box and then we set out together
to find a few more boxes in the area neither of us had found yet.
I had such a great time sharing LB stories, comparing notes and
hiking with this person, (don't get me wrong, Sophie is fun to
letterbox with too, but she sucks as a conversationalist)! I was so
impressed with this person, I've decided to write a poem in his
honor....


Jigsaw, my new friend,
Found looking for the Prozac.
Sophie thinks you're cool!





May all your boxes stay intact,

Sophie & Me
