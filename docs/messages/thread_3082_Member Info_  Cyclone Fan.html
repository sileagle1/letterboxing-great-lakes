I'm from Southeast Wisconsin and am a newbie at this. I forget how I
learned about letterboxing, but I made the effort to get a logbook and
buy a stamp before our family trip to Yellowstone last summer. I
attempted to find two boxes there and failed to find any (although I'm
confident on one I was close!) Then the school year started and I set

I'll have time to try again. My family is sometimes interested,

avid boxer. Also my lack of ability to tell one tree species from
another makes it difficult. But who knows? I've got the materials
handy if I want to try again and there appear to be a bazillion boxes
planted in my corner of the state!
