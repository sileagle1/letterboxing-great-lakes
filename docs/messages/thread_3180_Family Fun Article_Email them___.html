Family Fun Article/Email them!!!

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-10-16 15:20:54 UTC-05:00
To read the Family Fun article, go to http://familyfun.go.com In the upper right hand corner, type in the word letterboxing and click on search. A four page article will appear. Yes, they only mention LbNA.

Here's a novel suggestion: Email Family Fun Magazine and let them know the response and the results of their September 2006 feature article Go Letterboxing. Maybe they were misinformed or unaware that this somewhat "secretive" hobby (formerly secretive hobby?) requires some research into the rules and etiquette. Their article doesn't go into details about using caution when discovering a box nor so they give specific details about how to
carefully replace a box. I would venture to guess that many of the newbies just read this article and ran right out to find as many boxes as they ....gulp.... dare I say.....as many as they could find, toss aside on the ground, or simply take home.

Protect nature. Safe guard letterboxes. Hopefully, we can discourage Family Fun Magazine from reprinting this feature article again. Unless they are made aware of the problems that have resulted in publicizing this hobby to a large reading circulation of parents, children, and youth groups, then they may continue to print similar articles on letterboxing in future issues.

us!
