A Glorious Weekend to Remember

From: speedsquare_lbxr (ruhlette@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-03 22:55:43 UTC

I just returned from God's Country and report the 2005 letterboxing season has officially
begun for me!

The weather was absolutely perfect Saturday and Sunday. We left Indy Friday and ate
dinner at a new restaurant in Chicago, Hacienda del Sol ... 53rd and Pulaski. Not a Metra
stop, but near Midway and the Orange Line EL. We know the owners and perhaps a
letterbox will appear in the future?!? We had the Parrillada, tried grilled cactus, and the
dessert ... a chocolate cake with flan ... ooh.

Well, On to Wisconsin! We met up with our good friend and introduced him to
letterboxing with The Dragon's Deutsche Letterbox. The combination of hiking in a
beautiful location with historical interest and challenging clues made for a memorable
experience for all. Even reluctant dh was sorry we didn't have clues for more letterboxes
with us on Saturday. We'll return for the MCP series later! Quite a rush to find a box with
my rusty German translation.

This morning we travelled to get the PI Day Series and the ??? Mystery Box. We wanted to
get out early to avoid being seen. Springin' forward sleepy heads still at home. Thank
you, Dragon, for leaving the series out for me! I think I might be the first out-of-stater to
log in! I was thinking of a couple of more stamps ... perhaps ... Pickle Chiffon PI and
Sweetie PI? As time was short, I had to skip Triangles Triangles Everywhere. I see I need
to find that old TI-30. I knew I was in trouble when I found the green box and it looked
like the "WBSC" had been rubbed off the top and I couldn't find a trace of the number "on
top". I did find 7-digit serial number and the phone number, but I couldn't convince my
dh that we could successfully complete this series before 11:00 am. I'll be baaack.

Happy boxing 2005!
