It's December 26 - Boxing Day! 

 

Even if you aren't able to get out to find a box, you can get your Letterboxing Bingo cards completed.  Remember the cards are due in only 5 DAYS!  You can email them to me as a MS Word document, a pdf file, or just take a photo of your completed card and send me the .jpg file.  Your booty can follow - I just need to have the $2 and booty by the end of January.  (Remember that if you are going to the Dead of Winter event in Rockford, WI, you can give me your booty then.)

 

I'm hoping that we get many more players signed up in these final few days!  Checking to see if the "word of the day" is on your card gives you a little something to look forward to during the dreary winter season.  The thrill of victory could be yours this year!

 

Wisconsin Hiker

 

P.S.  Speaking of the Dead of Winter event, I think ggghiker is still looking for more carvings/boxes for the "fossils" theme.  Please contact her if you can contribute a stamp for the event.  I'm going to try carving Illinois's state fossil and Martini Man will be carving a spiked curled snail shell fossil.
