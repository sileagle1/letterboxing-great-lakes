Chicago Underground is back!

From: Matt Bergstrom (ratpatrolhq@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-23 11:28:46 UTC-07:00
Hi Great Lakes boxers,

Its been nearly a year since the Chicago Underground
box disappeared. It took me a long time to make
another stamp, logbook and box, but its finally ready
and hidden in place. My apologies for being so
sluggish about replacing the box.

I'm also ready to replace the Magic Hedge box tomorrow
morning, but I'll need to rewrite the clues a bit for
the new, improved hiding place. We'll see how long
this one lasts. I found such a great hiding spot, oooh
its perfect, you'll all love it!

Also, as FungusWoman alerted me to, the Chicago
Portage box is definitely gone. I guess they've been
cleaning up brush at the park and discovered it. I
took it off the clues list.

Matt the Rat
