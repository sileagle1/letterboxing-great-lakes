Hand-carved stamps

From: Chris Mothkovich (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2007-06-16 11:07:37 UTC-05:00
Just wanted to let everyone know that yesterday I braved 90-degree weather
and screeching, buzzing cicadas and rode Ginger in to update my
"Kinnickinnick" letterbox. I removed the original (commercial) rubber stamp
and replaced it with a hand-carved stamp. ALL of my planted boxes now have
hand-carved stamps (with the exception of "Sgt. Snuffy" which has a stamp
custom-made for that box).

My friend Fred (who goes by the nickname "krait") has been carving these
stamps for me. He is VERY talented at this! Unfortunately he has physical
problems that keep him from being able to hike very far, and so hasn't been
able to get out and do any letterboxing himself. But he likes the fact that
"his" stamps are out there for others to find and enjoy.

If anyone has any recommendations for easy "drive-by" stamps in the north
central area of Illinois I'd be glad to pass them on to Fred.

Trail Rider/Chris
