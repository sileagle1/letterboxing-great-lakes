Merry Christmas from up nort!

From: Jeni (jeners@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-12-26 00:15:21 UTC
So those of you not from Minnesota, I'm not sure if you know how much snow we have gotten recently...

First, the snow unleashed fury upon Minneapolis and surrounding areas around the second week of December, dropping about 20 inches in some places of snooooow!

The Metrodome collapsed, a blizzard was called by the national weather service, roads were closed everywhere because it was unsafe for even plows to travel!

After the storm passed, the snow didn't cease... we have recently gotten around another 6 to 10 inches along with the mess that accumulated from the blizzard...

Long story short, OLJ and myself have a tradition of going boxing on holidays, no matter what!

This year was no different, we put on the long underwear, snowpants, boots, gloves, scarves, multiple jackets and loaded in the car to seek out our holiday boxes!

Lucky for us it wasn't snowing today! However we had to trek through feet upon feet of snow just to get a drive-by!

We were semi-successful, we sought out winter friendly boxes but I don't think most boxers when placing winter friendly boxes are expecting 2+ feet of snow in most places, so we still had quite the challenge! We attempted 4 and found 2!

Hope everyone's holidays are filled with hope and the wish that no matter what the circumstances you remain steadfast when seeking out those winter boxes!

Merry Happy Christmas Holiday!
