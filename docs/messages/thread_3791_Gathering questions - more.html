From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2007-02-28 04:30:02 UTC
You can do any of those! You could also make one copy of the clues
to post in a central area (taped to a wall or table) and people could
write down their own copy if they wanted to look for the box (best
not to have clues that are too long for this approach).

We've also been to a gathering where people had temporary "tatoos" on
their arms that were actually clues to a box. Of course these were
really short clues!

Wisconsin Hiker
