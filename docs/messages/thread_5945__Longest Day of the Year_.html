Re: "Longest Day of the Year"

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-06-22 03:07:24 UTC
Wow, it's been so quiet here that everyone MUST have been out boxing! We had a very nice time in Green Bay. There is a new letterboxer in that area called AtHomeDad that has planted some very nice boxes. It is quite unusual for a new boxer to put out such high quality boxes (we certainly had a much longer learning curve on our carving and placements). Excellent carvings, good placements, double-baggies in camo boxes, etc.

We also found some very amusing Pugnasties boxes. Check out the Boxers n' Briefs series if you are in the area.

During our hunts we had a chance to visit many different parks, view Lake Michigan, a waterfall, follow along a river AND visit a custard stand!

We saw that CherokeeRose2 and Hawkeye were also in the area - they had stamped into several boxes we found on the same day. Too bad we missed actually seeing them in person!

Hope you all had a good weekend (and a Happy Father's Day)!

Wisconsin Hiker
