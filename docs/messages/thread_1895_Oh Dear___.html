From: LindaFay (coachlindafay@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-16 22:46:36 UTC
Sooo Sorry your box was lost. One of my boxes was found in bad shape
when I was taking a little girl out for the first time. When we got to
the #2 on the Maumee Dog Walk all that was there was the bone and the
log book. Then we started looking around a found the two stamps and
pads in the bushes. When we looked in the logbook it appeared that
some youngun' had found it and had stamped the stamps in the logbook
and then done finger prints of her name "Josie". I'm guessing she
found it, played with it and then someone come by and she just tossed
it. We got it back together and figured we were lucky! This was very
well hidden, but the last finder could have left it out.

Hope your other boxes stay safe.

LindaFay
