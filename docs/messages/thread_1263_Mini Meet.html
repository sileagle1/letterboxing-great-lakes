Doglvr's deserve a big round of applause for all their hard work getting
the gathering organized and it ran very smoothly. We had a fairly large
turnout and found some special boxes that were planted just for the mini
meet. Doglvr's will be posting those clues to the website soon for those of
you who could not make it. We had several young boxers along who were a joy
to have on the outing.
Several times it ended up being a battle of the sexes with the woman
proving to be victorious! I think the men outwitted themselves! I know it
was very hard for "R" of Doglvr's to keep from wagging his tongue but with
the threat of being skinned alive he managed to keep quiet. That and having
to help carry the stroller with Rainy Day Rabbit in it helped keep his mind
off helping with the clues.
I am so glad that we got to meet so many of you boxers and hope to see you
again at the Gathering.
