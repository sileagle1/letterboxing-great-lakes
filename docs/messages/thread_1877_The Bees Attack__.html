The Bees Attack!!

From: Jason (lewwdawg_840@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-09 17:52:54 UTC
Hi everyone!

I just wanted to post a little adventure we had out letterboxing
today. We got out real early this morning because we wanted to hit
a few boxes in the Western Springs (IL) area - the biggest draw
being the Haunted Trail Series that was just put out by CMoon. Well
we found a couple other boxes and then headed to Bemis Woods to
search for the Haunted Series. When we pulled into Bemis Woods, we
stopped at the trail map right next to the entrance to the Salt
Creek Bike Trail to check out where we were headed. We are standing
at the map minding our own business when out of nowhere a ton of
bees started attacking us. They weren't there one minute and the
next minute there were about 40 freakin bees surrounding us. And
these guys were agressive too. They chased us halfway down the
parking lot before we could get back into the car and drive away.
(Two of them even followed us back into the car) I was only stung
once on the back of my leg, but Jason got hit about 3-4 times.
Being the troopers that we are, we decided to go ahead and get the
series. But we called it a day after that. I wanted to go look for
Biker Chicks:Tandumb but there was NO WAY we were going to go back
by that trail map.

So just a warning to anyone headed out to Bemis Woods . . . be
careful of the bees. I don't know if we were near a hive or what
(we really didn't investigate) but maybe at least avoid that trail
map if you can.

Happy Boxing Everyone!!

Julie (Wormy Wanderers)
