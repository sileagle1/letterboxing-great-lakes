Dragon's Box Updates Part 2

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-29 02:37:41 UTC

Well, I got out again today, and in addition to getting my forehead
sunburned (believe it!) I have the following to report for any that
are interested:

Since I have some students needing to seek out some of the Mathman
boxes this spring break, I'm focusing on those boxes first. Please
note that the clues to these boxes are on our school's server, and I
will be updating the Mathman pages/clues on Tuesday morning as soon as
I can get up to school. If you go to any of these sites now, you'll
probably need to "refresh" your browser if you look at the pages later
on Tuesday to get the changes.

Mathman #7 (Luther's Love)- Ok, but frozen in place. I hope to pry it
out Tuesday afternoon and camouflage it.

Mathman #8 (OBEZAG)- Found that one of my students was the last one
there, and they didn't put the box back together right. Had to dry
out the logbook (which didn't take long today :-) ) and do other
maintenance, and it is fully available. You can be sure I will have a
talk with the young man when school gets back in session...

Mathman #5 (West Bend Park) - Was reported missing, but was actually
on the ground about 15 feet away from the hiding location and open.
Luckily the students that visited it last DID reseal everything and
everything survived pretty well except for the Mathman stamp which I
carved out of some grey material (not sure what it was - Mastercarve?
SpringChick, this was the stuff I gave to you at the gathering, so I
hope you didn't use it where it might freeze!) and it had cracked
after being frozen, so there are gaps in the stamp, which I thought
gives it "character". Anyway, it is all ok now.

Mathman #4 (Homestead Hollow) - Still doing fine. Had to clean the
stamps after a freshman left me a mess. I'll be talking to him too...

MAthman #6 (Remember the Vetrans) - I was hoping I could open this one
for the season, but alas, the grave is still under snow. I'll check
again later this week once more thawing has occurred.

The PI Day boxes are still all up and available until Monday afternoon
for any that are still interested!

And, I'm proud to announce the placement of the new Mathman boxes!
We've been waiting since January, but now the wait is over (John and
Sara, I know you just couldn't wait anymore! ;-) )

Mathman 12 (Practice Parking) - Travel around Mequon, WI and practice
your driving skills and math skills at the same time! Created by a
group of sophomores with their "temps", this series of 4 boxes has
four stamps that fit together like a small puzzle. Oh, and of course,
the box also contains the new 04-05 edition of the Mathman stamp as well!

Mathman 13 (Spassland Park) - This short but sweet hunt will make you
"spass out" at this small community park. Note: Find it on Mapquest
and print a map. The signs in Germantown pointing to the park are NOT
helpful! Rumor has it that Spassland II will be coming in May...

Mathman 14 (Cedar Creek) - Another short but sweet hunt will return
some of you to Covered Bridge Park in Cedarburg for another box to
find. And this time you don't have to wade through the river or
swampy area to get the box. Just don't fall off the ledge!

You can access the clues (noting I'll be updating the pages Tuesday
morning) via my home page at home.wi.rr.com/dragonlber

Enjoy!
Dragon
