I haven't visited Yahoo groups in quite a while and I am adjusting to the newer format.  Our vacation plans have been shortened, but we will be there this year.  I had previously added our lodging reservation to the database, but tonight we added Thursday night at the AmericInn.  For the life of me I cannot find the pencil to edit my original entry.  If the owner can fix this, that would be fine.  Or tell me where to find that pencil.


I wasn't able to create a new "best carve" this year due to other commitments.  Since there will be all types of boxes - including puzzlers, would it be okay and appropriate for me to bring my "Snickers" worksheets to the shelter as my best carve?  I will also have my LTC puzzler and, of course, my PT that requires some prior research, on my person on the trail.  If you are very observant you will notice an obvious clue to confirm your suspicions.


My absolutely best carve ever was The Rose Gardeners that appeared at the GLG 2006 in Wisconsin.  Quite a few found it, so I don't really want to overdo it, but for the newer boxers, I could be encouraged to bring it. Would there be any interest in this one?  If so, I can add it as event box on AQ.


If our plans work out, we plan to head back to Minneapolis Friday morning to do some shopping and box back to North Branch.  I have never stepped foot into Minnesota, so guess where we will shop??  Nope, not Mall of America.  Bauer Brothers Salvage is where I want to go.  I love the Rehab Addict.


Finally, are there any plans for a meet-n-greet Friday evening in the area?  If not, DH is interested in finding a decent place for dinner.  Taking suggestions.


We are so ready for a break!


See you all there,
