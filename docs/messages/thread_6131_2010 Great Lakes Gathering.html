2010 Great Lakes Gathering

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-10-21 10:44:34 UTC
There has been a lot of confusion surrounding the 2010 Great Lakes Gathering. Things kind of got off track and ahead of procedure, etc. and I am going to attempt to get the planning headed back in the right direction as diplomatically as possible.

At this point, the only thing that has been decided is that the event will be held in Illinois, based on the established rotation. Yet to be decided are the date and who will be hosting the event.

Here is what's going to happen:

1) Within the next day or two I will post a poll for LbGLK list members to vote on the date options.

2) There were 3 different people/groups who had expressed interest in hosting the event. I have contacted each individually to see if they are still interested. If there is anyone else who is interested in hosting the event in Illinois this year, please post to the list or contact me privately before Sunday -- you should have an approximate location in mind (do not reserve anything at this point -- just have a general area in mind) and some idea(s) as to theme.

3) If there is more than one potential host, a poll will be conducted based on proposed theme and approximate location.

SpringChick
