The K-Martha Story...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-03-27 10:44:26 UTC
The stamp up for bid on E-Bay looks to be an authentic LB
Hitchhiker/Event stamp that many folks on this list have stamped
into their log books.

K-Martha began as a joke by the infamous Legerdemaine from Maine.
Immediately it caused a stir in the letterboxing world both because
of the novelty of the stamp and also because it had been done by
Legerdemaine. One NE letterboxer in particular, who shall remain
unnamed, although I will say she is well-known for the great number
of LB finds she has acquired, was intent on getting her hands on
this stamp. It has been rumored that she frequently set out on wild
goose chases to boxes hundreds of miles from her home if it was
thought the K-Martha resided in one of them.

As an practical joke to confuse this insanely zealous boxer, a
second K-Martha stamp was carved, purportedly by Legerdemaine.
These two stamps were close to identical and to the average
letterboxer, it would be nearly impossible to distinguish between
the two. About this time, the K-Martha stamps began making the
rounds as event stamps rather than being placed in LB as HH's. Soon
afterward one of the stamps disappeared from circulation (it is
believed that was the original stamp). Rumor has it that the over-
zealous LB finally got her hands on it and it now resides in her
underwear drawer.

About 2 years ago, once it was realized that there was only 1 stamp
in circulation, a 3rd stamp was carved, again with the intent to
cause confusion and bewilder finders. This stamp was carved by an
individual who shall remain unnamed, although Legerdemaine certainly
had a hand in the mischief.

At the Morraine Hills Great Lakes Gathering, two of the K-Martha
stamps were present, supposedly #2 and #3. Since that time these
two have been making the rounds to gatherings although there is not
so much the hype and intrigue as they once generated.

It appears that the stamp for sale on E-Bay is one of these.

Disclaimer... This is related to the best of my knowledge. As
there are so many stories circulating about K-Martha, the facts have
gotten twisted and even this account, although not entirely hearsay,
may not be 100% accurate.
