Hi there!
I just wanted to let you know that I didn't get out to Waterford
today to relocate Jerome the Nomadic Gnome after all. So, if you
still wanted to find him in his Winter home, you have a little while
longer. At this point, I might just wait until Easter Sunday to go move him.

I did get some letterboxing adventures in today, though. We ended up
going back out to Maybury State Park again this week and found all
three boxes we didn't find last week! I think we had Winter Brain
Fog last week after 2 months of not finding any boxes! Actually, we
think that some other boxers had one of the boxes we were searching
for last week. There was a log entry from the same day. What are the odds!?

The other two, we stupidly followed the wrong trail markers because
we were trying to find three boxes at the same time and assumed the
trail marker #'s were the same numbers. Of course, we didn't realize
it because we didn't see any of the "other" style numbers. The one
box clues have you go to pole #2, then 3. The other boxes say 1, 2,
3, 4... so we figured they were referring to the same trail numbers
and we could meet up with #3 on those clues once we found the first
box. Nope! Totally took us to the wrong #4 pole. And to make
matters worse, there's a tree at the wrong #4 that "fits" the clues
to a tree mentioned for the other box. So, it really seemed like we
were in the right place.

Hope you all managed to get some letterboxing in this fine weekend!

Bali
