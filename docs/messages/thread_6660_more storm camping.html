From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-06-21 09:13:47 UTC-05:00
Well, we weren't CAMPING, but we were out biking on the "Yellow Brick Road"
(Hennepin Canal trail) Friday afternoon with Tiptoe & Tonto when dark clouds
appeared VERY suddenly, winds whipped the trees and our bikes and rain began
to fall. We pedaled furiously back to the van and arrived just before the
full force of the storm let loose. Later that night, just after dinner at
the B&B, we got to hear the weather warning sirens sounding a few times.
Luckily there was no serious damage and no power loss and we were back out
on the trail both Saturday & Sunday.
