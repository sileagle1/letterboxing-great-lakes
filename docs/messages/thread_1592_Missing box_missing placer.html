Re: [LbGLK] Missing box/missing placer

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-07-12 19:36:02 UTC-04:00
Welcome to the group! Hopefully somebody in the Chicago area might be able to provide some insight about the box you were looking for. I am not familiar with the planter name. I did want to clarify the date though. About 2 years ago, the letterboxing.org site (where the clues are located) underwent a major overhaul. All of the box clues from the old web site needed to be entered into the new website. Each planter was responsbile for moving over their own boxes. Boxes that were left, if it was known they were active at the time, were moved over by the webmasters and no date was assigned. These are the boxes that show up with a date of Dec 31, 1969. So don't be fooled by the stale date.

SpringChick

Welcome to Letterboxing!

Dec 31, 1969 will show up when the placer does not indicate the actual
date of placement. Some of the older boxes might show this date when a
conversion occured some time before I got involved. You may or may not
ever get a response from the placer. I have not looked for this box, so I
cannot confirm anything. I would try again, especially if others have
reported it alive and well. Good luck.
