LTC- New Years theme swap?

From: Ashley (americangirl02@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-12-03 22:20:08 UTC
Hey everyone,
Right now I am really excited about these trading cards, so I thought
I'd host a swap myself. As the title says, it would be New Years
themed. You can carve any stamp that has to do with New Years and
then design your cards around that. It would probably also be a good
idea to include "2008" somewhere on the card. Then if everyone likes
the idea, maybe we could do one each year.

I'm thinking the due date will probably be at the end of January, so
everyone has enough time to make their cards, but it would still be
relatively close to New Years. Also, it will most likely be a set of
20.

Anyone interested? Once I get a few replies, I will post a tracker
on Atlas Quest.

Lilac Butterfly
