Early Weekend LB report

From: artistampmi (desmene@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-07-10 00:06:49 UTC
Just returned from a very fine afternoon of box hunting along with my
partner in crime, azobox.

We scoured the meadows, streams & woods of Seidman Park, NE of Grand
Rapids (MI) for the Celtic II series, Rooms in the Park, and Parrots in the Park.
What a beautiful place, and what great stamps!

I'd say that these series, plus the other great Granvilla/Mobiusklein stamps at
Grand River Park, make the GR area a real draw as a daytrip for ambitious
letterboxers.

And if any of the above series names gives you colorful ideas, you wouldn't
be amiss to pack a variety of colored markers in your kit to take full
advantage of the stamps.

Congrats, G & M!
