A big thank-you to Beetle and Lock for another wonderful event! Nam Vet and I arrived around 12:30 and from then until closing time it was a feeding frenzy of stamping. SO many great stamps, not enough time for them all! I think I only managed to snabble a little over half the boxes on the list, grrrr . . .

I can't identify one of the boxes - there were 3 stamps in the box, one was a "P" and one was "Joe Boxer" - anybody know the name of that box (or series) and the carver?

Again - thanks so much, lots of fun, came home with over 40 new stamp images and 10 inky fingers :)

Trail Rider & Nam Vet

Thanks so much to Lock and Beetle for an incredible event! My head is still
spinning from all those amazing stamps ... ! I spent the whole evening in
withdrawal, thinking I had to ink about everything in sight ...

Only a few more questions, not already mentioned (I don't think) ...

- Was Hubert Cumberdale listed?
- How about Heckle and Jeckel?
- Is there a name for the third stamp with Full Metal Alchemist and Widget?
- What are the names of the four other stamps with 'Tribute to my Dad'?
- I also got a lovely stamp-in-progress (cow in a green field) - did it have
a name?

Other than that, I think I hit almost every box. (No wonder my head is
still spinning!) I was bummed to just miss Bartman, Darkwing Duck, and the
second Up Front box. (Garfied and Odie weren't there, right?) Popeye and
friends were fabulous, but I'll have to schedule time to find those in the
future!

Thank you so much to everyone who came and carved! What a fabulous day!

- Archaeofrog
