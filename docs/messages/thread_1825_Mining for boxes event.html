Re: Mining for boxes event

From: (katy@dsa132.org) | Date: 2005-09-16 15:36:09 UTC
Goodness, I hope every one is okay. I'm sorry to hear about the house.

You can definitely still come to the gathering. I mainly wanted to have
people let us know if they were coming so we had a rough idea of how many
to expect - or if we should just cancel the reservation on the pavilion
and stay home. Thankfully, people are coming and the weather forecast
looks decent. Store-bought stamps are fine; I was planning to bring a
few extra stamps and ink pads because I invited some non-experienced
boxers to come and see what it's all about, so don't worry about it.

I was out yesterday planting two boxes and noticed that the pavilion has
bathrooms attached, a playground next to it and a nice fireplace full of
wood. I'm thinking marshmellows...

See you tomorrow!!!

Katy
