Hi BINGO players,

I've added a new database called 2018 Bingo Winners where everyone can see
what games remain. Right now, the two intermediate games left are the
6-pack (a grid of 2x3 or 3x2) and the plus sign. The wildcards don't count
for the intermediate games. Then after that, its just the full card which
does include wild cards. And the first 3 full card places are winners.

So, you know where you stand, here is a pie chart. Start at midnight and
read to the right. The first purple is 4 words around to the second dark
red at at 13 words. The numbers listed are % of boxers with that many
words. About a quarter of you sit at 8 (with the wild card) which is the
median value.
