Since the forecast for the weekend includes warmer temperatures, Coffee
Beans and I are going to start our spring maintenance along the Des
Plaines River Trail. This will include pulling and rewriting a few of
our clues for our Singing and Boxing series. If you have plans to find
them this weekend, let us know ASAP and we can make mutual
plans...otherwise, please look for updated clues (and maybe a few more
box plants) early next week.

questionable box placements by us (B) a box or two being missing and (C)

original 'loop' hike. The straight hike is important because we are
going to link several boxes (including our HHH series, Beautiful Flower,
Future Noble, Squash, and a few new boxes) into one LARGE hike along
the trail.

Hope everyone has a nice weekend!
