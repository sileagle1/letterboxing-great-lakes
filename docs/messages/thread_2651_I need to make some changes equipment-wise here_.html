From: Dick and Anne (rhesse34@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-13 12:02:04 UTC
You don't need to worry about declination unless you're taking a
compass bearing off a topographic map (which is oriented to "true"
north) and need to convert it to magnetic north for the compass.
There's a compass rose on topo maps that shows the difference between
the two, along with the "annual change" (magnetic north is always
moving, you know). It's a fun exercise in defocusing your eyes
and/or making your brain feel like Jello.
Merlin
