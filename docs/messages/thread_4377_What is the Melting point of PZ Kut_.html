From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2007-08-20 05:06:53 UTC
We are going to one of my favorite locals next week . . . Galena, IL.

I will be planting some boxes along the way and hopefully finding
some as well - though Jo Davies County is rather sparse.

One of my boxes I am going to plant will be inside a hotel, the
Stoney Creek Inn and I have been rummaging the schematics of the
place in my mind thinking of where to hide a letterbox.

The pool area is great with a rocky waterfall going into the hot tub.
However, there are "keep off the rocks" signs and a video camera
pointed at the hot tub.

I thought my best bet would be in the sauna behind or under the
heating box. Thus my question, what is the melting point of PZ Kut?

Has anyone tried this, will the first finder find a melted glob of
goo? I'm planning on at least one more box to be hidden in the
breakfast room so I am scouring my mind for other ideas besides the
sauna . . . anyone? Anyone??

Thanks,
