Sounds like you did the right thing. You are supposed to stamp the image of
the HH into the "host" letterbox and stamp the "host" letterbox stamp into the
logbook of the HH.

You also put your personal stamp into both the traditional box and the HH's
logbook.

Technically if you find a HH and it doesn't have the image of the box you
found it in (or its image is not in the logbook of the box you found it in) you
can do that part.

HH's come in many shapes and sizes. Of course the larger the HH the harder
it is to find a new "host" that is large enough to hold it. I've seen several
that are just in a zip lock baggie... I've heard of them being in film
canisters or those little "bubbles" that small trinkits come in at the grocery store
for a few quarters.

I only have one HH out there in the wild and I put it in a small coin purse.
