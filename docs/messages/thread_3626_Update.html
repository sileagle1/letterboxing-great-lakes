Update

From: Irish Bain (irishbain@rocketmail.com) | Date: 2007-01-23 14:39:09 UTC
Because I came home Sunday night to 8 queries and because yesterday
there were 2 more, yes, I made it to Chicago alright.

The drive from Louisville to Urbana was miserable, predictably enough.
I crept out of MoWizLiz's place (thanks Mo!) at 6am and didn't make it
to Urbana until 12:30. I stopped counting the spin-outs/ditched cars
between there and there after I hit 30. Plenty more after, just didn't
count anymore. Two hands on the steering wheel! The freezing rain made
the _most_ interesting icicles running horizontally from my antenna.
I'd never seen that before :)

After the mini-meet broke up, I stopped at Garcia's pizza for the
first time in over 20 years. The place was dead empty. Ohhhh, yeah,
the Bears game. Forgot about that. I made the mistake of driving
through the downtown area after the game and had my van attacked by
college students equipped with snowballs. Halfway home, I realized I'd
forgotten to watch the gas gauge and pulled off road in Kankakee. What
a strange place! I literally had to give up on gas stations and go
into WalMart to find a rest room. Apparently, folks in Kankakee don't
pee.

A careful check of my gear, boxing kit, and car seem to result in
cootie-free success. Thanks, Andrew! I came home with the neatest set
of markers, a new appreciation of culture shock, and a real desire to
get carving again. I met nearly 60 folk! What a blast...

Ish
