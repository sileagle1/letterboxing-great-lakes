From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-09-10 05:28:25 UTC-07:00
I belong to a gym here in town (that would be Cleveland), and each season they have a new "fitness incentive program" that the members can sign up to participate in. It's supposed to be a fun way to get the members involved and at the gym and keep us on track with our fitness goals. For example, this past summer it was a baseball-themed program. They've started announcing the fall program, and its tag line is:

Get a Clue!

Oh, how I laughed when I saw that sign! Alas, it's not letterbox based, rather "solve the mystery at the gym!" based, where every week when you meet your fitness goals you'll get another clue to the mystery you're trying to solve. But surely all that time spent deciphering letterbox clues will give me an edge, right? :)
