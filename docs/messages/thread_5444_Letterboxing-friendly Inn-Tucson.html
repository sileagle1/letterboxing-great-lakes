How great to know that my beloved Tucson (lived there 6 years) is LB friendly. It was a very family friendly great place to have lived when our kids were little. We had a few "rules" to follow though, living in the desert that visitors might want to know if they are not familiar with desert critters:
Never reach where you cannot see;
Turn a light on before getting up in the dark for the bathroom, etc;
Place shoes on a shelf at night;
If you are lifting a rock, tilt it out away from you, not facing you.

We followed these guidelines and never had any serious issues with scorpions or snakes--always respect the native wildlife. We had a great adventure following a rattler along its path one trip to the Chiricahau's (our favorite place). Every now and again it let us know we were too close, but never showed any agression, just "advice".

Enjoy the beauty!!!!! I am really homesick right now.
