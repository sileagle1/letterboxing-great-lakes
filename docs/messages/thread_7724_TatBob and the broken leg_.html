TatBob and the broken leg.

From: tat2bob (tat2bob@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2013-04-06 13:56:34 UTC-05:00
Just wanted to let everyone know how he is doing. Surgery went very well. We have been to 2 post op appointments. The X-rays show the bone is healing very nicely. He is now able to start range of motion physical therapy. Still can not put weight on his leg but just being able to bend it is making a world of difference in his outlook. The nice weather is doing wonders too. He has figured out how to do almost everything, getting on and off the bed and sofa, up and down the stairs, and getting dressed, by himself. He just can’t put his sock and shoe on that side. No driving either. He is still going a bit stir crazy, but doing much better. Thank you for all the great cards, emails and texts. All the good vibes make him feel loved. His next appointment is in a month, I will let ya’ll know how it goes.


Kimberly of
