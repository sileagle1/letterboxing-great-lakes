My goose has been found and rescued!!!

The temps on Sunday were in the 40s, so the larger piles of shoveled snow melted down.
Then, I saw him....my goose! He was laying on his side with his little head poking out of the snow.
Now, my little goose is recovering nicely from the trauma, no broken parts, just a little cold
from being shoveled up and buried in snow for several days.
It's sort of funny how attached you get to something on your porch that is always there to greet
you when you come home, and then suddenly one day, it's gone.

Beetle's goose has been found, but the other question remains...has anyone found Fungus Woman's
pickle? I bet she hid it very well.
