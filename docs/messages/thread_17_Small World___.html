Small World...

From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-03-25 02:59:42 UTC
So I have a fun story to share... This evening I was out for dinner
with a few friends from the place I used to work. One topic led to
another and one of the gals asked if I was still involved with
letterboxing because she had recently read an article that mentioned
geocaching and letterboxing. I told her I was and explained for the
others there what exactly letterboxing was. When I finished, one of
the women excitedly said, "I have seen one of those boxes." I asked
her where and she said "at Crisp Point lighthouse" (on Lake Superior
in the U.P.) I burst out -- "that was my box" and she said "you are
Spring Chicken?"

Anyway, I thought it was uncanny especially since I had just
mentioned here the other day that there are a few boxes in the U.P.
I have not ever heard anything about (that is one of them).
Apparently the box was there last summer, although it was laying out
in the open, not in its hiding spot, and she left it there because
she wasn't really sure where else to put it. So who knows what has
become of it since then.

It really is a small world...
