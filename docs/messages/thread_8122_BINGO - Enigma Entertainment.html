Does my pathetic Bingo card count as embarrassing?

Our most embarrassing moment was a personal embarrassment.  The clues at this park said to follow the trail that went around the lake and started at the end of the parking lot.  We found the trail and started walking.  The clues made it sound like this was going to be a short hike, but almost an hour later, and after several false, "Is this the bridge they're talking about?" or "these must be the steps they call out!" moments, we were too far to turn back around, so we kept going, and realized as we reached the end of the trail and the clues, in reverse, started to fall into place . . . we had started at the wrong end of the trail.  At least we got plenty of exercise that day!
