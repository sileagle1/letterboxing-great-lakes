From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-08 06:27:29 UTC-08:00
My letterboxing goal for 2009 is simple, but yet has managed to elude me despite several trips this past year. In 2009, I want to finally find a letterbox in Pennsylvania!!! I have a good friend from college who lives in PA about 2 and a half hours away from me here in Cleveland, and my aunt also lives about 20 minutes outside of Pittsburgh. Last year I was in PA to visit either my aunt or my friend on several occasions, but Deedle and I have vowed to only search for letterboxes together, and he was only with me on one of those trips, and alas, although the boxes I picked to try to find in downtown Pittsburgh on that trip sounded really, really intriguing, the clues were too baffeling for us to figure out WHERE exactly to look for the box at each location without being completely suspicious, so we had to come home empty-handed. But this year, this year will be the year!

Doodle Bug.
