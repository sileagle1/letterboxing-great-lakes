From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-07-28 18:41:30 UTC-05:00
Funny you should mention that...

Two things about our adventure to get the Tunnel #3 box:

1) We'd already been boxing for a year, so some bushwhacking wasn't a
surprise. However it turned out more challenging because we didn't get near
the box until about 8:30 pm in early July last year. Needless to say, the
light was fading fast. To make matters worse, my arm was in a splint and
sling due to a biking injury and walking on the stone wall made me VERY
nervous with my "bad" arm. (You wouldn't think I would let that stop me from
boxing, do you?) Luckily we had a flashlight for the tunnel, so that helped
us locate the box. However just as we were stamping in, we heard two guys
on bikes approaching below...

2) They stopped just below us, so we had to stay still so we didn't attract
their attention. Instead of proceeding into the tunnel, they stopped and
talked briefly and then one guy started singing very loudly and
enthusiastically, but NOT loud enough to drown out the sound of him peeing
(like FOREVER!). It was hilarious, and we struggled to stay quiet.
Eventually he was drained dry and they entered the tunnel so we could finish
up OUR business of stamping in.

Wisconsin Hiker
