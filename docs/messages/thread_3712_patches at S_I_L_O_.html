Not to be a spoilsport, but remember Safety-Wise and GSUSA policy
will not let you collect money for any other organization. Your
troop can PROMOTE the fundraiser, but cannot actively collect money
for it. They can choose to donate part of their treasury (cookie
sale profits, etc.) to the cause however, and yes, they can wear the
patches on the back of their vests.

(I know, it's stupid. My daughter wanted to do a collection for a
local PD's K9 unit for her Gold Award and was shot down for this very
reason.)

As to the details of getting the patches, I defer to Scoutdogs....

--klk/fw
