Yes, I did not attend the Holiday Survivor Gathering and now I feel like a weenie too.

I was winter camping on Fri. evening through Sun. morning. I was prepared to drive down to Urbana with Fungus Woman. The weathermen were issuing a hazardous driving condition warning. In our area, the snow was melting and then refreezing; black ice conditions perfect for spin outs. There was limited visibility on the roads. We just didn't want to end up in a ditch or one of many fatalities in one of those multi-car pile ups.
So, we let common sense prevail, but common sense isn't fun! From the photos, it looks like we missed a good time.

Beetle
