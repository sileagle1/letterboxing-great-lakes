*** NEW CLUES FOR FISH OUT OF WATER 5: SWINGING BRIDGE ***

Locate the H. Wallace Caldwell Memorial Bridge spanning the
DesPlaines River between downtown Riverside, Illinois, and
unincorporated Riverside Lawn. To find this letterbox, in addition
to your regular supplies, you will need a canoe and paddles. Insert
the canoe into the river and board it. Paddle upstream. At the
point where the DesPlaines joins the Illinois River, take a right.
Continue to paddle. You may need to stop for lunch. And dinner.
And for the night. Or two. Anyway, keep paddling. When you reach
the Mississippi, take a left. Oh, and did I mention to watch for the
box along the way? If you reach the Mississippi delta, you've gone
too far. Maybe.
