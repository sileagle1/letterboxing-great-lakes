We're getting more serious about planning an LB trip for Labor Day
weekend. We plan to take the new ferry across from Milwaukee to
Muskegon and will probably bring our own car since convenient car
rental options don't seem to exist at this time.

We would plan to spend most of our time letterboxing and will
research locations and maps in advance, then probably post something

lot of boxes have been pulled due to the Michigan State Park rules

still in place, are all clues available via LBNA site or are they
published elsewhere?

SpringChick has already provided some info re: hotels and a perch
dinner recommendation (is it offered every night?). I'm interested
in any additional recommendations anyone may have re: places to stay
near a lot of letterboxes and especially places to eat.

We'd love to meet up with anyone in the area while we're there,
perhaps even meet somewhere for dinner one night.

You can contact me off-line if you wish (or post to the group in case
