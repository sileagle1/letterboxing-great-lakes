Well I can totally understand running into a fellow boxer on the
trail but these boxers I ran into at McDonalds! Now I casually
mention what a great day it would be to be out boxing... they smile
politely. Now that makes me wonder if I have mistaken them for
someone else. I have been wrong before ( once or twice ), so I ask
again if the three children with them, whom I am assuming are
grandchildren, have trail names. Trail names, what's that Grandma??
She smiles politely again and informs me that "they" (the children)
don't know what that is!! What a private past time that is not share
with our younger generation!!! How could that be?

As Groovy Girl is off playing on playland and their three no named
kids are as well, CCB slides over the to the booth seat closest to
mine and say's "now who are you?" Well I can't blame him for not
recognizing me from the gathering we just attended together as my
hair is about a foot shorter than it was in the fall. :-)

It was certainly nice to see them both and we chatted a while before
Groovy Girl and I were off to plant "Rabbit Pie". We had the Nature
Area all picked out as rabbits like grassy areas and forests too. We
get there and the park is closed.. for all things, gun deer hunting
season. Seems the area around the preserve has hunters on it! Dang
it! and now we have snow! Dang it again!! So the pie will have to
stay in the oven a few days longer until I get another chance to
plant it.
