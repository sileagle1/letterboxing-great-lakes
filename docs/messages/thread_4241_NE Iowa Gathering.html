From: olsentrio (hlo281@aol.com) | Date: 2008-05-05 18:34:31 UTC
I just wanted to give a great big "thank you" to the Doctors B for an
awesome gathering on Saturday!! It was so well organized, with tons of
amazing boxes, great food, great prizes, and some wonderful bagpipe
music to end our day. The clues were nice and straight to the point,
leading to some of the most beautifully carved stamps I've seen yet.
And to add to my excitement I got my 100th find that day too!

I had a great time seeing familiar faces, meeting some new people and
finally catching some that I always seem to miss. Thanks again for a
job well done.
