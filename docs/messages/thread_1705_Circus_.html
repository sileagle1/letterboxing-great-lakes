Damski i Dzentelmenska!

I have been informed by my colleague Phineas Boch that his famous
circus has recently completed a successful engagement in New York and
will soon be making their way to the great state of Maine.

After their performances in the Northeast, Phineas will allow me to
step into the ring to manage a second Midwest appearance for a select
group of performers. This time the traveling troupe will make their
way to the wilds of Wisconsin. We are hoping that no wild bears will
auditon for us!

Our opening performance will be on October 1 at Menomonee Park and we
expect to remain in the area for two full weeks. A program outlining
the various venues and stages that you will need to search out to
witness these amazing artists will be available at the local
Gathering.

I'm looking forward to the day when I join the performers and also
the day when I will meet you, our esteemed audience!
