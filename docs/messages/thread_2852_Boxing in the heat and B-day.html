From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-07-30 12:23:04 UTC-05:00
There is no rational reason for why letterboxers seek to find letterboxes. The weather is not a factor. If it's hot and humid and the heat index is over 100 degrees, that means you just wait till the sun goes down. It's cooler in the evening and you can go on a night hiking. It's more of a challenge to find a letterbox in the dark, Hey, any box with a hand carved stamp is worth finding any time day or night. You have 7 more boxes just sitting and waiting to be planted? Teaser! That's more boxes than in some of the series of boxes. Don't wait! We look forward to more of your boxes and great stamps!

TOday is Beetle's birthday. How OLD is Beetle? I'm not telling. You'll have to catch me and cut off my head and count the rings (the same way you determine the age of a tree). I invite all my friends in letterboxland to join me in celebrating my birthday. Go out and order a large ice cream sundae, put a candle in it and sing Happy Birthday to Beetle. Remember that calcium replacement therapy is important for bone mass, well, that's how I look at it. Enjoy! Keep Cool!

Beetle
