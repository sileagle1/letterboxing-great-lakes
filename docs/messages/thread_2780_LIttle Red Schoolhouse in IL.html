The kids and I found the box late last summer. We realized the
clues regarding the signposts were no longer accurate since it
looked like new signage was up, but we were able to find it based on
the clues relating to natural landmarks. If we had been brand new
boxers at that point, I'm not sure if we would have had the mindset
to look beyond the (now inaccurate)clues--having some experience
helped us figure out what to do. I emailed the placer and did get 2
responses back. Said they hadn't had a chance to check the box
lately because they were in the midst of plannning their wedding.
Emailed again to say they were updating the clues to say that they
were no longer accurate but it was possible to find the clues
anyway. Hope the box is still there--the logbook had stamps and
comments dating back a number of years.
