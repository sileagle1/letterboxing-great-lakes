I found exactly what I wanted here:
http://puzzlemaker.school.discovery.com/WordSearchWithMessageSetupForm.html
This works great! You use whatever word list you want, tell them
what you want your hidden message to be and they create the puzzle for you.

My only major problem is that I do not know how to get the puzzle to
appear as a grid on LBNA or AQ. :( So, my clue looks ridiculous when
people view it. I uploaded the doc file to this yahoo group and the
letterbox-usa group for people to refer to, but that's annoying.

I wonder if I can take a picture of the printout and upload that to
AQ as a photo clue. I'll have to give that a try!
