From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 10:30:05 UTC-07:00
Has anyone had the issue of discovering that someone else is using their trail name? About a month ago, towards the beginning of July, Deedle & I were out boxing. As we got ready to sign into the log book and were looking at the previous posts, there was someone else signed in as Doodle Bug! I was shocked and didn't know what to do. Since then, I've seen their stamp in 1 or 2 other area boxes. I'm pretty sure I came first... It's kind of like being the only Diane in your school forever, and then another Diane comes along...

But I'm not too terribly worried about it, esp. since our stamps are different and as I've said before, Deedle & I always box together, so our names are always signed in as Doodle & Deedle Bug (so you'll know it was me and not the other one). It just threw me that out of all the trail names in the world, someone else chose mine. Esp. if you know what a REAL doodlebug looks like! (If you want to see a picture, or find out how I got my trail name, visit our website. Oh, you have to hunt around for the link, though.)

Has anyone else discovered that someone else uses their trail name too?

Sign me "The Original Doodle Bug" aka Diane. :)
