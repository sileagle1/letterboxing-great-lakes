What a weekend! A thousand thank yous to our gracious
hosts Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man, GREAT JOB!
everything was wonderful. The location was very nice,
great trails, nice facility. The food was great (even
though I didn't eat much, I didn't want to get slowed
down). Meeting all of you was my favorite though. All
the time I've been letterboxing you are the only
people I've met who are boxers as well, and there are
so many of you! Thank you to SpringChick for carving
the new Great Lakes Group stamp with MINNESOTA!!! on
it. It's funny that I didn't even notice that MN. was
on there until someone said "Hey they added MN." I
can't wait for next year, this was my first gathering,
I suspect the wait for next years gathering will be
even longer. It was very nice to meet all of you, I
look forward to getting to know all of you better and
I look forward to seeing everyone next year. Take care
and box alot this summer, I know I will : )
