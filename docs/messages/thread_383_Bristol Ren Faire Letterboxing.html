Bristol Ren Faire Letterboxing

From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-07 03:12:14 UTC
Yes! It's back!!!!

There WILL be letterboxing at the Bristol Ren Faire starting this
Saturday (when the faire opens)!

We have some returning from last year and some new ones for your
letterboxing enjoyment! Since all of the clues have changed (even
though some stamps have not) we are declaring that any of the stamps
you get this year will be considered new finds, even if you got the
stamp last year!

The new clues will be posted a little later this week (before
Saturday), but here's a taste of what we have for you this year:

"LETTERPEOPLE"
Eric Grainger (was The Crier Stamp in 2003) (Available all faire days.)
St. Brigid's Cross(was The Sweet Scent of Bristol Stamp in 2003)
(Available all faire days.)
??Sleeping Beauty?? (was The Tintinnabulation Stamp in 2003 but may
have a new/different stamp this year) (Available all faire days.)
Letters, Letters, Everywhere (aka The Kilt-a-fied Stamp in 2003)
(This letterbox is available on Sundays only.)
The Knight of Swords (same as 2003) (Available all faire days)

"LETTERBOXES" (Altiod Tins - stamp and small logbook only)
The Great Food of Bristol Series (NEW!!)
Garlic Mushrooms
Pippins
Turkey Legs
Ale
Spicy XDXrXaXgXoXnX CHICKEN Wings

& The Letterboxing Bristol Ren Faire (LBBRF) stamp

Sorry, the following stamps are not available this year:

The Elusive Security Stamp (she quit)
The Thomas Cecil Stamp (he married and moved on)
The Hard Core Trad Stamp (Rua's in Ireland playing her many instruments)
The Gamemaster Stamp (??????)
Please No Rats in the Soup Box (she might actually be at the Dirty
Duck still - we don't know, this was an independant letterboxer)

Bristol gave their EMPHATIC approval of this activity, including the
new mircoboxes. The microboxes will be easy to find, but might not be
easy to sneak away! I suggest you start working on your "slight of
hand" techniques if you want these boxes!

Enjoy!
