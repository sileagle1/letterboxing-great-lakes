From: brat_pack07 (kevjean@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-16 20:02:36 UTC
Well, my parents, my kids and I went out in search for more letterboxes
as usual. We must had luck on our side, we found 2 geocache boxes
before we found the boxes we needed. LOL We rehid them, and laughed,
and soon found the box we needed. We also went on one box hunt, and
then went to do another, and found out it was on the other end of the
same path we were on!!!! We laughed even harder cause we didn't know
that when we started!!! We ended at the Chocolate Milk box, which was
worth stopping!!! =) We didn't realize until we got home that we found
8 boxes!!!

I hear alot of concerns in hiding the boxes, and we've been teaching
our kids since the day we started to make sure it's back in the same
spot and covered well. One of us goes with them to hide it, and we do
our best to "clean up" any boxes we found that were wet, or was missing
a bag. We were lucky to run into another couple letterboxing, Wormy
Wanderers, who gave us a run down of the dos and don'ts of letterboxing
and even showed the kids on how to hide the boxes as well. They were
very helpful and we've enjoyed letterboxing every weekend since then.
We're hoping to plant some boxes soon as well, but we're doing alot of
research and scoping out potential areas, and making sure we clues in
order.
