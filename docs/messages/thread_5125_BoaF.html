From: Lock, Shock & Barrel (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-23 23:20:45 UTC
Lock is typically our "post-er" here but, seeing as he (with Barrel)
headed off to scout camp EARLY Sunday morning for a week as a leader
for Barrel's troop, it seems fitting I step in sooner rather than later
to express our thanks, too, to the whole crew, from the planners and
families who organized this event with finesse, to all those who carved
such great stamps to be found, to everyone who came to find the great
boxes. As usual, we were awed and amazed by the creativity in this
group, both in carving and hiding, and are ever inspired to see how we
might help to push the envelope of this already creative hobby. We met
nowhere near everyone out there that day and look forward to yet
another event when we have a chance to meet more of you all! Thanks
for yet another great LB memory!

Shock (of Lock, Shock, and Barrel)
