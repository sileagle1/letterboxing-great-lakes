Ott-Lites at Wal-Mart

From: Lock, Shock & Barrel (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-15 22:48:47 UTC
Just a random post, really, but we were out at Wal-Mart in Gurnee, IL
earlier today, and found several Ott-Lites in the clearance aisle.
We've been using an Ott-Lite recently to provide better illumination
when carving stamps, and it makes a huge difference. (I'm told that
they are useful for other crafts as well...)

Anyway, we were surprised to see this brand, which is usually a bit on
the expensive side, at Wal-Mart for half price. We're not sure if it's
particular to Gurnee, but we thought we'd mention it, just in case Wal-
Mart is phasing Ott-Lite out as a vendor across all of its stores. If
so, then they might be on clearance elsewhere across the U.S.
