I was out today to check on two of my boxes before they are under snow. Hoosier Poet is
doing quite well! I could see the hostel exposed and so I had to recover it with more
leaves and sticks. I think the weather disturbed it more than letterboxers.

I promised some pictures ... couldn't retrieve them as fast as I might ... so I took my new
digital camera with me and I have some good shots for the PHOTO files to the left. Give
me a couple of moments to figure it out and let me know what you think, especially those
of you considering creating one. I will say I wish I had used better paper stock on the
pages as the ink will show through if any moisture is present. Otherwise I am pretty
happy with the present condition of the box. There are FIVE hitchhikers inside and I am
considering asking anyone to take them away when they visit. It seems like asking for an
even exchange does not help the hitchhikers circulate as much as they should. I'll update
the clues when I decide what to do next.

I tried to make the pictures as small as possible before download. I still have to read the
instruction book I have.
