The Twins Trailnames

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2011-05-16 05:13:03 UTC
Ok, so the twins passed their 5th birthday back in January and I still never made their trailnames "official" by announcing them.

I never thought this would be so hard. But with the older girls, I wanted their trailnames to be nicknames I use for them and hold a more sentimental meaning for me.

I had thought of calling Maddie "Skipper" and Will "Champ", but neither nickname comes naturally for me to say. I still call Katie "Skeeter" and Alex "Bug-a-Boo" and interchange their trailname with thier real name in every day conversation.

Sometime ago the girls asked if they could be called "The Wonder Twins" after some show. Skeeter has been inundating me with different Trailname idea's . . . but in the end it came down to what my little pet-name is for them.

So, their official trailnames are; The Wonder Twins: Mad-Mad & Bud-Bud. Not really original, but meaningful to me.

So, on behalf of Mad Mad & Bud Bud this is hx6 signing out.
