I missed the Gathering, but was in MI last weekend. It was fun
following some of my fellow Wisconsinites back home (the rest of the M
Clan was absent). Saw you guys and Dragon repeatedly in the Lansing
area, Kalamazoo, and in IN. Perhaps a series along the WI <--> MI

nice.

with this, but a strictly "I-94 Series" could be fun and convenient.

I'd be willing to help coordinate this. Let me know if anyone's
interested. Or let a newbie like me know if it's already out there.

M Clan
