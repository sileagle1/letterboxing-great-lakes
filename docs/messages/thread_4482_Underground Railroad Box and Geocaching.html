From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-10-03 20:05:11 UTC
After 9 or so months to trying, we finally have one of our
Underground RR boxes placed in the Dupage County Forest Preserves
(Graue Mill). The ranger was VERY nice to work with, but we kept
running into snags. The first site we found was too near some
geocaches. The next site ended up being in a restricted area. Then
our schedules kept conflicting--but it finally has happened. Clues
will go up later today on LbNA and AQ.

I do have one question. As I was leaving, the ranger said something
like "this will be up on the web site by Friday--the guy in charge of
geocaching will get it online". ??????????? I couldn't stay and
talk with him because I needed to get to a funeral, so I need to
email him about this. Does anyone know what website he is talking
about or if this is standard procedure for Dupage County? The clues
that I gave the ranger so that he could find the proposed site were
very simple and direct--I don't want those on a website. I like to
write my clues a little harder and with compass settings, so that
they don't disappear right away. Plus, if it is a list of geocaches--
I don't want my stamp getting traded out.
