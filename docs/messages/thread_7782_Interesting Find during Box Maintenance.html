Interesting Find during Box Maintenance

From: wormywanderers (lewwdawg_840@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-05-27 20:19:17 UTC
So we were out checking on a couple of our boxes today and inside our Adult Swim: Harvey Birdman box was a baggie marked "2013" with a handwritten note inside. Here is what the note said (including misspellings, etc.):

-------------------------------------------------------------------

-to dead boys -

Ladies and gentleman, skinny and stout.
i'll tell you a tale I know nothing about;
the admission is free, so pay at the door,
now pull up a chair, and sit on the floor

One bright day in the middle of night,
two dead boys got up to fight;
back to back they faced each other,
drew their swords and shot each other

A blind man came to watch fair play
a mute man come to shout "horray!"
a deaf man police heard the noise
and came to stop these two dead boys.

he lived on the corner in the middle of the block,
in a two story house on a vacant lot;
a man with no legs came walking by
and kicked the lawman in his theigh

he crashed through a wall without making a sound,
into a dry creek bed and suddenly drowned
the long black hearse came to cart him away
but he ran for his life and is still gone today.

I watched from the corner of the big round table,
the only eyewitness to facts my fable.
but if you doubt my lies are true,
just ask the blind man, he saw it too

-poem origananlly written bye Tyler Rager

------------------------------------------------------------------

Underneath the above poem, there are several signatures (can't really make any full names out), a drawing of an Easter egg in grass with flowers and the sun shining down and a "Happy Easter 2013".

I just wanted to share our interesting find with the group and to see if anyone knows anything about this poem that was left behind in our box. My first instinct was that it just HAD to be a letterbox clue so I did a little online research. All I could really find is that it is an old nursery rhyme . . .

Julie (Wormy Wanderers)

And googling Tyler Rager, I found this from back in 2010 -- hopefully it's not the same Tyler Rager that the letterboxer was quoting : 

Police have arrested five suspects in connection with a robbery and assault at a Richland Township motel.
The attack happened early Thursday morning at the Super 8 Motel.
Two men staying at the motel were severely beaten with a sawed-off shotgun.
Police said the victims met two women at a bar and invited them back to their motel room. One of the women let four men into the room. They beat the two victims so badly that room was covered in blood.
The attackers stole $200, a credit card, and a cell phone.
Five suspects were arraigned overnight Thursday. They were Daphne Parker, Jessica Maines, Grover Christopher Mitchell, Angel Rivera and Jason Carrasquillo.
Police are still looking for one suspect, Tyler Rager. Police said he is considered dangerous and could have access to weapons. Anyone with information is asked to contact Richland Township police at 814-266-8333.
One police officer said this one of the most violent attacks he'd ever seen.
"This type of crime, this type of violence is going to happen. I don't care if it's a residence or a hotel. The police in a united effort, just like in this case, we're going to come and find you and we're going to put you in jail," said Richland Township police Detective Tom Keirn.
Stay with WJAC-TV News for the latest on this developing story.
