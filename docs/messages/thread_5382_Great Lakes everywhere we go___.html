As with most of our trips, we encountered reminders or connections to
the Great Lakes region. Tonight we met a few local boxers in South
Carolina. One was from Georgia and she mentioned meeting a couple from
IL this past summer while vacationing in the Seattle area. We guessed
right on our first try - Cherokee Rose 2 and Hawkeye! Ghopper said
that they enjoyed drinking wine and hiking with the IL duo. We also
saw a fantastic stamp that The Red Cat had sent to Holly of the Talley
Valley Farm crew.

Enjoying the weather down here - it was low 70s today. Wishing we
could avoid that WI winter that is on its way.... but unfortunately
we'll be heading back home next weekend.
