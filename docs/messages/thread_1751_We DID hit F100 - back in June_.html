We DID hit F100 - back in June!

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-22 20:58:12 UTC-07:00
Ok, so I admit, I'm a little bit behind in the
letterboxing organization realm such as recording how
many finds we had at the gathering, but these things
like work and planning a wedding keep getting in the
way! So finally, 2 months later, I sat down tonight
to sort through all the wonderful images and try to
remember when we found what, because we also did some
boxing en route to and from the gathering, and then we
found a few boxes around Port Clinton at the end of
June....and we are officially at F101. Those 2 boxes
we found at Port Clinton were finds 100 & 101. We
missed out on doing the happy happy joy joy dance!
But maybe that's for the best, because we were
letterboxing around Port Clinton that weekend while we
waited for my friend to finish that day's leg of the
MS 150 bike ride, and we had her
never-heard-of-letterboxing-before boyfriend with us,
and us jumping up and down because it was find 100
might have been too much for him :)

But, this means all you who'll be at the Lake Eeeerie
Gathering in October - look for a new signature stamp
from us!

Doodle (and Deedle) Bug, who will officially begin
using our new stamp at Geneva-on-the-Lake this weekend.
