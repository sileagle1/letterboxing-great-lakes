Wonderland Temporary Boxes, etc.

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-07-07 02:11:09 UTC
Hi!

Just wanted to let you know that we pulled some remaining temporary
boxes tonight - I'm Late, I'm Late!!, Pack of Cards, and The Rose
Gardeners are no longer available. We'll be mailing them back to
their creators within the next few days.

While we were out on the trails, we checked a few other boxes that we
passed along the way. All of them were in good shape:

Alice In Weatherland
Enormous Puppy
The Gryphon Letterbox
Wasp in a Wig #3
White Rabbit #2
Why the Rabbit Was Late
===========================

We had fun the last 5 days boxing in the eastern half of Michigan's
UP (Upper Peninsula). We saw that J&B Books had stamped into many of
the Mackinac Island boxes a week or so before us and we also stamped
in a day or so different than Atom118 and CoffeeBeans, so they were
obviously enjoying the same area. Once or twice we stamped into a
box on the same day as someone else, but we never ran into any other
boxers on our trip. We did see a lot of stamps by people that we
didn't recognize. They must not be a member of this list, or perhaps
they are, but don't post much. Saw lots of great lighthouses,
waterfalls, rock formations and scenic shorelines during our visit.
In addition to hiking on a few trails, we also logged ~34 miles of
biking on Mackinac Island and Grand Island. In our box hunts we got
to see some great carvings by SpringChick, NorseGnome and TJ Mich!

One other item of note was seeing Dragon's entries as he logged his
99th and 100th finds in 2003!

Wisconsin Hiker

too. :)

Glad you enjoyed your trip to our native peninsula! We wanted to
say a public 'Thank You' for finding our "Old Well" box near
Manistique. We'd stopped by in May to check on that one, and

minutes.

A few folks had e-mailed us to ask about that box, so we're happy to
announce that the listing has now been 'unarchived' and is active
again... especially since it was the first box stamp T ever carved!
