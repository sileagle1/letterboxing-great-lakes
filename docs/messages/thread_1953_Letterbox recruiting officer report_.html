From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-11-03 23:35:17 UTC-06:00
FungusWoman reporting back from North Carolina, where a successful mission
to place new letterbox was completed. Unfortunately, mission to find three
only accomplished one find. However, I succeeded in possibly recruiting
several new operatives. Entire training class was informed of stealth
mission. Two were convinced to join me in placing my new box,
"Bee-O-Marry-You" (clues to follow shortly). Several took down the website,
including the instructor of my class (to whom adoption rights may be
passed), and a local coworker whose home base is right here in the 'burbs of
Milwaukee. (Say hello to Dave, everyone - I'm going to be forwarding the
digest containing this message to him!)



In other recruiting news, Boxer-in-TRAINing has himself been passing the
word, and has piqued the interest of my cousins in Texas. Can someone tell
me the name of the regional group for that part of the country so I can pass
the word to him?



--klk/FungusWoman
