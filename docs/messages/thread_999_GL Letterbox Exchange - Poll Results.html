From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-01-31 10:40:45 UTC

Okay, the votes are in and the poll is closed on a theme for the
letterbox exchange project, but we don't have a winner -- not yet
anyway.

It was a tie between Weird U.S. and Hometown Landmarks. I have set
up a run-off poll with just these two themes. Everyone gets one
vote and the poll will close on Wednesday at midnight.

Thanks everyone who suggested themes and to those who cast their
vote in the original poll. I am looking forward to getting this
thing off the ground!

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-01-31 12:29:31 UTC-05:00
That is a good idea and I did consider that before posting the 2nd poll.
But for ease in naming and identifying the series, I decided to stick with
the original plan of having one central theme. I would hope that nobody is
stuck enough in one theme that they wouldn't not play because the other was
chosen, but that is each person's choice. Actually the two that are up are
close enough to one another that if you really wanted to do the one and the
other was chosen, you could probably make it work -- after all, most of the
"Weird U.S." type things could be considered local landmarks, and many
landmarks could loosely fit into the "weird" category.
