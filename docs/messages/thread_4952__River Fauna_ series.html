I checked my "River Fauna" letterboxes this afternoon, not too worried as I knew they had all been found in late March. But much to my chagrin, I found that Box #1 has been washed away by the recent high water in the Kishwaukee River after all the recent rain. I was unable to check the status of Box #2 as I didn't care to wade through standing water. However, Boxes #3, #4, and #5 (with the logbook) are safe.

I will replace Box #1 in a HIGHER spot soon, and #2 if need be. In the meantime, if anyone has printed up the cluesheet for this series, be aware it is incomplete at this time.
