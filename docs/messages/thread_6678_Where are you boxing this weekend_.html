From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-07-01 12:42:26 UTC-05:00
Well, as at least a couple of you already know, we are heading east for
the long holiday weekend to hang out and do some letterboxing in the
Cleveland, OH and Erie, PA areas. We figured that this might be one of
the last weekends that we'll have to just get away for awhile, so we'd
better take advantage of it. Added bonus: NO FAMILY out that direction
to distract us from boxing. ;)

And since one of our letterboxing goals of the year was to add 2 new
states to the list of where we've boxed, we decided that PA was going to
be closest one to drive to -- we already added MO as our other new
boxing state this year back in March. So hopefully after this weekend,
we'll have found letterboxes in 18 different states together -- CA, IA,
IL, IN, KY, MA, ME, MI, MN, MO, NH, OH, OR, PA, VA, VT, WA, and WI if
anyone's interested. I've also found boxes in two other states (CT and
FL) but those were without Atom so they don't "count." :P

Hope everyone enjoys the weekend and has a happy 4th!
Coffee Beans (of Atomic Beans)
