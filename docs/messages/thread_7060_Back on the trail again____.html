Well, after a long absence, the CignaCats are heading back on the trail again. It's been 5 years! Two kids later, our oldest is now 5 and the youngest is 2. They are now finally old enough to keep focused (the older) and to not have to drag along a stroller (the younger). After a beautiful Sunday afternoon and reminiscing about past adventures, I dusted off our old supplies and am contemplating a box or two next weekend.

We are now located in West Bend, Wisconsin (our old stomping grounds were Waukesha, WI). Do any of the locals have suggestions for a few easy finds that we could attempt next weekend? Just a box or two to dip our toes back in the water?

Happy to see lots of old friends still on this group. See you on the trails!

The CignaCats
