From: Laurie Marselle (ltmarselle@msn.com) | Date: 2010-06-02 12:23:20 UTC-05:00

The route listed below is the one we use from Princeton. Another option if you prefer interstate driving all the way is take I-55 South to Bloomington IL and get on I-74 West. The park is right off of Route 150 and very close to I-74.



There are a few entrances to the park, but the easiest for our locations (camping/our shelter) is to enter by the Historic site - turn north off of Rt. 150 onto Jubilee-Princeville Rd. after a short ways you'll see a sign for the Jubilee College historic Site. Turn left west here to enter site. At the end of the road is a gate blocking enterance to the site. Turn right here and enter the park. Follow the road around and you'll see a signs for Quail Meadow Shelter (turn left/then right). To reach the camping host area to check in to camp, don't turn towards the shelters, just continue on and you'll come upon the camping area. There is a site map of the park in the files section of this group under Gatherings/GLG 2010.



I am still trying to find out what the check in/check out times for the campground are. Stay Tuned!
