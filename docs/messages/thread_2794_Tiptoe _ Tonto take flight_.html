Tiptoe & Tonto take flight!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-20 13:20:02 UTC
Although the authenticity of the article is dubious, a copy of the
Windy City Trib Travel section received by the Travel Team shows
Tiptoe and Tonto celebrating their "Around the World in 80 Days"
success aboard a hot air balloon!

Sure enough, the exited couple is pictured reveling in their
achievement of completing the 80 Days adventure within the requisite
time to gain the bonus stamp in addition to the "World Traveler" box.

Insider reports to the Travel Team also reveal that the Illinois
letterboxers logged more than 800 miles of mostly Michigan driving
over the last couple of months. As with many letterboxing couples, one
of the two exhibited tremendous patience through this adventure.

Congratulations to Tiptoe and Tonto!
