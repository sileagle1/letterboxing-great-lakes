On the poison ivy subject...Jewel weed is a plant that contains a
natural antidote to poison ivy, and is often growing nearby (or at
least in wetter areas). Also known as spotted touch-me-not, if you
rub the leaves of jewel weed on skin that has been exposed to poison
ivy, you improve your chances of avoiding a rash (or so I've heard -
I don't think I'm allergic to poison ivy, and I've definitely hiked
my way through it often enough). Jewel weed is a shrubby-type of
plant with lovely, small orange flowers (which bear some spots).
