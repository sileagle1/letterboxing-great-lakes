Florida

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2009-04-02 14:56:10 UTC
Ball Magnet and I made a short trip down to Fort Meyers--got back to the Midwest just in time for the sloppy snow. Just as I feared, the "Wild Pigs Run Amuck" have run away for good--or been buried under the new Visitor's Center (which is very nice).

Also made it out to "Sandy Dollar", which was long overdue for a new logbook as evidenced by all the scraps of paper in the box. Did see one Great Lakes boxer in there (the stamp, not the boxer LOL)--Bali Girl. Was surprised that so many folks had found the box, since the park had redone some of their trails since we were last there and the clues didn't totally fit. Guess it helped that the box is at the base of a cell phone tower so people could just head that way. There was a big osprey nest on top of the tower--a birdwatching bonus. Going to Florida really helps boost the birdwatching numbers. I've seen 63 species so far this year, and spring migration really hasn't even started yet!
