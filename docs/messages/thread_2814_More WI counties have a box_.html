More WI counties have a box!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-07-25 01:44:37 UTC
I'm very happy to announce that we are now 2/3 of the way to having a
box in every WI county thanks to recent placements by the following:

The Fantastic Five planted "Shannon The Dragonfly" in Vilas county

Atom 118 planted "Two for Three" in Lincoln county

Meandering Melee planted "Rainbow Falls" in Menominee county

48 WI counties now have a box, and 24 counties are waiting for a box.
I think aimlesst is planning to put one in Grant county in the near
future and we will try to plant some in the middle of the state if
they still need a box when we head up for a hiking weekend in SEP.

Both Martini Man & I have already each had 2 finders for the boxes we
posted earlier this month in formerly "boxless" counties (Waupaca and
Oconto). We were happy to see that there was quick interest in these
areas, even though we thought it might be quite a while before they
were found.

Here's a link to an updated map showing the "boxless" counties, in
case you might want to help the cause by planting a box:

http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

Wisconsin Hiker
