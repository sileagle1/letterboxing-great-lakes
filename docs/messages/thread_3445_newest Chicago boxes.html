From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-12 12:05:18 UTC-06:00
Hi everyone,

For those of you who missed the St. Nick's Gathering, you can have a
second chance at my box "The December Dilemma" as well as a modified
version of the anchor stamp to Atom's "Holiday Exchange HH Hostel" in
north Chicago. As an added bonus, we also planted an additional series
entitled "Festival of Lights" with stamps carved by Atom in the same
preserve. These boxes will be available for a limited time -- probably
until January 14th, depending on weather and our schedules.

Also planted this weekend was a replacement box to Atom's "Memories of
the White City" series in south Chicago. One box in the series went
missing around August/Sept, and the other 2 were retired due to downed
trees from a storm this fall. Anyway, this is a new stamp with updated
clues, so even if you found the series before, feel free to come back
again. :)

And with these recent plants, I'm happy to report we made it above
PFX500! Specifically P40, F389, X72... but who's counting? ;)

Enjoy the boxes and happy holidays!
