GL Letterbox Exchange

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-02-23 10:57:11 UTC

For those who are participating in the Letterbox Exchange, e-mails
went out last night with information as to who you are sending your
box to.

There are 24 participants and I am excited by the cool series
of "Hometown Landmarks" this project will produce. Each participant
will be putting together a complete, ready-to-plant letterbox --
hand-carved stamp, logbook and any inclusions you customarily put
into a letterbox. For further project guidelines and deadlines,
please refer to the file in the FILES section of this list
(FILES/GROUP PROJECTS...). If you have additional questions, feel
free to contact me.

Since the identities of who each person will be receiving a box from
are unknown, I have not posted a list of participants, although once
the project is done I would like to put together a comprehensive
list of everyone who participated and the "landmark" they chose -- I
will be contacting participants directly regarding this.

IMPORTANT: If you think you are participating but did not receive
an e-mail telling you who you are to send your box to, please
contact me offlist as soon as possible.

And, just a reminder... please do not share the real name, address
or e-mail address of the person you are sending to.

Thanks to everyone who suggested theme ideas and all who were able
to participate!

SpringChick
