You know you are into letterboxing when....

From: nojobro (nolarc@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-07-09 14:10:48 UTC
...you know you will be eaten alive by mosquitoes if you venture into the forest, but you go in anyway, because you think you will be the first person to find a letterbox...

80 bug bites later...I was the first person to find two boxes!

There are a couple more boxes at the site...I'll go back, but only with bug dope in hand. ;-)

My right calf is the worst. I think there's a diagram of a constellation on there. I was seriously regretting the decision to make my personal stamp a multi-colored one. It also has two parts...in the second box, I only stamped one part. LOL. I even ate a bug. Now *that's* dedication!

Nugget


RE: [LbGLK] You know you are into letterboxing when....

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-07-09 11:03:51 UTC-05:00

I recommend Repel 100% DEET. I have a small pump bottle in my bag. The mosquitoes must really hate this full strength stuff. A little spray here, there, and eerywhere, usually on my clothes more than directly on my skin, and those human eating pests don't even fly around me. But I need to be more careful thrashing around in bushes; my arms are all srapped up and I think I might have an allergy to Junipers. Long sleeves would help, but it's soooo hot!



Don't let 80 bugs stop you....have fun letterboxing!
