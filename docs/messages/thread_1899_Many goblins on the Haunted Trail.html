Many goblins on the Haunted Trail

From: pgranola2002 (p2jd@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-10-17 02:49:14 UTC
Today was a wonderful boxing day. Caliope and I took Jobadoba out to
the Haunted Trail Series in Northern Illinois. It was spooktacular!
C'Moon, Kookaburra and squeak did a horrifyingly wonderful job. We
found all the boxes and even had time to pick up 67 squirrels and oh
deer I can't sleep. What makes it even better was meeting other scary
people on the trail. I have never met anyone (other than gatherings)
before while out boxing and today we met two gruesome groups! First
we met the terrifying twosome of Tiptoe and Tonto and they were
traveling with thier new friend the rabid Prairie Dog. What fun to
exchange stamps and talk with some familiar names in person. Later we
saw some wicked Waltzing Pigs slinking through the woods and thought
we should investigate. We exchanged stamps with Mom and Dad guinea pig
and the piglets too!
Wow! Great weather, a proponderance of boxes and a few new evil fiends.

How could it get better? P.Granola
