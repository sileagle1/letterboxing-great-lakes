Bad News...Good News

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-04-17 03:38:22 UTC
BAD NEWS is that we had made plans to spend last night in Sheffield, IL for today's Munchkinland Event that had to be canceled due to weather. GOOD NEWS is that we had a great dinner, dreamy night's sleep and good conversation with other guests at the great B&B that we stay at when we go out that way.

BAD NEWS is that there was a drizzly rain and then lightening storms most of the day and evening yesterday. GOOD NEWS is that we got more done before leaving home than we had expected, and took our time stopping at a few cute shops along the way. One had greeting cards that, although a "bit" off color, had us crying and holding our sides in laughter...which by the way is good for the soul! Especially for two old souls that are dripping wet standing in Beetz Me!

BAD NEWS is it was still raining when we got up this morning and now added to the mix were powerful winds to match. GOOD NEWS is that we had that extra cup of coffee before heading out and snagging seven other drive-by letterboxes before the rain...Oh wait...make that horizontal blowing SNOW stopped!

BAD NEWS is that the rain stopped, but the wind gusts became so strong and biting that we only managed to get five more OZ boxes before we had to call it quits. GOOD NEWS is that we only have 19 more OZ boxes to go....and this time around we certainly weren't bothered by mosquitoes...or heat exhaustion!

BAD NEWS is that we did stop by the shelter just to see if any other crazy letterboxers were there to meet for the "group picture" of the canceled event hehehehe....Nope...not a single person in site! GOOD NEWS is that the only people we saw ALL day were two fisherman ...no muggles to worry about!

BAD NEWS is that we didn't have the potluck at Munchkinland. GOOD NEWS is that we had great leftovers and CHOCOLATE to snack on in the car while the sleet pelted the windshield. We then enjoyed a nice dinner on the way back home at Starved Rock Lodge. A perfect place to traipse in with muddy, wet clothes from our adventurous day!

BAD NEWS is that we had to head back home just as the sun broke through over the Illinois river after dinner. GOOD NEWS is we get to look forward to another OZ adventure later on this summer or fall!

BTW, we added a few pictures in the GLK photo file (4 pages back in TnT's Excellent Adventure file...wait 'till you see the waves!
Tiptoe & Tonto
