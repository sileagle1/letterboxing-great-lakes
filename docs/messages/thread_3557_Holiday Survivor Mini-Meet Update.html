Holiday Survivor Mini-Meet Update

From: lcoil79 (lcoil79@insightbb.com) | Date: 2007-01-11 02:23:50 UTC
Well, here's an update as to the current plans for our little
Mini-Meet on the 21st.

- Unveiling of our exciting event stamp

- Gabe wants to try and put on a small workshop about making logbooks
(Monkey Journals courtesy GLG 2005)

- Fungus Woman has offered to bring some event stamps from the Pirates
from Every Port gathering, some Oz stamps, as well as some egyptian
stamps her father had carved.

- A raffle to benefit the LBNA website with currently the following
prizes:
* Set of LePlume II Brush Markers
* 3 Great Lakes Regional patches
* Telescopic walking stick (courtesy of MRSHAT)
* Centrifuge tubes [great for microboxes] (courtesy of Katy)

- We also hope to have demonstrations of technology/equipment that
people use while "on the hunt". In addition to this, we hope to have
people bring along boxes/stamps in progress to give examples to new
boxers and maybe share techniques.

We've currently got 19 people signed up over at Atlas Quest, but I've
heard of at least a few others who still plan on coming, so as of now
we're expecting around 25-ish (hopefully more). We are also planning
on laying out the initial plans for an annual IL gathering and are
encouraging any IL boxers to show up to help with this. We hope to
plan/host this first one, then try to set it up to rotate through the
state and hit a new location every year.

Again, I ask anyone planning on coming to head on over to Atlas Quest
and sign-up for the event, that way we can keep an accurate count.
