Spring Chick, you are so wise!

Your three tips make a lot of sense and leave all of us with much food for thought. Sometimes it's easy to blame others, but these three recommendations would certainly minimize lost boxes. Thank you to new letterboxers for joining us and reminding us of that enthusiastic thrill that we all felt when we began to find letterboxes.

So in general, my feelings about improving the odds for your box to survive:

1) Make the clues a little more difficult than the standard 1-2-3 follow the directions variety
2) Put some distance between the parking lot and your letterbox
3) Be really, really picky about the hiding spot

(did I mention that you need to be really picky about the hiding spot?)
