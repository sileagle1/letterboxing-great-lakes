Haunted HH Series

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-10-17 14:29:07 UTC

Some of you may have caught the earlier message referencing the
Haunted HH Series. I deleted the message -- let me explain...

The Haunted HH Series is a series of 10 Halloween-themed HH that I
have planted and/or sent to other parts of the country to be planted
in boxes there.

The log book of each HH contains a URL specific to that HH. Each
URL provides a clue. Together all 10 clues will lead you to the
Haunted House Letterbox, which is an unposted permanently planted
letterbox.

You may be thinking -- what are the chances that I'll find all 10 HH
to get all 10 clues? While this is probably unlikely, especially
given the vanishing nature of HH, I would think that it would be
possible to figure out the Haunted House letterbox without actually
having all 10 clues, just a little more trial and error involved.

While I cannot control what people do privately as far as sharing
clues with one another any more than I can control group
collaboration on mystery boxes, I do ask that the URL's specific to
each Haunted HH not be posted to any public forum. Also, feel free
to trade HH with other boxes if they have one you are looking for.
Posting your HH finds to the letterbox travellers talklist will help
people to know where these little goblins have recently been spotted.

The first couple of Haunted HH were found yesterday at the Sweetest
Day mini-meet and the others are on the way to their destinations.
The clues are not available at the URL's provided in the log books
until October 31. So if you were the lucky finder of one of these
HH yesterday, write the URL down and move the HH along for somebody
else to find.

Here is the main info page for the series:

http://www.confessionsofdecember.com/lbna/series/hhh_main.asp

SpringChick
