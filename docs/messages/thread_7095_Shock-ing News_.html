Shock-ing News!

From: placedenfert1 (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-04-30 00:44:19 UTC
Actually, it's not shocking at all, but I wanted to let everyone in the group know about some exciting news in the Lock, Shock and Barrel household...

Nicole (aka Shock) has been working toward ordination as a minister in the Presbyterian Church, beginning with her decision to attend seminary in the fall of 2004. During the last year in particular, she's been meeting with several churches about ordained positions that were open, looking for that right mutual fit that would result in a call, or as we secular folk would say, a job offer.

Recently, we got word that Nicole has in fact received a call from First Presbyterian Church of Waukesha. She'll be their new pastor, beginning effective June 1. And she'll be officially ordained as a minister during a special ceremony on Sunday, June 5.

So it's an exciting, busy time in our house. But Nicole asked me to pass along the news to the group, and to extend an invitation to any and all who'd like to share in the ordination ceremony. The details are as follows:

When: Sunday, June 5 at 5:00 PM
Where: Wildwood Presbyterian Church, 18630 West Old Gages Lake Road, Grayslake, IL 60030

And to those of you who've offered your support and encouragement over the last few years, Nicole asked me to extend her sincere thanks. It's been a long road to get here, and now she finds herself at the start of a new journey on a new path.

(Kind of sounds like a good letterbox hunt, eh?) :)

Sincerely,

LSB
