From: Katrina Lustofin (drkaty@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-02 21:09:07 UTC-05:00
Don't feel silly about asking - it would be far sillier to continue to be
confused or uncertain.

Yes, once some one has won one of the interim games, no one else can win
that same way. For the big game (crossing off all 25 squares), however,
there will be three ranked winners (1st. 2nd, and 3rd). You can win more
than one game - say four corners plus the X - or none, but the fun is in the
playing, and in reading all the comments people make about the daily bingo
call.

Katy
