First Finder virgins no longer! (MI)

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-27 16:40:16 UTC
Thanks to Kara's Music in Motion series, Bindle Bum and I are no
longer "first finder" virgins. I'm sure the irony of it having
occured on the same day as our wedding anniversary will always bring
a smile when we think of our first time as first finders.

What a neat trail ... almost felt as if I had stepped into the
Jurasic Park movie set a couple of times. I found the biggest
feather I've ever found in my life. Not being a "birder" I wasn't
sure what kind of bird it came from. Of course I had visions of it
belonging to something really cool, like an eagle. Bindle Bum gently
reminded me that he thought it was unlawful to possess feathers from
the Bald Eagle. Oh.

We had to stop at the hardware store, so I took it in and asked if
they knew what kind of feather it was.
Yep.
Well?
It's a turkey feather.
Turkey?
Yep.
LOL! Ok, so now I've got a wild turkey feather.

One more thing, if you attempt these boxes, I would advise you to
bring some bug spray. After we found the second box, we decided not
to hike out, it was easier to take the Mosquito Express be air-
lifted back to the parking area. LOL!

Enjoy!
---Bindle Babe
