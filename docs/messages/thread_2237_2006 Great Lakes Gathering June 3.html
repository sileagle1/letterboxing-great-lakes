Hi!

I thought I'd post an update since I received the first box for the
gathering today (a great stamp from Beetle) AND I received our first
RSVP from a boxer in another region (Sewsowbizzy from Virginia)!

For those who have graciously offered to carve a stamp for the
gathering: Any local boxers are encouraged to hide their gathering
boxes themselves and email me clues. Those who will be sending me
boxes to plant for them can send them anytime (the sooner the better,
but May 10 at the latest). Just send me an email to get my mailing
address.

We currently have 64 people planning to come, and at least 35 boxes
planned for the event. If you don't see your name on the website:

http://2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com/attendees.html

and you are planning to come, also send me an email and I'll add you
to the list. If you have any kids coming, it would be great if you
could also let me know the age(s) and if they are a boy or girl.

Happy carving!
