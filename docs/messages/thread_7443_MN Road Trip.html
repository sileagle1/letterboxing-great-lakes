MN Road Trip

From: Mary (drewes.mary@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-04-23 01:41:04 UTC
What a wonderful letterboxing community you all are in the Twin Cities area! Thank you to Dartmoor Dreamer for organizing it, and to all of you who came to the meet-and-greet for us! We didn't get to do anywhere near as much boxing as we'd hoped, but we did get to discover the Ax Man Surplus Store - and its LB - what a nutty, quirky place!

We are happy to report that we shall probably be getting up to your neck of the woods a lot more often as DD hopes to go to college there in the fall of 2013.

And we recommend checking out the boxes along I-94 - one of my favorite hikes EVER took us to the American Paint LB at Trip Falls in Menomonie. The stamp is a commercial one (which I've recommended to the placer they mention in the listing). Heed the suggestions about waterproof footwear. The area is fantastically beautiful!

- Sunny, Peabody and FF
