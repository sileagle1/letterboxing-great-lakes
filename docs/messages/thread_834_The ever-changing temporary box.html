From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-29 01:07:44 UTC

While Bindle Bum and I are being land-dwellers this winter, we've
placed a letterbox that will stay in the same location, but have the
stamp change on a fairly frequent basis. Once we move back aboard
our sailboat in the Spring, the box will no longer be available.

The first stamp was placed for the Sweetest Day Mini-Meet and has
just been changed to the second stamp in this ever-changing rotation.

Clues are WOM only. (Word of Mouth) You can email me for them or ask
another member who attended the Mini Meet.

This is a very easy find, winter hardy as well as stroller and
handicap accessible.

Currently (10/28): box image consists of a Halloween theme
Next: Wedding theme in honor of my daughter (Enders Angel) who is
getting married in just about 2 weeks!
Then: Thanksgiving theme
Then: Hanukah them
Then: Christmas theme
Then: New Years theme
Then: Some kind of a Winter theme
Then: Valentine's theme
Then: St. Patrick's Day them
Then: Some kind of a Spring theme
Final stamp: so long, thanks for the memories

I hope to place all of these boxes in permanent locations after
their time in the newspaper tube. (Which might take all summer! LOL)

Enjoy!
