Martini Man & I checked on more of  our old boxes in the Milwaukee area recently (and other boxes that we passed along the way).  We also replanted some of Acorn's old boxes from 2004 & 2006.  Since she isn't very active anymore, she gave me some of the boxes she had pulled many years ago. They are back in place with slightly modified clues. 


Here is a report on the boxes we checked on MAY 21-24:


Boxes at Lapham Peak:

Out and About Box #2 (Kilroy) - missing because geocacher took the stamp, clues have been updated to only include Box #1.

Why the Rabbit Was Late - couldn't find, but we will recheck when area is less busy.

Bill the Lizard - trail rerouted so clues need to be tweaked, notified owner (Leapin Lizards)

Pool of Tears - dried out, but contents in good shape, notified owner (Leapin Lizards)

Boxacon II - Loop 3: The Wall - cleaned out geotrash stuff and marked stamp & logbook that nothing should be removed from the box, notified owner (aimlesst)

Boxacon II - Loop 3: Are you from Fred and George? - OK, notified owner (aimlesst)

Eggopolis: Last One to the Box...Is a Rotten Egg!  - Good shape, notified owner (samwashere)

Scary Vegetables - Midwest - both boxes confirmed missing, owner (LightninBug) archived the clues.
