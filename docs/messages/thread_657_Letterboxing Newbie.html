From: golfgalnopar (golfgalnopar@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-08 20:37:19 UTC
Just wanted to say how much I am enjoying this new outdoor activity.
I am part of a group of retired teachers that go by the name
of "WonderBroads of Grand Ledge" when we are "stalking the wild
letterboxes." We spent a week in the UP last month and "stalked" on
the way to the cabin and again on the way back down state. Needless
to say, we are hooked. Of course, as a former teacher, I can't help
but see what a great activity letterboxing would be as a class
project. I can just see the students learning about the area,
developing their own clues, publishing them in the school newsletter,
making their boxes, placing them around their local neighborhood or
community. Ah yes, old teachers never die, they just lose their
class. lol. Anyway, Thanks Spring Chick for you wonderful clues.
Your background historical information pertaining to the area of the
boxes really enriches the experience. Best regards to all fellow
letterboxers, and I hope you are having as much fun as we are when
we "stalk the wild letterboxes."
