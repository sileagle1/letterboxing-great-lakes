Hello fellow letterboxers!

Sorry for any cross-posting.

It is with sadness and excitement that I make the announcement: we are moving to Salt Lake City! My husband starts a new job there Sept. 5.

I am hoping that someone will adopt my boxes I have planted here, since I will not be around to look after them.

Hopefully when the dust settles, Ill start back up postal boxing again.
