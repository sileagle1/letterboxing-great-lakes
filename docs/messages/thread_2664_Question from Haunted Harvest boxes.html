Okay, this is a rather odd question, but I am asking anyway. We are
finishing up some boxes left from the Haunted Harvest gathering (last
fall) over the next few weeks since the park is very close and we can
go right after Mr. Cat Herder gets home from work - even if he works late.

A group of the clues refer to starting at the "Family Picnic
Area". The problem is - there is no "Family Picnic Area" on the map
or on the signage. The closest is the "Family Camping", but we are
not certain that this is the correct area. Can anyone (perhaps WI
Hiker or Martini Man) help me out with the correct name for the
starting point? Here is the link to the website for the map:

http://www.waukeshacounty.gov/cm/business%20units/parks%20and%20land%20use/Park%20System/Parks/Menomonee/Menomonee_Map.html

I will say though, it was very neat to watch the bats at sunset
flying around the lake eating bugs. Very, very cool!

The Cat Herders
