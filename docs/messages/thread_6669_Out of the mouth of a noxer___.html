From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-06-25 15:46:19 UTC
My sister went down to FL last week and checked on one of my letterboxes for me. There were reports of geocachers finding it. Here is some of her report:

"When we went to the smallest post office, we did find your letterbox. It was intact, the stamp was still there. I think you're right about geocachers finding it, though. There were a few trinkets left behind by someone. Unfortunately, we forgot to bring the sharpie when we went, but really, you had the box clearly marked as a letterbox. I don't think writing "this is not a geocache" would deter the geocachers anyway. I don't think they know how to read.

I took photos of all the messages people had left in your box. I'll attach one or two, just so you get the idea:

'Holy crap! This is not a geocache, you dolts!!' "

Oy!
