Re: [LbGLK] Re: May Day Derby

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-05-02 18:55:18 UTC-04:00
The earlier boxes were not as tough as I was trying to seed the area and get people interested and didn't want to scare them away with logic puzzles and cryptic codes, etc. Now that there are many boxes in the area, I am having some fun with different kinds of clues. I have learned though that I need a reliable tester because I am a very poor judge of the difficulty of my own clues. I have 6 boxes planted in a park in Grand Haven and I've always felt these were all quite *easy* and often recommend them to new boxers and families. While the hikes themselves are not extremely long or strenuous, many people have written that the clues are more difficult than I felt they were... well, I guess they were justified on the box whose major landmark had been hauled away in a truck at some point after I planted the box.

But it is true, most of them, especially these days, are not straight follow-the-directions clues... it's all about thinking outside the box.

SpringChick
