Box Maintenance Weekend

From: (mlcigna@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-10-10 16:12:57 UTC-07:00

In anticipation of next weekend's gathering, we were itching to hit the trails in the Milwaukee metro area, so we tackled some long overdue box maintenance.


After receiving news of a wet box, we brought Harry Potter Series Box #1 - Get Sorted home for a day to dry out. We cleaned him up, adjusted the clues a bit to account for some changes to the park over the past 10 years, and put him back for the next finders.


Having not heard a peep from It Also Rises in over three years, we were pleasantly surprised to find the box in great shape right where we left it so long ago. It had been found as recently as a few weeks ago, so we were so happy to see the first box we ever planted still getting some action. Also cool: Kermit Boxer had done a little TLC on the box a few years ago, replacing bags and also adding silica gel packets to help with moisture. How have I never heard of this ?!? Never again will I throw away those little packets that accompany my new shoes!


And finally, we headed out to Retzer Nature Center to replace what has always been one of my favorite boxes. It's such a great park with fantastic views and the box has such a nice history. It had been at Retzer for many years when the nature center staff did some clearing away of old trees and discovered the box. Rather than throwing it away, they contacted us to say that they were saving it and asked that we re-hide it, since it had brought so many people to the nature center. (Indeed, the logbook was very full!) We put it in a new location, where it survived until earlier this summer when we got the news that all remained of it was a box top. We re-carved the stamp, tucked it away in what we hope to be a perfect spot, and adjusted the clues accordingly. 


Looking forward to next weekend's event!! The CignaCats
