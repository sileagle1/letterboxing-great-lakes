This is GREAT! I've got a recent convert myself! My best friend and I ran away to Traverse City for the weekend to get away from, well - everything I guess - and we hunted on Old Mission peninsula. When we had made our way to SpringChick's Old Mission Light House box, she was amazed that the box was actually there...and her being an avid rubberstamper, she LOVED the stamp. So then! (sneaky me) I pull a brand new logbook out of my pack and say "Would you like an image of it for your very own?" She squealed and jumped up and down - saying "you're the best!". I let her "find" the next 2 boxes we were hunting, and I must say - she is thoroughly hooked! Be looking for her future stamp...her trailname is Pirate Jane!
