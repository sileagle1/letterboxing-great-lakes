Member Info: Becky, Tom, and Bryan Alexander ~The Cats Meow~

From: Becky (imdurangogirl@aol.com) | Date: 2006-09-17 22:09:25 UTC
Hello all, we're The Cats Meow. We reside in Niles OH which is in NE
Ohio. We've been boxing for about 2 1/2 years. We're fast
approaching our 100th find and have twenty boxes hidden in 2 states
and 6 counties. Our obsession started when my sister in law and
brother in law introduced us to the hobby. They live in Florida and
go by the trail name Kilroy. It took us about a year to get started
after she showed me the web page and her log book. It kept rolling
around in the back of my mind, but the thought of hand carving stamps
really intimidated me. I went out and bought a cat paw to be our
signature stamp. One nice New Years Eve it was about 55 degrees and
I was itching to get outside. I said to Tom, lets go find the box
your sister has hidden in McDonald Oh. So we set off to find it and
we were hooked. New Years Day it was still unseasonably warm out so
he asked me if I'd like to go to Nelsons Ledges in Parkman OH. There
were two boxes there that had been placed by Gamecock. I agreed to
go since it was winter. I figured there was no chance of coming
accross a snake. We sucessfully found the first box and set off to
find the second box. On that trip we didn't find the second box but
we did come accross a very slugish snake on the trail. Later that
spring we found the second box. We went to our first unoffical
gathering when gamecock held a get together at Brandywine Falls. She
was moving out of state and was going to be there with all her boxes
for those who wanted to get the rest of her series. At that time we
met Doodle and Deedle Bug, Blaze, Gamecock and oh my a few others who
names don't redily come to me. I smile everytime I think about that
day. That was the day that gamecock gave Doodle and Deedle a stamp
comemorating their engagement. :-) And now, they're joined together
in marital bliss. Fast forward to this summer when we attended our
first official gathering. The OOSA Cookout. We had a great time,
and met some really nice people. Safariman,Trailtracker,
Fasinatfinders (whose first box found was one of mine) Grandma
Butterfly, and many others.

Since we've introduced a few of our friends to boxing. We are truely
hooked! We love all the places our fellow boxers have taken us to
see. We're always amazed at the beautiful stamps you all carve. And
we look forward to meeting more of our fellow boxers over time.

This has been a family activity that we've used on vacation, to get
away from it all, to relieve stress, and just have fun.

I'm looking forward to reading more about everyone :-)
