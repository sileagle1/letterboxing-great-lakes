San Antonio

From: cerealcityboxers (cerealcityboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-08 22:55:06 UTC
My wife and I were in San Antonio this last January and we did several
letterboxes, however all of them were a bit away from the riverwalk
area. There used to be one on the grounds of the Alamo but as far as
we know it went missing some time ago. The boxes we did
were "Friedrich Warbler," (well north of town) "Peanuts," "Lonestar
Beer," "Longhorn," "Cowboys," "Dancers," (the last five are all in
McAllister Park near the airport, but a car or cab ride from
anywhere), "Swingle Singers" by the zoo, and finally, Mission San Juan
Capistrano" which is fairly south of the downtown area, but a very
nice Mission site. When we were there we were the only people
visiting. San Antonio is a great place to visit. Have a blast.
