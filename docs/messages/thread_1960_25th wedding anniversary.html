25th wedding anniversary

From: tiptoetonto (tiptoetonto@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-06 06:45:29 UTC
After much angsting as to where and how to spend a few days away
this past week to celebrate our 25th wedding anniversary, Tonto and
I ended up in Brown County Indiana!...A far cry from our other
choices of Maine, Asheville, Tuscon or Banff....that is another
story... suffice to say, time became an issue...But, we gratefully
report that all turned out to be a perfect time! The other
destinations are still on the list - Afterall, we can celebrate all
year! Right?

The weather was superb! Lots of great hiking, memorable meals,
quaint accomodations and yes, even a few letterboxes!
We thought we had officially found our 200th letterbox on CMoon's
Haunted Trail earlier in October, but upon further inspection we
were on 199! So, on our anniversary trip passing through
McCormick's Creek State Park (BTW another awesome Indiana SP!) we
found our official 200th, "Changing Foliage," by the BrownKnight - a
fitting image of a tree with a lovers heart & arrow carved into the
trunk...AHHHHH! For those of you that do the happy-dance at these
milestones - we felt it fitting to happy-smooch!

One of the most exciting finds was the "Connecticut State Seal" in
Bloomington, IN planted by the Dawnlanders of CT. They had given
the box to Mark & Sue Pepe to take with them on their trip to
Dartmoor and somehow it is now permanently planted in Bloomington!
(Armadillo Jo, what were they thinking?) As we read the logbook, it
once again dawned on us how we get such a happy feeling when we come
across a letterboxer's signature stamp that you have met and spent
time with. It is amazing how a little inked image on a page can
conjure up such pleasant memories....(Maybe 2Bentrider should delve
into that psychological phenomenon in her disertation!)

At the same park in Bloomington we found "The Wise Old." How ironic
that as we were leaving the box and heading back to the parking lot,
a Great Horned owl flew barely over our heads! We screeched in
delight over that great anniversary gift!

It all was a little earth shattering for Tiptoe (a Boilermaker
through and through) to be spending her 25th in IU country
thoroughly enjoying herself and finding such great boxes!

And to top off the LBing highlights, we commemorated our 25th
wedding anniversary by planting a new letterbox with the added bonus
of marriage advice! - "A Froggie Went A-Courtin'," at Brown County
State Park on Nov. 1st. I just listed the clues on LBNA tonight.
So, all you Hoosiers get ye down there!
Tiptoe...and Tonto
