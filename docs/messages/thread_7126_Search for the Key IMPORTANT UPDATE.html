From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-06-03 09:23:51 UTC-05:00
Ok, all the boxes, but one, have been posted for the Search for the
Key. That box should get planted soon in western MI. The series was
designed to be a bit of a challenge for the summer and have a few boxes
around the region (although focused around Chicago). The boxes are
posted on both AQ and LBNA, and they are titled so that a search for
'Search for the Key' should find them all.

I appreciate all the help from the volunteers who took part. Sadly, I
know I could have gotten alot more great volunteers, but I tried to
limit the series to doable number of boxes stretched over a larger, but
narrow, region.

For the newer people in the region, I'll freely admit that this series
was based, loosely, on Dragon's 'Holy-Grail' series planted several
years ago. As we were enthusiastic finders in that series, I thought it
was high-time to create a new challenge for the region. (although our
new addition made me scale down the scope to something that WE would now
find manageable).

Anyway, I hope you enjoy them, as I know we now have a few boxing
adventures to plan! I know the complexity of the quest might seem
daunting, but I hope you give it a try and enjoy the series.

Atom118 of the Atomic Beans
