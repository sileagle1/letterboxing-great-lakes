Tiptoe,

Its funny you should say, "With the little knick and little blood, maybe we
should rethink the trail name and make it Bug-a-Boo-boo!"

As Alex has always been accident prone. When we first started boxing she
would fall on the trail all the time.... well, ok, she still falls. She's
bloodied both palms of her hands (and that is when our search for a box ends).

Alie-Boo-Boo.

When she turned 4 we still called her that and she got this serious look on
her face and said, "Don't call me that again."

It was hard, but we have since refrained.
