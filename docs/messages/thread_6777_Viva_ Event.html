Viva! Event

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-10-24 17:05:49 UTC
Thanks to everyone who came out to the Viva, Grand Haven! event yesterday in Michigan. It was a fun day of letterboxing and silly poses that tested not only boxing skills, but also Elvis trivia knowledge and resourcefulness.

Congratulations to team "Hunka, Hunka Burnin' Boxers," who sneaked in at the very end and stole the win by a narrow margin. Some of the teams asked if we would post the point totals online, so here you go:

229.75 Hunka, Hunka Burnin' Boxers (Spartan Fans, Lunagirl & Papa)
224.5 Treasure Hunters (TrailMark, MichKathy, Mysterina, Mysterias, pjynx & Louie)
222.5 Box & Roll and Boxer Rebellion (BowlofCherries; Prince Igor & Firebringer)
219.75 Suspicious Minds (Stargirl & El Cajador; Angel Winks & Hunting Bear)
218.25 Viva las Letterbox Babes (Housepitality Hostess, Wound Tight, littlesister & SportsNuts)
206.25 Team Roustabout (Weary Travelers and JustHappy2CYa)
196.25 Double Trouble and Blue Hawaii (SZ Rocks; Ypsitucky Queen & Rascal)
193.75 Paradise, Yooper Style and Robinhood & MerryMen (Norsegnome; Robinhood & the MerryMen)
193 Fools Rush In (Grandvilla & MobiusKlein; Artistamp & Azobox)
189 Just Wanna Be Your Teddy Bear (Pale Puppy Posse, Groovy Girl, Bflysmiles, Strawberry Shortcake and Purple Dragonfly)
146.25 Orange Crush (TrailMonkeys, Fullmoon, The Kitten Cuddler and Allis Guy)
144.5 Blue Suede Shoe Boxers (Shooz, Barbie Girl and Huygens)
142.25 Strangers in the Crowd (4EyesMcGee, Old Poop, Sparky and Burt the Conqueror)
118 Team DBFamily (Momma, Batboy, Flutterby Girl, Little Jo, Gramma D and Mr. DB)
103 Melinda's Cable Got Cancelled and Here we are (The Mamanator, Pink Verbena and Wild Woodies)
??? Galloping Grannies (aunt katknees and gemgirl65)

Even though they came in at the bottom, I give Melinda's team the nod for the most original team name! A couple of teams earned some extra points for creativity -- the Hunka, Hunka team made a logbook that played Elvis songs and the Yooper team came dressed ready for a trip to Paradise (of the tropical variety, not of the Upper Peninsula variety).

SpringChick
