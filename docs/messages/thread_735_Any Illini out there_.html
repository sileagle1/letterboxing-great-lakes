From: Armadillo Jo (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-08 12:08:20 UTC-07:00
I think Indiana takes the cake for the least
represented state on the list.....
....and I'm definitely the only N. Indiana boxer that
I know of on the list (unless you Hoosiers are
lurking!)

Anyway...thanks for all of the great suggestions for
logbooks. I carved some stamps last night for a future
series to be planted this winter sometime and am
thinking of making more creative books for these
boxes. Artdog's Halloween boxes have inspired me to
make nicer logs, as her's are the cat's
pajamas!!!Scrapbook store, here I come!!!

As far as organization...right now all of my stuff is
divided into three bags: My trail pack that contains a
log book, bag of markers, stamp pads, and my personal
stamp, bug spray, compass (which I accidentally left
in Indy and have to get a new one), hitchhikers,
kleenex, beach glass from Lake Michigan, random
change, and a lighter (for god knows what reason). My
backpack which is exclusively used for long trips and
plants. It has ziplocs, first aid kit, and a bunch of
other garbage in it. The last bag is a messenger bag
in which I keep a rubbermaid with glue, scissors,
carving tools, twine, soft lead pencil, small pieces
