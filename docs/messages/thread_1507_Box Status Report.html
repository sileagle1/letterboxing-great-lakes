From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-06-25 21:44:47 UTC-05:00
As an addition to Martini Man's report, here are a few miscellaneous
comments on some of our searches:

Frog Preserves - great hike and interesting to know we were on part of the
North Country National Scenic Trail.

Go Blue! - loved the eclectic sculptures on this impressive campus. My
favorite was the "Wave Field".

Mystery Cabin - Arboretum very pleasant walk.

Breakfast Elves - nice hike and great stamps.

Beach View By The Dunes - a very hot hike! Even though we didn't find the
box, those dunes are simply amazing!!

Up, Up & Away! - a bit tricky since paths are VERY overgrown. We had fun
coloring this stamp and it turned out great.

Home in the Pines - started off on the wrong footpath immediately. Wandered
about for a while and then lucked into some signs that got us back on the
right path. Another hot hike.

Northeast Ann Arbor - saw a muskrat in the pond!

Walking Tree - ended up explored more of the trail while searching. Looks
like a new bridge is being installed. Logbook contained an entry from some
researchers studying the beech trees.


We're looking forward to more Michigan boxing next weekend when we'll be in
the U.P.!

Wisconsin Hiker
