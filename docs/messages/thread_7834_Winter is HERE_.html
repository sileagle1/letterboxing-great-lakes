With the frigid temperatures and sub-zero wind chills, I'm guessing many of you may be staying holed up in your cozy homes this week.  This would therefore be the PERFECT time to get those Bingo cards filled out!

 

Things are busy this month with all the holidays and parties, but think how dreary the next few months will be.  Why not give yourself a little something to look forward to?  The simple game of Bingo helps several Great Lakes letterboxers get out of bed each morning so they can check the word of the day and then get going to do whatever needs to be done.  (Although with all the smartphones and tablets, you may not even need to get out of bed to check the word...)
