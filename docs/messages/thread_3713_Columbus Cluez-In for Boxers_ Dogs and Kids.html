Van Man had posted a creative suggestion for everyone camping at Alum
Creek for this event to hand a pair of (letter)boxer shorts at their
campsite instead of or in addition to a lock-n-lock box to signify we
are letterboxers.

Since we have several dogs attending this event, I have put together a
PT for my dog, Gunner! This way our dogs can stamp in with their
signature stamps too.

I will also be bringing homemade dog bones for all the dogs who help
us hunt! I have a recipe I needed an excuse to try out!

I was wondering if everyone wanted to have their dogs all wear (the
same?) bandana around their necks during the event?! It would make a
fantastic picture if we can pull it off! Hobby Lobby has a bunch of
them. Thoughts?? I'm thinking that it would be cool to sew a GLK
patch on too!!! Green or blue bandanas would look great with the
patch. Shoot out your email asap if you need another patch as it was
just mentioned that there are only 10 left!

Last, if I get a chance, I will try to make some homemade playdough
for the kids attending this event. My kids loved this stuff when they
were little and it lasts forever!!!!!

Hales Angels, you did an amazing job making (was it) 20 stamps, JUST
FOR KIDS!!!! What a thoughtful thing you did!!!

Trailtracker
