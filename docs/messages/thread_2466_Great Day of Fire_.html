Checked in on this box today and was surprised to find there had been
only 2 teams to find it since it was planted over a year ago. These
are not difficult clues and no puzzle involved ;-) It is a bit of a
hike, but not difficult terrain and thoroughly rewarding as this is
one of the most awesome trails I've been on this spring. It is so
varied -- from deep, dark, mossy woods to bright, open meadowland.
Many wildflowers in bloom and also a great place to do some bird-
watching!
