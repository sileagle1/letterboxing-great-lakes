Coming Soon...Letterboxing Pinup

From: placedenfert1 (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-05-08 00:45:33 UTC
Yes a new pin-up that will be (as near as I can tell) the world's first letterboxer pin-up is on its way. The artwork is currently being worked on by Su Houston, a tattoo artist who travels between Chicago and New York, and occasionally does work throughout Europe. On May 28, she'll begin work on the tattoo, and once it's on my back, I'll have a photo to work with, and I can begin carving. (I can't wait!)

So somewhere between now and the end of the summer, keep an eye out for "The Great American Pin-Up Series: Marjorie"!
