From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-08-18 02:03:27 UTC
I just wanted to say thanks to everyone who takes the time to write me
a quick note when they find one of my letterboxes. I try to respond
with a "thanks for the report" but I'm sure I don't always catch them
all. Today I was reminded again how much I appreciate those little
status notifications when a box I thought was lonely and finderless
was found, only to learn that another group had been there earlier
this summer.

So again, thanks to all of you who do take the time to drop a note.
Keep them coming!

SpringChick
