Maing my introduction....

From: Diane (ek242dmh@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-29 10:38:42 UTC-07:00
Greetings! I had recently joined "the big list" and
had gotten a few "welcome to the club" messages,
including one from Dragon suggesting that I might want
to join this list, too. So here I am! For those who
didn't "meet" me on the big list yet, I'm Diane, aka
Doodle Bug. My boyfriend, Jeff (aka Deedle Bug) and I
learned about letterboxing last fall while trying to
find information on Squire's Castle here in Ohio. We
found our first box a few weeks ago, if the rain holds
off this weekend we're going to try to find box #2,
and I'm already working on plans to place our first
box. We're from Cleveland (Parma for me, Brooklyn for
him) and the more I read about this hobby the more I
love it.

I'm glad to be part of such a welcoming group! Oh,
and since I missed the original "trail name" thread, I
wanted to throw in the story behind our names. Jeff
started calling me his "Doodle Bug" because I
unconsiously sing "do do-do do do do-do" when I'm
doing things like the dishes or laundry or what-not.
And then he became Deedle in turn. But our stamp was
carved before we decided to use our "pet" names as our
letterboxing names, so it's more to do with how lucky
we feel we are to have found each other
*awwwwwwwwwwwweee*

Happy boxing, all!

Doodle Bug aka Diane.
