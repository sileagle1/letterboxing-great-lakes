From: jeniwahlig (jeners@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-08-23 14:07:46 UTC
Ah but that's all part of the fun! One of the great things about letterboxing is that it not only exercises your body when you're out hiking around in search for the box, but it also exercises your mind in the puzzles and clues you need to solve in order to reach your prize. :) After a while, you'll start developing what Lighthouse Keeper and I like to call "letterboxing instincts" (hehe). You'll start getting a feel for whether or not the clues are making sense when you're "out there" such that you'll have a pretty good idea whether you're in the right place or not. All this is to say nothing, of course, of the great creativity and art element that comes with letterboxing! i have to admit, i'm rather biased to this hobby over geocaching... ;)

But welcome to the joy of outdoor playtime! i hope you enjoy both hobbies and have many great adventures! perhaps we will meet you on the 3rd...

Take care,

One love journeying
