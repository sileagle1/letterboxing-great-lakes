From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-11-20 21:48:58 UTC
Hi!

I've posted the final sample of the new patch in the photos section
and have oredered a quantity of 200. There was more interest than I
anticipated and I really appreciate everyone entering their requests
in the database so I could easily get a handle on the amount to order.

The other good news is that with the larger quantity and final
pricing, the price per patch will again be $1.50.

Now we'll have to wait for them to actually arrive (probably mid-
December, but no guarantees). You can send me a check and self-
addressed envelope now, and I'll hold onto it until the patches
arrive, then get them immediately in the mail to you OR you can wait
to send in the check and SASE until after I announce that I've
received them.

Here's a link to see the final patch and get ordering info:
http://letterboxing.homestead.com/greatlakespatch.html

(Please don't forget the self-addressed envelope)

I'll repost this same info when they actually arrive.

Wisconsin Hiker
