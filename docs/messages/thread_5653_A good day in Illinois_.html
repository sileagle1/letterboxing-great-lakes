A good day in Illinois!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2009-02-15 02:05:10 UTC
Happy Valentine's Day!

We came down to Illinois a day early and had a great day of boxing. We
did 2 series on longer hikes and also did a few "drive-by" boxes. We
located 19 boxes and were able to log into 17 of them (yep, 2 more
FROZEN IN PLACE boxes that we'll have to return to sometime after
things thaw out!)

We're looking forward to seeing everyone (or at least a LOT of you - I
see the expected attendance is now 64 people!) tomorrow at the SILO
Mardi Gras event.

Wisconsin Hiker
