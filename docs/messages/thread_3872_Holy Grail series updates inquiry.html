The only boxes I know of that have issues are the following, and it is
noted on the website:

Black Beast - needs some first aid but in place
When Danger Reared - box missing, being replaced
Catapuled Cow - was reported missing but verified in place
Old Man from Scene 24 - confirmed to be still in the hands of Laughing
Coyote - email after email have been sent, but no delivery has been
made - trying again
Camelot - in the hands of the placer, but between weather and hiding
sites that haven't worked out the box hasn't found a home yet (not for
the lack of trying)

I know nothing about the Sir Lancelot box having any issues at all.

Also, as far as I know, the HH is still where it is listed on the
Grail site.

I can only report on what is told to me via email or the GL site. I
save all my emails and have just reviewed them, and all info on the
site is as up-to-date as I know.
