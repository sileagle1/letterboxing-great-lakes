From: TrailTroll (kanderson@american-community.com) | Date: 2007-07-30 12:05:05 UTC
Anybody who has the clues for Peace with Nature, in Rochester, MI - ths
box has been archived. This is a box that was abandoned, which I
adopted last year. It's still there, but the landscape has changed
dramatically and the clues don't make sense. Also, there have been "No
Trespassing" signs posted, and I don't approve of directing people onto
private property. I won't remove the box, since it wasn't mine to
begin with, but I will no longer be supporting it.

P.S. to BNL BaliGrl - how the heck did you find this one? With the
bridge open, the clues don't make sense, and yet you still found it.
Great nose for letterboxing!

TrailTroll
