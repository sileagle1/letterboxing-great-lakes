I had used them inside the baggies for the log books in my letterboxes and it DID help! As long as the baggie doesn't have a hole, it will help prevent the condensation.

I also stuffed one inside a microbox (film canister with a tiny stamp and roll of paper) but because it sometimes caused the container to be hard to close, it totally defeated it's purpose when the canister was not sealed completely.
