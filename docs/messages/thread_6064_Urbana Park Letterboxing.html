Re: [LbGLK] Re: Urbana Park Letterboxing

From: Katrina Lustofin (drkaty@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-09-24 19:56:23 UTC-04:00
It's actually in the Urbana City Parks, not the county parks. It has been
put together by an employee of the Urbana Park District, who prepped for it
by going after many of the local letterboxes in the past year. They do
reference LBNA in their clues and the boxes are listed on both LBNA and AQ.
The park district contacted me last year to make sure their boxes were not
planted too close to mine, a courtesy I appreciated. I don't think, though,
that any one there actually boxes, aside from this experience.

Katy
