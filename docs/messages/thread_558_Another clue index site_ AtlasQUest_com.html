Another clue index site: AtlasQUest.com

From: Eloise Mason (nee Beltz-Decker) (eloise@ripco.com) | Date: 2004-08-17 11:52:30 UTC-05:00
They have a rather nice interface, but I was shocked ot find that
out of the 980 US boxes they index, not one was in Illinois! I fixed that
by registering my three recent finds with them. They don't maintain a clue
'database', per se; they only provide links to clue pages elsewhere (as on
letterboxing.org).

Was this a long-known resource that I just missed, or have the
letterboxing.org group managed to not find out about atlasquest?

Hi!

I don't think Atlas Quest has been promoted much yet, but I know that at
least a few members of this regional group are registered at the site. I
agree that it looks like a very promising tool and have used it recently to
find boxes that are located on longer hikes. In an email correspondence
with Ryan last week he confirmed that only boxes submitted by placers are
included, so if any of us want our boxes included on this site, we need to
add the appropriate info.
