Mosquito repellent

From: (papeseed@tds.net) | Date: 2005-06-18 18:17:47 UTC-05:00
My sister accidentally discovered that one of her scented lotions makes an excellent mosquito repellent. We were boxing together for Fungus Woman's Fish Out of Water: spring break in MN. I got so many bites I was miserable! She got not one!!! So when I went out to check on one of my boxes on the way home, I tried the lotion and got no new bites. Today I put some on before going out hiking by a marsh, which was extremely mosquito-infested. We could hear them buzzing all around our ears and they would land on us, but then immediately leave. Only one bite! I'm sold! It's Bath & Body Works White Tea and Ginger Body Cream...and right now they are having their semi-annual clearance so you can get it cheap! Thought I'd pass this along. --Papeseed
