Box Maintenance 2013

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2013-01-10 15:20:36 UTC-06:00
Since the weather this year has been a bit warm, I figured it is never
too early to start maintaining boxes.

Continuing in the tradition of Dragon (and later Springchick), I'm
sponsoring a box maintenance prize for 2013 similar to last year.

The idea is that everyone should go out and check their clues and boxes
to make sure that they are still accurate and in good shape. Do your
part to end 'Attempted Finds!'

I started a database 'Box Maintenance 2013' where you can list any box
that you have recently maintained. For each box you maintain, you get
one entry into a drawing that will happen sometime in April (TBD). The
winner will get a great, letterboxing related, prize!

For a box to qualify:

1) You (or a member of your 'team') must be the person to visit a box
that you own.
2) You must physically check that the clue is accurate, the stamp and
logbook are intact and in good shape, and that the box container is
intact. If any part is incomplete, you need to fix it by March 31st to
be counted. If the box is gone, or you are not going to fix it, the box
can be counted if the status is changed to 'Retired' or 'Unknown.'
3) You must do maintenance between 1/1/13 and 3/31/13.
4) A box must have been plated before 1/1/13 to be counted.
5) Like most letterboxing finds, finds are recorded on the honor system.

Go find YOUR boxes!

On a personal note, team Atomic Beans was out this past weekend doing
maintenance on our 2006 series 'Singing and Boxing.' I'm happy to
report that 3 were fine, 1 was replanted, and only one was missing.

Atom118 of Atomic Beans
