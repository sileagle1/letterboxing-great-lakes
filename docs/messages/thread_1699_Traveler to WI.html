There was a box in Beloit ("Turtle"), but it's now gone missing.
Other than that, I haven't seen anything in the Janesville area.
Rockford, IL is only a stone's throw south of there.

I've got one box in New Glarus - Swiss Village Series #1. I've got 4
more boxes carved for that series, but haven't made it there to place
them yet, unfortunately. busyness, family emergencies, unexpected travel, etc. here.>

If they're coming in the fall, that series should be in place by mid-
September.

Also ... Madison's only a half hour-45 minutes north of Janesville and
has tons of boxes. Verona's only 15 minutes north of New
Glarus. "Ice Age Trail" is in Verona. "Ebenezer Says..." is 15
minutes west of Verona.

Here's to even more boxes in SW WI!

I'm in the Madison area and you're welcome to give them my email
address for any correspondence.

M Clan
