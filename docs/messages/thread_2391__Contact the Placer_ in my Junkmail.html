We were also having this problem for a while and after writing to the
LBNA webmaster I realized the problem. You need to put the address
that these updates come from into your address book and then change
your spam filter settings to always allow mail from addresses that are
in the address book. If you don't know how to do that your email
server's webmaster should be able to help.
