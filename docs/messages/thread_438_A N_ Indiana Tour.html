A N. Indiana Tour

From: Armadillo JMO (jes7o@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-24 19:05:46 UTC-07:00
Hello all! Today I went on my first letterboxing quest
around N. Indiana, my home. I visited 8 boxes total
and visited places I never even knew exsisted! I found
all that I was looking for. I am happy to report that
all boxes were in great shape, with the exception of
the Potato Creek box, which had a little water in it.
I emptied it out and attempted to dry it off as well
as I could.
I went to:
Neil's Letterbox (Warsaw)
Century of Progress (Elkhart Co.)
3 in St Patrick's Co. Park (South Bend)
Hunt for Limpids (New Carlisle)
Potato Creek (N. Liberty)
Grandpa's Gear (Bremen)

If ever going to Hunt for Limpids, WEAR TONS OF BUG
SCREEN!!!!! It is indeed Mosquito Hell.

Tomorrow I will be planting my first box. It will be
placed in Marshall County, south of Plymouth, IN. I'll
post the details once I get it planted. I did some
preliminary scouting today, found my location for the
box...now just a trip to Wally World for the baggies
and a stamp mount!

Until tomorrow,
Armadillo JMO
