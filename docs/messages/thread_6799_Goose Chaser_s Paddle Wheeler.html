Goose Chaser's Paddle Wheeler

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2010-11-23 03:15:37 UTC
So today I decided to check on an old box of Goose Chaser. It's been more than a year since I last checked on this box and was horrified as I rounded the back of the rest area building and found a new generator sitting on a large (4' x 6')cement pad.

My heart sank, the row of bushes the box was hidden in was no more.

I went inside and there was a worker in the small office. I went up to her and told her, "I see you have a new generator. There used to be a row of bushes, and under one of the bushes was a camouflage box. It doesn't happen to be in the office here."

"No," she said, "but the guy out there does."

She led me outside to another worker and told him to tell me about the box.

He started to lead me around back of the building and to another bush altogether. He bent over and started to dig around in the leaves and pulled the box out.

I thanked him and started to open the box, all the while telling him of Goose Chaser and how the box had been there for nearly 5 years. He told me that they actually threw the box away in the trash can and he went and dug around through the garbage, pulled it out and re-hid it under another bush.

I shook his hand, again thanking him for his time & care in retrival of the box and re-placing it. So, it remains for all to find and enjoy.
