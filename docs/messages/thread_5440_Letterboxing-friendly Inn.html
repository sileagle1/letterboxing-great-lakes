We just got a response back from a B&B in Tucson that we've stayed at before where we were
checking on a future availability for a conference we are attending. We had to chuckle
because in our inquiry for reservations we never mentioned that we were coming out to
letterbox, but in the return subject line they had written "Jeremiah Inn Welcomes
Letterboxers."
When we first stayed there we told them about letterboxing, but we thought it funny that they
recognize us by our hobby. We could only imagine their conversation probably went
something like ... "those crazy letterboxers are coming to stay with us again!"
If you are going to Tucson they are a great B&B and the area has some wonderful letterboxing
opportunities!
