I received this email today and thought some of you might be interested in it:


Dear Outdoor Recreation Enthusiast:

 

I would like to announce the publication of my new book titled The Family Camping Guide to Wisconsin, Michigan, Illinois & Indiana and ask that you tell other outdoor enthusiasts about this valuable resource for planning basic tent vacations to any of the four Lake Michigan states. The book was written for tent camping families who want to find safe and comfortable public campgrounds with large, level, grassy and shady campsites plus plenty of children’s and adult activities. It provides basic information about virtually all 720 state, federal, county, and municipal camping destinations rather than a few arbitrarily selected “favorite campgrounds” in the four states. Other unique features of this book are summarized below.

 

·         It summarizes popular activities and tourist attractions near each camping destination.

·         It organizes public campgrounds in each state by geographic location, management authority and number of campsites so that families can easily identify potential campgrounds near planned travel routes and ultimate destinations.

·         It identifies campgrounds that have been cited by other authorities as good camping destinations in addition to campgrounds that the author favors.

·         It explains how to plan safe and comfortable tent camping vacation trips to unfamiliar destinations and how to secure good campsites in popular campgrounds during busy summer weekends.

·         It uses a unique system of reference towns, highways, and landmarks to help readers easily locate each property on a highway map.

·         It provides phone numbers for each campground so that users can call the campground office if necessary and GPS coordinates so that campers can easily navigate to it, even in the dark.

·         It includes over a hundred photos of popular camping destinations in the four states.

 

The book was published by Trails Books in June 2014 and is listed for $19.95. To purchase a copy, visit www.Amazon.com and enter the title of the book or my name. Also, visit my personal website  www.BasicTentCamping.com and contact me if you have any questions.
