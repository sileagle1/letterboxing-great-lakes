Mud Hiking!

From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-01-06 23:09:05 UTC-06:00
So how many letterboxers took advantage of this winter thaw and 60 degree temps and went 'mud hiking' in search of letterboxers today? My hand is raised. I was out there. Up to my ankles in mud. Reminded me of the semi-frozen swamp at St. Nick's Gathering. Unfortunately, I think I twisted my leg and sprained my knee. Warning: Be careful when hiking in mud. It's easy to twist your leg when you're climbing over fallen trees and you loose your footing. OUCH! Beetle

Atomic Beans got out and enjoyed the weekend. We spent Saturday walking
around downtown scoping out places/clues for a downtown box (thats my
teaser). On Sunday, we ventured north to find some boxes
(unfortunately, it seems that a few of the older ones might be
missing). It was a great day, but our shoes and pants got coated with
mud. We managed to find a few by Beetle, Flying J, Mischief aka Lauren,
and Garden Elf. Not only our pants, but our car got coated too. Today,
I parked it on the roof of the garage at work...hopefully it will rain
and wash off some of that mud!

I hope you feel better Beetle! (for the rest of you, it was one of our
boxes that took her down.....oops/sorry!)

Atom 118
