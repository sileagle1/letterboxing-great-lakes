Re: [LbGLK] Personal Letterboxes

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-04 07:39:44 UTC-07:00
Usually a Personal Traveler requires a question to get
from another boxer, even if it's a simple, "Can we get
your Personal Traveler image?" Some people have you
tell them what a certain phrase means, or what their
favorite movie is, or you may have to say a certain
phrase to get their Personal Travelers.

If you ever meet Deedle & me on the trails, tell us
how we originally met to get our Personal Traveler
(which IS on our website, you just have to hunt for
the story ;)

Doodle Bug.
