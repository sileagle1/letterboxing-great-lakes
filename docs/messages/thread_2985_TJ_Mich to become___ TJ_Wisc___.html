TJ_Mich to become... TJ_Wisc???

From: TJ_Mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-08-21 13:12:48 UTC-07:00
Nah, we probably won't change our trail name, but we are going to be leaving Michigan and joining all of you folks on the other side of the lake... We're moving to Milwaukee!

I had the chance to stay with my current employer but move to a new job there, which we eagerly accepted. This will put us much closer to our hometown, as those of you who have solved our personal traveler can attest. Of course all of the grandparents are very excited to have more frequent visits with The Nickerboxer, Little Woodchuck, and our newest little guy. And we are very intrigued to start discovering the dozens (hundreds?) of boxes that await us in southeastern Wisconsin! But only, of course, after we've unpacked the dozens (hundreds?) of boxes that contain our household.... yeesh....

Happy to remain part of the Great Lakes group,
--TJ_Mich
