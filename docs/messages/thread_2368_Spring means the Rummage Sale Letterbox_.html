Our thanks to Dragon, who has sent his Rummage Sale
Letterbox to us for the month of April as we attempt

link http://tinyurl.com/ktpzg (the tiny url for the

Dragon did a great job on it! And if you don't have
our personal traveller yet, don't forget to tell us
how Deedle & I met to get it.
