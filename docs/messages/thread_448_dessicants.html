On the big list (don't always read it, but just happened to out of
curiosity) I saw someone mentioned including dessicant packets in a
box. While useless unless the bags are promptly sealed, has anyone
gone so far as to include them or even considered doing so? I ask
because I have access to an endless supply we discard daily at work.

very tiny, maybe .5x1", so they'd fit the 50 ml centrifuge tubes but
a larger box would need several.

I have used them on occasion, particularly in boxes where the log cover was
leather or something else that might mold/mildew easily. I hesitate to use
them more because I am unsure if they are safe for animals should the box
come open and the contents spill out -- after all they do have a big warning
on them that they are not for consumption.
