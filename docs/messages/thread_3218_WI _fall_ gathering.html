From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-26 23:28:54 UTC
I got out to scout the area today and I have a few more details:

The gathering will be at Mitchell Park in Waukesha (NOT the one in
Milwaukee). It is bounded by Barker Rd and Capitol and River Rd.

I'm going to go out on a limb and not officially rent one of the
picnic shelters. I'm hoping that we won't be fighting anyone for
either of the two that are there. We'll meet at one of the picnic
shelters for the gathering from 10 am until 5 pm. (The shelters are
closer to River Rd and are next to each other, so you can't miss us.)
I will bring our cross country tent which has sides in case it is
cold, and either I or someone will bring a grill to cook on (and maybe
keep warm?) After the gathering, those interested can meet at
Peppino's (an Italian restuarant) nearby for reminissing and
celebrating our filling the WI Italy boot to beat MI in the county
contest.

I would be looking for someone to volunteer to carve a gathering stamp
as I can't guarantee my time.

Also, the gift exchange will be voluntary - not applied to all that
come. Since there would be no postage expense for the gifts, I'll set
the gift cap off at $10. If you would like to be involved in the gift
exchange, let me know when you tell me you're coming.

Oh, and the shelters are near the Bark Park, so bring your dogs to
help keep us warm and to go find Sophie's Box!

I'll try to get a gathering website up sometime mext week.

Dragon
