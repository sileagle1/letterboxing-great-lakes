Sure, go ahead. Lots of us have changed our sig stamps from the images
we used when first getting started.

Some do it when milestones are reached i.e F-100; some do it when the
older stamp disintegrates, some do it when they just get tired of the
old one.
My sig stamp inlcudes my trailname as part of the stamp so when I
changed over to the present one, it was still obvious that it was the
same person. If you customarily sign your name, it'll still be clear
that it's doubledragonletterboxer.

Good luck.
