From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-18 19:16:54 UTC
For those of you who enjoy the challenge of mystery boxes, difficult
clues, or just like brain teasers AND if you are maybe a little bummed
that the days are too short for letterboxing and the weekends too full
(at least that's how B and I feel!), try this out:

http://www.weffriddles.com/

I admit, it is not as good as letterboxing, but it will suck up the
hours just as quickly! And it is very, very addictive. B and I have a
little competition between us on how quickly we can get through the
levels.... I won't mention who is winning right now, because I plan on
changing that very soon! :-)

Enjoy!
J of J&BBooks
