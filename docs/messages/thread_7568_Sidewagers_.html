Yes, that's right... if it's Bingo time, it must also be Sidewagers time!

I'll use the same set of rules that Atom118 used the last 2 years, which were pretty close to what Wisconsin Hiker had before that, and I can't really remember what Azobox had before her... but anyway, just check the files section and read the Sidewagers 2013.pdf file if you are unfamiliar with this game.

Basically, this is a side bet on what day the first player will complete a full card BINGO. You do not need to be part of the BINGO game to play. Your guess must be submitted before January 1, 2013.

PLEASE PUT 'SIDEWAGER' in the subject line, and send your guess to me at tj_mich -at- ameritech -dot- net

I'll reply (probably not right away) to confirm your email and let you know your date.

As of now, the game is OPEN.

GOOD LUCK!

--Tom of TJ_Mich
