Spring Fever and New Boxes

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-03-24 21:40:16 UTC
Beetle has Spring Fever! The symptoms are as follows: 1. Eagerness to
get out of the house and find boxes whether there is snow on the ground
or not 2. Determination to create new boxes for others to find 3.
Staying up till 3:00-5:00 am to accomplish this. (I'm more creative at
night.)

In the past month, I placed two new letterboxes and I have two more to
place tonight. However, this is an alert that I'm working on a new
series of about 12 letterboxes soon to be unveiled. These boxes are
being designed for those of us who don't always have a buddy to go with
us when the letterbox seeking bug bites us. I hope to have these new
boxes in place with a week or so. Watch AQ for the what's new
listing.

It really IS melting!

Beetle
