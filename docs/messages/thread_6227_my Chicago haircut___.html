my Chicago haircut...

From: Janna Odenthal (jannaode@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-01-06 12:27:58 UTC-08:00

During my stay in Chicago while Seth was in the hospital, to get my mind off things I decided to get a haircut. Since I didn't know my surroundings well, I stopped at the first place I could find, an African American haircutting establishment.

The lady asked me what I wanted done to my hair. I answered half jokingly, "Oh, I don't care, just make me look sexy."

She replied, "Honey, that ain't what they be lookin' at. Besides, you walk into a room wearin' a Cubs hat and they a White Sox fan, theysee that right away. But God forbid yo' hair should be on fire."

Needless to say, I left her a big tip for the much needed laughter.
