News from the TJ_Mich family

From: tj_mich (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2006-07-08 18:59:49 UTC
We're happy to announce that we welcomed our newest little
letterboxer, Sam, just nine days ago! 8lbs, 9oz, 23 inches, 10
fingers and 10 toes, for those who keep count. He is doing well and
keeping Mom and Dad busy. The Nickerboxer is quite interested in the
new baby, and has so far been helpful about getting his pacifier,
watching him get changed, etc. Little WoodChuck is vaguely curious,
but not much. And Maya just sits in the corner thinking "You guys
brought ANOTHER one of these things home? Remember when it was just
you two and me, and we'd go letterboxing every weekend???"

Sam's trail name is still TBD.... but we'll be sure to have his stamp
carved before the next gathering!

--Tom and Jill
