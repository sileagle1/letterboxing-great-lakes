Ciao Michigonian Boxers of Letters:

It has been a while since my last telegram to you and I thought you
would like to hear of our progress [stop.] The circo has left the
wonderful district of Virginia and the able leadership of Phineas Boch
[stop.] They have performed to sold out crowds and have been the envy
of our competition [stop.] Boch's Circo is now in transit to my
American home in Kensingtonia, where the reunion will be most joyous
[stop.] I am only a temporary ringmaster while we travel our way to
Michigonia but will do all in my power to ensure that we are rested
and ready to perform in the true circo tradition [stop.]

We are looking forward to seeing you all and performing at the Hudson
Mills grounds where we will be setting up our big top for our
exclusive one day only performance on 18 June 2005 [stop.]

Until later . . .

Marco Peperini
Owner, Pinecone Extravanganza Ciro
Venice, Italia
