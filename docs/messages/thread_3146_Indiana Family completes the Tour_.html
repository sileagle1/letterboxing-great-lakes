Indiana Family completes the Tour!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-02 20:49:51 UTC
The Travel Office at "Around the World in 80 Days" was beginning to
pack up the files and had almost posted the "Space for Rent" sign at
the Kalamazoo and Muskegon branches when the DUCA Family from Indiana
sent the required proof of passage. As noted earlier, the Passport
Office has already closed and the rest of the staff was inching
towards the door, but then the postal service brought notice of the
(final!?) travelers and their success.

The DUCA Family submitted their documentation in the waning days of
the very successful "Around the World in 80 Days" series and will
receive the appropriate stamps in recognition.

Congratulations, DUCA family. You join the road weary group of
travelers who have successfully completed the journey.

For the last time (?)
Safe Travels,
The Team at 80 Days
SpringChick & azobox

From: Brad Maynes (Brad.Maynes@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-10-02 17:00:41 UTC-04:00
Once more, for J and I, I just wanted to thank the travel staff for what was
a wonderful experience. The Around the World series were some of our first
letterboxes, and has been one of the defining experiences of letterboxing
for us to date. I can still remember the excitement we had waiting for our
Passport, and the fun we had traveling all over.

Thanks so much,

B of J&B Books
