West Bend, WI AUG 10-12

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2012-07-25 01:35:00 UTC
Just a heads up that West Bend, WI will be swarming with geocachers on the weekend of AUG 10-12, so it may not be the best time to be out hunting or planting letterboxes in that area.

This is the weekend that the city hosts a huge annual event called Cache Ba$h. It attracts people from all over the United States. One of the interesting things each team get in their goodie bag is a "Dash Card: You can display this postcard on your vehicle's dash to let law enforcement know that your vehicle is not abandoned while searching for caches." This town (where Martini Man's mom and one of my sister & her family live) really supports people getting out to explore the area (and spend their tourist dollars).

If you want more details about the event, you can find it here:

http://www.geocaching.com/seek/cache_details.aspx?wp=GC32A1X

and/or here: http://www.westbendcache.com/

Even if you don't geocache (we DON'T), it is interesting to read about all the activities at the event.

Wisconsin Hiker
