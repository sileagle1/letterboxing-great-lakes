Beetle's Weather Report - It's melting!

This snowy slop isn't going to keep my in the house any longer. I just
placed a new letterbox (Adler Memorial Park Letterbox), and I scoped
out several new locations for additional boxes that I plan to place
this week. The snow is not even ankle deep, so pull on some boots and
ignore these last traces of winter. Spring is here!

If you know people who think it's unusual to be hiking and looking for
hidden outdoor freezer boxes in the winter, guess what I saw today?
There are actually groups of people who play disc golf in the snow!
How's that for weird? Well, we all have our unique hobbies.
