How I picked my card

From: (hnjruetten@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-04-21 07:53:18 UTC-07:00

Congrats to all the winners. I did get the straight line this year in my first win in 4 years (I think) playing. I had two words left, but my wildcard was drawn so I was only looking for one more.  I thought everyone might find it interesting as to how I picked my card. I didn't have any trail names or boxes that I could write down this year. So I had a card full of general terms. I used a random number generator to fill out my card.  I numbered each general term and whatever the number generator picked is what I wrote down from top to bottom to fill out the grid.  I just kept going until the whole card was full.  So a totally random card and I was down to two words left. Not too bad :) It is the closest that I have ever gotten.


Looking forward to next year
