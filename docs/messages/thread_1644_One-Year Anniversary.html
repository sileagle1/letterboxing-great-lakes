One-Year Anniversary

From: Arlene Freeman (norsegnome@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-28 12:12:09 UTC-07:00
Count us in that group. We'd never heard of letterboxing before the Time magazine article. We were intrigued with the challenge of it, and amazed that these boxes were hidden in some of our favorite places! Our P3,F30,H1 may not sound like much for our first year, but in the rural Upper Peninsula of Michigan our boxing opportunities are limited. We are starting to supplement with postal letterboxing and plan to place more boxes in our area. (If we can lure more boxers to the UP, they may place some more boxes here for us to find!) :-) Norse Gnome
