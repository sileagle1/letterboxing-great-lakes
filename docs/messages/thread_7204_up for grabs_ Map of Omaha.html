Hey all!
Skootching Dog and I did a fantastic trip to Omaha this last spring.
Did you know there are over 100 active letterboxes in the Omaha area?

Anyway, after trial and tribulation, I finally got a street map of
Omaha. They're not popular in Mpls area bookstores. When I'd ordered
one off Amazon, I got a creepy clown book instead, too! So, I offer
this rare and difficult to find map to the first taker that wants it.
It's lived through only one letterboxing weekend, and we're not likely
to head back to Omaha any time soon, so, good condition and will go to
waste, otherwise.

Send me an email if you'd like it. We find local maps invaluable
resources to plot out letterboxing weekends and adventures!

Cheers!
E.N.
