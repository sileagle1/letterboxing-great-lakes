Tonto received our Secret Santa stash at the office today!
Apparently, Santa and Mrs. Claus have been spending a whole lot of
time at the Bristol Renaissance Faire - We always knew they just
couldn't spend ALL of their time at the North Pole! I think ol' Santa
and the Misses have even taken up alias identities somewhat out of
their usual character..Rumor has it that Santa may be involved with
the making of armour, and dare we metion that Mrs. Claus has been
dabbling in the art of....well, ...wenching!
Nonetheless, they delivered to us humble folk an assortment of pens,
notebooks, a journal, wooden Christmas cutouts, an afterbite stick, 2
pieces of PZcut and of course coupons to come visit them at the Faire!
We send our thanks your way Mr. & Mrs. Claus for finding an alternate
address - it was fine..Although, it did throw Tonto off a bit! We
hope you two don't loose too much jolly weight sweating away in those
hot costumes! We are booked up with a host of weddings and showers
for the next three weekends - But, we will pass on the coupons to some
potential letterboxing friends!
Tiptoe & Tonto
