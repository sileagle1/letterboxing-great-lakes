From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-04-05 21:46:34 UTC-05:00
You've probably already found similar links, but here are 2 for Madison:



http://www.wisegeek.com/what-are-some-things-to-do-in-madison-wisconsin-with
-children.htm



http://www.momsinmadison.com/madisonoutside.html



The Dells of course has MANY, MANY attractions. Since it is Easter time,
you might not find many discounts, but there are a ton of great hotels
w/indoor water parks in the Dells. We've been to several over the years
with our extended family. You could always call to see if they have any
last minute discounts if they aren't full. Wilderness is where we've been
most often, but also Chula Vista. Kalahari also looks great. Normally you
could get a better deal if you went during the week, but since this is
Easter break, that might not be the case.
