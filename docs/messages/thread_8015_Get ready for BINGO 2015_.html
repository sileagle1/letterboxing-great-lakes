Get ready for BINGO 2015!

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2014-12-03 16:40:24 UTC-08:00

I hope everyone had a nice Thanksgiving holiday with family and friends. Martini Man & I have much to be thankful for and one of those things is knowing so many fun letterboxers in the Great Lakes region!

As most of you know, part of our winter fun is playing the Great Lakes Letterboxing BINGO game started by SpringChick back in 2006. She ran the game for 5 years, then the illustrious Mr. Familyof6 took the reins for two years and now I'm honored to be hosting the BINGO game for a third time. 2015 will be our TENTH YEAR of Great Lakes Letterboxing BINGO. We're hoping to have a lot of participants for the 2015 game, so spread the word. You sure wouldn't want to miss experiencing the slow motion thrill of victory and agony of defeat that this game is famous for!

The necessary files are ready for your use in the "Files" section of the message board (just scroll down to the "Great Lakes Letterbox BINGO! folder, click on it, then click on the 2015 BINGO Game folder).

 

General instructions are in the Bingo 2015 FAQ.pdf file, the terms to fill your card are in the BINGO 2015 Word List.pdf  file and the blank card is found as two formats: BINGO GRID 2015.doc and BINGO GRID 2015.pdf.  You may wish to use the MS Word version to type (or cut & paste) your terms directly into the grid. You can then save a copy and send it as an attachment in an email to me. Then, make sure you mail a booty prize and $2 to my address (see FAQ file) by December 31 or you can give me your booty and $2 in person if you want to save the mailing costs. The absolute deadline for getting your booty to me is JAN 10, but you still need to get your BINGO card to me by the deadline of DECEMBER 31 because the game starts on January 1, 2015.

I wanted to get the files posted early enough so you could still get out to find some of the boxes on the Word List, if you haven't already found them. It will give you a few more choices for filling your grid and, more importantly, you'll get to find more great letterboxes!

 

New this year will be a prize for the BIGGEST LOSER - the person who has the LEAST squares crossed off at the completion of the game.

If you have any questions, just shoot me an email, or post the question to this board.

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. Martini Man doesn’t want to be deprived of playing, so he has not been involved in any planning for the game and won't be involved in any aspects of pulling words, etc.
