From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-21 17:19:14 UTC
For those train buffs out there...

Steam Engine 261, one of the largest ever built, is coming back to
Milwaukee today (Wed) to give a few excursions over the weekend.
(It was built in Milwaukee and used by the Milwaukee Road.) As best
as I can tell, it will be traveling the CP tracks to the Amtrak
station in Milwaukee tonight as well as during the excursion. These
are the tracks that are near the following boxes of mine:

Mitchell Park Domes (MCP Series)
Watery Grave
Squished Pennies*
Hansen Park (MCP Series)*

*best viewing site

Right now, I'm planning to be at my Squished Pennies box tonight
around 5:30 to watch it come in if anyone wants to join me and get
some squished pennies from a STEAM train!

Here's the link to an article:
http://www.jsonline.com/story/index.aspx?id=436818

(who's hoping the weather cooperates!)
