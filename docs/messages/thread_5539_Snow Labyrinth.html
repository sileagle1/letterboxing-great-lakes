Snow Labyrinth

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-11 06:28:59 UTC
When it snows this much, there's only one thing to do. Make a snow
labyrinth! See the photos on www.labyrinth-enterprises.com/snow.html
I made two snow labyrinths last year; one at GS camp and one in my
backyard. Tall glass jars with tea light or votive candles added along
the path of the labyrinth add a magical feel if you walk the path at
night. OOOOO! You can also create tiny arrangements along the way
with unique rocks, crystals, shells, long thick icicles, fallen
branches, and other items found in nature. Creativ fun with snow!
