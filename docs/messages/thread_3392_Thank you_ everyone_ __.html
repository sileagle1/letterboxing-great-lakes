From: junk4joann (junk4joann@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-03 14:20:34 UTC
Thank you to everyone! It was the first gathering my mom and I have
ever attended and we enjoyed it a lot. I hope you all liked the little
frozen gingerbread cookies. :) I think it's great how many moms are
into letterboxing.

I swear I will not complain about the heat at a summer gathering. I
had to leave early due to extreme shivers. I was more like
LibraryBrrrrrrrl! So, in a few minutes I'm on my way out there again
to find a few more boxes and collect my Gingerbread Boxes so they can
go out in a postal ring. The ring members were kind enough to let me
put the stamps at the gathering for a double use! THis hobby is a huge
improvement over the hobby of baking -- which adds calories to my
life, and now makes my dumb smoke alarm go off every time I turn on
the very clean oven....but that's another story.

I didn't get to exchange with everybody, so I'll for sure be coming to
future gatherings. Since I'm new to letterboxing, it felt like meeting
so many friends whose stamps I've seen in all the letterboxes we find!
You are all like famous people to my mom! Hehehe.

Until next time!
LibraryGrrl.
