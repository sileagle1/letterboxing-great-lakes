Placing boxes today!

From: bindlestiff_boxer (bindlestiff_boxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-25 17:57:34 UTC
The plan for the day was for my daughter and I to get started
placing some of the boxes for "THE FATE OF EASTER" series.

We had some boxes from one of those Dollar Store places that passed
the bathtub test so we were going to use them. (Fill box with rocks,
place lid on, sink in a full bathtub and leave for at least an
hour ... if it's dry inside when opened after this, it can be used
for boxing. LOL)

However, they have BRIGHT WHITE lids and we're thinking of using
parks around the GR area, so we expect heavy traffic in the area. My
daughter begged to be given time to "fix them up" (note: when
negoating with me, I don't hold up will to excessive repetiations of
the same request)and promised just to use whatever she could find
around here.

Long story short ... she covered them with black gesso and used
matte acrylics in a sort of camoflague pattern then covered it all
with a couple of coats of fluid matte medium. I'll take a picture
and upload it later.

I was thinking the stuff would scrape right off as she didn't sand
them or do any prep work on them. They did have a nice roughed up
texture on portions though. So far our "test scratchings" haven't
removed their camo coating.

We're going out now to get some of these six boxes hidden. The goal
is to have the series ready for this weekend. (We'll see how we do
with that.)

Enjoy!
