Finding a box without a stamp has only happened once to us. It
happened to be a geocache--letterbox combo so I assume that a
geocacher took the stamp at some point (which is the reason why we
don't look for these combos--don't want to get burned again). We
were bound and determined to get some sort of "stamp" from it--so we
inked up the bottom of the container (it was a microbox) and stamped
that "image" in. As far as counting yours as a FIND--I'm sure there
would be a variety of answers to that. I'd say--it is your PFX
count, so do what you like with it. It's not like the letterboxing
police are going to confiscate your logbook ;~)

I like your idea about tidying up your images with your markers. I
have some stamp images with those whited-out areas, so I may have to
give that a try.
