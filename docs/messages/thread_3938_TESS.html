From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-12 13:55:18 UTC
Well, the one sure way to record them is to stamp them in your logbook.

As one who long ago stopped recording
stamps/events/placements/finds/exchanges *anywhere* except in my logs,
I am curious about the desire to have it online as well. B ut I
understand that others have their own needs.

The TESS series was created for some event organizers to access stamps
for their events. There was great debate about whether throwing HHs on
the table with 'official' events stamps was appropriate and some box
creators did not want their stamps left out for general consumption.
Hence, TESS and a series *meant* to be shared at an event.

So, there they are.

I carved one for that series and it is in my logbook in the section
where I keep stamps that I've carved. They're not all letterbox stamps
for boxes I've planted -- some have been planted by others.
But, unlike boxes I've planted -- for which I continue to claim some
ownership -- the stamps are "out there". Their home, among other
places, is the TESS collection.


I hope the attendees enjoy the fun collection.


azobox
