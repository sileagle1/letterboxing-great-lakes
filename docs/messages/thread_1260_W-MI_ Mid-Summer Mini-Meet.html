W-MI: Mid-Summer Mini-Meet

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-05-02 09:39:45 UTC
Okay, mark your calendars -- Saturday, July 23, 2005 is the date for
the mid-summer mini-meet at Muskegon State Park in North Muskegon,
MI. This will be in the Channel Campground area. OldShoe and I are
camping there for the weekend, so we will use our campsite as the
pitstop -- I may check into renting a roof over our head just in
case it rains.

I am still tossing around ideas for event name/theme and will carve
up an event stamp.

This will be a VERY INFORMAL get-together. We will meet in the
morning at the campground to get to know everyone and exchange
stamps. The rest of the day will then be about letterboxing. Since
everyone has done different boxes and has interest in different
boxes, this is an "on your own" or with 2-3 other people kind of
thing. The intention is not for 16 people to go heading down any
one trail together at one time. There are several boxes already in
Muskegon State Park and the surrounding areas -- the
Whitehall/Montague area is only a short drive up the shoreline from
Muskegon State Park. Please check out clues on the LbNA web site,
AtlasQuest, etc. to plan the boxes you are interested in. I will
also put out a few new boxes in the area in anticipation of the
event. Anyone else is encouraged to do so also. There will NOT be
an organized potluck/meal -- there are many restaurants within the
area and picnic areas at the park. Everyone is on their own as to
what fits best into their letterboxing time table. In the evening
we will group back up for a while to enjoy a campfire at our
campsite.

No RSVP necessary at this time -- I may want an idea when we get
closer to the date, but we will be there regardless. Hope you can
make it!

SpringChick
