Okay, I guess it is safe to regal my story to the group.

I read the clues to this one day only box and was sad that I would be at
the Calhoun County fair all day and would not be
able to hunt down this box. I managed to get all of the clues deciphered at
the time except the name... as my husband and I were
"silently communicating" that evening I stopped him to ask if "FE" meant
anything to him.. he didn't understand what that had to do with
what we were doing at that moment but I couldn't get it out of my mind.. he
told me what he thought and I knew he was right!
Thursday night we are coming home from having dinner at Hooters in Kzoo and
I see a horse in black clothing from the state of 10k lakes with
the name "FE GT".., (the clues!) I screamed!!! Hubby asked what the problem
was and I explained that we had just passed the Woodward Dream Cruise box
before it had reached its location.. I made him (I was driving) write a
message on paper and put it to the window.. I was trying to get them to
pull over as a rest area was coming up, hoping they would trade stamps with
me. I passed them while flashing my interior light and hubby holding the
paper to the window. They must have thought I was a complete nut case! I
guess it was too dark to read the note and they flashed their lights back
at me and continued on their way... I was bummed! but excited at the same
time..
Way too cool, PPP
