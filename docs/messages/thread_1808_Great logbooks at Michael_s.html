Great logbooks at Michael's

From: beautifulpeople99 (jennifer.mccready@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-09-10 12:10:02 UTC
B and I stopped at Michael's last night and, as usual, I started
searching for good logbooks to place in boxes. I usually strike out in
this quest, but for once, I found perfect books! They are small enough
to fit in a gladware/tupperware container, have blank pages, and even
have a little matching pen. And the best part? They are $1/each. I'm
not sure if these were available at just our Michael's or if they are
available at every store, but it might be worth checking out. They are
in the $1 bins/sale items near the checkout. We stocked up on them... I
hope everybody else can, too; they are quite the deal!

Thriftily,
