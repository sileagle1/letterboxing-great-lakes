From: (katy@dsa132.org) | Date: 2006-11-08 18:29:58 UTC
I started counting the Illinois counties without boxes when the MI/WI
challenge first came up and stopped at 35 because it was too depressing.
I just have to remind myself how very few boxes there were in the central
region of the state when I started boxing (4 listings, I think) and
compare that to the 88 listings, some with multiple boxes, that is there
now. It's so nice to be able to go letterboxing without having to drive
several hours.

I don't know where you are in IL, Trail Rider, but we have a pretty
healthy population of boxers in Champaign county. Not nearly so big a
population as there seems to be in the Chicago area, but enough that
we've started talking about a winter mini-meet to put names to faces and
stamps. Sure beats the days when I was convinced that Kimono Girl and I
were the only boxers in the area!

Katy
