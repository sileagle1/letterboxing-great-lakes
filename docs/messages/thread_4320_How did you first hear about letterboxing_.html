How did you first hear about letterboxing?

From: blueskyinkazoo (blueskyinkazoo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-02 17:19:38 UTC
Been following the recent posts and concerns over the new popularity of
LB. I can certianly see the reasoning for restricting some boxes to WOM
or other private venues, but it would be a shame to not have the
availability of public web sites. Personally, I was introduced to LB
through Family Fun and although I would like to do more LB (wouldn't we
all?) - personal commitements have limited time my spent on the trail,
however I follow the postings regularly and have tried to adhere to the
objectives when finding and placing boxes. Alhtough the gatherings
sound great, I don't forsee being able to attend one in the near future
and only know of one other family (who I just met last week) that
letterboxes. If LB were restricted just to WOM, etc, people like myself
that enjoy and respect it, but don't have the personal contacts yet,
would be very restricted.
Okay, all this brings me to my question - there seems to be a "new"
wave of interest in LB via Family Fun and some SUV ads, but how did
those of you who have been doing it awhile (1+ years) hear about it?
Just curious - kind of an informal poll!
Thanks!

Blue Sky in Kazoo

Re: [LbGLK] How did you first hear about letterboxing?

From: (kevjean@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-08-03 14:58:44 UTC
I actually found out about letterboxing when I was doing my yearly planning on going to the Ren Fair on the Ren Fair site. I saw it listed under performing arts, I believe, and I was curious as to what was "new" at the Ren Fair. To my shock, it wasn't new!! LOL From there, I read about it on the Letterboxing.org and AQ sites, and then, told my parents about it, and next thing we knew, we were out on the trail with 5 kids in tow at the time, looking for our 1st box! Luckily on our first adventure out, we ran into the Wormy Wanderers, and they also showed us how to hide the box, and what to do on the trails. We've had so much fun ever since. I think my mom is an addict though....she has to find at least 2 or 3 a week or she goes through withdrawals. LOL =)

Magazine.
