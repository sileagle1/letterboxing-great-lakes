Intro- Team Rabbit

From: Matthew (jedisquirrel@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-04 01:07:51 UTC
Hello everyone!

This is BrownRabbit here... My wife (who goes by GrayRabbit) and I
would like to thank you, SpringChick, and all of you who have made
the last year so fun and memorable for us. We've been 'boxing since
last May (I believe a week AFTER the last gathering), and have had so
many adventures. After reading about the hobby in the Freep,
GrayRabbit surprised me with an anniversary-trip to the Traverse City
area. Now every trip or vacation we take has some 'boxing elements to
it ("gotta catch 'em all").

It's as if we know some of you, just from past logs you've made that
we've found. We feel part of a family, and we can grow together.
Our part of the state (metro Detroit) has a LOT of growing to do yet.
We're newbies. Slowly, but surely...

We'll have to keep our travels short for a while, however, due to an
addition to Team Rabbit: our daughter was born on March 23! We're
sure she'll like 'boxing (I'm currently working on her stamp)! La
Famiglia Gonzalez! Delirium! We need some more family-friendly hunts
out here! (We're working on some too). In the meantime, our hunts
will be virtual...

Once again, pleased to meet you all. Thank you, SpringChick, for the
invitation!

Matt & Renee
Team Rabbit

p.s. Tim the Dragon: Yes, we WERE excited to see the Easter series.
A very rabbity series, indeed!
