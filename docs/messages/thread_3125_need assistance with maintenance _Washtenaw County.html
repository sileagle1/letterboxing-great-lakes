need assistance with maintenance (Washtenaw County, MI)

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@letterboxing.info) | Date: 2006-09-24 14:16:41 UTC-04:00
With TJ Mich heading out of state, I need some assistance maintaining
the April Fool's box in Hudson Mills, MI
(http://www.letterboxing.info/box/marty/aprilfools.php). This was the
first box planted in MI and is one of the oldest letterboxers in the
US, now 7 years old. I "adopted" it a couple of years ago (along with
the original planter's boxes in PA & NJ) and have been working to
keep it alive and well with TJ Mich's assistance.

This box gets a fair amount of visitors, and could use some
maintenance pretty soon -- I think the container and log may need to
be replaced. I'll be happy to provide a replacement log and container
if someone could offer to plant it and occasionally check in on the
box. Since this box is by now a historical relic (in letterboxing, at
least!), you'd be doing a great service!

Please contact me directly by email if you can help out.

Thanks!

SD
