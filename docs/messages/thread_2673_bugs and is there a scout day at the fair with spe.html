The Asian long horn beetle is all over Wisconsin near camps we have visited, and it is a different type of bug then the green ash one found lately in Northern Il, that one is found in Michigan usually.
The long horns are actually really cool to see if you don't know how destructive they can be, they have a metallic body, and we saw them in many locations a couple summers ago. Once we realized what they were, and saw how wide spread they were, it was not so much fun to discover them.
