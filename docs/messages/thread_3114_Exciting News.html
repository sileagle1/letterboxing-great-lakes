Exciting News

From: Paula Collins (boxer@cinemaboxers.com) | Date: 2006-09-21 00:56:34 UTC
Some time back, months actually - I had asked a location for
permission to plant several Boxes there - a very public location
that I assumed would LIKELY say No, but what the heck.

Well, I emailed them initially explaining letterboxing and its
attitude towards the preservation of the surroundings, being
discreet, and what its all about. I got a reply about a week later
asking about the boxes, would it be disruptive for people to search
for them, etc...

I replied, and heard nothing for about a month or so.

Well... a few days ago, I got an email granting permission for
myself and my husband to go to the location and plant several Boxes
INSIDE for people to seek! We will be working with the zoo on the
boxes and locations, but expect them to be up somewhat soon!

So... watch for some NEW boxes soon at...

BROOKFIELD ZOO!!!!!!!!!! They WILL be educational, and animal
related, as per our discussion with our contact, but thats even
better!


Their actual words were "Sorry this has taken so long for a
response. Brookfield Zoo would be happy to be a sight."

VERY COOL!

IM VERY EXCITED!!!!! And a BIG thank you to the Zoo - please support
the zoo when you can for supporting our hobby!

Paula, Jim and Joel
CinemaBoxers
