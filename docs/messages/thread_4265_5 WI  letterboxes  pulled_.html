From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-24 01:09:13 UTC-06:00
To my letterboxing friends in LbGLK,

I just returned from a short, but relaxing vacation. I checked my out of state boxes and I have decided to 'pull' all 5 boxes in the Door County, WI area. Why? Vandalism....plain and simple. The last entries in the logbooks were first time letterboxing families with young, obviously unsupervised children. Stamps were damaged, boxes left open, contents tossed on the ground. YIKES! I may replace these boxes in the future. Just wanted to give you the update change on the status of my boxes in WI.

Beetle
