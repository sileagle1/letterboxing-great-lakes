From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2010-03-30 16:57:19 UTC-05:00
Hartx6 has an on-line tutorial about carving, as do a few other folks. If
you haven't already looked at them, they might be of interest to you.



Unlike other people, I've never had any luck with heat transfer. However I
usually do quite well with using acetone on a toner image (from a laser
printer or copy machine that uses old style toner). I simply lay the image
(face down) on top of the carving media, then firmly PAT (not rub) the back
of the image with a cloth dampened w/acetone.
