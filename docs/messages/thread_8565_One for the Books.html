Thank you, thank you, thank you, thank you, thank you to aimlesst, LibraryGrrl, shooting starz, punxsygal, and Twila75 for a wonderful Great Lakes Gathering!  We enjoyed the company, the hikes, the carves, the amazing weather, and everything else.  The Little Free Library was a great feature!  Extra kudos to the team for the Choose Your Own Adventure feature in the clue booklet.  It was great to know just where to transfer from one boxing trail to another to extend the hike and then be able to transfer back to finish the first trail.
