Oh, me, me! After making, writing out, and addressing my Lunar New Year's cards (made with the boar stamp I carved last night) I put on my snow suit and took the dogs to the forest preserve. Talk about cold! The dogs had little balls of slobber frozen to the fine whiskers around their lips and the wind was so cold it blew right through my snow suit! By the time we left I had a grimace frozen to my face and my nose was so cold you could have pierced it and I wouldn't have noticed. But I wasn't the only crazy one, we met a new friend and her two frozen owners on our short hike. The dogs, by the way, didn't take any notice of the cold and would have been happy to spend another 40 minutes out on the trails.

Then I went and spent this evening making myself a brand new bright yellow (fleece!) kimono to keep myself warm for the rest of the winter. Now all I need are some fleece-lined tabi socks and I'll be all set. In the mean-time I'll have to make due with the warm wool socks Flying Dragon knit me for Christmas. Bring on the cold, I'm ready for it!

Kimono Girl

Yes, Beetle was at Girl Scout camp this weekend for an adults only event called Sno-Joke. BRRRR! It was cold! It was too cold to go snowshoeing around the property, so we stayed indoors where it was warm and sipped coffee, tea, and hot chocolate.
