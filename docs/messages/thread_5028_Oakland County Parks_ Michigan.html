Oakland County Parks, Michigan

From: heather (heatherstreets@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-05-07 03:56:55 UTC
A few Oakland County Parks have designated "Geocaching Trailways".
I contacted them to find out, if we had an event at those parks could
we plant boxes. The rules on their website state you have to get a
permit, and be a member of the Michigan Geocaching Organization to
plant a cache.

They called me back today and told me that they were very interested
in letterboxing and were looking for someone to talk to them about it.
So I set a meeting up with them for Monday 10am.
Hopefully I can answer all their questions and we'll be able to
letterbox at Oakland County Parks!

They have a link on their website.
http://www.oakgov.com/parksrec/activities/geocaching.html

If any of you have experience dealing with your county/state advice
would be helpful.
