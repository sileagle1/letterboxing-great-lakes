Re: So what IS the story of Scheherezade?

From: scheherezadebox (scheherezade1@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-27 22:50:59 UTC

Aahhh, The story of the beautiful and wise Scheherezade.
It begins with a king so consumed with fear and hatred for women, that
he decides to marry a new every night and then have them murdered!
All the women in the Harem are terrified, the women in the city
whisper fear filled gossip.
The kings Vizier has a daughter named Scheherezade. She hears the
terrible news and tells her father that she will marry the king!
The king marries Scheherezade and that night she tells him a wonderful
story and just before dawn she begins another story but, she has grown
tired and "just wait until tomarrow night" she says.
She tells him stories for 1,001 nights until he is cured of his fear
and hatred and they live happily ever after.
Thus, we have "Arabian Nights" by Burton
Oh I do so love stories and that is why I chose the name and my
letterboxes that I place will reflect my love of all tales!
Scheherezade
