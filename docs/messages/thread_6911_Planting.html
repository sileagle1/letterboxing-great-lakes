Good morning Packer letterboxers! As promised, I am posting the schedule for
locations of the "superbowl XL: Packers vs Steelers" letterbox. To get this
letterbox, simply show up where One Love Journeying and I are, locate us (I
will be wearing this season's Packers knit sideline hat and OLJ will be
wearing a Jennings home game jersey) and ask us if we are members of "the G
Force"! :-)

Aaaaand!! As a special bonus for ur effort in finding us, we are offering my
"in enemy territory" box (usually only available at enemy stadiums during
the game weekends). All u have to do to gain this stamp is tell us the years
that the Packers have won the Superbowl!

So... start planning ur Packer Superbowl Sunday! Here is where we will be...

8a-10a: 1020 firethorn dr, west bend, wi 53090
11a-11:45a: location of OLJ's "for the love of starbucks" box (great time to
pick up this box as well if u haven't yet!)
3p-5p: currently undecided... we may be in search of a clever Chinese
rabbit... will update this time further when we know more
5p-end of game: Bluemound Bowl, Brookfield, WI (note: we are not likely to
be bowling during the game... look hard for us... we will be here
celebrating and probably shouting at some big tv screens with a crowd of
other Green and Gold fans!)

Looking forward to seeing/meeting you!
Don't forget to bring ur stamp for exchanges!

Go Pack Go!
