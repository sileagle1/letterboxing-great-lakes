From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-09-09 09:40:25 UTC-05:00
Fall is my favorite time of year, being an October baby and also an October
bride. This being my first fall to box, I will be continuing my pre-boxing
tradition of taking my GS to camp (how else did I discover all those cool
places to hide my LaSalle county boxes?), my favorite fall location.

My first boxing experience was late winter, March '04, when I visited
DebRock's Bass Ponds boxes near Bloomington, MN. No snow, so landmarks were
visible, boxes were not frozen solid, footsteps were not an issue, and the
starkness of the greyed landscape very surreal and, as others have said,
serene. I don't know if I'd make a concerted effort to go boxing in the
snow (for all the very good reasons previously mentioned), but if
opportunity provided me a box within reach of where I was, I certainly would
take it.
