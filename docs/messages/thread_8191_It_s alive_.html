From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-05-10 22:20:02 UTC
Went out letterboxing today for mother's day.  As part of the outing, we finally checked on the "Yes, You Are" box which was part of the state-wide Holy Grail series from way back in, oh, 2005.  We'd gotten reports over the years that the hiding spot had been compromised, but this is the first time we actually were able to make the time to go check.

And the box was still there!  Part of the tree where we had hidden the box had disintegrated with time, and the box was totally visible to anyone who walked past - but it was still there!  Ten years later???  How has it been ten years already?  The logbook had become soaked through, but the stamp is still OK, so we took it home to clean it up and we'll rehide it hopefully much sooner than it took us to finally check on it.

Doodle Bug.
