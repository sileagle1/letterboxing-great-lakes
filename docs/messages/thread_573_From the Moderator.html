Okay, I have see enough of this topic. There are obviously
differences of opinion, which is fine. But there is no need for
blatant disregard for tradition or the way letterboxing is currently
done in the U.S.

Everyone seems to agree on one thing -- letterboxing offers
something for everyone. So take what suits you and try to do so
without disrespect for the person next to you or the way that they
choose to play the game.

This has until now been a peaceful group, a nice respite from the
flames and discontent on the big list, and I would like to keep it
that way. I would appreciate no more posts on this thread and will
delete any that do come through.

Thanks.

SpringChick
