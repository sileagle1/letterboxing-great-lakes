New Great Lakes patches have arrived!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-12-13 05:58:04 UTC
Hi!

The new patches have arrived! I had already received money and self-
addressed envelopes from several people, so the first 50 patches will
be in the mail tomorrow.

If you entered your name in the database on this message board with a
patch request, I will hold the patches for you until mid-January.
[Note: I deleted the database after I exported it to a spreadsheet to
track the requests.] If I don't receive your money and envelope by
January 15th, I'll assume you changed your mind and will then make
the patches available to someone else. If you didn't have your name
in the database, please contact me before sending any money, to be
sure I have enough patches to satisfy your request.

Here's a link to see the final patch and get ordering info:
http://letterboxing.homestead.com/greatlakespatch.html

(Please don't forget the self-addressed envelope)


Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. Please include your trailname when you send your $ and envelope
so I know who you are. Can the following people that already sent
money also send me a note with your trailname so I can cross you off
the list? (Your patches will be mailed tomorrow, but since I didn't
recognize your "real name", I couldn't associate it with your trail
name).

out...

From: ginjoliet69 (mgdudek@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-12-15 21:40:35 UTC
I am sitting at my daughter's school today, having rehearsals for the
Christmas Concert, and thinking, "Why do I do this to myself?" The
Preschoolers and Kindergarten sing louder than the 8th graders (and
they can read and have the song book in their hands). My head is
throbbing, and my phone rings. It's the husband. Can't be good if
he's calling in the middle of the day.

"What?" I snap!
"I picked up the mail"
"So?"
"Our patches are here"

Patches? OOOOOhhh, PATCHES! I smile. The children keep singing,
and all I am thinking is where I am placing the patches and will I
get in trouble if I end practice a little early. Nope. We have
practice again Monday! I sent them back to class, took an Excedrin
with Pepsi - it was that bad, and went home and looked at my
beautiful patches. Headache gone!

The Traveling Dudeks
