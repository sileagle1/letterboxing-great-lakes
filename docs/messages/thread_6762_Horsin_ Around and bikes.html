From: Mothkovich, Chris (chris@mothkovich.com) | Date: 2010-10-13 07:11:14 UTC-05:00

The paved bike trail does have some hills. Eight letterboxes are planted
along one section of the paved trail; most the rest of the boxes are on
UNpaved trails which do not allow bikes. Six boxes are planted on a
3/4-mile loop of unpaved trail that DOES allow bikes. Don't know if this
info is of much help to you or not. Please feel free to email me with any
other questions.

Trail Rider/Chris
