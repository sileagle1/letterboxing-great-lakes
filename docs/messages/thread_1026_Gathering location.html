Gathering location

From: Matthew & Renee (jedisquirrel@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-16 02:44:01 UTC

Hello everyone!

Sorry if we kept you waiting...

Our scouting is done - the weekend weather certainly helped! Please
come one and all to the 2005 Great Lakes Gathering: Mo' Boxin' In
Motown on June 18, at Hudson Mills Metropark. A $4 entrance fee is
required, but we promise lots of fun and treasures to find (and, as
an added bonus - MI's VERY FIRST letterbox!). The park is easily
accessable from both US-23 and I-94...

Visit this link for a map (left side of the page). Our shelter is
#5, the secluded "Pine View".

http://www.metroparks.com/parks/pk_hudson_mills.php

Thank you to TJ_Mich, Dragon, and the Cichlid Guy in helping narrow
the many choices down to just one!

As always,
Team Rabbit
at your service
