Hi, We go by the trail name "The Pirates" and live in the Parma, Ohio area. We have only been letterboxing since mid August 2006. There are 4 of us: my wife and I and 2 sons. We found out about letterboxing by accident. We were looking for a fun day at our local park and visited the website to see if there were any events. We saw a link on the website to "Geocaching" and thought how cool it sounded. We do not have a GPS unit but I Googled the co-ordinates and thought I could find one in a nearby park. I found out The "little park" is huge when you don't know where to look, but in the search we found a letterbox totally by accident in a tree. Well that led us the letterboxing.org and we got some clues and headed out that evening for our first hunt (with clues). Since then we have placed 4 letterboxes of our own and found probably 10 others. We are still working on our carving skills and our first boxes were store bought stamps.
Hope to see some of you on the trails!
