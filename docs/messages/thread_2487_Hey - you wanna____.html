From: Doglvrs (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2006-05-08 17:05:32 UTC
Hello all,

I'm cooking up a new box series based on Classic books involving dogs hmmmmm
wonder why I would pick dogs.

It would be most excellent to have a conglomeration of Great Lakes letterboxers work
involved in this series so if you are interested in carving one of these titles, that would be
TOTALLY AWESOME!
I have 9 titles chosen and have pictures picked for each ~ however I am NOT attached to
any of these pictures, so the ultimate choice is up to you. The only requirement is your
stamp must PLEASE include the book title.
It will not be necessary to include logbooks, as I am a logbook junkie, and can provide
those for all the boxes.
Each contributing individual will receive a special surprise involving all the stamped
images for this series.
When completed, I will be placing this series at Hemlock Crossings County Park in West
Olive, Michigan. I want to have the series up and running no later than July 4th so no
procrastinating allowed. : )

The 9 titles are:
The Hound of the Baskervilles ~ Sir Arthur Conan Doyle
The Call of the Wild ~ Jack London
White Fang ~ Jack London
Where the Red Fern Grows ~ Wilson Rawls
Shiloh ~ Phyllis Reynolds Naylor
Sounder ~ William H. Armstrong
Cujo ~ Stephen King
Old Yeller ~ Fred Gipson
The Incredible Journey ~ Sheila Burnford

If you are interested or have any questions, please contact me off list at doglvrs_4 at msn
dot com

Thanks!

Doglvrs
