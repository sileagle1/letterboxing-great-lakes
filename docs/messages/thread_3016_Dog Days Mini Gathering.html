Dog Days Mini Gathering

From: Pale Puppy Posse (bleubreac@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-28 16:41:14 UTC
Well we couldn't have picked a more humid day to go letterboxing! Ms.
Doglvrs, the skull, Spartan fans, the hoot owl, pale puppy posse and
Billy Bad Boy were all in attendance. Spartan fans brought an
additional package which made the trails a little harder for them but
the hunt was on!

We found two stamps hidden in honor of the event as well as tackling
Azoboxes "Breakfast Elves". "Too the rescue" will have to be rehidden
as Ms. Doglvrs informed me that it was in the middle of a poison ivy
patch! It didn't look like any I had ever seen before but she assured
me it was. But Spartan fan had pants on so she took the plunge! Bird
dog was a delight to everyone. These clues will be put on the LB
sight for everyone to enjoy soon. The Breakfast elves had a little
different spin. Snap was safe but his house was damp. We wiped him
out as best as possible and returned him. We didn't notice on the
clues that Crackle was MIA but because we didn't know we searched for
him anyway and some how found him and rescued him for the jaws of a
toothy creature. The monster ate part of his hat! Pop's house was
completely flooded! Azobox please contact me off line so I can return
them home to you.

It was really great to see my old friends and meet a few new ones too!
