From: Shutterfly (angi0105@aol.com) | Date: 2005-03-17 00:57:53 UTC

I checked on my 151 Questions box and, miracle of miracles, it made
it through the winter. It is currently frozen to the ground, but it
is still there. I pulled the lid back a ways, so it is possible to
stamp in if you were thinking of finding it.

My letterboxing bug has been satisfied for the moment. I got to walk
through the mud and get my coat full of moss and other good stuff.
And it only took 10 minutes! Wiener Mouse would be proud of me.

Now, if only it stays nice enough to go check on the others...
