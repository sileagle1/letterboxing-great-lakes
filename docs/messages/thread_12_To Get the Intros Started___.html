From: Deb (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-03-23 10:44:18 UTC
I go by the trail name of SpringChick, live in West Michigan, and
have been letterboxing for about a year and a half. Sometimes my
other half, OldShoe, is my hiking buddy, but most often I go it
alone, which I actually enjoy. When I first began boxing there were
just a couple of boxes over here on the west side of the state (in
the Saugatuck area). Since there wasn't much to find, I planted.
Since then the involvement has increased significantly and there are
several boxes in the area I have yet to find.

Most of the boxes I have found and planted are in the West Michigan
area, although we have found a few around Chicago and I took an
extended trip to the Mid-Atlantic area where I found several boxes
in OH, NJ, DE and PA, and planted a few along the way. We also
planted several boxes in the U.P. which we have heard very little
about since they were put in place over a year ago. We took a trip
to Door County, WI just prior to getting involved with letterboxing,
but are talking about heading back that way this summer, and I look
forward to hunting for some boxes over on the other side of the lake.

For me letterboxing combines several interests -- hiking, stamp
artistry, puzzles... and gives me that little extra push I need to
get out on the trail sometimes. In addition to letterboxing, I
enjoy photography, writing and do various types of artwork. In the
short time I have been boxing, I've met some really wonderful people
and look forward to the acquaintences yet to come.

I have a letterboxing web site, Stamp-in-the-Box here:

http://www.confessionsofdecember.com/lbna/

Nice to meet you all!

SpringChick
