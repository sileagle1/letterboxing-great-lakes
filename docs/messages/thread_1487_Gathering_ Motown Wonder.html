From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-06-21 09:54:54 UTC-05:00
Hey everyone - I just heard from TJMich who said after they found the
Motown Wonder box they were stopped by a Metropark policeman on the way
back to their car. Apparently parking at the canoe camp area is only
for authorized group campers. In fact, the policeman said the driveway
is usually locked.

So..... if you go looking for this box you should probably park in the
Rapids View area and then, on foot, follow the paved pathway north,
then through the camping area to get to the Canoe Camp area.
Apparently it is "legal" to be at that end of the park -- they just
don't want anyone parking there.

Wisconsin Hiker
