We also checked on a cuckoo clue box that I planted way back in 2007.  Amazingly it was still there, but not in good shape.  It seems an animal had chewed off an entire side of the box, but luckily someone had put the whole thing into a large Ziploc bag. Nut surprisingly, the logbook was pretty sodden and moldy, but mostly readable and the stamp seems fine.  I'll be making a new logbook and putting it all into a new container to put it back out.  So if by any remote chance you actually have one of the copies of the cuckoo clue in your possession, don't look for it  until I post that it is back in place.


And lastly the worst news - Martini Man's very first "Musical Mystery Tour" box seems to be MIA.  He may decide to recarve and replant it.  If he does, he'll post on this board when it is back in place.
