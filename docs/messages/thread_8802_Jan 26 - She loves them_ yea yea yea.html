Jan 26 - She loves them, yea yea yea

From: Adam E (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2019-01-26 07:44:35 UTC-06:00
The long and winding clues
That leads to her box
Will never disappear
I've tried those clues before
It always leads me here
Lead me to her box!
The wild and windy night
That the rain washed away
Has left a pool of tears
Crying for the day
Why leave me standing here
Let me know the way!
Many times I've been alone
And many times I've cried
Any way you'll never know
The many ways I've tried
And still they lead me back
To the long winding clues
She led me wandering here
A long long time ago
Don't leave me waiting here
Lead me to her box
But still they lead me back
To the long winding clues
She left me standing here
A long long time ago
Don't keep me waiting here
Lead me to her box!
N - Beetle
