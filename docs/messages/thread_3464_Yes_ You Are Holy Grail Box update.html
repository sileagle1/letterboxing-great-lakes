After Deedle & I left the 'Boxers in your Neighborhood
mini-meet this afternoon (shout out to Travel'n

box. This box is now camo-duct taped to make it less

geocache is (we thought we found the only good hiding
spot for a box in that park!) and determine if a
relocation will be called for.

We'll keep you posted...
