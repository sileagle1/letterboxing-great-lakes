Team Red Rose Reigns Regal in Race

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-12 11:19:35 UTC
The Ann Arbor, MI Team Red Rose has completed the "Around the World in
80 Days" series just before the snow began flying around Michigan.
Completing the journey before their footprints could be tracked on the
ground at the fifteen ports of call, Team Red Rose appears to be the
final finishers of the 80 Days series.
And TRR did the trek in time to qualify for the bonus box.
Congratulations, Team Red Rose.

As noted earlier, the Passport Office has closed. The Travel Staff has
packed, and the fifteen `foreign' city's sites are shutting their
gates as well. Thanks to all who participated.

Congratulations to Team Red Rose and the many other successful visitors.
For a list of the previous travelers, see:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_travelers.asp

The (retiring) Travel Team,

SpringChick
azobox
