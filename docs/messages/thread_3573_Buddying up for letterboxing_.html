Re: Buddying up for letterboxing?

From: sbostik (bozbaskt@shout.net) | Date: 2007-01-13 14:02:47 UTC
I'm in Central IL and we do that around here. I go with my Mom,
Sister-in-Law or Step-Daughter, while Daddy (or Grandpa) watch my
little one. He goes with us a lot, but if the weather
or "conditions" are questionnable, he stays home.

I happend to hit one box, in the dark, by myself because it had been
placed near a town I was working in for a couple weeks, I knew of
the Placer's work and wanted to try & be first finder (which I
was...LOL!) I didn't have to go into the woods very far, but in
retrospect it wasn't very smart.

It's not only smart to go with someone else for your safety, it's
FUN to share the "thrill of the hunt!"

Hope you're able to find someone & if you're ever heading to Central
IL to hit the *many* that we have here, shoot me an email, I'd be
happy to tag along for company.

A quick P.S. - take a look at the Events Calendar. We're having a
Holiday Survivor Mini Meet in Urbana, next Sunday, Jan 21st. You
can check out all the details there.

Good Luck!

Basket Case
