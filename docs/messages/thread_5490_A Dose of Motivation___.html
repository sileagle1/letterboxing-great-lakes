A Dose of Motivation...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-12-30 14:54:33 UTC
While Bingo is a fun way to help pass the snowy, cold winter months
here in the Great Lakes area, there are other, more productive things
to be doing with that time also -- namely carving letterbox stamps!

Once the holidays have passed and the cold has set in, it's a great
time to pull out all those ideas and images and get some stamps carved
for boxes to plant once the weather warms up in the spring.

This past week I spent a few days doing some major clean-up on my
computer, including consolidating and organizing the thousands of BW
images I've saved in various places on 3 different computers over the
past few years -- after deleting duplicates and discarding some of
poor quality there are 3796 images left! Talk about an overdose of
inspiration! If you need ideas or inspiration, just hunt up some of
the image directories online... and happy carving!
