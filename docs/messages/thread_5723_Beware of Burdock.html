Beware of Burdock

From: Carol (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2009-03-16 03:45:18 UTC
Warning: Beware of burdock...those porcupine-like balls that stick to your clothes like Velcro. We've all come in contact with those pesty things. But today, I got them in my hair!!!

Removal Tips: If they are dry and crunchy, break them apart into smaller pieces. Don't try to pull them out of your hair. Next, use your fingers to slowly remove the individual seeds from your hair. You might have to wiggle them around while you slowly work them out of very small sections of your hair. Of course, this also depends on the length of your hair. Then, comb the last of the remaining crumbs out of your hair. Another reason to wear a hat or bandana while hiking!
So frustrating!!!

---Beetle
