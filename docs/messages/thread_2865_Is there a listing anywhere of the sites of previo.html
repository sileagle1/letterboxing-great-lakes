Yup, on the group site, look under Files, then under Gatherings. There is a document called glg-faq.txt that has information about the Great Lakes Gatherings and a list of all the places they've been held.

Also on the group site, under Links, then Events & Gatherings, you can see information about several of the other gatherings in the area (past and future).

I thought there was a document somewhere that listed all past gatherings in one spot, but I can't seem to locate it just now. Maybe someone else knows.

Re: [LbGLK] Is there a listing anywhere of the sites of previous Great Lake gatherings?

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-01 09:15:36 UTC-04:00
I don't know of anything that lists all past gatherings that have been held
in the region. If the host created a folder for info in the FILES section
of the group or a link to a gathering web site in the LINKS (and left them
there after their gathering), there is a record of past gatherings, but it
is by no means a comprehensive collection.

Other individuals may have a record of past gatherings on their personal web
sites -- for instance, I list the gatherings I have previously attended and
link to the gathering info (it is remains available) at my web site
(http://www.stampinthebox.com/events.asp). But again, that is limited to
gatherings that I have personally planned/hosted or attended/planned to
attend.

I usually leave information on past gatherings that I have hosted available
at my web site as a historical reference for anyone planning a future
gathering, but I know a lot of others just delete the gathering web site
once the event is passed.

Gathering boxes are not always permanent, but those that are usually get
listed at LbNA after the event so they are then available for anyone to
find.

SpringChick
