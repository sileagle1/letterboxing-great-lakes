I have never used either of the PZcut products myself. I would highly recommend trying Speedball's Speedy Cut. It's off white in color, carves like butter and is reasonably priced. Orange PZ Kut! Let me tell you - >*&$%#!

I hate the stuff. I ordered 2 sheets of the A grade in the new orange.

Their website says same as old white stuff.

Don't believe them. I have been carving like crazy and the stuff is awful.

I complained to Justin Pease and he sent me 2 sheets in B grade white for
free - all I did was send the money for shipping.



Alafair stopped by our house a few nights ago and I showed her my carvings
and the flaking problems I was having.

She verified that the consistency was even different.

I've been able to manage it by constantly changing my carving nibs to sharp
one.

Once it gone - I'm through with it!
