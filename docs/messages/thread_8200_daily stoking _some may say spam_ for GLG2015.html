Just a few more days until GLG 2015!

To update yesterday's update, clues are currently at the printers and
will be sent out this evening to the early supporters.

In a few places here and on AQ, I might have incorrectly posted that the
shelter at the top of the hill is 'Prairie View'...it is actually called
'Valley View.'

If you are bringing some type of snack to the event, can a few of you
please leave them at the Valley View shelter at the top of the hill.
That shelter is near three trailheads and will support people coming off
the trail (and give us a bit more room to stretch out)

Are you ready to go boxing?!

If you are interested in any of the following, please email me directly
(aatom118 at gmail):

1. I'm still looking for a few people that would be willing to hold down
the fort in the Valley View shelter for an hour or two (sit and stamp
with folks but keep eyes on our stuff at the shelter) on Saturday.

2. Replanting any of the boxes in Illinois at a later date.

3. Helping pick up a loop of boxes on Sunday.

A of Atomic Beans
