From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-02-10 14:58:56 UTC-06:00
Hi!

You can get detailed info on the Bingo game by looking at files in the FILES
section of the message board. Basically it's a game SpringChick invented
for us to pass the long winters. This is the third year she is running the
game. To play, you needed to send some sort of "prize" to her by the end of
December and make out a customized BINGO card. She generally calls one word
a day until all all the winning combinations have been claimed. All the
prizes that were sent in are divided up by SpringChick and she then mails
them out to the various winners.
