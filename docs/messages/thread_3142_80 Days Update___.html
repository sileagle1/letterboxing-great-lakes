80 Days Update...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-09-29 21:12:21 UTC
Time is running short for those who are still trying to finish up
the 80 Days series. A couple of notes:

- If you are hoping to complete the entire series and have not yet
done Box #9, you will want to get there ASAP because this park
closes for the season at the end of September!

- Box #1 was verified as missing and has been replaced. New clues
can be found on the status page:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_status.asp

- As far as we are aware all boxes are currently in place. As of the
end of September, we will no longer replace boxes that go missing.
At the end of October, we will be picking up the boxes. We had
originally thought to leave them in place indefinitely and possibly
even post the clues for general availability but after consideration
decided against this for two reasons:

1) In fairness to the teams who worked the series from the passport,
we have decided not to make the boxes publicly available.
2) This was a very special series for us personally and we would
like to keep the boxes and logbooks rather than just allow them to
slowly disappear.

So, in short... we are no longer sending out new passports. If you
have a passport and still want to complete the series, you have
until the end of October, although if a box goes missing between now
and then, it will not be replaced. We will attempt to keep the
status page updated through the end of October to avoid unnecessary
trips to boxes that may be known as missing.

Oh, and if you have already completed the series... and I have heard
a rumor that another postcard is on the way to the travel office as
we speak... the bonus boxes are still making the rounds via US Mail.
You can check the World Travellers Page to see where they currently
are (and thus how long before they get to you):

http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_travelers.asp

Thanks to everyone who spent the time and energy (and gas money) to
give this series a whirl. We really enjoyed this adventure and all
of the positive comments we received from those who participated!

SpringChick & Azobox
