Don't all the best people have alliterative trailnames? Those
with 'T's are obviously the highest caliber! I find that when I
consider alternate trailnames, I keep trying on different pairs of
letters.

I considered ditching "TrailTroll" early on because of the negative
connection to "trolls". I chose the name because I collect the little
dolls with neon hair, but to a lot of other people, especially on the
internet, troll is a dirty word. However, as I considerd it, I decided
that TrailTroll fit me pretty well, and I had already come to identify
with it, so I'm keeping it!
