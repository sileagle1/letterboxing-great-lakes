2nd Anniversary!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-07-14 00:03:32 UTC
Just wanted to say that today is the 2nd anniversary of finding our
first letterbox (E-Trails in Madison, WI). Woo- Hoo!! We've had
loads of fun with this pasttime and are looking forward to much more!

As you might remember, we're planning a gathering in Wisconsin on OCT
1 and are already expecting 35 people. Hopefully a few more of you
will also be able to make it. I'm starting to spend a bit more time
on planning now, so I'd like to know if anyone has an interest in
an "Event T-shirt" and if anyone has any particular workshops they'd
like to attend or present themselves. I know this will be the first
gathering for several of the attendees, so even if something has been
done before, it will be new for many people. However if there isn't
a big interest, we don't have to have a shirt or workshops - hunting,
gabbing and eating is plenty of fun all by itself!

I know it's early to think about planting, but if you live nearby and
are creating a box, I encourage you to hide it yourself and either
send the clues to me for inclusion with other clues, or
post/distribute clues at the event. In the remote posssibility that
you discover a poker chip with a number on it when you're looking for
a spot for your box, please leave the chip there and choose another
spot. We're going to be scouting out locations and writing clues
well in advance of receiving the other boxes and will be using the
poker chips as "placeholders". (I may be out of town a fair amount
in the 2 weeks before the gathering, so need to get ready early.)

Of course I'm hoping that some of you will bring a WOM (Word of
Mouth) box - I've seen some very creative ones in the few gatherings
I've attended.

Thanks to all of you who have contributed to our fun the past 2 years!

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
