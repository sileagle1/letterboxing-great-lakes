Montmorency County: Mission Complete!

From: pennypenny_sl (PennyPenny@cablespeed.com) | Date: 2006-11-13 16:23:33 UTC
Hi friends,

A quick 4 hour trip Up North (lol) to place the first letterbox in Montmorency county is
now complete.

I found a cozy resting place for "The Owl's Nest" letterbox, and my mom was so excited to
have the chance to see the whole process up close: Painting the letterbox cammo...
transferring the image to the block, carving the stamp, testing and tweaking the stamp
with more carving, putting all the other various parts together for the box, then sealing it
up with it's "First Finder's" certificate snuggly in place and hiding it in the snow!

It was a fun opportunity to have a quiet weekend doing this hobby that I love but never
have much time to devote to! (I actually had none of my 3 kids with me.... woo hoo!)
Suffice it to say it was a nice break, and although so cold up there we still had a great
time. Thanks to SpringChick and TrailTroll for keeping this thing rolling to get all our
Michigan counties done! And a special thanks to TrailTroll who offered to take my box way
up there to plant: I almost took you up on it, TT, but got lucky to have my DH off work
long enough to allow me to get away for a day-and-a-half!

Have a great week everyone,

PennyPenny
South Lyon, MI
