Bingo Advisory

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2011-02-05 15:05:59 UTC
......The National Whiner Service has issued a Bingo ADVISORY for the Great Lakes region, beginning immediately......Bingo cards may be experiencing WHITE-OUT conditions, with LIMITED VISIBILITY of any terms being marked off......Isolated spots have received a BLIZZARD of Bingo Words or FLURRIES of BOS terms, but many areas are experiencing GLACIAL progress with their cards as they are impeded by DRIFTS of useless terms......There is the possibility of FROSTY expressions and mutterings of SLEET in the early morning after players check their cards......Players may also experience ICY demeanors and STORMY dispositions as they survey these cards......The word-caller should take precautions in this "WINTER of our Discontent" as conditions may be HAZARDOUS......FROSTBITE is a possibility from the COLD shoulders of unhappy players......We urge all players to stay in the game, as the FREEZE may break at any time, with desired terms "BLOWIN' in the WIND"......If you should experience an AVALANCHE of terms, be prepared with a SHOVEL for all of your Bingo Bootie......Again, the National Whiner Service has issued a Bingo ADVISORY for the Great Lakes region, beginning immediately (and lasting until I get a Bingo!)......
