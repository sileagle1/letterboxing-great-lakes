...when the perks of your job are the supply of specimen cups, centrifuge tubes, and small ziplock bags the bottles of reagents come in. Let's see what else...oh, that self-adhesive cardstock used for covering microtiter plates is great for stamp mountings. Scalpels double as exactos and syringe needles as tiny little detail cutters. I recycle the blank sheets between printoutsthat come off our pin-fed printer to print my clues at home, too. And discarded test tube racks make great holders for markers.
