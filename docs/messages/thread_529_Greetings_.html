From: Jim Ratliff (JRATLIFF@KVCC.EDU) | Date: 2004-08-10 11:43:58 UTC-04:00
Greetings from Kalamazoo,

I just signed up for the LbGLK group, have been letterboxing
occasionally for about 4 months with my family and have signed up on the
listserv's Daily Digest mode to get all my e-letterboxing info in one
electronic message bundle.

We've only Letterboxed in SW Michigan and thus far our favorite finds
are:

SpringChick's Serendipity (we had to rent a canoe for that one!)
Lucky Charm's Election '04 Series (nice series which led us to a place
we'd never been in Yankee Springs SP where we picked up out first
hitchhiker, Tag Along Tiger)
Azobox's I Spy (great story and BEAUTIFUL walk and view)
and
Bindle Babe's Misplaced Manatee (Good story, nice big rock, and at a
low key, they don't make 'em like that anymore rest stop).

Warm regards, Roller (and Sprite, Sam and Molly)
