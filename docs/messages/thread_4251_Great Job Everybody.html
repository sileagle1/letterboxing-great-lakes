Great Job Everybody

From: jose_quiti (jose_quiti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-17 00:49:42 UTC
I meant to post this several weeks ago, but I forgot. Until today.

When my husband and I went out to collect some of the boxes we had
planted for the event, he made a comment about how well hidden they
were. The first group we picked up was the Berma Shave series. He
didn't think they had been found at all because they were exactly like
we left them. When we got back to the car we looked in the logbook of
one of the boxes and saw that tons of people had stamped in. We were
in shock.
Then today I went out and picked up the last several boxes to be sent
back to the carver. Again they were expertly rehidden.

So, thanks for being such a great group of letterboxers!
