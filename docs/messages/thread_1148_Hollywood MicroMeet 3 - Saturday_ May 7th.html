Hollywood MicroMeet 3 - Saturday, May 7th

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-04-06 22:28:58 UTC-05:00
Mark your calendars! Only one month until the third Hollywood MicroMeet at
the Paw Caf in beautiful Hollywood, Illinois (aka the east end of
Brookfield and the west end of Riverside, with a touch of Lyons thrown in
for good measure), conveniently located across the street from the Zoo Stop
on the Burlington Northern Santa Fe Metra line, as well as near both I-55
and I-90 (First Avenue exits). Many local letterboxes nearby, and hopefully
Ill get some new one placed by then.



Special Mothers Day event stamp: I love my Mommy, with your choice of
addendums, more than letterboxing, as much as letterboxing or almost as
much as letterboxing. We promise not to tell your Mommy which one you
choose, unless of course she is attending with you!



We dont have a definite time set up yet, so please make your preferences
known. The Cyberdog (adjoining coffeeshop) opens at 10 am, but the Paw has
better seating arrangements. They open at 11 am. Both close at 5 pm. They
welcome us to meet there, and actually enjoy having us around.
