From: jeansk37 (jskelso@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-03-10 01:24:51 UTC
Just got back from Florida. Was looking for "Fun n Sun" in Lake
Griffin State Park, Leesburg, Fl.--not exactly a tourist spot. Walked
along taking pictures on the trail enjoying myself when I came around
a curve to the spot where the box was to be near a bench. The couple
sitting on the bench, with stuff spread all around them, looked at me
with my backpack and sheet of paper and said "Do you want to do an
exchange?" I met Old Hounder and Siamese from Littleton, Mass.
Siamese ran back to their car for their exchange book while Old
Hounder and I chatted a bit. They have done about 800 boxes in their
first year of boxing. I mentioned the Great Lakes Gathering to them.
