Superior National Forest letterbox policy

From: Drew Family (drewclan@aol.com) | Date: 2008-05-30 17:27:53 UTC
Hi and happy spring to everyone!

We just recieved this note from Superior National Forest at LbNA and
would like to pass it on to you.

If you submit clues to letterboxing.org for a box within this NF,
please be sure to clearly state in the clues that you have followed
the rule imposed by the land managers.

Thanks very much, Jay in CT

Message to Webmasters,

From: Judy Ness:

Message:
It has been brought to our attention that letterboxes have been
placed on National Forest lands. We, the Superior National Forest
have a policy on placement of geocaches and letterboxes. Our policy
is available on our website: www.fs.fed.us/r9/superior. We would
appreciate it if you would place this information on your website or
at least notify your members to know who owns the lands they are
placing their caches on.
We have removed one of the letterboxes off of National Forest land
and are contacting the letterbox owner to inform them of the removal.
I can be reached at the above address or by calling 218-626-4322.
I thank you in advance. Judy Ness

From: Judy Ness, E-mail: jness at fs.fed.us
