I found out that if you make a copy through a FAX machine (they use toner) that you can then get a nice, dark black, crisp transfer with a few seconds of cotton setting heat and pressure from an iron!

Also, if you're looking for some basics AND details I have a tutorial on carving:
http://sunnisan.com/jacqui/tute-carving.html

For the person who just started carving with an exacto, I recommend a #1 speedball blade/tool. I found it MUCH easier than the Exacto (which I kept undercutting what I wanted to keep) especially on curves :-)

You can get that at some Michaels/hobby lobby or at www.Stampeaz.com
