Just a note, "The December Dilemma" has been removed for the season.
Look for it to be replanted in early winter.

Odd, only three sets of people found it this year. I don't know if that
is because: (A) many folks in the area already have this stamp (B) it
was a cold/snowy winter with less boxing opportunities or (C) we coded
it this year. If it is 'A', please let us know and we'll carve a new
stamp for this next year.

Oh, and its ALWAYS fun to be removing a holiday box in late March...IN
THE SNOW! ;-)
