THE WILD CARD
Q. What are my choices for the wild card?
A. You have only 1 choice. You must choose either one of the Great Lakes (Erie, Huron, Michigan, Ontario or Superior) OR one
of the islands on each lake (Kelley’s Island, Mackinac Island, Washington Island, Wolfe Island, or Isle Royale).
Q. How does my wild card become active?
A. One day of each month will be "Lucky Lake" day. On that day I will randomly choose one lake or island in addition to the
regular Bingo word for the day. Once your wild card has been called, it is active and can be used to substitute for any space on
your card as you mark off words for the full card game.
Q. Which day of the month?
A. It will be different each month – I’m not telling. Just be ready!
Q. Can I use the wild card for any game?
A. NO! The wild card can only be used toward a full card, not the interim games.
Q. Must I decide right away which word I will substitute my wild card for?
A. No, you can use it as needed - so basically you just need to get 24 of the words on your card called and then use the wild
card in place of the last word.
