Year of the Dog back in place

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-11-13 04:01:35 UTC
Thanks to h.Owl, I received the stamp for the Year of the Dog box in
the mail recently. The rest of the box had apparently been destroyed
by animals, but h.Owl saved what she could and discarded the rest. The
original stamp is now back in place in a new box with a new logbook.

This weekend we also found 5 boxes in the nearby area (including the
fun new one by Brat Pack IL) and checked on four other boxes we planted
to check on their status. One of these checks was prompted by the
following "Contact the Placer" message I received a few days ago:

"Message:
Lots of fun. Why the Rabbit Was Late Letterbox was placed just as
expected and in good shape as of 11/06/06. Our playgroup, which
included 7 women and lots of 3,4, and 5 year olds had a blast. Thanks."

Well, that sounded like a mob to us, so thought we'd better check on
things. Luckily the box was rehidden well and they only used a single
page to stamp in. However the stamp bag was not resealed at all and
the logbook was laying loose in the box, not in a baggie at all!
Thankfully everything was still dry (even after all that rain & snow
this week). We fixed things up and I sent a carefully worded reply
(didn't want to dampen their enthusiasm, but did caution about being
discreet and being sure to have an adult reseal everything carefully,
especially the logbook).

Only 3 weeks until the St. Nick's gathering!

Wisconsin Hiker
