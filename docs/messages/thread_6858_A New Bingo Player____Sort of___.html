From: grayrabbit_teamrabbit (reborn_harmony@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-01-03 03:27:46 UTC
Rainy-Day Rabbit (our 6-year-old "bunny") has decided to join in the Bingo fun! She has filled up her own Bingo and Springchick cards using all applicable words from the lists and is going to play along with us at home. We told her that if she gets a Bingo before any of us "official" players we would get her a prize...so it will be interesting to see if her Bingo strategy pans out. (Her strategy being to choose the cutest sounding and/or most girly sounding terms and boxes on the lists :-)

Good luck to all of our fellow Bingo players out there!! :-) And thanks to Familyof6 for providing the fun this year! :-)

-Gray Rabbit of Team Rabbit :-)
