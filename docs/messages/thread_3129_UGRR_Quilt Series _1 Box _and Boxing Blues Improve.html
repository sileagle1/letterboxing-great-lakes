UGRR/Quilt Series #1 Box (and Boxing Blues Improve)

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2006-09-26 03:03:21 UTC
We pulled this box for a few days--in case anyone was going to hunt
for it soon. We hope to re-place it in a few days with updated clues.

We had received a missing box report telling us that the tree hiding
the box had been chopped down--with the sawn logs and sawdust
littering the site. That indeed was true, as we discovered when Do-Re-
Mi Gal and I went to look for it today. After heaving one of the sawn
logs to the side and digging through the muck, we were able to find
it!! It would have been impossible to find without knowing its exact
location since it was thoroughly buried. We had to rush off to a
cross country meet (Ball Magnet finished 9th!) so we didn't have time
today to find a new spot.

I had been feeling down a week or so ago, after getting a number of
missing box reports so I was glad to find this one wouldn't need to be
recarved. We found another of our missing boxes in a nearby
geocache. I don't know why someone took it from its hiding spot and
tossed it into the geocache, but it is back in its rightful home. Now
if we could just head to Florida and check on those missing boxes--not
so hopeful there.
