From: momblees (eshop@blees.com) | Date: 2012-03-20 15:19:17 UTC
Back in the fall of 2008, I letterboxed for two full days in Chicagoland while "I/m w/Her" was not with me (he was in meetings). The first day, I started early and took the train out of town. I went to the zoo, saw some biker chicks and ate some candy. The next day strolled a couple of cemeteries, ate some ice cream and finally headed toward Tarzan. The Tarzan clue indicated that the letterbox might be a "tall order" for me (I'm only 5'5") ... so to increase my chances of being able to reach the box, I had lugged around a thick Chicago phone book in my backpack all day.

Aha! I reach the correct building. But alas, there is a maintenance guy in the hallway ... plastic sheeting on the floor ... he is not just dusting. I walked around the neighborhood and came back in 30 minutes. Mr. Clean Team gave me the stink eye and I headed back to the train!

So, I had a good workout carrying that phone book. Found a lot of boxes. Arrived back at the hotel to meet up with I'm w/Her with enough time to change into my formal wear for the evening ... just couldn't get all of the ink off my fingers :)

Thanks for the memories!
