Hi!

For those of you that have an AtlasQuest account, there is a way to
change a setting so you get an email notification in your regular email
account when you get AtlasQuest email. Go to AtlasQuest, My Page,
Preferences, and then check the box if you "Would like an e-mail
notification whenever new mail arrives for you on Atlas Quest".

This assumes that your Account Info lists your normal email address.

I've found this to be helpful because I don't think to check AQ very
often for email.
