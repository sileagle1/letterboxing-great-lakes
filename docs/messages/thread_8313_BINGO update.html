Howdy BINGO players!  


I just wanted to let you know that as of today, at least one person has officially crossed the mark of having over half their card filled.  We'll see if the second half of the card takes as long to fill as the first part...


But before (hopefully) we have a Full Card winner, I'd like to remind you that there is one more interim game still in play.  It is the "+" game which means you need to have the entire N column filled and the middle row of your card filled, thereby making a "+" symbol.


Good luck to all of you!
