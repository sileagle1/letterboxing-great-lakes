guess what I saw this weekend

From: pell_lake_girl (mishiekins@prodigy.net) | Date: 2006-11-06 18:10:06 UTC
So I went letterboxing this weekend and I found in a logbook here in Illinois....

SPRING CHICK'S STAMP!

ha!

I felt like I had caught a glimpse of some celebrity or something. I was just looking at the
log and then I saw the name and I was like...sayyyyy... do I know that name?!?!!? hee hee

I am so tempted to ask for help on finding the rest of this series of 7 boxes... the clues are
just impossible. I don't know how anyone found these first 2 boxes without help. I have
been searching this darn forest preserve for weeks. And then I had to ask the placer why I
could not figure it out based on her directions and she shared with me what she left out
-- uuuggg -- so I found the 2, and I could not find the next 3 based on the clues either.
"Where would an elephant hide?" Heck if I know. Elephants are huge. They have no natural
predators. Not even lions would attack a full grown elephant. And they are native to Africa
and India -- grasslands and jungle. Not woods. How do I even know WHY an elephant
would hide?!?!? It wouldn't!

Grrr. Very annoying. I keep telling myself to give up on these but it's bugging me.

Anyway, it was a bit surreal to see thqt stamp, is all I'm saying.

Freelance Mystic
