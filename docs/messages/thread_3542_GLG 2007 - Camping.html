From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-08 11:23:10 UTC-08:00
If anyone's interested in camping at Alum Creek State
Park for the Great Lakes Gathering, here's a link to
the park's website:

http://www.ohiodnr.com/parks/parks/alum.htm

There are campgrounds at the park, the rate is $26 a
night the weekend of the gathering. There's a number
you can call to reserve a campsite, or you can reserve
your site online, but note, "Reservation must include
2 nights minimum if you reserve Friday or Saturday."
So hey, stay Friday night, too, and be the first one
at the gathering Saturday morning.

Here's an idea, for those camping (like Deedle & I
will be), if we all book our camp sites now we'll have
first choice and can stake out an entire area for
ourselves and make it a weekend-long party ;)

There are actually 2 camping areas, separated by a
main road. Here's a map, for those interested:

http://www.ohiodnr.com/parks/parkmaps/alumcreekcamp0506.pdf

Doodle Bug.
