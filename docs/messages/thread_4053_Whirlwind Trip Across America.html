Whirlwind Trip Across America

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-05-14 13:40:56 UTC
For Mother's Day, I said that I wanted a picnic lunch and an afternoon
of letterboxing. Yesterday was the perfect sunny day for this. After
church, we headed up to Wright Woods and had a picnic by the river.
Then it was off to visit 10 states in rapid fashion--west to Hawaii and
east to NJ, north to Idaho and south to Arkansas. This is an awesome
series by Cherokee Rose2. Beautiful and humerous stamps and a pretty
forest preserve to walk through. Can't wait to visit all 50 states.
Ball Magnet was wondering if they were going to do all the US
territories as well (I think he is studying some of them in school).

BirdWoman/Waltzing Pigs
