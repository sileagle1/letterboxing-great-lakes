MI U.P. Letterboxing Report

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-10-03 15:58:10 UTC

The following MI U.P. letterboxes were all found during the week of
9/25 - 10/2. We replaced a few baggies and dried off some stamps,
but for the most part everything was in good repair, with the
exception of TJ_Mich Leelinaw box -- everything was soaked inside
the box and much of the soggy logbook was unreadable. I had an
extra log book so I replaced it with the dry one and took the wet
one -- it has dried out and I will send it to you via U.S. Mail.

before leaving the house but neglected to add them to my "list" and
