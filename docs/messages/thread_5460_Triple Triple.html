From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-12-19 05:59:59 UTC
I'm not a big basketball fan anymore, nor was I before the Chicago
Bulls first championship year. I was fortunate enough to catch the
wave and enjoy the Bulls dynasty.

I'm embarrassed to admit when I first started watching and the
commentators said, "Jordan has a triple double" that I had no idea
what the heck a triple double was!

Of course it was having double figures in rebounds, points and
assists.

Now, in the letterboxing world I can say I have a triple triple. I
have triple figures in my PFX counts!!

Today in the 10 degree weather, with 4 inches of snow on the ground I
hiked a mile and planted 5 boxes.

The first box I planted today was my 100th!

My official stats are; P104 F446 X153

Looking forward to hitting F500 in the spring.

hx6
