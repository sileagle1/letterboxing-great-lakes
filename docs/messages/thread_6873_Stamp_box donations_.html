Stamp/box donations?

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2011-01-11 01:22:39 UTC
Martini Man & I are planning a trip later this year to Wyoming, Montana and North & South Dakota. We plan to look for many of the boxes planted by Half-Empty (formerly known as ~KittyMail when he lived in Wisconsin).

He has been trying to seed his area of northeastern WY w/boxes, but there still aren't many letterboxers out that way, so he doesn't have many to look for himself. We of course plan to plant a few while we are out there. However if any of you would like to give us any additional stamps/boxes, we'd be happy to take them with us to plant so he has more to look for. So if you have any extra stamps/boxes laying around that you don't think you'll get to planting, or if you'd like to create something specifically for one of those 4 states, just let us know.

We plan to attend the Dead of Winter event this Saturday, the SILO event next month and the GLG in June. If you have any donations, these events would be a great time to give them to us.

Thanks!

Wisconsin Hiker

P.S. Half-Empty is the guy who has created many fantastic clues including the "Search for Rexlettob", and these hilarious video clues http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rHz9rrpj9z8 and
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ohT6vuvnNlc as well as many others (including some long hikes we're looking forward to!).
