From: Kate J (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-08-07 02:05:28 UTC
I know Tim has worked with the Faire management for many years to have so many boxes there. I also know that a few have been planted just outside the gates.

I would recommend contacting the Faire management team and talk to them about what letterboxing is, how having a series there will bring in more patrons, and that it won't disrupt general activities of the Faire. Otherwise, you can try to place them secretly, but it seems that those go missing much more quickly.

MN Faire is a really big place, so you'll have lots of potential hiding places. Good luck!

-- Kate aka Gibson Girl


Re: Bristol Clues updated

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-08-07 15:15:17 UTC
I strongly suggest you do NOT hide them secretly. Faire security is very tough, and they particularly hone in on anything out of order, especially if it may resemble stealing from the sellers in any way. In order to hide the boxes at Bristol, I need to provide a map indicating where the boxes are each year so security can be informed. (The first year, one of the security people was actually a letterperson!) Working with the management would probably lead to you being able to plant more boxes than you normally could have in the first place.


The MN ren faire is a great place. Go for it! (I might be willing to carve a Puke n' Snot box for you!)
