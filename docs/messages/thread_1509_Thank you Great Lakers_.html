Canada! But we loved every minute of it.

All we can say is Thank you - Thank you to all of our new friends:

Thanks to SpringChick and Azobox for encouraging this trip, your tips

Looking forward to another trip to your fine state in a few years -
but this time we won't have any smelly animals or crazy clowns sharing
the ride with us!
