It was a good day for me to check up on my card:  I found two words to cross off (because I'm too busy unpacking boxes to play Bingo).


Segue to another story:

Estella Naparella helped me pack up my sewing studio and consequently boxed up my LBing logbooks.  As you all know, she has a wonderful sense of humor.  She labeled the box of logbooks "porn".

Fast forward six months.  Our house is rebuilt and we have asked Boy Scouts from our troop to help unload the PODs.  A 14-year-old picked up "that" box and sheepishly asked my 28-year-old son if the box should go into his room.  Without missing a beat, my son replied that the box belonged to me,  The poor scout was so flustered!

Have a great day, everyone!
