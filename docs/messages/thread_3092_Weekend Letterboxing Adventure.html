Arrr, i tra'eled t' Lewes Delaware o'er the weekend from the plains o'
Ohio. The tall ship Kalmar Nyckel was in port and the crew was
wanderin' the city plunderin' the bars and lootin' the shops. Me
happened upon some wanderin' ministerials and asked if they had heard
o' the Drunken sailor. Aye, they broke int' song and told me he was in
the long boat sobberin' up. I found se'eral treasures near the Kalmar
and looted them for their stamp. I do belie'e some hitchhikin'
chiggers followed me back or maybe some bad crabs. Aye, itchin' like
crazy, gar!

Ahoy, on the win'ed flight home, Me finally figureded out whar Silent
Doug's Delaware mystery was. Gar, Where can I find a bottle o'rum?
Drat - now I need to go back. Arrrgh
