You said your from Jenison Right?? Here are directions from Jenison.

1. Starting in JENISON, MI on BALDWIN ST - go < 0.1 mi
2. Turn Left on COTTONWOOD DR - go 0.3 mi
3. Turn Right on CHICAGO DR - go 0.7 mi
4. Turn Right onto I-196 WEST toward HOLLAND - go 13.0 mi
5. Take exit #55/I-196 WEST onto BYRON RD[I-196-BL] toward
HOLLAND/ZEELAND - go 0.9 mi
6. BYRON RD becomes E MAIN AVE - go 0.3 mi
7. Turn Right on N FAIRVIEW RD - go 1.0 mi
8. Turn Left on E RILEY ST - go 0.3 mi
9. Arrive at 9479 RILEY ST, ZEELAND
These directions are from Yahoo Maps On the web.
