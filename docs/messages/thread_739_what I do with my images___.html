My system so far is using a 6-ring daytimer-type binder, which I've filled
with cardstock punched to fit. Unfortunately, I've been using texturized
stock (the kind with corduroy-type ribbing), which can make it difficult to
get a good image sometimes, but what I like is the portability of the pages.
I make a mistake...out it comes. I don't want to risk message up existing
images on the road...I take them out. This week I want to sort
chronologically...next week I want to sort by Finds, Places, Exchanges,
etc...no prob! (Which BTW is my current system). I've even taken some
pictures which I've punched and included amongst the pages. I have a little
pouch that fits in it to hold my stamps and cats eye pads, and it has a loop
for a pen. Best is that it zips shut, so it all stays neat and together.
Tuck a FF certificate in and it doesn't get lost.

What do I actually DO with the saved images...nothing much. Just look at
them and reminisce, and as others have said, show them off to those worthy
of appreciating them and becoming Converts to the Cause(!).
