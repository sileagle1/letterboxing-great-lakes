Ah Nuts

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-22 13:36:31 UTC-07:00
By any chance, has anyone recently found the Nuts letterbox in Cuyahoga County, Ohio, planted near the Chalet? Deedle & I tried to find it last night, and we searched and searched and searched, and either that's a very well hidden box, we didn't follow the clues properly (but all the landmarks fell into place, so we think, unlike our 100 Acres trip, we were on the right path), or alas, it's gone. I've emailed the placer, but out of curiosity thought I'd ask the list if anyone found it recently.

To assuage ourselves, we went to get ice cream afterwards. I had a Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Cone...as in, the cone was actually made out of chocolate chip cookie dough. Yummm....if you're on Pearl Road in Strongsville, Ohio, visit Olympia's :)

Doodle aka Diane.



Letterboxers wear Khakis. Or jeans. Or shorts, if they're not scared of poison ivy. And rainjackets when they're crazy enough to go 'boxing in a downpour. And sunscreen, bug spray, big backpacks to hold all those stamp pads and box first aid supplies and extra bug spray and their very important water, and good hiking boots, and...

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2004-07-22 17:16:09 UTC-04:00
Not recently, but when I found this box in Fall 2002, it had been vandalized
and the handmade log book was missing from the pieces I found lying
scattered on the ground. I put what was there back in its place and
e-mailed the placer at that time. There is a note on the clues that the box
would be out of service 8/03 - 9/03. As the note was never removed, I would
suspect there is a good chance the box was never put back.

SpringChick
