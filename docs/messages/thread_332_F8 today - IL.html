F8 today - IL

From: Angela (missangi1210@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-23 05:30:42 UTC
My friend Becky and I went out letterboxing today. What a day! She
came over at 8:45 this morning to carve her stamp. We didn't get home
till after 9:00 this evening. A 12-hour boxing day! She picked Wiener
Mouse as her trail name, after the stamp she made. It is a copy of
her tattoo (a mouse looking up at a star), which the tattoo artist
nicknamed Wiener Mouse because it looks like a "wiener dog."

We found 4ft Under in Rockford, Milk Capital (Finally! We were going
the wrong way before...lol), and all the Fool's Errand boxes. Eight
total!

A few things we learned:

* sometimes bugspray DOESN'T work (I heard rubbing Bounce dryer
sheets works wonders...anyone else hear the same?)
* mosquitos can bite through jeans
* deer ticks are out and ready to bite (make sure to check yourselves)
* seeing "Team Taco" in EVERY log book makes Becky hungry for Mexican
food
* when all else fails, mosquitos aim for the face (really, those big
lumps on my face aren't zits...)
* I before E except after C (Becky learned how to spell "wiener"
after about 3 boxes)

That's all! Just wanted to share in our excitement!

Angi ~ Shutterfly

Sounds like a really great day. That many boxes in one day is amazing!

And as for learning to spell your trail name...there are some early finds of mine where "Delirium" is spelled with two "e"s. Very nice, considering I'm an English teacher. *grin*

Thank you, Thank you, Thank you!

Just want to say many thanks to Shutterfly for finding our box and
commenting on our first attempt. Now I understand why everyone gets so crazy after
they place a box. When Dumpster Diva and I didn't hear anything about our
first placement we felt a little out of the loop. You know "LBA" (Letter boxer's
Anxiety).

Not yet having found a box, we had zero idea of what we were up against.
This did not thwart our attempt at placing our own box...We decided to go with
the "Live and learn" attitude. Plus, sometimes the most memorable moments
are also the most embarrassing. I mean, we had a couple of weeks of LB faux
pas, didn't we? We may be newbies, but we are fearless!

I noticed that several of you have team names. Dumpster Diva and I are best
friends who live in two different states (MI and IL) I believe it's high
time we come up with a team name. I have a couple ideas, but of course, I'll
have to check with my teammate. I'll be getting back to you all on this. We
have nothing in common, so this ought to be fun to work on.

Talk about team spirit...Like an idiot, I promised her that I wouldn't try
to look for a box that we were unable to find while she was visiting me here
in MI, unless she was with me again. What was I thinking? This box is not
even 5 miles from my home (Who's at Hagar Park) I drive by it almost every day.
A promise is a promise...

Lipstick Chick
