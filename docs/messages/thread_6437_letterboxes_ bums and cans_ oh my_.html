letterboxes, bums and cans, oh my!

From: estella_naparella (estella_naparella@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-15 18:40:42 UTC

I have found a great ruse for when letterboxing: I carry a bag of
aluminum cans I collect along the way, makes me look a bit like a crazy
bag lady but keeps suspicion away. :)

anyway, Skootching dog and I were checking out plants to make sure they
wintered well in MN. I had my bag of cans with me, and was merrily
picking them up as we went along. We were homeward bound when SD found
an old bums' nest from last fall, and in it (wait for it...) was the
TREASURE TROVE of abandoned aluminum cans!

fortunately, I had a second bag in my hoodie, so I swiped all the bums'
cans. Later I remembered how many letterboxes get defaced by bums et
al. So, I took one back for the letterboxing community. SD was lookout
for any errant bums, and we got away safely.

Just wanted to share a Sunday story of LB adventures.

cheers!

E.N.

Oooh, I am so jealous of you finding the aluminum can stash!  Deedle & I are regular aluminum can scavengers, making our trip to the scrap yard every few months to turn our cans in.  Every time I see a can on the side of the road that we can't pick up I wimper, "But that's money lying there!"

I'm, um, making us sound kind of desperate for cash, aren't I?  But we do NOT raid curb-side recycling bins; evidently earlier this week, on the day of the weekly trash pick-up, someone took all the aluminum cans out of a friend's recycle box!
