Not sure where in Michigan you are, but here on the west side, we headed out in an attempt to get a bunch of boxes at Pigeon Creek park in West Olive. Just as we were leaving the house, it started to sprinkle. Full on rain by the time we got there. We waited about a 1/2 hour to see if it would let up, but it wouldn't. And there wasn't (or didn't appear to be) much tree coverage. Trees appeared to be mostly pines which didn't block the rain. And we didn't want all our clues & books to get wet. So after a 1/2 hour of waiting, we gave up and left. Of course, it's done raining now that we're home, hahaha!

~pjynx
