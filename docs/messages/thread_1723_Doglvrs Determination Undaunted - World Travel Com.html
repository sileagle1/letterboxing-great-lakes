Doglvrs Determination Undaunted - World Travel Completed!

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-17 18:24:34 UTC
Attention: World Travelers and those following the exploits of
"Around the World in 80 Days" adventurers; and arm-chair
travelers everywhere.

From: Travel Documents Division, "80 Days"
Department of Passports
Itinerary Special Section
Certification Office

The Travel Staff and all associated ancillaries are pleased to
announce that another resourceful traveling troupe has successfully
circled the world, "80 Days" fashion.

drumrolltrumpet fanfare

The determined and relentless Doglvrs have completed the "Around
the World in 80 Days" series and have provided certification of proof
as required. In addition, Doglvrs have completed their journey within
the time constraints required by the original challenge.

So therefore, let it be known, that Doglvrs will receive not only the
"World Traveler" stamp for completing their journey, but will
receive in addition, the coveted "Around the World in 80 Days"
special bonus stamp. Their names will also be entered into that select
company of those who have also accomplished the same feat.

Again the "80 Days" staff is left speechless at this accomplishment.
(Yet they do seem to go on and on, don't they?)

The Travel Staff is receiving regular reports from and about other
world travelers. There have been near-collisions at some foreign
cities, according to reports. And recent dispatches also include
stories of younger travelers receiving Social Studies credit for their
journeys.
The travel office neither endorses nor disputes these claims.

To learn more about the potential this adventure offers, click on:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days.asp

For those already underway, we remind you that a status page is
available:
http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/80days_status.asp

You may contact the travel staff at:
80days@letterbox-mi.com

Respectfully at your service,
Travel staff

azobox
