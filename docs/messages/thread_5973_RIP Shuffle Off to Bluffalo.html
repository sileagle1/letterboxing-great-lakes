From: Kathi (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2009-07-18 05:07:06 UTC
Bummer...I discovered this one missing today, just a week shy of its five year placement anniversary on July 23, 2004. I went to Buffalo Rock State Park to place the box Nitrocat and I created for the GLG's State Park series and decided to check up on the little guy while I was there. The stump that hid it so well all these years had completely rotted away. Darn fungi! Why do they have to rot away dead organic material like that!

The box was last found in April, so my guess is that either critters carried it away or it was exposed to the point where a human took it. Oh, well! Part of the game.........
