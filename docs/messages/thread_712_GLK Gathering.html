Carol,
I'll start and hope others chime in with more.
Generally, a Gathering is an opportunity to meet other letterboxers,
to exchange stamp images, to search for specially placed event-boxes,
and to share some skills, interests and stories about letterboxing.
There can be classes -- stamp carving, logbook binding, etc, -- and
often potluck meal(s).
Accomodations are sometimes scoped out by the locals for travellers
though the events are most often just one day (a verrry long day in
some cases!)
As noted in earlier posts, the date for the next Great Lakes
Gathering has been set and it will be in the greater Detroit area.

Stay tuned as details emerge from the planners in the months ahead.
