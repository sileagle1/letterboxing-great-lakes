From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-09-28 22:23:32 UTC-05:00
Have to watch that camo tape. We spoke about this a while ago - some of it
has a scent, almost chocolately! The stuff my husband bought for me (I
think at Gander Mtn??) is on a big roll, and it definitely has a scent. It
is also much shinier (less camouflage if the light catches it). I really
like the Walmart stuff, especially because they have those flattened rolls
which are more compact to carry along.



I always check out the various dollar store containers, but you do have to
be very careful of the seals. Some are way less than optimal. Dollar Tree
currently has some that look promising. I found some cute little
rectangular snack size boxes at the Container Store - a pack of 4 for $5 -
with good seals. I think those are the ones you've been using lately,
aren't they, CMoon??



--klk/FungusWoman
