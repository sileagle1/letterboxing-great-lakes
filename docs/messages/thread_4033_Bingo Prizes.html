We, too, would like to echo Wisconsin Hiker and Azobox and others
who received their bingo prizes. We received our box yesterday.

socks from Flying Dragon, soon.

prizes, keeping track of the databases, etc. That's such a huge
job. Someone else mentioned this not too long ago, but I also have
to say that I looked forward to finding out what the word of the day
was each day, and couldn't wait to get home to check the message
board to see if I had another square to check off. Even if we didn't
win, it was a lot of fun and a great wintertime diversion. Winning
of course, made it more fun. We feel like we won the lotto or
something with all of the great stuff. Thanks again everyone!

Bingo Prizes

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-07-20 02:46:28 UTC
I just wanted to clear the air about this year's Bingo prizes. I received a rather disturbing note today pretty much accusing me of keeping the prizes for myself (mind you, this was not from one of the winners, rather just a random busybody who claims to be "speaking for many other people"). While it is true that the prizes have not been sent out yet, I have no intention of keeping them and I'm sorry if the delay has given that appearance. I may be guilty of over-committing or even procrastinating, but I am not dishonest.

In a perfect world, the prizes would have hit the mail two days after the game was done, not still be sitting in my office 8 weeks later. Unfortunately my world is not perfect. Life is not all play and sometimes real life takes priority over our games. In real life I have a job that not only is extremely demanding during the summer but requires me to live onsite at my work. Since the Bingo game went a couple of weeks longer this year than in previous years, I wasn't able to get everything sorted and packaged up for mailing before I moved up to camp, which I truly feel bad about. I have communicated this to the winners.

If I sound upset, I am. For anyone to make an accusation like that, to even think that, after the time and effort I have put into coordinating this game the past several years is unfounded and out of line.

SpringChick
