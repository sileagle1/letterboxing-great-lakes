Lake Kegonsa Bluebird

From: M Clan (joshmonty@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-09-19 22:00:06 UTC
After a devastating tornado in Stoughton, WI a lone bluebird was
lost. Did he ever return home? Clues have been posted.

His mate, however, was blown over a great lake and onto a pleasant
peninsuala. She found herself in the northern portion of this
peninsula with an injury that prevented flight. Longing for her mate,
she decided to bravely hitchhike around the great lake on her way back
home. If you find her, please advance her a toward Stoughton, WI.
But don't take her too far at once (no more than 50 miles per leg of
the journey). Her injury means that lengthy travel is impossible and
dangerous, but over time - and with your help - she hopes to be able
to see her partner on the shores of Lake Kegonsa again.

Father M Clan
