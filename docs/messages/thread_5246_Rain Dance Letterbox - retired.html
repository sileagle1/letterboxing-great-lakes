From: amylouc3po (altadlock@juno.com) | Date: 2008-08-12 15:34:49 UTC
Hi Everyone -

I rarely post here (and lately, I rarely letterbox - which I'm not thrilled about) but I wanted
to let you all know that I retired my Rain Dance letterbox in St. Charles, IL. The stamp was
carved from a pink eraser and the unfortunate reality is that erasers were made to "erase
away" when they come contact with paper... and so, the stamp is quite worn. The box was
out there for three years and had a lot of activity - thanks so much to everyone who took
the time to visit, stamp, write notes and re-hide so well. I'm impressed it lasted as long as
it did since it's a mere two feet off a fairly well traveled path. It's a great little park so
maybe I'll place something new there, we'll see.

Happy Boxing!
