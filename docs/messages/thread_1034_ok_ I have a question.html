Essentially, yes. Although some feel they are not real
letterboxes. They are kinda like gathering hitchhikers. This kinda
puts a onus on them, whether deserved or not. The more famous ones
(KMartha, LBNa, and LBNWA, etc.) have quite a history behind them
which make them good conversational pieces at gatherings. I started
one at the last Great Lakes gathering, simply called "The
Gathering". I have asked for it to be sent to our gathering in
June, but I haven't had word...

We do seem to be getting quite a few "Mo' Boxin'" themed boxes for
the gathering, which is fine, but I would like to take this
opportunity to remind everyone that you can make other themed boxes
for the gathering as well. Some can be permanent plants in the
area, or you can bring a temporary box to the gathering and decide
to plant it permanently near home (or never again!). The more boxes
the better!!!!

Dragon
