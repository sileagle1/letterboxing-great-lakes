Thank heavens this was called early and will not be the reason for loosing the full card when the game gets down to the wire!  This elusive box (at least for us!) has haunted us since early fall!  We hiked every trail at that park going around three times and could not for the life of us figure out the directions...it was maddening because we knew the confusion was not with the clues, but the problem was clearly due to our lack of interpretation....pathetic letterboxers!  We left empty handed and disappointed.


Several weeks later at an event, we actually asked someone we knew who had snagged it to see where we were going wrong...after a quick recount the "Ah Ha" moment hit...then the frustration took on a new form for us as we weren't able to get back up there to redo our steps. So frustrating!!!  We both SO wanted to put this box on our BINGO card.

Oh well...if not for BINGO, we still have it high on our "to do" boxing list...but we are going to wait until the temps move out of the ridiculous minus wind chills we are presently experiencing!

Happy Bingo everyone!  The game really is helping us get through January so far!

Well, thanks for all of the attempts to make us feel better, but, I guess for us WiHi's description is truer than the actual box name implies:


Dream - we were so excited to go for this box on a perfectly beautiful fall day...we had such high hopes!
Destiny - we were certain this box was going to be OURS!  We were going to have that sweet ecstasy of finding it at last!  It was ours for the taking!  Pure letterboxing bliss!
Desire - it was our heartfelt desire to follow the clues and actually get this box before WiHi and MM..many of you can relate to that!
Despair - Imagine our utter discouragement and despair at not finding it the first time around!  That is around the entire park!
Delirium - We were pulling our hair out...ready to do harmful things to each other after the 3rd time around!
Destruction - So upset and frustrated, we reluctantly called it quits and were saying unintelligible babble about quitting this terrible hobby!
Death - Ultimately...death to our qualification to put this box on our BINGO card....we're still in mourning!
