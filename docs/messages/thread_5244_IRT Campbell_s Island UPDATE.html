IRT Campbell's Island UPDATE

From: (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-08-10 18:06:34 UTC-04:00
After being under water for 6 months!! I attempted to check Campbells Island
in June, the ground was STILL saturated.

Finally today, the Mississippi was down enough for me to bushwhack my way to
the boxes location . . . and it was still there!! With only a few drops of
water!!!

THANKS LocknLocks!!!

I did, however, move the box, not necessarily to higher ground per say (it
is on a flat island after all). The new spot will allow me to check on it more
quickly and allows you to find it easier when the water does make its way up
again.

I will post new clues this evening.

My IRT Welcome to Illinois is still there and in good shape, this after a
report of an attempt. The original stamp is still there and waiting for the
Road Trip to come out this way.

I want to thank all who found Campbells Island . . . esp. Garfan (last
finder Nov. 2007) for piling on the rocks so that this box (my 3rd placed box)
didn't end up in New Oreleans.
