In an effort to clean up past loose ends/mistakes before embarking on
anything new, I have replaced the On the Waterfront box in downtown
Rockford and updated the clues this weekend. Hopefully, this new spot
will be a little more permanant.

Also, be on the lookout for some new clues in Northern Illinois along
the Wisconsin border - especially if you're in the mood for a halfway
decent hike. I'm not the official placer, but I promise - I helped!
