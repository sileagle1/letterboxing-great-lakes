Des Plaines River boxes announce retirement

From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-08-18 09:25:41 UTC-05:00
Over the weekend, Coffee Beans and I did some maintenance on our boxes
along the Des Plaines river trail since we've recently gotten a few
reports of 'missing' boxes. Fortunately, our boxes were still in
place. Yea! Unfortunately, much of the landscape has changed over the
past two years since we first planted the boxes making the clues hard to
follow. So, we decided to retire 'Talking about the HHH,' 'Des Plaines
River,' and the 'Hunt for the Hidden Hollow' boxes. The 'Cruising for
the HHH' box is still in place (for the time being).

For those of you who live in the area, don't worry. We have five boxes
at home, ready to be planted!
