Last weekend for Bristol Ren Faire

From: Kate J (wench116@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-08-31 17:11:02 UTC
The past 8 weeks have flown by and this will be the final weekend of the Bristol Renaissance Faire for 2011. Dragon made some fabulous images for this season and I highly recommend a visit if you haven't done so this year. I should be there on Saturday and Monday, collecting the wench boxes before we leave (which should be fairly late on Monday).

There is a great coupon for closing weekend - $15 adults and free kids!
http://www.renfair.com/bristol/tickets/discountcoupons.asp
