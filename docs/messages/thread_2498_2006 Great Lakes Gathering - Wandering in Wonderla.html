2006 Great Lakes Gathering - Wandering in Wonderland

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-05-10 01:06:17 UTC
Well the next event is only a little over 3 weeks away!

We've received 16 boxes (with more expected to arrive in the mail
soon) that we'll be planting over the next two weekends. We've also
received clues for 4 boxes that have already been planted by some of
the local boxers. I've heard back from almost everyone else that
they are still planning to create the boxes listed on the website, so
we'll probably have at least 40 boxes at the gathering.

As expected, we've had a few recent attendee cancellations but also
more people have been signing up to attend. A few are
still "maybes", but our current count is an amazing 113 people!!

With those two bits of information, I wanted to give an early "heads
up" that you will want to be sure your logbook has enough room for
all the box and exchange stamps you'll be collecting. If you're
getting short on space in your book, you may want to go get (or make)
another logbook before the event. I also know some of you use a
smaller logbook, so wanted to warn you that some of the stamps we've
received are on the LARGE size(they must have eaten some of that
mushroom from the "bigger" side!). Anyway, if you use a smaller
logbook, you may want to bring some extra paper along to use for the
larger stamps (the current largest is 4"x6").

I've been trying to keep the website updated on a regular basis, so
if you want to know what boxes have been received, who is planning to
attend, what's planned for the potluck, or descriptions of some of
the workshops, just check the site:

http://www.2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com

We're looking forward to seeing everyone on June 3rd!

Wisconsin Hiker
