From: Coffee Beans (coffeebeans06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-08-03 08:18:00 UTC-07:00
The boxes with this sort of concept that spring to my mind are ones we found when we first started boxing in 2006 -- The Cottontails had several boxes planted in/around Elgin, IL that were famous paintings and the stamps were layered so that when (for example) all 3 stamps/colors were layered together just right, the final image was of the Van Gogh of "Starry Night" painting. They did this for several paintings -- very fun finds, but it was sad when one layer would be missing from the final masterpiece.


We also have a series like this, hidden for the last presidential election in 2008, where all 3 stamps are layered on top of each other and form one of the Obama election posters. All 3 of ours are still active near O'Hare at Catherine Chevalier Woods called "Obamafest: Black, White, Red, and Blue."


Coffee Beans
