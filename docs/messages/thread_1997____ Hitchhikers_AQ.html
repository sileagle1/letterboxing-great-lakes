I would have to agree wholeheartedly with the response given by
BindleBabe in regards to listing HH's, unlisted letterboxes and
online logging.

As for AtlasQuest... I think there are fewer users from our area on
AQ than from the PNW and the East Coast, in part because there are
fewer letterboxers in the this region than those, but also perhaps
because we have this talk list and don't feel the need for the
message boards provided there.

As for listing boxes at AQ, at one time I had my boxes listed there
but have recently removed them because I simply do not have the time
to maintain clues in multiple places and prefer to use
letterboxing.org as it is the original/official LbNA clue site. I
think that Ryan has done a fantastic job with the AQ site and
applaud his generous contribution of time and talent to the
letterboxing community. I maintain my membership at AQ and peek in
from time to time, but I am not a regular user. To me AQ is like
some of the new features on letterboxing.org -- not really necessary
to play the game, just nice-to-haves, and a person can take them or
leave them and use them as suits their needs.
