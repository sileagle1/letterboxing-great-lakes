"Cemetery Stroll"-related Events

From: cdem_ltrbox (wp4@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2012-06-17 12:37:56 UTC
The Chicago-area "cultural calendar" has events relating to two of our Cemetery Stroll Boxes. The Lookinglass Theater in downtown Chicago (which is actually in the Water Tower Water Works) has just opened with "Eastland: The Musical" (Stroll #8: Chicago's Titanic). It tells about the disaster through the stories of 3 people involved in the tragedy. It is running now through July 29th. Haven't seen any reviews yet. We are going later this summer--actually on it's very last day. Here is a link to the theater http://lookingglasstheatre.org/content/box_office/eastland


Forest Park is presenting the first ever "Forest Park Flapper Ghost Coffin Races" (Stroll #3: Forest Park Flapper) on October 27th. There isn't much info yet, but I gather that teams build a coffin from provided specs, decorate the coffin (and themselves), and then race them down the street. Should be interesting to watch.
