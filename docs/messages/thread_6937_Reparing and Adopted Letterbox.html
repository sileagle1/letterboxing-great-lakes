From: Kathleen Brade (brade@comcast.net) | Date: 2011-02-11 09:03:43 UTC-05:00
Hi.
I haven't found that box so I can't help with the stamp image. I would
encourage you to consider possible reasons that the box went missing.
The most common reason is a poor location.

I love to see old boxes maintained/updated/replaced. But I also know of
many boxes that should never have been placed in their hiding locations
to begin with and I don't think those should be replaced.

As I said, I'm not familiar with this box or its location so I can't
give you an opinion, I just don't want you to invest a lot of time if
the box is not worth the effort.

-- MichKathy
