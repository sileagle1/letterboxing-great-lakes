What Has Letterboxing Done To Scheherezade???

From: scheherezadebox (scheherezade1@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-08-23 16:15:41 UTC
Sorry about this being a cross post, but I wanted to say thank you to
everyone on this list for their contribution to my life!

I have spent my life at home, hiding from the world. This list is why. . .

Social Anxiety

Agoraphobia- Fear of open spaces or of being in crowded, public places
like markets. Fear of leaving home.

Ataxophobia- Fear of disorder or untidiness.

Atychiphobia- Fear of failure.

In October, 2004 I read an article about Letterboxing. I was so
thrilled, that all fear left me. I packed a little backpack, printed
some clues and swallowed my fear.

I found a Letterbox called "Spotted Puppy" by Spring Chick and I was
hooked!

I have learned that in the woods I feel safe, I have gone from being
terrified of the world and computers, to a heavy addiction to
Letterboxing and reading Letterboxing Websites.

I have attended 2 mini-gatherings, planted 16 boxes of my own and um,
the computer I love soooo much that now my family diconnects it so
that I can't play on Letterboxing websites all day!

One year ago, I didn't know how to turn the computer on, now I can
figure out exactly what they have done to it, so that I can get back on.

So, thank you for Letterboxers for planting.
Thank you for this beautiful board.
And thanks to everyone for being so kind.
Also, thanks for helping me figure out what this strange thing called
a computer is for, LETTERBOXING!!!

I like leaving my house now, I know there is always a treasure waiting
for me in a safe and beautiful place.

Forgive me if this is one of those "Too Much Personal Information Posts!"

Scheherezade
