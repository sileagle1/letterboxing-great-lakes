Gathering Plants

From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-24 01:02:36 UTC
Hi all!

I will want to make sure that the planting of boxes at the gathering
is done so there are not too many in the same location. It would be
best if they were spread out at the various picnic/parking areas so
we're not tripping over each other as we find them.

To that extent, if you are planting a box of your own there, please
at least let me know the parking lot you intend for your clues to
start so I can keep track of where the boxes generally are. If you
intend for me to print your clues in the gathering booklet, be sure
to get them to me by the Wednesday before the gathering. Otherwise,
you'll have to bring copies to the gathering or rely on WOM.

Thanks!

Dragon
