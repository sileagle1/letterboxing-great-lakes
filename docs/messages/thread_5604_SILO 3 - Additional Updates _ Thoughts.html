SILO 3 - Additional Updates & Thoughts

From: Lock, Shock & Barrel (placedenfert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-29 12:42:23 UTC
Hello all! I just wanted to toss in a few additional thoughts
regarding SILO 3.

1) Floats - The floats don't need to hold stamps and logbooks. I can
see where some designs might very well end up with a sealed box, and
that's fine. If there happens to be a stamp and logbook inside a
float, that's cool too.

2) Carving Workshop - Beetle has been wonderfully complimentary in
her notes about the carving workshop, and I'm looking forward to
sharing some of what I've learned as I've worked on stamps over the
last couple of years. But to me, the ideal situation would have us
sitting around a large table, sharing ideas of what's worked for us in
the past, perhaps doing some carving, and helping one another past
some parts that might get us all hung up if we were sitting at home
carving by ourselves. So I'll be preparing some of my thoughts, but
I'd love to get your ideas as well.

That said, please feel free to bring carving medium, carving tools,
images, etc. For those who are interested, the workshop may finish,
then transition into a time to just carve and try some new things.

That's all I have for now. I can't wait to see the stamps, floats,
masks, and of course, all of you on the 15th!

Best Regards,

Lock (of Lock, Shock and Barrel)
