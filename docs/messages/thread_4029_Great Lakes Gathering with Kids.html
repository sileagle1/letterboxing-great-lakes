Great Lakes Gathering with Kids

From: halesangel2222 (halesangel2222@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-05 17:22:32 UTC
This is just a headsup for all of you letterboxing parents that will be
bringing the kids along to the Gathering on June 2. I have made a
series of boxes, 24 so far, "Just For Kids". I plan to hide them for
the kids to find. It's going to be like an Easter Egg hunt. There will
be no clues and they won't be too hard to find so the little ones can
find them too. A few will be left on a table for them to stamp while
you are stamping in the event stamps. I will have some washable ink
pads and some hand wipes for them to clean up. All I ask of you is to
make them a little logbook to stamp into or at least help them make
one. I have numbered them all as well as named them to make it easier
for you to find them when you go to log them on AQ.

I'm looking forward to meeting as many of you as I can. See you in
June.

Hales Angels
