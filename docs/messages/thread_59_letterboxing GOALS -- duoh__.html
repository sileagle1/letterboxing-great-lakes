I can't believe what a doofus I can be!

I just went and posted how I have a goal of catching a letterboxer
in the wild but haven't managed to do it yet.

This morning, my daughter and I decided go out and quick put the
dates into the logbooks of the Fate of Easter series. (Yea, I fogot
to do it while we were placing them in the rain this weekend.)

So we stop at the park and hit the first box in the series. Hmmmm...
it's been stamped by Grandvilla and Mobiusklien on todays date.
(They were also kind enough to have put the placed date into the log
book per a message I posted earlier, too!)

We figured they might be out on the trails doing the series at that
very moment... and they were!

Hurrah!!! Letterboxers "in the wild" just as I had hoped for!

Uh-oh. My logbook and stamp were back home. LOL!! Enders Angel
wasn't so foolish. She rushed back to her car for her logbook and
got her first exchanges.

Lesson Learned: Never, never, EVER go anywhere without your stamp.
Especially (quoting Grandvilla here) "if you're going to be anywhere
near where a box might be." You can bet I won't be making this
mistake again!
