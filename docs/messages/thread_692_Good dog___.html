Good dog!!!

From: Kathi LK Krankoski (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2004-09-20 23:00:42 UTC-05:00
Hershey and I went out for our belated letterboxing trip today and I am
happy to say she was a very good girl! She sat smack-dab in the middle of
the back seat and just moved her happily sniffing nose from the left side to
the right. Didn't go near those windows!

It actually wasn't so much a boxing trip as a maintenance trip...checking up
on FOOW2 and LOOW and the boxes at camp. Hersh is one pooped pup tonight.
She's never had so much space to freerange offleash before! She almost
decided to go for a creekwalk. I found a new spot for another box off IL71,
so I have to get busy so I can place it on Friday when I go back for the
weekend.

--klk/FungusWoman
"If you place them, they will come...."

PS: We have a wooden cutout of Smokey the Bear at camp where everyone takes
the obligatory "picture with Smokey" over the years to see how their girls
have grown. My parents did it with me when I went there as a girl. I
myself have taken any number of Michelle. Today I took Hershey's pic there!
Normally she runs towards you when you stoop to try to take her picture.
Not today. She was so tired (it was just before going home) I could barely
keep her from laying down!
