Giant Coaster missing

From: corvisia (bunnyhugger@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-08-15 23:50:39 UTC
I just got back from checking on Giant Coaster, which someone reported not being able to find. Sadly, it is gone. I'm crushed because it was the first stamp I ever carved myself, and it took me three tries to get it right. I had thought it was hidden quite well and securely; I'm puzzled about how it could have vanished.

Even worse, when I got home from checking, I found an email from someone saying that my other box, Say Yes to Michigan, may also be missing (which would make the second time). The letterboxing gods don't like me. :(

I will be replacing Giant Coaster and checking on the status of Say Yes as soon as I can.


Heather F.

Update on Giant Coaster and Say Yes to Michigan:

Well, there is good and bad news. The good news is that Grandvilla contacted me to let me know that although Giant Coaster was flooded out (not such a good hiding place after all!) and the log is gone, she amazingly managed to find the stamp still in good shape. So as soon as I get it back from her, I will be replacing it with a new log in a slightly different location.

The bad news is that I have just gotten back from checking on Say Yes and it's gone again. That makes twice in four months. I'm not sure whether I should try to replace it again or not. I don't really want to have to replace it every two months. :(
