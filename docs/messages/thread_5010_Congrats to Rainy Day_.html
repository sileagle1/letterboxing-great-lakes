Congrats to Rainy Day!

From: brownrabbit_teamrabbit (teamrabbit@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-04-30 02:27:51 UTC
Today our daughter, little Rainy Day Rabbit, found her 200th box! (She
came SO CLOSE at the Fairy Gathering...). She pulled it out herself
(with some adult supervision, of course), and when she and I emerged
from the woods she exclaimed (to my wife, Gray Rabbit) "Look at what I
found behind that tree, under some bark!!", while waving the box in the
air for all to see :) After stamping in (in pink, of course), she
rewarded herself with an exhausting chase around on a nearby
playground... Funny thing - we were just at the same playground (and
Nature Center) this past Friday and didn't know a box was here! Duh!!


Brown Rabbit
of Team Rabbit
