The Iditarod

From: Familyof6 (familyof6portland@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-07 23:45:33 UTC
It was a GREAT day to be outside, beautiful and sunny with weather in
the 40's/50's here in Portland, MI. Several teams competed to win The
Iditarod, and our very own Tiptoe and Tonto and Spartanfans teamed up
to slaughter the competition. After drying off, everyone met at The
Chocolate Moose to see who won, exchange stamps, have lunch, and enjoy
homemade ice cream. Here are the results:

1st Place: (a whopping 360 points!) Team Tiptoe and Tonto and
Spartanfans.
2nd Place: (250 points) Team of Pale Puppy Posse, Gibson Girl, Billy
Bad Bird, and Groovy Girl.
3rd Place: (210 points) Bowl of Cherries
4th place: (205 points) Springchick and Azobox (needless to say,
Azobox walked right into the river...not sure if he was planning for a
swim or not!)
5th Place: (185 points) Mich Kathy and Site Sleuths
6th Pace: (120 points) JustHappy2Cya and Doglvrs

Three teams chose to participate in a special race where they had to
pull a team mate across a field on a sled, collect an ice block, come
back to the starting area, and chip their prize out with ice picks.
Needless to say, this DID help the 1st and 2nd place winners win! In
fact, Tiptoe nearly jumped out of her car while it was still moving.

It was fabulous and pictures are coming soon. Hope everyone had a
great day and enjoyed the event. We had a fabulous time hosting it.

Familyof6
