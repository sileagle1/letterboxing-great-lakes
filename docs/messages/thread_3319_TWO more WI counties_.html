Woo Hoo! Our friends from Minnesota help out again!! The Red Cat (a former
Wisconsinite) covered two more counties on the western border of WI, which
leaves only 8 more WI counties that need a box. I think MI is at 4, which
is only half of what we have left. HOWEVER, each state has actually covered
the same number of new counties since the friendly competition began. WI
thought it was starting out with a 2 county disadvantage, but that soon
changed to 4 when the wily Wolverines discovered that 2 boxes had not been
listed on letterboxing.org in the county they actually resided in (but I'm
not sure if both have been officially "corrected" yet, so maybe they have 5
counties left....).

It sounds like MI has a definite plan in place to cover their remaining 4,
but even if we don't beat them, I'm really happy that we've seen the surge
in placements in these barren areas! Maybe someone wants to make a trip up
there to find the new boxes, and then plant a few in the neighboring
counties while they are up there....

To see the counties remaining, click here:
http://letterboxing.homestead.com/WIcounties.html

GO Wisconsin AND Minnesota!!

Wisconsin Hiker
