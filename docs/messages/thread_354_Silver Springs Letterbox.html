Re: [LbGLK] Re: Silver Springs Letterbox

From: Brenda (advantagek9@voyager.net) | Date: 2004-06-30 10:39:01 UTC-04:00
They can be sewn on their capes, bandanas or backpacks. Some people put
them as displays on the dogs crates. It is really a lot of fun. We carve
personal stamps for ourselves and our dogs. The dog is required to carry
all the necessary items needed for letterboxing. It really is a lot of fun.
Our main goal is to nurture the humane/canine bond because a well trained
dog is a pleasure to own. Most dogs end up in the pound because of behavior
problems. Most behavioral problems are because the dog is bored. They need
mental as well as physical stimulation... just like us!
