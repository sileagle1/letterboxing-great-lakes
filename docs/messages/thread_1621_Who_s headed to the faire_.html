From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-07-20 18:14:59 UTC-05:00
We won't be at the faire, but do have a funny story about getting friends
involved. We went out this past HOT Sunday with 2 friends, one a boxer and
one not. The non-boxer has said for a while that she didn't have any
interest in boxing but wouldn't mind going along for the walk and the
company. Well the non-boxer found one of the boxes that the other 3 of us
had already spent a while searching for, and she found it quickly! We told
her this made her a "natural" boxer and she should really get a stamp and
book. A few days later she told me she had decided to make a signature
stamp, but didn't really want to have a logbook and collect the box images.
I told her that was fine (lure them in s l o w l y ....). We have several
other friends who have said they "wouldn't mind going along....", so we'll
probably get a few more converted by the end of the year.

Wisconsin Hiker
