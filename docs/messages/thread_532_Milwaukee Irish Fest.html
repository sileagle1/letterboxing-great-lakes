Milwaukee Irish Fest

From: Anne Stibor (aine_42@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-10 16:28:04 UTC-07:00
Hi all.

I was hoping I'd have time to do a temporary box for Milwaukee's Irish Fest (8/19 - 8/22), but
summers have a way of getting away from me and I had neither the time to do up a box nor the time
to clear it with the festival directors. So, it'll have to wait for next year. Next year's better
anyhow, as it's the Fest's 25th anniversary and my carving skills will hopefully have improved.

However, as I'll still be there all 4 days, I will be bringing my personal stamp AND a
brand-spankin' new little personal traveller with me.

If anyone is planning on visiting the Fest (http://www.irishfest.com/), I'm pretty easy to find,
as I work at the Leinie rock stage Fri-Sun. Here's what you need to know if you want to get my
stamps:

I'll be working the following hours in the booth on the west side of the stage: Fri - 8 to
midnight; Sat - 4 to 8pm; Sun - 6 to 10pm. If I'm not actually in the booth during those times,
I'm probably backstage, gathering beer. :-) If you don't see me -- or don't know what I look like
-- feel free to ask anyone else in the booth if I'm around. You'll want to ask for Anne, though,
not Czeltic Girl.

If you think you'll be attending the Fest but don't think you'll be there when I'm working, feel
free to shoot me an e-mail and we can arrange to meet up. I'm on the grounds most of Thursday
night and a sizeable chunk of the rest of the weekend other than my work hours.

There's no special question or anything for the traveller at this time. Just getting yourself to
the Fest and finding me will be challenge enough. :-) But if you want to make me extra happy, you
can tell me who the Irish Pirate Queen was (in English or Irish).

Czeltic Girl
(Anne)
