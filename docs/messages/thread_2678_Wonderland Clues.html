Hi!

Well, I have finally distributed each "stand alone" clue for the
permanent boxes we planted for people at the gathering. Since we
wouldn't want a box to be dependent on another box's directions (in
case it disappeared), each of the clues was rewritten to start at one
of the parking lots. I see that some of you have already posted the
clues, as have many of you that created and planted your own boxes.
If my count is correct, it looks like 17 boxes have been posted and
there are 12 more remaining to post.

If any of you didn't get an email message from me with your clue,
please send me a message and I will resend it. Martini Man & I are
willingly to check on any of the boxes if/when necessary if you don't
live nearby. If you'd prefer that we post the clues under our name
(giving credit to you as the carver), we could also do that.

The only clue I haven't sent is for the Rose Gardeners. Martini Man
& I are uneasy about the hiding spot and have already mentioned this
to Speedsquare. After the last temporary boxes are removed, we think
it would be better to replant the gardeners in one of those spots.
So if you haven't found it by the 4th of July, please be sure to
check with us to see if it has been moved. After we relocate it,
we'll send the new clues to Speedsquare so she can post them.

One last thing - we will be at the park later on Sunday to pickup 2
of the temporary boxes. If anyone needs us to check on anything
(rumors of a missing box or whatever) please send me a note and we
will try to follow-up on it.

... and yes, I'm still planning to post some photos, including the
group photo with names. However we've been pretty busy with a hiking
weekend at Devil's Lake, a party at our house, planning some LB
weekend trips and of course our "real" jobs. Hopefully we'll get
something up within the next week or so.

Good luck to all on your Grail quests, we hope to venture forth this
weekend to add to our collection!

Wisconsin Hiker
