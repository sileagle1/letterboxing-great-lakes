From: klk-fw (krank@ameritech.net) | Date: 2011-11-06 16:25:50 UTC
I think I've posted this before, but it bears repeating. We use similar (albeit more rigid plastic with actual hinged flaps, not just molded like LnL's)boxes as anaerobic chambers at work. The instructions say to always open or close opposite ends simultaneously (i.e., both long flaps then both short flaps) rather than one at a time in random order or going around the box. This prevents torquing and missealing (is that a word? you know what I mean...) of the lid.
