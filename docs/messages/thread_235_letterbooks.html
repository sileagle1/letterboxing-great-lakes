Kathi,
I've done quite a bit with books and niches.
Although I considered using one for a letterbox container in a
library, my plans were slowed when librarian acquaintences cautioned
me that the books are regularly moved within a libray's shelving
systems. That, of course, would preclude a successful and secret
placement without the "intruder" book being detected by staff.
So that plan went to the back burner.
Nevertheless, I have made many "hollowed-out" books and developed a
rather interesting method of removing a major part of the page block
without using a craft knife.
I'll bring some samples to the gathering, although the method is not
one that can be done without special power tools. And it does not
lend itself to conventional book altering.
