From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-01 02:32:39 UTC
Now that CC season is over, I'm gonna be pretty much back in
letterboxing action, provided school work and weather doesn't slow me
down! First order of business it to check on, replace, and place
boxes up in Green Bay and Door county this weekend as our volleyball
girls go for thier 4th straight state title. I love it when they give
me a day off of school and force me to go up to Green Bay to
letterbox, er, I mean go watch them play! (I really do go to watch
them - they're great!)

If there are any of you out there with boxes up that way that you'd
like for me to check on, let me know - particularly if you had a
report that it may be missing or damaged. (Sorry LL, I won't have
time to check ALL your boxes! :-) )

Dragon
