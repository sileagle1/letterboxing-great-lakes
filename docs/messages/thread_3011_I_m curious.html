We found out about letterboxing from a September 2003 article in
Family Fun magazine. I had kept the article around for a long while
and didn't do anything about it. My son had a doctor appointment in
May 2004. In the waiting room, he was reading a nature magazine
(Ranger Rick, I think) and it had a letterboxing article in it. He
asked if we could do it sometime, and I told him about the Family Fun
article. Still, we waited until July 2004 to try for the first time.
When we found our first letterbox, a family that we knew had signed in
2 days before. I called the mom and talked to her about it.
Interestingly, she had read the same article I did and also waited
until July to start looking. We were going on a trip to the western
half of MI and I mentioned a letterbox we were going to try. Her
family was going to Indiana and planned to search for some boxes, as
well. When we found the letterbox in W. MI, her family had left a note
for us! We were hooked!
