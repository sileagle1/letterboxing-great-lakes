Dear Deborah:



We would like to invite you to participate in the Treasures of Oz adventure
and celebration on July 24th. This is a FREE, family-friendly event that
will appeal to anyone who loves the environment, likes photography, bird
watching, biking, hiking or geocaching.



Nine preserves in Ozaukee County will be offering an open house from 9 a.m.
until 3 p.m. with some docents and volunteers to show you around and tell
you about the site. Our celebration at Forest Beach Migratory Preserve
(formerly the Squires Golf Course) begins at 1 p.m. and goes until 5 p.m.
with music, food, exhibits and a silent auction.



Get all the details, passport, map and directions at the website:
http://www.treasuresofoz.org . Download a
passport and get it stamped at any of the sites you visit. You will be able
to have your stamps checked off and get raffle tickets for certificates from
local merchants and restaurants. Bikers and geocachers have opportunities
for additional stamps. The raffle happens after the event and winners will
be notified by email or phone.



We are advertising by email and word-of-mouth, so please forward this freely
with our thanks. We hope to see you on the 24th.
