I was just wondering how everyone is counting the boxes from the Monty
Python Exchange. Do you count the boxes you receive and plant as
placed boxes (since you placed them) or as found boxes (since they
were created by someone else)? And then how do you count the box you

From: duncan53211 (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-19 03:56:22 UTC
Unofficially...

Since the general senario is that you make one and get to plant one in
return, two halves make a whole plant. And since you could use the
clues to find a box you created, and you have a stamp of a box you did
not create, two halves make a whole find! For those not exchanging,
you might have a 1/2 count in there until the next exchange.

Also, a reminder that clues should be sent to me, not put up on LBNA.
Yes, this will undoubtedly mess up some P and F counts on the LBNA
site for some.
