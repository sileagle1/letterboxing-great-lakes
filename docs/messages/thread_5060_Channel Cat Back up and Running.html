Channel Cat Back up and Running

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2008-05-24 18:55:26 UTC
Today, as I sat there with knees together in pain my wife suggests we
drive to Kewanee (an hour away) to find Pitties new box. I can hardly
even walk to the door . . . we dismissed boxing for today (though the
weather is perfect).

Contemplating a kid friendly image of a new box to plant
titled, "Saddest Little Weiner in Town," while driving over the I-74
bridge we saw the Channel Cat out and about.

Back home we went to pick up the "Channel Cat" box and it is now up and
running for another season. The Channel Cat runs from Memorial Day to
Labor Day weekend and I have several stamps in the series, one on the
boat and at least one at every stop the Cat makes along the Mighty
Mississippi. http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/showinfo.html?gBoxId=43364

I hope many of you make the Quad Cities a travel (boxing) destination
this summer and get an opportunity to ride the Channel Cat.
