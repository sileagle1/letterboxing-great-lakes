Too much time in retail myself (10 yrs at Target) and I am always
horrified at how early stuff gets set up - still! On that same note,
I have been looking for a dress to wear to my SIL's wedding next
Saturday. I started looking a couple of weeks ago thinking that was
plenty of time. WRONG! Nice summer dresses (ie stuff you would wear
to a wedding) are on clearance or at least massively on sale. I am
half expecting in a couple of weeks to see the nice dresses and the
swimsuits replaced with winter coats....Arggg! I will say, I did
find a dress, but I went to the mall on the other side of town from
me (at mine, there was nothing unless you are under a size 12 - and
that, I am not - have not been since I was 22, LOL). Anyway, I found
a wonderful dress at 60% off AND matching shoes for half off. I
spent less than $50 on the set! You have to love that! But the fact
that the retailers think I am done shopping for summer (which does
not even start for another week or 2) in early June, well - there are
a whole lot of us out there shopping for things as we need them,
LOL! I have never understood the mindset, LOL!

What I hate to see is Back To School displays in July! The kids are just getting out of school and starting to enjoy their summer vacations, and the stores start to clear the shelves for school supplies. My husband still gets sick when he walks into a store and smells notebook paper, pencils and crayons. He never really like school.

But Christmas....that's a whole different thing! There are actually year round Christmas stores. I know that some people think that's weird too.

I just wish the spirit of Christmas, people thinking about others, people celebrating, people coming together, would last longer than those few busy weeks before Christmas and then....tear it all down!
