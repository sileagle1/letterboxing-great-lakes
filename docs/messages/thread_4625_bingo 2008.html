You'll have to pick from the general terms at the bottom to fill in
your sheet. Last year, I had no boxes, events or exchanges, so I had
all general words. It really won't make much difference which words
you pick once the calling starts, it's all random. The different
categories just add some fun and make it so the words get spread out
and not everyone picks the same 15 words.

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-16 11:27:33 UTC-05:00
Actually there are more boxes from Michigan than any other state, since I weighted it a little to account for the lower and upper peninsulas. There are the same number of MI lower peninsula boxes as there are boxes from IL, OH, WI and then there are a few additional boxes from the upper peninsula. When you consider the geographic size of the 6-state GL region, and that there were only a total of 45 boxes included, don't be surprised that there may be only 1 or 2 boxes within a reasonable distance from where you live. The goal was to spread them out evenly across the entire region.

SpringChick
