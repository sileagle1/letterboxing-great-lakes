Great Lakes Gathering 2011!

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-10-18 19:07:38 UTC
It's that time of year again -- time to begin thinking about the annual Great Lakes Gathering!

Michigan is back up as the next state in the rotation. Although the event has traditionally been held on the Saturday after Memorial Day, it was decided by group poll last fall to allow the event organizer to choose a date within the May - September time frame to allow for more flexibility in securing park facilities, etc. So the exact date will be determined by the selected host.

To start things off, I would like to hear from anyone in Michigan who is interested in either hosting the event or helping out. At this time, this is not a commitment, rather I am just trying to get an idea of what kind of interest there is. Please email me prior to the end of the month via AQ mail or at letterbox@comcast.net. When you email me, let me know how you would like to be involved and if you are up for hosting the event and have ideas, please feel free to pass along anything you're considering.

If there are multiple parties interested in hosting, we will either try to form a hosting team or will vote as a group on the options for date, place, etc.

SpringChick
