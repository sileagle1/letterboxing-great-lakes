From: Katrina Lustofin (drkaty@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-12-07 16:40:43 UTC-05:00
I think it really depends on a) what you are looking for and b) how your
feet are shaped. Do you want really warm boots? Waterproof? I have a pair
of Salomon boots that I adore. They are fairly lightweight, waterproof
(actually the rubber comes halfway up the body of the shoe part), and fit my
feet perfectly. They are also pretty rigid, which some people do not
appreciate. They aren't necessarily designed for winter wear but since my
feet get hot all the time anyway, with a pair of wool socks, they are
perfect for me.

One thing I have found, though, is that it doesn't matter how many people
tell me a particular brand or style is awesome, because all too often they
just don't fit me right. So I end up just trying on tons of boots and shoes
to find the ones that feel good, that bend in the right places, have the
arch in a comfortable location and of a comfortable height, and most
importantly for me, have a narrow enough heel to fit me.

Katy
