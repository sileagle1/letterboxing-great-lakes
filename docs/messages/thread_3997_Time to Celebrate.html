Time to Celebrate

From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2007-04-22 21:54:18 UTC
Well, after 6 weeks of being submerged under the surface of the mighty
Mississippi my Campbell's Island box has been found.

The waves were lapping at my feet still as I climbed my way to it.

Much to my surprise my cheap-o disposable gladware container only had a
couple tablespoons of water in it and a HUGE thank you goes out to the
Pitties (the last ones to find the box before it went under on St.
Patricks Day) for re-sealing the baggies so well!!

The logbook and stamp were dry as a whistle and my jaw dropped (6 weeks
underwater -- a river no less).

I brought the box home with me to tidy up and re-plant -- will probably
use a lock & locks next time for added security.

Should be back out in the same spot within the next week or two.

Hx6
