Hi Carol! Oh gosh, those stairs are about enough to kill you,
aren't they? And they are getting pretty rickety -- I wouldn't be
surprised if the city of Saugatuck closed them soon, unless they are
going to repair them.

Sorry to hear you didn't find the box. I went twice for that one.
The first time, I climbed those freaking stairs twice, not willing
to give up... I also went down (and subsequently back up) that huge
hill off to the left (south) of the top, thinking perhaps it was
that way -- after all, I think the clues said "southerly direction."

Anyway, the second time I found the box, but I am pretty sure that
the originally referred to "centipede" is no longer in place so it
makes it harder to find. As a matter of fact, I accidentally
stumbled on the box hidden under a little pile of sticks and leaves
as I was scouring the area around the dancing trees.

I actually went back a third time, last April, with OldShoe because
he had not stamped into the book and we were staying at a B&B right
near there one weekend. So I can verify that it was there as of
early April 2003. We replaced the box under a centipede that was a
short way down the hillside from the dancing trees, as it was the
nearest thing we could find to a centipede and that is what the
clues indicated.

Good to hear from you again!

Deb
