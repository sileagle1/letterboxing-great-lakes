Here's the schedule:

Before 10am -
Some will be arriving early and may set-up the shelter without me

at that time. Otherwise you are on your own.

10am - Official start

Noon - Group picture
12:15 - Potluck lunch (I plan to bring charcoal, etc. if anyone has need.)

7 pm - Gathering over (after gathering get-together?)

Hope I didn't miss anything. I wasn't expecting this week to be so
busy at school...
