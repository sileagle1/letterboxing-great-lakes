Forgive the cross-post, but I am trying to get the word out... We
are in NJ, so we don't know too many 'boxers in OH.

In honor of mystery day, I planted a box while visiting OH
recently. We were ostensibly out there to visit my aunt, who is
currently fighting cancer, and I had dedicated the box to her. I
was surprised by the interest she took in it, and she is eagerly
awaiting its first visitors. So I wondered if some boxers in NE
Ohio could help me out! The clue is here:

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?
boxnum=26109&boxname=Friend_and_Foe

It is also listed on Atlas Quest. I don't think the clues are that
hard, esp for someone who lives in that area, but heck, I'll even
help out if things aren't dead obvious after a little searching on
Google. All I ask is that people who visit the box leave her a
little note in the logbook. It is nice to see her so excited about
something, and I know that the 'boxing community is a very generous
one.

Thanks in advance for your help with this little project!

-- Queen B of the B's
