Hi Grapevine Girl,

I was in Vegas over Valentine's weekend, and I hunted down what is
reported to be the only surviving box on the strip - Feeling Lucky
(Reprise). It's right on the strip, which makes it nearly impossible
to hunt undetected - my cousin started tap-dancing to create a
diversion to draw attention away from me when I replaced it. It's a
really cute stamp, and worth the hunt.

I didn't get to search for any other boxes in Vegas (like the Neon
Boneyard), so I can't report on those.

Enjoy your trip!
