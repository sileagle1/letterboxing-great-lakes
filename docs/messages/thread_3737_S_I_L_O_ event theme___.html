From: Carol Osada (cjosada@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-02-16 02:21:23 UTC-06:00
Because the Special Indoor Letterboxing Outing (SILO) was originally scheduled to be held on Chinese New Years (Sun, Feb, 18) only to learn that the restaurant doesn't open for lunch on Sundays, so we switched the event to Sat. - Chinese New years eve. Then, some creative minds started suggesting ideas for the SILO event...like a Chinese New Years theme, participants should come dressed head to toe in the color RED, and each person was asked to hand carve a stamp with their trail name in Chinese characters (English Chinese Translator on line), and then other wacky things started happening. The SILO event will be such a fun way to spend an afternoon. It's also a great excuse to get out of the cold and have a "RED" hot time with friends. Not to mention the other funny things that Fungus Woman and I have up out kimono sleeves!

Beetle
