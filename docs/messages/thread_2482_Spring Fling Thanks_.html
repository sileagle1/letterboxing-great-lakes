Spring Fling Thanks!

From: brownrabbit_teamrabbit (teamrabbit@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-07 21:25:02 UTC
We just wanted to extend a BIG THANK YOU to everyone at the Spring
Fling yesterday for contributing to a spectacular day of
letterboxing. Thank you TJ_Mich for sparing what limited and
valuable time you have to host this event, and JB Books for lending
them a hand. Thank you to everyone who attended: those we met for
the first time, and those whom we've previously had the honor to
know, those who live a scant 10 minutes away, and those who came from
across (or outside of) the state, those who just began letterboxing a
month ago, and those who have been at it for years. It was most
excellent to run into so many of you on the trails time and time (and
time) again, and to trade tales and stories from our collective
myriad of experiences... It's days like this that make for a feeling
of family and belonging in the letterboxing world...

A special thank you to Trail Troll for hangin' with us for most of
the day. We found as many boxes as we could, Raine permitting. We
hope you had luck with the Lovebirds!

We'll be back for the other seven boxes soon!

Thank you!
Team Rabbit
Gray, Brown, & Rainy Day
