Re: LB Exchange Box Clues Posted

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-05-31 13:25:55 UTC
hmmmm... part of the mystery maybe as I do tend to often use
puzzles conjunction with mystery letterboxes. Making up puzzles,
codes, riddles and mysteries is one of the things I enjoy most about
doing letterbox clues and I would probably do so for all of my boxes
if people were more into solving them. But alas, mystery boxes in
this neck of the woods seem to be destined for a lonely existence --
I think I currently have at least 3 mystery boxes that have never
been found and 3-4 others that have not had a finder within the past
year. I was overjoyed to receive an e-mail from Team Rabbit the
other day sharing that they had recently cracked the Case of the
Missing Salmon. They also completed the Wine Country puzzle to find
those 2 boxes, as well as finding the Pink Cadillac mystery box.

The only reason I gave the city on the Tuckaway box and didn't make
it a true mystery box was because I made the "rule" that the exchange
boxes couldn't be planted as mystery boxes and had to at least give
the city to ensure that they would have some finders. No fun for the
creator if nobody ever finds their box!

Anyway, about the puzzles... if you want an inside peek at the
process, plan on attending the Building a Mystery workshop at the
June GL Gathering.

SpringChick
