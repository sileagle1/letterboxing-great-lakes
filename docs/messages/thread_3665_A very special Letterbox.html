From: beckers410 (Mathw613@aol.com) | Date: 2007-02-01 03:57:40 UTC
Hey group, this one is for your eyes only! I will not be posting the
clues for this letterbox on any website. I will ONLY provide them to
you, dear letterboxers.

This letterbox can be found somewhere on my driveway, underneath the
snow that is now falling.

To find it you must shovel, starting at the street and work your way
back to my garage.

WARNING!! This is a time sensitive box that will only be there for 10
hours. So, you must quickly make your way to my home and search your
heart out BEFORE I go to work in the morning.

~Hart x6

P.S. This will be the first in a series of letterboxes -- a new one
will be hidden EVERY time it snows. Enjoy the hunt!!
