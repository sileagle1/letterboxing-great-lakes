But we also got this nice message about a box we planted up in the U.P. almost 9 years ago:


Greetings from the North Country

Two more of our grandchildren who we have introduced to letterboxing adventures. They don’t have trail names yet, so they haven’t posted on the letterboxing site. We have six grandchildren and all are letterboxers!


Your box is in great shape with that large piece of outer bark holding up surprisingly well. The box is completely dry inside so your journal is safe. A wonderful stamp and a wonderful subject. One of our grandfathers was indeed a shanty boy.
