Boxacon thoughts

From: jannaode (jannaode@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-10-05 04:44:23 UTC
Just wanted to say what an awesome time I had! My husband said I acted like a kid at Christmas. It was my first event, and certainly will not be my last.

Thank you to all who took part in making it happen. I can't imagine all of the work it took to organize it all. It was so fun to put faces with trail names and personalities.

Probably one of my favorite parts was doing all of the pre-event background research for the personal travelers. Thanks to everyone who put those mysterious questions out there that picqued my interest and helped me get to know you better.

I have a few questions about some of the personal travelers. You can email me the answers if you want, assuming you want to use the same info for future events. I will be try to not have my questions be a spoiler for anyone.

Altchar - I did look up and find the answer to "Oh Horrors!" I would love to know more about your experience with all of that. How interesting.

Atomic Beans - I forgot to congratulate you! What did you do your paper on?

Lock Shock and Barrel - I brought you some beads but couldn't find you. Waaah. I'd love to hear about your project though.

tat2bob and kimberly - I found the info but am still a bit confused since the era was before your time, I would assume. Can you explain a bit more? Was that a relative of yours or something?

Terrio - P.R.?

Trail Rider - You totally have me baffled on your question, but I might have an idea or two. Any more clues?

Pugnasties - What are "chucks?"

Wormy Wanderers - Any self-respecting 12 yr old boy knows the person you asked about, lol. Unfortunately I didn't find you, however. Hopefully next time.

One love jouneying - I was ready to give you the answer, but couldn't find you. Grrr.

Thanks again to everyone. Looking forward to seeing you again!

Revode P.S. To those who asked - my stamp was a carving of me sitting at my computer, with keyboard, mouse, and my cat watching.
