Home Page Photos

From: SpringChick (springchick@letterbox-mi.com) | Date: 2005-12-06 15:52:51 UTC
I'm thinking the group home page can use a facelift for the new
year. I would like to post photos from group members taken while
letterboxing in the GL Region, rotating them out at the start of each
month.

I created a folder in the PHOTOS section called ~Home Page Photos (it
is the very last folder). If you have any scenic photos taken while
letterboxing in the GL Region that you would like to see on the group
home page, please feel free to upload to this folder. I'm looking
primarily for scenery photos, not so much people photos -- unless you
have an absolutely adorable shot (with a scenic background) of your
little one or four-legged friend letterboxing.

Please resize your photo to be 500 pixels wide with a 72 dpi
resolution before you upload it. In the comments, include your name
or trail name, the place where the photo was taken and the date it
was taken. I will use photos from this folder for the home page
based on appropriateness to the month, trying to give everyone who
submits something a chance. Preference will be given to letterboxers
who actually live in the GL Region, although any group member can
submit a photo taken in the area.

Looking forward to seeing some nice photographs from around the GL
states! Thanks!

SpringChick
