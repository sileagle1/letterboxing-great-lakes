Bingo side-wagers

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-18 20:14:37 UTC
When I saw SpringChick's announcement about Letterboxing Bingo I
started to wonder how long that game might go. After all, there are
150 items on the list. How long might it take for your 30-item list to
be `called' before you've got BINGO?
Maybe early to mid-February? How about March? Could be April or even
May. Who knows?
If you'd like to add some excitement to the Letterboxing Bingo game,
here's how:
Email me the date you think the game will end. In other words, when
will she pull the item that finally completes some player's list?

I must have your guess before January 1, 2006.
Mail to jparkerg at Yahoo dot com
(my usual address for this group)
In the subject line, put "Bingo".

I'll award the winner's prize to the person who comes closest to the
actual day or is closest before the day the Bingo Game ends. If you
choose February 15th, for example, and someone else chooses the 20th,
and the game ends on the 19th, you win. In other words, you'll hold
all of the days until the next guesser hits the calendar. But you
don't know who has chosen which days or how far apart they've fallen
(and neither does SC!).

I'll select a prize for the lucky winner.
You do not need to be a player in the big game to participate;
however, if you are a player in the Letterbox Bingo Game and you win
the side-wager, you'll get a special prize.

For more about SpringChick's game, check the files section of our
Great Lakes Letterboxing Group. Go to: LBBING, towards the bottom of
the files list.
Both the main list and the rules are included.
There is still time to get into the game.
