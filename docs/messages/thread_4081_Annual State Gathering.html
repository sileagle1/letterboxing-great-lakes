From: doubledragonletterboxer (M8KC5008@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-21 21:57:23 UTC
I think that we should host annual state gatherings. Initially the
early GL Gathering were small enough and the interest in the sport
itself was small enough that it could accommodate everyone. There are
new members every day and more and more articles every year that bring
in new members. It would be nice that each year it could be held in
different areas of the state. I think it would be helpful for not just
teachers but everyone who can't spend the extra money on gas to have
it locally. It isn't like people from out of state wouldn't be able to
come to the other state's events.
