I wanted to let everyone know that there will be 2 drawings held at
the Fairies event and am posting this beforehand so you have time to
do what it takes to meet the requirements before Saturday.

First Drawing -- Open to any attendee who has planted a new letterbox
in 2008. Event boxes don't count -- it must be a box you created, you
planted and you made clues for. Any type of box, including mystery
boxes are okay, except WOM. It must be publicly listed at either LbNA
or AQ (otherwise how would we know it exists?). If you planted the
box as a team (i.e. family, husband/wife, etc.) each person who
participated in the planting of the box can enter.

Second Drawing -- Open to any attendee who has gone out in 2008 and
done maintenance on at least 4 of their previously planted boxes.
This means you physically went to the box yourself, checking the
validity of clues as you went. Once there, you took the box out of
its hiding spot and checked the condition of the container and
contents, replacing torn, worn and missing items as necessary. This
is on your honor, but be prepared to tell us what boxes you've given TLC.

For both drawings you must be at the gathering at some point during
the day and enter yourself before 3:00 p.m. Drawings will be held
sometime shortly after 3:00 p.m. You need not be present at the
drawing to win, only to enter yourself.

There are prizes ;-)

Looking forward to seeing everyone at the gathering -- it is a
wonderful park -- perfect spot for a spring gathering!

SpringChick
