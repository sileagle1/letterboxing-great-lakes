Hey all --- just writing to let you know that I've pulled the 2 boxes I had in the Hudsonville Nature Center and have retired them.

They have been in place since 2005, and since I have a totally sweet new series, I thought I'd plant them there. (Stayed tuned for those clues)

"They" being "K1P2" and "Teacher's Helper".
"K1P2" just couldn't be found, and when I went to find it yesterday, the trails have REALLY changed, so I deeply appologize to everyone who had such a hard time finding that box -- how lame of me.

"Teachers Helper", on the other hand, had a very full logbook. On one page it said "Stamp missing" and then 2 finds later, there was a note about the neat stamp. So, whoever it was the took the stamp and then replaced it -- THANK YOU!!! I can't begin to tell you how many people mentioned that this was their first find. How stinkin' cool!!!

So anyway, thanks again... and stay tuned for the cool new series coming up in September!
