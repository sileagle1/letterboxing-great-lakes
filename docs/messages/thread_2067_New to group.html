New to group

From: Brenda McAloon (persnickety_mac@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-03 08:33:05 UTC-08:00
New to the group but not to letterboxing. I and my
family have been doing occasional letterboxing for
about 2 years and we are wanting to do more. It is
really surprising to see how this hobby has taken off
in the past two years. There were only abut 20 boxes
in northern Ill. when I started. We have only found
21 boxes so far and are looking foward to doing more.
I hope it is now easier to post boxes we hide. I hid
one when we first started but had a hard time getting
it posted on the sight. It came up on the new list
but never got over to the Ill. list. After that I got
discouraged. A few boxers did find it then one
e-mailed that it had been destroyed. By that time I
was unable to hike out to replace it because of a bone
spur in my foot. Thats all better now and we are back
on the trails. I know changes have been made and am
looking forward to placing some boxes.

Look forward to chatting with all of you. Thank you
Fungas Woman for telling me about this group.

Brenda
