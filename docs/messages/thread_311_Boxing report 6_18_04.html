Boxing report 6/18/04

From: (duncan53211@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-19 17:00:06 UTC
Well, I did end up making it back down to northern IL on Friday. I
picked up some of the gathering boxes, left some, and found some
others on the way down and on the way home. He's the scoop:

These are permament boxes left where found:

Smoking Fish - In great shape. Lots of fun, though was confusing
for a bit. Please make more!

Playing Possum - in good shape. Fun journey thru the woods.

Cooler by the Lake (Pico and 'D) - I found it down the hill a bit.
Snuggled under root so hopefully it will stick to its spot now.

Fool's Errand #17 (The Star) - In good shape. The mushrooms made me
hungry. The mosquitos were hungry too.

Cooler by the Lake (Team Taco) - Found scattered around at hiding
location. I think the hiding spot is a living quarters for an
animal. Packaged things back up and placed back in spot, but you
might want to check on it and/or move it.

Fool's Errand #1 (The Magician) - in good shape.

Fool's Errand #18 (Moon) - in good shape.

Fool's Errand #19 (Sun) - in good shape.

Big Foot - in good shape. Go do Smoking Fish to learn about the
real Big Foot ;-)

Chocolate City #1 - First finder!!!! In good shape. Made me hungry.

Chocolate City #2 - NOT first finder (go figure). In good shape.
Made me hungrier. Too bad the concessions was closed.

These were temporary boxes, and are now picked up to be sent back to
their owners or replanted:

Wherever Corn is Grown
Cooler by the Lake (Artistamp)
Cooler by the Drake (TJ Mich)
Michi-gami (SpringChick)
RainyDay Rabbit (Team Rabbit)
Freebird (Delirium)
Cooler by the Lake (Team Bindlestiff)
Spirit of the Lake (SpringChick)- found scattered about and wet.
Hope it is not too damaged.

And the COOLEST of the bunch yesterday.....

Dragon's Hitchhiker!!!

Team Taco, Acorn and Golden Boy made and hid this HH/box just for
me. They secretly put clues under my wiper blade both on Friday at
Chain o' Lakes and on Sat at the gathering, but I didn't have time
to go look for this "Mathman" box (as it was called in the clues)
until yesterday. I also almost gave up on this one, until I
happened upon an area that looked like what was described in the
clues as I was searching for the other boxes. Even then it wasn't
easy. Great clues, great spot, great box. Maybe I'll hide a box in
the same spot with the same clues for you guys to try.

As for the HH, I'm still deciding where to plant it. I'm thinking
of sending it down to the Atlanta gathering with all the other
traveling event stamps I'm sending, but then those of you up here
will miss out on seeing the great stamp they made! Decisions,
decisions...

Well, after all that, I'm now

E 2 P 75 F 345 X 39 H 22 and very mosquito bitten!

Dragon
