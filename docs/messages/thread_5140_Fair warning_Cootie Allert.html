From: Atom118 (Aatom118@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-06-24 14:03:00 UTC-05:00
Ideally, you should stamp in with a fingerprint (and maybe draw
something around it to mimic your sig-stamp). Then find someone else to
infect!

AQ says "A type of letterbox where one tries to sneak around hiding the
box on another personperhaps in an open backpack or jacket pocket. Once
you 'catch a cootie', you stamp in and try to pawn it off onto another
unsuspecting letterboxer. Cooties are often signed into with the stamp
of your thumb or fingers that you draw on to embellish instead of the
traditional signature stamp, although many people prefer to use their
signature stamp instead. For more cootie talk, visit our Cooties board."

Atom118

I knew if I admitted to my secret love of cooties that I would become a target for them in the future. I know that some people really despise them. But a cootie is still a stamp. A stamp is a stamp, or at least that's how I look at it.

Oh, we can always count on this friendly local bunch of letterboxers to 'share.' The generosity knows no limits. I went home from a gathering and 3 weeks later I was still finding cooties that had been slipped into the pockets of the removable inner lining of my coat. I couldn't figure out what that hard lump was. I burst out laughing!

I think the reason this group has frequent gatherings is just to unload cooties. But whatever the reason, I love you all, and I'm sorry I missed out on all the fun at BofaF.
