Winter Olympics...and the winners are.....

From: Familyof6 (familyof6portland@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-02-27 23:00:50 UTC
Wow! What an great day we had today at the Letterboxing Winter Olympics. The weather held out (even if the trails were DEEP with snow)and fun was had by all. So, who won the gold?

The Gold (1st Place) went to Bahama Mama and Martini Mon (Bahamas) aka as Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man...although this was not much of a surpirsie since we didn't see them at the lodge from 10am until nearly 3:30pm.

The Silver (2nd Place) went to Australia ...aka Atomic Beans. See" Those beans do work!

The Bronze (3rd Place) went to USA....aka known as Booty Bandits.

All three teams walked off with a good stash of prizes, but perhaps the BEST part was presenting Spartanfans (Greece)with boxes upon boxes of diapers so when the newest Spartanfan arrives in April, they'll have PLENTY of togas to go around! Justhappy2cya even knitted a baby blaket in their favorite colors (green & white). ENJOY those diapers, Spartanfans.

Now, talk about creativity! Tiptoe and Tonto were both sporting some golden locks and EVERYONE represented their countries well! WOW!

Thanks everyone. I'm glad you all had a great time. Please post pictures if you have them as we forgot out camera.

-Familyof6 (Canada)
