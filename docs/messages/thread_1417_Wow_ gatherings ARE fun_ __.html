Wow, gatherings ARE fun! :)

From: Doodle & Deedle Bug (doodle_n_deedle@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-12 14:44:23 UTC-07:00
We had an unofficial mini gathering here in Northern
Ohio today to say good-bye to one of our area
letterboxers, Gamecock, whose job is taking her
further South, and sadly, her wonderful letterboxes
with her. Although small, it was lots of fun putting
some faces with trail names, and now we're more
excited than ever for next weekend!

In addition to meeting Gamecock, we met Celtic Cat
from PA (the friend who got Gamecock into letterboxing
initially), the husband & wife team The Cat's Meow,
and Blaze (of Ohio, since I know there is/was someone
else from this list who uses the trailname Blaze) who
planted the letterbox that got Deedle & I into
letterboxing initially.

Doodle Bug.
