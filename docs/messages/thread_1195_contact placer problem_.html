Re: contact placer problem?

From: cerealcityboxers (cerealcityboxers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-21 15:56:23 UTC

We've had sporadic luck at best over the last two years using the
contact the placer feature of the web site. That's why we place our
email in our box clues, so anyone who wants to can contact us
directly. We've seen others do this also. Whenever we find an email
address in the clues, we report back to the placer on the condition of
their box. We don't trust the contact the placer feature. Too many
variables. Is the feature broke? Is the placer still active? Did
the placer ignore your message? (I know for a fact that this does
happen) Does the box have a new owner and is your message still going
to the old owner? Way too many variables.
