From: Tom & Jill (tj_mich@ameritech.net) | Date: 2005-05-31 17:38:08 UTC-04:00
Hmmm... nothing right by the airport that I've ever heard of. Mirkwood's web
site does show the stamp images from a planned series of 8 boxes at Crosswinds
Marsh, which is several miles south of the airport (and is in fact a wetland
created to replace some wetlands lost during a Metro airport
expansion), but to
my knowledge only the first 2 boxes were ever planted.

http://www.ruthannzaroff.com/letterboxing/index.htm

Mirkwood also had 2 boxes at Lower Huron Metropark, a few miles west of the
airport, and Clickermom3 has a new box at Oakwoods Metropark, a few miles
south.

We found the 4 local Mirkwood boxes in summer 2003, but do not know their
present status. The locations are all enjoyable places to visit, in
any event!

--TJ_Mich
