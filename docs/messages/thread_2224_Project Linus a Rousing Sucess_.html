Project Linus a Rousing Sucess!

From: Doglvrs RASW (doglvrs_4@msn.com) | Date: 2006-01-30 15:56:46 UTC

Thank you to all who came to lend a hand and hook at the Project Linus
Mini-meet!
We had a great time and got a few things accomplished - although we did not
manage to finish an entire blanket, we did get a lot of squares edged. A
couple of letterboxes were found and a book donated to the library ~ about
letterboxing of course.

We were 6 strong with hooks in hand - thank you to Jacks, SpringChick, Boot
Prints, Howling Coyote and Tiptoe for giving an afternoon to a good cause ~
I believe there are enough squares to make 3 blankets (correct me Jacks if
I'm wrong) to donate to Project Linus, that means at least 3 warm kids for
years to come!

I think it would be great to make this an annual event - maybe a mini-meet
that meets on the same day in multiple locations ~ how cool would that be?

After the library closed, 5 of us had a lovely dinner together, and we all
left "very happy"!! ; )

Thank you again - Letterboxers ROCK!


Doglvrs
