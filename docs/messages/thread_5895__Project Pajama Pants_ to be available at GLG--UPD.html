FungusWoman has generously offered to give any donations from this
letterbox of hers and any of your donations----to my son's unit rather
than the IL group. My son's unit is getting ready to deploy out of Fort
Bragg NC with the 82nd Airborne. They are a heavy field artillery unit
and are front line combat soldiers that shoot the 105 and 155mm
Howitzers. (The big cannon looking things that recoil after firing.)

My son, Greg, is not deploying with them this time (which was not by
choice---but by orders to become a recruiter in Rochester NY for 3
years.) He would much rather be deploying with his guys but they all
have to take their turn with a 3 year rotation doing something else
before returning to a 4th deployment if they are career military. These
are his soldiers that are going this time without him and he is
struggling with this---even though they are in good hands with the
soldier who Greg recommended to become their leader---Sgt. Chris Hall.
I talked to Greg tonight to see if sending his unit these items was
possible and it sounds like he is really grateful to know they are going
to get the same kind of generous support he and his unit got when he was
deployed---so mega thanks and blessing klk/fw for your generous offer.
