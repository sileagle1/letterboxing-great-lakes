5th Anniversary!

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-07-14 04:50:08 UTC
Today is the 5th anniversary of finding our very first letterbox (E-
way trails - Madison, WI). We've found a LOT of boxes since then, but
it was fun to think about that first box today. We were out in
sandals and I had some very basic supplies (2 colors of ink pads, our
sig stamps and our brand new logbooks) in an old summer canvas purse
as we headed down the trail. We were SO excited to actually find it!

So today, we also found some boxes. We had better supplies (and
better footwear) and still had a lot of fun!

Thanks to all of you who place boxes!!

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
