Looking back at my stats from this year, I really didn't do a whole
lot of traditional letterboxing. :( My favorite had to be going back
to Starved Rock with Manic Mechanic and finding the Illinois Canyon
box I missed with Wiener Mouse on the previous trip. We only found
that box, but the hike was a ton of fun. Especially trying to put the
box back when a group of 8 or 10 hikers came down the trail and
stopped very close to the area. We had a chance to do a lot of hiking
and I got a few really beautiful photos.

If I could do one series in the coming year I would go find the two
Shipwrecked boxes by Agent Ink in Milwaukee. Manic Mechanic and I sat
at his kitchen table for quite a long time until we figured out those
clues. It has been over a year since then and I think by now I have
actually lost the decoded version, but I don't want them to go
missing before I get the chance to find them! :) Doing any
letterboxing at all would be good, though. ;)

Shutterfly
