Coffee Beans and Atom118 leave the Second City to achieve The World

From: azobox (jparkerg@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-07-20 13:21:47 UTC
Coffee Beans and Atom118 submitted the required documentation to grant
their admittance into the growing group of letterboxers who have
successfully completed the "Around the World in 80 Days" series.
The Chicago couple ventured to the hinterlands of Michigan to quietly
wrap up their tour of `foreign' cities.
The Travel Team was aware of some Michigan roots in the successful
searchers, but doubt that it proved any advantage to the travelers.
However, they did make short work of the 15 city tour.

Congratulations to Coffee Beans and Atom118.
