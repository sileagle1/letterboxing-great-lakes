MI Inches Up Alongside WI...

From: SpringChick (letterbox@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-31 10:47:47 UTC
According to last count, MI needed boxes in 12 counties and WI stood
at 11 to go. But, hold on! A technicality has been brought to my
attention, which puts Michigan neck and neck with Wisconsin (thanks
Moonduck).

It seems that the Pink Cadillac box (yes, belonging to yours truly),
which was listed on LbNA as being in Wexford County, is actually
located in Missaukee County. I knew this when I originally listed
it and chose to list it otherwise to help in identifying the city
(it is a mystery box that gives county but not city), but had
forgotten about this discrepancy when compiling the list of first in
the county boxes.

So for purposes of accuracy in identifying the first box in each
county, and of course to inch MI one box closer to the finish line,
I have corrected the LbNA listing for Pink Cadillac to be Missaukee
County, which as luck would have it, was one of the Michigan boxless
counties! So this brings the Michigan count down to 11.

You can see the map/list of first boxes in each MI county here:

http://www.stampinthebox.com/projects/micounties.asp

SpringChick
