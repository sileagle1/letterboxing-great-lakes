Boxes for 4th Annual Great Lakes event on JUNE 3

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2006-03-28 02:11:05 UTC
Thanks to all of you who volunteered to make boxes for the event! I
have already received 6 boxes from "out of towners" and wanted to
remind the rest of you that we'd like to have all the boxes in our
hands by April 30 (less than 5 weeks away). That will give us several
weekends in May to get out and hide those boxes. When you're ready to
send the box, just contact me for my mailing address.

We really appreciate the local boxers placing their own boxes since
that will lighten the load of planting AND it gives us a few to look
for ourselves! If you're planting your own box, please email me the
clues by May 26 or sooner if you want them included in the clue
booklet.

In case you haven't looked at the event website
(http://2006greatlakesgathering.homestead.com) in a while, we will
probably have 40+ boxes and are approaching 100 expected attendees.

Let's hope for good weather!

Wisconsin Hiker
